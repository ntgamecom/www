# Beginner Guide — Getting Started

{/* ===== 游戏概述 ===== */}

## 游戏概述 / Game Overview

《双影奇境》是一款 **强制双人合作** 的动作冒险游戏。你必须与另一位玩家一起游玩，无论是本地分屏还是在线联机。游戏节奏紧凑，每章都会交替呈现科幻与奇幻两种截然不同的世界观，并引入全新的能力与机制。

Split Fiction is a **mandatory co-op** action-adventure game. You must play with another player, either via local split-screen or online co-op. The game has a tight pace, alternating between sci-fi and fantasy worlds every chapter, introducing new abilities and mechanics each time.

{/* ===== 如何开始 ===== */}

## 如何开始 / How to Start

## 本地分屏 / Local Split-Screen

1. 确保两个控制器连接至同一台设备
2. 启动游戏后选择 **本地合作 (Local Co-op)**
3. 主玩家使用主账号，第二位玩家使用访客账号
4. 主玩家推进的进度双方共享

1. Make sure two controllers are connected to the same device
2. Launch the game and select **Local Co-op**
3. P1 uses the main account, P2 uses a guest account
4. Progress is shared between both players

## 在线联机 / Online Co-op

1. 一位玩家购买完整版，另一位下载 **免费友票版 (Friend's Pass)**
2. 主玩家在主菜单选择 **在线合作 (Online Co-op)** → **邀请好友**
3. 友票玩家接受邀请后自动加入
4. 只有主玩家的存档会记录章节进度，但收集品双方独立保存

1. One player buys the full game; the other downloads the **Free Friend's Pass**
2. The host selects **Online Co-op** → **Invite Friend** from the main menu
3. The Friend's Pass player accepts and joins automatically
4. Only the host's save tracks chapter progress, but collectibles are saved per player

## 合作机制概述 / Co-op Mechanics Overview

《双影奇境》的合作机制比前作更进一步。每一章不仅给 Mio 和 Zoe 不同的能力，还要求两人在完全不同的规则下协作配合。

Split Fiction's co-op mechanics go further than the previous title. Each chapter not only gives Mio and Zoe different abilities but also demands cooperation under completely different rule sets.

## 双世界协作 / Dual-World Cooperation

科幻章节中 Mio 的能力偏向科技侧，如能量护盾和重力操控；奇幻章节中 Zoe 的能力偏向魔法侧，如元素法术和召唤。两人需要在自己的强项时刻承担主要推进角色，在对方的章节中提供辅助配合。

In sci-fi chapters, Mio's abilities lean toward technology like energy shields and gravity manipulation; in fantasy chapters, Zoe's abilities lean toward magic like elemental spells and summoning. Each player takes the lead in their own story while supporting the other in their partner's world.

## 同步操作 / Synchronized Actions

- **双人开关** / Dual switches — 两人必须同时按下两个相隔较远的开关
- **接力跳跃** / Relay jumps — 一人搭建平台，另一人借助平台前进
- **镜像谜题** / Mirror puzzles — 两人在镜像空间中执行对称动作
- **合力搬运** / Carry together — 部分大型物体需要两人同时搬运

## 新手合作技巧 / Co-op Tips for Beginners

## 沟通至关重要 / Communication Is Key

几乎所有谜题都需要 **两个人同步行动**。建议使用语音聊天保持实时沟通。即使无法语音，游戏内置的 ping 系统也能帮助你指示目标位置。

Almost every puzzle requires **synchronized actions**. Voice chat is highly recommended for real-time coordination. Even without voice, the in-game ping system helps indicate target locations.

## 视角管理 / Camera Management

- 平台跳跃时，控制视角的玩家应将镜头拉远
- 不要两人同时疯狂转动视角
- 战斗时尽量让两个角色都在视野范围内

- During platforming, the camera-controlling player should zoom out
- Don't both spin the camera wildly
- During combat, try to keep both characters on screen

## 分工探索 / Divide and Explore

许多场景有两条分支路径，建议两人分别探索后共享发现。这样可以更快找到隐藏的手稿和收集品。

Many areas have branching paths. Have each player explore a different route and share findings. This speeds up finding hidden manuscripts and collectibles.

## 无限续关 / Unlimited Retries

游戏有 **无限重试** 机制，死亡后会在最近检查点立即复活，没有任何惩罚。大胆尝试不同的解法吧。

The game has **unlimited retries**. You respawn immediately at the nearest checkpoint with no penalty. Feel free to experiment with different solutions.

## 分屏设置 / Split-Screen Settings

- 分屏方向可设置为 **水平** 或 **垂直**
- 垂直分屏更适合超宽显示器
- 可在设置中随时切换

- Split-screen can be set to **horizontal** or **vertical**
- Vertical split works better on ultrawide monitors
- You can switch at any time in the settings

## 常见问题 / FAQ

**Q: 我可以和不同平台的玩家一起玩吗？**
A: 不可以。PC 版 (Steam/EA App 可以互通)，主机版只能同平台联机。

**Q: Can I play cross-platform?**
A: No. PC (Steam/EA App can play together), consoles are platform-locked.

**Q: 友票版需要 EA Play 订阅吗？**
A: 不需要。友票版完全免费，无需任何订阅。

**Q: Does the Friend's Pass require EA Play subscription?**
A: No. The Friend's Pass is completely free with no subscription required.

**Q: 游戏需要多少存储空间？**
A: 大约需要 85GB 可用空间。

**Q: How much storage space is needed?**
A: Approximately 85GB of free space is required.

**Q: 支持光线追踪吗？**
A: 支持。PS5 和 Xbox Series X 以及 PC 版都有光线追踪选项。

**Q: Does it support ray tracing?**
A: Yes. Ray tracing is available on PS5, Xbox Series X, and PC.

## 章节选择 / Chapter Select

完成游戏后，可以从主菜单的 **章节选择 (Chapter Select)** 回到任意关卡补收集品或挑战更高难度。章节选择会保留你当前所有的能力和升级，方便回溯通关。

After completing the game, use **Chapter Select** from the main menu to revisit any chapter for missed collectibles or higher difficulty challenges. Chapter Select retains all your current abilities and upgrades.
