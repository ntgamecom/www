## 友票系统 / Friend's Pass

《双影奇境》延续了 Hazelight 经典的 **友票系统 (Friend's Pass)**：

Split Fiction continues Hazelight's signature **Friend's Pass** system:

- 只需要 **一位玩家购买完整版游戏**
- 另一位玩家可免费下载 **友票版 (Friend's Pass)** 并畅玩全部关卡
- 支持跨平台联机：PC 端 Steam 与 EA App 玩家可互通
- 主机端支持同平台联机 (PS5 ↔ PS5, Xbox ↔ Xbox)
- 支持本地分屏合作与在线联机
- 友票玩家也可获得成就/奖杯

- Only **one player needs to purchase** the full game
- The other player can download the **free Friend's Pass** and play through the entire game
- Cross-platform support: PC Steam and EA App players can play together
- Console supports same-platform play (PS5 ↔ PS5, Xbox ↔ Xbox)
- Supports both local split-screen and online co-op
- Friend's Pass players can earn achievements/trophies too

## 故事简介 / Story Overview

Mio 和 Zoe 是两位年轻作家，她们被一家科技公司诱骗参与一项实验，意识被困在各自的虚构故事世界中。Mio 创作科幻故事，充满赛博朋克城市与外星文明；Zoe 创作奇幻故事，遍布魔法森林与古老城堡。两人必须穿越彼此的世界，合作解开谜题，最终逃离这个危险的虚拟牢笼。

Mio and Zoe are two young writers lured into a tech company's experiment, trapping their consciousness inside their own fictional story worlds. Mio writes sci-fi stories filled with cyberpunk cities and alien civilizations; Zoe writes fantasy tales filled with magical forests and ancient castles. Together they must traverse each other's worlds, cooperate to solve puzzles, and ultimately escape this dangerous virtual prison.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanics

- **双世界交替** / Dual-world switching — 每章交替在科幻与奇幻世界间切换
- **作家能力** / Writer abilities — 每章获得基于故事主题的全新能力
- **同步解谜** / Synchronized puzzles — 多数谜题需要两人精确配合
- **叙事选择** / Narrative choices — 分支对话影响角色关系发展
- **隐藏手稿** / Hidden manuscripts — 收集散落的手稿碎片解锁背景故事

## 实用提示 / Pro Tips

1. **语音沟通至关重要** / Voice communication is essential — 大部分双机制解谜需要实时协调
2. **探索隐藏路径** / Explore hidden paths — 手稿和升级材料遍布关卡的角落
3. **交替扮演角色** / Alternate roles — 每章能力不同，轮流体验两个角色的玩法
4. **注意环境互动** / Watch for environmental interactions — 发光物体通常可以互动
5. **善用检查点** / Use checkpoints wisely — 游戏自动保存频繁，可以大胆尝试
6. **两个世界都要搜** / Search both worlds — 收集品分布在科幻和奇幻两个世界中
