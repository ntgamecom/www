## 核心战斗机制 / Core Combat Mechanics

《双影奇境》的战斗系统延续了 Hazelight 一贯的理念：**没有单人战斗**。战斗中的每个敌人、每个场景都是为双人配合设计的。单人无法有效应对多数战斗。

Split Fiction's combat system continues Hazelight's core philosophy: **no solo fighting**. Every enemy and every scenario is designed for dual-player cooperation. A single player cannot effectively handle most encounters.

## 基础战斗规则 / Basic Combat Rules

- 每章的能力决定了战斗风格，从远程射击到近战格斗各不相同
- 敌人有明确的弱点提示，通常需要一名玩家制造机会，另一名玩家输出
- 场景中散布可交互的机关和环境陷阱，合理利用可大幅降低战斗难度
- 战斗中倒地后可由队友复活，无需从头开始

- Each chapter's abilities define the combat style, ranging from ranged shooting to melee brawling
- Enemies have clear weak point indicators, usually requiring one player to create an opening while the other deals damage
- Interactive mechanisms and environmental traps are scattered throughout arenas — using them wisely greatly reduces difficulty
- Downed players can be revived by teammates without restarting

## 第一章战斗 / Chapter 1 Combat

## 数据之城 / Data City

**Mio 战斗风格：远程黑客 / Ranged Hacker**

Mio 使用黑客手套从远处攻击敌人。她的电磁脉冲可以瘫痪机械敌人的行动，数据破解可以对敌人造成持续伤害。

Mio uses the hacker gauntlet to attack from range. Her EMP blast can paralyze mechanical enemies, and data breach deals持续 damage over time.

**Zoe 战斗风格：敏捷弓手 / Agile Archer**

Zoe 使用精灵之弓进行中远程输出。她需要保持移动，利用绳索箭矢在战场中快速 reposition。

Zoe uses the elven bow for mid-to-long range damage. She must keep moving, using rope arrows to quickly reposition across the battlefield.

| 敌人 / Enemy | 弱点 / Weakness | 策略 / Strategy |

## 第二章战斗 / Chapter 2 Combat

## 赛博工厂 / Cyber Factory

**Mio 战斗风格：防御冲锋 / Defensive Charger**

Mio 使用能量护盾，既可以防御也可以作为攻击手段。她的护盾冲撞可以击倒多个敌人，能量弹跳可以从上方压制敌人。

Mio uses the energy shield for both defense and offense. Her shield charge can knock down multiple enemies, and energy bounce lets her crush enemies from above.

**Zoe 战斗风格：变形刺客 / Shapeshifting Assassin**

Zoe 利用变形术在战斗中取得战术优势。变成老鼠潜行绕过敌人防线，变成麻雀从空中突袭。

Zoe uses polymorph for tactical advantage in combat. Becoming a rat lets her sneak past enemy lines, while becoming a sparrow enables aerial strikes.

## 第三章战斗 / Chapter 3 Combat

## 外星空间站 / Alien Space Station

**Mio 战斗风格：重力战士 / Gravity Warrior**

Mio 的重力靴让战斗变成三维空间操作。她可以在墙壁和天花板上移动，从意想不到的角度攻击敌人。

Mio's gravity boots turn combat into three-dimensional maneuvering. She can move on walls and ceilings, attacking enemies from unexpected angles.

Zoe 的元素法杖根据战斗需求切换模式。火焰对付集群敌人，寒冰控制单个强敌，雷电对付机械单位。

Zoe's elemental staff switches modes based on combat needs. Fire handles grouped enemies, ice controls single strong foes, and lightning deals with mechanical units.

## 第四章战斗 / Chapter 4 Combat

## 虚拟网络 / Virtual Network

**Mio 战斗风格：刺客 / Cyber Assassin**

Mio 的数据匕首在虚拟网络中具有极高杀伤力。裂隙传送让她能在敌人之间快速穿梭，数据切割可以禁用敌人的特殊能力。

Mio's data dagger deals high damage in the virtual network. Rift teleport lets her quickly穿梭 between enemies, and data slice can disable special enemy abilities.

**Zoe 战斗风格：自然守护者 / Nature Guardian**

Zoe 的植物能力在战斗中兼具攻击和控制。藤蔓可以束缚敌人，荆棘屏障创造安全区域，花苞炸弹提供远程范围输出。

Zoe's plant abilities combine attack and control in combat. Vines can restrain enemies, thorn barriers create safe zones, and bud bombs provide ranged area damage.

## 合作战斗策略 / Co-op Combat Strategies

## 通用原则 / General Principles

1. **不要站在一起** / Don't cluster — AOE 攻击会同时击中两人，保持间距
2. **复活优先级** / Revive priority — 谁更容易存活谁去救人
3. **技能轮换** / Skill rotation — 不要同时交掉控制技能，轮流使用保持压制
4. **环境利用** / Use the environment — 场景中的爆炸物和机关是你的盟友
5. **沟通目标** / Call targets — 明确当前集火哪个敌人

## 职业分工 / Role Division

| 角色 / Role | 职责 / Duty | 适合谁 / Best For |
|---|---|---|
| 主攻手 / Main DPS | 主要伤害输出 | 当前章节能力优势方 |
| 支援 / Support | 控制、防御、辅助 | 当前章节能力劣势方 |
| 坦克 / Tank | 吸引火力 | 有护盾或防御技能的一方 |
| 游走 / Flanker | 绕后和机动输出 | 有位移技能的一方 |

## 进阶技巧 / Advanced Tips

- 许多普通敌人可以被 **环境击杀**，如推下悬崖或引入陷阱
- **空中连击**：一人将敌人打浮空，另一人空中追击造成额外伤害
- 当屏幕边缘出现 **红色警示** 时，立即停止攻击开始闪避
- 部分章节有 **隐藏武器** 藏在场景中，找到后可以大幅提升战斗力

- Many common enemies can be **environmentally killed**, such as pushed off cliffs or lured into traps
- **Air combos**: one player launches enemies, the other follows up with aerial damage
- When **red warnings** appear at screen edges, stop attacking and dodge immediately
- Some chapters have **hidden weapons** in the environment — finding them greatly boosts combat power
