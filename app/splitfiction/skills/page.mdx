## 技能系统概述 / Skill System Overview

《双影奇境》的技能升级系统采用 **章节解锁 + 芯片强化** 的双轨模式。每章获得基础能力后，可以通过收集升级芯片来强化这些能力，解锁新的技能分支。

Split Fiction's skill upgrade system uses a **chapter unlock + chip enhancement** dual-track model. After obtaining basic abilities each chapter, you can strengthen them by collecting upgrade chips, unlocking new skill branches.

## 升级策略建议 / Upgrade Strategy Recommendations

## 新手推荐 / Beginner Recommendations

优先升级 **EMP 连锁** 和 **变形时间**，这两个升级性价比最高，可以大幅降低前中期的战斗和探索难度。

Prioritize **EMP Chain** and **Morph Time** — these two upgrades offer the best value, significantly reducing combat and exploration difficulty in the early to mid game.

## 进阶推荐 / Advanced Recommendations

- Mio 优先：过载模式 > 重力投掷 > 传送突袭
- Zoe 优先：元素融合 > 终极变形 > 双龙召唤
- 这些终极技能是挑战最终 BOSS 和挑战模式的关键

- Mio priority: Overdrive > Gravity Throw > Teleport Strike
- Zoe priority: Element Fusion > Ultimate Morph > Twin Dragons
- These ultimate skills are key to tackling the final boss and challenge modes

## 全收集策略 / Completionist Strategy

全收集至少需要通关两次。第一次不刻意收集，了解关卡布局；第二次使用章节选择回溯收集遗漏的升级芯片。

Full completion requires at least two playthroughs. Play the first time without focusing on collectibles to learn level layouts; use Chapter Select on the second run to collect missed upgrade chips.
