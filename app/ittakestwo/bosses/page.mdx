# Boss Guide — All Boss Fights Detailed

{/* ===== BOSS概述 ===== */}

## BOSS战概述 / Boss Fight Overview

《双人成行》包含 **10 场以上的主要 BOSS 战**，每场都需要两名玩家密切配合。BOSS 设计极具创意，战斗方式完全不同，没有重复的 BOSS 模板。以下按章节顺序详解每个 BOSS 的打法。

*It Takes Two* features **over 10 major boss fights**, each demanding close cooperation. Boss designs are highly creative with no重复 patterns. Below is a chapter-by-chapter breakdown of every boss.

---

{/* ===== 吸尘器 BOSS ===== */}

## 第一章 BOSS：吸尘器 / Vacuum Cleaner

### BOSS 信息 / Boss Info

- **章节**：第一章 — 棚屋 / Chapter 1 — The Shed
- **难度**：★☆☆☆☆ (简单 / Easy)
- **能力要求**：钉子枪 + 锤子 / Nail Gun + Hammer

### BOSS 介绍 / Introduction

棚屋中的吸尘器被意外激活，变成了狂暴的家电巨兽。它会四处移动并吸入周围物体，试图将两个主角吸进去。

The shed's vacuum cleaner is accidentally activated, becoming a raging appliance beast. It moves around sucking in objects, trying to inhale both protagonists.

### 阶段一：追逐与破坏 / Phase 1: Chase & Destroy

- 吸尘器会在地图中缓慢移动，产生吸力
- 躲避被吸入的方向
- 吸尘器撞到墙壁后会短暂眩晕
- 趁眩晕时用锤子敲击吸尘器侧面
- 重复 3-4 次后进入第二阶段

- The vacuum moves slowly, creating suction
- Dodge away from the suction direction
- It stuns briefly when hitting a wall
- Strike its side with the hammer during stun
- Repeat 3-4 times to trigger phase 2

### 阶段二：狂暴模式 / Phase 2: Rampage

- 吸尘器移动速度加快，吸力范围扩大
- 开始喷出之前吸入的杂物作为攻击
- Cody 可以用钉子枪远程攻击
- May 需要绕到侧面进行近战
- 一人吸引注意，另一人攻击

- Speed increases, suction范围 widens
- Spits out debris as projectiles
- Cody can attack at range with nail gun
- May flanks for melee damage
- One player distracts, the other attacks

### 合作策略 / Co-op Strategy

**Cody 角色**：保持距离射击，在 May 攻击时提供火力掩护。注意躲避抛射物。

**May 角色**：BOSS 眩晕窗口很短，提前站好位置，眩晕瞬间立刻输出。

**Cody's role**: Stay at range, provide cover fire while May attacks. Dodge projectiles.

**May's role**: The stun window is short — pre-position and strike immediately.

## 第二章 BOSS：松鼠将军 / Squirrel General

### BOSS 信息 / Boss Info

- **章节**：第二章 — 大树 / Chapter 2 — The Tree
- **难度**：★★☆☆☆ (中等偏易 / Easy-Medium)
- **能力要求**：树液枪 + 树液靴 / Sap Gun + Sap Boots

### BOSS 介绍 / Introduction

武装松鼠军的将军，骑在一只巨大的甲虫上，挥舞着火柴棍长矛。

The general of the armed squirrel army, riding a giant beetle, wielding a matchstick spear.

### 阶段一：冲锋攻击 / Phase 1: Charge Attack

- 松鼠将军骑着甲虫向你冲锋
- 利用 Cody 的树液制造弹跳点
- May 弹跳踩踏躲避冲锋
- 冲锋后 BOSS 会短暂停顿
- May 趁停顿近战攻击

- The general charges on his beetle
- Cody creates sap bounce points
- May bounces to dodge charges
- Boss pauses briefly after charging
- May attacks during the pause

### 阶段二：远程攻击 / Phase 2: Ranged Attack

- 松鼠将军开始投掷橡果
- 橡果落地后爆炸
- Cody 用树液减缓投掷物的速度
- 地面会出现树液可以弹跳的区域
- May 弹跳到空中踩踏 BOSS

- Boss throws acorns that explode on impact
- Cody slows projectiles with sap
- Sap patches appear on the ground for bouncing
- May bounces into the air to stomp the boss

### 合作策略 / Co-op Strategy

注意甲虫的冲锋路径，不要站成一条直线。May 的弹跳时机很重要——弹早了会被击中，弹晚了踩不到。

Watch the beetle's charge path — don't stand in a line. May's bounce timing is critical — too early and you get hit, too late and you miss the stomp.

## 第五章 BOSS：蜂后 / Wasp Queen

### BOSS 信息 / Boss Info

- **章节**：第五章 — 花园 / Chapter 5 — The Garden
- **难度**：★★★★☆ (困难 / Hard)
- **能力要求**：重力反转 + 滑翔 / Gravity Flip + Gliding

### BOSS 介绍 / Introduction

巨大的蜂后守卫着蜂巢的核心，它会召唤蜂群并使用蜂刺攻击。

The giant wasp queen guards the heart of the hive, summoning swarms and attacking with stingers.

### 阶段一：蜂群召唤 / Phase 1: Swarm Call

- 蜂后召唤大量工蜂
- 利用 Cody 的重力反转使蜂群失去方向
- May 利用滑翔在空中躲避
- 清理完蜂群后蜂后会露出腹部弱点
- 两人集中攻击弱点

- Queen summons大量 worker wasps
- Cody's gravity flip disorients swarms
- May glides to dodge in the air
- After clearing swarms, the queen exposes her abdomen weak point
- Both focus fire on the weak point

### 阶段二：蜂刺齐射 / Phase 2: Stinger Barrage

- 蜂后向地面发射蜂刺
- 蜂刺落地后留下毒液区域
- 利用重力反转在毒液区域的天花板上行走
- 蜂后会俯冲攻击
- May 滑翔躲避并向蜂后反击

- Queen fires stingers into the ground
- Stingers leave venom pools
- Use gravity flip to walk on ceilings above venom
- Queen dive-bombs
- May glides to dodge and counterattack

### 阶段三：暴怒 / Phase 3: Enrage

- 蜂后狂暴化，攻击频率翻倍
- 地面大范围毒液覆盖
- 需要频繁利用重力反转切换行走面
- 保持移动，不要在一个位置停留超过 3 秒
- 耐心磨血，不要贪刀

- Queen enrages, attack frequency doubles
- Large areas covered in venom
- Frequently use gravity flip to switch walking surfaces
- Keep moving, don't stay in one spot over 3 seconds
- Patience — don't get greedy

## 第三章 BOSS：太空猴子 / Space Monkey

### BOSS 信息 / Boss Info

- **章节**：第三章 — 罗斯的房间 / Chapter 3 — Rose's Room
- **难度**：★★★☆☆ (中等 / Medium)
- **能力要求**：火柴 + 水枪 / Matches + Water Gun

### BOSS 介绍 / Introduction

罗斯房间中的猴子玩具变成了太空侵略者，它驾驶着飞碟形状的玩具。

A toy monkey from Rose's room becomes a space invader, piloting a UFO-shaped toy.

### 阶段一：飞碟攻击 / Phase 1: UFO Attack

- 太空猴子的飞碟发射激光
- Cody 用火柴点燃场景中的蜡烛照亮 BOSS
- May 用水枪浇灭飞碟上的火焰
- 飞碟被水淋湿后会短路坠落
- 趁坠落时攻击猴子本体

- UFO fires lasers
- Cody lights candles with matches to illuminate the boss
- May sprays water on the UFO to short-circuit it
- The wet UFO crashes
- Attack the monkey while it's down

### 阶段二：机器人形态 / Phase 2: Robot Mode

- 飞碟变形成步行机器人
- 发射追踪导弹
- 利用蒸汽云遮蔽 BOSS 视线
- Cody 点燃水枪制造的水面产生蒸汽
- May 在水中导电攻击机器人弱点

- UFO transforms into a walking robot
- Fires tracking missiles
- Use steam clouds to block boss vision
- Cody ignites water surfaces to create steam
- May conducts electricity through water to damage the robot

### 合作策略 / Co-op Strategy

水和火的配合是本战核心。注意场景中的电源插座——用水导电可以造成额外伤害。导弹可以引诱撞击场景中的障碍物。

Water-fire cooperation is key. Watch for power outlets — electrifying water deals extra damage. Lure missiles into obstacles.

## 第六章 BOSS：酷蒂大象 / Cutie the Elephant

### BOSS 信息 / Boss Info

- **章节**：第六章 — 阁楼 / Chapter 6 — The Attic
- **难度**：★★★★☆ (困难 / Hard) — 情感上更困难 / Emotionally harder
- **能力要求**：分身 + 变身 / Doppelganger + Transformation

### BOSS 介绍 / Introduction

酷蒂是大象玩具，被罗斯不小心画上了奇怪的涂鸦后变得疯狂。这场 BOSS 战以情感冲击力著称——许多玩家反映这是游戏中最令人心碎的战斗。

Cutie is a stuffed elephant toy that goes mad after Rose accidentally draws strange graffiti on her. This boss fight is famous for its emotional impact — many players call it the most heartbreaking battle in the game.

### 阶段一：玩具暴走 / Phase 1: Toy Rampage

- 酷蒂开始疯狂破坏周围环境
- 它用鼻子横扫攻击
- Cody 用分身吸引 BOSS 注意力
- May 变身坦克形态从侧面接近
- 在 BOSS 攻击分身时输出伤害

- Cutie goes on a rampage
- Attacks with trunk sweeps
- Cody uses his clone to distract the boss
- May transforms into tank form and flanks
- Deal damage while the boss attacks the clone

### 阶段二：悲伤暴怒 / Phase 2: Sad Rage

- 酷蒂的移动模式更加混乱无章
- 投掷周围的玩具和物品
- 需要两人在音乐盒齿轮间穿梭
- Cody 分身踩住齿轮机关
- May 利用潜行形态绕过 BOSS 正面

- Cutie's movement becomes more erratic
- Throws toys and objects around
- Navigate between music box gears
- Cody's clone holds gear switches
- May uses stealth form to bypass the boss

### 阶段三：终局 / Phase 3: Finale

- 酷蒂不断撞击墙壁试图逃走
- 需要两人合作引导它撞向脆弱的墙壁
- 墙壁撞破后露出弱点
- 两人同时攻击弱点结束战斗

- Cutie repeatedly rams walls trying to escape
- Guide it to crash through fragile walls
- Weak point exposed after wall breaks
- Both attack the weak point simultaneously

### 合作策略 / Co-op Strategy

这场战斗在情感上带有争议，但玩法上需要精准配合。分身的坦克价值在这场战斗中尤为突出——优先升级分身的持续时间。注意避开酷蒂的眼泪坠落区域。

This fight is controversial emotionally but demands precise cooperation mechanically. The clone's tanking value is paramount here — prioritize clone duration upgrades. Watch for falling tear drops.

## 第七章 终极 BOSS：德金博士 / Dr. Hakim

### BOSS 信息 / Boss Info

- **章节**：第七章 — 旅程终点 / Chapter 7 — The Finale
- **难度**：★★★★★ (极难 / Very Hard)
- **能力要求**：全部能力 / All abilities combined

### BOSS 介绍 / Introduction

神秘的情爱治疗书 Dr. Hakim 实际上是最终 BOSS。它为了阻止 Cody 和 May 变回人类以维持自己的存在，使出浑身解数。

The mysterious love therapy book Dr. Hakim is actually the final boss. It uses all its power to prevent Cody and May from returning to human form.

### 阶段一：书本形态 / Phase 1: Book Form

- Dr. Hakim 翻开书页释放各种攻击
- 每页攻击对应一个前章节的 BOSS 能力
- 需要迅速回忆并使用对应章节的能力反制
- 场景会不断切换之前的主题
- 保持冷静，不要被快节奏搞乱

- Dr. Hakim opens pages releasing various attacks
- Each page corresponds to a previous chapter's boss power
- Quickly recall and counter with the appropriate chapter ability
- Scenes切换 between previous themes
- Stay calm despite the fast pace

### 阶段二：融合形态 / Phase 2: Fusion Form

- Dr. Hakim 融合了所有 BOSS 的能力
- 同时出现吸尘器的吸力 + 蜂后的毒液 + 太空猴子的激光
- 需要超高频率的能力切换
- Cody 需要同时使用时间暂停 + 重力反转
- May 需要连续使用钩爪 + 滑翔 + 变身

- Dr. Hakim combines all boss abilities
- Simultaneously: vacuum suction + wasp venom + space monkey lasers
- Requires rapid ability switching
- Cody uses Time Stop + Gravity Flip in quick succession
- May chains Grappling Hook + Gliding + Transformation

### 阶段三：最终形态 / Phase 3: Final Form

- BOSS 的本体形态，巨大化
- 攻击覆盖全屏
- 核心机制是两人同步攻击
- 当出现同步提示时，两人同时攻击
- 完美的同步可以造成巨额伤害

- Boss's true giant form
- Screen-wide attacks
- Core mechanic: synchronized attacks
- When sync prompts appear, both attack simultaneously
- Perfect sync deals massive damage

### 终极策略 / Ultimate Strategy

1. **语音倒数** / Voice countdown — 在需要同步攻击时倒数 "3-2-1"
2. **分工明确** / Clear分工 — 提前决定谁负责什么类型的攻击
3. **不要慌张** / Don't panic — 即使被击中也有无限续关
4. **学习攻击模式** / Learn patterns — 每个阶段攻击模式固定，熟悉后即可预判
5. **检查点利用** / Use checkpoints — 最终战有多个检查点，充分利用

{/* ===== BOSS战综合技巧 ===== */}

## BOSS战综合技巧 / General Boss Tips

1. **观察阶段变化** / Watch for phase transitions — BOSS 变红或其他颜色通常是进入新阶段的标志
2. **环境利用** / Use the environment — 很多 BOSS 战场景中有可交互的机关
3. **不要两个人都倒地** / Don't both go down — 一人倒地后另一人可以存活等待复活
4. **弹药管理** / Ammo management — 战斗中注意能力的冷却时间和弹药余量
5. **享受过程** / Enjoy the fight — 每场 BOSS 战都是独特的体验，不用着急一次通过
