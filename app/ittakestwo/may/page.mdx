# 梅攻略 — 全章节能力与策略
# May Guide — Abilities & Strategies by Chapter

---

{/* ===== 梅简介 ===== */}

## 角色简介 / Character Overview

May 是故事中的妻子角色，性格直率，行动力强。她的能力偏向 **近战攻击、强力破坏、高速机动**。May 通常是解谜中的"执行者"，负责利用 Cody 创造的条件完成目标。

May is the wife in the story — straightforward and action-oriented. Her abilities lean toward **melee combat, heavy destruction, and high-speed mobility**. May is typically the "executor" in puzzles, using conditions created by Cody to accomplish goals.

## 第一章 — 棚屋 / The Shed

### 梅能力：锤子 / Ability: Hammer

May 获得 **锤子 (Hammer)**，这是她最具标志性的武器：

- 可以敲碎木箱和脆弱的墙壁
- 敲击钉子让钉子钉得更深
- 重击地面产生冲击波
- 近战攻击对敌人造成高额伤害
- 可以用来压下大型机关按钮

May receives the **Hammer**, her most iconic weapon:

- Smash wooden crates and fragile walls
- Hammer nails deeper into surfaces
- Ground pound creates a shockwave
- Melee attacks deal high damage
- Press down large mechanism buttons

### 策略 / Strategy

May 在棚屋章节是主力输出和破障者。Cody 布置好钉子后，May 需要跳到钉子上，然后跳跃到更高处。锤子的重击可以解开很多机关——比如敲打巨大的齿轮让它们转动。战斗中 May 的锤子范围大、伤害高，适合吸引敌人仇恨。

May is the primary damage dealer and obstacle breaker in The Shed. After Cody places nails, May jumps onto them. The hammer's heavy strike solves many mechanisms — like hitting giant gears to make them turn. In combat, May's hammer has wide range and high damage, perfect for tanking aggro.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- 跳到 Cody 的钉子上获得高度优势
- 锤子击打 Cody 标记的目标
- 战斗时掩护 Cody 远程输出
- Jump on Cody's nails for height advantage
- Hammer targets that Cody marks
- Protect Cody during his ranged attacks

## 第二章 — 大树 / The Tree

### 梅能力：树液靴 / Ability: Sap Boots

May 获得 **树液靴 (Sap Boots)**，可以在树液上弹跳：

- 在 Cody 射出的树液上弹跳
- 弹跳高度随蓄力时间增加
- 到达正常跳跃无法触及的区域
- 踩踏敌人造成伤害

May gets **Sap Boots** for bouncing on sap:

- Bounce on sap patches created by Cody
- Bounce height increases with charge time
- Reach normally inaccessible areas
- Stomp enemies for damage

### 策略 / Strategy

树液靴让 May 成为了高机动战士。在 Cody 制造弹跳点后，May 蓄力跳跃可以到达极高的平台。弹跳有节奏感——落地瞬间再次跳跃可以弹得更高。战斗中可以利用弹跳踩踏敌人造成范围伤害。

Sap Boots turn May into a high-mobility warrior. After Cody creates bounce points, May can charge and jump to极高 platforms. Bouncing has rhythm — jumping immediately upon landing gives extra height. In combat, use bounce stomps for area damage.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- 告诉 Cody 你需要弹跳点的位置
- 利用弹跳躲避地面攻击
- 弹跳踩踏对付密集的敌人群
- Tell Cody where you need bounce points
- Use bounces to dodge ground attacks
- Stomp密集 enemy groups

## 第三章 — 罗斯的房间 / Rose's Room

### 梅能力：水枪 / Ability: Water Gun

May 获得 **水枪 (Water Gun)**，可以喷射水流：

- 浇灭火焰和蜡烛
- 清理脏污表面
- 推动水轮机关
- 与水结合产生蒸汽
- 冲走小型敌人

May gets a **Water Gun** that sprays water:

- Extinguish flames and candles
- Clean dirty surfaces
- Push water wheel mechanisms
- Combine with fire for steam
- Wash away small enemies

### 策略 / Strategy

水枪本身不造成伤害，但在解谜中至关重要。May 可以浇灭火墙让 Cody 通过，或用水冲击水轮转动机关。水与 Cody 的火结合会产生蒸汽云，可以升到高处或遮挡视线。在积木城堡关卡，水枪也可用来触发远处的机关。

The Water Gun doesn't deal damage but is crucial for puzzles. May extinguishes fire walls for Cody or uses water to spin wheels. Water combined with Cody's fire creates steam clouds for floating upward or obscuring vision. In the block castle, water can trigger distant mechanisms.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- May 浇水 → Cody 点火 → 蒸汽机关
- 清理 Cody 前方的火焰路障
- 水流推动旋转楼梯和吊桥
- May waters → Cody ignites → steam mechanisms
- Clear fire barriers ahead of Cody
- Water pushes spiral stairs and drawbridges

## 第四章 — 雪景球 / The Snowglobe

### 梅能力：钩爪 / Ability: Grappling Hook

May 获得 **钩爪 (Grappling Hook)**，大幅提升机动性：

- 钩住特定锚点进行摆荡
- 跨越巨大间隙
- 拉动物体或敌人
- 钩住飞行敌人拉近攻击

May gets a **Grappling Hook** that greatly improves mobility:

- Hook onto specific anchor points and swing
- Cross large gaps
- Pull objects or enemies
- Hook flying enemies and pull them close

### 策略 / Strategy

钩爪让 May 能到达 Cody 无法直接前往的位置。在冰封的雪景球世界中，May 可以通过钩爪摆荡跨越冰裂缝和深渊。战斗中钩爪可以把飞行敌人拉到地面，让 Cody 的时间停止更容易命中。

The Grappling Hook lets May reach places Cody cannot. In the frozen snowglobe world, May swings across ice crevasses and chasms. In combat, hook flying enemies to the ground so Cody's Time Stop hits more easily.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- 钩爪飞跃到高处激活机关
- 拉近敌人让 Cody 冻结
- 利用摆荡通过 Cody 暂停的旋转平台
- Hook to high places and activate switches
- Pull enemies close for Cody to freeze
- Swing through Cody's paused rotating platforms

## 第五章 — 花园 / The Garden

### 梅能力：滑翔 / Ability: Gliding

May 获得 **滑翔能力 (Gliding)**，可以短距离滑翔：

- 在跳跃后按住跳跃键滑翔
- 利用上升气流提升高度
- 从高处滑翔到远处平台
- 空中调整方向躲避障碍

May gains **Gliding**, allowing short-distance flight:

- Hold jump after leaping to glide
- Use updrafts to gain altitude
- Glide from high places to distant platforms
- Adjust direction mid-air to dodge obstacles

### 策略 / Strategy

滑翔能力在花园的广阔地图中非常有用。May 可以利用 Cody 的重力反转跳得更高，然后滑翔穿过大片区域。在蜜蜂敌人的战斗中，滑翔可以穿越密集的花粉区。收集花蜜时滑翔也能帮助快速移动。

Gliding is invaluable in the Garden's open maps. May can use Cody's gravity flip to jump higher, then glide across large areas. In bee enemy fights, glide through dense pollen zones. Gliding also speeds up nectar collection.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- Cody 反转重力 → May 跳高 → 滑翔穿越
- 利用上升气流延长滑翔距离
- 滑翔时注意避开尖刺和敌人
- Cody flips gravity → May jumps high → glides across
- Use updrafts to extend glide distance
- Watch for spikes and enemies while gliding

## 第六章 — 阁楼 / The Attic

### 梅能力：变身 / Ability: Transformation

May 获得 **变身能力**，可以变成不同的形态：

- 变成 **坦克 (Tank)** — 低矮沉重，可以压碎障碍
- 变成 **潜行形态 (Stealth)** — 可以隐身和无声移动
- 不同形态对应不同解谜需求
- 变身有时间限制和冷却

May gains **Transformation**, taking different forms:

- **Tank form** — short and heavy, crushes obstacles
- **Stealth form** — invisible and silent movement
- Different forms solve different puzzles
- Forms have time limits and cooldowns

### 策略 / Strategy

变身是 May 在阁楼章节的核心机制。坦克形态可以踩碎脆弱的平台、压住机关。潜行形态可以避开视线类敌人(如手电筒守卫)。在音乐积木关卡中，需要灵活切换形态来配合 Cody 的分身。

Transformation is May's core mechanic in The Attic. Tank form crushes fragile platforms and holds switches. Stealth form evades vision-based enemies (like flashlight guards). In the music block section, switch forms dynamically to配合 Cody's clone.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- 坦克形态踩机关 → Cody 分身配合
- 潜行形态绕后 → 给 Cody 信号同步行动
- Tank form holds switch → Cody's clone cooperates
- Stealth flanks → signal Cody for同步行动

## 第七章 — 旅程终点 / The Final Chapter

### 梅能力：终极形态 / Ultimate Form

在终章，May 获得最终形态，综合了前面所有能力的特点。最终战斗要求 May 灵活运用钩爪、滑翔和变身来应对各种场景。

In the final chapter, May achieves her ultimate form, combining all previous abilities. The final fight demands flexible use of grappling hook, gliding, and transformation.

### 最终战策略 / Final Boss Strategy

- May 是近战主力，需要正面吸引 BOSS 注意
- 利用钩爪快速接近和远离 BOSS
- 注意躲闪大范围攻击
- 与 Cody 保持语音沟通切换策略
- May is the melee主力, drawing the boss's attention
- Use grappling hook to close in and retreat quickly
- Dodge large-area attacks
- Communicate with Cody to switch strategies

{/* ===== 梅进阶技巧 ===== */}

## 梅进阶技巧 / Advanced May Tips

1. **锤子蓄力攻击** / Charged hammer — 长按攻击键可以蓄力，造成更大的范围和伤害
2. **钩爪取消** / Grapple cancel — 在落地前用钩爪可以取消掉落伤害
3. **滑翔时攻击** / Attack while gliding — 在滑翔过程中也可以使用远程攻击
4. **变身的时机** / Transform timing — 不要在敌人面前变身，变身后的前摇较长
5. **踩跳** / Bounce jump — 在树液上连续弹跳比单次大跳更容易控制落点
