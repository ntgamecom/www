## 友票系统 / Friend's Pass

《双人成行》采用独特的 **友票系统 (Friend's Pass)**：

- 只需要 **一位玩家购买完整版游戏**
- 另一位玩家可免费下载 **友票版 (Friend's Pass)** 并畅玩全部关卡
- 跨平台支持：PC 端 Steam 与 Origin 玩家可以联机
- 主机端支持跨代联机 (PS4 ↔ PS5, Xbox One ↔ Series X/S)
- 支持本地分屏合作与在线联机

*It Takes Two* features a unique **Friend's Pass** system:

- Only **one player needs to purchase** the full game
- The other player can download the **free Friend's Pass** and play through the entire game
- Cross-platform: Steam and Origin players on PC can play together
- Console supports cross-generation play (PS4 ↔ PS5, Xbox One ↔ Series X/S)
- Supports both local split-screen and online co-op

## 故事简介 / Story Overview

Cody 和 May 是一对即将离婚的夫妻。他们的女儿 Rose 因父母争吵而伤心流泪，眼泪意外滴落在代表父母的两个手工玩偶上，使 Cody 和 May 的灵魂被困在玩偶中。在一本神秘的情爱治疗书 Dr. Hakim 的引导下，两人必须合作穿越各种奇幻世界，修复他们破裂的关系，同时寻找变回人类的方法。

Cody and May are a married couple on the verge of divorce. Their daughter Rose's tears accidentally fall onto two handmade dolls representing her parents, trapping Cody and May inside them. Guided by a mysterious love therapy book named Dr. Hakim, they must cooperate through fantastical worlds, repair their broken relationship, and find a way to become human again.

## 实用提示 / Pro Tips

1. **语音沟通是关键** / Voice communication is essential — 大部分解谜需要实时协调
2. **探索每个角落** / Explore every corner — 隐藏小游戏和收集品遍布各章节
3. **轮流尝试角色** / Try both roles — 每个章节的能力都不同，值得切换体验
4. **不要跳过小游戏** / Don't skip minigames — 它们不仅是娱乐，还关联奖杯成就
5. **耐心配合** / Be patient with your partner — 有些机关需要多次尝试才能默契
