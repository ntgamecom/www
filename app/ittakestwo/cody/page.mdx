# 科迪攻略 — 全章节能力与策略
# Cody Guide — Abilities & Strategies by Chapter

---

{/* ===== 科迪简介 ===== */}

## 角色简介 / Character Overview

Cody 是故事中的丈夫角色，一位工程师。他在游戏中的能力偏向 **远程攻击、操控环境、利用重力与时间**。Cody 的技能通常需要 May 的配合才能发挥最大效用。

Cody is the husband in the story, an engineer by profession. His abilities lean toward **ranged attacks, environmental manipulation, gravity and time control**. Cody's skills generally require May's cooperation to reach maximum effectiveness.

## 第一章 — 棚屋 / The Shed

### 科迪能力：钉子发射器 / Ability: Nail Gun

在棚屋章节，Cody 获得 **钉子发射器 (Nail Gun)**。它可以：

- 发射钉子到木制表面，作为 May 的攀爬点
- May 可以站在钉子上到达高处
- 战斗时对敌人造成伤害
- 破坏脆弱的木板障碍

In The Shed, Cody receives the **Nail Gun**. It can:

- Fire nails into wooden surfaces as climbing points for May
- May can stand on nails to reach high areas
- Deal damage to enemies in combat
- Break fragile wooden barriers

### 策略 / Strategy

作为 Cody，你的核心任务是 **为 May 创造路径**。看到高处的平台时，先在木制表面射入钉子。May 则可以爬到钉子上，再利用锤子敲击机关。

战斗方面，Cody 的钉子枪是远程武器，适合在 May 近战吸引火力时输出。注意钉子有弹药限制，但会随时间自动恢复。

As Cody, your main job is **creating paths for May**. When you see a high platform, fire nails into wooden surfaces first. May can then climb onto the nails and use her hammer to hit switches.

In combat, Cody's Nail Gun is a ranged weapon — stay back while May engages enemies up close. Note that nails have limited ammo but regenerate over time.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- Cody 钉钉子 → May 攀爬 → May 敲开关
- Cody 远程压制 → May 近战清理
- Cody nails → May climbs → May hits switch
- Cody ranged → May melee

{/* ===== 第二章：大树 ===== */}

## 第二章 — 大树 / The Tree

### 科迪能力：树液发射器 / Ability: Sap Shooter

Cody 获得 **树液发射器 (Sap Shooter)**，可以发射一种具有弹性的粘性树液：

- 树液黏在墙上后会形成弹性表面
- May 可以利用树液像蹦床一样弹跳更高
- 树液也可以黏住敌人使其减速
- 树液球可以覆盖某些机关

Cody gets the **Sap Shooter**, which fires sticky elastic sap:

- Sap creates bouncy surfaces on walls
- May can use sap patches like trampolines to jump higher
- Sap can also slow down enemies
- Sap blobs can cover certain mechanisms

### 策略 / Strategy

Cody 的树液是 May 机动性的关键。在一人无法到达的高处，先用树液在墙上制造弹跳点，让 May 弹上去操作机关。树液发射需要预判——树液球飞行较慢，需要提前瞄准。

Cody's sap is key to May's mobility. When a platform is too high, create bounce points on walls with sap so May can reach it. Aim ahead — sap balls travel slowly and require提前瞄准.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- Cody 射树液 → May 弹跳 → 到达高处
- 战斗中 Cody 用树液减速 → May 用锤子输出
- Cody shoots sap → May bounces → reaches height
- Cody slows enemies with sap → May deals damage with hammer

## 第三章 — 罗斯的房间 / Rose's Room

### 科迪能力：火柴发射器 / Ability: Matchstick Shooter

Cody 获得 **火柴发射器**，可以点燃各种物体：

- 点燃蜡烛照亮黑暗区域
- 烧毁藤蔓和障碍物
- 点燃火药桶造成爆炸
- 融化冰面障碍

Cody gets a **Matchstick Shooter** that ignites objects:

- Light candles to illuminate dark areas
- Burn vines and obstacles
- Ignite powder kegs for explosions
- Melt ice barriers

### 策略 / Strategy

Cody 在本章扮演追踪 **点火者** 角色。May 的水枪可以清理路径，但有些障碍物需要火烧才能清除。两人需要紧密配合——May 浇水清理后，Cody 点火烧掉残留。部分谜题需要同时使用水和火。

Cody acts as the **igniter** this chapter. May's water gun clears paths, but some obstacles need fire. Work closely — May sprays water to clear, then Cody burns what remains. Some puzzles require simultaneous water and fire.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- May 浇水 → Cody 点火 → 产生蒸汽推动机关
- Cody 点燃火药桶 → May 避开爆炸 → 障碍清除
- May waters → Cody ignites → steam powers mechanisms
- Cody lights kegs → May avoids blast → obstacles cleared

## 第四章 — 雪景球 / The Snowglobe

### 科迪能力：时间停止 / Ability: Time Stop

Cody 获得强大的 **时间停止 (Time Stop)** 能力：

- 可以暂停旋转的机关和平台
- 冻结敌人使其无法移动
- 停滞飞行中的投射物
- 短暂暂停水流和瀑布

Cody gains the powerful **Time Stop** ability:

- Pause rotating mechanisms and platforms
- Freeze enemies in place
- Halt projectiles mid-flight
- Temporarily stop water flows and waterfalls

### 策略 / Strategy

时间停止是《双人成行》中最强大的能力之一。Cody 需要精准判断时机：当 May 需要穿越旋转风扇或移动平台时，及时暂停机关。注意能力有冷却时间，不能连续使用。在城堡战斗中，合理使用时间停止可以让战斗难度大幅降低。

Time Stop is one of the most powerful abilities in the game. Cody must time it precisely: pause mechanisms when May needs to cross rotating fans or moving platforms. The ability has a cooldown, so use it wisely. In castle fights, good Time Stop usage dramatically reduces difficulty.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- 暂停旋转机关 → May 通过 → Cody 取消暂停后跟上
- 冻结敌人 → 两人集中消灭逐个击破
- Pause rotating traps → May passes → Cody follows after unpausing
- Freeze enemies → focus them down one by one

## 第五章 — 花园 / The Garden

### 科迪能力：重力反转 / Ability: Gravity Flip

Cody 获得 **重力反转** 能力，可以反转指定区域的引力方向：

- 在限定区域内改变重力方向
- 可以让 May 在天花板上行走
- 使敌人从平台掉落
- 改变投射物的轨迹

Cody gains **Gravity Flip**, reversing gravity in targeted areas:

- Change gravity direction within a zone
- Let May walk on ceilings
- Drop enemies off platforms
- Alter projectile trajectories

### 策略 / Strategy

重力反转是空间解谜的核心。Cody 需要观察场景中的紫色区域——只有在这些区域内才能使用重力反转。May 可以在翻转后到达正常重力下无法到达的位置。在蜜蜂战斗中，反转重力可以让蜜蜂群失去方向。

Gravity Flip is central to spatial puzzles. Cody must look for purple zones — gravity reversal only works within them. May can reach normally inaccessible areas after flipping. In bee fights, reversing gravity disorients swarms.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- Cody 反转重力 → May 在天花板行走 → 到达隐藏区域
- 战斗中利用重力使敌人坠落
- Cody flips gravity → May walks on ceiling → reaches secrets
- Use gravity to drop enemies during combat

## 第六章 — 阁楼 / The Attic

### 科迪能力：分身 / Ability: Doppelganger

Cody 获得 **分身** 能力，可以创造自己的复制体：

- 分身可以踩住机关不放
- 分身可以搬运物体
- 分身会自动复制 Cody 的动作
- 分身有持续时间限制

Cody gains a **Doppelganger** ability, creating a copy of himself:

- The clone can hold switches
- The clone can carry objects
- The clone mirrors Cody's movements
- The clone has a time limit

### 策略 / Strategy

分身能力让 Cody 可以"同时出现在两个地方"。利用分身来踩住需要持续按压的机关，本体则去做其他操作。在音乐主题的谜题中，分身和本体可以分别激活不同的音符机关。

The Doppelganger lets Cody be "in two places at once." Use the clone to hold persistent switches while the real Cody operates elsewhere. In music-themed puzzles, the clone and original can activate different note mechanisms.

### 配合要点 / Teamwork Key

- Cody 分身踩机关 → 本体和 May 通过大门
- 分身搬运物体 → 本体用能力解决其他谜题
- Clone holds switch → Cody and May pass through gate
- Clone carries objects → original solves other puzzles

## 第七章 — 旅程终点 / The Final Chapter

### 科迪能力：情感共鸣 / Ability: Emotional Resonance

在最后一章，Cody 的能力达到完全形态，可以与其他情感能量产生共鸣。这一章更注重情感叙事和两人配合，而非单一技能。

In the final chapter, Cody's abilities reach their full form, resonating with emotional energy. This chapter focuses more on emotional storytelling and paired cooperation rather than a single power.

### 最终战策略 / Final Boss Strategy

- Cody 负责吸引 BOSS 的远程攻击
- 利用前六章学到的所有技能应对不同阶段
- 最终战斗需要极高的配合默契
- 不要贪刀，注意走位和机关变化

- Cody draws the boss's ranged attacks
- Use all skills learned across six chapters for different phases
- The final fight demands peak coordination
- Don't get greedy — watch positioning and mechanism changes

{/* ===== 科迪进阶技巧 ===== */}

## 科迪进阶技巧 / Advanced Cody Tips

1. **弹药管理** / Ammo management — 钉子枪和树液发射器都自动回复，但回复速度不同。战斗前确保弹药充足
2. **时间停止的预判** / Time Stop anticipation — 提前 1-2 秒使用时间停止，给 May 足够的反应时间
3. **分身位置选择** / Clone positioning — 分身放置在高处或机关旁，最大化利用其存在时间
4. **重力翻转的方向控制** / Gravity direction control — 注意翻转后 May 的落点，不要让她掉到危险区域
5. **沟通第一** / Communicate first — 每次使用关键能力前，先告诉 May 你的意图
