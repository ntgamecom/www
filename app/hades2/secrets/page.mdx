# 隐藏要素 / Secrets & Hidden Content

哈迪斯2延续了初代的隐藏内容传统——从隐藏Boss到秘密结局，从NPC支线到收集品。本章汇总所有需要探索才能发现的内容 / Hades 2 continues the tradition of rich hidden content from the original — from hidden bosses to secret endings, NPC side quests to collectibles. This chapter covers everything requiring exploration to discover.

---

| 武器 / Weapon | 解锁条件 / Condition | 特点 / Feature |
|---|---|---|
| **暗影之杖 / Shadow Staff** | 默认解锁 / Default | 平衡型——远程+近战混合 / Balanced — ranged + melee hybrid |
| **双刃斧 / Twin Axe** | 收集1个"暗影" / Collect 1 Shadow | 高伤害慢速——近战爆发 / High damage, slow — melee burst |
| **月光石斧 / Moonstone Axe** | 收集3个"暗影" / Collect 3 Shadows | 中距离AOE——攻防一体 / Mid-range AOE — offense and defense |
| **白骨法杖 / Bone Skull** | 收集5个"暗影" / Collect 5 Shadows | 远程法杖——召唤骷髅头攻击 / Ranged staff — summons skull projectiles |
| **银色双刃 / Silver Blades** | 收集7个"暗影" / Collect 7 Shadows | 快速双持——攻速最快 / Fast dual-wield — highest attack speed |
