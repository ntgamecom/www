## 隐藏区域 / Hidden Zones

### 第一章隐藏区域 / Act 1 Hidden Zones

**被遗忘的洞穴 / Forgotten Caverns**: 在溺亡海岸的一个隐蔽瀑布后——用**位移技能**穿过瀑布发现入口。里面有一个**黄金宝箱**和一个独特戒指"溺亡者的低语"——+20冰霜抗性+冰霜伤害光环。每次创建新角色都可以来拿——是开荒期最强戒指之一 / **Forgotten Caverns**: Behind a waterfall in the Drowned Coast — use **mobility skill** to pass through. Contains a **Golden Chest** and unique ring "Drowned Whisper" — +20 cold resist + cold damage aura. Can be looted per new character — one of the strongest leveling rings.

**僧院密道 / Monastery Secret Passage**: 在僧院一层东侧的书架后面——点击书名为"虚空之门"的书打开暗门。密室内有**天赋重置书x5**和一个隐藏的**附魔师之桌**——可以免费为一件装备附魔。每个角色仅限一次——建议给终局装备使用 / **Monastery Secret Passage**: Behind a bookshelf on the east side of Monastery L1 — click book titled "Gate of the Void". Contains **Orb of Regret x5** and hidden **Enchanting Table** — free enchant for one item. One-time per character — save for endgame gear.

**监狱地下藏宝室 / Prison Hidden Vault**: 在监狱最深处的刑讯室——地板上有血迹的砖块——踩上去触发隐藏楼梯。地下藏宝室有**独特项链"囚徒的忍耐"**——+30全元素抗性+减少15%受到的物理伤害——非常强力的防御项链 / **Prison Hidden Vault**: Deepest interrogation room in prison — bloodstained floor tile — step on to trigger hidden stairs. Contains unique amulet "Prisoner's Endurance" — +30 all elemental resist + 15% reduced phys damage taken — very strong defensive amulet.

### 第二章隐藏区域 / Act 2 Hidden Zones

**瓦尔地下城 / Vaal UnderCity**: 在瓦尔主城的下水道入口——需要"瓦尔下水道钥匙"(从瓦尔商人购买或在沙漠精英掉落)。地下有三层——每层有精英Boss——最终Boss掉落**瓦尔宝珠x3**。第三层还有一个隐藏宝箱——需要击败隐藏精英"下水道之王"——刷新位置随机 / **Vaal UnderCity**: Sewer entrance in Vaal City — need "Vaal Sewer Key" (buy from merchant or drop from elites). Three levels with elite bosses — final boss drops **Vaal Orb x3**. Floor 3 has hidden chest — need to kill "Sewer King" hidden elite — random spawn location.

**黄金沙漠密室 / Golden Desert Chamber**: 在沙漠西部金字塔形建筑——破解符文谜题(周围四个神像的顺序提示)。解开后获得**黄金之肤**——独特胸甲——10%物理伤害转化为混沌伤害——非常适合混沌Build。谜题顺序每次新角色不同——需要观察神像的朝向 / **Golden Desert Chamber**: Pyramid structure in western desert — solve rune puzzle (hints on four surrounding statues). Rewards **Golden Skin** — unique chest — 10% phys damage taken as chaos — great for chaos builds. Puzzle order changes per character — observe statue orientations.

**隐匿绿洲 / Hidden Oasis**: 在沙漠中有一个低概率出现的隐藏绿洲——入口每次地图刷新位置不同。绿洲中有**沙漠之花**(卡西娅任务的必需材料)和一个**纯净之泉**——饮用获得2小时的经验加成Buff——死亡后消失 / **Hidden Oasis**: Low-probability spawn in desert — entrance location changes per map instance. Contains **Desert Bloom** (Cassia quest material) and **Pure Spring** — drink for 2-hour XP buff — lost on death.

### 第三章隐藏区域 / Act 3 Hidden Zones

**废弃工厂暗室 / Abandoned Factory Dark Room**: 工厂中看起来是死路的走廊——攻击墙上颜色不同的砖会露出暗门。内有**独特武器设计图**——交给铁匠可制作一把绑定账号的传奇武器。设计图有两种: 剑/弓——取决于你的职业 / **Abandoned Factory Dark Room**: Dead-end corridor in factory — attack differently colored brick reveals secret. Contains **Unique Weapon Blueprint** — give to blacksmith for account-bound legendary weapon. Two blueprint types: Sword/Bow — depends on class.

**圣域藏书馆 / Sanctum Hidden Library**: 在圣域试炼第三层有隐藏岔路——需要高移速(30%+)冲刺过陷阱走廊到达。藏书馆里有**3本辅助宝石经验书**——每本提升辅助宝石1级。这个区域没有敌人——但陷阱密度极高——推荐带位移技能 / **Sanctum Hidden Library**: Hidden path on Sanctum floor 3 — need 30%+ movespeed to sprint through trap corridor. Contains **3 Support Gem XP Books** — each levels a support gem by 1. No enemies but very high trap density — bring mobility skills.

## 隐藏Boss / Secret Bosses

### 第一章隐藏Boss：缝合怪 / Act 1 Secret Boss: The Stitched

**触发条件**: 在监狱区域收集5具尸体(分散在监狱各牢房中)→带回监狱中心的缝合台上→点击缝合台触发战斗 / **Trigger**: Collect 5 corpses (scattered in prison cells) → bring to stitching table in prison center → click table to initiate.

**Boss特性**: 高血量低伤害——不断召唤小怪。核心机制**缝合**——将两个小怪缝合变成精英怪。优先清理小怪不让Boss缝合。如果缝合超过3次会召唤**巨型缝合怪**——极难对付——但掉落更好 / **Boss Traits**: High HP, low damage — constantly summons adds. Core mechanic **Stitch** — combines two adds into elite. Clear adds quickly to prevent stitching. More than 3 stitches summons **Mega Stitched** — very hard but better drops.

**掉落**: **缝合者之袭**——独特手套——击杀怪物有几率召唤缝合体小怪。**腐肉**——制作独特的召唤相关词缀的材料 / **Drop**: **Stitcher's Grasp** — unique gloves — chance to summon stitched minion on kill. **Carrion** — crafting material for unique minion affixes.

### 第三章隐藏Boss：机械核心 / Act 3 Secret Boss: The Core

**触发条件**: 在工厂区域收集4个**核心碎片**(四个角落精英掉落)→带到工厂中央机器核心插入 / **Trigger**: Collect 4 **Core Fragments** (elite drops in four corners) → insert into central machine core.

**Boss特性**: 机械Boss——阶段一旋转叶片(弱点击破后进入阶段二)。阶段二核心暴露——极限输出。每30秒**电磁脉冲**——破坏所有护盾——需要提前走位躲避。阶段二Boss会释放**机械虫群**——多只追踪小机器人——需要AOE清理 / **Boss Traits**: Mechanical boss — Phase 1 rotating blades (stagger to expose). Phase 2 exposes core — burst DPS. Uses **EMP** every 30 sec — destroys all shields — dodge preemptively. Phase 2 also releases **Drone Swarm** — homing robots — needs AoE clear.

**掉落**: **核心驱动**——独特腰带——移动速度+15%——位移后获得5秒护盾充能加速。**机械核心残骸**——制作护盾类装备的特殊材料 / **Drop**: **Core Drive** — unique belt — +15% movespeed — shield recharge boost after mobility. **Core Wreckage** — special material for crafting shield-based gear.

### 第五章隐藏Boss：暗影使者 / Act 5 Secret Boss: Shadow Bringer

**触发条件**: 在深渊区域中击杀50只暗影生物——之后暗影使者会在深渊最深处出现。暗影生物的击杀计数器在当前角色在线时持续——下线重置 / **Trigger**: Kill 50 Shadow creatures in Abyss zone — Shadow Bringer appears at deepest point. Counter persists while online — resets on logout.

**Boss特性**: 极其快速的近战Boss——使用**暗影突袭**——瞬间移动到玩家背后攻击。**暗影之幕**——全场变暗——只有Boss的眼睛发光——此时Boss伤害x2。**暗影爆发**——范围暗影爆炸——击飞玩家。打法: 暗影之幕时保持移动并观察Boss眼睛的位置——那是Boss的真实位置 / **Boss Traits**: Extremely fast melee boss — uses **Shadow Strike** — teleports behind player instantly. **Shadow Screen** — darkness falls — only boss eyes glow — boss damage x2. **Shadow Burst** — AoE shadow explosion — knocks back. Strategy: keep moving during Shadow Screen — track boss eyes for position.

**掉落**: **暗影之刃**——独特匕首——攻击时20%几率触发暗影突袭。**暗影核心**——用于为装备添加暗影词缀 / **Drop**: **Shadow Blade** — unique dagger — 20% chance to trigger Shadow Strike on attack. **Shadow Core** — add shadow affixes to gear.

### 隐藏NPC配方 / Hidden NPC Recipes

**卡西娅的承诺**: 第二章沙漠中找到流浪NPC卡西娅——给她"沙漠之花"(隐匿绿洲找到)→她给一把**随机独特武器**。每天一次——刷新16小时。建议在60级后再去找她——掉落物等级取决于你的角色等级 / **Cassia's Promise**: Find Cassia in Act 2 desert — give her "Desert Bloom" (Hidden Oasis) → random **unique weapon**. Once per day — 16-hour reset. Recommended to visit after level 60 — drop ilvl scales with character level.

**赌徒的交换**: 第三章兵营隐藏赌徒——用**100个机会石**交换**随机传奇装备**。位置每次新角色不同——通过找酒馆老板获得线索。赌徒也可能出售**命运卡**(独特的通货物品)——可以用混沌石购买 / **Gambler's Exchange**: Hidden Gambler in Act 3 barracks — trade **100 Opportunity Orbs** for **random legendary**. Location changes per character — ask tavern owner for clues. Gambler also sells **Fate Cards** (unique currency) — buy with Chaos Orbs.

## 特殊遭遇事件 / Special Encounters

**流浪商人**: 异界地图中偶尔出现——出售独特物品，只收**混沌石**。库存30分钟刷新一次。推荐每次见到他至少买一个6孔连接物品——开荒期极有价值。他也有小概率出售**猎首**——最稀有的腰带之一——约0.1%几率出现在库存中 / **Wandering Merchant**: Randomly in Atlas maps — sells unique items for **Chaos Orbs** only. Stock refreshes every 30 min. Recommended to buy at least one 6-link item each encounter. Small chance to sell **Headhunter** — one of rarest belts — ~0.1% inventory spawn rate.

**角斗场事件**: 地图中概率触发——场地中央出现竞技场圈→进入触发3波精英。奖励**角斗场宝箱**——开出高阶地图和独特珠宝。第三波有几率出现**角斗场冠军**——一个独特精英——掉落角斗士头盔幻化 / **Arena Event**: Randomly triggers in maps — arena circle at center → enter for 3 elite waves. Rewards **Arena Chest** — high-tier maps and unique jewels. Wave 3 may spawn **Arena Champion** — unique elite — drops Gladiator helmet transmog.

**深渊裂隙**: 紫色裂隙——进入后有5层战斗和奖励房间。每层Boss掉落**深渊精华**——为装备添加混沌词缀。在T14+地图出现率最高。第五层有几率遇到**深渊之主**的幻影——击败获得独特深渊珠宝 / **Abyss Rift**: Purple rift — 5 floors of battles and rewards. Each floor boss drops **Abyss Essence** — add chaos affixes to gear. Most common in T14+ maps. Floor 5 may spawn **Abyss Lord** phantom — drops unique Abyss jewel.

## 隐藏天赋点 / Hidden Passive Points

除了主线任务奖励的天赋点外还有隐藏天赋点: **第一章**: 点击溺亡海岸远端的海妖雕像——获得1天赋点。**第二章**: 在沙漠中找到神秘的沙漠符文——按正确顺序激活——获得2天赋点。**第四章**: 在混沌神殿的隐藏房间中找到古老之书——获得1天赋点。总计4个隐藏天赋点——全收集后可以多出4点天赋——这相当于一个小型天赋大点的价值 / Beyond main quest passive points: **Act 1**: Click Siren statue at far end of Drowned Coast — 1 passive point. **Act 2**: Find mysterious desert runes — activate in correct order — 2 passive points. **Act 4**: Find ancient book in Chaos Temple hidden room — 1 passive point. Total 4 hidden points — equivalent to one notable passive.
