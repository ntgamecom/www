## 共鸣者系统概述 / Resonator System Overview

鸣潮的可玩角色称为「共鸣者」，每一位共鸣者拥有一种元素属性和一种武器类型。元素属性决定伤害类型和元素反应，武器类型决定攻击模组和操作手感。

Playable characters in Wuthering Waves are called Resonators. Each Resonator has one Element and one Weapon type. The Element determines damage type and reactions, while the Weapon type determines attack patterns and playstyle.

## 六种元素 / Six Elements

**衍射 / Spectro**：光属性伤害，通常附带治疗或增益效果。代表角色：漂泊者（衍射）、维里奈、鉴心。Spectro deals light-element damage, often with healing or buffing effects. Representative Resonators: Rover (Spectro), Verina, Jianxin.

**湮灭 / Havoc**：暗属性伤害，高爆发输出。代表角色：漂泊者（湮灭）、卡卡罗、安可。Havoc deals dark-element damage with high burst output. Representative Resonators: Rover (Havoc), Calcharo, Encore.

**冷凝 / Glacio**：冰属性伤害，可以冻结敌人或降低敌人速度。代表角色：凌阳、雪豹、散华。Glacio deals ice damage, capable of freezing enemies or slowing them. Representative Resonators: Lingyang, Snow Leopard, Sanhua.

**气动 / Aero**：风属性伤害，擅长聚怪和群体控制。代表角色：秧秧、漂泊者（未来形态）。Aero deals wind damage, excelling at grouping enemies and crowd control. Representative Resonators: Yangyang.

**导电 / Electro**：雷属性伤害，高速连击和能量恢复。代表角色：渊武、卡卡罗（导电形态）。Electro deals lightning damage with fast combos and energy regeneration. Representative Resonators: Yuanwu.

**热熔 / Fusion**：火属性伤害，持续灼烧和范围攻击。代表角色：炽霞、马小芳。Fusion deals fire damage with burn effects and area attacks. Representative Resonators: Chixia.

## 五种武器 / Five Weapon Types

**长刃 / Broadblade**：重型大剑，攻击范围大、单发伤害高，但攻击速度慢。适合主力输出手。Heavy greatsword, wide attack range, high single-hit damage, slow attack speed. Ideal for main DPS.

**佩枪 / Pistols**：双持手枪，远程攻击，灵活机动。适合持续输出和风筝敌人。Dual-wield pistols, ranged attacks, agile and mobile. Suitable for sustained DPS and kiting.

**臂铠 / Gauntlets**：拳套武器，攻速极快、连段丰富，近战压制力强。Fast attack speed, rich combo chains, strong melee pressure.

**音感仪 / Rectifier**：法器/法球，远程法术攻击，通常用于辅助和治疗角色。Ranged magic attacks, typically used by support and healing characters.

**迅刀 / Sword**：单手剑，平衡型武器，攻守兼备，连招流畅。Balanced weapon, offense and defense in one, fluid combos.

## 五星共鸣者评级 / 5-Star Resonator Tier List

以下评级基于1.0至1.x版本环境，仅供参考。抽卡建议优先T0和T1角色。

Tier ratings based on 1.0 to 1.x meta, for reference only. Pull priority: T0 and T1 first.

**T0 — 顶级 / Top Tier**

维里奈 / Verina：五星衍射辅助，全能治疗+增益，几乎适用于所有队伍。全游最强辅助之一。Spectro support, universal healer and buffer, fits nearly every team. One of the best supports in the game.

漂泊者（湮灭）/ Rover (Havoc)：免费赠送，强度在线。湮灭属性主C，大招爆发极高。Free, strong Havoc main DPS with high burst ultimate.

**T1 — 强力 / Strong**

卡卡罗 / Calcharo：湮灭长刃主C，连招流畅，爆发伤害优秀。Havoc Broadblade main DPS, smooth combos, excellent burst damage.

安可 / Encore：湮灭音感仪主C，远程持续输出，操作简单。Havoc Rectifier main DPS, ranged sustained damage, easy to play.

炽霞 / Chixia：热熔佩枪输出，远程灵活，对单伤害可观。Fusion Pistols DPS, ranged and agile, solid single-target damage.

**T2 — 可用 / Viable**

凌阳 / Lingyang：冷凝臂铠主C，近战爆发，需要一定操作技巧。Glacio Gauntlets main DPS, melee burst, requires some skill.

鉴心 / Jianxin：衍射臂铠辅助，护盾和聚怪，生存能力强。Spectro Gauntlets support, shield and grouping, strong survivability.

雪豹 / Snow Leopard：冷凝臂铠输出，对群能力强，但对单稍弱。Glacio Gauntlets, strong AoE but weaker single-target.

**T3 — 特定用途 / Situational**

渊武 / Yuanwu：导电臂铠副C/辅助，雷系挂件，适合特定队伍。Electro Gauntlets sub-DPS/support, electro enabler for specific teams.

## 四星共鸣者推荐 / Recommended 4-Star Resonators

**秧秧 / Yangyang**：气动迅刀，聚怪能力强，大范围牵引，前期开荒利器。Aero Sword, strong grouping ability with wide pull, excellent for early game.

**散华 / Sanhua**：冷凝迅刀，技能冷却短，连招快，冰系副C优秀选择。Glacio Sword, short skill cooldown, fast combos, solid ice sub-DPS.

**马小芳**：热熔佩枪，朴实好用的火系输出，前期过渡主力。Fusion Pistols, simple and effective fire DPS for early game transition.

**白芷 / Baizhi**：冷凝音感仪治疗，四星中最佳治疗角色，维里奈的下位替代。Glacio Rectifier healer, best 4-star healer, Verina substitute.

## 配队思路 / Team Building

标准配队通常为：1主C + 1副C/辅助 + 1治疗/盾。

Standard team: 1 Main DPS + 1 Sub-DPS/Support + 1 Healer/Shielder.

**主C / Main DPS**：承担主要输出任务，站场时间最长。Requires the most field time, deals primary damage.

**副C / Sub-DPS**：在后台提供伤害，或通过延奏技能为下一个角色增伤。Provides off-field damage or buffs the next character via Outro Skill.

**辅助/治疗 / Support/Healer**：维持队伍生存，提供攻击力或元素增伤等增益。Maintains team survivability, provides ATK or elemental damage buffs.

**热门配队示例 / Popular Team Examples**：

湮灭队 Havoc Team：卡卡罗（主C）+ 散华（副C）+ 维里奈（治疗）
维里奈队 Verina Team：任意主C + 副C/辅助 + 维里奈
新手队 Beginner Team：漂泊者（主C）+ 秧秧（聚怪）+ 白芷（治疗）

## 角色培养材料 / Character Ascension Materials

角色突破需要采集特定区域的特产素材和击败世界Boss掉落的专属材料。

Character ascension requires local specialty materials from specific regions and unique boss materials.

突破材料可在地图上追踪，Boss材料每周可用波片反复刷取。建议提前规划要培养的角色，每日收集对应材料。

Ascension materials can be tracked on the map. Boss materials can be farmed repeatedly with Waveplate. Plan ahead for characters you want to build and collect materials daily.

## 命座系统 / Sequence System

抽取重复角色获得命座（鸣链 Sequence）。每个角色有6个命座，逐步解锁强力被动效果。

Pulling duplicate characters grants Sequences (constellations). Each character has 6 sequences, gradually unlocking powerful passive effects.

命座对角色提升幅度不一：部分角色核心机制在0命即可完整体验，部分角色需要1命或2命才能发挥全部实力。

Sequence power varies: some characters are complete at S0, while others need S1 or S2 to reach full potential.

抽到重复5星给2个余波珊瑚，可在商店兑换命座材料或抽卡道具。

Duplicate 5-stars give 2 Afterglow Coral, exchangeable for sequence materials or gacha currency in the shop.

## 角色机制详解 / Character Mechanics

**共鸣能量 / Resonance Energy**：每个角色需要80-140点共鸣能量来释放共鸣解放。能量通过普通攻击（+5）、共鸣技能（+15-20）、受击（+5）和协奏能量切换（+10）积累。The energy cost for Resonance Liberation varies by character, from 80 to 140. Energy is gained through basic attacks, Resonance Skill, taking damage, and Concerto swaps.

**固有技能 / Inherent Skills**：角色突破后解锁两个固有被动技能，通常提供基础属性加成或战斗增益。Unlocked after ascension, providing base stat bonuses or combat buffs.

**共鸣链 / Sequence Nodes**：每个命座提供不同的强化效果，部分关键命座能改变角色的输出循环。Some key sequences fundamentally change a character's rotation.

**倍率分析 / Multiplier Analysis**：不同角色的技能倍率差异巨大。主C角色的共鸣解放总倍率通常在800%-1500%区间，辅助角色的倍率则较低但附带增益效果。Skill multipliers vary greatly between characters. Main DPS ultimates range from 800%-1500% total multiplier, while supports have lower multipliers with buff effects.

## 元素共鸣 / Elemental Synergy

虽然鸣潮的元素系统以克制为主，但部分元素之间存在配合潜力：

While the element system focuses on counters, some元素的 combinations have synergy potential:

**衍射+湮灭循环 / Spectro+Havoc Rotation**：利用克制关系打出高额额外伤害。Using the counter relationship for bonus damage.

**热熔+冷凝配合 / Fusion+Glacio Combo**：两种元素交替攻击可触发特殊异常状态。Alternating attacks can trigger special status effects.

**导电高频充能 / Electro High-Frequency Energy**：导电角色的高速攻击能快速积累协奏能量，适合作为队伍润滑剂。Electro characters' fast attacks build Concerto Energy quickly, making them good team lubricants.
