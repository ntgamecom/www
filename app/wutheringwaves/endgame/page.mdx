## 终局玩法概述 / Endgame Overview

当玩家达到较高Union等级并将角色培养到一定强度后，可以挑战终局玩法。终局内容是获得养成资源和证明实力的主要途径。

After reaching higher Union Levels and sufficiently building characters, players can tackle endgame content. Endgame content is the primary way to obtain progression resources and prove your strength.

## 逆境深塔 / Tower of Adversity

逆境深塔是鸣潮的主要挑战模式，类似其他游戏中的深渊/忘却之庭。玩家使用多支队伍挑战多层高难度战斗，每层有时间限制。

Tower of Adversity is the main challenge mode, similar to Spiral Abyss in other games. Players use multiple teams to clear several floors of high-difficulty combat, each with a time limit.

**层数结构 / Floor Structure**：

- 分为稳定区和危险区，每半月刷新一次
- 每区包含3个层，每层包含1-2波敌人
- 越往上层数敌人强度越高，时间限制越严格
- 完成每层可获得星声奖励

- Divided into Stable and Hazard zones, resetting every two weeks
- Each zone has 3 floors, each with 1-2 waves of enemies
- Higher floors have stronger enemies and stricter time limits
- Clearing each floor rewards Astrite

**层数要求 / Floor Requirements**：

- 初始层：单队挑战，敌人等级约70-80
- 中间层：单队挑战，敌人等级80-90
- 最高层：两支队伍分别挑战两侧，综合评分计算总分
- 最高层需要两支完整的独立队伍，一般是两队主C+辅助组合

- Starting floors: single team, enemies level 70-80
- Mid floors: single team, enemies level 80-90
- Top floors: two teams on separate sides, combined score
- Top floors require two full independent teams, typically with their own DPS+support combos

**奖励 / Rewards**：

- 首次通关：星声、材料、限定称号
- 刷新重置：每月的星声奖励（约600-1200星声/月）
- 满星奖励：额外材料奖励

- First clear: Astrite, materials, limited title
- Reset: monthly Astrite rewards (about 600-1200 per month)
- Full star rewards: bonus materials

**挑战技巧 / Challenge Tips**：

1. 优先击破Boss弱点 / Prioritize breaking boss weaknesses
2. 合理分配两队的元素属性应对不同敌人抗性 / Allocate elements across two teams for enemy resistances
3. 利用延奏技能循环最大化爆发出伤速度 / Master Outro Skill rotations for max burst DPS
4. 最后一波敌人登场前预留技能CD / Save cooldowns before last wave spawns
5. 生存压力大时可携带治疗角色确保生存 / Bring healers if survivability is an issue

## 深境之域 / Depths of Illusive Realm

深境之域是Roguelite模式的挑战玩法，玩家在秘境中通过一系列随机战斗收集强化buff，最终挑战Boss。

Depths of Illusive Realm is a Roguelite challenge mode. Players navigate a series of randomized combat encounters, collecting buffs, and ultimately challenge a boss.

**玩法机制 / Gameplay Mechanics**：

- 选择一名角色进入秘境，初始携带基础技能
- 每通关一层选择增益效果（信物），效果可叠加
- 迷宫布局随机，包含战斗房、精英房、宝箱房和休息房
- 随着深度增加敌人强度提升，增益效果越来越关键
- 最终层需要击败秘境Boss

- Select one character to enter, starting with basic skills
- Choose buffs (relics) after each floor cleared, effects stack
- Random maze layout: combat, elite, chest, and rest rooms
- Enemy strength increases with depth, buffs become critical
- Final floor requires defeating the domain boss

**信物类型 / Relic Types**：

- 伤害增益：提升普攻/技能/大招伤害倍率
- 生存增益：护盾、生命恢复、减伤
- 机制强化：延长极限闪避时间、增加弹刀判定窗口
- 特殊效果：额外攻击弹射、元素爆破、概率连击等

- Damage buffs: increase basic attack/skill/ultimate multipliers
- Survival buffs: shields, HP regen, damage reduction
- Mechanics: longer dodge window, wider parry window
- Special effects: extra attack bounces, elemental explosions, chance combos

**奖励 / Rewards**：

- 首通奖励：星声、限定头像框、材料
- 成就系统：完成特定条件获得额外奖励
- 每周刷新：部分奖励每周重置

- First clear: Astrite, limited avatar frame, materials
- Achievement system: extra rewards for specific conditions
- Weekly reset: some rewards reset weekly

深境之域不需要消耗波片，是免费获取星声和材料的好途径。高难度适合测试角色单走能力和极限操作。

Depths of Illusive Realm costs no Waveplate, making it a great source of free Astrite and materials. Higher difficulties test solo character strength and mechanical skill.

## 无音区 / Forgery Challenge

无音区是武器突破材料的专属副本，分为不同元素属性（衍射、湮灭、冷凝、气动、导电、热熔）。

Forgery Challenge is the domain for weapon ascension materials, divided by element type.

**难度等级 / Difficulty Tiers**：

| 难度 / Tier | 推荐等级 / Recommended Level | 掉落 / Drops |
|:----:|:----:|:----:|
| I | 20 | 基础材料 |
| II | 30 | 基础+中级材料 |
| III | 40 | 中级材料 |
| IV | 50 | 中级+高级材料 |
| V | 60 | 高级材料 |
| VI | 70+ | 最高级材料 |

**挑战规则 / Challenge Rules**：

- 消耗40-60波片领取奖励
- 限时挑战（通常5-8分钟）
- 可联机匹配合作通关
- 每天不限制挑战次数，但波片有限

- Cost 40-60 Waveplate to claim rewards
- Time-limited challenge (usually 5-8 minutes)
- Co-op matchmaking available
- No daily attempt limit, but Waveplate is limited

## 模拟训练 / Simulation Training

模拟训练是角色技能升级材料的主要来源，分为多种类型对应不同材料系列。

Simulation Training is the main source of skill upgrade materials, divided into series for different material types.

**训练类型 / Training Types**：

- 燃素训练：产出热熔/导电系技能材料
- 蒸馏训练：产出冷凝/衍射系技能材料
- 脉动训练：产出湮灭/气动系技能材料

- Combust Training: Fusion/Electro skill materials
- Distill Training: Glacio/Spectro skill materials
- Pulse Training: Havoc/Aero skill materials

**难度梯度 / Difficulty Tiers**：同样分为6个难度等级，随Union等级解锁。Six difficulty tiers, unlocked with Union Level.

## 资源规划建议 / Resource Planning

**每周必做 / Weekly Must-Do**：

1. 逆境深塔结算（刷新两次/月） / Tower of Adversity clear (resets twice monthly)
2. 周本Boss 3次奖励次数 / 3 Weekly boss reward claims
3. 深境之域每周奖励 / Depths of Illusive Realm weekly rewards
4. 版本活动任务 / Version event quests

**资源分配优先级 / Resource Allocation Priority**：

1. 主力队伍角色等级拉满 / Max main team character levels
2. 主力队伍武器等级拉满 / Max weapon levels
3. 主力队伍核心技能升满 / Core skills to max
4. 主力队伍回声主属性正确 / Correct Echo main stats
5. 二队培养准备深渊 / Build second team for Tower
6. 追求副词缀优化 / Optimize substats

**长线目标 / Long-term Goals**：

- 两支完整队伍（每种元素至少一个主力） / Two full teams (at least one main per element)
- 逆境深塔稳定满星 / Consistent full stars in Tower of Adversity
- 深境之域最高难度通关 / Clear highest difficulty Depths
- 主力角色全技能满级 / Max all skills on main characters
- 完美副属性回声毕业 / Perfect substat Echo builds

终局内容是检验角色养成成果的试金石。通过合理规划和持续努力，每位漂泊者都能征服索拉里斯大陆的所有挑战。

Endgame content is the ultimate test of character progression. Through proper planning and persistent effort, every Rover can conquer all challenges across the Solaris continent.
