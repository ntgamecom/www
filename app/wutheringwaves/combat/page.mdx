## 战斗系统概述 / Combat Overview

鸣潮的战斗系统是游戏的核心亮点，以高速、流畅的实时动作为特色。队伍由三名角色组成，战斗中可随时切换角色，配合协奏能量系统打出华丽连招。

Wuthering Waves' combat system is the game's standout feature, characterized by fast, fluid real-time action. Teams consist of three characters that can be freely swapped mid-combat, combining with the Concerto Energy system for stylish combos.

## 基础操作 / Basic Controls

**普通攻击 / Basic Attack**：连续点击进行多段攻击，不同角色的连段数量和动作模组各不相同。持续点击或按住可触发终结技。Tap repeatedly for multi-hit combos, varying by character. Hold or sustained press triggers finishers.

**重击 / Heavy Attack**：消耗部分体力进行强力一击，部分角色重击有特殊效果如蓄力或范围攻击。Consumes stamina for a powerful strike. Some characters have special charged or AoE heavy attacks.

**空中攻击 / Aerial Attack**：跳跃后在空中进行攻击，部分技能可在空中释放，增加操作维度。Jump then attack in mid-air. Some skills can be used airborne, adding a vertical dimension.

**闪避 / Dodge**：快速位移躲避敌人攻击，消耗闪避耐力（黄色能量条）。闪避有短暂的无敌帧。Quick movement to evade attacks, consumes dodge stamina (yellow bar). Provides brief invincibility frames.

**钩索攻击 / Grapple Hook Attack**：使用钩索命中敌人后拉近并追击，可用于起手或连段衔接。Use the Grapple Hook to pull toward an enemy for a follow-up attack, useful as an opener or combo linker.

## 极限闪避与反击 / Extreme Dodge & Counter

在敌人攻击命中前的瞬间进行闪避，触发极限闪避——进入子弹时间，敌人动作变慢，同时触发特殊反击。此时按下攻击键可发动极限闪避反击，造成额外伤害并击退敌人。

Dodge precisely at the moment before an enemy attack hits to trigger Extreme Dodge, slowing time and enabling a special counter. Press attack to execute an Extreme Dodge Counter, dealing bonus damage and knocking enemies back.

极限闪避反击是新手最重要的生存技巧——不要贪刀，观察敌人动作，掌握闪避节奏。

Extreme Dodge Counter is the most important survival technique for beginners. Don't greed for damage, observe enemy patterns, and master dodge timing.

**极限闪避触发时机 / Extreme Dodge Window**：在敌人攻击判定框即将接触角色时闪避，约0.2秒的判定窗口。Dodge when the enemy's hitbox is about to connect with the character, roughly a 0.2-second window.

## 弹刀与招架 / Parry Mechanic

部分敌人发动特定攻击时，身体或武器会发出金色光芒——这是「弹刀预警」信号。在这些攻击命中前使用攻击（而非闪避）正面迎击，可以触发弹刀（招架），大幅削减敌人的韧性值（白条）。

When certain enemies glow gold during specific attacks, use an attack instead of dodge to meet them head-on, triggering a Parry. This significantly depletes the enemy's poise bar (white bar).

弹刀成功后：敌人会陷入短暂硬直，韧性条大幅减少，韧性归零时敌人进入瘫痪状态，受到的伤害大幅提升。

Successful parry results in: brief enemy stagger, major poise reduction, and when poise reaches zero, the enemy enters a瘫痪 (paralyzed) state, taking greatly increased damage.

**弹刀小贴士 / Parry Tips**：注意金色闪光而非敌人动作本身。每个Boss的可弹刀攻击有固定模式，多练习即可掌握。Watch for the gold flash, not the enemy animation. Each boss has fixed parryable patterns, learnable through practice.

## 协奏能量系统 / Concerto Energy System

协奏能量是战斗的核心资源，位于角色头像旁的圆形进度条。通过攻击敌人和切换角色积累协奏能量。

Concerto Energy is the core combat resource, shown as a circular progress bar next to the character avatar. Accumulated by attacking enemies and swapping characters.

协奏能量充满后，当前角色头像会发光。此时切换角色将触发：

When Concerto Energy fills, the current character's avatar glows. Switching at this point triggers:

**变奏技能 / Intro Skill**：新入场角色释放的特殊技能，通常附带高伤害或控制效果。Special skill performed by the incoming character, often with high damage or control effects.

**延奏技能 / Outro Skill**：退场角色释放的结束技能，通常为下一个角色提供增益（如增伤、元素附魔、能量回复等）。Exit skill performed by the outgoing character, typically buffing the next character (damage bonus, elemental infusion, energy regen, etc.).

协奏能量循环是鸣潮战斗的策略核心——通过合理安排角色切换顺序，让每个角色的延奏技能为下一位角色增益，形成最大化输出循环。

The Concerto Energy rotation is the strategic core of combat. By carefully planning swap order, each character's Outro Skill buffs the next, creating a maximized damage rotation.

**协奏能量积累 / Concerto Energy Gain**：普通攻击（小量）、共鸣技能（中量）、共鸣解放（大量）、完美闪避/弹刀（中量）、切换角色入场攻击（中量）。

## 共鸣技能与共鸣解放 / Resonance Skill & Liberation

**共鸣技能 / Resonance Skill**：每个角色的核心技能，冷却时间短（通常5-12秒），是主要的伤害和功能来源。按E键释放。Each character's core skill, short cooldown (usually 5-12 seconds), primary source of damage and utility. Press E to use.

**共鸣解放 / Resonance Liberation**：角色大招，需要积攒共鸣能量（角色头像旁的能量条）。能量通过攻击和受击积累，充满后按R键释放。共鸣解放伤害高、范围大，通常附带特殊演出动画。Each character's ultimate, requires Resonance Energy (bar next to avatar). Build through attacks and taking damage, press R to unleash. High damage, wide area, usually with special animation.

共鸣解放是队伍的主要爆发手段，建议在Boss被瘫痪或延奏技能增伤期间释放以获得最大收益。

Resonance Liberation is the team's main burst tool. Use during enemy瘫痪 windows or with Outro Skill buffs active for maximum benefit.

## 共鸣回路 / Forte Circuit

每个角色拥有独特的Forte Circuit（共鸣回路/核心被动），是角色玩法的精髓所在。Forte Circuit通常提供特殊的资源条或状态机制，需要特定操作来触发。

Each character has a unique Forte Circuit, the essence of their playstyle. It typically provides a special resource bar or state mechanic activated through specific actions.

例如：部分角色的Forte Circuit在特定连击后强化普通攻击，部分角色在施放共鸣技能后进入特殊状态获得额外效果，部分角色需要积攒特殊资源条来释放强化版技能。

For example: some characters enhance basic attacks after specific combos, some enter special states after using Resonance Skill, and some accumulate a special resource bar for enhanced skills.

## 队伍连招循环 / Team Rotation Strategy

标准队伍循环模板：

Standard team rotation template:

1. 起手辅助技能 → 副C入场 → 积累协奏能量后切主C（触发延奏增伤）
2. 主C站场输出 → 使用共鸣解放 → 打满协奏能量
3. 切回辅助/副C → 释放技能 → 循环

1. Start with support skill, swap to sub-DPS, build Concerto Energy then swap to main DPS (triggering Outro Skill buff).
2. Main DPS stays on field, uses Resonance Liberation, fills Concerto Energy.
3. Swap back to support/sub-DPS, use skills, repeat.

**进阶技巧 / Advanced Tips**：利用变奏技能的无敌帧躲避攻击，在延奏增伤期间内打出最高伤害，合理分配共鸣解放的释放时机。Use Intro Skill i-frames to dodge, maximize damage during Outro Skill buff windows, and time Resonance Liberation usage carefully.

## 元素反应 / Elemental Reactions

鸣潮的元素系统不同于传统元素反应游戏，各元素间存在克制关系而非复杂反应：

Wuthering Waves' element system features simple克制 (advantage) relationships rather than complex reactions:

- 衍射克制湮灭 / Spectro counters Havoc
- 湮灭克制衍射 / Havoc counters Spectro
- 热熔克制冷凝 / Fusion counters Glacio
- 冷凝克制热熔 / Glacio counters Fusion
- 导电与气动互不克制 / Electro and Aero are neutral

利用元素克制可获得额外伤害加成。屏幕上方会显示敌人的元素抗性信息。

Using elemental advantage grants bonus damage. Enemy elemental resistance info is shown at the top of the screen.

## 进阶战斗技巧 / Advanced Combat Techniques

**闪避取消 / Dodge Cancel**：大多数攻击动作的后摇可以通过闪避取消，提前进行下一次操作。这是提高输出效率的基础技巧。Most attack recovery animations can be canceled with dodge for earlier follow-up actions. This is fundamental DPS optimization.

**快速协奏 / Fast Concerto Building**：不同角色的协奏能量积累速度不同。利用共鸣技能接普攻的特定连段可以快速充满协奏能量。Swapping quickly after specific combos can rapidly fill Concerto Energy.

**多目标策略 / Multi-Target Strategy**：面对群敌时，优先使用聚怪技能集中敌人，再用范围攻击清场。注意精英怪的仇恨优先级。Prioritize grouping skills on mobs, then AoE clear. Watch elite monster aggro priority.

**Boss战站位 / Boss Positioning**：掌握Boss的攻击范围和安全点位，避免被逼入角落。大部分Boss的正面范围攻击最强，侧面和背后相对安全。Learn boss attack ranges and safe spots. Most bosses have the strongest attacks in front, with sides and rear being safer.

**空中战斗 / Aerial Combat**：部分敌人对地面攻击有高抗性，利用钩索升空进行空中连击可以规避地面攻击并获得额外伤害加成。Some enemies have high ground-attack resistance. Use Grapple Hook for aerial combos to avoid ground attacks.

**韧性管理 / Poise Management**：注意Boss的韧性条（白色）。使用弹刀和重击快速削减韧性，将Boss打入瘫痪状态是爆发输出的关键窗口。Watch the boss poise bar (white). Use parries and heavy attacks to quickly deplete it for the window to burst.

**团队协同 / Team Coordination**：在联机模式中，注意队友的位置和状态。不要同时使用多个聚怪技能造成重叠浪费，合理分配治疗和增益的时机。In co-op, watch teammates' positions and status. Avoid overlapping grouping skills, coordinate healing and buff timing.

## 伤害计算基础 / Damage Calculation Basics

伤害的大致计算公式 / Simplified damage formula：

`最终伤害 = 基础攻击力 × 技能倍率 × (1 + 伤害加成%) × (1 + 暴击伤害%) × 防御减免 × 抗性减免`

`Final DMG = Base ATK × Skill Multiplier × (1 + DMG Bonus%) × (1 + Crit DMG%) × DEF Reduction × RES Reduction`

了解伤害构成有助于合理搭配装备：攻击力、元素伤害和暴击暴伤应均衡发展，避免单一属性过度稀释。

Understanding damage composition helps with gear choices: balance ATK, elemental damage, and crit stats to avoid diminishing returns.

## 实战示例 / Combat Examples

**单体Boss战 / Single Boss Fight**：起手使用辅助角色的共鸣技能（如维里奈的领域），切换副C打满协奏能量，触发延奏增伤后切主C，开启共鸣解放+回声技能爆发输出。一套完整循环约15-20秒。Start with support skill, swap to sub-DPS to fill Concerto, trigger Outro buff, swap to main DPS, ult + Echo skill burst. Full rotation roughly 15-20 seconds.

**群怪场景 / Multi-Mob Encounter**：用秧秧或类似聚怪角色将敌人牵引到一起，切换范围输出角色释放AOE技能和共鸣解放清场。Group enemies with pull skills, then swap to AoE DPS for burst clear.

**高难挑战 / High Difficulty Challenge**：优先确保生存，利用极限闪避的无敌帧规避伤害。不急于输出，等待Boss出招后的硬直窗口再反击。Prioritize survival, use Extreme Dodge i-frames. Don't rush, wait for boss attack windows to counter.
