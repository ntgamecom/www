## 欢迎来到鸣潮 / Welcome to Wuthering Waves

鸣潮是库洛游戏开发的一款开放世界动作角色扮演游戏，于2024年5月23日全球发布。故事设定在一个被「悲鸣」毁灭后又重生的末世世界，玩家扮演「漂泊者」，在苏醒后失去记忆，与伙伴们一同穿越广阔的索拉里斯大陆，揭开世界毁灭的真相。

Wuthering Waves is an open-world action RPG developed by Kuro Games, released globally on May 23, 2024. Set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by the Lament, players take on the role of the Rover, an amnesiac awakening in a strange land, traveling across the vast Solaris continent to uncover the truth behind the world's destruction.

**平台 / Platforms**: PC, iOS, Android, PS5 | **发行商 / Publisher**: 库洛游戏 / Kuro Games

## 故事背景 / Story Overview

世界曾因「悲鸣」而毁灭——这是一种席卷整个大陆的灾难性频率，几乎所有生命都在这场浩劫中消逝。然而，世界在数十年后奇迹般地重生，新的文明在废墟之上建立。在这片重生的大地上，残留着大量名为「幻象」的怪物和被称为「回响」的奇异力量。漂泊者从实验室中苏醒，失去了所有记忆，身上却蕴含着能够对抗悲鸣的特殊频率。

The world was destroyed by the Lament, a catastrophic frequency that swept across the entire continent, wiping out nearly all life. Decades later, civilization miraculously rebuilt itself upon the ruins. The land is now filled with monsters called TDs and mysterious powers known as Echoes. The Rover awakens in a lab with no memories, yet carries within them a special frequency capable of opposing the Lament.

漂泊者与今州城的秧秧、炽霞等伙伴相遇，逐渐了解到「共鸣者」的存在——这些人类能够与特定频率共鸣，获得超凡能力。同时也发现，那股毁灭世界的悲鸣力量还未完全消失。在旅途中，漂泊者穿越瑝珑大地，抵达黑海岸，面对更深的阴谋和更强的敌人。

The Rover meets Yangyang, Chixia, and other companions in Jinzhou, gradually learning about Resonators, humans who can resonate with specific frequencies to gain extraordinary abilities. The Lament's destructive power has not yet fully dissipated. Throughout the journey, the Rover traverses the Huanglong region, reaches the Black Shores, and confronts deeper conspiracies and stronger enemies.

## 核心玩法 / Core Gameplay

### 实时动作战斗 / Real-Time Action Combat

鸣潮的战斗系统以高速、流畅的实时动作为核心。队伍由三名角色组成，战斗中可随时切换角色，打出连招。核心机制包括：普通攻击、共鸣技能、共鸣解放（大招）、变奏技能、延奏技能、极限闪避、弹刀（招架）和钩索攻击。

Wuthering Waves features a fast-paced real-time action combat system. Teams consist of three characters that can be swapped mid-combat for combo chains. Core mechanics include: Basic Attack, Resonance Skill, Resonance Liberation (Ultimate), Intro Skill, Outro Skill, Extreme Dodge, Parry, and Grapple Hook Attack.

战斗的深度来源于「协奏能量」系统——通过攻击和切换角色积累协奏能量，充满后可触发变奏技能（入场技）和延奏技能（退场技），形成流畅的输出循环。弹刀机制允许玩家在敌人攻击瞬间进行格挡，大幅削弱敌人韧性。

The combat depth comes from the Concerto Energy system, accumulated through attacks and character swaps. Filling it triggers Intro Skills (on entry) and Outro Skills (on exit), creating fluid rotation cycles. The Parry mechanic lets players block at the precise moment of enemy attacks, significantly depleting enemy poise.

### 回声系统 / Echo System

回声是鸣潮独特的装备系统，相当于可收集并使用的怪物能力。击败幻象后，有一定几率掉落其回声。每个回声不仅提供主属性和副属性加成，还可以作为主动技能使用——召唤怪物进行攻击或施加增益效果。回声分为不同Cost（费用），队伍总Cost有上限。同一套回响套装提供Sonata效果（套装加成）。

Echoes are Wuthering Waves' unique equipment system, functioning as collectible monster abilities. After defeating TDs, there is a chance to obtain their Echo. Each Echo provides main stats and substats, and can also be used as an active skill, summoning the monster for an attack or buff. Echoes have different Cost values with a team Cost limit. Echoes of the same set provide Sonata effects (set bonuses).

### 共鸣者养成 / Resonator Progression

角色养成包括等级提升、共鸣突破、技能升级、武器搭配和回声装配。每位共鸣者有六种技能：普通攻击、共鸣技能、共鸣解放、共鸣回路（核心被动）、变奏技能和延奏技能。技能需要消耗特定材料升级，材料可通过周本、模拟训练和野外采集获得。

Resonator progression includes leveling, Ascension, skill upgrades, weapon selection, and Echo equipping. Each Resonator has six skills: Basic Attack, Resonance Skill, Resonance Liberation, Forte Circuit (core passive), Intro Skill, and Outro Skill. Skills require specific materials found in weekly bosses, Simulation Training, and field gathering.

### 开放世界探索 / Open-World Exploration

索拉里斯大陆拥有广阔的开放世界，包含瑝珑、黑海岸等主要区域。探索方式包括跑步、攀爬、游泳和使用钩索（抓钩）高速移动。地图上分布着宝箱、采集物、挑战点和野外首领。鸣潮的探索设计强调垂直移动——你可以攀爬几乎任何表面，用钩索在悬崖间荡跃。

The Solaris continent features a vast open world with regions like Huanglong and Black Shores. Exploration includes running, climbing, swimming, and using the Grapple Hook for rapid traversal. The map is dotted with chests, collectibles, challenge points, and field bosses. Wuthering Waves' exploration emphasizes vertical mobility, allowing you to climb nearly any surface and swing between cliffs with the Grapple Hook.

## 特色系统 / Special Features

**无体力限制的探索 / Stamina-Free Exploration**：在野外奔跑和攀爬不消耗体力（耐力仅用于攀爬特定表面和游泳），让你可以自由探索世界。Running and climbing in the overworld costs no stamina, allowing unrestricted exploration.

**无相之面 / Tacet Discords**：野外分布着各种幻象怪物，击败它们可获得经验、材料和回声。A wide variety of Tacet Discords roam the land, providing EXP, materials, and Echoes upon defeat.

**合成与锻造 / Synthesis & Forging**：使用采集材料合成药剂和食物，使用锻造系统打造武器和升级材料。Use gathered materials to synthesize potions and food, and forge weapons and upgrade materials.

**联机合作 / Co-op Mode**：达到一定等级后可与其他玩家联机，共同挑战世界首领和副本。Reach a certain level to unlock co-op, allowing you to challenge world bosses and domains together.

## 探索区域 / World Regions

**瑝珑 / Huanglong**：游戏的主要区域，以中国传统文化为灵感，包含今州城、归墟港市、虎口山脉等多个子区域。The main region, inspired by traditional Chinese culture, encompassing Jinzhou, Guixu Port, and Tiger's Maw.

**黑海岸 / Black Shores**：以海洋文明为主题的区域，拥有独特的水域探索和建筑风格。An oceanic-themed region with unique water exploration and architecture.

## 常见问题 / FAQ

**问：鸣潮是买断制还是免费游戏？** 免费游玩，内含抽卡内购。角色和武器通过卡池抽取。Free to play with gacha mechanics. Characters and weapons are obtained through banner pulls.

**问：游戏有保底机制吗？** 角色池80抽保底，50%概率歪。歪掉后下次必出当期UP角色。武器池80抽保底，不会歪。80-pull pity on character banner with 50/50 system. Weapon banner has 80-pull pity without 50/50.

**问：推荐新手抽什么卡池？** 新手应在常驻池用80抽获取一名五星角色，随后将资源投入限时UP池。Beginner 80 pulls on standard banner for a 5-star, then save for limited banners.

**问：回声怎么刷？** 前往幻象密集区域刷对应怪物，也可通过副本和活动获取。Farm TDs in dense areas, or obtain through domains and events.

**问：多久更新一次版本？** 约每6周一个大版本，包含新角色、新区域和新活动。Major version update every 6 weeks with new characters, regions, and events.

**问：可以联机吗？** Union等级15级后解锁联机功能，最多可三人组队。Co-op unlocks at Union Level 15, up to 3 players per party.

**问：需要每天上线吗？** 体力和每日任务每24小时刷新，每天上线10-15分钟即可完成日常。波片上限240约24小时回满。Daily quests and Waveplate refill on a 24-hour cycle. 10-15 minutes per day is enough.

## 版本更新 / Version Updates

鸣潮采用约6周一个大版本的更新节奏。每个大版本带来：

Wuthering Waves follows a roughly 6-week version cycle. Each major version brings:

**新角色 / New Characters**：每版本推出1-2位新五星共鸣者，通常搭配专属武器池。One or two new 5-star Resonators per version, usually with accompanying weapon banner.

**新区域 / New Areas**：大型版本可能开放全新探索区域，扩展索拉里斯大陆的世界版图。Major versions may open new exploration areas, expanding the Solaris continent.

**新剧情 / New Story**：主线剧情持续推进，每版本更新1-2章主线任务。Main story advances with 1-2 new chapters per version.

**版本活动 / Version Events**：包含主题活动、挑战关卡和福利签到，是免费抽卡资源的重要来源。Themed events, challenge stages, and login bonuses provide free gacha currency.

**系统优化 / System Improvements**：根据玩家反馈持续优化游戏体验，包括UI改进、性能优化和便利功能添加。Continuous QoL improvements based on player feedback.

## 新手建议 / Tips for New Players

1. 主线优先：尽早完成主线解锁所有核心功能
2. 集中资源：前期不要分散培养多个角色，主练1-2名主力
3. 不要浪费星声：星声尽量用于限时UP角色池
4. 探索积累：日常探索收集特产材料，为后续角色突破做准备
5. 规划体力：优先刷角色突破Boss材料，其次技能材料
6. 利用工具人：白芷、秧秧等四星角色前期培养性价比极高

1. Prioritize main quests for feature unlocks
2. Focus resources on 1-2 main characters
3. Save Astrite for limited character banners
4. Explore daily to stockpile materials for future characters
5. Plan Waveplate usage: boss materials first, skill materials second
6. 4-star characters like Baizhi and Yangyang are great early investments
