## 首领系统概述 / Boss System Overview

鸣潮的首领分为三种类型：世界Boss（野外刷新）、周本Boss（每周限次奖励）和幻象（普通精英怪）。每种Boss掉落不同的材料和回声。

Wuthering Waves bosses fall into three types: World Bosses (field respawn), Weekly Bosses (limited weekly rewards), and TDs (normal elites). Each type drops different materials and Echoes.

## 世界Boss / World Bosses

世界Boss位于地图固定位置，击败后数分钟刷新。消耗60波片可领取角色突破专属材料。Boss还会掉落高品质回声。

World Bosses are at fixed map locations, respawning minutes after defeat. Cost 60 Waveplate to claim character ascension materials. They also drop high-quality Echoes.

**奏谕乐师 /**

位于今州城附近。机制围绕音波攻击，会释放多段音波弹幕和范围震荡波。推荐携带远程角色或利用闪避逼近输出。

Located near Jinzhou. Mechanics revolve around sonic attacks, releasing multi-stage sonic barrages and area shockwaves. Recommended to bring ranged characters or dodge in close.

掉落材料：角色突破素材（奏谕乐师之心）
推荐属性：导电、热熔攻击
Echo套装：彻空冥雷、熔山裂谷

Drops: Character ascension material (heart crystal)
Recommended elements: Electro, Fusion
Echo sets: Void Thunder, Molten Mountain

**哀声鸷 / Lamenting Crane**

位于哀声崖的大型鸟类Boss。攻击模式包括俯冲爪击、风刃横扫和召唤小型幻象。注意它的俯冲攻击，触发弹刀可使其坠地瘫痪。

A large avian boss at Lamenting Cliff. Attack patterns include dive claws, wind blade sweeps, and summoning small TDs. Watch for its dive attack, parrying it causes a crashlanding瘫痪.

掉落材料：角色突破素材（哀声鸷之羽）
推荐属性：湮灭、冷凝攻击
Echo套装：沉日劫明、冻结极寒

Drops: Character ascension material (Lamenting Crane feather)
Recommended elements: Havoc, Glacio
Echo sets: Sun-sinking Eclipse, Freezing Frost

**无冠者 / Crownless**

人形Boss，招式华丽，攻击频率高。拥有二阶段变身，变身后攻击范围扩大、速度提升。需要熟练掌握弹刀和闪避节奏。

A humanoid boss with flashy moves and high attack frequency. Has a second phase transformation, expanding attack range and increasing speed. Requires mastery of parry and dodge rhythm.

掉落材料：角色突破素材（无冠者之核）
推荐属性：衍射、气动攻击
Echo套装：浮星祛暗、啸谷长风

Drops: Character ascension material (Crownless core)
Recommended elements: Spectro, Aero
Echo sets: Celestial Light, Soothing Breeze

**辉萤军势 / Glacio Army**

大型机械单位Boss，拥有多部位可破坏。攻击模式包括导弹齐射、激光扫射和近身碾压。破坏部位可使其短暂瘫痪。

A large mechanical boss with multiple destructible parts. Attack patterns include missile barrage, laser sweep, and close-range crush. Destroying parts causes temporary瘫痪.

掉落材料：角色突破素材（辉萤军势核心）
推荐属性：热熔攻击
Echo套装：熔山裂谷

Drops: Character ascension material (core)
Recommended elements: Fusion
Echo sets: Molten Mountain

**云闪之鳞 / Lightning Scale**

雷电属性巨龙，飞行类Boss。常在归墟港市上空出现。需要利用钩索拉近距离进行空中战斗。会释放落雷和雷电吐息。

An electro-element dragon, a flying boss often seen above Guixu Port. Requires Grapple Hook to close distance for aerial combat. Uses thunder strikes and lightning breath.

掉落材料：角色突破素材（云闪之鳞片）
推荐属性：冷凝攻击
Echo套装：冻结极寒

Drops: Character ascension material (Scale)
Recommended elements: Glacio
Echo sets: Freezing Frost

**怒火巨像 / Rage Colossus**

巨型石像Boss，双臂可破坏。弱点是头部，推荐使用远程攻击和高机动角色周旋。注意它的全屏震地攻击和投石技能。

A giant stone statue boss with destructible arms. Weak point is the head, recommend ranged attacks and high-mobility characters. Watch for its full-screen ground pound and rock throw.

## 周本Boss / Weekly Bosses

周本Boss每周有三次奖励次数，每次消耗60波片。掉落高阶技能升级材料和武器锻造图纸。

Weekly Bosses have three reward runs per week, costing 60 Waveplate each. Drops advanced skill upgrade materials and weapon forging blueprints.

**无妄者 / The Unnamed**

位于孤云塔。人形Boss，拥有三个阶段。每阶段增加新的攻击模式和更快的攻击速度。第三阶段会释放全屏攻击，需要连续弹刀或极限闪避应对。

Located at Solitary Cloud Tower. Humanoid boss with three phases. Each phase adds new attack patterns and faster speed. Phase 3 features a full-screen attack requiring consecutive parries or Extreme Dodges.

掉落：无妄者之心、无妄者之骨（技能升级材料）
核心奖励：武器锻造图纸、高阶技能材料

Drops: Heart of the Unnamed, Bone of the Unnamed (skill materials)
Core reward: Weapon forging blueprints, advanced skill materials

**角斗士 / Gladiator**

在特定竞技场区域出现的周本Boss。持盾战士型，需击破盾牌才能造成有效伤害。盾牌破碎后Boss进入虚弱状态。

A shield-bearing warrior boss appearing in specific arena zones. Must break the shield to deal effective damage. Boss enters weakened state after shield break.

掉落：角斗士之证、角斗士之魂
核心奖励：技能升级进阶材料、角色突破通用材料

## 幻象精英 / Elite TDs

地图上分布着大量精英幻象，它们虽然不是Boss，但强度高于普通怪物，掉落特定品质的回声。

Numerous elite TDs are scattered across the map. While not bosses, they are stronger than normal mobs and drop specific quality Echoes.

常见精英包括：流放者精英、碎裂者精英、暗灵精英等。它们通常有固定刷新位置，是刷取特定回声套装的最佳选择。

Common elites include Exile elites, Fracture elites, and Umbral elites. They typically have fixed spawn points and are the best choice for farming specific Echo sets.

## Boss战通用技巧 / General Boss Tips

**观察起手动作 / Watch Startup Animations**：每个Boss的出招有固定起手动作，掌握后可以提前预判。Every boss has fixed startup animations for attacks; learn them to predict.

**弹刀优先 / Prioritize Parries**：可弹刀的攻击成功后收益巨大，优先练习弹刀。Parryable attacks yield huge rewards when executed successfully.

**利用钩索 / Use Grapple Hook**：钩索可以快速拉近与Boss的距离，也可以用来躲避部分地面攻击。Grapple Hook closes distance quickly and can dodge some ground attacks.

**延奏循环 / Outro Rotation**：在Boss瘫痪或虚弱期间，提前安排好延奏技能增伤，用主C大招爆发。Set up Outro Skill buffs before the boss is瘫痪 or weakened for main DPS burst.

**注意Boss变阶段 / Watch Phase Transitions**：Boss在变阶段时通常有无敌动画，不要浪费大招。Bosses are often invulnerable during phase transition animations, don't waste ultimates.

**对应属性攻击 / Use Correct Elements**：查看Boss的元素抗性，选择克制属性造成额外伤害。Check boss elemental resistance and bring counter elements for bonus damage.

## Boss刷新时间 / Boss Respawn Timer

世界Boss击败后约3-5分钟刷新，可反复挑战但不消耗波片无法领取奖励。周本Boss无次数限制但每周仅3次奖励机会。

World bosses respawn 3-5 minutes after defeat. Repeatable without Waveplate but no rewards without consumption. Weekly bosses have no attempt limit but only 3 weekly reward claims.

## 多人联机Boss战 / Co-op Boss Fights

达到Union等级15后，可以与其他玩家联机组队挑战Boss。联机时Boss生命值会根据队伍人数按比例增加。推荐组队时至少有一名治疗角色。

After Union Level 15, team up with other players. Boss HP scales with party size. Recommended to bring at least one healer in co-op.
