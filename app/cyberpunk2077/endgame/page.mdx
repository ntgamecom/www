## 终局概述 / Endgame Overview

**中文：** 赛博朋克2077的终局内容围绕结局选择和全收集展开。在完成主线"夜曲"后，游戏会根据你的选择进入不同结局。2.0版本取消了主线后的自由探索限制——完成结局后游戏会自动回退到"夜曲"之前的存档点，允许你继续探索和完成其他内容。

**English:** Endgame revolves around ending choices and full completion. After completing "Nocturne OP55N1", your choices determine the ending. Patch 2.0 removed the post-main-story lockout — after any ending, the game reverts to the save before the point of no return, letting you continue exploration.

**终局前准备清单 / Endgame Checklist**:
- 等级达到50级 / Reach level 50
- 完成所有主要角色支线（帕南、朱迪、克里、罗格）/ Complete all character sidequests
- 收集至少一套完整传说装备 / Collect at least one full legendary gear set
- 决定最终结局路线 / Decide your ending path
- 准备好40+治疗物品和10+手雷 / Stock 40+ healing items and 10+ grenades

## 结局路线详解 / Ending Paths Detailed

**中文：** 每个结局都有独特的流程、战斗和结局动画。以下是各结局的详细攻略。

**English:** Each ending has a unique sequence, combat encounters, and ending animation. Here are detailed guides for all endings.

**太阳结局 / The Sun Ending (罗格线)**:

1. 在"夜曲"中选择联系罗格 / Choose to call Rogue at Embers
2. 和罗格一起攻入荒坂大厦 / Raid Arasaka tower with Rogue
3. 面对重锤——罗格可能牺牲 / Face Smasher — Rogue may die
4. V接受最后一个任务——袭击水晶宫 / V takes one last gig — raid the Crystal Palace

**战斗难度 / Combat Difficulty**: ★★★★（极高难度，需要满级装备）/ Very high, requires max gear
**推荐Build / Recommended Build**: 时停武士刀或技术散弹流 / Sandy Katana or Tech Shotgun

**星星结局 / The Star Ending (帕南线)**:

1. 在"夜曲"中选择联系帕南 / Choose to call Panam at Embers
2. 和阿德卡多流浪者一起突袭荒坂塔 / Raid Arasaka tower with the Aldecaldos
3. 大型团队战斗，有多段载具追逐 / Large-scale team fight with vehicle chase segments
4. V和流浪者一起离开夜之城 / V leaves Night City with the clan

**战斗难度 / Combat Difficulty**: ★★★（团队合作降低了难度）/ Team support lowers difficulty
**推荐Build / Recommended Build**: 任意Build，团队配合优先 / Any build, prioritize team synergy

**恶魔结局 / The Devil Ending (荒坂线)**:

1. 在"夜曲"中选择联系华子 / Choose to meet Hanako at Embers
2. 和荒坂合作攻击荒坂塔内的叛军 / Work with Arasaka against internal rebels
3. V被送上太空站进行实验治疗 / V is sent to a space station for experimental treatment
4. 最终决定签署合同或返回地球 / Sign the contract or return to Earth

**战斗难度 / Combat Difficulty**: ★★（最少战斗） / Least combat
**关键选择**: 在太空站上选择是否签署合同 / On the space station, choose to sign or refuse

**节制结局 / Temperance Ending (强尼线)**:

1. 在"夜曲"中选择让强尼控制身体 / Let Johnny take control
2. 可选：和罗格/帕南一起攻击荒坂塔 / Optional: raid Arasaka with Rogue or Panam
3. 最后V将身体交给强尼 / V gives the body to Johnny
4. 强尼离开夜之城开始新生活 / Johnny leaves Night City

**特殊**: 这是唯一一个让你以强尼视角体验结局的路线 / Only ending experienced from Johnny's perspective

## DLC终局 / Phantom Liberty Endgame

**中文：** 往日之影DLC的终局围绕百灵鸟和里德之间的抉择展开。最终任务"比远方更远"中，你需要在月球设施做出最终决定。DLC结局会影响主线"夜曲"中的可选选项。

**English:** The Phantom Liberty endgame centers on the choice between Songbird and Reed. In the final mission "Somewhat Damaged", you decide their fate at the lunar facility. The DLC ending affects options available during the main game's point of no return.

**DLC结局选项 / DLC Ending Choices**:

| 选择 / Choice | 结果 / Result | 奖励 / Reward |
|---|---|---|
| 帮百灵鸟逃跑 / Help Songbird escape | 百灵鸟获得自由，V获得量子调谐器 / Songbird freed, V gets Quantum Tuner | 传说操作系统"量子调谐器" / Legendary OS |
| 通知里德 / Betray to Reed | 百灵鸟被FIA控制，V获得新身份保护 / Songbird captured, V gets identity protection | 传说武器"埃雷布斯" / Legendary weapon "Erebus" |

## 终局强力Build / Endgame Builds

**中文：** 终局内容需要高强度的Build来应对最高难度挑战。以下两个Build经过验证可以在非常困难难度下轻松通关。

**English:** Endgame content demands high-power builds for Very Hard difficulty. These two proven builds handle the toughest content with ease.

**过载黑客流 / Overclock Netrunner**:
- 属性：智力20 + 镇定20 + 技术能力18 / 20 Int + 20 Cool + 18 Tech
- 义体：网络行者接入仓、生物传导器、记忆植入 / Netwatcher deck, Biosynergy, Memory Implant
- 武器：应龙（不朽智能冲锋枪）、四相涟漪（不朽智能步枪）/ Yinglong (SMG), Four-Phase Ripple (rifle)
- 玩法：过度clock起手，传染扩散，短路收割 / Overclock, Contagion spread, Short Circuit cleanup

**时停散弹流 / Sandy Shotgun**:
- 属性：反应20 + 肉体20 + 技术能力16 / 20 Ref + 20 Body + 16 Tech
- 义体：阿波克拉芙·西尼斯福斯安威斯坦、生物监测器、弹射系统 / Apogee Sandy, Biomonitor, Projectile Launch
- 武器：主权（不朽双管散弹）、问题解决者（技术散弹）/ Sovereign (shotgun), The Problem Solver (tech shotgun)
- 玩法：开时停 — 绕后 — 一枪一个 / Activate Sandy, flank, one-shot each enemy
