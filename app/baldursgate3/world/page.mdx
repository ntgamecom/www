### 诅咒之地生存指南 / Shadow Lands Survival

诅咒之地全程需要光源——没有光源每回合受到暗蚀伤害。**光源方案**: 月光灯(第一章从蜘蛛女或德鲁伊处获得)、火把(人手一把)、光耀术(牧师/圣武士法术)。旅店地下室有一个永续月光灯——必拿。第二章推荐顺序: 最后之光旅店(安全点)→诅咒之地探索→月光之塔(侦察)→莎尔试炼场(影心任务)→月光之塔(突袭) / Full zone requires light — take shadow damage each turn without. **Light sources**: Moon Lantern (Act 1 from spider lady or druids), Torches, Light cantrip. Basement of Last Light Inn has a permanent Moon Lantern — must-pick. Recommended order: Last Light Inn → explore Shadow Lands → scout Moonrise → Gauntlet of Shar → assault Moonrise.

## 探索技巧 / Exploration Tips

1. **跳跳乐 / Jump Everywhere**: BG3的跳跃距离很长——很多隐藏区域需要跳悬崖过去 / BG3 has long jumps — many hidden areas require jumping
2. **拆解陷阱 / Disarm Traps**: 游荡者/游侠应该全程开察觉模式——宝箱周围有陷阱 / Rogue/Ranger should keep perception active — traps around chests
3. **敲击术 / Knock**: 2环法术——可以开锁——省了游荡者的活 / 2nd level spell — opens locks — saves needing a rogue
4. **对话前存档 / Save Before Conversations**: 重要对话前存档——失败掷骰可能导致永久错过内容 / Save before important convos — failed rolls can lock content
5. **动物交谈 / Speak with Animals**: 1环法术/药水——和动物对话可以获取隐藏信息和任务 / Talk to animals for hidden info and quests
6. **死者交谈 / Speak with Dead**: 3环法术/护符——和尸体对话获取线索 / Talk to corpses for clues
7. **羽落术不占准备位 / Feather Fall**: 1环仪式法术——不消耗法术位——跳崖/坠落场景全员上一个 / Ritual spell, no slot cost — cast on all before any big jump
8. **迷踪步卷轴储备 / Stock Misty Step Scrolls**: 全游戏最有用的卷轴——战斗/探索双用途——见到就买 / Most useful scroll in the game — combat + exploration — buy whenever available
