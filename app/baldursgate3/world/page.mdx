## 探索技巧 / Exploration Tips

1. **跳跳乐 / Jump Everywhere**: BG3的跳跃距离很长——很多隐藏区域需要跳悬崖过去 / BG3 has long jumps — many hidden areas require jumping
2. **拆解陷阱 / Disarm Traps**: 游荡者/游侠应该全程开察觉模式——宝箱周围有陷阱 / Rogue/Ranger should keep perception active — traps around chests
3. **敲击术 / Knock**: 2环法术——可以开锁——省了游荡者的活 / 2nd level spell — opens locks — saves needing a rogue
4. **对话前存档 / Save Before Conversations**: 重要对话前存档——失败掷骰可能导致永久错过内容 / Save before important convos — failed rolls can lock content
5. **动物交谈 / Speak with Animals**: 1环法术/药水——和动物对话可以获取隐藏信息和任务 / Talk to animals for hidden info and quests
6. **死者交谈 / Speak with Dead**: 3环法术/护符——和尸体对话获取线索 / Talk to corpses for clues
