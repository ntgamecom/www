## 卷轴机制详解 / Scroll Mechanics

| 类型 / Type | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|
| **威力卷轴 / Power Scroll** | 三选一属性——全加主属性。双色卷轴(如暴虐+战术)可以选主属性 / Choose one of 3 stats — always pick your main. Dual scrolls let you stay on-color |
| **碎片卷轴 / Scroll Fragment** | 收集特定数量合成一个完整卷轴——全图探索确保不漏 / Collect X fragments for a full scroll — full clear to ensure no misses |
| **诅咒卷轴 / Cursed Scroll** | 给2级主属性但附加诅咒——10连杀后解除。高风险高回报 / +2 main stat but applies curse — lift after 10 kills. High risk, high reward |

> **卷轴分配**：全加一种属性！每3点同属伤害翻倍——全紫/全红/全绿是唯一正确选择 / **Scroll Rule**: ALL into one color! Every 3 points in the same color doubles your damage — commit fully
