## 首领战概览 / Boss Overview

《崩坏：星穹铁道》中拥有众多首领敌人，包括主线剧情Boss、周常Boss和模拟宇宙精英。了解每个Boss的机制和弱点是取胜的关键。

Honkai: Star Rail features numerous boss enemies including story bosses, weekly bosses, and Simulated Universe elites. Understanding each boss's mechanics and weaknesses is key to victory.

## 主线剧情Boss / Story Bosses

### 末日兽 / Doomsday Beast

早期剧情Boss，出现在黑塔空间站反物质军团入侵事件中。拥有物理、雷和虚数弱点。

Early story boss during the Antimatter Legion invasion of Herta Station. Weak to Physical, Lightning, and Imaginary.

**策略**：优先击破腿部弱点使其瘫痪。注意它的全屏AOE技能需要及时回复。

Strategy: Prioritize breaking leg weaknesses to stagger. Watch for its full-screen AoE and heal accordingly.

### 可可利亚 / Cocolia

雅利洛-VI的最终Boss，前任守护者。战斗分为两个阶段，以第二阶段更加困难。弱点火、雷和风。

Final boss of Jarilo-VI, the former Guardian. Two-phase fight with Phase 2 being harder. Weak to Fire, Lightning, and Wind.

**策略**：第一阶段快速削韧打断技能；第二阶段注意冰封技能，提前布置护盾和净化。推荐使用火主和娜塔莎。

Strategy: Break quickly in Phase 1 to interrupt skills. In Phase 2, watch for freeze技能, bring shields and cleanse. Recommended: Fire MC and Natasha.

### 幻胧 / Phantylia

仙舟「罗浮」的最终Boss，绝灭大君。拥有极高的伤害输出和召唤能力。弱点属性：火、雷和虚数。

Final boss of Xianzhou Luofu, an Emanator of Destruction. Extremely high damage output with summoning abilities. Weak to Fire, Lightning, and Imaginary.

**策略**：优先清理幻胧召唤的「魔阴身」小怪，避免它们叠加Buff。注意幻胧的蓄力技能需要击破打断。

Strategy: Clear summoned "Mara-Struck" adds first to prevent buff stacking. Interrupt Phantylia's charging skill via weakness break.

### 神主日 / Sunday

匹诺康尼的最终Boss，拥有独特的「哲学」机制。弱点火、雷和虚数。

Final boss of Penacony with a unique philosophy mechanic. Weak to Fire, Lightning, and Imaginary.

**策略**：应对神主日的特殊机制需要携带特定角色或使用特定策略。注意转阶段时的全屏攻击。

Strategy: The special mechanic requires specific characters or approaches. Watch for the phase transition AoE attack.

### 泰坦们 / Titans

翁法罗斯版本中的泰坦级Boss，代表不同概念的巨大存在。每个泰坦有独特的属性和机制。

Titan-level bosses in Amphoreus, representing different concepts. Each Titan has unique attributes and mechanics.

## 周常Boss / Weekly Bosses (Echo of War)

周常Boss每周可挑战三次，掉落角色行迹升级所需的材料。每次消耗30开拓力。

Weekly bosses can be challenged 3 times per week for Trace upgrade materials. Cost: 30 Trailblaze Power per run.

### 历战余响 — 毁灭之足 / Destruction's Foot

对应末日兽的周常版本。掉落毁灭命途的行迹材料。

Weekly version of Doomsday Beast. Drops Destruction Trace materials.

### 历战余响 — 寒潮的深冬 / Frozen Feast

对应可可利亚的周常版本。掉落丰饶、存护和巡猎的行迹材料。

Weekly version of Cocolia. Drops Abundance, Preservation, and Hunt Trace materials.

### 历战余响 — 不死的神实 / Divine Seed

对应幻胧的周常版本。掉落同谐、毁灭和虚无的行迹材料。

Weekly version of Phantylia. Drops Harmony, Destruction, and Nihility Trace materials.

### 历战余响 — 协乐回响 / Harmony's Echo

对应神主日的周常版本。掉落同谐、智识和虚无的行迹材料。

Weekly version of Sunday. Drops Harmony, Erudition, and Nihility Trace materials.

周常Boss的策略与主线版本相似但略有调整，难度更高。建议组满四人队伍应对。

Weekly boss strategies are similar to story versions with adjustments and higher difficulty. Bring full teams.

## 模拟宇宙首领 / Simulated Universe Bosses

### 守门者 / Guardian

模拟宇宙的常见精英怪，拥有多种形态。弱点和技能机制随每次挑战不同。

Common elite in Simulated Universe with multiple forms. Weaknesses and skills vary per run.

### 卡芙卡 / Kafka (模拟宇宙版)

模拟宇宙中的精英版卡芙卡，擅长精神控制和持续伤害。弱点属性：物理和雷。

Elite version of Kafka specializing in mind control and DoT. Weak to Physical and Lightning.

**策略**：携带净化角色解除控制状态。优先击杀被她控制的我方角色（无法直接解除时）。

Strategy: Bring cleanse to remove mind control. Prioritize defeating charmed allies if cleanse is unavailable.

### 史瓦罗 / Svarog

机械Boss，擅长召唤机械手臂控制角色。弱点属性：火、雷和风。

Mechanical boss specializing in summoning robotic arms that control allies. Weak to Fire, Lightning, and Wind.

**策略**：优先击破机械手臂解救被控角色。史瓦罗的本体相对脆弱。

Strategy: Destroy robotic arms to free controlled allies. Svarog's core is vulnerable.

### 银枝 / Argenti (模拟宇宙版)

模拟宇宙中的精英银枝，拥有高额物理AOE伤害和护盾。

Elite Argenti with high physical AoE damage and shields.

**策略**：在银枝蓄力时使用高削韧技能打断，否则将承受巨额伤害。

Strategy: Interrupt Argenti's charge with high Toughness damage to avoid massive damage.

## 精英敌人 / Elite Enemies

以下是在各世界中遇到的精英敌人类型：

Elite enemy types encountered across worlds:

**外宇宙之冰 / Ice Out of Space**：冰属性精英，擅长冻结和减速。

Ice elite, excels at freezing and slowing.

**外宇宙之炎 / Fire Out of Space**：火属性精英，擅长灼烧和AOE。

Fire elite, excels at burning and AoE.

**蚕食者之影 / Shadow of the Devourer**：量子属性精英，攻击造成纠缠效果。

Quantum elite, attacks cause Entanglement.

**金人勾魂使 / Aurumaton Gatekeeper**：虚数属性仙舟精英，擅长禁锢。

Imaginary elite from Xianzhou, excels at Imprisonment.

**惊梦剧团的成员 / Dream Troupe Members**：匹诺康尼的各种舞台主题精英。

Penacony's various stage-themed elites.

## Boss挑战通用建议 / General Boss Tips

1. 出战前检查Boss弱點，调整队伍属性覆盖
2. 生存位角色必不可少，优先保证队伍存活
3. 学习Boss的技能循环，在关键节点使用终结技打断
4. 保持战技点充裕，不要过度消耗
5. 利用秘技系统在战前获得优势

1. Check boss weaknesses and adjust team elements
2. Always bring a sustain character
3. Learn boss skill rotations, interrupt key moves with Ultimates
4. Maintain adequate SP, do not over-consume
5. Use Techniques for pre-combat advantage

Boss挑战不仅考验角色练度，也考验玩家对机制的理解。观察和适应比单纯堆数值更重要。

Boss fights test both character investment and mechanical understanding. Observation and adaptation matter more than raw stats.
