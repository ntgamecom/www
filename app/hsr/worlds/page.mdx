## 星穹铁道世界导览 / Worlds Overview

《崩坏：星穹铁道》的旅程横跨多个风格迥异的星球和空间站。每个世界都有独特的文化、生态和危机，等待开拓者去探索和拯救。

The journey of Honkai: Star Rail spans multiple unique planets and space stations. Each world has its own culture, ecology, and crisis awaiting the Trailblazer.

## 黑塔空间站 / Herta Space Station

空间站由天才俱乐部成员#83黑塔建造，是游戏的起始区域。空间站分为三个主要区域：

Built by Genius Society member #83 Herta, this is the starting area. The station has three main zones:

**主控舱段 / Master Control Zone**：空间站的核心区域，包含控制中心、月台和黑塔的办公室。主线剧情在此展开，玩家在此结识黑塔、艾丝妲和阿兰。

The core area with the control center, platform, and Herta's office. Main story unfolds here. Meet Herta, Asta, and Arlan.

**基座舱段 / Base Zone**：空间站的生活和实验区域。包含模拟训练室、收容区和各种实验室。大量支线任务发生于此。

Living and research area with simulation rooms, containment zones, and labs. Many side quests take place here.

**收容舱段 / Storage Zone**：存储珍贵文物和危险物品的区域。反物质军团的入侵从这里开始。

Storage area for valuable artifacts and dangerous items. The Antimatter Legion invasion begins here.

空间站探索可获得丰富的资源，包括模拟宇宙的解锁和黑塔系列任务。奇物和虫洞等随机事件丰富了探索体验。

Exploration rewards include Simulated Universe unlocks and Herta-related quests. Curios and wormhole events enrich the experience.

## 雅利洛-VI / Jarilo-VI

被星核引发的永冬覆盖的星球，文明退缩至最后城市——贝洛伯格。城市分为上下两层：

A planet trapped in eternal winter by a Stellaron. Civilization has retreated to the last city, Belobog, divided into two layers:

**上层区 / Overworld**：贝洛伯格的地表区域，保留着文明的光芒。包含行政区、商业区、歌德宾馆和克里珀堡。守护者可可利亚在此统治。

The surface area retaining civilization's light. Contains the Administrative District, Goethe Hotel, and Qlipoth Fort. Guardian Cocolia rules here.

**下层区 / Underworld**：深入地表以下的矿区城镇，包括磐岩镇和边缘通路。地火组织在此抵抗裂界的侵蚀。娜塔莎和希露瓦在此活动。

The mining towns deep underground, including Boulder Town and the Corridor of Fading Echoes. Wildfire resists the Fragmentum here. Natasha and Serval operate here.

**重要地点 / Key Locations**：

- 克里珀堡：守护者的居所，也是最终决战的地点
- 永冬岭：可可利亚的Boss战舞台
- 大矿区：下层区的主要资源产地
- 机械聚落：螺丝咕姆相关的隐藏区域

- Qlipoth Fort: Guardian's residence, final battle location
- Everwinter Hill: Cocolia boss arena
- Great Mine: Primary resource production for the Underworld
- Robot Settlement: Screwllum-related hidden area

## 仙舟「罗浮」/ Xianzhou Luofu

仙舟联盟的六大旗舰之一，融合了仙侠与科幻的设计风格。这艘巨大的星舰以神话中的神树「建木」为核心动力来源。

One of six flagships of the Xianzhou Alliance, blending martial arts fantasy with sci-fi. The ship is powered by the mythical tree, the Ambrosial Arbor.

**主要区域 / Main Areas**：

- 星槎海：仙舟的入口港口区域
- 流云渡：商业和交通枢纽
- 长乐天：娱乐休憩区域
- 工造司：仙舟的兵工厂
- 丹鼎司：炼丹和医药机构
- 鳞渊境：饮月君之乱的发生地
- 绥园：捉鬼小队活动的区域

- Stargazer Navalia: Entry port
- Cloudford: Trade and transit hub
- Exalting Sanctum: Entertainment district
- Artisanship Commission: Arsenal and crafting
- Alchemy Commission: Medicine and alchemy
- Scalegorge Waterscape: Imbibitor Lunae's fall
- Fyxestroll Garden: Ghost-hunting squad area

**故事核心 / Story Core**：围绕药王秘传（丰饶民组织）、饮月之乱和幻胧（绝灭大君）的阴谋展开。丹恒的身世在此被揭开。

Revolves around the Disciples of Sanctus Medicus, the Sedition of Imbibitor Lunae, and Phantylia the Emanator of Destruction. Dan Heng's past is revealed here.

## 匹诺康尼 / Penacony

梦想之地，原为星际监狱，后被「家族」改造成梦幻般的度假胜地。这里的设定融合了梦境与现实的交错。

The Planet of Festivities, originally an interstellar prison transformed into a dream resort by the Family. Reality and dreams intertwine here.

**主要区域 / Main Areas**：

- 黄金的时刻：匹诺康尼的中心区域，永恒的白昼
- 稚子的梦：梦境深处，充满童趣与谜题
- 朝露公馆：家族的所在地
- 克劳克影视乐园：以钟表小子为主题的游乐园
- 匹诺康尼大剧院：最终章的核心场景

- Golden Hour: Central district, eternal daylight
- Child's Dream: Deep dream layers with puzzles
- Dewlight Pavilion: Family headquarters
- Clock Studios Theme Park: Clockie-themed park
- Penacony Grand Theater: Final chapter setting

匹诺康尼的剧情涉及多方势力的博弈：家族、星穹列车组、星核猎手、流光忆者和神秘的星期日。版本更新量大，内容丰富。

Penacony's story involves multiple factions: the Family, Astral Express crew, Stellararon Hunters, Garden of Recollection, and the mysterious Sunday. Content-rich update cycle.

## 翁法罗斯 / Amphoreus

最新开放的世界，灵感源自希腊罗马神话。永恒之地翁法罗斯是多个版本的主舞台，拥有巨大的地图和高自由度探索。

The newest world inspired by Greco-Roman mythology. Amphoreus, the Eternal Land, spans multiple versions with large maps and high exploration freedom.

## 探索机制 / Exploration Mechanics

**次元扑满 / Trotters**：地图上会随机出现次元扑满，击败后获得星琼奖励。

Randomly appearing creatures. Defeating them rewards Stellar Jade.

**可破坏物 / Destructibles**：地图中的可破坏物品掉落材料、信用点和偶尔的隐藏宝箱。

Breakable objects in maps drop materials, credits, and occasionally hidden chests.

**解密谜题 / Puzzles**：每个世界有独特的解密玩法，如空间站的三消、贝洛伯格的立方谜题、仙舟的魔方和匹诺康尼的梦境迷宫。

Each world has unique puzzles: station match-3, Belobog block puzzles, Luofu hexanexus, Penacony dream mazes.

**宝箱分布 / Chest Distribution**：每个地图区域有固定数量的普通宝箱、贵重宝箱和奢华宝箱。收集后可获得星琼和养成材料。

Each area has a fixed number of Basic, Bountiful, and Precious chests. Collect for Stellar Jade and materials.

## 世界任务 / World Quests

每个世界包含丰富的任务体系，包括开拓秘闻（主线）、冒险任务（支线）和日常任务。完成支线任务可以获得星琼、角色经验和隐藏成就。

Each world has a comprehensive quest system including Trailblaze Missions (main story), Adventure Missions (side quests), and daily tasks. Side quests reward Stellar Jade, character EXP, and hidden achievements.

**隐藏任务 / Hidden Quests**：部分任务需要特定条件触发，如与特定NPC多次对话、在特定时间前往特定地点等。

Some quests require specific conditions to trigger: multiple NPC conversations, visiting locations at certain times, or using specific characters.

**世界商店 / World Shops**：每个世界有独特的商店，出售该世界特产物品。如仙舟的工造司出售锻造材料，贝洛伯格的商店出售当地消耗品。

Each world has unique shops selling region-specific items. The Artisanship Commission sells forging materials. Belobog shops sell local consumables.

## 世界等级 / World Level

随着开拓等级提升，世界等级也会提升，影响敌人强度、掉落物数量和品质。均衡等级可以手动调整来降低难度。

World Level increases with Trailblaze Level, affecting enemy strength and drop quality. Equilibrium Level can be manually adjusted to reduce difficulty.
