## 欢迎来到星穹铁道 / Welcome to the Astral Express

《崩坏：星穹铁道》是米哈游开发的回合制角色扮演游戏，于2023年4月26日全球发布。玩家将扮演「开拓者」，乘坐星穹列车穿越星海，解决各世界的星核危机。游戏融合了科幻与奇幻元素，拥有丰富的剧情、策略性战斗和深度的角色养成系统。

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based RPG developed by HoYoverse, released globally on April 26, 2023. Players take on the role of the Trailblazer, traveling aboard the Astral Express across the stars to resolve Stellaron crises on various worlds. The game blends sci-fi and fantasy elements with rich storytelling, strategic combat, and deep character progression.

## 故事背景 / Story Overview

故事始于主角在空间站「黑塔」上醒来，发现自己体内被植入了一颗星核。在三月七和丹恒的帮助下，主角加入星穹列车，与列车组一同踏上旅程。列车从空间站出发，先后抵达雅利洛-VI（贝洛伯格）、仙舟「罗浮」、匹诺康尼等世界。每个世界都有独特的文明与危机，而星核正是这些危机的根源。随着剧情推进，玩家逐渐揭开星神、「毁灭」纳努克以及列车组各成员的过去。

The story begins with the protagonist waking up aboard the Herta Space Station, finding a Stellaron implanted within them. With help from March 7th and Dan Heng, they join the Astral Express and embark on a journey. The train visits Jarilo-VI (Belobog), the Xianzhou Luofu, Penacony, and beyond. Each world has its own civilization and crisis, all stemming from Stellarons. As the story progresses, players uncover the truth about the Aeons, Nanook the Destruction, and the past of each crew member.

## 核心玩法 / Core Gameplay

### 回合制战斗 / Turn-Based Combat

战斗采用经典的回合制模式，队伍由四名角色组成。每个角色拥有普通攻击、战技、终结技和天赋。战斗中最重要的机制是「弱点击破」——使用对应属性攻击敌人的弱点可以削减其韧性值，击破后会触发额外效果并延迟敌人行动。

Combat follows a classic turn-based system with four characters per team. Each character has a Basic ATK, Skill, Ultimate, and Talent. The key mechanic is Weakness Break — attacking an enemy's weakness with matching elements reduces their Toughness. Breaking triggers bonus effects and delays enemy actions.

### 角色养成 / Character Progression

角色养成包括等级提升、行迹（技能树）、星魂（命座）、光锥和遗器。行迹系统提供主动技能和被动加成，星魂通过抽到重复角色获得。光锥相当于角色的武器，遗器则是圣遗物系统，分为洞穴遗器和位面饰品。

Character progression includes leveling, Traces (skill trees), Eidolons (constellations), Light Cones, and Relics. Traces provide active skills and passive bonuses. Eidolons are gained through duplicate pulls. Light Cones serve as weapons, while Relics are artifact sets divided into Cavern Relics and Planar Ornaments.

### 跃迁系统 / Warp System

角色和光锥通过跃迁（抽卡）系统获取。跃迁分为常驻跃迁、角色活动跃迁和光锥活动跃迁。每十次跃迁保底四星，每九十次保底五星。角色跃迁池有小保底和大保底机制，歪掉后下次必出当期UP角色。

Characters and Light Cones are obtained through the Warp (gacha) system. Warps include Stellar Warp, Character Event Warp, and Light Cone Event Warp. A 4-star is guaranteed every 10 warps, and a 5-star every 90 warps. Character event banners feature a 50/50 system, guaranteeing the featured character on the next 5-star if the first is lost.

## 世界介绍 / World Introductions

**黑塔空间站 / Herta Space Station** — 游戏起始地点，由天才俱乐部成员黑塔建造的研究设施。空间站分为主控舱段、基座舱段和收容舱段，是玩家了解基础操作的教学区域。

The starting location, a research facility built by Herta of the Genius Society. It has three main sections: Master Control Zone, Base Zone, and Storage Zone, serving as the tutorial area.

**雅利洛-VI / Jarilo-VI** — 被星核引发的永冬覆盖的星球。城市贝洛伯格分为上层区和下层区（磐岩镇）。这里的故事聚焦于地火组织与守护者可可利亚的对抗。

A planet trapped in eternal winter due to a Stellaron. Belobog is divided into the Overworld and the Underworld (Boulder Town). The story focuses on the Wildfire resistance and Guardian Cocolia.

**仙舟「罗浮」/ Xianzhou Luofu** — 仙舟联盟的六大旗舰之一，融合了中国科幻与仙侠风格。故事围绕「药王秘传」、饮月之乱和幻胧的阴谋展开。

One of six flagships of the Xianzhou Alliance, blending Chinese sci-fi with xianxia aesthetics. The story revolves around the Disciples of Sanctus Medicus, the Sedition of Imbibitor Lunae, and Phantylia's schemes.

**匹诺康尼 / Penacony** — 梦想之地，原为星际监狱后被家族改造成梦幻度假胜地。这里是《匹诺康尼》版本的主要舞台，充满梦境与现实交织的谜题。

The Planet of Festivities, originally an interstellar prison transformed into a dream resort by the Family. This is the main setting of the Penacony arc, filled with mysteries where dreams and reality intertwine.

## 角色导览 / Character Overview

游戏拥有众多可玩角色，分为七大命途（毁灭、巡猎、智识、同谐、虚无、存护、丰饶）和七种属性（物理、火、冰、雷、风、量子、虚数）。每个角色都有独特的定位和技能组。

The game features many playable characters across seven Paths (Destruction, Hunt, Erudition, Harmony, Nihility, Preservation, Abundance) and seven Elements (Physical, Fire, Ice, Lightning, Wind, Quantum, Imaginary). Each character has a unique role and skill set.

热门角色包括：镜流（毁灭·冰）、丹恒·饮月（毁灭·虚数）、布洛妮娅（同谐·风）、符玄（存护·量子）、罗刹（丰饶·虚数）以及流萤（毁灭·火）等。

Popular characters include: Jingliu (Destruction Ice), Dan Heng Imbibitor Lunae (Destruction Imaginary), Bronya (Harmony Wind), Fu Xuan (Preservation Quantum), Luocha (Abundance Imaginary), and Firefly (Destruction Fire).

## 战斗系统解析 / Combat System Analysis

战斗的核心策略围绕元素克制与弱点击破展开。每个敌人拥有最多三种弱点属性，使用对应的属性攻击可以累积削韧值。击破弱点后，敌人将承受击破伤害并行动延后，不同属性的击破效果各异：

- 物理：造成额外物理伤害并附加裂伤状态
- 火：造成持续火焰伤害
- 冰：冻结敌人并阻止行动
- 雷：造成触电伤害
- 风：造成风化伤害
- 量子：造成纠缠伤害并附加额外伤害
- 虚数：造成禁锢效果大幅延迟行动

Core combat strategy revolves around elemental coverage and Weakness Break. Each enemy has up to three weakness types. Breaking deals bonus damage and delays enemy action, with unique effects per element:

- Physical: Additional Physical DMG with Bleed
- Fire: Burns over time
- Ice: Freezes the enemy
- Lightning: Shock DoT
- Wind: Wind Shear DoT
- Quantum: Entanglement with bonus damage
- Imaginary: Imprisonment with major action delay

## 列车组 / Astral Express Crew

核心成员包括开拓者（你）、三月七、丹恒、姬子和瓦尔特·杨。他们在旅途中担任不同的角色，也是剧情的重要推动者。

The core crew includes the Trailblazer (you), March 7th, Dan Heng, Himeko, and Welt Yang. Each plays a distinct role in both gameplay and story progression.

此外，星核猎手（卡芙卡、银狼、刃、流萤）和黑塔等角色也在剧情中扮演重要角色，虽非列车常驻成员，但与列车组有密切关联。

Additionally, the Stellaron Hunters (Kafka, Silver Wolf, Blade, Firefly) and Herta play important roles in the story. Though not permanent crew members, they have close ties to the Express.

## 模拟宇宙 / Simulated Universe

模拟宇宙是游戏中最重要的常驻玩法之一，结合了Roguelike元素。玩家选择祝福和奇物来强化队伍，挑战随机生成的关卡。通过模拟宇宙可以获得大量星琼和位面饰品。

Simulated Universe is one of the most important permanent modes, combining roguelike elements. Players select Blessings and Curios to empower their team through procedurally generated stages. Rewards include Stellar Jade and Planar Ornaments.

## 系统与功能 / Systems and Features

**无名勋礼 / Nameless Honor**：类似大月卡的通行证系统，分为免费和付费两条线路。

Battle Pass system with free and paid tracks.

**好友系统 / Friends**：可以借用好友的支援角色协助战斗。

Borrow a friend's support character for battle assistance.

**命途 / Paths**：每个角色属于一个命途，决定战斗定位。

Each character belongs to one Path, determining their combat role.

**星魂 / Eidolons**：重复获得同一角色解锁星魂，提供额外能力加成。

Duplicate character pulls unlock Eidolons, granting extra abilities.

## 常见问题 / FAQ

**问：游戏是免费的吗？/ Is the game free to play?**
是的，游戏免费下载，内含可选消费项目。所有剧情内容均可免费体验。Yes, the game is free-to-download with optional purchases. All story content is free to experience.

**问：推荐如何开始？/ How should beginners start?**
跟随主线任务开拓秘闻，尽快提升开拓等级解锁更多功能。优先完成每日实训和模拟宇宙。Follow the main Trailblaze Missions to unlock features quickly. Prioritize Daily Training and Simulated Universe.

**问：如何配队？/ How to build teams?**
标准配队包含：主C + 辅助 + 生存位 + 副C/辅助。根据敌人弱点属性调整队伍组成。Standard team: Main DPS + Support + Sustain + Sub-DPS/Support. Adjust based on enemy weaknesses.

**问：星琼怎么用？/ How to use Stellar Jade?**
星琼用于兑换星轨通票和星轨专票。建议优先用在限时角色跃迁上。Stellar Jade is used to purchase Star Rail Passes and Star Rail Special Passes. Priority should go to limited character banners.

**问：什么是均衡等级？/ What is Equilibrium Level?**
均衡等级影响世界等级和敌人强度，提升后获得更好的掉落奖励。可以手动降低。Equilibrium Level affects world difficulty and drop quality. Can be manually reduced.

**问：新手优先刷什么？/ What should new players farm?**
优先提升角色等级和行迹等级，40开拓等级后再专注刷遗器。Prioritize character levels and Traces first. Focus on Relics after Trailblaze Level 40.

## 版本内容 / Version Content

游戏定期更新，每个版本约六周。更新内容包括新角色、新剧情、新活动和新的常驻玩法。重要版本活动通常提供大量星琼和养成材料奖励。

The game receives regular updates every six weeks. Each version brings new characters, story chapters, events, and gameplay modes. Major version events typically offer generous Stellar Jade and material rewards.
