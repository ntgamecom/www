## 欢迎开拓者 / Welcome Trailblazer

欢迎登上星穹列车。本指南将帮助你快速上手《崩坏：星穹铁道》，了解游戏的核心系统和每日流程。

Welcome aboard the Astral Express. This guide will help you get started quickly with Honkai: Star Rail, covering the core systems and daily routine.

## 初始选择 / Starting Choice

游戏开始时，你可以在两个角色中选择一位作为初期主力：开拓者本身以及通过主线获得的角色。开局赠送的角色包括丹恒、三月七、希露瓦和娜塔莎。新手五十连跃迁必定获得一个五星角色，建议优先抽到强力主C。

At the start, you can choose between two main characters. Free characters include Dan Heng, March 7th, Serval, and Natasha. The beginner 50-pull warp guarantees a 5-star character. Aim for a strong main DPS.

## 开拓等级系统 / Trailblaze Level System

开拓等级是游戏的进度核心，类似于其他游戏中的冒险等级。提升开拓等级可以解锁更多功能和玩法：

Trailblaze Level is the core progression system, similar to adventure ranks in other games. Leveling up unlocks more features:

- 开拓等级1-20：解锁主线剧情、跃迁系统、每日实训
- 开拓等级20-30：解锁忘却之庭、模拟宇宙更高难度
- 开拓等级30-40：解锁遗器本、行迹材料本的最高难度
- 开拓等级40-50：解锁历战余响（周本Boss）
- 开拓等级50-65：解锁全部最高难度副本和终局玩法

- TB 1-20: Main story, Warp system, Daily Training
- TB 20-30: Forgotten Hall, higher Simulated Universe
- TB 30-40: Max difficulty Relic and Trace domains
- TB 40-50: Echo of War (weekly bosses)
- TB 50-65: All max difficulty content and endgame modes

经验获取主要来自每日实训、开拓力消耗和主线任务。每消耗10点开拓力获得5点经验，每日实训提供大量经验。

EXP comes from Daily Training, Trailblaze Power usage, and main missions. Each 10 Power spent gives 5 EXP. Daily Training provides substantial EXP rewards.

## 开拓力系统 / Trailblaze Power System

开拓力（体力）系统限制你每天可以获取的资源量。初始上限为180点，每6分钟恢复1点。开拓力用于挑战各种副本：

The Trailblaze Power (stamina) system limits daily resource acquisition. The cap starts at 180, recovering 1 point every 6 minutes. Power is used for:

- 拟造花萼（金）：信用点和角色经验书
- 拟造花萼（赤）：行迹材料
- 侵蚀隧洞：遗器
- 历战余响：周本Boss材料
- 凝滞虚影：角色突破材料

- Calyx Golden: Credits and character EXP
- Calyx Crimson: Trace materials
- Cavern of Corrosion: Relics
- Echo of War: Weekly boss materials
- Stagnant Shadow: Character ascension materials

建议前期优先刷取行迹材料和角色经验书，后期再专注刷遗器。

Early on, prioritize Trace materials and EXP books. Focus on Relic farming in the late game.

## 跃迁系统详解 / Warp System Explained

### 跃迁类型 / Warp Types

**始发跃迁 / Departure Warp**：新手专属，50抽内必定获得一个五星角色，原价八折优惠。建议抽完此池再考虑其他。

Beginner-exclusive banner. Guarantees a 5-star within 50 pulls at 20% discount. Complete this banner first.

**星轨通票 / Star Rail Pass**：常驻跃迁，使用星轨通票抽取。可获得常驻五星角色和光锥。

Standard banner using Star Rail Passes. Awards standard 5-star characters and light cones.

**星轨专票 / Star Rail Special Pass**：限时角色跃迁和光锥跃迁。角色池90抽保底，光锥池80抽保底。

Limited character and Light Cone banners. Character banner: 90-pull guarantee. Light Cone banner: 80-pull guarantee.

### 保底机制 / Pity System

角色跃迁池有一个重要机制：小保底（第一次出五星时50%概率为当期UP角色）和大保底（如果第一次歪了，下次五星必为当期UP）。保底次数跨卡池继承。

The character banner features a 50/50 system. If you lose the 50/50, the next 5-star is guaranteed to be the featured character. Pity carries over between banners.

光锥跃迁池的保底机制不同：75%概率获得当期UP光锥，吃满保底后仍有概率歪掉，但概率较低。

The Light Cone banner has a 75% chance for the featured cone. The system is less forgiving than character banners.

### 星琼获取 / Stellar Jade Sources

星琼是跃迁的通用货币，可以通过以下方式获取：

Stellar Jade is the premium warp currency, obtainable from:

- 每日实训：每天60星琼
- 模拟宇宙：每周最多225星琼
- 忘却之庭/混沌回忆：每期最多800星琼
- 虚构叙事：每期最多800星琼
- 末日幻影：每期最多800星琼
- 版本活动：每版本约2000-3000星琼
- 主线/支线任务：一次性固定奖励
- 无名勋礼（大月卡）：付费版提供额外星琼

- Daily Training: 60 per day
- Simulated Universe: Up to 225 per week
- Forgotten Hall/MoC: Up to 800 per cycle
- Pure Fiction: Up to 800 per cycle
- Apocalyptic Shadow: Up to 800 per cycle
- Version events: 2000-3000 per version
- Main/side quests: One-time rewards
- Nameless Honor (BP): Paid track bonus

每月零氪玩家约可获得8000-10000星琼，相当于50-60抽。

F2P players can earn approximately 8000-10000 Stellar Jade per month, equal to 50-60 pulls.

## 每日必做 / Daily Routine

高效利用时间的每日流程：

An efficient daily routine:

1. 完成每日实训（约10分钟）— 获得60星琼和大量经验
2. 消耗开拓力（约10-15分钟）— 刷取当前需要的材料
3. 派遣委托（1分钟）— 获得角色经验书和信用点
4. 模拟宇宙每周积分（周常）— 获得星琼和位面饰品
5. 每周历战余响（3次）— 获得周本材料

1. Complete Daily Training (10 min) — 60 Stellar Jade
2. Spend Trailblaze Power (10-15 min) — farm needed materials
3. Assign assignments (1 min) — EXP books and Credits
4. Weekly SU points (weekly) — Stellar Jade and Planar Ornaments
5. Weekly Echo of War (3x) — weekly boss materials

## 基础战斗概念 / Basic Combat Concepts

- **速度与行动条**：速度决定角色行动顺序，行动条显示下一个行动的单位
- **战技点**：使用战技消耗战技点，普攻回复战技点，上限为5点
- **终结技**：充满能量后可以释放终结技，可在任意时机插入使用
- **秘技**：进入战斗前使用的特殊技能，提供战斗优势
- **弱点击破**：使用对应属性削减敌人韧性条，击破后造成额外效果

- **SPD and Action Order**: Speed determines turn order. The action bar shows next unit
- **Skill Points**: Skills consume SP, basic attacks restore SP. Maximum 5 points
- **Ultimate**: Usable when energy is full, can be used at any time
- **Technique**: Pre-combat skills that provide battle advantages
- **Weakness Break**: Matching attacks reduce Toughness, triggering bonus effects on break

## 初期资源分配 / Early Resource Allocation

建议优先培养一队四名角色（主C + 副C/辅助 + 奶妈 + 盾位），不要分散资源。主C优先拉满等级和光锥，辅助优先升级关键行迹。

Focus on building one team of four (Main DPS + Sub-DPS/Support + Healer + Shielder). Do not spread resources thin. Max your main DPS level and Light Cone first. Prioritize key Traces for supports.

推荐初期培养角色：娜塔莎（免费奶妈）、三月七（免费护盾）、主角（可切换命途）、以及你抽到的第一个五星角色。

Recommended early characters: Natasha (free healer), March 7th (free shielder), Trailblazer (free, switchable Paths), and your first 5-star.

## 无名勋礼 / Nameless Honor

无名勋礼相当于大月卡系统，分为免费和付费两条线路。付费线路提供额外的星轨专票、限定光锥和养成材料。对于长期玩家性价比较高。

Nameless Honor is the Battle Pass system with free and paid tracks. The paid track offers extra Special Passes, a limited Light Cone, and materials. Good value for regular players.

## 注意事项 / Important Notes

- 不要用星琼购买常驻通票
- 前期不要过度刷遗器（40级后再考虑）
- 每周记得打满3次历战余响
- 优先完成主线解锁功能
- 每日实训是星琼的主要稳定来源

- Do not spend Stellar Jade on standard passes
- Do not farm Relics heavily before TB 40
- Complete 3 weekly Echo of War runs
- Prioritize main story to unlock features
- Daily Training is your main stable Stellar Jade source

## 总结 / Summary

新手期的目标是快速提升开拓等级，解锁全部功能，并建立一支可靠的初始队伍。合理规划资源，不要冲动跃迁，耐心积累，很快你就能挑战更高难度的内容。

The goal of the beginner phase is to quickly raise Trailblaze Level, unlock all features, and build a reliable starting team. Plan resources wisely, do not pull impulsively, and accumulate patiently. You will soon be ready for harder content.
