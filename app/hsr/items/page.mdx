## 物品系统概述 / Item System Overview

《崩坏：星穹铁道》拥有丰富的物品系统，包括角色装备、养成材料和消耗品。合理管理这些资源是提升战斗力的关键。

Honkai: Star Rail features a comprehensive item system including character equipment, upgrade materials, and consumables. Proper resource management is crucial for power growth.

## 光锥 / Light Cones

光锥是角色装备的武器，提供基础属性加成和特殊效果。每个光锥属于特定命途，角色只能装备同命途光锥。

Light Cones are character weapons providing base stats and special effects. Each Light Cone belongs to a specific Path. Characters can only equip Light Cones of their own Path.

**光锥稀有度 / Light Cone Rarity**：

- 五星光锥：基础属性高，特效强大。通常通过限时光锥跃迁获得
- 四星光锥：性价比较高，部分特效优秀。可通过跃迁、活动和商店获取
- 三星光锥：前期过渡使用，后期基本无用

- 5-star: High base stats, powerful effects. Usually from limited banners
- 4-star: Good value, some have excellent effects. From warps, events, shops
- 3-star: Early game transitional. Mostly useless in late game

**光锥养成 / Light Cone Upgrade**：

光锥需要消耗经验材料和信用点来提升等级。突破需要特定素材。提升光锥等级可以增加基础攻击、防御和生命值。

Light Cones require EXP materials and Credits for leveling. Ascension requires specific materials. Leveling increases base ATK, DEF, and HP.

**叠影 / Superimposition**：通过获得重复的光锥可以提升叠影等级，增强光锥的特效效果。

Acquiring duplicate Light Cones increases Superimposition level, enhancing special effects.

## 遗器 / Relics

遗器是类似圣遗物的装备系统，分为**洞穴遗器**（4件套）和**位面饰品**（2件套）。每个角色可装备四件洞穴遗器和两件位面饰品。

Relics are the artifact equipment system, divided into **Cavern Relics** (4-piece) and **Planar Ornaments** (2-piece). Each character equips 4 Cavern Relics and 2 Planar Ornaments.

### 遗器部位 / Relic Slots

**洞穴遗器 / Cavern Relics**：

- 头部：固定生命值主属性
- 手部：固定攻击力主属性
- 躯干：主属性可选 攻击/防御/生命/暴击率/暴击伤害/治疗加成/效果命中
- 脚部：主属性可选 攻击/防御/生命/速度

- Head: Fixed HP main stat
- Hands: Fixed ATK main stat
- Body: ATK/DEF/HP/Crit Rate/Crit DMG/Heal Bonus/Effect Hit Rate
- Feet: ATK/DEF/HP/SPD

**位面饰品 / Planar Ornaments**：

- 位面球：主属性可选 攻击/防御/生命/各属性伤害加成
- 连接绳：主属性可选 攻击/防御/生命/能量回复效率/击破特攻

- Planar Sphere: ATK/DEF/HP/Elemental DMG Bonus
- Link Rope: ATK/DEF/HP/ERR/Break Effect

## 角色升级材料 / Character Upgrade Materials

**等级突破材料 / Ascension Materials**：

通过挑战凝滞虚影获得，每种属性对应不同的材料副本。每个角色需要特定属性的突破材料。

Obtained from Stagnant Shadows. Each element has a corresponding material domain. Characters need materials matching their element.

**行迹材料 / Trace Materials**：

通过挑战拟造花萼（赤）获得，分为绿色、蓝色和紫色稀有度。不同命途的角色需要不同的行迹材料。

Obtained from Calyx Crimson. Available in green, blue, and purple rarity. Different Paths require different materials.

**周本材料 / Weekly Boss Materials**：

通过挑战历战余响获得。用于解锁行迹的高级节点（天赋等级提升至8级以上）。

Obtained from Echo of War. Used for unlocking high-level Trace nodes (Talent level 8+).

## 消耗品 / Consumables

游戏中存在多种消耗品和合成材料：

Various consumables and synthesis materials exist in the game:

**合成材料 / Synthesis Materials**：通过破坏地图物品和派遣获得，用于合成消耗品。

Obtained from map destruction and assignments. Used to craft consumables.

**食物和药剂 / Food and Potions**：战斗外使用提供临时增益，如增加攻击力、防御力或经验获取。

Used outside combat for temporary buffs: ATK up, DEF up, EXP gain boost.

**合成消耗品 / Crafted Consumables**：

- 恢复类：恢复生命值或战技点
- 增益类：提供战斗属性加成
- 特攻类：对特定类型敌人增伤

- Recovery: Restore HP or SP
- Buff: Combat stat bonuses
- Specialist: Bonus damage vs certain enemy types

## 商店物品 / Shop Items

**星芒兑换 / Starlight Exchange**：通过抽取重复四星和五星角色/光锥获得星芒，可兑换星轨专票、光锥和角色。

Obtained from duplicate 4-star and 5-star pulls. Exchange for Special Passes, Light Cones, and characters.

**余烬兑换 / Ember Exchange**：通过抽取三星物品获得余烬，每月可兑换有限数量的星轨通票和专票。

Obtained from 3-star pulls. Monthly limited exchange for Star Rail Passes and Special Passes.

**无名勋礼 / Nameless Honor**：提供光锥、星琼和养成材料。

Battle Pass rewards including Light Cones, Stellar Jade, and materials.
