## 角色系统概述 / Character System Overview

《崩坏：星穹铁道》拥有数十位可玩角色，每位角色隶属于一个命途（Path）和一种属性（Element）。命途决定角色的战斗定位，属性决定其能够击破的弱点类型。

Honkai: Star Rail features dozens of playable characters. Each belongs to one Path and one Element. The Path determines the combat role, while the Element determines which weaknesses they can break.

## 七种命途 / Seven Paths

**毁灭 / Destruction**：攻守兼备的万能型角色，兼具输出与生存能力。代表角色：镜流、丹恒·饮月、克拉拉、阿刃。适合作为队伍核心输出。

Balanced attackers with both offense and survival. Representatives: Jingliu, Dan Heng IL, Clara, Blade. Ideal as team core DPS.

**巡猎 / Hunt**：对单特化型输出角色，拥有极高的单体伤害和速度。代表角色：希儿、托帕、真理医生、彦卿。适合应对精英敌人和Boss战。

Single-target specialists with extremely high damage and speed. Representatives: Seele, Topaz, Dr. Ratio, Yanqing. Best for elites and boss fights.

**智识 / Erudition**：群攻特化型输出角色，擅长处理多个敌人。代表角色：银枝、姬子、景元、黑塔。适合清杂和虚构叙事模式。

AoE specialists excelling against multiple enemies. Representatives: Argenti, Himeko, Jing Yuan, Herta. Best for wave clearing and Pure Fiction.

**同谐 / Harmony**：增益辅助角色，为队友提供攻击力、速度、暴击等加成。代表角色：布洛妮娅、停云、阮·梅、花火。几乎所有队伍必带。

Buffer supports providing ATK, SPD, Crit bonuses. Representatives: Bronya, Tingyun, Ruan Mei, Sparkle. Essential in almost every team.

**虚无 / Nihility**：减益辅助角色，通过施加负面效果削弱敌人或造成持续伤害。代表角色：卡芙卡、黑天鹅、银狼、黄泉。DoT队的核心。

Debuff supports that weaken enemies or deal DoT. Representatives: Kafka, Black Swan, Silver Wolf, Acheron. Core of DoT teams.

**存护 / Preservation**：护盾防御角色，通过护盾保护队友免受伤害。代表角色：符玄、杰帕德、开拓者·存护、砂金。提供队伍生存保障。

Shielders who protect the team with defensive barriers. Representatives: Fu Xuan, Gepard, Trailblazer Preservation, Aventurine. Ensure team survival.

**丰饶 / Abundance**：治疗回复角色，为队友恢复生命值。代表角色：罗刹、藿藿、白露、玲可。维持队伍血线健康。

Healers who restore HP to the team. Representatives: Luocha, Huohuo, Bailu, Lynx. Keep the team healthy.

## 七种属性 / Seven Elements

**物理 / Physical**：击破造成裂伤（持续物理伤害）。代表角色：克拉拉、素裳、银枝。

Break causes Bleed (physical DoT). Representatives: Clara, Sushang, Argenti.

**火 / Fire**：击破造成灼烧（持续火伤害）。代表角色：姬子、托帕、流萤、开拓者·炎枪。

Break causes Burn (fire DoT). Representatives: Himeko, Topaz, Firefly, Trailblazer Preservation.

**冰 / Ice**：击破造成冻结（无法行动）。代表角色：镜流、彦卿、三月七、黑塔。

Break causes Freeze (cannot act). Representatives: Jingliu, Yanqing, March 7th, Herta.

**雷 / Lightning**：击破造成触电（持续雷伤害）。代表角色：景元、卡芙卡、希露瓦、停云。

Break causes Shock (lightning DoT). Representatives: Jing Yuan, Kafka, Serval, Tingyun.

**风 / Wind**：击破造成风化（持续风伤害）。代表角色：布洛妮娅、黑天鹅、丹恒、刃。

Break causes Wind Shear (wind DoT). Representatives: Bronya, Black Swan, Dan Heng, Blade.

**量子 / Quantum**：击破造成纠缠（附加额外伤害）。代表角色：希儿、符玄、银狼、花火、青雀。

Break causes Entanglement (extra damage on hit). Representatives: Seele, Fu Xuan, Silver Wolf, Sparkle, Qingque.

**虚数 / Imaginary**：击破造成禁锢（行动大幅延迟）。代表角色：丹恒·饮月、罗刹、真理医生、驭空。

Break causes Imprisonment (major action delay). Representatives: Dan Heng IL, Luocha, Dr. Ratio, Yukong.

## 角色强度评级参考 / Character Tier Reference

请注意：以下评级基于版本环境，仅供参考。不同场景下角色的表现可能不同。

Note: Ratings are version-dependent and for reference only. Performance varies by scenario.

### S级 / S-Tier

**镜流 / Jingliu** (毁灭·冰)：高爆发冰伤输出，自我强化能力强，AOE和单体兼顾。版本顶级主C。

High burst Ice DPS with strong self-buff. Strong in both AoE and single target. Top-tier main DPS.

**丹恒·饮月 / Dan Heng Imbibitor Lunae** (毁灭·虚数)：虚数属性强力主C，强化普攻输出极高。需要较多战技点。

Imaginary destruction DPS with extremely high enhanced basic attack damage. Requires significant SP.

**布洛妮娅 / Bronya** (同谐·风)：拉条增伤辅助，可以让队友立即行动并提供大量增伤。通辅最强之一。

Action advance support. Grants immediate turns and massive damage buffs. One of the best universal supports.

**符玄 / Fu Xuan** (存护·量子)：分摊伤害并提供暴击率加成。顶级生存位，兼顾辅助。

Damage mitigation tank with crit rate buff. Top-tier sustain with supportive capabilities.

**阮·梅 / Ruan Mei** (同谐·冰)：全队增伤、弱点击破效率和速度加成。击破队核心辅助。

Team-wide damage, break efficiency, and SPD buffs. Core support for break teams.

**卡芙卡 / Kafka** (虚无·雷)：DoT队核心，能触发并增强队友施加的持续伤害。机制独特。

Core of DoT teams. Triggers and amplifies allies' DoT effects. Unique mechanic.

**黄泉 / Acheron** (虚无·雷)：无需战技点的特殊输出模式，终结技无视弱点削韧。独立输出。

Special DPS that does not consume SP. Ultimate ignores weakness types for Toughness reduction. Self-sufficient.

### A级 / A-Tier

**希儿 / Seele** (巡猎·量子)：击杀再动机制，对单高压制力强。需要击杀启动节奏。

Resets turn on kill. Extremely strong single-target pressure. Requires kill pacing.

**银枝 / Argenti** (智识·物理)：强力群攻输出，终结技能量灵活。虚构叙事顶级角色。

Strong AoE DPS with flexible energy requirements. Top-tier in Pure Fiction.

**罗刹 / Luocha** (丰饶·虚数)：自动治疗驱散负面效果。治疗量高且不耗战技点。

Auto-heal with debuff cleanse. High healing without skill point cost.

**花火 / Sparkle** (同谐·量子)：战技点回复和单体拉条增伤。适合战技点消耗大的队伍。

SP recovery and single-target action advance. Ideal for SP-hungry teams.

**黑天鹅 / Black Swan** (虚无·风)：奥迹叠层机制，持续伤害爆发高。需要搭配卡芙卡。

Arcana stacking mechanic with high DoT burst. Best paired with Kafka.

**流萤 / Firefly** (毁灭·火)：击破特化型输出，对火弱敌人伤害极高。击破队主力。

Break-specialized DPS. Extremely high damage against fire-weak enemies. Core break DPS.

### F2P友好角色 / F2P-Friendly Characters

以下角色容易获取且强度在线：

These characters are accessible and strong:

**开拓者 / Trailblazer**：可切换毁灭、存护、同谐三种命途，免费获取。

Switchable between Destruction, Preservation, and Harmony Paths. Completely free.

**三月七 / March 7th**：存护·冰，提供护盾和反击，新手友好。

Preservation Ice, provides shields and counterattacks. Beginner-friendly.

**娜塔莎 / Natasha**：丰饶·物理，免费治疗角色，前期可用。

Abundance Physical, free healer. Good for early game.

**停云 / Tingyun** (四星·同谐)：顶级四星辅助，充能增伤功能强大。

Top-tier 4-star support. Provides energy regen and damage buffs.

**佩拉 / Pela** (四星·虚无)：群体减防辅助，泛用性强。

AoE defense debuff support. Highly versatile.

**黑塔 / Herta** (四星·智识)：清杂能力优秀，虚构叙事好用。

Excellent wave clearer. Strong in Pure Fiction.

## 角色养成优先级 / Character Priority

推荐养成顺序：主C（80级满突破）> 生存位（70级+）> 辅助（70级+）。主C优先升满行迹关键节点，辅助优先升功能性行迹。

Recommended order: Main DPS (80 fully ascended) > Sustain (70+) > Support (70+). Max key Traces first for DPS.

## 版本趋势 / Version Trends

随着版本更新，角色强度会发生变化。当前版本中，击破队、超击破体系、追击队和DoT队各占一席之地。请关注官方公告和版本更新说明。

Character strength shifts with updates. Current meta includes Break teams, Super Break, Follow-up Attack teams, and DoT teams. Follow official patch notes for changes.
