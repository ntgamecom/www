## 配队基础 / Team Building Basics

在《崩坏：星穹铁道》中，合理的队伍配置是通关高难度内容的关键。一支标准队伍通常包含四种职能：主C、辅助、副C/副辅助和生存位。

In Honkai: Star Rail, proper team composition is key to clearing difficult content. A standard team typically includes four roles: Main DPS, Support, Sub-DPS/Off-Support, and Sustain.

## 标准配队框架 / Standard Team Framework

### 双核心输出 / Dual DPS

主C + 副C/DoT手 + 辅助 + 生存位。适合拥有两个强力输出角色的玩家。

Main DPS + Sub-DPS/DoT dealer + Support + Sustain. For players with two strong DPS units.

### 三保一 / Hypercarry

主C + 双辅助 + 生存位。极致强化一个核心输出，适合单核体系。

Main DPS + Dual Supports + Sustain. Maximizes one core DPS. Ideal for single-core setups.

### 双辅助混搭 / Dual Support Hybrid

主C + 辅助 + 副C + 生存位。平衡输出和辅助的泛用配置。

Main DPS + Support + Sub-DPS + Sustain. Balanced and versatile.

## 热门配队体系 / Popular Team Archetypes

### 量子队 / Quantum Team

**核心成员**：希儿（主C）或青雀 + 银狼 + 符玄 + 花火

**Core**: Seele/Qingque + Silver Wolf + Fu Xuan + Sparkle

银狼可以植入量子弱点，配合花火的战技点回复和符玄的暴击增益，形成强大的量子纯色队。

Silver Wolf implants Quantum weakness. Paired with Sparkle's SP recovery and Fu Xuan's crit buff, forming a powerful mono-Quantum team.

**变体**：雪衣也可以作为副C或主C加入量子队。

Variant: Xueyi can join as sub-DPS or main DPS.

### DoT队 / DoT Team

**核心成员**：卡芙卡 + 黑天鹅 + 阮·梅 + 藿藿

**Core**: Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo

卡芙卡触发持续伤害，黑天鹅叠加奥迹层数，阮·梅提升击破效率和全队增伤，藿藿提供治疗和能量回复。

Kafka triggers DoT, Black Swan stacks Arcana, Ruan Mei boosts break efficiency and team damage, Huohuo provides healing and energy.

**F2P版本**：卡芙卡 + 桑博/卢卡 + 艾丝妲 + 娜塔莎/玲可

**F2P version**: Kafka + Sampo/Luka + Asta + Natasha/Lynx

### 追击队 / Follow-up Attack Team

**核心成员**：托帕 + 账账 + 克拉拉/真理医生 + 砂金/藿藿

**Core**: Topaz + Numby + Clara/Dr. Ratio + Aventurine/Huohuo

以追击为核心输出机制，托帕提供追击增伤，克拉拉反击或真理医生追加攻击输出。

Centered on follow-up attacks. Topaz boosts follow-up DMG, Clara counterattacks or Dr. Ratio executes follow-ups.

**变体**：姬子 + 黑塔组合适合虚构叙事的双追击阵容。

Variant: Himeko + Herta duo for Pure Fiction follow-up synergy.

### 击破队 / Break Team

**核心成员**：流萤 + 同谐开拓者 + 阮·梅 + 加拉赫/玲可

**Core**: Firefly + Harmony Trailblazer + Ruan Mei + Gallagher/Lynx

利用超击破机制造成巨额伤害。同谐开拓者提供超击破能力，阮·梅提升击破效率。

Utilizes Super Break for massive damage. Harmony Trailblazer enables Super Break, Ruan Mei boosts break efficiency.

**F2P版本**：同谐开拓者 + 雪衣/素裳 + 艾丝妲 + 加拉赫

**F2P version**: Harmony TB + Xueyi/Sushang + Asta + Gallagher

### 冰队 / Ice Team

**核心成员**：镜流 + 布洛妮娅 + 佩拉 + 罗刹/符玄

**Core**: Jingliu + Bronya + Pela + Luocha/Fu Xuan

镜流拥有极高的冰伤爆发，布洛妮娅拉条增伤，佩拉群体减防，罗刹提供治疗和净化。

Jingliu delivers massive Ice burst. Bronya advances action and buffs. Pela provides AoE DEF shred. Luocha heals and cleanses.

### 虚数队 / Imaginary Team

**核心成员**：丹恒·饮月 + 花火 + 停云 + 罗刹/符玄

**Core**: Dan Heng IL + Sparkle + Tingyun + Luocha/Fu Xuan

饮月消耗大量战技点打出高额虚数伤害，花火和停云提供充能和战技点回复。

Dan Heng IL consumes heavy SP for massive Imaginary damage. Sparkle and Tingyun provide energy and SP recovery.

## 配队原则 / Team Building Principles

**属性覆盖 / Element Coverage**：确保队伍能击破敌人的主要弱点，至少覆盖2-3种属性。

Ensure the team can break enemy weaknesses. Cover at least 2-3 elements.

**战技点平衡 / SP Balance**：队伍中需要有产点角色（多用普攻）和耗点角色（多用战技）。理想比例是1-2个产点角色配1-2个耗点角色。

Include both SP-positive and SP-negative characters. Ideal ratio: 1-2 generators + 1-2 consumers.

**速度配速 / Speed Tuning**：辅助速度通常高于主C，以便先手上增益。可以根据速度阈值（134/160）进行配速。

Supports should generally be faster than the main DPS for pre-buffing. Tune to speed breakpoints (134/160).

**生存能力 / Survival**：确保队伍有足够的治疗或护盾应对高压环境。输出再高也需要站得住。

Ensure sufficient healing or shielding for high-pressure content. High damage requires survivability.

## 专武优先级 / Light Cone Priority

大多数情况下，优先抽取新角色优于抽取专属光锥。光锥可以用四星替代品过渡，角色的机制和命途效果无法替代。

Generally, pulling new characters takes priority over signature Light Cones. 4-star alternatives can suffice for Light Cones, but character mechanics are irreplaceable.

## 总结 / Summary

优秀的配队需要综合考虑属性、职能、战技点循环和速度配速。建议先培养一个核心队伍，再逐步扩展角色池。关注版本更新和角色复刻信息，规划好自己的抽卡策略。

Good team building requires balancing elements, roles, SP rotation, and speed tuning. Build one core team first, then expand your roster. Watch for version updates and rerun banners to plan pulling strategies.
