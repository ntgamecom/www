## 战斗基础 / Combat Basics

《雨中冒险2》的战斗核心是**持续移动**与**道具驱动的伤害输出**。不同于传统射击游戏，你的伤害主要来自道具叠加而非武器本身。

The core of *Risk of Rain 2* combat is **constant movement** and **item-driven damage output**. Unlike traditional shooters, your damage comes primarily from item stacking rather than the weapon itself.

## 战斗走位 / Combat Movement

### 基础原则 / Core Principles

1. **永远不要停** / Never stop moving — 站桩输出等于送死
2. **利用Z轴** / Use the Z-axis — 高地优势在3D环境中极其重要
3. **绕圈走位** / Circle strafing — 围绕敌人旋转射击
4. **预判敌人弹道** / Predict enemy projectiles — 火球和激光都有飞行时间

1. **Never stop** — standing still equals death
2. **Use verticality** — high ground advantage is crucial in 3D
3. **Circle strafe** — rotate around enemies while shooting
4. **Predict projectiles** — fireballs and lasers have travel time

### 针对不同敌人的走位 / Positioning Against Different Enemies

- **对抗石傀儡 / vs Stone Golems**: 侧向移动躲避激光 / Strafe to dodge lasers
- **对抗蜥蜴人 / vs Lemurians**: 保持中距离，不要被围困 / Maintain mid-range, avoid being surrounded
- **对抗鬼火 / vs Wisps**: 优先击杀，飞行单位很难躲避 / Prioritize kills, flying units are hard to dodge
- **对抗精英怪 / vs Elites**: 观察光环颜色，针对性走位 / Observe aura color, position accordingly

## 精英战斗策略 / Elite Combat Strategies

### 火焰精英 / Blazing Elite

火焰精英在近身时会持续灼烧玩家。不要靠近，使用远程攻击消耗。

Blazing elites continuously burn nearby players. Do not approach, use ranged attacks to wear them down.

### 冰霜精英 / Glacial Elite

冰霜精英的弹幕会减速，一旦被减速容易被后续攻击命中。优先处理，利用翻滚解除减速效果。

Glacial elite projectiles slow you down, making you vulnerable to follow-up attacks. Prioritize them and use dodges to mitigate slow effects.

### 过载精英 / Overloading Elite

过载精英拥有可再生的护盾。使用持续伤害道具可以阻止护盾再生。

Overloading elites have regenerating shields. Use damage-over-time items to prevent shield regeneration.

### 玛瑙精英 / Malachite Elite

玛瑙精英的攻击会降低你的抗性，使你更易受伤。保持距离，不要在抗性降低时交战。

Malachite elite attacks reduce your resistance, making you more vulnerable. Keep distance and avoid engagement when debuffed.

## 团队协作 / Team Combat

### 多人模式战术 / Multiplayer Tactics

- **分工明确** / Divide roles — 坦克在前，输出在后 / Tank in front, DPS in back
- **集中火力** / Focus fire — 优先秒杀高威胁目标 / Prioritize high-threat targets
- **互相复活** / Revive each other — 3秒读条，注意保护 / 3-second channel, protect the reviver
- **道具分配** / Item distribution — 攻击道具给DPS，防御道具给前排 / Damage items for DPS, defense for frontline
- **不要分散** / Do not split — 团队分散容易被逐个击破 / Split team gets picked off

### 多人BOSS战术 / Multiplayer Boss Tactics

BOSS战在多人模式下更具挑战性，因为BOSS生命值会按玩家人数倍增。需要更好的沟通和站位。

Boss fights are more challenging in multiplayer since boss HP scales with player count. Better communication and positioning are required.

## 实用技巧 / Combat Tips

1. **利用跳跃** / Use jumping — 跳跃中可以改变方向，规避地面攻击
2. **弹道预判** / Lead your shots — 远程敌人的弹道可以预判躲避
3. **角落卡视野** / Corner peeking — 利用地形阻挡敌人弹道
4. **道具联动** / Item proc chains — 某些道具可以触发其他道具效果
5. **不要贪刀** / Do not overcommit — 血量低时优先撤退恢复
