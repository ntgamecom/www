## 故事背景 / Story Background

在《雨中冒险2》中，玩家扮演名为"幸存者"的角色，被困在神秘的外星球 Petrichor V 上。在一次迫降后，主角发现自己置身于一个充满危险生物和远古遗迹的世界。唯一回家的希望是通过传送器抵达星球深处，寻找 Contact Light 飞船的信号，并最终逃离这颗星球。

In *Risk of Rain 2*, players take on the role of a "Survivor" stranded on the mysterious alien planet Petrichor V. After a crash landing, the protagonist finds themselves in a world filled with dangerous creatures and ancient ruins. The only hope of returning home is to use teleporters to reach the planet's core, locate the Contact Light's signal, and ultimately escape.

## 核心玩法循环 / Core Gameplay Loop

每个游戏的循环如下 / Each run follows this loop:

1. **坠落到随机关卡** / Crash onto a random stage
2. **探索并收集道具和金钱** / Explore and collect items and gold
3. **找到并激活传送器** / Find and activate the teleporter
4. **击败传送器BOSS** / Defeat the teleporter boss
5. **前往下一关** / Proceed to the next stage
6. **重复直到击败最终BOSS或到达天空关卡** / Repeat until defeating the final boss or reaching the celestial level

## 实用提示 / Pro Tips

1. **保持移动** / Keep moving — 静止是死亡的主要原因
2. **道具叠加很强大** / Stacking items is powerful — 同样道具叠加效果远超单件
3. **时间管理是关键** / Manage your time — 不要在每个关卡停留过久
4. **善用3D视角** / Use the 3D camera — 利用高低差进行战斗
5. **多尝试不同角色** / Try different survivors — 每个角色都有独特玩法
6. **多人游戏道具共享** / Share items in multiplayer — 合理分配道具提升团队战力
