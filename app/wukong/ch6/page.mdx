# Chapter 6: Mountain of Flowers and Fruit Complete Guide

## Version Info

Compatible with the latest release version

## Route Walkthrough

1. Take the boat from Flaming Mountain dock back to **Mountain of Flowers and Fruit · Water Curtain Cave Entrance**
2. Enter Water Curtain Cave, activate **Water Curtain Cave Shrine** — this is the final chapter's safe zone
3. Exit Water Curtain Cave to reach **Mountain of Flowers and Fruit Base**, clear Heavenly Soldiers
4. Climb the mountain path, encounter the **Four Heavenly Kings** one by one (fixed order)
5. After defeating the Four Heavenly Kings, reach **Mountain Peak**
6. At the peak, trigger the **Erlang Shen** boss fight
7. Defeat Erlang Shen and enter **Heavenly Rift** to face the final boss **Broken Sage / Great Sage's Remnants**
8. Watch the ending cutscene after defeating the final boss
9. New Game+ unlocked: inherit some equipment and restart with increased difficulty

## Boss Strategies

### Virūḍhaka (Growth Heavenly King · First)

- **Mechanics**: Longsword + cyan light shield + summon soldiers
- **Weakness**: Cyan shield requires sustained attacks to break
- **Dodge Timing**: Dodge sideways during sword thrust
- **Combo**:
  - Open with sustained attacks to break shield → light ×5 + heavy after shield break
  - Clear summoned soldiers with AoE
- **Cheese**: Keep moving, his thrust has a predictable trajectory

### Dhṛtarāṣṭra (Dharma Heavenly King · Second)

- **Mechanics**: Universe Umbrella + lightning attacks + teleport
- **Weakness**: He can't move while the umbrella is open
- **Dodge Timing**: Lightning strikes from above, blue circles on the ground
- **Combo**:
  - Circle behind him for damage when his umbrella opens
  - Dodge after lightning circles appear, don't stay in one spot
- **Cheese**: Only attack when his umbrella opens, keep distance otherwise

### Virūpākṣa (Wide-Eyed Heavenly King · Third)

- **Mechanics**: Red dragon缠绕 + flame breath + dragon dive
- **Weakness**: The red dragon is his weak point — attacking its head interrupts skills
- **Dodge Timing**: Run sideways when flame breath turns toward you
- **Combo**:
  - Prioritize attacking the dragon's head → interrupt breath then hit the main body
  - Side dodge during dragon dive
- **Cheese**: Focus the dragon — once it's down, the king is half-defeated

### Vaiśravaṇa (Wealth Heavenly King · Fourth)

- **Mechanics**: Lute sonic waves + area stun + summon Four Kings combined attack
- **Weakness**: Sonic attacks have visible wave patterns
- **Dodge Timing**: Watch the ground sonic wave spread during lute playing
- **Combo**:
  - Close distance after sonic wave dodge to interrupt his playing
  - Full focus on dodging during the combined attack, don't try to damage
- **Cheese**: Stay glued to him to prevent lute playing

### Erlang Shen (Yang Jian)

- **Mechanics**: Three-pointed double-edged blade + heavenly eye laser + 72 Transformations (various beast forms)
- **Weakness**: Heavenly eye laser has a fixed charge position — stand behind him to avoid
- **Phase 1**: Human form + three-pointed blade combos
  - Flowing sword style, each strike comes from a different angle
  - Final hit of the combo is a wide sweep
- **Phase 2**: Heavenly eye laser + 72 Transformations
  - Circle behind for damage during laser charge
  - 72 Transformations: tiger form → same attacks as Tiger Vanguard; eagle form → ranged wind blades
- **Combo**:
  - Phase 1: Dodge the full combo → Immobilize + fully charged heavy
  - Phase 2: Circle behind to dodge laser → damage during transformation switch
  - Match his transformations with Guangzhi transformation
- **Cheese**: The laser phase is the best damage window, chip at other times

### Great Sage's Remnants (Final Boss)

- **Mechanics**: Ruyi Jingu Bang (extendable) + Jindou Cloud teleport + clone technique + Immobilize
- **Weakness**: During clone technique, only the real body casts a shadow
- **Phase 1**: Basic combat
  - Jingu Bang has huge range, watch your dodge rhythm
  - He uses the SAME Immobilize as you! Switch to Guangzhi transformation to break free
  - Jindou Cloud teleport is followed by a charging attack
- **Phase 2**: Full power
  - Clone technique summons three phantoms attacking simultaneously
  - Extended Jingu Bang sweeps the entire arena
  - At critically low HP, triggers a full-screen AoE that requires a perfect dodge
- **Combo**:
  - Phase 1: Wait for him to finish a set → light ×3 + heavy → immediately retreat
  - Phase 2: When clones appear, find the real one (check shadows) → Immobilize + full charge
  - Full-screen AoE: jump + dodge when the golden ring appears on the ground (perfect dodge timing)
- **Cheese**: Stay at max range, approach only after he finishes a high-recovery skill

## Tips & Pitfalls

- The Four Heavenly Kings can be defeated one at a time — return to Water Curtain Cave to rest between each
- Erlang Shen's transformation switches follow a fixed pattern — learn it for consistent damage windows
- Great Sage's Remnants' Immobilize works just like yours — break it with Guangzhi transformation
- Don't hoard potions for the final battle — this is the last fight
- Look for shadows to identify the real body during clone technique — fastest method
- The perfect dodge timing for the full-screen AoE is when the golden ring contracts to its smallest size
- New Game+ carries over gourd upgrades, rare materials, and spells — recommended to complete at least NG+ for the true ending
- After defeating the final boss, you can freely explore all previously unlocked areas
