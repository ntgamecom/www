# Chapter 3: New Western Paradise Complete Guide

## Version Info

Compatible with the latest release version

## Route Walkthrough

1. Take the boat from Yellow Wind Ridge dock to **New Western Paradise · Bitter Sea Coast**
2. Walk along the icy shore, clear snow wolf yaoguai, reach **Bitter Sea North Shore Shrine**
3. Cross the frozen lake to **Thunder Temple (Leiyinsi)** outskirts, encounter elite **Bukong**
4. Defeat Bukong and enter **Thunder Temple Square**, triggering **Yellow Brow Emperor** Phase 1
5. After Yellow Brow Emperor retreats, chase him into **Thunder Temple Main Hall** for Phase 2
6. Defeat Yellow Brow Emperor and head through the back mountain to **Snow Mountain Peak**
7. At the peak, encounter **Azure Dragon (Qingbeilong)**, optional boss
8. Descend along the snowy cliff path to reach **Floating Prison Realm**, clear jailers and trapped souls
9. At the deepest part of Floating Prison Realm, trigger **Bubai** boss fight
10. Defeat Bubai, take the teleport back to Thunder Temple rear, face the final boss **Golden Gong Abbot**

## Boss Strategies

### Bukong

- **Mechanics**: Ice spells + monk's staff melee, can freeze the Destined One
- **Weakness**: Long cast animation for spells
- **Dodge Timing**: Immediately dodge out of range when frost circle appears
- **Combo**: Dodge frost circle → rush light ×4 + charged heavy → switch to Guangzhi transformation if frozen
- **Cheese**: Use terrain height differences, charged heavy from range during his casting recovery

### Yellow Brow Emperor (Phase 1 · Square)

- **Mechanics**: Golden gong sonic attacks + summon gong clones
- **Weakness**: Gong clones appear at fixed positions each time
- **Dodge Timing**: Sonic waves have spread trajectories, dodge to the sides
- **Combo**: Clear gong clones → Immobilize + light ×5 + heavy → retreat
- **Cheese**: Clear clones first then focus the main body, don't face multiple targets

### Yellow Brow Emperor (Phase 2 · Main Hall)

- **Mechanics**: Giant golden body + area shockwave + summon Buddha phantoms
- **Weakness**: Shockwave has a golden ring indicator before landing
- **Dodge Timing**: Dodge when the golden ring contracts to maximum (perfect timing)
- **Combo**:
  - Dodge shockwave → rush to his feet for 2 seconds of damage → retreat
  - Prioritize interrupting Buddha summon with Immobilize
  - Don't attack during golden body phase, wait for him to return to normal size
- **Cheese**: Keep mid-range the whole time, approach for one combo after his shockwave then retreat

### Azure Dragon (Optional Boss)

- **Mechanics**: Flight + frost breath + falling ice
- **Weakness**: Brief recovery period after landing
- **Dodge Timing**: Run sideways for frost breath, don't retreat
- **Combo**: The moment he lands → Immobilize + fully charged heavy + Guangzhi transformation pursuit
- **Cheese**: Wait for him to land naturally, don't fight during flight phase

### Bubai

- **Mechanics**: Giant skeleton form + skeleton summon + bone spike AoE
- **Weakness**: Stationary while summoning skeletons
- **Dodge Timing**: Dodge three times consecutively during bone spike charge
- **Combo**:
  - Clear skeletons → rush in for 3 seconds → retreat
  - Continuous side dodges during bone spike AoE
  - At low HP, skeleton form grows larger — attack from behind
- **Cheese**: Keep distance and let skeletons group up, use Guangzhi fire AoE to clear

### Golden Gong Abbot (Final Boss)

- **Mechanics**: Ranged gong attacks + melee staff + sonic traps
- **Weakness**: Standing on sonic traps immobilizes you
- **Form 1**: Ranged-focused, flying gong attacks
- **Form 2**: Melee-focused, staff combos + sonic traps
- **Combo**:
  - Form 1: Dodge gong → approach while he recovers
  - Form 2: Watch for ground sonic traps, circle behind for damage
  - Switch to Guangzhi transformation to break free if trapped
- **Cheese**: Fight with your back to the wall to reduce sonic trap threat area

## Tips & Pitfalls

- The ice on Bitter Sea Coast can be broken — there are hidden caves and treasure chests underneath
- Ring the copper bell in Thunder Temple Square to temporarily stun nearby mobs
- Don't stand directly in front of Yellow Brow Emperor during Phase 2 — the shockwave has the widest range in front
- Skipping Azure Dragon doesn't affect the main quest, but his scale crafts excellent ice weapons
- The trapped souls in Floating Prison Realm can be skipped, but defeating them gives lots of Will
- Several icy sections on the snow mountain cliff path are slippery — hold the walk key
- Golden Gong Abbot's sonic traps double in density at low HP, watch your step
