# 第三章：新西天 / Chapter 3: New Western Paradise

## 版本说明 / Version Info

适配最新正式版 / Compatible with the latest release version

## 路线流程 / Route Walkthrough

1. 从黄风岭渡口乘船抵达**新西天·苦海岸** / Take the boat from Yellow Wind Ridge dock to **New Western Paradise · Bitter Sea Coast**
2. 沿冰海岸边前行，清理雪地狼妖，抵达**苦海北岸土地庙** / Walk along the icy shore, clear snow wolf yaoguai, reach **Bitter Sea North Shore Shrine**
3. 穿过冰封湖面到达**雷音寺**外围，遭遇精英怪**不空** / Cross the frozen lake to **Thunder Temple (Leiyinsi)** outskirts, encounter elite **Bukong**
4. 击败不空后进入**雷音寺广场**，触发**黄眉大王**第一阶段战斗 / Defeat Bukong and enter **Thunder Temple Square**, triggering **Yellow Brow Emperor** Phase 1
5. 黄眉大王败退后追入**雷音寺大雄宝殿**，进行第二阶段战斗 / After he retreats, chase him into **Thunder Temple Main Hall** for Phase 2
6. 击败黄眉大王后从后山前往**雪山之巅** / Defeat Yellow Brow Emperor and head through the back mountain to **Snow Mountain Peak**
7. 在雪山之巅遇到**青背龙**，可选BOSS / At the peak, encounter **Azure Dragon (Qingbeilong)**, optional boss
8. 沿雪山栈道下行抵达**浮屠界**，清理狱卒和囚魂 / Descend the snowy cliff path to **Floating Prison Realm**, clear jailers and trapped souls
9. 在浮屠界最深处触发**不白**BOSS战 / At the deepest part, trigger **Bubai** boss fight
10. 击败不白后通过传送门回到雷音寺后方，面对最终BOSS**金铙主持** / Defeat Bubai, take the teleport back to face the final boss **Golden Gong Abbot**

## BOSS打法 / Boss Strategies

### 不空 / Bukong

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 冰系法术+禅杖近战，能冻结天命人 / Ice spells + monk's staff melee, can freeze the Destined One
- **核心弱点 / Weakness**: 施法期间有较长前摇 / Long cast animation for spells
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 冰霜圈出现时立刻闪避出范围 / Immediately dodge out of range when frost circle appears
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 闪避冰霜圈 → 冲脸轻×4 + 蓄力重棍 → 被冻结时切广智变身解控 / Dodge frost circle → rush light ×4 + charged heavy → switch to Guangzhi if frozen
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 利用地形高低差，远程蓄力等他施法硬直 / Use terrain height, charged heavy from range during his casting recovery

### 黄眉大王（第一阶段·广场）/ Yellow Brow Emperor (Phase 1 · Square)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 金铙音波攻击+召唤金铙分身 / Golden gong sonic attacks + summon gong clones
- **核心弱点 / Weakness**: 金铙分身每次出现的位置固定 / Gong clones appear at fixed positions each time
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 金铙音波有扩散轨迹，向两侧闪避 / Sonic waves have spread trajectories, dodge to the sides
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 清掉金铙分身 → 定身术 + 轻×5 + 重击 → 拉开 / Clear gong clones → Immobilize + light ×5 + heavy → retreat
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 先清分身再打本体，不要同时面对多个目标 / Clear clones first then focus main body

### 黄眉大王（第二阶段·大雄宝殿）/ Yellow Brow Emperor (Phase 2 · Main Hall)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 巨大化金身+范围冲击波+召唤佛陀幻影 / Giant golden body + area shockwave + summon Buddha phantoms
- **核心弱点 / Weakness**: 冲击波落地前有金色光圈提示 / Shockwave has a golden ring indicator before landing
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 金色光圈收缩到最大时闪避（完美时机） / Dodge when the golden ring contracts to maximum (perfect timing)
- **连招建议 / Combo**:
  - 闪避冲击波 → 冲到他脚下输出2秒 → 拉开 / Dodge shockwave → rush to his feet for 2 seconds → retreat
  - 召唤佛陀时优先用定身术打断 / Prioritize interrupting Buddha summon with Immobilize
  - 金身状态时不要硬打，等他变回正常大小 / Don't attack during golden body phase
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 全程保持中距离，等他出完冲击波再靠近打一轮就跑 / Keep mid-range, approach after his shockwave

### 青背龙（可选BOSS）/ Azure Dragon (Optional Boss)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 飞行+冰霜吐息+落冰攻击 / Flight + frost breath + falling ice
- **核心弱点 / Weakness**: 落地后短暂硬直 / Brief recovery period after landing
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 冰霜吐息向侧方跑，不要后退 / Run sideways for frost breath, don't retreat
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 他落地瞬间 → 定身术 + 满蓄力重棍 + 广智变身追击 / The moment he lands → Immobilize + fully charged heavy + Guangzhi pursuit
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 等他自己落地，不打飞行阶段 / Wait for him to land naturally

### 不白 / Bubai

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 巨大白骨形态+骷髅召唤+骨刺AOE / Giant skeleton form + skeleton summon + bone spike AoE
- **核心弱点 / Weakness**: 召唤骷髅时本体不动 / Stationary while summoning skeletons
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 骨刺蓄力时闪避三次（连续） / Dodge three times consecutively during bone spike charge
- **连招建议 / Combo**:
  - 清骷髅 → 冲脸输出3秒 → 拉开 / Clear skeletons → rush in for 3 seconds → retreat
  - 骨刺AOE时连续侧闪 / Continuous side dodges during bone spike AoE
  - 残血时白骨形态变大，此时攻击他身后 / At low HP, attack from behind
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 保持距离让骷髅聚集，用广智变身火焰AOE清场 / Keep distance, let skeletons group, use Guangzhi fire AoE

### 金铙主持（最终BOSS）/ Golden Gong Abbot (Final Boss)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 金铙远程攻击+近身禅杖+音波陷阱 / Ranged gong attacks + melee staff + sonic traps
- **核心弱点 / Weakness**: 音波陷阱踩到后会被定在原地 / Standing on sonic traps immobilizes you
- **第一形态 / Form 1**: 远程为主，金铙飞出攻击 / Ranged-focused, flying gong attacks
- **第二形态 / Form 2**: 近战为主，禅杖连击+音波陷阱 / Melee-focused, staff combos + sonic traps
- **连招建议 / Combo**:
  - 第一形态：闪避金铙 → 趁他收回时靠近输出 / Dodge gong → approach while he recovers
  - 第二形态：注意地面音波陷阱，绕到他背后输出 / Watch for traps, circle behind
  - 陷阱被定住时立刻切广智变身解控 / Switch to Guangzhi if trapped
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 贴着墙壁打可以减少音波陷阱的威胁范围 / Fight with back to wall to reduce trap threat area

## 避坑要点 / Tips & Pitfalls

- **苦海岸的冰面可以打破**，下方有隐藏洞穴和宝箱 / **The ice can be broken** — there are hidden caves and chests underneath
- **雷音寺广场的铜钟可以敲响**，会暂时眩晕周围小怪 / **Ring the copper bell** to temporarily stun nearby mobs
- **黄眉大王第二阶段不要站在正前方**，冲击波正面范围最大 / **Don't stand in front** during Phase 2 — shockwave is widest there
- **青背龙不打不影响主线**，但鳞片能造非常好的冰属性武器 / **Azure Dragon is optional** but his scale crafts excellent ice weapons
- **浮屠界的囚魂可以不打**，但击败后给的灵蕴很多 / **Trapped souls are optional** but give lots of Will
- **雪山栈道有几处冰面滑脚**，走的时候按住慢走键 / **Several icy sections are slippery** — hold the walk key
- **金铙主持的音波陷阱在低血量时密度翻倍**，注意脚下 / **Sonic traps double at low HP** — watch your step
