# 第一章：黑风山 / Chapter 1: Black Wind Mountain

## 版本说明 / Version Info

适配最新正式版 / Compatible with the latest release version

## 路线流程 / Route Walkthrough

1. **初始土地庙（苍狼林）**出发，沿唯一路径前进，击败路边小狼妖获取灵蕴 / Start at the **initial Shrine (Canglin Woods)**, follow the only path forward, defeat roadside wolf yaoguai for Will
2. 穿过溪谷抵达**第一座寺庙废墟**，左拐上山坡遇到第一个精英怪**牯护院** / Cross the stream to reach the **first temple ruins**, turn left uphill to encounter the first elite **Bullguard (Gu Huyuan)**
3. 击败牯护院后继续沿山脊走，进入**竹林深处**，遇到第二个精英怪**广智** / After defeating Bullguard, continue along the ridge into the **bamboo forest depths** to find the second elite **Guangzhi**
4. 击败广智获得**广智变身**能力，原路返回寺庙废墟右前方通往**白雾泽** / Defeat Guangzhi to obtain the **Guangzhi Transformation**. Return to the temple ruins and proceed right toward **White Mist Marsh (Baiwuze)**
5. 白雾泽中有多个岔路 / White Mist Marsh has multiple paths:
   - 主路直走触发**灵虚子**BOSS战 / Main path triggers **Lingxuzi** boss fight
   - 右侧岔路通往**马哥**支线 / Right fork leads to **Brother Ma** side quest
   - 左侧水塘有隐藏道具 / Left pond has a hidden item
6. 击败灵虚子后穿过石门进入**翠竹林**，沿路下行遇到**刀狼教头** / After defeating Lingxuzi, go through the stone gate into **Jade Bamboo Grove (Cuizhulin)**, head downhill to encounter **Blade Wolf Instructor**
7. 继续深入抵达**蛇径**，小心毒池和蛇妖 / Continue deeper to reach **Snake Path**, watch out for poison pools and snake yaoguai
8. 穿过蛇径到达**卧虎寺**门前，触发**虎先锋**BOSS战 / Through Snake Path arrives at **Crouching Tiger Temple (Wohusi)** gates, triggering **Tiger Vanguard** boss fight
9. 击败虎先锋进入**卧虎寺地窖**，面对第一章最终BOSS**黑风大王** / Defeat Tiger Vanguard and enter the cellar to face the chapter's final boss **Black Wind King (Heifeng Dawang)**
10. 击败黑风大王后进入**黑风洞**，挑战隐藏BOSS**金池长老**（可选）/ After defeating Black Wind King, enter **Black Wind Cave** to challenge hidden boss **Elder Jinchi** (optional)

## BOSS打法 / Boss Strategies

### 牯护院 / Bullguard (Gu Huyuan)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 近战三板斧（挥砍+突刺+下砸），动作较慢 / Close-range three-hit combo (slash + thrust + slam), slow attacks
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 每次出招后有约2秒硬直，可在其身后输出 / ~2 second recovery after each attack, punish from behind
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 轻攻击×3 → 重攻击蓄力 → 闪避拉开 / Light attack ×3 → charged heavy → dodge away
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 远程蓄力棍法，保持距离风筝 / Ranged charged staff, keep distance and kite

### 广智 / Guangzhi

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 双刀快速连击，有火焰附魔状态 / Dual blades fast combo, fire enchantment state
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 火焰附魔时不要对拼，等附魔结束再输出 / Don't trade during fire enchantment, wait for it to expire
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 等他三连斩打完 → 轻×2 + 重击 → 后撤步 / Wait for three-slash combo → light ×2 + heavy → backstep
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 利用地形柱子卡位，蓄力重棍偷刀 / Use terrain pillars to block, charged heavy for safe damage

### 灵虚子 / Lingxuzi

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 大型狼妖，扑击范围大，甩尾有击退 / Large wolf yaoguai, wide pounce range, tail swipe has knockback
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 扑击前有起跳动作，向侧方闪避 / Pounce has a jump tell, dodge sideways
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 闪避扑击后 → 轻×4 → 重击 → 定身术 / Dodge pounce → light ×4 → heavy → Immobilize
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 保持中距离，等他出完扑击再靠近打一套就跑 / Keep mid-range, punish after his pounce then retreat

### 虎先锋 / Tiger Vanguard

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 老虎形态二连扑+三连抓，残血时进入狂暴 / Tiger form double pounce + triple claw swipe, enrages at low HP
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 二连扑第一次向侧闪，第二次向后闪 / First pounce dodge sideways, second dodge backward
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 闪避完二连扑 → 轻×3 + 重击 → 广智变身压起身 / Dodge double pounce → light ×3 + heavy → Guangzhi transformation for wake-up pressure
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 利用广智变身的火属性克制，配合定身术打一轮就跑 / Use Guangzhi's fire advantage + Immobilize, one combo then retreat

### 黑风大王（最终BOSS）/ Black Wind King (Final Boss)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 第一阶段人形（剑术连招），第二阶段黑风形态（范围AOE）/ Phase 1 human form (sword combos), Phase 2 black wind form (AoE attacks)
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**:
  - 人形阶段：剑光闪烁时立刻闪避 / Human phase: dodge when you see sword flash
  - 黑风阶段：地面黑圈出现后1秒闪避 / Black wind phase: dodge 1 second after black circles appear on ground
- **连招建议 / Combo**:
  - 人形 / Human: 等他五连斩打完 → 定身术 + 满蓄力重棍 / Wait for five-slash combo → Immobilize + fully charged heavy
  - 黑风 / Black wind: 闪避黑风冲击 → 轻×5 + 重击收尾 / Dodge black wind冲击 → light ×5 + heavy finisher
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 保持远距离用蓄力棍磨血，定身术CD好了就打一套 / Keep distance and use charged staff to chip, combo when Immobilize is off cooldown

### 金池长老（隐藏BOSS）/ Elder Jinchi (Hidden Boss)

- **技能机制 / Mechanics**: 召唤分身+范围佛法攻击 / Summons clones + AoE Buddhist attack
- **躲避时机 / Dodge Timing**: 分身出现时优先清分身 / Prioritize clearing clones when they appear
- **连招建议 / Combo**: 清完分身 → 靠近打一套 → 拉开等下一波 / Clear clones → approach for one combo → retreat for next wave
- **逃课打法 / Cheese**: 攒满四段棍势，等他出招硬直时一发满蓄力打掉半管血 / Build 4 focus points, unleash fully charged heavy during his recovery to chunk half his HP

## 避坑要点 / Tips & Pitfalls

- **不要跳过广智**：广智变身是第一章最有用的能力，后期打虎先锋和黑风大王都靠它 / **Don't skip Guangzhi**: his transformation is the most useful ability in Chapter 1, crucial for Tiger Vanguard and Black Wind King
- **白雾泽毒池不要硬闯**：从右侧绕路过去，直接踩毒池会快速掉血 / **Don't charge through the poison pool**: take the right path around, direct contact drains HP fast
- **虎先锋门前先清小怪**：卧虎寺门前有大量刀狼教众，不清直接触发BOSS会被群殴 / **Clear trash mobs before Tiger Vanguard**: many Blade Wolf followers at the gates
- **灵蕴不要乱花**：优先升级葫芦和丹药，武器可以等第二章再换 / **Don't waste Will**: prioritize upgrading gourd and potions, weapons can wait until Chapter 2
- **黑风大王二阶段别贪刀**：黑风形态的AOE伤害极高，打一套就撤 / **Don't get greedy in Phase 2**: the black wind AoE hits extremely hard, one combo then retreat
- **金池长老不打也不影响通关**：但是打了给大量灵蕴和佛珠 / **Elder Jinchi is optional**: but defeating him gives lots of Will and Prayer Beads
