# Chapter 1: Black Wind Mountain Complete Guide

## Version Info

Compatible with the latest release version

## Route Walkthrough

1. Start at the **initial Shrine (Canglin Woods)**, follow the only path forward, defeat roadside wolf yaoguai for Will
2. Cross the stream to reach the **first temple ruins**, turn left uphill to encounter the first elite **Bullguard (Gu Huyuan)**
3. After defeating Bullguard, continue along the ridge into the **bamboo forest depths** to find the second elite **Guangzhi**
4. Defeat Guangzhi to obtain the **Guangzhi Transformation** ability. Return to the temple ruins and proceed right toward **White Mist Marsh (Baiwuze)**
5. White Mist Marsh has multiple paths:
   - Main path straight ahead triggers the **Lingxuzi** boss fight
   - Right fork leads to the **Brother Ma** side quest
   - Left pond has a hidden item
6. After defeating Lingxuzi, go through the stone gate into **Jade Bamboo Grove (Cuizhulin)**, head downhill to encounter **Blade Wolf Instructor**
7. Continue deeper to reach **Snake Path**, watch out for poison pools and snake yaoguai
8. Through Snake Path arrives at **Crouching Tiger Temple (Wohusi)** gates, triggering the **Tiger Vanguard** boss fight
9. Defeat Tiger Vanguard and enter the **Crouching Tiger Temple cellar** to face the chapter's final boss **Black Wind King (Heifeng Dawang)**
10. After defeating Black Wind King, enter **Black Wind Cave** to challenge the hidden boss **Elder Jinchi** (optional)

## Boss Strategies

### Bullguard (Gu Huyuan)

- **Mechanics**: Close-range three-hit combo (slash + thrust + slam), slow attacks
- **Dodge Timing**: ~2 second recovery after each attack, punish from behind
- **Combo**: Light attack ×3 → charged heavy → dodge away
- **Cheese**: Ranged charged staff, keep distance and kite

### Guangzhi

- **Mechanics**: Dual blades fast combo, fire enchantment state
- **Dodge Timing**: Don't trade during fire enchantment, wait for it to expire
- **Combo**: Wait for his three-slash combo to finish → light ×2 + heavy → backstep
- **Cheese**: Use terrain pillars to block, charged heavy for safe damage

### Lingxuzi

- **Mechanics**: Large wolf yaoguai, wide pounce range, tail swipe has knockback
- **Dodge Timing**: Pounce has a jump tell, dodge sideways
- **Combo**: Dodge pounce → light ×4 → heavy → Immobilize
- **Cheese**: Keep mid-range, punish after his pounce then retreat

### Tiger Vanguard

- **Mechanics**: Tiger form double pounce + triple claw swipe, enrages at low HP
- **Dodge Timing**: First pounce dodge sideways, second dodge backward
- **Combo**: Dodge double pounce → light ×3 + heavy → Guangzhi transformation for wake-up pressure
- **Cheese**: Use Guangzhi's fire advantage + Immobilize, one combo then retreat

### Black Wind King (Final Boss)

- **Mechanics**: Phase 1 human form (sword combos), Phase 2 black wind form (AoE attacks)
- **Dodge Timing**:
  - Human phase: dodge when you see sword flash
  - Black wind phase: dodge 1 second after black circles appear on the ground
- **Combo**:
  - Human: Wait for his five-slash combo → Immobilize + fully charged heavy
  - Black wind: Dodge black wind冲击 → light ×5 + heavy finisher
- **Cheese**: Keep distance and use charged staff to chip, combo when Immobilize is off cooldown

### Elder Jinchi (Hidden Boss)

- **Mechanics**: Summons clones + AoE Buddhist attack
- **Dodge Timing**: Prioritize clearing clones when they appear
- **Combo**: Clear clones → approach for one combo → retreat for next wave
- **Cheese**: Build 4 focus points, unleash a fully charged heavy during his attack recovery to chunk half his HP

## Tips & Pitfalls

- Don't skip Guangzhi: his transformation is the most useful ability in Chapter 1, crucial for Tiger Vanguard and Black Wind King
- Don't charge through the poison pool in White Mist Marsh: take the right path around, direct contact drains HP fast
- Clear trash mobs before Tiger Vanguard: many Blade Wolf followers at Crouching Tiger Temple gates, triggering the boss without clearing them leads to being overwhelmed
- Don't waste Will: prioritize upgrading gourd and potions, weapons can wait until Chapter 2
- Don't get greedy in Black Wind King Phase 2: the black wind AoE hits extremely hard, one combo then retreat
- Elder Jinchi is optional: but defeating him gives lots of Will and Prayer Beads, recommended
