# Chapter 4: Spider Cave Complete Guide

## Version Info

Compatible with the latest release version

## Route Walkthrough

1. Take the boat from New Western Paradise dock to **Spider Cave · Cavern Entrance**
2. Traverse the web-covered tunnels to reach **Spider Cave Front Hall Shrine**
3. Clear spider eggs and small spiders in the front hall, encounter the elite **Black Hand Daoist**
4. Defeat Black Hand Daoist to obtain the **Spider Token**, which opens mechanism doors ahead
5. Go deeper into the cave to reach **Spider Nest**, triggering the **Purple Spider** boss fight
6. After defeating Purple Spider, descend through the shaft behind the nest to the bottom level
7. The bottom level is a **Poison Pool** area, watch out for toxic water, stick to the walls
8. Cross the poison pool to reach **Spider Cave Main Hall**, face **Hundred-Eyed Daoist**
9. After defeating Hundred-Eyed Daoist, enter the **Sacrificial Altar** through the hall's back door
10. At the altar, trigger the hidden boss **Poison King** (optional, requires Poison King Token)

## Boss Strategies

### Black Hand Daoist

- **Mechanics**: Multi-arm attacks + throwing daggers + smoke invisibility
- **Weakness**: Leaves footprints while moving in smoke
- **Dodge Timing**: Arm retraction animation before throwing daggers
- **Combo**:
  - During invisibility, predict position from footprints → charged heavy ready for reveal
  - Unleash full charge the moment he appears
  - Back off during his multi-arm flurry
- **Cheese**: Fight against a wall to reduce his pursuit range during smoke

### Purple Spider

- **Mechanics**: Web wrap + egg-laying summon small spiders + poison spit
- **Weakness**: Stationary while laying eggs
- **Dodge Timing**: Dodge sideways when web fires
- **Combo**:
  - Open with Immobilize to interrupt web → light ×5 + heavy
  - Prioritize clearing eggs during egg-laying, otherwise small spiders pile up
  - Poison spit has long startup and recovery, attack from the side
- **Cheese**: Circle run until small spiders group → Guangzhi fire AoE clear → attack main body

### Hundred-Eyed Daoist

- **Mechanics**: Multi-eye laser + petrifying gaze + summon poison spiders
- **Weakness**: Looking at his eyes petrifies you — face away or use the **Warding Charm**
- **Phase 1**: Laser sweep + petrifying gaze
  - Don't look directly at his eyes, keep him at the edge of your screen while strafing
  - Laser has a charge trajectory, run perpendicular
- **Phase 2**: Summon poison spiders + full-screen poison rain
  - Poison rain has ground markers, dodge after they appear
  - Prioritize clearing poison spiders to avoid being overwhelmed
- **Combo**:
  - Phase 1: Strafing avoids gaze → rush to his side light ×4 + heavy → retreat
  - Phase 2: Clear poison spiders → wait for poison rain to finish → Immobilize + full charge
- **Cheese**: Keep mid-to-long range, approach for damage only after his laser attack

### Poison King (Hidden Boss)

- **Trigger**: Enter the deepest part of the Sacrificial Altar with the Poison King Token
- **Mechanics**: Poison mist field + poison claw combo + toxic bombs
- **Weakness**: Poison mist has a limited range, leaving it removes the poison status
- **Dodge Timing**: The last hit of poison claw combo is a heavy strike, big recovery after dodge
- **Combo**:
  - Exit poison mist when it appears → wait for him to come out → light ×3 + heavy
  - Dodge the last poison claw hit → Immobilize + charged heavy
- **Cheese**: Stay outside the poison mist field the whole fight, chip with ranged charges

## Tips & Pitfalls

- Destroy spider eggs on sight — they hatch into chasing spiders if you walk past them
- During Black Hand Daoist's smoke invisibility, don't run randomly — watch for footprint trails
- Purple Spider's web bind can be burned off with Guangzhi transformation's fire
- Hundred-Eyed Daoist's petrifying gaze is completely nullified by the Warding Charm — grab it before the boss fight
- Poison Pool water deals massive damage — don't try to swim through it
- Don't miss the Poison King Token — otherwise you'll skip the hidden boss and the best poison weapon material
- The Sacrificial Altar teleports connect to various Spider Cave areas, use them for fast travel
