# Chapter 2: Yellow Wind Ridge Complete Guide

## Version Info

Compatible with the latest release version

## Route Walkthrough

1. From the Chapter 1 dock at **Black Wind Mountain**, go through the cave to reach **Yellow Wind Ridge Entrance**
2. Follow the sandy path to the first shrine (**Yellow Wind Ridge Pass**), activate it and continue
3. Cross the sandstorm area to reach **Sand Gate Village (Shamencun)**, clear the rat yaoguai inside
4. Deeper in Sand Gate Village, trigger the elite **Sand King**, defeat him for a **Wind Tamer Bead** fragment
5. From the back hill of Sand Gate Village, enter the **Quicksand River** area, search the riverbed for **Sand Lang** boss
6. Defeat Sand Lang, go through the underground cave to reach **Yellow Wind Temple** outskirts
7. Outside the temple, encounter **Tiger Vanguard (Yellow Wind version)** — stronger than Chapter 1
8. Defeat Tiger Vanguard and enter the **Yellow Wind Temple main hall** to face **Yellow Wind Sage**
9. Use the Wind Tamer to weaken Yellow Wind Sage's sandstorm shield, then defeat him
10. From the temple's back gate, enter **Floating Pagoda** to challenge the hidden boss **Golden Cicada** (optional)

## Boss Strategies

### Sand King

- **Mechanics**: Sandstorm shield + ranged sand spikes, ground has quicksand slow zones
- **Weakness**: Sandstorm shield has a duration, he becomes vulnerable when it expires
- **Dodge Timing**: Dodge sideways when sand spikes fire, jump over quicksand zones
- **Combo**: Wait for his sandstorm to end → Immobilize + light ×3 + charged heavy
- **Cheese**: Ranged charged staff, kite until his sandstorm dissipates

### Sand Lang

- **Mechanics**: Giant manticore, poisonous tail stab + burrow ambush
- **Weakness**: Long recovery when surfacing after burrowing
- **Dodge Timing**: Keep moving while he burrows, dodge sideways the moment he surfaces
- **Combo**: Dodge the burrow ambush → light ×5 + heavy → Guangzhi transformation pursuit
- **Cheese**: Stand on a high platform, jump down and combo when he hits the wall after burrowing

### Tiger Vanguard (Yellow Wind Version)

- **Mechanics**: Gains a clone skill compared to Chapter 1, summons two phantoms to attack simultaneously
- **Weakness**: The real Tiger Vanguard charges a big move while clones exist — destroying clones interrupts it
- **Dodge Timing**: Prioritize dodging and clearing clones when they appear
- **Combo**: Clear clones → Immobilize + fully charged heavy → retreat
- **Cheese**: Hit and run on clones, rush in for a combo when the real one's big move is interrupted

### Yellow Wind Sage

- **Mechanics**: Massive sandstorm + sand tornadoes + summon minions
- **Weakness**: Using the **Wind Tamer** dispels his sandstorm shield, putting him in a downed state
- **Phase 1**: Sandstorm shield + ranged sand blades, attacks are ineffective
- **Phase 2**: After shield is dispelled, melee combos + tornadoes
- **Combo**:
  - Open with Wind Tamer → rush in light ×5 + heavy
  - After he gets up, dodge tornadoes → wait for Wind Tamer cooldown → use again
  - Repeat
- **Cheese**: Keep distance, cycle Wind Tamer + charged heavy, avoid melee

### Golden Cicada (Hidden Boss)

- **Trigger**: Find five Prayer Beads in the Floating Pagoda, then activate at the top floor hall
- **Mechanics**: Buddhist golden light + teleport strike + binding curse
- **Dodge Timing**: Continuous sideways dodges during golden light charge
- **Combo**: Dodge golden light → light ×4 + heavy → use Immobilize to break free from binding curse
- **Cheese**: Use pillars to block line of sight, charged heavy sneak attacks

## Tips & Pitfalls

- The Wind Tamer is THE key item for this chapter! Make sure to collect all three fragments and synthesize it before fighting Yellow Wind Sage
- Don't charge into the rat horde in Sand Gate Village — use Guangzhi's fire AoE for maximum clear efficiency
- Right side of the Quicksand River poison area has a hidden passage to a treasure chest
- During Tiger Vanguard's clone phase, don't get greedy — clear clones first
- Yellow Wind Sage's sandstorm phase makes him immune to damage, don't waste mana
- Floating Pagoda requires jumping skill — if you fall, you'll have to climb from the bottom
- Defeat by Golden Cicada teleports you back to the Floating Pagoda entrance, it's not death
