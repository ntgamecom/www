# Chapter 5: Flaming Mountain Complete Guide

## Version Info

Compatible with the latest release version

## Route Walkthrough

1. Take the boat from Spider Cave dock to **Flaming Mountain · Scorched Earth Shrine**
2. Cross the scorched plain, clear fire imps, enter **Blazing Canyon**
3. Midway through the canyon, encounter the elite **Flame Demon**, defeat for fire resistance
4. Cross the canyon to reach **Bull King's Castle** outskirts, encounter **Red Boy**
5. Defeat Red Boy and enter the castle's front gate to face **Bull King** Phase 1
6. After Bull King retreats, chase him into the **Lava Hall** deep within the castle
7. Defeat Bull King Phase 2 in the Lava Hall
8. Enter **Fire Cloud Cave** through the castle's back gate, face the final boss **Yaksha King**
9. After defeating Yaksha King, discover the hidden boss **Princess Iron Fan** in the deepest part of Fire Cloud Cave (optional)

## Boss Strategies

### Flame Demon

- **Mechanics**: Fire shield + magma eruption + fireball volley
- **Weakness**: Fire shield has a stamina bar, extinguishes when depleted
- **Dodge Timing**: Ground turns red before magma eruption, run outward
- **Combo**: Wait for his shield to die out → Immobilize + light ×5 + heavy → retreat
- **Cheese**: Use the environment's water pools to extinguish fire, fight from the water for reduced fire damage

### Red Boy

- **Mechanics**: Flame trident + fiery spear thrusts + summon fire phoenix
- **Weakness**: The fire phoenix can be immobilized with Immobilize
- **Dodge Timing**: The last hit of trident thrust has the widest range, dodge backward
- **Combo**:
  - Immobilize the phoenix → attack Red Boy's main body
  - Dodge spear thrusts → light ×4 + heavy
  - His phoenix summon is an attack window
- **Cheese**: Let the phoenix crash into a wall and disappear before attacking the main body

### Bull King (Phase 1 · Outside Castle)

- **Mechanics**: Giant axe sweep + stomp shockwave + summon bull minions
- **Weakness**: Leg-raising tell before stomp shockwave
- **Dodge Timing**: Dodge sideways the moment he raises his leg
- **Combo**:
  - Dodge stomp → rush to his feet for 2 seconds → retreat
  - Clear minions with Guangzhi transformation when summoned
  - Dodge axe sweep by rolling behind him
- **Cheese**: Keep mid-range to bait his stomp, punish the recovery

### Bull King (Phase 2 · Lava Hall)

- **Mechanics**: Giant form + lava AoE + charge attack
- **Weakness**: Charge crashes into walls, stunning him for seconds
- **Dodge Timing**: Run sideways when he charges, bait him into the wall
- **Combo**:
  - Bait wall charge → during stun, rush in with fully charged heavy + light ×5
  - Stand behind him during lava AoE
  - Repeat wall charge → damage cycle
- **Cheese**: Use safe zones near the lava pools to position and chip

### Yaksha King (Final Boss)

- **Mechanics**: Three-headed six-armed form + flame blade + dark realm
- **Weakness**: In dark realm, watch for the red glow of his eyes to track position
- **Form 1**: Three heads six arms + flame blade flurry
  - Flame blade has huge range, stay behind him for damage
  - Six-arm attacks have a rhythm, dodge the first then reverse dodge the second
- **Form 2**: Dark realm + teleport ambush
  - Keep moving in the dark realm
  - Dodge immediately on seeing red glow (his eyes)
  - Counterattack after the dark dissipates
- **Combo**:
  - Form 1: Circle behind light ×4 → heavy → retreat
  - Form 2: Dodge ambush in darkness → Immobilize + full charge the moment dark dissipates
- **Cheese**: Hug the wall during dark realm to reduce ambush approach angles

### Princess Iron Fan (Hidden Boss)

- **Trigger**: Use the Plantain Fan (found in castle chest) at the deepest part of Fire Cloud Cave
- **Mechanics**: Plantain fan storm + Universe Ring ranged attacks
- **Weakness**: She's invulnerable during the fan storm
- **Dodge Timing**: Hide behind stone pillars when the storm appears
- **Combo**:
  - Hide from storm → wait for her to retract the fan → Immobilize + damage
  - Universe Ring has a trajectory, side dodge is sufficient
- **Cheese**: Use stone pillars to block both the storm and Universe Ring the whole fight

## Tips & Pitfalls

- Lava ground in Flaming Mountain deals continuous damage — watch ground color to tell safe zones
- Flame Demon's fire resistance is crucial — skipping him makes the rest of the chapter painful
- Red Boy's phoenix only has a short window after being immobilized, charge up in advance
- Baiting Bull King into wall charges is THE most important damage opportunity in Phase 2
- Don't panic roll in Yaksha King's dark realm — watch for the red glow
- Grab the Plantain Fan from the castle's second floor even if you skip Princess Iron Fan — it's useful later
- Lava Hall has a hidden checkpoint behind the right-side lava waterfall
