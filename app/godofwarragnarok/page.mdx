## 游戏概述 / Overview

**中文：** 战神：诸神黄昏是2018年《战神》的续作，延续了克雷多斯（Kratos）和他的儿子阿特柔斯（Atreus）在北欧神话世界的冒险。游戏以前作结尾的"芬布尔之冬"（Fimbulwinter）为开端，北欧神话中的"诸神黄昏"（Ragnarök）即将来临。克雷多斯必须面对命运的安排，与阿特柔斯一起穿越九界，对抗阿斯加德众神之王奥丁（Odin）和他的儿子雷神索尔（Thor）。本作在战斗系统、探索机制和叙事深度上均有全面升级，是PS4/PS5时代的里程碑之作。

**English:** God of War Ragnarök is the sequel to the 2018 God of War, continuing the journey of Kratos and his son Atreus through the Nine Realms of Norse mythology. Set against the backdrop of Fimbulwinter, the game builds toward the prophesied Ragnarök. Kratos must grapple with fate while Atreus explores his identity as Loki. Together they must travel across all Nine Realms to stand against Odin, the All-Father, and his son Thor. With enhanced combat mechanics, deeper exploration, and an emotionally resonant story, this title stands as a landmark achievement on PS4 and PS5.

## 故事背景 / Story Background

**中文：** 故事发生在前作事件三年之后。芬布尔之冬笼罩了米德加尔特，预示着诸神黄昏的降临。克雷多斯试图保护阿特柔斯免受命运预言的影响，但随着旅程的展开，阿特柔斯逐渐发现了自己作为"洛基"（Loki）的真实身份。父子二人必须在信任与命运之间找到平衡，同时集结盟友——包括矮人布罗克和辛德里、女巫芙蕾雅、战神提尔等——共同对抗奥丁的暴政。游戏探讨了关于命运、家庭和救赎的深刻主题，结局令人动容。

**English:** Set three years after the 2018 game, Fimbulwinter has engulfed Midgard as a harbinger of Ragnarök. Kratos seeks to protect Atreus from the prophecy of Loki, but Atreus must discover the truth about his identity. Father and son journey across the realms recruiting allies — the Huldra brothers Brok and Sindri, the witch Freya, the god of war Tyr, and more — to stand against Odin's tyranny. The story explores themes of fate, family, and redemption, building to an emotionally powerful conclusion that redefines both characters.

## 游戏特色详解 / Game Features Deep Dive

**中文：** 战神：诸神黄昏在2018年作品的基础上进行了全方位的进化。以下是最值得关注的特色系统：

**动态战斗系统（Dynamic Combat）** — 三种武器加上盾牌、斯巴达之怒和符文攻击，构成了游戏深度的战斗系统。每种武器有超过20种独立技能，并且在战斗中切换武器不会打断连击。武器之间的元素联动（冰冻结+火爆炸）为高段玩家提供了丰富的战术选择。

**双主角叙事（Dual Protagonist Story）** — 本作首次在系列中加入可操控的第二主角章节。玩家可以在特定段落操作阿特柔斯，体验不同的战斗风格和叙事视角。阿特柔斯的战斗以弓箭为主，辅以符文召唤，战斗风格更偏向远程和辅助。

**无障碍辅助功能（Accessibility Features）** — Santa Monica Studio在本作中提供了业界领先的无障碍选项，包括：高对比度模式、字幕大小和背景调整、自动拾取、导航辅助、战斗辅助（自动盾反、自动闪避）等超过60项设置。这使得不同能力的玩家都能享受游戏。

**照片模式（Photo Mode）** — 功能丰富的照片模式允许玩家在战斗中暂停并自由调整镜头、滤镜、角色表情等。九界的壮丽风景配合照片模式可以拍出令人惊叹的作品。

**English:** Ragnarök evolves every system from the 2018 game. Key highlights:

**Dynamic Combat** — Three weapons + shield + Spartan Rage + runic attacks create enormous depth. Each weapon has 20+ unique skills. Weapon swapping mid-combo is seamless. Elemental interactions (freeze then shatter with fire) reward skilled play.

**Dual Protagonist Story** — First in the series to feature playable second protagonist segments. Atreus plays differently — bow-focused with runic summons, emphasizing ranged combat and support.

**Accessibility Features** — Industry-leading accessibility with 60+ settings: high contrast mode, auto-loot, navigation assist, combat assists (auto-parry, auto-dodge). Santa Monica Studio set a new standard for inclusive game design.

**Photo Mode** — Pause mid-combat, adjust camera, filters, character expressions. The Nine Realms' stunning vistas create incredible photo opportunities.

## 开发者手记 / Developer Notes

**中文：** Santa Monica Studio在本作的开发中投入了超过5年时间，团队规模扩大到300人以上。以下是几个有趣的开发幕后：

- **Eric Williams首次担任总监**：本作是Eric Williams首次担任游戏总监（此前2018年作品由Cory Barlog执导）。Williams为本作带来了更快的战斗节奏和更丰富的角色互动。
- **一次性录制完成**：克雷多斯的配音演员Christopher Judge（克里斯托弗·贾奇）和阿特柔斯的配音演员Sunny Suljic（桑尼·苏尔吉克）在新冠疫情期间通过远程方式完成了全部配音录制。Judge在2022年TGA上发表了著名的长篇获奖感言。
- **德劳普尼尔长枪的诞生**：开发团队希望为克雷多斯提供一种全新的战斗方式，于是从北欧神话中的德劳普尼尔戒指获得灵感，创造了这把可以无限复制和引爆的长枪。
- **音乐创作**：Bear McCreary再次负责作曲，使用了北欧传统乐器如nyckelharpa和tagelharpa，创造了独特的北欧音乐风格。

**English:** Santa Monica Studio spent over 5 years developing Ragnarök with a team of 300+. Behind-the-scenes facts:

- **First-time director** — Eric Williams took the helm (Cory Barlog directed 2018's God of War). Williams brought faster combat pacing and richer character interactions.
- **Remote recording** — Christopher Judge (Kratos) and Sunny Suljic (Atreus) recorded all dialogue remotely during COVID-19. Judge delivered his famously long TGA 2022 acceptance speech.
- **Draupnir Spear origin** — The team wanted a fresh combat style. Inspired by the Draupnir ring from Norse myth, they created an infinitely replicating, detonatable spear.
- **Music** — Bear McCreary returned as composer, using Nordic traditional instruments like nyckelharpa and tagelharpa to create the authentic Norse soundscape.

## 最佳体验建议 / Best Experience Tips

**中文：** 以下是一些让游戏体验更佳的小建议：

1. **使用PS5版游玩**：如果条件允许，PS5版提供了更流畅的60fps体验和DualSense的触觉反馈，特别是拉弓和投掷斧头时的自适应扳机反馈极其沉浸。
2. **开启字幕和导航辅助**：游戏的对话内容丰富且有历史背景说明，开启字幕和导航辅助可以更好地理解故事。
3. **不要急于通关**：诸神黄昏的支线任务质量很高，很多支线的剧情深度不亚于主线。适当停下主线进度完成一些支线会让游戏体验更丰富。
4. **音效设置**：推荐使用耳机游玩，游戏的3D音频效果让九界的氛围感大幅提升，战斗中的音效定位也非常精准。
5. **定期整理装备**：不要积压未使用的装备，及时拆解或出售可以积累大量升级材料。

**English:** Best experience tips:

1. **Play on PS5** — 60fps performance mode + DualSense haptics. The adaptive trigger resistance on the bow and axe throw is incredibly immersive.
2. **Enable subtitles + nav assist** — Rich dialogue with lore context. These features help you catch every detail.
3. **Don't rush the story** — Favor quests have main-story-quality writing. Pacing side content between main chapters enriches the experience.
4. **Use headphones** — Tempest 3D Audio makes the Nine Realms feel alive. Combat audio direction is pinpoint accurate.
5. **Manage inventory regularly** — Salvage or sell unwanted gear. Don't let upgrade materials sit idle in unused equipment.
