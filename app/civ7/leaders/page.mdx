# 领袖与文明 / Leaders & Civilizations

文明7中领袖和文明分离——你可以自由搭配，创造独一无二的组合。领袖决定你的策略倾向，文明决定你的单位/建筑/特性。合理搭配可以产生强大的协同效应 / In Civ VII, leaders and civilizations are separate — freely mix and match for unique combos. Leaders determine strategic inclination, civs determine units/buildings/traits. Smart combos create powerful synergies.

### 领袖详细策略 / Leader Strategy Notes

| 领袖 / Leader | 核心打法 / Core Strategy |
|---|---|
| **图拉真 / Trajan** | 免费纪念碑=每个新城立刻+2文化——开局爆铺的最佳领袖。古典时代4-5城起步。早期政策槽优先塞+文化/+生产力的卡 / Free monuments = instant culture in every new city — best leader for early expansion. 4-5 cities in Antiquity. Early policy cards: prioritize culture and production |
| **克利奥帕特拉 / Cleopatra** | 沿河建城——泛滥平原高食物+生产力让首都迅速发展。贸易路线多连城邦和AI经济强的文明。探索时代转阿拔斯后科技爆发 / Settle along rivers — flood plains give fast capital growth. Trade routes to city-states and rich civs. Exploration: pivot to Abbasid for science burst |
| **秦始皇 / Qin Shi Huang** | 抢奇观的关键是科技走先——先解锁奇观需要的科技再建造。古典时代必抢: 大金字塔、神谕。15%加成的奇观优势在他手上更明显 / Key to wonders: unlock required techs first. Must-builds: Great Pyramids, Oracle. The 15% bonus is even stronger on high-difficulty |
| **蒙特祖玛 / Montezuma** | 奢侈资源越多越强——开局探路找奢侈→开发→出战士。每多1种奢侈=全单位+2战斗力——到中期攒8种奢侈=全单位+16战斗力 / More luxuries = stronger. Scout for luxuries early → improve → build warriors. 8 luxury types = +16 combat to ALL units |
