## 游戏概述 / Game Overview

**异星工厂 (Factorio)** 是由 Wube Software 开发的自动化沙盒游戏。你扮演一名坠落在外星球的工程师，必须开采资源、建立工厂、研究科技，最终发射火箭逃离。游戏的核心循环是：开采 → 加工 → 组装 → 科研 → 扩张。

Factorio is an automation sandbox game developed by Wube Software. You play as an engineer stranded on an alien planet who must mine resources, build factories, research technologies, and ultimately launch a rocket to escape.

## 核心特色 / Core Features

### 自动化是最终目标 / Automation Is the Goal
每一件物品都可以由机器生产。手动操作只是暂时的，你的目标是用机器代替双手，再用机器生产机器。

Every item can be produced by machines. Handcrafting is temporary — your goal is to let machines build machines.

### 无限世界 / Infinite World
地图是程序生成的，无边无际。资源会耗尽，你必须不断向外扩张。

The map is procedurally generated and infinite. Resources deplete, forcing you to expand outward.

### 敌人与污染 / Enemies and Pollution
你的一切生产活动都会产生污染。污染会激怒外星虫群，它们会发动攻击。你必须平衡发展与防御。

Every production activity generates pollution. Pollution angers the native alien biters, who will attack. You must balance growth with defense.

### 模块化设计 / Modular Design
从传送带到铁路，从电路网络到机器人物流，游戏提供了丰富的工具让你设计优雅的自动化系统。

From belts to trains, from circuit networks to logistic bots, the game provides rich tools for elegant automation design.

## Space Age DLC / 太空时代扩展

2024年发布的太空时代 (Space Age) DLC 将游戏扩展至多个星球。每个星球有独特的资源和挑战：

Released in 2024, the Space Age DLC expands the game to multiple planets. Each planet has unique resources and challenges:

- **纳鲁维斯 (Nauvis)** — 起始星球，基础资源 / Starting planet, basic resources
- **伏尔卡努斯 (Vulcanus)** — 火山世界，熔岩金属 / Volcanic world, lava metals
- **福尔格里 (Fulgora)** — 闪电风暴，废弃城市 / Lightning storms, ruins
- **格莱巴 (Gleba)** — 真菌沼泽，生物产物 / Fungal swamp, biological products
- **阿奎洛 (Aquilo)** — 冰封冻原，冷冻科技 / Frozen tundra, cryogenics
- **破口 (Shattered Planet)** — 终局挑战 / Endgame challenge

## 为何选择 Factorio / Why Factorio

Factorio 连续多年在 Steam 上获得"好评如潮"评价。它被广泛认为是自动化游戏类型的巅峰之作，拥有极其活跃的模组社区和定期的免费内容更新。

Factorio has maintained an "Overwhelmingly Positive" rating on Steam for years. It is widely considered the pinnacle of the automation genre, with a highly active modding community and regular free content updates.
