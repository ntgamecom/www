## 护符图鉴 / Charm Compendium

护符(Charms)是《空洞骑士》中最重要的自定义系统之一。通过在长凳上装备不同护符，你可以改变角色的能力、增强属性或解锁新技能。本页面详细介绍游戏中所有护符的信息。

Charms are one of the most important customization systems in Hollow Knight. By equipping different charms at a bench, you can change your character's abilities, enhance attributes, or unlock new skills. This page details information on all charms in the game.

## 护符槽位扩展 / Charm Notch Expansion

游戏中共有 11 个护符槽位可以获得:

There are 11 charm notches to acquire:

1. 初始自带 / Start with (3 槽 / notches)
2. 萨鲁布拉购买 x4 / Purchase from Salubra x4 (共消费约 2300 Geo)
3. 击败收藏家 / Defeat the Collector (1 槽 / notch)
4. 击败梦境 Boss x2 / Defeat Dream Bosses x2 (2 槽 / notches)
5. 格里姆 DLC 任务 / Grimm Troupe quest (1 槽 / notch)

每个护符的组合都会带来完全不同的游戏体验。大胆尝试不同的搭配，找到最适合你的风格！

Every charm combination creates a completely different gameplay experience. Experiment with different builds to find the style that suits you best!
