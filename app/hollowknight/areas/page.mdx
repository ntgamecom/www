## 区域探索 / Area Exploration

圣巢(Hallownest)是一个庞大而相连的地下王国，包含超过 10 个风格迥异的区域。每个区域都有独特的敌人、环境和秘密等待被发现。本页面将详细介绍每个区域的特点、关键物品和连接路线。

Hallownest is a vast and interconnected underground kingdom with over 10 distinct areas. Each area features unique enemies, environments, and secrets waiting to be discovered. This page details the characteristics, key items, and connection routes for every area.

## 遗忘十字路 / Forgotten Crossroads

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

遗忘十字路是游戏的起点区域，色调偏暗，以棕色和灰色为主。这里曾经是圣巢的交通枢纽，现在破败不堪，被感染生物占据。

Forgotten Crossroads is the game's starting area, with dark tones dominated by brown and gray. Once a transportation hub for Hallownest, it is now dilapidated and overrun with infected creatures.

**关键地点 / Key Locations**:

| 地点 / Location | 说明 / Description |
|---|---|
| 德里茅斯入口 / Dirtmouth Entrance | 连接地上世界与地下圣巢的入口 / Connects surface world to underground Hallownest |
| 鹿角站 / Stag Station | 可解锁快速旅行 / Unlockable fast travel point |
| 萨鲁布拉的商店 / Salubra's Shop | 出售护符和护符槽 / Sells charms and charm notches |
| 黑卵神殿 / Temple of the Black Egg | 游戏最终区域 / Endgame area |
| 假骑士竞技场 / False Knight Arena | 首次 Boss 战地点 / First boss battle location |

**关键物品 / Key Items**:

- 城市徽章 (City Crest) — 击败假骑士获得 / Defeated from False Knight — 打开泪水之城大门 / Opens the City of Tears gate
- 灯笼 (Lumafly Lantern) — 在德里茅斯购买 / Purchased in Dirtmouth — 照亮黑暗区域 / Lights up dark areas
- 地图和羽毛笔 (Map and Quill) — 从 Cornifer 购买 / Purchased from Cornifer

## 苍绿之径 / Greenpath

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

当你从遗忘十字路右侧进入苍绿之径时，整个世界突然充满了生机。绿意盎然的植被、蜿蜒的河流和优美的背景音乐让这里成为游戏中最令人难忘的区域之一。

When you enter Greenpath from the right side of Forgotten Crossroads, the world suddenly bursts with life. Lush vegetation, winding rivers, and beautiful background music make this one of the most memorable areas in the game.

**主要特性 / Main Features**:

- 植被茂密，部分可以攀爬 / Dense vegetation, some climbable
- 首个主线上锁门 / First story-locked door encountered
- 遇到 Hornet 的第一次战斗 / First encounter and battle with Hornet
- 获得蛾翼披风 (Mothwing Cloak) 冲刺能力 / Acquire Mothwing Cloak dash ability

**连接区域 / Connected Areas**:

遗忘十字路 (Forgotten Crossroads) → 苍绿之径 (Greenpath) → 真菌荒地 (Fungal Wastes) via 雾之峡谷 (Fog Canyon)

## 真菌荒地 / Fungal Wastes

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

真菌荒地以紫色和蓝色的真菌群落为主，地面上布满了会弹跳的大型蘑菇。这里的跳跃机制非常独特——在特定蘑菇上按下跳跃键可以弹得很高。

Fungal Wastes is dominated by purple and blue fungal colonies, with large bouncing mushrooms covering the ground. The jumping mechanic here is unique — pressing jump on specific mushrooms launches you high into the air.

**关键地点 / Key Locations**:

| 地点 / Location | 说明 / Description |
|---|---|
| 螳螂村 / Mantis Village | 螳螂族的家园，可获得螳螂爪 / Home of the Mantis tribe, obtain Mantis Claw |
| 智者之泪 / Leg Eater | 出售易碎护符 / Sells fragile charms |
| 女王驿站 / Queen's Station | 鹿角站和快速旅行点 / Stag Station and fast travel point |
| 石之庇护所 / Stone Sanctuary | 包含隐藏护符 / Contains hidden charm |

**特殊机制 / Special Mechanics**:

- 蘑菇弹跳 (Mushroom Bounce): 在大蘑菇上按下跳跃键可以弹到高台
- 螳螂族外交 (Mantis Diplomacy): 击败螳螂三领主后螳螂族会变为友好

## 泪水之城 / City of Tears

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

泪水之城是圣巢最大的核心区域，一座永恒的雨水之都。这里曾经是圣巢最繁华的城市，现在只剩下无尽的雨声和游荡的感染者。

The City of Tears is the largest central area of Hallownest, an eternal city of rain. Once the most prosperous city in Hallownest, now only endless rain and wandering infected remain.

**关键地点 / Key Locations**:

- 灵魂圣殿 (Soul Sanctum) — 获得复仇之魂升级 / Obtain Vengeful Spirit upgrade — 击败灵魂大师 / Defeat Soul Master
- 守望者尖塔 (Watcher's Spire) — 梦境者之一的地点 / Location of one of the Dreamers
- 鹿角总站 (Stag Station Hub) — 最大枢纽站 / Main hub station
- 纪念广场 (Memorial Plaza) — 著名地标 / Famous landmark

**关键物品 / Key Items**:

- 庄严之刃 (Channeled Nail) — 铁匠升级 / Nail upgrade from Nailsmith
- 地图 (Map) — Cornifer 在喷泉广场附近 / Cornifer near Fountain Plaza
- 苍白矿石 (Pale Ore) — 可在多个地点找到 / Found in multiple locations

## 皇家水道 / Royal Waterways

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

皇家水道是泪水之城下方的下水道系统，充满了水和有毒物质。获得伊斯玛的眼泪(Isma's Tear)前部分水域无法通过。

The Royal Waterways are the sewer system beneath the City of Tears, filled with water and toxic substances. Some water areas cannot be crossed before acquiring Isma's Tear.

**关键物品 / Key Items**:

- 伊斯玛的眼泪 (Isma's Tear) — 获得穿越酸水能力 / Grants acid water traversal
- 防御者纹章 (Defender's Crest) — 击败粪虫防御者 / Defeated from Dung Defender
- 生命血核心 (Lifeblood Core) — 隐藏房间内 / In a hidden room

## 深邃巢穴 / Deepnest

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

深邃巢穴是游戏中最为恐怖和压抑的区域。这里几乎完全黑暗，充斥着蜘蛛和虫类敌人。脚步声和背景营造出强烈的心理恐怖氛围。

Deepnest is the most terrifying and oppressive area in the game. Almost completely dark, it is filled with spider and insect enemies. The sound effects and background create a strong psychological horror atmosphere.

**关键地点 / Key Locations**:

- 遥远的村庄 (Distant Village) — 梦之钉升级地点 / Dream Nail upgrade location
- 野兽巢穴 (Beast's Den) — 梦境者之一赫拉所在地 / Location of Dreamer Herrah
- 螳螂村入口 (Mantis Village Entrance) — 从真菌荒地可通往 / Connected from Fungal Wastes

**提示 / Tips**:

- 强烈建议携带灯笼(强烈亮光护符更好) / Strongly recommend carrying the Lantern (Glowing Womb charm helps)
- 地面会塌陷，掉落至下层区域 / Floors collapse, dropping you to lower areas
- 部分通道非常狭窄 / Some passages are very narrow
- 被部分敌人抓住后会传送到其他地点 / Certain enemies can grab and teleport you

## 王国边缘 / Kingdom's Edge

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

王国边缘位于圣巢最东侧，是一个充满巨大骨骼和废墟的区域。这里下方是无尽的深渊，需要一定的技术才能安全探索。

Kingdom's Edge is located at the easternmost part of Hallownest, an area filled with massive bones and ruins. Below lies an endless abyss that requires skill to safely navigate.

**关键地点 / Key Locations**:

- 骨钉大师奥罗 (Nailsmith Oro) — 第三次骨钉升级 / Third Nail upgrade
- 柯尼法 (Colosseum of Fools) — 战斗挑战竞技场 / Combat challenge arena
- 深渊入口 (Abyss Entrance) — 需要国王碎片打开 / Requires King's Brand to open

## 王后花园 / Queen's Gardens

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

王后花园是苍绿之径的进阶版，更加复杂和危险。这里曾经是蜂巢女王和王后的居所。

Queen's Gardens is an advanced version of Greenpath, more complex and dangerous. It was once the residence of the Bee Queen and the White Lady.

**关键物品 / Key Items**:

- 爱之钥 (Love Key) — 打开特定门 / Opens a specific door
- 蜂巢之血 (Hiveblood) — 击败蜂巢骑士 / Defeated from Hive Knight
- 白夫人的束缚 (White Lady's Binding) — 获得国王之魄的一半 / Get half of Kingsoul

## 水晶山峰 / Crystal Peak

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

水晶山峰覆盖着发光的水晶，是游戏中视觉最震撼的区域之一。这里曾是采矿场，现在充满了水晶化生物。

Crystal Peak is covered in glowing crystals, one of the most visually stunning areas. Once a mining site, it is now filled with crystallized creatures.

**关键物品 / Key Items**:

- 水晶之心 (Crystal Heart) — 超级冲刺能力 / Super dash ability
- 水晶守卫 (Crystal Guard) — 护符 / Charm
- 苍白矿石 (Pale Ore) — 水晶山峰区域 / In Crystal Peak area

## 深渊 / The Abyss

### 区域概况 / Area Overview

深渊是圣巢最底部的区域，也是所有容器的诞生地。这里气氛压抑，充满了死亡之翼和虚空生物。

The Abyss is the lowest area of Hallownest, the birthplace of all Vessels. The atmosphere is oppressive, filled with Winged Death and Void creatures.

**关键物品 / Key Items**:

- 虚空圣器 (Void Heart) — 结合国王之魄和虚空之心 / Unifies Kingsoul and Void Heart
- 暗影披风 (Shade Cloak) — 冲刺升级 / Dash upgrade
- 生命血核心 (Lifeblood Core) — 可在此升级 / Upgradable here

## 探索技巧 / Exploration Tips

1. 随身携带简单钥匙以备不时之需 / Carry a Simple Key at all times
2. 每个区域都寻找 Cornifer 购买地图 / Find Cornifer in each area for maps
3. 记录暂无法打开的门和通道 / Note doors and passages you cannot yet open
4. 获得新能力后回顾旧区域 / Revisit old areas after acquiring new abilities
5. 使用梦之钉读取 NPC 和 Boss 的内心 / Use Dream Nail to read minds of NPCs and bosses
6. 部分区域需要后期能力才能 100% 探索 / Some areas require late-game abilities for 100% completion

圣巢的每一个角落都在讲述故事。探索本身就是一种奖励。不要急于推进主线，花时间去感受这个王国曾经的辉煌与悲壮。

Every corner of Hallownest tells a story. Exploration itself is a reward. Do not rush through the main quest — take time to feel the former glory and tragedy of this kingdom.
