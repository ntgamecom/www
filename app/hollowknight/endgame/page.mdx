## 终局内容 / Endgame Content

当你击败了辐光、看完了制作人员名单，圣巢的冒险并未就此结束。《空洞骑士》拥有丰富的终局内容，包括三个大型 DLC、高难度挑战和 112% 完成度目标。本页面将带你了解所有可挑战的内容。

After you defeat The Radiance and watch the credits roll, the adventure in Hallownest is not over. Hollow Knight features extensive endgame content, including three major DLCs, high-difficulty challenges, and the 112% completion target. This page guides you through all the challenges available.

## 寻神者 DLC / Godmaster DLC

### 概述 / Overview

寻神者是《空洞骑士》的第三个也是最大的免费 DLC，于 2018 年 8 月 23 日发布。它增加了全新的 Boss 连续挑战模式——诸神殿(Pantheon)，以及新的 NPC、护符和结局。

Godmaster is the third and largest free DLC for Hollow Knight, released on August 23, 2018. It adds a brand new boss rush mode — the Pantheons — along with new NPCs, charms, and endings.

### 进入方式 / How to Enter

在皇家水道的垃圾坑(Junk Pit)中找到寻神者(Godseeker)的尸体，使用梦之钉进入其梦境，即可开启寻神者内容。随后在德里茅斯附近会出现全新的神殿。

Find the Godseeker's corpse in the Junk Pit in Royal Waterways, use the Dream Nail to enter their dream, unlocking the Godmaster content. A new temple will appear near Dirtmouth.

### 诸神殿系统 / Pantheon System

诸神殿是连续的 Boss 挑战，你在其中必须打倒一系列 Boss 而中间不能休息。每个 Boss 之间有短暂的恢复时间，可以通过生命血茧补充状态。

The Pantheons are consecutive boss challenges where you must defeat a series of bosses without resting. Brief recovery periods between bosses allow you to refill health using Lifeblood cocoons.

| 诸神殿 / Pantheon | 解锁条件 / Unlock Condition | Boss 数量 / Boss Count | 最终 Boss / Final Boss |
|---|---|---|---|
| 大师神殿 / Pantheon of the Master | 初始可用 / Available from start | 10 | 变形兄弟 / Brothers Oro and Mato |
| 艺术家神殿 / Pantheon of the Artist | 通过大师神殿 / Clear Master | 14 | 乌姆 / Uumuu |
| 贤者神殿 / Pantheon of the Sage | 通过艺术家神殿 / Clear Artist | 18 | 泽莫 / Sly |
| 骑士神殿 / Pantheon of the Knight | 通过贤者神殿 / Clear Sage | 22 | 纯粹容器 / Pure Vessel |
| 万神殿 / Pantheon of Hallownest | 通过骑士神殿 / Clear Knight | 42 | 绝对辐光 / Absolute Radiance |

### 束缚系统 / Binding System

每个诸神殿可以附加"束缚"(Binding)来增加难度。束缚会让你的某些能力受限，但同时会提高完成度计数。

Each Pantheon can be attempted with Bindings to increase difficulty. Bindings restrict certain abilities but increase completion percentage.

**四种束缚 / Four Bindings**:

| 束缚 / Binding | 效果 / Effect |
|---|---|
| 护符束缚 / Charm Binding | 禁用所有护符 / Disables all charms |
| 灵魂束缚 / Soul Binding | 灵魂容量减半 / Half Soul capacity |
| 面具束缚 / Mask Binding | 生命上限减为 4 格 / Max health reduced to 4 |
| 骨钉束缚 / Nail Binding | 骨钉伤害降为 13 / Nail damage reduced to 13 |

寻神者 DLC 中增加了数个全新的或改版的 Boss：

Several new or remixed bosses were added in the Godmaster DLC:

| Boss | 描述 / Description |

| 纯粹容器 / Pure Vessel | 空洞骑士的纯净版，更快更强 / Pure form of the Hollow Knight, faster and stronger |
| 梦魇之王格林 / Nightmare King Grimm | 格林之子的完全形态 / Complete form of Grimm |
| 变形兄弟 / Sisters of Battle | 三螳螂领主同时攻击 / All three Mantis Lords simultaneously |
| 绝对辐光 / Absolute Radiance | 辐光的最终形态，最高难度 / Final form of the Radiance, hardest difficulty |
| 编织者万神殿 / Winged Zote | 左特的万神殿版本 / Zote's Pantheon variant |

### 寻神者结局 / Godseeker Endings

完成诸神殿后可以解锁两个新结局：

Two new endings unlock after completing the Pantheons:

- **拥抱虚空 (Embrace the Void)**: 完成万神殿后，小骑士释放虚空吞噬一切 / After completing the Pantheon of Hallownest, the Knight releases the Void to consume everything
- **虚空共情 (Void Given Focus)**: 完成全部约束的万神殿后解锁的额外结局 / Additional ending unlocked after completing all Bound Pantheons

## 格林剧团 DLC / Grimm Troupe DLC

### 概述 / Overview

格林剧团是第一个免费 DLC，于 2017 年 10 月 26 日发布。它增加了全新的支线任务线、Boss、护符和格林剧团主题内容。

The Grimm Troupe is the first free DLC, released on October 26, 2017. It adds a brand new side quest line, bosses, charms, and Grimm Troupe themed content.

### 启动方式 / How to Start

在呼啸悬崖(Howling Cliffs)的一个隐藏区域中找到格林剧团的灯笼，回到德里茅斯后会出现格林(Divine)和剧团帐篷。

Find the Grimm Troupe lantern in a hidden area in Howling Cliffs. Returning to Dirtmouth will reveal Grimm (Divine) and the troupe tent.

### 格林剧团任务线 / Grimm Troupe Questline

1. 在德里茅斯点燃剧团灯笼 / Light the troupe lantern in Dirtmouth
2. 与格林对话接受任务 / Talk to Grimm and accept the quest
3. 收集火焰精华 / Collect flame essence
4. 挑战格林 / Battle Grimm
5. 选择：完成仪式或放逐剧团 / Choose: complete the ritual or banish the troupe

### 格林之子的升级 / Grimmchild Upgrade

格林之子护符可以通过任务升级，共 4 个阶段：

The Grimmchild charm can be upgraded through the quest, in 4 stages:

| 阶段 / Stage | 效果 / Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | 发射小火球攻击 / Shoots small fireballs |
| 2 | 火球变大 / Larger fireballs |
| 3 | 发射持续性火焰 / Continuous flame attack |
| 4 | 变身为梦魇之王协助战斗 / Transforms into Nightmare King for attacks |

## 隐秘之梦 DLC / Hidden Dreams DLC

### 概述 / Overview

隐秘之梦是第二个免费 DLC，于 2017 年 8 月 3 日发布。它增加了两个新的梦境 Boss 和新的快速旅行能力。

Hidden Dreams is the second free DLC, released on August 3, 2017. It adds two new Dream Bosses and a new fast travel ability.

### 新增内容 / New Content

**梦境之门升级 / Dreamgate Upgrade**:
在收集 800 梦境精华后从梦境揭示者处获得。允许在任意位置放置传送点，方便快速返回。

Obtained from the Seer after collecting 800 Essence. Allows placing a teleport point anywhere for quick return travel.

**新增 Boss / New Bosses**:

| Boss / Boss | 位置 / Location | 描述 / Description |
|---|---|---|
| 灰色王子左特 / Grey Prince Zote | 德里茅斯 — 左特帐篷 / Dirtmouth — Zote's Tent | 左特的梦境版本，可反复挑战共 10 级 / Zote's dream version, 10 tiers |
| 白色防御者 / White Defender | 皇家水道 / Royal Waterways | 防御者粪虫的梦境版本，可反复挑战共 5 级 / Dung Defender's dream version, 5 tiers |

## 愚人斗兽场 / Colosseum of Fools

### 概述 / Overview

愚人斗兽场位于王国边缘(Kingdom's Edge)，是一个战斗竞技场，分为三波试炼。这是游戏中考验战斗技能的最佳场所之一。

The Colosseum of Fools is a combat arena located in Kingdom's Edge, divided into three waves of trials. It is one of the best places in the game to test your combat skills.

| 试炼 / Trial | 波数 / Waves | 奖励 / Reward |
|---|---|---|
| 愚人试炼 1 / Trial of the Fool 1 | 4 波 / 4 Waves | 150 Geo |
| 愚人试炼 2 / Trial of the Fool 2 | 11 波 / 11 Waves | 苍白矿石 / Pale Ore |
| 愚人试炼 3 / Trial of the Fool 3 | 16 波 / 16 Waves | 2600 Geo + 面具碎片 / Mask Shard |

### 试炼攻略 / Trial Strategies

**试炼 1 攻略 / Trial 1 Strategy**:
- 最简单的试炼，主要测试基础战斗能力 / Easiest trial, mainly tests basic combat
- 注意飞行敌人和地面敌人交替出场 / Watch for alternating flying and ground enemies
- 保持移动，不要被包围 / Keep moving, do not get surrounded

**试炼 2 攻略 / Trial 2 Strategy**:
- 中期难度，需要一定的护符搭配 / Medium difficulty, requires decent charm setup
- 推荐使用萨满之石和法术增强护符 / Recommend Shaman Stone and spell-boosting charms
- 部分波次需要快速的清场能力 / Some waves require fast crowd clearing
- 灵魂管理至关重要 / Soul management is critical

**试炼 3 攻略 / Trial 3 Strategy**:
- 最难的试炼，多波次中包含尖刺和深坑地形 / Hardest trial, includes spikes and pit terrain
- 需要熟练的钉跳技巧 / Requires proficient nail pogo skills
- 强烈推荐快速劈砍和力量护符 / Strongly recommend Quick Slash and Strength charm
- 注意没有地面的飞行波次 / Watch for no-floor flying waves
- 死亡之梯队(Death Lobby)是最大难点 / Death Lobby section is the hardest part

## 钢铁之魂模式 / Steel Soul Mode

### 概述 / Overview

在完成一次游戏后解锁的硬核模式。核心规则：只有一条命，死亡即游戏结束（进度删除）。

Hardcore mode unlocked after completing the game once. Core rule: only one life, death means game over (save deleted).

**特点 / Features**:

- 所有 Boss 和敌人伤害翻倍 / All boss and enemy damage doubled
- 死亡后回到主菜单，存档被标记为已死亡 / Return to main menu on death, save marked as dead
- 在德里茅斯可以查看死亡次数 / Can check death count in Dirtmouth
- 必须在整个游戏过程中活下来才能获得钢铁之魂成就 / Must survive entire playthrough for Steel Soul achievement

**钢铁之魂攻略 / Steel Soul Tips**:

1. 优先升级生命值——在早期购买面具碎片 / Prioritize health upgrades — buy Mask Shards early
2. 保持足够 Geo 以防万一 / Keep sufficient Geo reserve
3. 在未知区域时格外谨慎 / Be extra cautious in unknown areas
4. 萨鲁布拉的护符槽每个都很关键 / Each charm notch from Salubra is critical
5. 远程法术是你最好的朋友——保持距离 / Ranged spells are your best friend — maintain distance
6. 避开深邃巢穴直到装备齐全 / Avoid Deepnest until fully equipped
7. 白色宫殿的梦境平台挑战非常危险 / White Palace dream platforming is very dangerous
8. 如果接近死亡，立即回到长凳 / If near death, immediately return to a bench

### 钢铁之魂奖励 / Steel Soul Rewards

完成钢铁之魂模式后，你可以与德里茅斯的某个 NPC 对话获得完成度验证。通关后可以在同一存档上继续探索（但死亡依然会结束游戏）。

After completing Steel Soul, you can talk to a certain NPC in Dirtmouth for completion verification. After beating the game, you can continue exploring on the same save (but death still ends the run).

完成 100% 钢铁之魂可以在德里茅斯获得额外的金色雕像作为奖励。

Completing Steel Soul at 100% grants an additional golden statue in Dirtmouth as a reward.
