## 欢迎来到空洞骑士 / Welcome to Hollow Knight

《空洞骑士》(Hollow Knight) 是由澳大利亚独立游戏团队 Team Cherry 开发的一款手绘风格银河恶魔城(Metroidvania)动作冒险游戏。游戏于 2017 年 2 月 24 日登陆 PC 平台，随后陆续登陆 Nintendo Switch、PlayStation 4、Xbox One 等平台。凭借其精美的手绘美术、深邃的世界观、富有挑战性的战斗和庞大的地图，游戏获得了极高的评价，被誉为独立游戏史上最杰出的作品之一。

Hollow Knight is a hand-drawn Metroidvania action-adventure game developed by the Australian independent studio Team Cherry. Released on February 24, 2017 for PC and later for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One, it has received widespread acclaim for its beautiful hand-drawn art, deep lore, challenging combat, and expansive world.

## 游戏特色 / Key Features

### 手绘美术风格 / Hand-Drawn Art Style
每一帧画面都由开发者精心手绘完成，色彩丰富且风格独特。角色、场景和敌人都有着生动的动画和独特的设计语言。游戏中的圣巢(Hallownest)世界充满了神秘感和探索欲望。

Every frame is meticulously hand-drawn by the developers, featuring rich colors and a unique art style. Characters, scenes, and enemies all have vivid animations and distinctive design language. The world of Hallownest is filled with mystery and the desire to explore.

### 广阔互联的地图 / Expansive Interconnected Map
游戏包含超过 10 个风格迥异的区域，通过升降梯、捷径、交通工具(如鹿角站)和隐藏通道紧密连接。地图设计遵循标准的银河恶魔城理念——你会经常遇到暂时无法到达的区域，需要后期获得能力后才能返回探索。

The game features over 10 distinct areas tightly connected by elevators, shortcuts, transport systems (Stag Stations), and hidden passages. The map design follows classic Metroidvania principles — you will frequently encounter areas that are temporarily unreachable until you acquire later abilities.

### 富有挑战性的战斗 / Challenging Combat
战斗系统以骨钉(Nail)攻击为核心，辅以灵魂法术(Soul Spells)、护符(Charms)搭配和多种移动技巧。Boss 战设计精妙，每个 Boss 都有独特的攻击模式和节奏，需要玩家学习和掌握。

The combat system centers around Nail attacks, supplemented by Soul Spells, Charm builds, and various movement techniques. Boss battles are brilliantly designed, each with unique attack patterns and rhythms that require learning and mastery.

### 深邃的剧情叙事 / Deep Narrative Storytelling
游戏采用碎片化叙事方式，通过 NPC 对话、背景场景、物品描述和梦境对话等元素逐步揭示圣巢的历史。主线讲述了一个陨落王国、一种肆虐的瘟疫。故事背景宏大而忧伤。

The game employs fragmented storytelling, gradually revealing Hallownest's history through NPC dialogues, background scenes, item descriptions, and dream dialogues. The main story tells of a fallen kingdom and a ravaging plague. The narrative backdrop is grand and melancholic.
