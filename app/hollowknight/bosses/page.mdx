## BOSS攻略 / Boss Strategy Guide

《空洞骑士》以其极具挑战性和设计精良的 Boss 战闻名。每个 Boss 都有独特的攻击模式和节奏感。本页面提供全部 Boss 的详细攻略，包括推荐护符和分阶段策略。

Hollow Knight is renowned for its challenging and well-designed boss battles. Each boss has unique attack patterns and rhythm. This page provides detailed strategies for all bosses, including recommended charms and phase-by-phase tactics.

## 主线 Boss / Main Story Bosses

### 假骑士 / False Knight

**位置 / Location**: 遗忘十字路 — 竞技场 / Forgotten Crossroads — Arena
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 灵魂捕手 / Soul Catcher, 幼虫之歌 / Grubsong

假骑士是玩家的第一个 Boss 战。它主要使用巨槌进行大范围砸击，间歇性地跳跃和撞击天花板掉落岩石。

False Knight is the player's first boss battle. It primarily uses a massive hammer for wide slams, intermittently jumping and head-butting the ceiling to drop rocks.

**阶段 1 策略 / Phase 1 Strategy**:
- 保持中距离，引诱它使用砸地攻击 / Maintain mid-range, bait its slam attack
- 砸地后头部会显现弱点，这是攻击窗口 / Head exposes as a weak point after slam — attack window
- 打够伤害后 Boss 会倒地，猛烈攻击 / Boss topples after enough damage — attack fiercely
- 注意躲避天花板掉落的岩石 / Dodge falling rocks from the ceiling

**阶段 2 策略 / Phase 2 Strategy**:
- Boss 会更频繁地跳跃和砸击 / Boss jumps and slams more frequently
- 墙壁会破碎出现新逃跑路线 / Walls break to reveal escape routes
- 战斗节奏加快，保持冷静 / Battle tempo increases — stay calm

### 大黄蜂 (第一次) / Hornet (First Encounter)

**位置 / Location**: 苍绿之径 / Greenpath
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 稳定之体 / Steady Body, 长钉 / Longnail

大黄蜂是小骑士的第一个真正的技巧考验。快速敏捷，使用针和线进行攻击。

Hornet is the little knight's first true skill test. Fast and agile, she attacks with a needle and thread.

**策略 / Strategy**:
- 她会在房间不断跳跃移动 / She constantly jumps around the arena
- 针投掷可以预判并躲避 / Needle throw can be predicted and dodged
- 线阵攻击时蹲下或冲刺躲避 / Dodge downward by crouching or dashing during thread trap
- 攻击窗口在她攻击后的短暂停顿 / Attack window is the brief pause after her attacks
- 不要过于激进 — 等待她的攻击露出破绽 / Do not be too aggressive — wait for her attacks to leave openings

### 灵魂大师 / Soul Master

**位置 / Location**: 泪水之城 — 灵魂圣殿 / City of Tears — Soul Sanctum
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 快速聚焦 / Quick Focus, 冲刺大师 / Dashmaster

灵魂大师是很多新手面临的第一道难关。它拥有多种法术攻击和传送能力。

Soul Master is many beginners' first major hurdle. It has multiple spell attacks and teleportation abilities.

**阶段 1 策略 / Phase 1 Strategy**:
- 灵魂球弹幕可以冲刺躲避 / Soul orb barrage can be dashed through
- 地面冲击波需要跳跃躲避 / Ground shockwave requires jumping to dodge
- 传送后迅速定位并侧面攻击 / Quickly locate after teleport and attack from the side
- 不要站在正下方 — 会被下坠攻击命中 / Do not stand directly below — hit by drop attack

**阶段 2 策略 / Phase 2 Strategy**:
- Boss 变为幻影进行连续冲刺攻击 / Boss becomes phantom for continuous dash attacks
- 在房间中央等待冲刺后反击 / Wait in center and counter after each dash
- 幻影冲刺有固定节奏可以预判 / Phantom dashes have fixed rhythm to predict

### 守望者骑士 / Watcher Knights

**位置 / Location**: 泪水之城 — 守望者尖塔 / City of Tears — Watcher's Spire
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 快速劈砍 / Quick Slash, 骄傲印记 / Mark of Pride, 力量护符 / Fragile Strength

6 个装甲骑士的连续战斗，通常 2 个同时上场。极具挑战性。

A consecutive battle against 6 armored knights, with usually 2 active at once. Highly challenging.

**策略 / Strategy**:
- 跳斩为主要攻击方式，跳跃躲避 / Jump slash is their main attack — dodge by jumping
- 滚动攻击时可以下劈弹跳 / Can pogo bounce during their rolling attack
- 拉开距离逐个击破 / Create distance and take them one by one
- 利用上方绳子使一个骑士提前掉下消灭 / Use the chandelier to kill one knight early
- 护盾可以通过下劈破防（但非常困难） / Shield can be broken by pogo (very difficult)
- 这场战斗是 DPS 测试——攻击力越高越好 / This is a DPS check — higher attack is better

## 梦境 Boss / Dream Bosses

### 失败冠军 / Failed Champion

**位置 / Location**: 假骑士竞技场（梦境形态）/ False Knight Arena (Dream Variant)
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 快速劈砍 / Quick Slash, 力量护符 / Unbreakable Strength

假骑士的强化版，伤害更高，节奏更快。

Enhanced version of False Knight, higher damage, faster pace.

**策略 / Strategy**:
- 基本模式与假骑士相同，但各阶段切换更快 / Same patterns as False Knight but transitions are faster
- 砸地后梦境能量会追踪你 / Dream energy tracks you after slam
- 攻击窗口缩短，需要更精准的输出 / Attack windows are shorter — need precise output
- 建议使用法术来安全输出 / Recommend using spells for safe damage

### 灰色王子左特 / Grey Prince Zote

**位置 / Location**: 德里茅斯 — 左特的帐篷 / Dirtmouth — Zote's Tent
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 快速聚焦 / Quick Focus, 萨满之石 / Shaman Stone

可多次挑战的强化 Boss，每一次都更难。共有 10 个等级。

A rematchable boss that gets harder each time. Has 10 tiers.

**策略 / Strategy**:
- 地面冲刺可跳起下劈 / Ground dash can be avoided by jumping and pogoing
- 空中冲击波在落地前有提前轨迹 / Aerial shockwave has a preview before landing
- 召唤的小左特优先清理 / Prioritize clearing summoned Zotelings
- 更高等级时幻影左特会加入战斗 / Phantom Zote joins at higher tiers
- 注意地面的爆炸延迟 / Watch for delayed ground explosions

### 梦魇之王格林 / Nightmare King Grimm

**位置 / Location**: 呼啸悬崖 — 格林剧团 DLC / Howling Cliffs — Grimm Troupe DLC
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 尖锐暗影 / Sharp Shadow, 冲刺大师 / Dashmaster, 骄傲印记 / Mark of Pride

游戏中最难的 Boss 之一，速度极快，伤害极高。

One of the hardest bosses in the game, extremely fast, extremely high damage.

**策略 / Strategy**:
- 火焰蝙蝠弹幕需要跳跃和冲刺结合 / Flame bat barrage requires jumping and dashing
- 地面冲刺刺击需要精准跳跃躲避 / Ground dash stab requires precise jump dodge
- 空中俯冲后会在原地停留，攻击窗口 / Aerial dive leaves him stationary — attack window
- 针火柱需要躲在间隙中 / Needle flame pillars require dodging in gaps
- 这个战斗的节奏是防御—反击—防御 / The rhythm is defend — counter — defend
- 尖锐暗影护符可以利用冲刺造成额外伤害 / Sharp Shadow can deal extra damage through dashes

## 最终 Boss / Final Bosses

### 空洞骑士 / The Hollow Knight

**位置 / Location**: 黑卵神殿 / Temple of the Black Egg
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 快速劈砍 / Quick Slash, 骄傲印记 / Mark of Pride, 快速聚焦 / Quick Focus

最终 Boss，被瘟疫支配的容器骑士。战斗充满悲壮感。

The final boss, a Vessel knight consumed by the Infection. The battle is filled with tragedy.

**阶段 1 策略 / Phase 1 Strategy**:
- 三连斩需要向后保持距离 / Three-slash combo requires maintaining distance
- 地面突刺可以跳跃躲过并反击 / Ground thrust can be jumped over and countered
- 触手攻击需要快速移动躲避 / Tentacle attack requires fast movement to dodge
- 空洞骑士会刺自己产生大量灵魂 / The Hollow Knight stabs itself to generate massive Soul

**阶段 2 策略 / Phase 2 Strategy**:
- 地面产生大量瘟疫触手 / Ground spawns numerous infection tentacles
- Boss 攻击速度加快 / Boss attacks faster
- 趁 Boss 刺自己时猛烈输出 / Attack fiercely while Boss stabs itself
- 治疗时机在触手攻击后 / Healing window is after tentacle attack

### 辐光 / The Radiance

**位置 / Location**: 梦境／黑卵神殿内部 / Dream Realm — Inside Black Egg
**推荐护符 / Recommended Charms**: 萨满之石 / Shaman Stone, 灵魂收割者 / Soul Eater, 法术扭曲者 / Spell Twister

真正的最终 Boss，梦境中的光明之神。终极挑战。

The true final boss, the god of light in the dream realm. The ultimate challenge.

**阶段 1 / Phase 1**:
- 光剑弹幕需要保持持续移动 / Light sword barrage requires constant movement
- 光柱攻击看地面预警躲避 / Light pillar attack — dodge by watching ground warning
- 攻击头部是唯一的伤害方式 / Attack the head is the only way to deal damage

**阶段 2 / Phase 2**:
- 平台出现，需要在平台间跳跃战斗 / Platforms appear, requiring platform-to-platform combat
- 尖刺球会追踪玩家 / Spike balls track the player
- 俯冲攻击后是主要攻击窗口 / Dive attack leaves the main attack window

**阶段 3 / Phase 3**:
- 辐光变为巨型形态，只有头部可攻击 / Radiance becomes giant, only head is attackable
- 大量光弹幕覆盖整个屏幕 / Mass light bullets cover the entire screen
- 使用升空法术(尖啸幽灵)效果极佳 / Abyss Shriek is extremely effective here
- 保持上升，不要被逼到底部 / Keep ascending, do not be forced to the bottom

## 诸神殿 Boss / Pantheon Bosses

寻神者 DLC 中的诸神殿(Pantheon)包含所有 Boss 的连续挑战。共 5 个级别：

The Godmaster DLC's Pantheons contain consecutive challenges against all bosses. 5 tiers total:

| 级别 / Tier | 名称 / Name | Boss 数量 / Count | 特点 / Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 大师神殿 / Pantheon of the Master | 10 | 基础 Boss / Basic bosses |
| 2 | 艺术家神殿 / Pantheon of the Artist | 14 | 强化 Boss / Enhanced bosses |
| 3 | 贤者神殿 / Pantheon of the Sage | 18 | 高难 Boss / Hard bosses |
| 4 | 骑士神殿 / Pantheon of the Knight | 22 | 极难 Boss / Very hard bosses |
| 5 | 万神殿 / Pantheon of Hallownest | 42 | 全部 Boss / All bosses |

在诸神殿中需要注意资源管理——在 Boss 之间要善用生命血凝聚来补充状态。

In the Pantheons, resource management is critical — use Lifeblood cocoons between bosses to replenish.
