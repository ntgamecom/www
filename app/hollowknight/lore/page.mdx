## 剧情解析 / Lore Analysis

《空洞骑士》的叙事方式非常独特——没有直白的对白或过场动画来解释整个故事。相反，剧情通过 NPC 对话、物品描述、梦境对话和场景环境来碎片化呈现。本页面将整合这些信息，为你呈现圣巢完整的历史脉络。

Hollow Knight's storytelling is uniquely fragmented — there are no straightforward dialogues or cutscenes explaining the entire story. Instead, the narrative is presented through NPC conversations, item descriptions, dream dialogues, and environmental storytelling. This page integrates this information to present the complete history of Hallownest.

## 圣巢的起源 / The Origin of Hallownest

### 辐光与古老文明 / The Radiance and the Ancient Civilization

在圣巢建立之前，一片虫族文明崇拜着一位梦境中的光之神——辐光(The Radiance)。她是梦境中的实体，是所有虫族的集体信仰所创造的光明之神。当时的虫族在梦境中敬拜辐光，她成为了他们的指引和庇护。

Before Hallownest was established, an ancient bug civilization worshipped a god of light in dreams — The Radiance. She was an entity born from dreams, a god of light created by the collective faith of all bugs. The ancient bugs worshipped her in dreams, and she became their guide and protection.

**关键证据 / Key Evidence**:

- 梦境战士的回忆提到了"古老的光" / Dream Warriors mention the "ancient light"
- 王后花园中白夫人的对话提及了之前的信仰 / White Lady's dialogue references the previous faith
- 物品"虚空之心"的描述暗示了更古老的力量 / Void Heart's description hints at older powers

### 苍白之王的到来 / The Arrival of the Pale King

苍白之王(The Pale King)是一位拥有高等智慧的虫族神明，来自圣巢之外。他降临于圣巢，用自己的光芒照亮了虫族的思想，赋予了它们智慧和理性。虫族逐渐转向崇拜苍白之王，遗忘并背叛了辐光。

The Pale King was a highly intelligent bug deity from outside Hallownest. He descended upon the kingdom, enlightening the bugs with his own light, granting them wisdom and reason. The bugs gradually turned to worship the Pale King, forgetting and betraying The Radiance.

**苍白之王的成就 / Pale King's Achievements**:

- 建立圣巢文明 / Founded Hallownest civilization
- 建立鹿角站交通系统 / Established the Stag Station transport system
- 创建灵魂圣殿研究灵魂力量 / Created the Soul Sanctum to study Soul energy
- 建造泪水之城等宏伟城市 / Built magnificent cities like the City of Tears

## 瘟疫的诞生 / The Birth of the Infection

### 辐光的复仇 / Radiance's Revenge

被遗忘的辐光在梦境中陷入愤怒和嫉妒。她开始用自己的光芒感染虫族的梦境，将其变成一种名为"瘟疫"(The Infection)的橙色物质。被感染的虫族失去理智，变得具有攻击性，身体开始流出橙色液体。

Forgotten and betrayed, The Radiance fell into rage and jealousy in the dream realm. She began infecting the dreams of bugs with her own light, transforming it into an orange substance called The Infection. Infected bugs lost their minds, became aggressive, and their bodies began oozing orange fluid.

**感染的表现 / Symptoms of Infection**:

- 眼睛变为橙色 / Eyes turn orange
- 身体流出橙色脓液 / Body oozes orange fluid
- 失去自我意识 / Loss of self-awareness
- 变得极度攻击性 / Become extremely aggressive
- 部分生物进化出特殊变异 / Some creatures develop special mutations

### 苍白之王的应对 / The Pale King's Response

苍白之王为了拯救他的王国，尝试了多种方法来阻止瘟疫：

The Pale King attempted multiple methods to stop the plague and save his kingdom:

1. **灵魂研究 / Soul Research**: 灵魂圣殿的学者们研究灵魂能量的力量试图找到解药，但最终堕落，变成了怪物。 / Soul Sanctum scholars researched Soul energy for a cure but ultimately corrupted into monsters.

2. **梦境者计划 / Dreamer Plan**: 苍白之王找到了三位梦境者——守望者卢瑞恩(Lurien the Watcher)、教师莫诺蒙(Monomon the Teacher)和野兽赫拉(Herrah the Beast)——自愿沉睡，用自己的梦境封印黑卵神殿中瘟疫的源头。 / The Pale King recruited three Dreamers — Lurien the Watcher, Monomon the Teacher, and Herrah the Beast — who voluntarily slept to seal the Infection source within the Temple of the Black Egg using their dreams.

3. **虚空容器计划 / Void Vessel Plan**: 苍白之王在深渊(The Abyss)利用虚空的黑暗力量创造了无数的容器(Vessels)，希望能够制造出一个没有思想、不会感染的空壳来封印辐光。 / The Pale King created countless Vessels in the Abyss using the dark power of the Void, hoping to create a hollow shell with no mind to seal The Radiance.

## 容器与空洞骑士 / The Vessels and The Hollow Knight

### 容器的诞生 / Birth of the Vessels

苍白之王在深渊深处发现了虚空(Void)的力量。他与虚空结合，创造了许多容器——黑色的、没有思想的虫形生物。这些容器原本的目的是成为封印辐光的完美容器。

The Pale King discovered the power of the Void deep in the Abyss. He combined with the Void to create many Vessels — black, mindless bug-shaped beings. These Vessels were originally intended to be the perfect containers to seal The Radiance.

**容器的特性 / Vessel Characteristics**:

- 由虚空物质构成 / Composed of Void substance
- 理论上没有思想 / Theoretically mindless
- 能够困住辐光 / Capable of trapping The Radiance
- 大多数容器被认为不完美而遗弃 / Most Vessels were deemed imperfect and discarded

### 空洞骑士的选择 / The Choice of the Hollow Knight

苍白之王从众多容器中挑选了一个他认为"纯净"的容器，将其命名为空洞骑士(The Hollow Knight)，并培养它成为封印辐光的工具。但空洞骑士在成长过程中对苍白之王产生了情感——这意味着它并不完美，瘟疫最终还是从它体内泄露了出来。

The Pale King selected one Vessel he considered "pure," named it The Hollow Knight, and raised it to become the tool for sealing The Radiance. However, during its growth, The Hollow Knight developed emotions toward the Pale King — meaning it was not truly hollow, and the Infection eventually leaked from within it.

## 圣巢的陨落 / The Fall of Hallownest

尽管苍白之王付出了巨大的努力，瘟疫最终还是爆发了。圣巢在数年间从辉煌的文明沦为了废墟。大部分虫族居民死亡或被感染，鹿角站停止运营，城市变为空城。

Despite the Pale King's enormous efforts, the Infection eventually erupted. Hallownest fell from a glorious civilization to ruins over several years. Most bug residents died or became infected, Stag Stations ceased operation, and cities became ghost towns.

**圣巢陨落的时间线 / Timeline of the Fall**:

1. 苍白之王发现瘟疫 / Pale King discovers the Infection
2. 灵魂圣殿的堕落 / Corruption of the Soul Sanctum
3. 梦境者封印黑卵神殿 / Dreamers seal the Black Egg
4. 空洞骑士中开始泄露瘟疫 / The Hollow Knight begins leaking Infection
5. 苍白之王消失 / The Pale King disappears
6. 圣巢全面崩溃 / Complete collapse of Hallownest
7. 小骑士来到圣巢 / The Little Knight arrives in Hallownest

## 主要势力分析 / Major Faction Analysis

### 虚空势力 / The Void

虚空是深渊中的原始黑暗力量，与光和梦境相对。它是辐光的天然对立面。小骑士和空洞骑士都是由虚空的物质构成的。

The Void is the primordial dark power in the Abyss, opposite to light and dreams. It is the natural antithesis of The Radiance. Both the Little Knight and the Hollow Knight are composed of Void substance.

## 多重结局解析 / Multiple Endings Analysis

游戏共有 5 个结局，取决于玩家的选择和收集进度：

The game features 5 endings depending on player choices and collection progress:

**结局 1: 空洞骑士 / The Hollow Knight**
击败空洞骑士后，小骑士替代空洞骑士成为新的封印者。瘟疫继续存在，但被暂时压制。

After defeating The Hollow Knight, the Little Knight replaces it as the new seal. The Infection continues but is temporarily contained.

**结局 2: 辐光陨落 / The Radiance Falls**
收集虚空之心后，在击败空洞骑士后使用梦之钉进入辐光的梦境领域并将其彻底消灭。

After collecting the Void Heart, use the Dream Nail on the Hollow Knight to enter The Radiance's dream realm and destroy her completely.

**结局 3: 拥抱虚空 / Embrace the Void**
在寻神者 DLC 中，击败万神殿中的所有 Boss 后，进入绝对辐光的领域并释放虚空的力量。

In the Godmaster DLC, defeat all bosses in the Pantheon to enter the Absolute Radiance's realm and unleash the Void's power.

**结局 4 和 5: 寻神者结局 / Godseeker Endings**
完成不同的诸神殿挑战后解锁的额外结局，展示了虚空控制宇宙的场景。

Additional endings unlocked by completing different Pantheon challenges, showing the Void consuming the cosmos.

## 主要角色分析 / Main Character Analysis

### 小骑士 / The Little Knight

玩家控制的主角，是从深渊逃出的容器之一。与空洞骑士一样是虚空造物。它回到圣巢的目的在故事中并未明说——是被命运召唤，还是出于自己的意志？

The protagonist controlled by the player, one of the Vessels that escaped from the Abyss. Like the Hollow Knight, it is a Void creation. Its purpose for returning to Hallownest is never explicitly stated — is it called by fate, or acting of its own will?

### 大黄蜂 / Hornet

苍白之王与赫拉的女儿，拥有王的血统。她守护着圣巢，测试小骑士的能力，最终在关键时刻提供帮助。她是游戏中少有的拥有自己意志和目标的角色之一。

Daughter of the Pale King and Herrah, possessing royal blood. She guards Hallownest, tests the Little Knight's abilities, and ultimately provides crucial help. She is one of the few characters with her own will and goals.

## 故事主题探讨 / Thematic Analysis

《空洞骑士》的故事核心是关于遗忘与记忆、光明与黑暗、思想与空虚的对抗。它探讨了一个文明面对无法避免的衰亡时的挣扎，以及牺牲与传承的意义。

At its core, Hollow Knight's story is about the conflict between forgetting and remembering, light and darkness, thought and emptiness. It explores a civilization's struggle against inevitable decay, and the meaning of sacrifice and legacy.

这些碎片化的叙事正是游戏的美妙之处——每个玩家都可以拼凑出自己理解的圣巢历史。

This fragmented storytelling is the beauty of the game — each player can piece together their own understanding of Hallownest's history.
