## 物品收集 / Item Collection

《空洞骑士》中有大量可收集物品，从强化角色能力的升级材料到解锁秘密的钥匙道具。本页面提供完整的收集指南，帮助你实现 100% (或 112%) 完成度。

Hollow Knight features a vast array of collectible items, from upgrade materials that strengthen your character to key items that unlock secrets. This page provides a comprehensive collection guide to help you achieve 100% (or 112%) completion.

苍白矿石用于在铁匠处升级骨钉。游戏中共有 6 块苍白矿石。

## 收集提示 / Collection Tips

1. 记录已收集和未收集的物品 / Keep track of collected and uncollected items
2. 在德里茅斯查找地图上标记的 Grub 图标 / Check Dirtmouth map for marked Grub icons
3. 获得新能力后立即回顾之前无法到达的区域 / Review unreachable areas immediately after getting new abilities
4. 使用梦之钉扫描所有 NPC 和 Boss / Scan all NPCs and bosses with Dream Nail
5. 收集顺序不重要——按照你自己的节奏来 / Collection order does not matter — go at your own pace
6. 某些收集需要非常精细的平台操作 / Some collections require very precise platforming
7. 不要忘记白色宫殿的隐藏房间 / Do not forget hidden rooms in the White Palace
8. 使用指南地图标记每个收集点 / Use guide maps to mark each collection point

收集是《空洞骑士》中最有成就感的部分之一。祝你好运！

Collection is one of the most rewarding parts of Hollow Knight. Good luck!
