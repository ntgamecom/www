# 武器装备 / Weapons & Equipment

绝地潜兵2拥有丰富多样的武器库，从突击步枪到能量武器，从手枪到重型反装甲炮——了解每把武器的特性是成为精英绝地潜兵的关键 / Helldivers 2 features a diverse arsenal, from assault rifles to energy weapons, pistols to heavy anti-tank cannons — knowing each weapon's characteristics is key to becoming an elite Helldiver.

---

主武器是你最常用的装备——选择适合你和任务类型的主武器至关重要 / Your primary is your most-used tool — choosing the right one for your playstyle and mission type is crucial.
