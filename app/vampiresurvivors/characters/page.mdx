# 角色详解 / Characters Guide — 吸血鬼幸存者 / Vampire Survivors

{/* 全角色数据解析，包含解锁条件和最佳使用场景 / All character data analysis including unlock conditions and best use scenarios */}

吸血鬼幸存者拥有超过 40 个可玩角色，每个角色都有独特的初始武器、被动加成和成长曲线。本页面将逐一分析每个角色的特性，帮助你选择最适合当前目标和关卡的角色。

Vampire Survivors has over 40 playable characters, each with unique starting weapons, passive bonuses, and growth curves. This page analyzes each character to help you choose the best one for your current goals and stages.

## 角色选择总原则 / Character Selection Principles

- 新手优先选高生存角色（Antonio, Krochi）
- 刷金币选经验加成角色（Imelda, Trouser）
- 高难关卡选高成长角色（Pugnala, Sigma）
- DLC 关卡选 DLC 角色（获得加成效果）
- 无尽模式选带缩放能力的角色

- Beginners: high survival characters (Antonio, Krochi)
- Gold farming: XP bonus characters (Imelda, Trouser)
- Hard stages: high growth characters (Pugnala, Sigma)
- DLC stages: DLC characters (for synergy bonuses)
- Endless mode: characters with scaling abilities
