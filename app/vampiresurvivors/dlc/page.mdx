# DLC攻略 / DLC Guide — 吸血鬼幸存者 / Vampire Survivors

{/* Legacy of the Moonspell, Tides of the Foscari, Emergency Meeting, Operation Guns 完整攻略 / Complete guide for all four DLC expansions */}

吸血鬼幸存者的 DLC 为游戏带来了大量新内容：新角色、新武器、新关卡和新机制。每个 DLC 都有独特的主题和玩法风格。本页面涵盖所有 DLC 的完整攻略。

Vampire Survivors DLCs bring a wealth of new content: new characters, new weapons, new stages, and new mechanics. Each DLC has a unique theme and play style. This page covers complete攻略 for all DLCs.
