# 解锁指南 / Unlocks Guide — 吸血鬼幸存者 / Vampire Survivors

## 解锁 Sigma / Unlocking Sigma

Sigma 是游戏中最强的角色，解锁条件非常苛刻：

Sigma is the strongest character in the game, with very strict unlock conditions:

1. 收集全部 6 个遗物 / Collect all 6 relics
2. 解锁全部角色 / Unlock all characters
3. 解锁全部武器 / Unlock all weapons
4. 解锁全部道具 / Unlock all items
5. 在 Cappella Magna 中找到 Sigma 徽章 / Find Sigma badge in Cappella Magna

完成后 Sigma 将自动解锁。她拥有全属性 +1% 的逆天被动，是挑战任何关卡的最佳选择。

After completion, Sigma is automatically unlocked. She has an overpowered passive of +1% to all stats, making her the best choice for any stage.
