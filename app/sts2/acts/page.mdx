# 章节与敌人 / Acts & Enemies

杀戮尖塔2共有4个章节，每个章节都有独特的主题、敌人群组和最终BOSS。了解各章节的特点和敌人是通关的关键 / Slay the Spire 2 has 4 Acts, each with a unique theme, enemy roster, and final boss. Understanding each Act's characteristics and enemies is key to victory.

---

## 第一章：残垣废墟 / Act 1: Crumbling Remains

**主题 / Theme**：高塔底层，古老的废墟遗迹，充满低阶怪物和机关造物 / The Spire's base, ancient ruins filled with lesser monsters and mechanical constructs.

**核心挑战 / Core Challenge**：考验初期卡组的输出能力。敌人血量普遍不高，但攻击模式直接——你需要在被击倒前先击倒他们 / Tests your early deck's damage output. Enemies have low HP but direct attack patterns — kill them before they kill you.

**地图特点 / Map Features**：
- 篝火较多（适合升级核心卡）/ More campfires (good for upgrading key cards)
- 精英怪较少但强度适中 / Fewer elites with moderate difficulty
- 商店少——谨慎使用金币 / Fewer shops — spend gold wisely

### 普通敌人 / Regular Enemies

| 敌人 / Enemy | HP | 行为模式 / Behavior | 应对策略 / Strategy |
|------|:---:|------|------|
| **废墟史莱姆 / Ruin Slime** | 12-18 | 每回合攻击4-6，分体后变成2个小史莱姆 / Attacks 4-6, splits into 2 smaller slimes | AOE优先，阻止分体后增加场面压力 / Prioritize AoE to prevent split |
| **生锈哨兵 / Rusty Sentinel** | 16-20 | 攻击6-8或上格挡6。生命低于50%时狂暴 / Attacks 6-8 or blocks 6. Enrages below 50% HP | 攒一波爆发打穿格挡直接带走 / Burst through its block in one turn |
| **暗影蝙蝠 / Shadow Bat** | 8-12 | 攻击3-5，每回合偷一张你的牌直到战斗结束 / Attacks 3-5, steals a card from your deck each turn | 快速击杀——牌被偷走会永久丢失 / Kill fast — stolen cards are gone for the entire combat |
| **破损构造体 / Broken Construct** | 22-28 | 攻击8-10，每3回合蓄力（下回合攻击18-22）/ Attacks 8-10, charges every 3 turns (next attack 18-22) | 在蓄力回合上格挡或虚弱 / Block or apply Weak on charge turns |

### 精英敌人 / Elites

| 精英 / Elite | HP | 行为模式 / Behavior | 应对策略 / Strategy |
|------|:---:|------|------|
| **巨像守卫 / Colossus Guardian** | 40-50 | 攻击12-15，每2回合释放AOE震波（对全部敌人8-10伤害）/ Attacks 12-15, AoE shockwave (8-10 all) every 2 turns | 保证有AOE清理小兵，本体吃伤害硬扛就行 / Bring AoE for the adds, tank the main hits |
| **暗影刺客团 / Shadow Assassins** | 25×2 | 两只刺客，一只攻击一只上易伤。击杀一只后另一只狂暴 / Two assassins — one attacks, one applies Vulnerable. Remaining one enrages | 集中火力逐個擊破，不要平均削血 / Focus fire one at a time, don't spread damage |
| **熔岩傀儡 / Lava Golem** | 55-65 | 攻击10，每回合生成一个自爆小火元素（2回合后爆炸15伤害）/ Attacks 10, spawns exploding fire elemental (15 dmg after 2 turns) | 优先击杀小火元素，减少场面压力 / Prioritize killing fire elementals to avoid blowups |

### BOSS：废王 / The Rust King

一个巨大的锈蚀机械王，坐落在废墟王座上 / A massive rusted mechanical king seated on a ruined throne.

| 阶段 / Phase | HP阈值 / Threshold | 行为 / Behavior |
|------|:---:|------|
| **第一阶段 / Phase 1** | 100% - 50% | 每次攻击12-15。每3回合召唤2个废墟史莱姆 / Attacks 12-15. Summons 2 Ruin Slimes every 3 turns |
| **第二阶段 / Phase 2** | 50% - 0% | 攻击提升至18-22。不再召唤史莱姆，改为每回合全屏AOE 8点伤害 / Attack increases to 18-22. Stops summoning, instead deals 8 AoE damage each turn |

**打法要点 / Tips**:
- 第一阶段带AOE清理小兵，同时输出BOSS / AoE the adds in Phase 1 while damaging the boss
- 第二阶段是DPS检测——必须在你的防御被耗光前击杀 / Phase 2 is a DPS check — kill before your defenses are overwhelmed
- **虚弱**效果在这场战斗中非常有效 / **Weak** is highly effective here

## 第二章：幽暗深渊 / Act 2: Abyssal Depths

**主题 / Theme**：高塔中层，被黑暗侵蚀的区域，生物更加扭曲致命 / The Spire's mid-section, corrupted by darkness — creatures are more twisted and deadly.

**核心挑战 / Core Challenge**：考验卡组的防御能力和AOE输出。群怪增多，debuff攻击频繁出现 / Tests your deck's defense and AoE. More multi-enemy fights, frequent debuff attacks.

**地图特点 / Map Features**：
- 篝火减少 / Fewer campfires
- 精英增加且更强 / More elites, significantly harder
- 问号房间增多，部分高风险高回报 / More ? rooms, some high-risk high-reward

### 普通敌人 / Regular Enemies

| 敌人 / Enemy | HP | 行为模式 / Behavior | 应对策略 / Strategy |
|------|:---:|------|------|
| **深渊蠕虫 / Abyss Worm** | 20-25 | 攻击7-9，每2回合施加1层脆弱 / Attacks 7-9, applies 1 Brittle every 2 turns | 脆弱会让你下次受伤翻倍——要么秒杀它，要么带净化 / Brittle doubles next damage — kill fast or bring cleanse |
| **暗影法师 / Shadow Mage** | 28-35 | 攻击6-8，召唤暗影蝙蝠。每3回合释放黑暗弹幕（全体12伤害）/ Attacks 6-8, summons Shadow Bats. Dark Barrage (12 AoE) every 3 turns | 优先击杀法师，蝙蝠后续再清 / Kill the mage first, clean up bats after |
| **深渊猎手 / Abyss Hunter** | 30-38 | 潜行一回合（不可选中），下回合攻击20-25。循环 / Stealth for 1 turn (untargetable), then attacks 20-25. Loops | 潜行回合上格挡/回复，攻击回合输出 / Block/heal during stealth, attack when it's vulnerable |
| **腐蚀史莱姆 / Corrosive Slime** | 22-30 | 攻击5-7，每次攻击降低你1点力量或敏捷（永久）/ Attacks 5-7, permanently reduces your Strength or Dexterity by 1 per hit | **最危险的小怪之一**——尽快击杀以减少属性损失 / **One of the most dangerous** — kill ASAP to minimize stat loss |

### 精英敌人 / Elites

| 精英 / Elite | HP | 行为模式 / Behavior | 应对策略 / Strategy |
|------|:---:|------|------|
| **深渊守卫 / Abyss Guardian** | 65-80 | 攻击15-18，每次攻击附带1层虚弱+易伤。每4回合释放黑暗冲击（全体20）/ Attacks 15-18 with Weak + Vulnerable. Dark Impact (20 AoE) every 4 turns | 净化debuff的手段至关重要 / Debuff cleanse is essential |
| **暗影三兄弟 / Shadow Trio** | 35×3 | 三兄弟共享血量池。一个攻击、一个上buff、一个治疗。必须同时击杀（三体合一后复活）/ Share a health pool. One attacks, one buffs, one heals. Must kill simultaneously or they revive | 平衡削血到全部残血，然后AOE收尾 / Balance HP across all three, then AoE finish |
| **虚空吞噬者 / Void Devourer** | 70-90 | 每回合吃掉你牌组最上面的一张牌并窃取其效果 / Eats the top card of your deck each turn and steals its effect | 速战速决——拖越久你的牌组越弱 / End the fight quickly — the longer it lasts, the weaker your deck becomes |

### BOSS：深渊之主 / The Abyss Lord

一个由纯粹黑暗构成的巨型人形生物 / A giant humanoid formed of pure darkness.

| 阶段 / Phase | HP阈值 / Threshold | 行为 / Behavior |
|------|:---:|------|
| **第一阶段 / Phase 1** | 100% - 60% | 攻击12-15，每回合施加1层脆弱+虚弱。每3回合召唤1个深渊蠕虫 / Attacks 12-15, applies Brittle + Weak each turn. Summons 1 Abyss Worm every 3 turns |
| **第二阶段 / Phase 2** | 60% - 30% | 攻击提升至18-22，召唤增加到2个蠕虫。释放黑暗领域（暗黑场地，每回合全体伤害5）/ Attack 18-22, summons 2 worms. Dark Aura (5 AoE damage per turn) |
| **第三阶段 / Phase 3** | 30% - 0% | 攻击25-30，黑暗领域伤害提升至10。不再召唤，但每回合把你一张牌变成虚空（不可用）/ Attack 25-30, Dark Aura damage 10. Stops summoning, instead converts 1 card to Void (unplayable) each turn |

**打法要点 / Tips**:
- 净化debuff的能力至关重要——脆弱+虚弱的组合极为致命 / Debuff cleanse is critical — Brittle + Weak is a deadly combination
- 第一阶段和第二阶段处理好小兵，不要让场面失控 / Control adds in Phases 1 and 2 to avoid being overwhelmed
- 第三阶段是全力冲刺——拖得越久你失去的牌越多 / Phase 3 is all-out sprint — the longer it goes, the more cards you lose

## 第三章：苍穹之巅 / Act 3: Celestial Summit

**主题 / Theme**：高塔上层，接近天空的区域，敌人多为天界造物和元素生物 / The Spire's upper levels, near the sky — enemies are celestial constructs and elementals.

**核心挑战 / Core Challenge**：考验卡组的完整运转能力和缩放能力。敌人拥有复杂的机制和强大的减益效果 / Tests your deck's engine completeness and scaling. Enemies have complex mechanics and powerful debuffs.

**地图特点 / Map Features**：
- 篝火稀少 / Very few campfires
- 精英极强 / Elites are extremely tough
- 有商店但商品昂贵 / Shops exist but are expensive
- **隐藏路径**可能通向第四章 / **Secret paths** may lead to Act 4

### 普通敌人 / Regular Enemies

| 敌人 / Enemy | HP | 行为模式 / Behavior | 应对策略 / Strategy |
|------|:---:|------|------|
| **天界哨兵 / Celestial Sentinel** | 32-40 | 攻击10-12，自带5点护甲（每回合恢复）/ Attacks 10-12, has 5 Armor (regenerates each turn) | 需要能够穿透护甲的手段或高单次伤害 / Need armor-piercing or high single-hit damage |
| **风暴元素 / Storm Elemental** | 25-35 | 攻击8-10，每回合给你的牌组加入一张"闪电"（0费打4，消耗）/ Attacks 8-10, adds "Lightning Strike" (0-cost 4 dmg, exhaust) to your deck each turn | 填充牌不是大问题，但注意不要因此稀释关键牌的抽率 / Flood cards aren't terrible, but watch for deck dilution |
| **命运编织者 / Fate Weaver** | 38-45 | 攻击12-15，每3回合"预知"你的下一张抽牌并选择强化自己或削弱你 / Attacks 12-15, every 3 turns "scries" your next draw and either buffs itself or debuffs you | 高DPS快速解决——预知机制的威胁随回合数增加 / High DPS to finish fast — the scry mechanic gets worse over time |
| **光耀守卫 / Luminous Guard** | 40-50 | 攻击8-12，每2回合释放护盾（免疫所有伤害1回合）/ Attacks 8-12, emits a shield every 2 turns (immune to all damage for 1 turn) | 护盾回合停止攻击、上buff或回复 / Skip attacking on shield turns — buff or heal instead |

### 精英敌人 / Elites

| 精英 / Elite | HP | 行为模式 / Behavior | 应对策略 / Strategy |
|------|:---:|------|------|
| **天界审判官 / Celestial Inquisitor** | 85-100 | 攻击18-22，每回合施加1层"审判"（累积3层后受到30伤害）/ Attacks 18-22, applies 1 "Judgment" per turn (3 stacks = 30 damage) | 审判层数需要关注——净化或快速击杀 / Watch Judgment stacks — cleanse or kill fast |
| **元素巨像 / Elemental Colossus** | 90-110 | 切换四种元素形态（火/冰/雷/暗），每种形态有不同的攻击模式。元素相克造成额外伤害 / Cycles through 4 elemental forms with different attack patterns. Element weakness yields bonus damage | 用对应元素的攻击造成额外伤害——如果你有元素使角色，这里是大优势 / Use matching element attacks for bonus damage |
| **虚空先知 / Void Prophet** | 75-95 | 不直接攻击，每回合施放预言——你的下一次攻击伤害减半、下一次技能费用加倍等 / Doesn't attack directly — casts prophecies: your next attack deals half damage, next skill costs double, etc. | 用低费卡或非关键卡试探预言效果后再出核心卡 / Test prophecies with cheap/non-critical cards before committing key cards |

### BOSS：天穹守望者 / The Celestial Watcher

一个漂浮在空中的巨大天界生物，周身环绕星辰 / A giant celestial being floating in the air, surrounded by stars.

| 阶段 / Phase | HP阈值 / Threshold | 行为 / Behavior |
|------|:---:|------|
| **第一阶段 / Phase 1** | 100% - 66% | 攻击14-18。每回合召唤1颗"星辰"（2回合后自爆，造成12-15伤害）/ Attacks 14-18. Summons 1 "Star" each turn (explodes after 2 turns for 12-15 damage) |
| **第二阶段 / Phase 2** | 66% - 33% | 攻击18-22，同时释放"星光"（全体10伤害并施加1层易伤）。召唤2颗星辰 / Attacks 18-22 + "Starlight" (10 AoE + 1 Vulnerable). Summons 2 Stars |
| **第三阶段 / Phase 3** | 33% - 0% | 攻击25-30。不再召唤星辰，改为"星辰坠落"——对所有敌人（你）造成15-20伤害。星光变为全体15伤害+2易伤 / Attacks 25-30. Stops summoning Stars, instead "Starfall" — 15-20 damage to all. Starlight becomes 15 AoE + 2 Vulnerable |

**打法要点 / Tips**:
- 控制星辰数量——不要让它们同时爆炸 / Control Star count — don't let them explode simultaneously
- 第一阶段尽量打输出，减少战斗时长 / Maximize damage in Phase 1 to shorten the fight
- 第二阶段的易伤需要注意叠加层数，超过2层非常危险 / Watch Vulnerable stacks in Phase 2 — 2+ is dangerous
- 第三阶段是纯粹的DPS竞赛——尽快结束战斗 / Phase 3 is a pure DPS race — end it fast

## 第四章：虚空之心 / Act 4: The Heart of Void

**主题 / Theme**：高塔核心，高塔真正的力量源头。只有找到隐藏路径才能到达 / The Spire's core, the true source of its power. Only reachable by finding the secret path.

**解锁条件 / Unlock Condition**：在Act 3获得**3个宝石碎片**（从特定精英和BOSS战斗中获得特殊遗物），然后在Act 3末尾的篝火处选择"进入虚空" / Collect **3 Gem Shards** (special relics from specific elites and bosses in Act 3), then choose "Enter the Void" at the Act 3 campfire.

### BOSS：虚空之心 / The Heart of Void

高塔的真正核心，一个巨大的暗影心脏 / The Spire's true core — a massive shadow heart.

| 阶段 / Phase | HP阈值 / Threshold | 行为 / Behavior |
|------|:---:|------|
| **第一阶段 / Phase 1** | 100% - 75% | 每回合攻击15（固定伤害，无视格挡）。每2回合释放"虚空脉冲"——从你的牌组移除2张牌（本场战斗）/ Attacks 15 each turn (unblockable). "Void Pulse" every 2 turns — removes 2 cards from your deck for this combat |
| **第二阶段 / Phase 2** | 75% - 40% | 攻击提升至20（无视格挡）。虚空脉冲改为移除3张牌。释放"暗影复制"——复制你的手牌中费用最高的牌打向你 / Attack 20 (unblockable). Void Pulse removes 3 cards. "Shadow Copy" — copies your highest-cost card and plays it against you |
| **第三阶段 / Phase 3** | 40% - 0% | 攻击30（无视格挡）。每回合虚空脉冲（移除2张）。释放"末日倒计时"——5回合后直接斩杀（999伤害）/ Attack 30 (unblockable). Void Pulse every turn (removes 2 cards). "Doom Countdown" — kills you in 5 turns (999 damage) |

**打法要点 / Tips**:
- **这是游戏最难的一场战斗** / **This is the hardest fight in the game**
- 每个阶段都是DPS检测——你需要在牌组被完全瓦解前击杀 / Every phase is a DPS check — kill before your deck is dismantled
- 高缩放（力量、元素充能、暗影印记等）是取胜关键 / High scaling (Strength, Elemental Charges, Shadow Marks, etc.) is essential
- 第三阶段的倒计时意味着你最多只有5个回合——全力输出 / The 5-turn countdown in Phase 3 means all-out offense
- 某些遗物（如保留格挡的遗物）在这里帮助有限，因为大部分伤害无视格挡 / Block-related relics are less useful here since most damage is unblockable
