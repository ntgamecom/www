# 章节与敌人 / Acts & Enemies

杀戮尖塔2共有4个章节，每个章节都有独特的主题、敌人群组和最终BOSS。了解各章节的特点和敌人是通关的关键 / Slay the Spire 2 has 4 Acts, each with a unique theme, enemy roster, and final boss. Understanding each Act's characteristics and enemies is key to victory.

---

## 第一章：残垣废墟 / Act 1: Crumbling Remains

**主题 / Theme**：高塔底层，古老的废墟遗迹，充满低阶怪物和机关造物 / The Spire's base, ancient ruins filled with lesser monsters and mechanical constructs.

**核心挑战 / Core Challenge**：考验初期卡组的输出能力。敌人血量普遍不高，但攻击模式直接——你需要在被击倒前先击倒他们 / Tests your early deck's damage output. Enemies have low HP but direct attack patterns — kill them before they kill you.

**地图特点 / Map Features**：
- 篝火较多（适合升级核心卡）/ More campfires (good for upgrading key cards)
- 精英怪较少但强度适中 / Fewer elites with moderate difficulty
- 商店少——谨慎使用金币 / Fewer shops — spend gold wisely
- 问号事件相对友善 / ? events are relatively friendly

**推荐路线策略 / Recommended Path Strategy**：
- 至少走2条精英路线——第1章的精英是最容易打的 / Take at least 2 elite fights — Act 1 elites are the easiest
- 篝火优先升级核心输出卡（攻击牌） / Prioritize upgrading core damage cards at campfires
- 经过至少1个商店用于删牌 / Visit at least 1 shop for card removal
- 问号事件值得探索——第1章的负面事件较少 / ? events are worth exploring — fewer negative outcomes in Act 1

- 铸甲师在这场战斗中有天然优势——护甲可以有效对抗AOE / Ironwarden has a natural advantage — Armor is effective against AoE

**主题 / Theme**：高塔中层，被黑暗侵蚀的区域，生物更加扭曲致命 / The Spire's mid-section, corrupted by darkness — creatures are more twisted and deadly.

**核心挑战 / Core Challenge**：考验卡组的防御能力和AOE输出。群怪增多，debuff攻击频繁出现 / Tests your deck's defense and AoE. More multi-enemy fights, frequent debuff attacks.

**地图特点 / Map Features**：
- 篝火减少 / Fewer campfires
- 精英增加且更强 / More elites, significantly harder
- 问号房间增多，部分高风险高回报 / More ? rooms, some high-risk high-reward
- 商店物品更贵但质量更高 / Shop items are more expensive but higher quality

**推荐路线策略 / Recommended Path Strategy**：
- 谨慎评估后再打精英——第2章精英是游戏中最危险的 / Carefully evaluate before fighting elites — Act 2 elites are the most dangerous in the game
- 篝火优先用于回复——第2章的敌人会让你掉更多血 / Prioritize campfire healing — Act 2 enemies will cost more HP
- 至少走1个商店，但不要花光所有金币——留钱删牌 / Visit at least 1 shop but don't spend all gold — keep some for removal
- 问号事件需谨慎——部分事件的代价非常大 / Be cautious with ? events — some have heavy costs

**精英战详解 / Elite Encounter Deep Dive**:

**深渊守卫 / Abyss Guardian**:

- 时间线：建议在第4回合黑暗冲击前进入第2阶段或击杀 / Timeline: aim to push phase or kill before Dark Impact on turn 4

**暗影三兄弟 / Shadow Trio**:
- 核心机制：必须同时击杀，否则复活/**绝对不能**一个一个杀 / Core mechanic: must kill simultaneously — NEVER kill one at a time
- 推荐打法：对所有三兄弟平均造成伤害，全部打到残血后使用AOE收尾 / Strategy: spread damage evenly, then AoE finish
- 陷阱：新手常犯的错误是集中火力——这样三兄弟会无限复活 / Trap: beginners often focus fire — this causes infinite revives

**虚空吞噬者 / Void Devourer**:
- 核心威胁：每回合吃掉你牌库顶一张牌——如果你的关键卡被吃掉就没了 / Core threat: eats top card of your deck each turn — losing your key card is devastating
- 最优策略：速战速决，不要给吞噬者太多回合 / Optimal strategy: end fight fast, don't give it many turns
- 如果DPS不够：考虑用低价值卡牌消耗吞噬者回合 / If DPS is low: consider using low-value cards to waste its turns

- 精英极强 / Elites are extremely tough
- 有商店但商品昂贵 / Shops exist but are expensive

- 如果你有3个宝石碎片，寻找通往第4章的隐藏路径 / If you have 3 Gem Shards, look for the hidden path to Act 4

- 精英战只打你能稳赢的——死在第3章精英面前很可惜 / Only fight elites you can confidently beat — dying to Act 3 elites is heartbreaking

- 元素相克：火克冰，冰克雷，雷克暗，暗克火——用对应元素类型攻击造成1.5倍伤害 / Element weakness: Fire beats Ice, Ice beats Lightning, Lightning beats Dark, Dark beats Fire — 1.5x damage with matching element

**虚空先知 / Void Prophet**:

- 对策：先出一张低费攻击/技能，看看预言是什么，再决定后续操作 / Counter: play a cheap attack/skill first to see the prophecy, then decide next actions
- 切记：预言影响的是"下一次"，所以同一回合你可以先出无用牌再出关键牌 / Remember: prophecy affects "the next" — so you can play a junk card first then your key card

| **第二阶段 / Phase 2** | 66% - 33% | 攻击18-22，同时释放"星光"（全体10伤害并施加1层易伤）。召唤2颗星辰 / Attacks 18-22 + "Starlight" (10 AoE + 1 Vulnerable). Summons 2 Stars |
| **第三阶段 / Phase 3** | 33% - 0% | 攻击25-30。不再召唤星辰，改为"星辰坠落"——对所有敌人（你）造成15-20伤害。星光变为全体15伤害+2易伤 / Attacks 25-30. Stops summoning Stars, instead "Starfall" — 15-20 damage to all. Starlight becomes 15 AoE + 2 Vulnerable |

**打法要点 / Tips**:
- 控制星辰数量——不要让它们同时爆炸 / Control Star count — don't let them explode simultaneously
- 第一阶段尽量打输出，减少战斗时长 / Maximize damage in Phase 1 to shorten the fight
- 第二阶段的易伤需要注意叠加层数，超过2层非常危险 / Watch Vulnerable stacks in Phase 2 — 2+ is dangerous
- 第三阶段是纯粹的DPS竞赛——尽快结束战斗 / Phase 3 is a pure DPS race — end it fast
- 推荐角色：时计师（回溯可重置星辰爆炸时间差）/ Recommended: Chronomancer (Rewind resets Star explosion timing)

## 第四章：虚空之心 / Act 4: The Heart of Void

**主题 / Theme**：高塔核心，高塔真正的力量源头。只有找到隐藏路径才能到达 / The Spire's core, the true source of its power. Only reachable by finding the secret path.

**解锁条件 / Unlock Condition**：在Act 3获得**3个宝石碎片**（从特定精英和BOSS战斗中获得特殊遗物），然后在Act 3末尾的篝火处选择"进入虚空" / Collect **3 Gem Shards** (special relics from specific elites and bosses in Act 3), then choose "Enter the Void" at the Act 3 campfire.

**第4章特点 / Act 4 Features**：
- 只有一个篝火（在精英战前）/ Only 1 campfire (before the elite)
- 一个商店 / 1 shop
- 一个精英战（虚空守护者）/ 1 elite fight (Void Guardian)
- 最终BOSS（虚空之心）/ Final boss (Heart of Void)
- **没有回头路**——一旦进入第4章，只能向前 / **No turning back** — once in Act 4, you can only go forward

**第4章精英：虚空守护者 / Act 4 Elite: Void Guardian**

| 属性 / Stat | 数值 / Value |
|------|:----:|
| **HP / HP** | 100-120 |
| **攻击 / Attack** | 20-25（部分无视格挡）/ 20-25 (partially unblockable) |
| **特殊 / Special** | 每回合移除你1张牌（本场战斗）/ Removes 1 card per turn (this combat) |

**打法策略 / Strategy**: 这是虚空之心的"热身"——如果打这个精英都吃力，那你打虚空之心必死。建议在精英前用完篝火升级，保留药水给虚空之心 / This is a "warm-up" for the Heart — if this elite gives you trouble, you won't beat the Heart. Use your campfire before this fight, save potions for the Heart.

### BOSS：虚空之心 / The Heart of Void

高塔的真正核心，一个巨大的暗影心脏 / The Spire's true core — a massive shadow heart.

| 阶段 / Phase | HP阈值 / Threshold | 行为 / Behavior |
|------|:---:|------|
| **第一阶段 / Phase 1** | 100% - 75% | 每回合攻击15（固定伤害，无视格挡）。每2回合释放"虚空脉冲"——从你的牌组移除2张牌（本场战斗）/ Attacks 15 each turn (unblockable). "Void Pulse" every 2 turns — removes 2 cards from your deck for this combat |
| **第二阶段 / Phase 2** | 75% - 40% | 攻击提升至20（无视格挡）。虚空脉冲改为移除3张牌。释放"暗影复制"——复制你的手牌中费用最高的牌打向你 / Attack 20 (unblockable). Void Pulse removes 3 cards. "Shadow Copy" — copies your highest-cost card and plays it against you |
| **第三阶段 / Phase 3** | 40% - 0% | 攻击30（无视格挡）。每回合虚空脉冲（移除2张）。释放"末日倒计时"——5回合后直接斩杀（999伤害）/ Attack 30 (unblockable). Void Pulse every turn (removes 2 cards). "Doom Countdown" — kills you in 5 turns (999 damage) |

**打法要点 / Tips**:
- **这是游戏最难的一场战斗** / **This is the hardest fight in the game**
- 每个阶段都是DPS检测——你需要在牌组被完全瓦解前击杀 / Every phase is a DPS check — kill before your deck is dismantled
- 高缩放（力量、元素充能、暗影印记等）是取胜关键 / High scaling (Strength, Elemental Charges, Shadow Marks, etc.) is essential
- 第三阶段的倒计时意味着你最多只有5个回合——全力输出 / The 5-turn countdown in Phase 3 means all-out offense
- 某些遗物（如保留格挡的遗物）在这里帮助有限，因为大部分伤害无视格挡 / Block-related relics are less useful here since most damage is unblockable
- **推荐卡牌**：高缩放能力牌、低费高效输出牌、过牌牌——防御牌价值很低 / **Recommended cards**: high scaling Powers, low-cost efficient damage, draw cards — defense cards have low value
