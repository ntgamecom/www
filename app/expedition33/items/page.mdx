# 装备与物品 / Items & Gear

33号远征队的装备系统包括武器、防具、遗物和消耗品。武器可以升级，遗物提供强力被动效果——合理搭配能让战斗力翻倍 / Expedition 33's equipment system includes weapons, armor, relics, and consumables. Weapons can be upgraded, relics provide powerful passives — proper combinations can double your combat effectiveness.

---
