## 隐藏区域 / Hidden Zones

### 序章隐藏区域 / Prologue Hidden Zones

**学院钟楼密室 / Academy Clock Tower Secret Room**: 钟楼最顶层——爬到钟楼外部跳到对面平台。跳跃精度要求高——失败可复活重试。密室内有**学院创始人的笔记**和**时间怀表**——装备后战斗外移速+20%。创始人的笔记揭示学院地下的一个隐藏实验——触发后续隐藏任务线 / **Academy Clock Tower Secret Room**: Top of clock tower — climb external then jump across. Platforming challenge — retry on failure. Contains **Founder's Notes** and **Pocket Watch** — +20% out-of-combat movespeed. Founder's Notes hint at a hidden experiment beneath the academy — triggers follow-up hidden quest.

**地下档案室 / Underground Archives**: 学院地下室——看起来无法打开的保险门。解法: 找到4本"学院历史"书(不同书架上)→全部阅读后门自动打开。内有**技能重置卷轴x3**和**经验书**(5000经验)。档案室还有一个隐藏抽屉——DC 18察觉检定——里面有**古代钥匙**——用于第一章市政厅的一个隐藏箱子 / **Underground Archives**: Sealed vault door in academy basement — find 4 "Academy History" books (different shelves) → read all to unlock. Contains **Skill Reset Scroll x3** and **XP Book** (5000 XP). Hidden drawer — DC 18 Perception — contains **Ancient Key** — for hidden chest in Ch.1 City Hall.

### 第一章隐藏区域 / Chapter 1 Hidden Zones

**市政厅地下金库 / City Hall Underground Vault**: 光辉之城市政厅——找市长钥匙(他办公室抽屉，DC 15巧手)。金库里有**金条x10**和**光辉之盾**——格挡时产生光盾墙反射伤害。金库后方还有一个隐藏隔间——需要检测到墙壁上的暗缝——里面有**光之碎片x1** / **City Hall Underground Vault**: Radiant City Hall — find mayor's key (his office desk drawer, DC 15 Sleight). Contains **Gold Bars x10** and **Radiant Shield** — creates light wall on block that reflects damage. Hidden compartment behind vault — detect wall crack — contains **Light Fragment x1**.

**废弃光塔 / Abandoned Light Tower**: 光辉之城边缘的破旧高塔——入口被藤蔓覆盖——用火焰技能烧毁。塔顶有**光之碎片x1**和观景点。观景后如果队中有特定角色(莉莎)，她触发一段关于她童年的剧情对话——获得好感度提升 / **Abandoned Light Tower**: Ruined tower at city edge — vine-covered entrance — burn with fire skill. Top has **Light Fragment x1** and scenic overlook. If specific character (Lisa) in party, triggers childhood dialogue — increases affinity.

**地下竞技场 / Underground Arena**: 光辉之城下水道的隐藏入口——需要"竞技场邀请函"(下水道精英怪掉落)。连续挑战5波敌人——每波更强。第五波Boss出现**角斗场冠军**——击败获得**角斗士之证**(全属性+5)和**角斗士套装**——一套外观幻化 / **Underground Arena**: Hidden entrance in city sewers — need "Arena Invitation" (elite drop). Face 5 consecutive waves — each harder. Wave 5 spawns **Arena Champion** — drops **Gladiator's Crest** (+5 all stats) and **Gladiator's Set** — transmog appearance.

**画家的工作室 / Painter's Studio**: 光辉之城艺术区——敲门后无人应答——用撬锁工具或DC 18力量开门。里面是一个画家的生前工作室——墙上一幅未完成的画——如果队中有画家角色可以完成画作——获得**艺术品"光辉之城日落"**——可以卖出高价或装饰藏身处 / **Painter's Studio**: Radiant City art district — knock, no answer — lockpick or DC 18 Strength to open. Inside is deceased painter's studio — unfinished painting on wall — if party has painter character, complete it — get **Artwork "Radiant City Sunset"** — sell for high price or hideout decoration.

### 第二章隐藏区域 / Chapter 2 Hidden Zones

**隐藏机械工厂 / Hidden Mech Factory**: 机械平原西部隐蔽小山谷——入口被巨石挡住(力量20+推石)。内有一条完整的机械生产线——可以制作**蓝色品质机械装备**(比商店货好30%)。工厂深处有一个**废弃的机械Boss**——击败获得**机械核心**——用于制作机械系终极武器 / **Hidden Mech Factory**: Hidden valley in western Machine Plains — boulder block (Str 20+). Contains complete production line — craft **blue-quality mech gear** (30% better than shop). Deeper area has **Abandoned Mech Boss** — drops **Mech Core** — ultimate mech weapon crafting.

**风车密室 / Windmill Secret Room**: 机械平原风车群——其中一个风车叶片位置异常——站在特定叶片阴影下5秒触发机关。风车底座打开入口——内有**古代机械核心**和**机械设计图·终章**——解锁机械系最强武器制作。注意机关有时间限制——30秒内进入否则重新触发 / **Windmill Secret Room**: One windmill has misaligned blades — stand in specific blade shadow for 5 sec. Base opens — contains **Ancient Mech Core** and **Mech Blueprint: Final** — unlocks best mech weapon craft. 30-second time limit — recast shadow if late.

**地下矿道遗迹 / Underground Mine Ruins**: 机械平原东部有一处塌陷矿道入口——需要炸药(找军需官购买或者用火焰法术)炸开。矿道深处有**稀有矿石x5**和一个**矿车追逐迷你游戏**——成功到达终点获得**矿工帽**——照亮黑暗区域的头盔——对探索地下区域极为有用 / **Underground Mine Ruins**: Collapsed mine entrance in eastern Machine Plains — need explosives (quartermaster or fire spell) to blast. Deep area has **Rare Ore x5** and **Minecart Chase minigame** — reach end for **Miner's Helmet** — illuminates dark areas — very useful for underground exploration.

### 第三章隐藏区域 / Chapter 3 Hidden Zones

**魂之井 / Soul Well**: 深渊裂隙第四层——岔路通向发光井。互动消耗30%当前HP——返还**永久+2全属性**。可多次使用——消耗递增(30%→40%→50%→60%→70%→80%)——最多6次。第五次和第六次额外给予**魂之碎片**——用于制作独特饰品"魂之护符"——战斗开始时自动获得护盾 / **Soul Well**: Floor 4 of Abyss Rift — branching path to glowing well. Consumes 30% current HP — grants **permanent +2 all stats**. Repeatable — cost increases (30%→40%→50%→60%→70%→80%) — max 6 uses. 5th and 6th grant **Soul Fragment** — craft "Soul Amulet" — auto-shield at combat start.

**深渊画廊 / Abyss Gallery**: 深渊裂隙第六层隐藏墙壁后——按特定顺序点亮三根火炬(线索在画作上)。画廊展示世界背景故事——每幅画给少量经验。全部看完获"鉴赏家"成就和**暗影之戒**(暗影区域伤害+15%)。画廊最深处还有一幅特殊的画——互动后触发隐藏Boss战**画中恶魔** / **Abyss Gallery**: Behind hidden wall on floor 6 — light three torches (order clue in paintings). Displays world lore — each painting grants small XP. All viewed grants "Connoisseur" achievement and **Shadow Ring** (+15% shadow area damage). Deepest painting triggers hidden boss **Painting Demon**.

**遗忘之厅 / Hall of Forgotten**: 在深渊裂隙第八层——需要携带至少3个"光之碎片"才能激活入口(碎片作为钥匙)。大厅中有8个雕像——每个代表一个角色。和对应角色的雕像互动获得该角色的**专属技能强化**——例如主角的光之技能+20%伤害 / **Hall of Forgotten**: Floor 8 of Abyss Rift — need 3+ "Light Fragments" to activate entrance (used as keys). 8 statues — each representing a character. Interact with matching character's statue for **exclusive skill upgrade** — e.g., protagonist's Light skill +20% damage.

## 隐藏Boss / Secret Bosses

**画中恶魔 / Painting Demon**: 在深渊画廊最深处的画作中触发。Boss会使用**颜色攻击**——红色(火焰)要躲、蓝色(冰冻)要格挡、黑色(即死)要跳跃。击败后掉落**画家的调色板**——独特副手装备——可以切换元素属性 / **Painting Demon**: Triggered at deepest gallery painting. Uses **Color Attacks** — red (fire) dodge, blue (ice) block, black (instant death) jump. Drops **Painter's Palette** — unique off-hand — switch elemental attributes.

## 特殊收集品 / Special Collectibles

**光之碎片(Light Fragments)**共20个——分布在全游戏各章节。收集全部后在最终战前解锁隐藏剧情和**隐藏结局"。碎片位置: 序章3个、第一章5个、第二章5个、第三章4个、终章3个。每个碎片也提供永久+1%光系伤害——全收集共+20%光系伤害 / 20 **Light Fragments** across all chapters. Collecting all unlocks hidden story before final boss and **hidden ending**. Locations: Prologue 3, Ch.1 5, Ch.2 5, Ch.3 4, Finale 3. Each grants +1% Light damage permanently — total +20% Light damage.

## 多重结局条件 / Multiple Ending Conditions

游戏共有**3个结局**: 普通结局(通关即可)、好结局(收集全部20光之碎片+在最终对话中选择原谅)、隐藏结局(全部碎片+全角色存活+特殊道具"光之核心")。好结局和隐藏结局有额外尾幕动画和角色后日谈。隐藏结局需要最苛刻条件但在新游戏+中继承进度 / Game has **3 endings**: Normal (clear game), Good (all 20 Light Fragments + Forgive choice), Hidden (all fragments + all characters alive + special item "Light Core"). Good and Hidden endings have extended epilogues. Hidden ending has strictest requirements but progress carries in NG+.
