# 章节攻略 / Chapter Walkthrough

本页面提供游戏全16章的流程攻略，包含谜题解法、收集品位置和战斗策略。

This page provides a complete walkthrough for all 16 chapters, including puzzle solutions, collectible locations, and combat strategies.

---

## 第1章：抵达村庄 / Chapter 1: Arrival

### 开场剧情 / Opening

里昂抵达村庄外围，开始搜索阿什莉的下落。跟随路径前进，会遇到第一个村民。

Leon arrives at the village outskirts and begins searching for Ashley. Follow the path forward and encounter the first villager.

### 流程要点 / Walkthrough

- **进入小屋 Go to Cabin**: 跟随光线前进，进入第一间小屋 / Follow the light and enter the first cabin
- **获取霰弹枪 Get Shotgun**: 在小屋二楼墙壁上获得 W-870 霰弹枪 / Obtain the W-870 shotgun on the second floor wall
- **遭遇村民 Villager Encounter**: 学习基础战斗教学 / Learn basic combat tutorial
- **通讯 Contact**: 与汉尼根（Hannigan）首次联络 / First contact with Hannigan
- **撤离点 Evacuation Point**: 进入山谷，触发电锯姐妹花剧情 / Enter the valley, trigger Bella Sisters cutscene

### 战斗要点 / Combat Tips

| 敌人 Enemy | 推荐策略 Strategy |
|---|---|
| 普通村民 Villager | 爆头+体术 combo / Headshot + melee combo |
| 电锯姐妹花 Bella Sisters | 拉开距离霰弹枪射击头部 / Keep distance, shotgun headshots |

### 收集品 / Collectibles

| 物品 Item | 位置 Location |
|---|---|
| 绿色草药 Green Herb | 第一间小屋外 / Outside first cabin |
| 绿草 + 红草 Herb Combo | 山坡小路 / Hillside path |
| 火药 Gunpowder | 路旁箱子 / Roadside crate |

## 第2章：村庄广场 / Chapter 2: The Village Square

### 流程要点 / Walkthrough

- **广场战斗 Village Square**: 敲钟引来大量村民，坚持到钟声响起 / Large group fight, survive until the bell rings
- **进入民宅 Enter Houses**: 搜刮各个房间获取物资 / Search houses for supplies
- **蓝徽章悬赏 Blue Medallions**: 村庄区域有5个蓝徽章 / 5 blue medallions in village area
- **湖畔区域 Lakeside**: 穿过隧道到达湖畔 / Through the tunnel to the lakeside
- **获得小钥匙 Get Small Key**: 在特定房屋内找到 / Found in specific house

### 教堂钥匙谜题 / Church Key Puzzle

在村庄中寻找 **教堂钥匙（Church Key）**，用于打开教堂大门。钥匙位于村庄某间房屋的二层。

Find the Church Key in a house on the second floor of the village. It unlocks the church door.

### 收集品 / Collectibles

| 物品 Item | 位置 Location |
|---|---|
| 教堂钥匙 Church Key | 村庄房屋二楼 / Village house 2F |
| 宝藏 Treasure | 炉灶里 / Inside oven |
| 尖晶石 Spinel | 蓝徽章奖励 / Blue medallion reward |

## 第3章：湖畔与洞穴 / Chapter 3: Lakeside & Cave

### 流程要点 / Walkthrough

- **渔场 Fishing Village**: 清理渔场区域的敌人 / Clear the fishing village
- **洞穴探索 Cave Exploration**: 进入洞穴，解决谜题 / Enter the cave, solve puzzles
- **六角形徽章 Hexagon Emblem**: 找到并放置两个徽章 / Find and place two hexagon emblems
- **开启大门 Open Gate**: 使用徽章打开通向湖的道路 / Use emblems to open the lake path
- **蓝徽章悬赏 Blue Medallions**: 湖畔区域5个蓝徽章 / 5 medallions at lake area

### 洞穴谜题 / Cave Puzzle

将两个六角形徽章插入基座。一个在洞穴入口附近，一个在洞穴深处。

Insert two hexagon emblems into the pedestals. One is near the cave entrance, one is deep in the cave.

### 商人出现 / Merchant

商人首次出现在湖畔安全屋 / Merchant appears at the lakeside safe room.

## 第5章：教堂与豪宅 / Chapter 5: Church & Manor

### 流程要点 / Walkthrough

- **进入教堂 Enter Church**: 使用教堂钥匙 / Use the church key
- **拯救阿什莉 Rescue Ashley**: 在教堂找到阿什莉 / Find Ashley at the church
- **守卫战 Defense Battle**: 保护阿什莉不被村民抓走 / Protect Ashley from being taken
- **前往豪宅 Go to Manor**: 穿过墓地到达豪宅 / Cross the cemetery to the manor
- **大屋探索 Manor Exploration**: 解决大屋谜题 / Solve the manor puzzle

### 石板谜题 / Tablet Puzzle

找到两块石板插入门上的机关。一块在餐厅，一块在书房。

Find two stone tablets and insert them into the door mechanism. One in the dining room, one in the study.

Rotate three gears to the correct positions to open the gate. Align the markings on the gears.

Find red and blue keycards for their respective doors. Red in the officer office, blue in the lab.

### 再生者打法 / Regenerator Strategy

使用热成像仪瞄准再生者体内的蓝色物体射击。打掉所有蓝色物体后击杀。

Use the thermal scope to aim at the blue objects inside the Regenerator. Destroy all blue objects to kill it.
