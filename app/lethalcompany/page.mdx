## 故事背景 / Story & Lore

在《致命公司》的世界中，玩家受雇于一家神秘的大型公司。公司为你配备了基础的太空飞船和导航系统，要求你前往各个星球收集有价值的废品。每个星球都充满了危险——从恶劣的天气条件到致命的原生生物。

In the world of *Lethal Company*, players are employed by a mysterious corporation. The company provides a basic spaceship and navigation system, tasking you with collecting valuable scrap from various planets. Each moon is filled with dangers — from harsh weather conditions to deadly native creatures.

公司会设定利润配额，如果你无法在限定天数内达到目标，你将被解雇（即游戏结束）。这种死亡与配额的循环构成了游戏的核心紧张感。

The company sets profit quotas. If you fail to meet the target within the given days, you will be fired (game over). This cycle of death and quotas forms the core tension of the game.

关于公司的本质和星球的秘密，游戏中隐藏着许多线索和传说碎片，等待玩家去拼凑。有人说公司其实已经不存在了，这些指令只是自动系统的遗存。

Scattered throughout the game are clues and lore fragments about the nature of the company and the secrets of the planets. Some say the company no longer exists and these directives are merely remnants of an automated system.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanics

## 工作流程 / Workflow

- **出发前往星球**：从飞船的终端电脑选择星球降落
- **搜索废品**：进入建筑物和洞穴搜索有价值的物品
- **运回飞船**：将废品搬运回飞船货舱
- **面对怪物**：躲避或对抗星球上的危险生物
- **返回公司**：在每日结束前返回飞船起飞
- **销售废品**：返回公司后将废品出售获得金钱
- **完成配额**：在截止日前凑够目标金额

- **Travel to a moon**: Select a landing destination from the ship's terminal
- **Search for scrap**: Enter buildings and caves to find valuable items
- **Haul back to ship**: Carry scrap back to the ship's cargo bay
- **Face monsters**: Avoid or fight dangerous creatures on the moon
- **Return to ship**: Get back to the ship before the day ends
- **Sell scrap**: Return to the company to sell scrap for credits
- **Meet quota**: Reach the target amount before the deadline

## 时间管理 / Time Management

每天从早上8点开始，到午夜结束。你必须在天黑前返回飞船，或者准备好应对夜晚的危险。每个星球每天可以探索的时间有限，合理规划是生存的关键。

Each day starts at 8:00 AM and ends at midnight. You must return to the ship before dark or be prepared to face nocturnal dangers. Limited exploration time per day makes planning essential for survival.

## 星球系统 / Moon System

游戏中的星球分为三个难度等级：

The game's moons are divided into three difficulty tiers:

| 等级 / Tier | 特征 / Characteristics | 示例 / Examples |
|---|---|---|
| 简单 / Easy | 小型地图、少数怪物、低价值废品 | Experimentation, Assurance, Vow |
| 中等 / Medium | 中型地图、多种怪物、中等价值废品 | Offense, March, Adamance |
| 困难 / Hard | 大型地图、危险怪物、高价值废品 | Rend, Dine, Titan, Artifice |

每个星球都有可收集废品数量和风险水平的评级。高回报通常伴随着高风险。

Each moon has a rating for available scrap quantity and danger level. Higher rewards typically come with higher risks.

## 给新手的小贴士 / Tips for New Players

1. **听力是生命** / Your ears are your lifeline — 大多数怪物都有独特的声音提示，戴耳机游玩有巨大优势
2. **团队分散拾荒** / Split up to scavenge — 多人组队时分开搜索建筑能大幅提高效率
3. **不要贪心** / Know when to leave — 天黑或听到危险声音时立即撤回飞船
4. **通讯器是必备** / Always bring a walkie-talkie — 与队友保持沟通至关重要
5. **学习怪物行为** / Learn creature behavior — 大多数怪物可以避开，不需要正面战斗
6. **升级飞船装备** / Upgrade ship gear — 使用废品收入购买飞船升级品提高生存率
7. **记住地图** / Memorize the map — 熟悉地图布局能帮你更快找到出口

## 结语 / Final Words

《致命公司》是一款将恐怖与搞笑完美融合的独特游戏。你可能会在队友被怪物拖走时笑得喘不过气，也可能会在黑暗中被突如其来的声音吓得魂飞魄散。无论哪种体验，这都是值得与朋友一起享受的旅程。

*Lethal Company* is a unique game that perfectly blends horror with humor. You may laugh breathlessly as your teammate gets dragged away by a monster, or scream in terror at an unexpected sound in the dark. Either way, this is a journey worth sharing with friends.
