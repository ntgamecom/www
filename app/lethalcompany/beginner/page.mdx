## 游戏概述 / Game Overview

《致命公司》是一款 **合作恐怖拾荒模拟器**。你和其他最多三名玩家扮演公司员工，驾驶飞船前往不同星球收集废品。每个任务周期为三天，你需要在期限内达到公司规定的利润配额，否则就会被解雇（删除存档）。

*Lethal Company* is a **co-op horror scavenging simulator**. You and up to three other players play as company employees, piloting a ship to different moons to collect scrap. Each work cycle lasts three in-game days, and you must meet the company's profit quota within that time or be fired (save deleted).

游戏极其强调 **团队沟通** 和 **风险管理**。你需要在危险中平衡收益与安全，做出明智的决策。

The game heavily emphasizes **team communication** and **risk management**. You must balance profit against safety while making smart decisions under pressure.

## 废品经济 / Scrap Economy

## 收益机制 / Profit Mechanics

废品是《致命公司》中的核心资源。你需要将找到的废品运回飞船，然后返回公司在总部出售。

Scrap is the core resource in *Lethal Company*. You must find scrap, haul it back to your ship, and sell it at the company headquarters.

| 废品类型 / Scrap Type | 价值范围 / Value Range | 常见度 / Rarity |
|---|---|---|
| 普通废品 / Common Scrap | 10-50 | 高 / High |
| 稀有废品 / Rare Scrap | 60-150 | 中 / Medium |
| 极稀有废品 / Very Rare Scrap | 150-500 | 低 / Low |
| 神器级 / Artifact Tier | 500+ | 极低 / Very Low |

## 配额系统 / Quota System

- 初始配额：约 100-200 之间的随机值
- 后续配额：每轮增加约 50-100%
- 截止日期：通常 3 个游戏日（可以在商店购买额外天数）
- 失败惩罚：存档被删除，游戏从头开始

- Initial quota: Random value between 100-200
- Subsequent quotas: Increases by roughly 50-100% each round
- Deadline: Usually 3 in-game days (extra days can be purchased in the store)
- Failure penalty: Save file deleted, game restarts from scratch

## 物品栏管理 / Inventory Management

每个玩家只能携带 **最多四件物品**。你需要合理分配每个队员的携带空间，优先搬运高价值废品。

Each player can carry a maximum of **four items**. You must allocate carrying capacity wisely, prioritizing high-value scrap.

## 死亡与损失 / Death & Loss

## 死亡后果 / Consequences of Death

- 你掉落的全部物品将留在原地
- 队友可以捡起你的物品带回飞船
- 如果全队死亡，任务直接结束，所有未运回飞船的废品全部损失
- 死亡玩家在下一轮开始时复活

- All items you were carrying are dropped at your death location
- Teammates can retrieve your items and bring them back to the ship
- If the entire team dies, the day ends immediately and all unshipped scrap is lost
- Dead players respawn at the start of the next day

## 避免死亡 / Avoiding Death

1. **不要单独深入** — 至少与一名队友结伴行动
2. **随时确认出口位置** — 迷路等于等死
3. **天黑前半小时撤离** — 夜晚的怪物更加活跃
4. **学会使用路标** — 用废品或涂鸦标记已探索区域

1. **Don't go deep alone** — Always pair up with at least one teammate
2. **Know your exit** — Being lost equals death
3. **Evacuate 30 minutes before dark** — Night creatures are more active
4. **Use waypoints** — Mark explored areas with scrap or graffiti

## 新人生存建议 / Beginner Survival Tips

## 第一条：别死 / Rule One: Don't Die

听起来像废话，但这是最重要的规则。活着才能赚钱。如果情况不妙，立即撤退。废品可以明天再捡，命只有一条。

Sounds obvious but it's the most important rule. You need to stay alive to make money. If things go wrong, retreat immediately. Scrap can wait until tomorrow; you only have one life.

## 第二天：用耳朵玩 / Rule Two: Use Your Ears

《致命公司》的声音设计是一流的。大多数怪物在接近你之前会发出明显的声音提示。戴耳机游玩能给你巨大的生存优势。

*Lethal Company* has exceptional sound design. Most creatures make distinct audio cues before approaching. Playing with headphones gives you a huge survival advantage.

## 第三天：分工合作 / Rule Three: Divide and Conquer

- **搜索阶段**：两人一组分头搜索不同建筑
- **搬运阶段**：一人负责在飞船导航，其他人搬运废品
- **防守阶段**：有人留守飞船应对接近的怪物

- **Scavenge phase**: Split into pairs to search different buildings
- **Haul phase**: One person navigates from the ship while others haul scrap
- **Defense phase**: Someone guards the ship against approaching creatures

## 第四天：了解你的星球 / Rule Four: Know Your Moon

在高级星球上送死毫无意义。先从简单的 Experimentation 或 Assurance 开始练习，熟悉游戏机制后再挑战更难的星球。

Dying on high-tier moons is pointless. Start with Experimentation or Assurance to practice, then move to harder moons once you understand the mechanics.

{/* ===== 常见问题 ===== */}

## 常见问题 / FAQ

**Q: 可以单人游玩吗？**
A: 可以。单人模式难度较高，但可行。建议先熟悉怪物行为再尝试单人挑战。

**Q: Can I play solo?**
A: Yes. Solo mode is much harder but possible. Learn creature behavior before attempting solo runs.

**Q: 存档位置在哪里？**
A: Windows 版存档在 `%AppData%/Lethal Company/` 目录下。

**Q: Where is the save file located?**
A: Windows saves are in the `%AppData%/Lethal Company/` directory.

**Q: 有中文吗？**
A: 游戏官方支持简体中文翻译，在设置中切换即可。

**Q: Does this game have Chinese?**
A: Yes. The game has official Simplified Chinese support. Switch it in the settings.
