## 物品分类 / Item Categories

《致命公司》中的物品分为三大类：**废品**（可回收出售）、**装备**（辅助工具）和 **飞船升级**（永久性提升）。合理选择和管理物品是成功的关键。

Items in *Lethal Company* fall into three categories: **scrap** (recyclable for credits), **equipment** (utility tools), and **ship upgrades** (permanent improvements). Choosing and managing items wisely is key to success.

## 物品管理技巧 / Item Management Tips

1. **优先占用双手的装备**：手电筒和武器共用双手空间，合理搭配
2. **废品堆叠**：某些小型废品可以堆叠携带（如螺丝刀、齿轮）
3. **运输策略**：先集中搬运高价值废品到出口附近，再一起运回飞船
4. **物品栏分配**：建议每人至少带一个对讲机和一个照明工具，剩下两格装废品
5. **标记好物品**：使用对讲机告知队友你找到了高价值物品的位置

1. **Hands management**: Flashlight and weapons share hand slots; choose combinations wisely
2. **Scrap stacking**: Some small scraps can be stacked (screwdrivers, gears)
3. **Transport strategy**: Move high-value scrap to exits first, then haul to ship in bulk
4. **Inventory allocation**: Carry at least one walkie-talkie and one light source, leaving two slots for scrap
5. **Mark items**: Use walkie-talkies to inform teammates about high-value scrap locations
