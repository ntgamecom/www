# Secrets Guide — Easter Eggs, Hidden Content & Unsolved Mysteries

{/* ===== 隐藏要素概述 ===== */}

## 隐藏内容 / Hidden Content

《致命公司》虽然看起来简单，但其中隐藏着许多秘密和彩蛋。Zeekerss 在游戏中埋下了大量有趣的发现，等待玩家去发掘。本章汇总了所有已知的隐藏要素。

*Lethal Company* may seem simple on the surface, but it hides numerous secrets and easter eggs. Zeekerss embedded many interesting discoveries for players to find. This chapter compiles all known hidden content.

## 隐藏生物 / Hidden Creatures

## 鬼魂女孩 / Ghost Girl

鬼魂女孩是游戏中最神秘的隐藏生物之一。她的出现没有任何预兆，只会随机出现在任何星球上。关于她的起源和机制，玩家社区仍在探索中。

The Ghost Girl is one of the most mysterious hidden creatures in the game. She appears without warning on any moon. The player community is still exploring her origins and mechanics.

**已知机制** / Known mechanics:
- 她只会在你视线之外移动 / She only moves when out of your line of sight
- 被缠住的玩家会看到她的幻影 / The targeted player sees apparitions of her
- 其他玩家完全看不到她 / Other players cannot see her at all
- 持续逃跑最终她可能会离开 / Sustained escape may eventually make her leave

## 面具怪物 / Masked Creature

有传言说在某些极低的概率下，玩家会发现一个戴着白色面具的神秘生物。关于它的信息极少，目击报告也不多。

Rumors persist of a mysterious creature wearing a white mask appearing at extremely low probability. Very little is known about it, with few sighting reports.

**已知信息** / Known info:
- 只在困难星球出现过 / Only reported on hard moons
- 不会主动攻击，只会观察玩家 / Does not attack, only observes players
- 如果被发现会消散消失 / Disappears if spotted

## 已知彩蛋 / Known Easter Eggs

## 公司大楼里的秘密 / Secrets in the Company Building

在公司总部出售废品时，如果你仔细观察周围环境，会发现一些有趣的东西：

When selling scrap at the Company headquarters, look closely at the environment:

- 墙上有一张Zeekerss早期项目的海报 / A poster on the wall shows Zeekerss' earlier project
- 某些收银台后面有隐藏的涂鸦 / Hidden graffiti behind some cash registers
- 墙角有奇怪的符号和文字 / Strange symbols and text in the corners

## 船员名册彩蛋 / Crew Roster Easter Egg

在飞船终端输入特定的名称可以看到隐藏信息：

Entering specific names into the ship terminal reveals hidden messages:

- 输入 `Zeekerss` — 显示开发者致谢信息
- 输入 `help` 多次 — 终端会显示不同的提示信息
- 输入特殊命令可能会触发隐藏的终端响应

- Entering `Zeekerss` — Shows developer credits
- Entering `help` multiple times — Terminal shows different hints
- Special commands may trigger hidden terminal responses

## 剧情传说 / Lore Discoveries

## 公司到底是什么？ / What Is the Company?

玩家社区的传说研究者们拼凑出了关于公司的几个理论：

Lore researchers in the player community have pieced together several theories about the Company:

**理论一：公司已经不存在了** — 我们收到的配额指令可能是自动化系统的残留程序。公司早在很多年前就倒闭了，但我们仍在向一个空荡荡的总部运送废品。

**Theory 1: The Company no longer exists** — The quota instructions are likely remnants of an automated system. The Company went defunct years ago, yet we still deliver scrap to an empty headquarters.

**理论二：废品另有用途** — 公司为什么要收集这些看起来毫无价值的废品？有些玩家认为这些废品被用来制造某种大型装置。

**Theory 2: Scrap has another purpose** — Why does the Company collect seemingly worthless scrap? Some players believe the scrap is used to construct some large device.

## 星球上的遗迹 / Ruins on the Moons

许多星球上散落着明显不是自然形成的建筑遗迹。这些建筑似乎比玩家所在的"公司时代"更加古老。

Many moons have ruins that are clearly not natural formations. These structures appear to be older than the player's "Company era."

- Experimentation 的地下墓室 / Underground chambers
- Assurance 的神秘隧道 / Mysterious tunnels
- Titan 的巨大空洞 / Massive cavities on Titan

这些遗迹暗示在人类（或公司）到来之前，这些星球上曾经存在过智慧文明。

These ruins suggest that intelligent civilizations existed on these moons before humans (or the Company) arrived.

## 隐藏游戏机制 / Hidden Game Mechanics

## 脚步声系统 / Footstep System

大多数玩家不知道的是，游戏有一套精细的脚步声系统。不同地面材质会发出不同音量的脚步声：

Most players don't know there is a detailed footstep system. Different floor materials produce different volume levels:

| 地面材质 / Surface | 脚步声大小 / Footstep Volume |
|---|---|
| 金属 / Metal | 大 / Loud |
| 混凝土 / Concrete | 中 / Medium |
| 泥土 / Dirt | 小 / Quiet |
| 水 / Water | 中等但特殊 / Medium but distinct |

## AI 行为隐藏机制 / Hidden AI Behaviors

- 怪物之间也会互相攻击 / Creatures can attack each other
- 某些怪物会偷取你放在地上的废品 / Some creatures steal scrap left on the ground
- 怪物有疲劳机制，长时间追逐后会休息 / Creatures have a fatigue mechanic and rest after long chases
