## 简单星球详解 / Easy Moons

## Experimentation

**地图复杂度**：低。建筑结构简单，通常只有二层。**Map complexity**: Low. Simple building layout, usually two floors.

Experimentation 是最适合新手的星球。怪物数量有限，天气条件相对温和。建筑物入口明显，内部结构简单。建议新玩家在此星球度过前三轮以熟悉游戏机制。

Experimentation is the best moon for beginners. Limited creature spawns, relatively mild weather. Building entrances are obvious and interiors are straightforward. New players should spend their first few rounds here learning the mechanics.

**推荐策略** / Recommended strategy:
- 全队一起搜索主建筑
- 不要浪费时间去小型外围建筑
- 天黑前半小时撤离

- Search the main building together as a team
- Don't waste time on small peripheral structures
- Evacuate 30 minutes before dark

## Assurance

**地图复杂度**：低。有地下区域，但入口明显。**Map complexity**: Low. Features underground areas with obvious entrances.

Assurance 比 Experimentation 稍难，因为有更多隐藏空间和潜在怪物点。地下区域包含额外废品，但也是怪物藏身之处。

Assurance is slightly harder than Experimentation with more hidden spaces and potential creature spawns. Underground areas contain extra scrap but also serve as creature hiding spots.

**推荐策略** / Recommended strategy:
- 先清空地面建筑
- 派遣两人进入地下搜索
- 注意聆听隧道内的回声判断怪物位置

- Clear ground-level buildings first
- Send two players underground to search
- Listen for echoes in tunnels to detect creatures

## Vow

**地图复杂度**：中。有多个入口的大型建筑。**Map complexity**: Medium. Large building with multiple entrances.

Vow 是简单星球中最难的。地图更复杂，废品价值也更高。这里有可能出现中等威胁的怪物，新手要格外小心。

Vow is the hardest of the easy moons. Complex map layout with higher-value scrap. Medium-threat creatures may appear here, so beginners should be extra cautious.

## 中等星球详解 / Medium Moons

## Offense

**地图复杂度**：中。多层建筑，有地下通道。**Map complexity**: Medium. Multi-story building with underground passages.

Offense 是进入中等难度的良好起点。废品价值有明显提升，但怪物威胁也在增加。建议团队有至少一轮经验后再挑战。

Offense is a good starting point for medium difficulty. Scrap value increases noticeably but so does creature threat. Teams should have at least one round of experience before attempting.

## March

**地图复杂度**：大。开阔区域加多个建筑。**Map complexity**: Large. Open areas with multiple buildings.

March 地图面积大，探索需要更多时间。废品分布较散，运输效率是关键。建议携带喷气背包提高移动速度。

March has a large map area requiring more exploration time. Scrap is spread out, making transport efficiency critical. Bring jetpacks to improve movement speed.

## Adamance

**地图复杂度**：中。紧凑但结构复杂。**Map complexity**: Medium. Compact but structurally complex.

Adamance 的室内区域非常复杂，容易迷路。建议使用对讲机保持通讯，并在进入时记住路线。

Adamance has very complex interior areas where it is easy to get lost. Use walkie-talkies for communication and memorize your route upon entry.

## 困难星球详解 / Hard Moons

## Rend

**地图复杂度**：大。多层室内区域。**Map complexity**: Large. Multi-level interior areas.

Rend 是困难星球的入门。大量高价值废品伴随着高致命性的怪物。不建议新手直接挑战。

Rend serves as the entry point for hard moons. Abundant high-value scrap comes with lethal creatures. Not recommended for beginners.

## Dine

**地图复杂度**：大。迷宫式布局。**Map complexity**: Large. Maze-like layout.

Dine 的布局极其复杂，极容易迷路。强烈建议携带多个对讲机并标记路线。

Dine features an extremely complex layout where getting lost is very easy. Bring multiple walkie-talkies and mark your path.

## Titan

**地图复杂度**：极大。全游戏最大地图。**Map complexity**: Very Large. Largest map in the game.

Titan 是目前游戏中最大且最难的地图。这里有最高的废品价值和最强力的怪物。只有最有经验的团队才能在此生存。

Titan is currently the largest and hardest map in the game. It offers the highest scrap value and the most dangerous creatures. Only the most experienced teams can survive here.

Each moon's weather can affect your exploration strategy:

## 路线规划建议 / Route Planning Tips

1. **前期** (配额 100-500)：选择 Experimentation 或 Assurance，稳扎稳打
2. **中期** (配额 500-1500)：升级到 Offense 或 March，获取更高价值废品
3. **后期** (配额 1500+)：挑战 Rend 或 Titan，高风险高回报
4. **最后一搏**：如果时间紧迫且差距不大，尝试 Vow 快速捡拾

1. **Early game** (Quota 100-500): Stick to Experimentation or Assurance for consistent profits
2. **Mid game** (Quota 500-1500): Upgrade to Offense or March for higher-value scrap
3. **Late game** (Quota 1500+): Challenge Rend or Titan for high risk, high reward
4. **Last resort**: If time is short and quota gap is small, try Vow for quick pickings
