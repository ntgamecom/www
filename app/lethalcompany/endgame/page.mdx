# Endgame Guide — High Quotas & Extreme Survival

## 什么是挑战模式 / What Is Endgame?

当你的配额超过 1000 时，游戏进入了"后期"阶段。此时团队的压力急剧增加：怪物更危险，配额缺口更大，容错率更低。真正的《致命公司》从这时候开始。

When your quota exceeds 1000, the game enters the "endgame" phase. Team pressure increases dramatically: creatures are more dangerous, quota gaps are larger, and error margins are thinner. The real *Lethal Company* begins here.

## 高配额生存指南 / High Quota Survival Guide

## 配额增长规律 / Quota Growth Pattern

| 轮次 / Round | 典型配额 / Typical Quota | 建议星球 / Recommended Moon |
|---|---|---|
| 1-3 | 100-400 | Experimentation, Assurance |
| 4-6 | 400-1000 | Offense, March |
| 7-10 | 1000-2500 | Rend, Dine |
| 11+ | 2500-5000+ | Titan, Artifice |

## 高配额策略 / High Quota Strategy

**第一天**：进行侦察式探索。不要带太多废品回船，重点是了解当天怪物的分布情况。

**Day 1**: Conduct reconnaissance exploration. Don't bring too much scrap back; focus on learning the day's creature distribution.

**第二天**：全力搜索。全员出动，分工明确。这是最重要的收获日。

**Day 2**: Full-scale search. All hands on deck with clear role division. This is the most important harvest day.

**第三天（最后一天）**：如果已经达标，可以提前回去。如果还差一些，冒险去高风险区域补差额。

**Day 3 (final day)**: If quota is met, head back early. If still short, take risks in high-danger areas to fill the gap.

## 极限挑战 / Extreme Challenges

## 单人模式 / Solo Mode

单人模式是《致命公司》中最难的挑战。以下是单人生存的关键要点：

Solo mode is the hardest challenge in *Lethal Company*. Here are the key survival points:

- **专注于一个星球** / Focus on one moon — 选择 Experimentation 或 Assurance，反复刷
- **保守为上** / Play conservatively — 任何伤害都是致命的，不要冒险
- **充分利用对讲机和监控** / Use walkie-talkies and monitors — 虽然只有一个人，但可以放在不同位置监听
- **优先飞船升级** / Prioritize ship upgrades — 医疗站和扫描器对单人尤其重要
- **耐心** / Be patient — 单人模式一局可能长达数小时

## 无死亡挑战 / No Death Challenge

从第一轮到第 N 轮，整个团队不能有任何死亡：

From round 1 to round N, the entire team must have zero deaths:

- **最佳星球**：Vow — 难度适中，可预测性强
- **绝对不要**在日食天气探索
- **全员回船**策略：一个人不落地不要起飞
- **装备优先**：每人配备 Pro 手电筒和高级武器

- **Best moon**: Vow — Moderate difficulty with predictability
- **Never** explore during eclipses
- **All aboard** policy: Don't take off until everyone is on the ship
- **Gear priority**: Equip everyone with Pro Flashlights and advanced weapons

## 后期飞船管理 / Late-Game Ship Management

## 必备升级 / Essential Upgrades

进入后期前，以下飞船升级必须完成：

Before entering endgame, these ship upgrades must be completed:

1. **医疗站 / Medical Station** (200) — 减少死亡概率
2. **燃料罐 / Fuel Tank** (100) — 可以到达所有星球
3. **扫描升级 / Scanner Upgrade** (150) — 提高搜索效率
4. **信号发射器 / Signal Transmitter** (120) — 关键时刻救命

## 库存管理 / Inventory Management

后期建议在飞船上保留以下物品：

In the endgame, keep these items on the ship:

- 2-3 个备用 Pro 手电筒
- 至少 2 个信号枪
- 1 个开锁器
- 备用对讲机
- 额外的铲子或铁锹

- 2-3 spare Pro Flashlights
- At least 2 Signal Guns
- 1 Lockpicker
- Spare walkie-talkies
- Extra shovels or spades

## 最终建议 / Final Advice

1. **团队默契最重要** / Team synergy matters most — 配合多年的团队才有机会冲击最高记录
2. **学会止损** / Learn to cut losses — 有时放弃一轮比全员阵亡更明智
3. **保持冷静** / Stay calm — 高配额下压力巨大，冷静决策才能生存
4. **不断学习** / Keep learning — 社区每天都有新发现，保持关注
5. **享受过程** / Enjoy the process — 即使失败，与朋友一起的回忆才是无价的

1. **Team synergy matters most** — Only well-coordinated teams can challenge top records
2. **Learn to cut losses** — Sometimes skipping a round is smarter than total team wipe
3. **Stay calm** — High quota pressure is intense; only calm decisions lead to survival
4. **Keep learning** — The community discovers new things daily; stay informed
5. **Enjoy the process** — Even in failure, memories with friends are priceless
