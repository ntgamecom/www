## 主线剧情 / Main Story

黄金树幽影的主线围绕**米凯拉**和**玛丽卡**的故事展开——玩家进入幽影之地追寻米凯拉的足迹——最终面对约定之王拉塔恩。主线本身不长——大约4-6小时——但大量内容藏在支线和探索中 / Shadow of the Erdtree main story revolves around **Miquella** and **Marika** — player follows Miquella's footsteps through the Land of Shadow — culminating in Promised Consort Radahn. Main story is short — ~4-6 hours — but most content is in side quests and exploration.

### 主线步骤 / Main Quest Steps

1. **进入幽影之地**: 在蒙格温王朝触碰米凯拉的茧 / Enter Land of Shadow: touch Miquella's Cocoon in Mohgwyn Palace
2. **探索墓地平原**: 找到地图碎片——激活所有幽影树碎片——前往塔之镇贝瑞特 / Explore Gravesite Plain: find map fragments — activate Scadutree Fragments — go to Belurat
3. **挑战塔之镇贝瑞特**: 击败神兽舞狮——获得进入幽影亚坛的资格 / Challenge Belurat: defeat Divine Beast Dancing Lion — gain access to Scadu Altus
4. **探索恩希斯城**: 击败双月骑士蕾拉娜——打开通往幽影亚坛的道路 / Explore Castle Ensis: defeat Rellana — open path to Scadu Altus
5. **幽影亚坛自由探索**: 探索高原——寻找米凯拉的十字——强化幽影树碎片 / Scadu Altus free exploration: explore plateau — find Miquella's Crosses — upgrade Scadutree Fragments
6. **暗影要塞**: 深入暗影要塞——击败穿刺者梅瑟莫——前往艾尼尔-伊利姆 / Shadow Keep: delve deep — defeat Messmer — proceed to Enir-Ilim
7. **艾尼尔-伊利姆**: 最终区域——击败约定之王拉塔恩——见证结局 / Enir-Ilim: final zone — defeat Promised Consort Radahn — witness ending

## 主要NPC任务线 / Major NPC Questlines

### 蕾妲 / Needle Knight Leda

DLC的核心NPC之一——她在第一区域和你碰面——之后多次在关键地点相遇。她是米凯拉的坚定追随者——你的选择会影响她的态度。**任务**: 和她对话选择支持或质疑米凯拉的动机——这会解锁分支——最终在暗影要塞你会面临抉择——帮助她还是对抗她。**奖励**: 蕾妲的刺剑套装——如果你在最后选择对抗她——击败她获得她的全套装备 / One of the DLC's core NPCs — meets you in first zone — encountered at key locations. She is Miquella's devoted follower — choices affect her attitude. **Quest**: dialogue choices to support or question Miquella — branches — final choice in Shadow Keep to help or oppose her. **Reward**: Leda's Rapier set — if you oppose and defeat her, gain full armor set.

### 角人 / Hornsent

在墓地平原遇到——他是幽影之地的原住民——对黄金树一族有深仇大恨。**任务链**: 帮助他清除几个区域的敌人→他会给你关键物品→之后在暗影要塞再次相遇→他的态度取决于你是否支持米凯拉。**关键选择**: 在暗影要塞中——他请求你帮助复仇——如果你同意——他在最终战前会帮助你——但如果米凯拉线走到终点——他可能成为敌人 / Met in Gravesite Plain — native of Land of Shadow — deep hatred of Golden Order. **Questline**: help clear enemies → key items → meet again in Shadow Keep — attitude depends on Miquella support. **Key choice**: in Shadow Keep — he asks for help with revenge — if yes, he helps before final fight — but may become enemy at journey's end.

### 休里耶 / Thiollier

毒药NPC——在墓地平原的一个悬崖边遇到——他研究米凯拉的梦境。**任务链**: 给他找到**黑铁毒药**→他会兴奋→之后告诉你关于**米凯拉睡眠**的秘密→给你进入谷底森林的指引。**奖励**: 强力毒药投掷物品——以及谷底森林的解锁 / Poison NPC — found at cliff in Gravesite Plain — studying Miquella's dream. **Questline**: find **Black Iron Poison** → he gets excited → tells about **Miquella's Sleep** secret → gives access to Abyssal Woods. **Reward**: powerful poison throwing items + Abyssal Woods access.

### 红狮子骑士 / Redmane Freyja

在幽影亚坛遇到——拉塔恩的旧部——她来幽影之地寻找拉塔恩的秘密。**任务线**: 简短——对话了解拉塔恩的背景——最终她会作为敌对NPC之一出现。**奖励**: 关于拉塔恩和米凯拉契约的关键剧情信息 / Found in Scadu Altus — Radahn's old ally — seeks Radahn's secrets. **Questline**: short — dialogue for Radahn lore — eventually appears as hostile NPC. **Reward**: key lore info on Radahn and Miquella's pact.

## 隐秘事件 / Hidden Events

**米凯拉的十字标记**: DLC中存在约25个米凯拉的十字——每个十字有和他相关的记忆——阅读全部可以完整理解DLC剧情。这些十字同时也是快速旅行的解锁点。**幽影之地的神兽**: 在塔之镇舞狮之外——幽影亚坛还有其他神兽Boss——击败所有神兽获得**神兽套装**——大幅提升跳跃攻击伤害。**地下洞窟**: DLC有3个大型地下洞窟——每个包含独特Boss和护符——推荐全部探索 / **Miquella's Crosses**: ~25 crosses — each holds memory of Miquella — reading all reveals full DLC story. Also serve as fast travel unlocks. **Divine Beasts**: beyond Belurat's Dancing Lion — more divine beast bosses in Scadu Altus — defeat all for **Divine Beast Set** — big jump attack boost. **Underground Catacombs**: 3 large catacombs — each with unique boss + talisman — explore all.

## 结局选择 / Ending Choices

DLC的结局和本体一样不直接显示——击败拉塔恩后触发过场——基于你的选择和对米凯拉的态度。**主要结局差异**: 如果你在整个游戏中对米凯拉持支持态度——结局中你会**协助米凯拉达成目的**(具体含义保留神秘感)。如果你质疑米凯拉或对抗他的追随者——结局可能是**米凯拉失败**。两个结局都回到原来的世界——但最终CG不同——对本体游戏结局没有直接影响 / DLC ending, like base game, is not directly displayed — triggers cutscene after Radahn — based on choices toward Miquella. **Main ending difference**: if supportive of Miquella throughout — you **help Miquella achieve his goal** (spoilers omitted). If you question Miquella or oppose his followers — ending may be **Miquella's failure**. Both endings return to base world — different final CG — no direct effect on base game ending.

## 全剧情时间线 / Complete Story Timeline

**基础背景**: 米凯拉在破碎战争后进入幽影之地——寻找剥离自身神性的方法——以及和拉塔恩的契约——约定成为新世界的王与神。幽影之地是玛丽卡的诞生地——也是黄金树的源头。**你的角色**: 褪色者追寻米凯拉的足迹——逐渐揭示他的真实目的。**核心矛盾**: 米凯拉想创造一个没有死亡和痛苦的世界——但代价是所有人失去自由意志。你的选择决定这个新世界的命运 / **Background**: Miquella entered Land of Shadow after Shattering — seeking to shed his divinity — and his pact with Radahn — to become the new world's Lord and God. The Land of Shadow is Marika's birthplace — and the Erdtree's origin. **Your role**: Tarnished follows Miquella's footsteps — gradually revealing his true purpose. **Core conflict**: Miquella wants to create a world without death and suffering — but at the cost of everyone's free will. Your choices determine this new world's fate.

## NPC好感度系统 / NPC Affinity System

DLC引入了一个隐藏的好感度系统——你和NPC的对话选择影响他们在关键节点的态度。**高好感度好处**: NPC在Boss战中可以作为召唤出现——某些NPC会给你珍贵的道具——部分NPC在最终战中站在你这一边。**低好感度结果**: NPC在关键点攻击你——失去他们的支线任务线——某些区域可能无法进入。好感度主要通过对话选择影响——选择同情、支持、或帮助NPC会提升好感度。选择冷漠、攻击、或拒绝会降低好感度 / DLC introduces a hidden affinity system — dialogue choices affect NPC attitudes at key moments. **High affinity**: NPCs appear as summons in boss fights — give precious items — side with you at final battle. **Low affinity**: NPCs attack at key points — lose their questlines — some areas become inaccessible. Affinity is mostly affected by dialogue choices — sympathy, support, and help increase it. Cold, attack, or refusal decrease it.
