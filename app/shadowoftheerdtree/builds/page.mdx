## DLC新增Build思路 / New Build Concepts

DLC带来了8种新武器类型、大量新战技和法术——让Build多样性大幅提升。和本体不同——DLC的敌人更适合**高冲击力Build**(能打断敌人动作)——因为敌人攻击力太高——没有打断能力容易被打到死 / DLC adds 8 new weapon types, new AoWs and spells — massively expanding build diversity. Unlike base game — DLC enemies favor **high poise damage builds** (stagger enemies) — enemies hit too hard — no stagger = get stunlocked to death.

## 力量大盾流 / Strength Greatshield Build

**属性分配(150级)**: 力量60——体力50——耐力40——其余满足武器最低要求 / Stats (SL150): STR 60 — VIG 50 — END 40 — rest meet weapon minimum
**武器**: **大槌**或**火焰骑士大剑**(厚重变质)——副手**指纹石盾**或**圣树纹大盾** / Weapons: **Great Hammer** or **Fire Knight GS** (Heavy) — offhand **Fingerprint Shield** or **Haligtree Greatshield**
**护符**: 大盾护符——龙徽大盾护符——珍珠龙辉护符+3——绿龟护符 / Talismans: Greatshield — Dragoncrest Greatshield — Pearldrake +3 — Green Turtle
**战技**: **决心**(增加下次攻击80%伤害)或**狮子斩**(高冲击力+高伤害) / AoW: **Determination** (+80% next hit) or **Lion's Claw** (high poise + dmg)
**玩法**: 全程举盾——Boss连招结束后用战技反击——大盾护符让举盾几乎无限耐力——安全高效。对魔法Boss换**圣抗大盾** / Playstyle: Hold shield — after boss combo, counter with AoW — Greatshield talisman makes shield near infinite stamina — safe and efficient. Swap to **Holy-def greatshield** for magic bosses.

## 出血反手剑流 / Bleed Backhand Blade Build

**属性分配(150级)**: 敏捷60——感应50——体力45——力量满足最低装备需求 / Stats (SL150): DEX 60 — ARC 50 — VIG 45 — STR meet minimum
**武器**: **反手剑**(血质变)+**任意出血匕首**(副手)/ Backhand Blade (Blood) + any bleed dagger (offhand)
**护符**: 鲜血君王的欢愉——腐烂翼剑徽章——米莉森的义手——亚历山大碎片 / Talismans: Lord of Blood's Exultation — Rotten Winged Sword Insignia — Millicent's Prosthesis — Shard of Alexander
**战技**: **死角一击**(反手剑专属——前冲上挑——出血累积极高)/ Backhand Blade AoW: **Blind Spot** (forward lunge uppercut — very high bleed buildup)
**玩法**: 双持反手剑——跳劈+死角一击循环——出血触发后伤害爆炸。配合**鲜血君王的欢愉**——出血后攻击力+20%——配合灵药进一步提升出血效率。注意: 这个Build很脆——需要精准走位——但DPS全游戏最高 / Playstyle: Dual wield Backhand Blade — jump attack + Blind Spot loop — bleed proc = insane damage. Paired with Lord of Blood's Exultation (+20% ATK on bleed) + physick for more bleed. Note: very squishy — requires precise positioning — but highest DPS in game.

## 信仰火焰骑士流 / Faith Fire Knight Build

**属性分配(150级)**: 信仰60——体力55——耐力30——力量22(火焰骑士大剑要求)/ Stats (SL150): FTH 60 — VIG 55 — END 30 — STR 22 (for Fire Knight GS)
**武器**: **火焰骑士大剑**(焰术质变)+**火焰骑士盾**/ Fire Knight Greatsword (Flame Art) + Fire Knight Shield
**护符**: 火对蝎——亚历山大碎片——龙徽大盾——群聚信徒的立誓布 / Talismans: Fire Scorpion — Shard of Alexander — Dragoncrest Greatshield — Two Fingers Heirloom
**战技**: **火焰之矛**(新战技——远程火焰枪——对人形敌人有特攻)/ AoW: **Flame Spear** (new — ranged fire spear — bonus vs humanoids)
**法术**: 火焰庇护(减伤)——黄金树立誓(Buff)——火焰啊!(清小怪)——野兽活力(回血)/ Incants: Flame Protect (DEF) — Golden Vow (buff) — O, Flame! (AoE) — Bestial Vitality (heal)
**玩法**: 近战以火焰骑士大剑为主——战技火焰之矛可以在安全距离输出。配合火焰庇护+黄金树立誓——减伤约40%。火焰属性在DLC大部分敌人身上有效——没有特别抗火的Boss。**推荐理由**: 最平衡的Build——能打能抗——对新玩家最友好 / Playstyle: Fire Knight GS main DPS — Flame Spear AoW for safe ranged damage. Flame Protect + Golden Vow = ~40% total defense. Fire damage effective against most DLC enemies — no especially fire-resistant bosses. **Best balanced build** — can fight and tank — most beginner-friendly.

## 敏系大刀流 / Dexterity Great Katana Build

**属性分配(150级)**: 敏捷70——体力50——耐力35——力量18(大刀要求)/ Stats (SL150): DEX 70 — VIG 50 — END 35 — STR 18 (for Great Katana)
**武器**: **大刀**(锋利质变——双持)/ Great Katana (Sharp — dual wield)
**护符**: 米莉森的义手——腐烂翼剑徽章——亚历山大碎片——绿龟护符 / Talismans: Millicent's Prosthesis — Rotten Winged Sword — Shard of Alexander — Green Turtle
**战技**: **转啊转**(新战技——持刀旋转——范围大——削韧高)/ AoW: **Spinning** (new — spin attack — large range — high poise dmg)
**玩法**: 大刀的攻击范围超大——保持中距离——利用范围优势在敌人攻击范围外打击。转啊转战技在大群敌人中表现极佳——大型Boss也能用转啊转打出高额削韧。**缺点**: 体力消耗大——需要很多精力管理 / Playstyle: Great Katana has massive range — maintain mid-range — hit enemies outside their attack range. Spinning AoW excels vs groups — also good vs big bosses for poise damage. **Weakness**: high stamina consumption — need good stamina management.

## DLC新增物品适配Build / New Item-Based Builds

DLC新增了大量物品可以围绕它们构建全新Build——以下是几个强力推荐 / DLC adds many items to build around — several powerful recommendations below.

### 米凯拉之光Build / Light of Miquella Build

**核心**: 米凯拉之光祷告(圣属性AOE——对亡灵2倍伤害)——配合圣对蝎护符——黄金树立誓Buff / Core: Light of Miquella incantation (holy AoE — 2x vs undead) — Holy Scorpion Charm — Golden Vow buff
**属性**: 信仰60——体力50——耐力30——力量20(火焰骑士盾牌需求) / Stats: FTH 60 — VIG 50 — END 30 — STR 20 (for Fire Knight Shield)
**装备**: 圣树纹大剑(备用近战)——火焰骑士盾——圣对蝎护符——龙徽大盾护符——双头龟护符——亚历山大碎片 / Gear: Haligtree GS (backup melee) — Fire Knight Shield — Holy Scorpion — Dragoncrest GS — Two-Headed Turtle — Shard of Alexander
**玩法**: 平时用米凯拉之光远程输出——Boss战保持距离——亡灵系敌人2-3发解决。圣属性在DLC中对大部分敌人有效——对最终Boss也有不错伤害 / Playstyle: Light of Miquella for ranged DPS — maintain distance — 2-3 shots kill undead. Holy damage effective against most DLC enemies — decent vs final boss too.

### 腐败龙飨Build / Rot Dragon Communion Build

**核心**: 腐败吐息祷告(累积腐败状态——对Boss百分比掉血)——配合腐败龙徽护符+腐败剑翼护符——腐败状态效率大幅提升 / Core: Rot Breath incantation (builds rot — %HP dmg vs bosses) — Rot Talisman + Rot Winged Sword — rot efficiency boost
**属性**: 信仰30——感应60——体力45——耐力25 / Stats: FTH 30 — ARC 60 — VIG 45 — END 25
**装备**: 龙飨印记(感应补正)——腐败骑士铠甲(腐败抗性)——腐败龙徽——腐败剑翼——群聚信徒立誓布(信仰+5) / Gear: Dragon Communion Seal (ARC scaling) — Rot Knight Armor (rot resist) — Rot Talisman — Rot Winged Sword — Two Fingers Heirloom
**玩法**: Boss战开局吐一口腐败吐息——等待腐败状态慢慢扣血(约30%总HP)——期间全力生存——腐败消失后再吐一口。这个Build打Boss非常安全——主要是在跑动和闪避——输出靠腐败被动。**注意**: 腐败对部分DLC Boss免疫——如双月骑士和梅瑟莫——需要备用输出手段 / Playstyle: Opening rot breath — wait for rot to tick (~30% total HP) — focus on survival — reapply when rot fades. Very safe boss strategy — mostly running and dodging — damage from rot passive. **Note**: rot doesn't work on some DLC bosses — Rellana and Messmer — need backup DPS.
