## 制作材料 / Crafting Materials

DLC增加了新的制作材料。**幽影蘑菇**: 只在幽影亚坛地下洞窟中生长——用于制作大型迸发灵药。**龙鳞碎片**: 尖刺山古龙掉落——用于强化龙飨祷告威力。**亡魂花瓣**: 谷底森林独有——制作驱兽香和镇静香 / DLC adds new crafting materials. **Scadumushroom**: Scadu Altus underground only — large boosted physick. **Dragon Scale Fragment**: Jagged Peak dragon drop — boosts Dragon Communion incantations. **Ghost Petal**: Abyssal Woods exclusive — beast repellent and calming powder.

## 锻造石与强化 / Smithing Stones & Upgrades

DLC中可以获得大量**古龙岩锻造石**和**失色古龙岩锻造石**——适用于将武器强化到+25/+10。每周目DLC内稳定获得约8颗古龙岩锻造石和4颗失色古龙岩锻造石——加上本体的数量——可以全武器满强化。**古龙推荐刷点**: 尖刺山——击杀古龙塞努萨克斯后刷新附近的3只小龙——每只掉落1颗普通古龙岩 / DLC provides many **Ancient Dragon Smithing Stones** and **Somber Ancient Dragon Stones** — for +25/+10 weapons. ~8 normal + 4 somber per NG cycle — combined with base game = max all weapons. **Farm spot**: Jagged Peak — after killing Ancient Dragon Senusax, 3 small dragons respawn nearby — each drops 1 normal Ancient Dragon stone.
