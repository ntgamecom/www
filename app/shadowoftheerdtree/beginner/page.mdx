## 幽影之地入门 / Land of Shadow Basics

黄金树幽影是艾尔登法环的大型DLC——需要击败**碎星拉塔恩**和**鲜血君王蒙格**后才能进入。通过蒙格温王朝宫殿中的**米凯拉的茧**进入幽影之地。难度曲线相比本体大幅提升——即使是高等级角色也会感到吃力——建议至少150级进入 / Shadow of the Erdtree is the major DLC for Elden Ring — requires defeating **Starscourge Radahn** and **Mohg, Lord of Blood**. Enter via **Miquella's Cocoon** in Mohgwyn Palace. Difficulty is significantly higher than base game — even high-level characters will struggle — recommend at least level 150.

## 幽影树碎片全收集 / Scadutree Fragment Locations

幽影树碎片是DLC最重要的收集品——每个碎片提升5%攻击力和减伤率——共50个。收集20个后收益递减(每碎片2.5%)。**开局后第一件事**: 探索初始区域收集至少10-15个碎片——否则你会在第一个精英怪面前被秒杀 / Scadutree Fragments are the DLC's most important collectible — each boosts ATK and DEF by 5% — 50 total. Diminishing returns after 20 (2.5% each). **First thing after entering**: explore starting area for 10-15 fragments — or first elite will one-shot you.

### 关键碎片位置 / Key Fragment Locations

1. **墓地平原**(初始区域): 3个在教堂废墟——2个在道中——1个在击杀第一个小Boss后 / Gravesite Plain (starting area): 3 in church ruins — 2 on path — 1 after first mini-boss
2. **恩希斯城**: 2个在城堡内——1个在城顶——击败双月骑士蕾拉娜后旁边1个 / Castle Ensis: 2 inside — 1 on roof — 1 near Rellana after defeating her
3. **幽影亚坛**: 5个分布在高原各处——需要绕路才能全部获取 / Scadu Altus: 5 scattered across plateau — requires detours
4. **谷底森林**: 2个在森林深处——需要处理视线干扰机制 / Abyssal Woods: 2 deep in forest — requires managing madness buildup
5. **尖刺山**: 3个在龙族区域——和古龙战斗后能额外获得1个 / Jagged Peak: 3 in dragon area — +1 after ancient dragon fight

## 灵灰全收集 / Revered Spirit Ash Locations

灵灰共25个——主要影响灵魂召唤物和灵马的强度。和本体不同——DLC的召唤物在强化前非常脆——不收集灵灰会被Boss两下打散 / 25 total spirit ashes — affect summon and Torrent strength. Unlike base game — DLC summons are very fragile unupgraded — without these, boss two-shots them.

**关键灵灰位置**: 墓地平原(4个)——塔之镇贝瑞特(3个)——幽影亚坛(6个)——暗影要塞(3个)——艾尼尔-伊利姆(9个)。在找到每个米凯拉的十字印记时周围通常有灵灰——注意观察 / Key locations: Gravesite Plain (4) — Belurat (3) — Scadu Altus (6) — Shadow Keep (3) — Enir-Ilim (9). Crosses usually have nearby spirit ashes — pay attention.

## 开局Build推荐 / Starting Build Recommendations

**力量流(推荐新手)**: 刚体大盾+重武——配合幽影树碎片——防御力极高——容错率最高。推荐武器: **大槌**或**火焰骑士大剑**。主点力量/体力/耐力。**敏捷流**: 反手剑或大刀——出血累积快——配合灵灰强化召唤物吸引仇恨——在背后输出。**法术流**: DLC的敌人普遍抗性高——法术流在DLC较弱——但仍可用**米莉安法杖**打Night Comet。**信仰流**: 黄金树幽影新增大量信仰加成装备——最推荐的新流派——**火焰骑士大剑**+**火焰庇护**Buff效果极佳 / **Strength (recommended for beginners)**: Greatshield + heavy weapon — with Scadutree fragments — highest defense — most forgiving. **Dexterity**: Backhand Blade or Great Katana — fast bleed — summon aggro while you DPS. **Sorcery**: DLC enemies have high magic resist — weaker option — use Milady's Staff for Night Comet. **Faith**: DLC adds many faith-scaling items — best new option — Fire Knight GS + Flame Protect = excellent synergy.

## 死亡惩罚与魂量管理 / Death Penalty & Rune Management

幽影之地的死亡惩罚和本体一致——死亡后掉落在死亡位置的所有魂(卢恩)——再次死亡则永久丢失。DLC的卢恩掉落量远高于本体——后期一个精英怪掉落约5-10万卢恩——一个Boss掉落30-50万。**建议**: 身上有大量卢恩时——使用**牺牲之枝**道具(把魂传回最后一次坐篝火的位置)——或者使用**附虫戒指**降低丢失风险。也可以反复刷墓地平原的巨人(约2万卢恩/次——30秒击杀)来快速升级 / Death penalty same as base game — drop all runes on death — second death = permanent loss. DLC rune drops much higher — late game elite drops 50-100k — boss drops 300-500k. **Tip**: use **Sacrificial Twig** (sends runes to last bonfire) or **Ring of Sacrifice** (reduces loss risk). Can farm Gravesite Plain giants (~20k runes/30s kill) for quick levels.

## 新手常见错误 / Common Beginner Mistakes

1. **不收集幽影树碎片就推图**: 最大的错误——没有碎片的情况下DLC的敌人伤害翻倍、你伤害减半——相当于高周目难度。/ Not collecting Scadutree Fragments before progressing — biggest mistake — without them enemies deal 2x dmg, you deal 0.5x dmg = NG+ difficulty.
2. **使用和本体一样的Build不改**: DLC的敌人配置不同——纯法术Build在DLC较弱——纯出血Build对某些Boss无效——建议至少准备2种输出方式。/ Using same build as base game without adjustment — DLC enemy resistances differ — pure sorcery is weak — pure bleed doesn't work on some bosses — prepare 2 damage types.
3. **轻视灵灰强化**: 召唤物在DLC中不加灵灰非常脆——几乎召唤出来就死——建议至少强化到5级以上再去打大型Boss。/ Ignoring Revered Spirit Ashes — unupgraded summons die instantly — upgrade to +5 before major boss fights.
4. **在尖刺山同时拉太多小龙**: 尖刺山的龙群会互相支援——打一只会引来附近2-3只——建议逐个击破或远程引过来单挑。/ Pulling too many dragons on Jagged Peak — they support each other — aggro one at a time with ranged attacks.
5. **不探索隐藏墙壁就离开区域**: DLC的隐藏墙壁数量是本体2-3倍——很多区域需要打碎墙壁才能找到关键道具和碎片。/ Not breaking hidden walls before leaving an area — DLC has 2-3x more hidden walls than base game — many key items and fragments behind them.

## 联机与PVP / Online & PVP

DLC的PVP非常活跃——新武器和战技改变了原有的人口Meta。**推荐PVP武器**: 反手剑(死角一击配合高出血——PVP非常强势)、火焰之矛(远程压制——逼对手走位)、大刀(范围压制——滚戳流)。PVP活动区域主要在幽影亚坛的大桥区域——这里已经成为新的PVP热门地点。联机合作时——幽影树碎片和灵灰的强化进度是账号绑定的——不是你强化的高就高——而是和你本体的进度有关——所以带新人时注意他的强化等级 / DLC PVP is very active — new weapons and AoWs changed the meta. **PVP weapons**: Backhand Blade (Blind Spot + bleed — very strong), Flame Spear (ranged pressure), Great Katana (range control — roll-poke). PVP hotzone: Scadu Altus bridge area — new dueling hotspot. Co-op note: Scadutree and Spirit Ash upgrades are account-bound — relate to base game progress — be mindful when helping lower-level hosts.
