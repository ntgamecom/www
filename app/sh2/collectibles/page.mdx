### 白金奖杯技巧 / Platinum Trophy Tips

1. **第一轮 First Playthrough**: 随意玩，享受故事，尽量收集 (Play freely, enjoy the story, collect as much as possible)
2. **第二轮 Second Playthrough**: 新游戏+，收集所有漏掉的收集品 (New Game+, collect all missed items)
3. **第三轮 Third Playthrough**: 速通和特殊条件跑 (Speed run and special condition run)
4. 使用章节选择功能可以快速补收集 (Use chapter select to quickly补 collectibles)
5. 困难难度通关建议在熟悉游戏后进行 (Recommended to attempt Hard difficulty after familiar)
