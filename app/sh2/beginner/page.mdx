# 新手入门指南 / Beginner Guide

{/* 欢迎来到寂静岭 / Welcome to Silent Hill */}

## 欢迎来到寂静岭 / Welcome to Silent Hill

本页面向初次接触《寂静岭2重制版》的玩家，帮助你快速上手游戏。游戏的核心是探索、解谜与生存，而不是纯粹的暴力战斗。保持冷静，仔细探索每个角落，你才能发现隐藏的秘密。

(This page is for players new to Silent Hill 2 Remake, helping you get started quickly. The core of the game is exploration, puzzle-solving, and survival, not pure combat. Stay calm and explore every corner carefully to discover hidden secrets.)

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanics

### 健康系统 / Health System

游戏没有传统的生命值条。你需要通过以下方式判断 James 的健康状态：

(The game has no traditional health bar. You need to judge James health status through the following methods:)

| 状态 Status | 表现 Indication |
|---|---|
| 健康 Healthy | 正常呼吸 Normal breathing |
| 受伤 Injured | 轻微喘息 Slight panting |
| 严重受伤 Severe | 剧烈喘息，弯腰 Heavy panting, hunched over |
| 濒死 Critical | 剧烈咳嗽，扶墙 Heavy coughing, holding wall |

使用恢复物品时按需使用，不要浪费。绿色草药(Green Herb)恢复少量生命，红色草药(Red Herb)恢复大量生命，急救包(First Aid Kit)完全恢复。

(Use healing items as needed without wasting. Green Herbs restore a small amount of health, Red Herbs restore a large amount, and First Aid Kits fully restore health.)

### 地图系统 / Map System

地图是你在寂静岭中最可靠的伙伴。按 M (PC) 或触控板 (PS5) 打开地图。James 会自动在地图上做标记：

(The map is your most reliable companion in Silent Hill. Press M (PC) or the touchpad (PS5) to open the map. James will automatically mark the map:)

| 标记 Mark | 含义 Meaning |
|---|---|
| 红色× Red X | 当前位置 Current position |
| 红色笔记 Red writing | James 的备注 James notes |
| 锁图标 Lock icon | 锁住的门 Locked door |
| 灰色区域 Gray area | 已探索区域 Explored area |

## 探索技巧 / Exploration Tips

### 手电筒使用技巧 / Flashlight Usage

手电筒(Flashlight)是你的主要光源。在黑暗区域必须使用，但请注意：光线会暴露你的位置给敌人。有时关闭手电筒可以悄悄绕过敌人。

(The flashlight is your primary light source. It must be used in dark areas, but be aware: light will expose your position to enemies. Sometimes turning off the flashlight can help you stealthily bypass enemies.)

### 收音机机制 / Radio Mechanics

James 随身携带的收音机(Radio)会在附近有敌人时发出杂音。这是一个重要的警戒工具：

(The radio James carries will emit static noise when enemies are nearby. This is an important warning tool:)

- 收音机杂音增强 = 敌人接近 (Radio static intensifies = enemy approaching)
- 杂音方向 = 敌人方位 (Static direction = enemy location)
- 可以关闭收音机减少注意，但会失去预警 (You can turn off the radio to reduce attention but lose the warning)

### 资源管理 / Resource Management

寂静岭中的资源非常有限。以下是一些管理建议：

(Resources in Silent Hill are very limited. Here are some management suggestions:)

- **子弹 Bullets**: 不要浪费在普通敌人上，很多时候逃跑是更好的选择 (Do not waste on common enemies; running away is often the better choice)
- **草药 Herbs**: 受伤后不要立即使用，等健康下降到黄色再使用 (Do not use immediately after getting hurt; wait until health drops to yellow)
- **保存 Save**: 使用红色保存点(红色光圈红色圆圈)保存进度，游戏没有自动保存功能 (Use red save points to save progress; the game has no autosave function)
- **探索 Exploration**: 每个房间都要搜索，抽屉和柜子可能藏有物品 (Search every room; drawers and cabinets may hide items)

## 新手生存法则 / Beginner Survival Rules

1. **保持冷静 Stay Calm** - 恐慌会导致错误操作和资源浪费 (Panic leads to wrong moves and wasted resources)
2. **善用地图 Use the Map** - 经常查看地图，标记未探索区域 (Check the map often, mark unexplored areas)
3. **节约弹药 Conserve Ammo** - 枪械主要用于BOSS战和紧急情况 (Guns are mainly for boss fights and emergencies)
4. **近战优先 Melee First** - 能用近战武器解决的敌人就不要开枪 (If you can solve enemies with melee weapons, do not shoot)
5. **聆听收音机 Listen to the Radio** - 收音机杂音是重要的预警信号 (Radio static is an important early warning signal)
6. **探索每个角落 Explore Everything** - 关键物品可能藏在最不起眼的地方 (Key items may be hidden in the most inconspicuous places)
7. **管理背包 Manage Inventory** - 合理分配物品栏空间 (Reasonably allocate inventory space)
8. **阅读备忘录 Read Memos** - 很多线索藏在收集到的备忘录中 (Many clues are hidden in collected memos)
