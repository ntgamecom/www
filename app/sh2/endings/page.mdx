## 结局系统概述 / Ending System Overview

(Silent Hill 2 Remake has 8 different endings. The ending you get depends on various choices and actions throughout the game. Three are classic endings from the original, and five are new in the remake.)

## 结局1: 离开 / Leave

这个结局被认为是游戏的"好结局"。詹姆斯离开寂静岭，带着对玛丽的记忆和真相继续生活。

(This ending is considered the good ending. James leaves Silent Hill, continuing his life with the memory and truth of Mary.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 在物品栏中频繁查看玛丽的信和照片 (Frequently check Mary letter and photo in inventory)
- 仔细阅读医院 2 楼医生办公室中的玛丽病历 (Carefully read Mary medical record in the doctor office on hospital 2F)
- 在湖景酒店听完玛丽的全篇信件 (Listen to the full letter from Mary at Lakeview Hotel)
- 尽量减少对玛丽亚的关注 (Minimize attention to Maria)

| 行动 Action | 影响 Influence |
|---|---|
| 查看信件 Check Letter | 高 High |
| 查看照片 Check Photo | 中 Medium |
| 关注玛丽亚 Care for Maria | 负 Negative |
| 阅读病历 Read Medical Record | 中 Medium |

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
詹姆斯和劳拉一起离开寂静岭。詹姆斯终于接受了玛丽去世的事实，准备开始新的人生。劳拉捡到了玛丽写给詹姆斯的信。阳光洒在小镇的出口，迷雾渐渐散去。

(James leaves Silent Hill with Laura. He finally accepts Mary death and prepares to start a new life. Laura picks up the letter Mary wrote to James. Sunlight shines on the town exit as the fog gradually clears.)

## 结局2: 玛丽亚 / Maria

詹姆斯带着玛丽亚离开寂静岭，试图用玛丽亚替代玛丽。

(James leaves Silent Hill with Maria, trying to use Maria as a replacement for Mary.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 尽可能多地与玛丽亚互动 (Interact with Maria as much as possible)
- 保护玛丽亚不受到敌人伤害 (Protect Maria from enemy damage)
- 经常查看玛丽亚的健康状态 (Frequently check Maria health status)
- 忽视玛丽的物品（信和照片）(Neglect Mary items - letter and photo)

| 行动 Action | 影响 Influence |
|---|---|
| 保护玛丽亚 Protect Maria | 高 High |
| 与玛丽亚对话 Talk to Maria | 高 High |
| 查看信件 Check Letter | 负 Negative |
| 查看照片 Check Photo | 负 Negative |

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
詹姆斯和玛丽亚离开寂静岭。詹姆斯似乎把玛丽亚当成了玛丽，但玛丽亚开始咳嗽，暗示她和玛丽的命运可能相同。詹姆斯陷入了新的幻觉循环。

(James leaves Silent Hill with Maria. He seems to treat Maria as Mary, but Maria starts coughing, suggesting her fate may be the same as Mary. James falls into a new cycle of illusion.)

## 结局3: 在水中 / In Water

詹姆斯无法承受内心的罪孽，选择了结生命。

(James cannot bear his inner guilt and chooses to end his life.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 查看医院中的玛丽病历 (Read Mary medical record in hospital)
- 在 Brookhaven 医院中听完整段对话 (Listen to the full conversation in Brookhaven Hospital)
- 健康经常处于低状态 (Health frequently in low status)
- 查看关于死亡和水的备忘录 (Read memos about death and water)
- 对 Jill 的来信不感兴趣 (Show no interest in certain events)

| 行动 Action | 影响 Influence |
|---|---|
| 健康低下 Low Health | 高 High |
| 阅读死亡相关笔记 Read Death Memos | 高 High |
| 查看病历 Check Medical Record | 中 Medium |
| 查看信件 Check Letter | 负 Negative |

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
詹姆斯坐在车里，车子沉入水中。他在车中闭着眼睛，脸上带着平静的表情。这暗示詹姆斯选择了在水中结束自己的生命，以惩罚自己的罪行。

(James sits in a car as it sinks into the water. His eyes are closed with a peaceful expression. This suggests James chose to end his life in the water as punishment for his crime.)

## 结局4: 重生 / Rebirth

詹姆斯试图通过各种神秘仪式让玛丽复活。

(James attempts to resurrect Mary through various occult rituals.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 收集以下四件关键物品 (Collect the following four key items):
  - 白女巫之石 White Chrism (历史协会 Historical Society)
  - 猩红圣杯 Crimson Ceremony (湖景酒店 Lakeview Hotel)
  - 复活之书 Book of Resurrection (托卢卡监狱 Toluca Prison)
  - 黑曜石珠 Obsidian Goblet (南谷西区停车场 West South Vale parking lot)
- 在最终 BOSS 战前确保物品栏中有所有四件物品 (Ensure all four items are in inventory before the final boss fight)

**关键物品位置 Key Item Locations**:

| 物品 Item | 位置 Location | 区域 Area |
|---|---|---|
| 白女巫之石 White Chrism | 历史协会 2 楼祭坛 | Historical Society 2F altar |
| 猩红圣杯 Crimson Ceremony | 酒店 1 楼餐厅 | Hotel 1F restaurant |
| 复活之书 Book of Resurrection | 监狱图书馆 | Prison library |
| 黑曜石珠 Obsidian Goblet | 南谷西区蓝色卡车 | West South Vale blue truck |

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
詹姆斯带上四件仪式物品，划船前往湖中心。他希望通过仪式让玛丽复活。这是一个开放式的结局，暗示詹姆斯可能通过黑暗力量实现了他的愿望。

(James takes the four ritual items and rows a boat to the center of the lake. He hopes to resurrect Mary through the ritual. This is an open ending, suggesting James may have fulfilled his wish through dark forces.)

## 结局5: 极乐 / Bliss (新增)

詹姆斯陷入了错觉，认为玛丽还活着。

(James falls into an illusion, believing Mary is still alive.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 在游戏中不使用恢复物品 (Do not use any healing items in the game)
- 或者 (Or)
- 在整个游戏过程中尽可能少地受到伤害 (Take as little damage as possible throughout the game)

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
詹姆斯拿着玛丽的照片微笑，完全忘记了真相。他活在自己创造的幸福幻觉中，认为玛丽还活着且健康。这是一个令人不寒而栗的幸福结局。

(James smiles while holding Mary photo, having completely forgotten the truth. He lives in a happy illusion he created, believing Mary is alive and healthy. This is a chillingly happy ending.)

## 结局6: 寂静 / Stillness (新增)

詹姆斯陷入了永恒的沉默。

(James falls into eternal silence.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 在游戏进行过程中不对收音机进行任何互动 (Do not interact with the radio at all during the game)
- 不收集任何备忘录 (Do not collect any memos)
- 或者 (Or)
- 在关键剧情选择中保持沉默 (Stay silent during key story choices)

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
詹姆斯站在空无一人的寂静岭街道上，一动不动。画面逐渐变成灰色。这个结局暗示詹姆斯选择将自己从世界中抽离，陷入永恒的寂静。

(James stands motionless on the empty streets of Silent Hill. The image gradually turns gray. This ending suggests James chooses to withdraw from the world,陷入 eternal stillness.)

## 结局7: UFO / 飞碟 (趣味结局)

詹姆斯被外星人带走，这是一个恶搞结局。

(James is taken by aliens; this is a joke ending.)

**触发条件 Requirements**:
- 收集蓝色宝石 Blue Gem (在游戏开始时的便利商店后的巷子里) (Find the Blue Gem in the alley behind the convenience store at the start)
- 在特定位置使用蓝色宝石 (Use the Blue Gem at specific locations):
  - 第一次: 在伍德赛德公寓屋顶 (First: Wood Side Apartments rooftop)
  - 第二次: 在托卢卡监狱屋顶 (Second: Toluca Prison rooftop)
  - 第三次: 在湖景酒店屋顶 (Third: Lakeview Hotel rooftop)
- 在最终 BOSS 战前使用三次 (Use it three times before the final boss fight)

**剧情描述 Story Description**:
游戏最终场景中，UFO 出现并照射詹姆斯，他被传送到飞碟上。一个穿着外星人服装的"Silent Hill 制作团队"出现。这是一个完全为了娱乐的结局。

(In the final scene, a UFO appears and beams James up. A Silent Hill production team member in an alien costume appears. This is a purely comedic ending.)

## 结局8: 隐藏结局 / Hidden Ending (新增)

这是重制版新增的隐藏结局，达成条件尚未完全确认。

(This is a hidden ending added in the remake; the requirements have not been fully confirmed.)

- 收集所有奇异照片 (Collect all Strange Photos)
- 在特定时机查看特定备忘录 (Read specific memos at specific times)
