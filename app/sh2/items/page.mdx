## 物品系统概述 / Item System Overview

《寂静岭2重制版》中的物品分为几大类：武器、恢复道具、钥匙物品、备忘录和收集品。合理管理物品栏是生存的关键。物品栏容量有限，你需要决定携带哪些物品。

(Items in Silent Hill 2 Remake are divided into several categories: weapons, healing items, key items, memos, and collectibles. Managing your inventory wisely is key to survival. Inventory space is limited, and you must decide which items to carry.)

## 备忘录系统 / Memo System

备忘录(Memos)是游戏中最重要的信息来源。它们提供谜题线索、剧情背景和隐藏秘密。

(Memos are the most important source of information in the game. They provide puzzle clues, story background, and hidden secrets.)

| 类型 Type | 数量 Count | 内容 Content |
|---|---|---|
| 故事类 Story | 15+ | 角色背景和剧情 (Character background and story) |
| 谜题提示 Puzzle Hint | 20+ | 谜题解法和密码 (Puzzle solutions and codes) |
| 环境类 Environmental | 10+ | 世界背景设定 (World background setting) |
| 收集类 Collectible | 5+ | 收集品提示 (Collectible hints) |

**备忘录阅读技巧 Memo Reading Tips**:
- 每个备忘录都要仔细阅读 (Read every memo carefully)
- 注意备忘录中的数字和符号标记 (Note numbers and symbol marks in memos)
- 部分备忘录可以翻面查看 (Some memos can be flipped to check the other side)
- 在物品栏中按 △ (PS5) 或 E (PC) 查看备忘录细节 (Press △ (PS5) or E (PC) in inventory to view memo details)

## 物品栏管理 / Inventory Management

1. **丢弃重要吗 Discard Important?** - 不要丢弃钥匙物品，它们不会被误删 (Do not discard key items, they cannot be accidentally deleted)
2. **武器切换 Weapon Switching** - 常用武器放前三个槽位 (Place commonly used weapons in the first three slots)
3. **药品携带 Medicine Carry** - 携带 2-3 个草药和 1 个急救包 (Carry 2-3 herbs and 1 first aid kit)
4. **备忘录阅读 Memo Reading** - 备忘录不占物品栏，随时可读 (Memos do not occupy inventory, readable anytime)
5. **补给管理 Supply Management** - 进入新区域前确保补给充足 (Ensure sufficient supplies before entering new areas)
