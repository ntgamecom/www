# 寂静岭2重制版攻略指南 / Silent Hill 2 Remake Guide

{/* 游戏概述 / Game Overview */}

## 游戏概述 / Game Overview

《寂静岭2重制版》(Silent Hill 2 Remake) 由 Bloober Team 开发，使用 Unreal Engine 5 引擎重制。游戏于 2024 年 10 月 8 日登陆 PlayStation 5 和 PC (Steam) 平台。本作是对 2001 年经典生存恐怖游戏《寂静岭2》的完全重制，保留了原作的灵魂与故事，同时在画面、战斗系统和探索体验上进行了全面升级。

(Silent Hill 2 Remake is developed by Bloober Team using Unreal Engine 5. It was released on October 8, 2024 for PlayStation 5 and PC (Steam). This is a complete remake of the classic 2001 survival horror game, preserving the original spirit and story while fully upgrading the graphics, combat system, and exploration experience.)

## 与原作的关键区别 / Key Differences from Original

| 方面 Aspect | 原版 Original | 重制版 Remake |
|---|---|---|
| 视角 Camera | 固定视角 Fixed camera | 越肩第三人称 Over-the-shoulder third-person |
| 战斗 Combat | 简单攻击 Simple attack | 闪避+连击 Dodge+combo system |
| 地图 Map | 自动标记 Auto-marking | 更详细的手动标记 More detailed manual marking |
| 画面 Graphics | PS2 时代 PS2 era | Unreal Engine 5 次世代 UE5 next-gen |
| 音频 Audio | 立体声 Stereo | 3D 空间音频 3D spatial audio |
| 谜题 Puzzles | 固定难度 Fixed difficulty | 三档难度可选 Three difficulty levels |
| 探索 Exploration | 线性探索 Linear | 更开放的区域 More open areas |
| 音效 Audio | 传统音效 Traditional | 3D 空间音频，PS5 手柄反馈 3D spatial audio, PS5 haptic feedback |
| 保存 Save | 打字机保存 Typewriter save | 红色光圈保存点 Red save points |

## 开发团队与技术支持 / Development and Technology

本作由 Bloober Team 开发，该团队以《层层恐惧》(Layers of Fear)和《灵媒》(The Medium)等心理恐怖游戏闻名。Konami 提供原作授权和监修。游戏使用 Unreal Engine 5 引擎，支持 Lumen 全局光照和 Nanite 几何系统，呈现了令人惊叹的视觉效果。

(This game is developed by Bloober Team, known for psychological horror games like Layers of Fear and The Medium. Konami provided original license and supervision. The game uses Unreal Engine 5 with Lumen global illumination and Nanite geometry system, delivering stunning visual quality.)
