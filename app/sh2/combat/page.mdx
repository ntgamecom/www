# 战斗系统指南 / Combat System Guide

{/* 武器与战斗机制详解 / Weapons and Combat Mechanics */}

## 战斗系统概述 / Combat System Overview

《寂静岭2重制版》的战斗系统相比原版有了显著提升。游戏采用了越肩视角的即时战斗，加入了闪避机制和更丰富的攻击组合。理解每一种武器的特性是生存的关键。

(Silent Hill 2 Remake combat system has been significantly improved compared to the original. The game features over-the-shoulder real-time combat with a dodge mechanic and richer attack combinations. Understanding each weapon characteristics is key to survival.)

## 近战武器 / Melee Weapons

### 钢管 (Steel Pipe)

钢管是游戏早期获得的第一件武器，也是你最可靠的伙伴。

(The steel pipe is the first weapon obtained early in the game and your most reliable companion.)

| 伤害 Damage | 中 Medium |

| 范围 Range | 短 Short |
| 耐久 Durability | 无限 Infinite |
| 获得位置 Location | 公寓入口处 Apartment entrance |

**战斗技巧 Combat Tips**:
- 普通攻击 x3 然后后退等待体力恢复 (Attack x3 then back off to recover stamina)
- 蓄力重攻击可以击倒大多数敌人 (Charged heavy attack can knock down most enemies)
- 对倒地敌人追加攻击造成额外伤害 (Follow-up attack on downed enemies deals extra damage)

**战斗技巧 Combat Tips**:

- 范围略长，可以保持一定距离攻击 (Slightly longer range, can maintain some distance)
- 适合清理普通护士怪 (Suitable for clearing regular nurse enemies)

### 大刀 (Great Knife)

- 蓄力攻击可以穿透多个敌人 (Charged attack can pierce through multiple enemies)

## 战斗机制 / Combat Mechanics

### 闪避系统 / Dodge System

闪避(Dodge)是本作新增的核心战斗机制。按 L1 (PS5) 或 Space (PC) 进行闪避。

(Dodge is a core combat mechanic added in this remake. Press L1 (PS5) or Space (PC) to dodge.)

| 闪避类型 Type | 操作方法 Method |
|---|---|
| 后闪 Backward dodge | 按下闪避键 Press dodge button |
| 侧闪 Side dodge | 左/右方向+闪避 Left/right + dodge |
| 前闪 Forward dodge | 前方向+闪避 Forward + dodge |
| 连续闪避 Chain dodge | 闪避后立即再次闪避 Dodge then immediately dodge again |

**闪避技巧 Dodge Tips**:
- 闪避有无敌帧，正确时机可以完全避免伤害 (Dodge has invincibility frames; correct timing can completely avoid damage)
- 攻击后立即闪避可以取消攻击后摇 (Dodge immediately after attack to cancel recovery animation)
- 连续闪避消耗更多体力，注意管理体力条 (Chain dodging consumes more stamina, manage stamina bar)
- 熟悉敌人攻击节奏，预判闪避时机 (Learn enemy attack patterns, predict dodge timing)

### 体力管理 / Stamina Management

所有攻击和闪避都消耗体力(Stamina)。体力条在屏幕左下角。

(All attacks and dodges consume stamina. The stamina bar is in the bottom-left corner of the screen.)

- 体力耗尽时无法攻击和闪避，非常危险 (Cannot attack or dodge when stamina is depleted, very dangerous)
- 停止行动时体力快速恢复 (Stamina recovers quickly when not acting)
- 建议不要将体力完全用尽，留一些用于闪避 (Recommended not to fully deplete stamina, keep some for dodging)
- 轻攻击消耗体力较少，重攻击消耗较多 (Light attacks consume less stamina, heavy attacks consume more)

## 战斗策略总结 / Combat Strategy Summary

1. **近战优先 Melee Priority** - 尽量使用近战节省子弹 (Try to use melee to save bullets)
2. **闪避为王 Dodge is King** - 掌握闪避时机比攻击更重要 (Mastering dodge timing is more important than attacking)
3. **地形利用 Use Terrain** - 走廊和狭窄地形限制敌人移动 (Corridors and narrow terrain limit enemy movement)
4. **资源管理 Resource Management** - 每种武器都有其用途，合理切换 (Each weapon has its use, switch wisely)
5. **敌我识别 Identify Enemies** - 有些敌人不值得战斗，绕过更高效 (Some enemies are not worth fighting, bypassing is more efficient)
