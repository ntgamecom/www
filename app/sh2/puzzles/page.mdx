## 谜题系统概述 / Puzzle System Overview

《寂静岭2重制版》保留了原版经典的谜题系统，并新增了谜题难度选择。在开始新游戏时，你可以选择谜题难度：简单(Easy)、标准(Standard)或困难(Hard)。难度影响谜题的解法和密码，不影响故事发展。

(Silent Hill 2 Remake retains the classic puzzle system from the original and adds puzzle difficulty selection. When starting a new game, you can choose puzzle difficulty: Easy, Standard, or Hard. Difficulty affects puzzle solutions and passwords but does not affect story progression.)

## 伍德赛德公寓谜题 / Wood Side Apartments Puzzles

### 时钟谜题 / Clock Puzzle

在 205 房间找到时钟，调整指针到正确位置。

(In room 205, find the clock and adjust the hands to the correct position.)

| 难度 Difficulty | 解法 Solution |
|---|---|
| 简单 Easy | 将时钟调到 9:15，根据字条提示 (Set clock to 9:15, follow note hints) |
| 标准 Standard | 将时钟调到 2:45，需要收集三个指针提示 (Set to 2:45, need to collect three hand hints) |
| 困难 Hard | 将时钟调到 10:00，密码来自解谜 (Set to 10:00, code comes from puzzle solving) |

**提示收集 Hint Collection**:
- 203 房间的备忘录提示小时数 (Memo in room 203 hints at the hour)
- 208 房间的备忘录提示分钟数 (Memo in room 208 hints at the minutes)
- 206 房间的备忘录提供最终线索 (Memo in room 206 provides final clue)

(Combine three coins in the coin box in apartment room 108.)

| 男人硬币 Man Coin | 公寓庭院中的尸体上 (On a body in the apartment courtyard) |
| 蛇硬币 Serpent Coin | 207 房间的壁橱里 (In the closet of room 207) |
