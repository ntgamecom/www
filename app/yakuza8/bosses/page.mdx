## 主线Boss攻略 / Story Boss Guides

### 第一章Boss：海藤 / Ch.1 Boss: Kaito

**位置**: 横滨地下避难所 / Location: Yokohama Underground Shelter
**推荐等级**: 6-8 / Recommended Level: 6-8
**阵容建议**: 春日+难波+足立 / Party: Ichiban + Nanba + Adachi

**阶段一**: 海藤使用**铁拳打击**(单体物理)+**抗冲击**(自身防御上升)。打法: 普通攻击积累极技条→第一次极技打Boss→撑到剧情结束即可胜利(不要求击败)。**核心**: 这个战斗是教学——练习使用极技和防御——不需要获胜——QTE别按错就行 / Phase 1: **Iron Fist** (single phys) + **Brace** (DEF up). Strategy: Normal attacks build Heat → use first Extreme Skill → survive until story ends. **Core**: Tutorial fight — practice Extreme Skills and guarding — don't need to win — just don't fail QTEs.

**阶段二**(剧情后追车战): QTE玩法——按提示按键即可——失败扣血但不死亡——可以重试。完成后春日正式加入队伍。**追车战技巧**: 记住按键顺序——不用看画面——看提示位置按——反应更快 / Phase 2 (post-story chase): QTE segment — press prompted buttons — failure costs HP but no death — can retry. **Chase tip**: Memorize button sequence — don't watch screen — feel button positions — faster reaction.

### 第五章Boss：真岛吾朗 / Ch.5 Boss: Majima

**位置**: 苍天堀地下竞技场 / Location: Sotenbori Underground Arena
**推荐等级**: 18-22 / Party: Ichiban + Saeko + Nanba + Adachi (或Joon-gi Han)
**核心机制**: 真岛的**分身**——开场直接召唤3个分身——所有分身有独立行动条。真岛使用**鬼炎斩**(火属性AOE)和**蛇形突进**(单体物理+倒地)。分身使用相同的技能但伤害减半。**难点**: 分身如果不快速清掉——3个分身+真岛本体4个攻击源——治疗压力极大 / Core: **Clones** — summons 3 clones at start — each independent turn. Majima uses **Oni Flame Slash** (fire AoE) + **Serpent Rush** (single phys + knockdown). Clones use same skills at half damage. **Challenge**: 4 attackers (3 clones + Majima) — massive healing pressure if clones stay alive.

**打法**: 使用**全体范围技能**清分身——推荐难波的冰系技能(AOE+减速——让分身行动变慢)或厨师的火焰AOE(顺便燃烧真岛)。清完分身后再集中打真岛——真岛蓄力放**毒蛇之舞**时必被破防——此时所有技能伤害x2——用春日的3格觉醒极技能直接打爆发。**推荐携带**: 全体中回复道具至少5个——这场战斗的治疗压力大 / **Strategy**: Use **AoE skills** to clear clones — Nanba's ice (AoE+slow, delays clone turns) or Chef's flame AoE (burns Majima too). Focus Majima after clones cleared — **Serpent Dance** charge is guaranteed stagger — all skills deal 2x damage during stagger — use Ichiban's 3-bar awakening Extreme for burst. **Items**: 5+ party mid-heals — this fight has heavy healing pressure.

### 第十章Boss：桐生一马 / Ch.10 Boss: Kiryu

**位置**: 神室町剧场前 / Location: Kamurocho Theater Square
**推荐等级**: 40-45 / Party: Ichiban + Saeko + Joon-gi + Adachi
**核心机制**: 桐生是如龙系列的传奇主角——这一战是他作为Boss登场。桐生使用**堂岛之龙**风格——**极技·龙神の拳**(全体物理+50%破防几率)和**极技·受流反击**(受到攻击后立刻反击——伤害极高)。他的**虎落**是特殊技能——你攻击他时概率触发——一拳约2000伤害 / Core: Kiryu is Yakuza series legend — debuts as boss. Uses **Dragon of Dojima** style — **Extreme: Dragon God Fist** (party phys + 50% stagger) and **Extreme: Parry Counter** (immediate counter after hit — very high damage). His **Tiger Drop** is a special — chance to trigger when you attack — ~2000 damage per hit.

**打法**: **不要连续攻击**——桐生的受流反击和虎落在你连续攻击时触发率极高——打一回合、停一回合(防御或道具)。使用纱荣子的降攻Debuff——把他的攻击力降到最低。趁他使用龙神の拳后的硬直期(约2回合)输出——用韩俊基的杀手极技打爆发。**推荐**: 携带**防御提升**道具——这场战斗的生存比输出更重要 / **Strategy**: **Don't attack consecutively** — Kiryu's Parry Counter and Tiger Drop trigger often on consecutive attacks — attack one turn, guard/item the next. Use Saeko's ATK debuff — minimize his damage output. DPS window after Dragon God Fist (~2 turn recovery) — use Joon-gi's Hitman Extreme for burst. **Tip**: Carry **DEF buff** items — survival > DPS in this fight.

### 终章Boss：大道寺 / Final Boss: Daidoji

**位置**: 终极千禧塔顶层 / Location: Ultimate Millennium Tower Top Floor
**推荐等级**: 50-55 / Recommended Level: 50-55

**阶段一——人形态**: 大道寺使用**铁壁防御**(减伤50% 3回合)+**急速连突**(单体5连物理)+**终极一拳**(单体高伤害+破防)。**核心策略**: 不要在他铁壁防御时输出——用驱逐技能或待机拖过3回合——铁壁结束后再爆发。急速连突必须防御——不防的话约4000伤害。**推荐**: 给坦加防御Buff——让足立抗急速连突 / Phase 1 — Human Form: **Iron Defense** (50% DR 3 turns) + **Rapid Thrust** (5-hit single phys) + **Ultra Fist** (single high dmg + stagger). **Strategy**: Don't attack during Iron Defense — wait 3 turns — burst after. Guard during Rapid Thrust or ~4000 damage. Give tank DEF buff — let Adachi tank Rapid Thrust.

**阶段二——强化形态**: 血量低于40%——进入**真·最终决战形态**——全技能强化。**全屏能量波**——蓄力2回合——蓄力期间必须使用**极技·全队防御**否则接近秒杀(约6000伤害)。**召唤保镖**——召唤2个精英保镖——保镖会帮你回血(打掉后Boss进入虚弱状态)。**核心循环**: 积蓄极技条→在保镖阶段爆发清理保镖→虚弱期全力输出→循环。**道具**: 携带大量全体回复道具——至少10个。这个Boss主要考验资源管理和爆发时机 / Phase 2 — Enhanced: Below 40% HP — **True Final Form** — all skills enhanced. **Full-Screen Energy Wave** — 2-turn charge — must use **Extreme: Party Guard** or near-1HKO (~6000 dmg). **Summon Bodyguards** — 2 elite bodyguards — killing them makes boss vulnerable. **Core loop**: Build Heat → burst down bodyguards → full DPS during vulnerability → repeat. **Items**: 10+ party heals. This boss is a resource management and burst timing test.

## 竞技场Boss / Arena Boss

**横滨竞技场冠军篇**(通关后解锁): 连续挑战10个Boss——第10个是**终极番长**——全属性极高——拥有4种不同形态(每25%HP转换)。**推荐阵容**: 春日(勇者/物理坦)、纱荣子(女招待/治疗)、韩俊基(杀手/物理DPS)、足立(刑警/防御)。**打法**: 每种形态对应不同的弱点——转换形态时及时调整装备和技能。终极番长的"番长之怒"是必杀级大招——用全队防御极技顶住——消耗1槽极技槽——保证够用。**竞技场奖励**: 每次通关获得大量经验和金钱——最高难度奖励终极武器材料——推荐通关后刷满装备再来挑战 / **Yokohama Arena Champion** (post-game): 10 consecutive bosses — #10 is **Ultimate Boss** — max stats — 4 forms (every 25% HP). **Party**: Ichiban (Hero), Saeko (Hostess), Joon-gi (Hitman), Adachi (Detective). **Strategy**: Each form different weakness — adjust during transitions. Ultimate Boss's "Boss Rage" is lethal — use Party Guard Extreme — costs 1 Heat bar. **Rewards**: Massive XP and money — highest difficulty drops ultimate weapon materials — gear up before challenge.

## 隐藏Boss / Hidden Boss

**传说的如龙 / Legendary Dragon**: 完成所有羁绊剧场和全支线后——在终极千禧塔最深处挑战。桐生一马的幻影——全游戏最高的攻击力和速度。**核心机制**: 桐生使用**极技·龙神の拳**(全体物理+即死率30%)和**极技·受流反击**(反击所有攻击)。**打法**: 使用纱荣子的全属性Buff和难波的降攻Debuff——让桐生的伤害降低到可接受范围。不要连续攻击——他受流反击后的反击伤害极高——打两回合停一回合。**推荐等级**: 60+ / **Legendary Dragon**: Complete all Bond Dramas and side quests — challenge at deepest Ultimate Millennium Tower. Kiryu's phantom — highest ATK and SPD in game. **Core**: Uses **Extreme: Dragon God Fist** (party phys + 30% instant death) + **Extreme: Parry Counter** (counters all attacks). **Strategy**: Saeko's all-stat buff + Nanba's ATK debuff — reduce Kiryu's damage to manageable. Don't attack consecutively — his parry counter deals massive damage — attack 2 turns then skip 1. **Level**: 60+
