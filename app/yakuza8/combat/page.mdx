## 核心战斗机制 / Core Combat Mechanics

如龙8采用**新动态指令RPG**战斗——保留回合制指令但加入了实时走位和QTE要素。战场是3D空间——角色的位置影响攻击范围和伤害。和传统回合制不同——你可以自由移动角色——这和敌人/队友的相对位置直接影响战局 / Yakuza 8 uses a **New Dynamic Command RPG** system — turn-based commands with real-time positioning and QTE elements. Battlefield is 3D space — character positions affect attack range and damage. Unlike traditional turn-based — you can free-move characters — relative positioning with enemies and allies directly affects battle.

### 移动与位置 / Movement & Positioning

战斗中角色可以**自由移动**——移动不消耗行动次数——但走的太远可能会浪费时间。**攻击方向**: 从背后攻击敌人伤害+20%+暴击率+15%——所以尽量绕后。**连携站位**: 两个角色站在相邻位置时——一方使用极技后另一方获得+50%极技槽——保持站位紧凑有巨大收益。**环境互动**: 靠近墙壁/路障/道具时可以使用场景攻击——造成大量额外伤害(约等于2格极技的伤害但0消耗)。**站位禁忌**: 不要站在Boss的AOE红圈内——吃了很痛——特别是终章Boss的蓄力AOE几乎秒杀 / Characters can **free-move** in combat — movement doesn't consume turn — but traveling too far wastes time. **Attack Direction**: Back attacks deal +20% damage +15% crit rate — always reposition behind. **Synergy Positioning**: Adjacent characters — when one uses Extreme Skill, other gains +50% Heat gauge — stay close. **Environment Interaction**: Near walls/barricades/items — use environment attacks for massive bonus damage (~2-bar Extreme for 0 cost). **Positioning taboo**: Don't stand in boss AoE red zones — especially final boss charged AoE — near lethal.

### 极技系统 / Extreme Skill (Heat Action) System

每个职业有3-6个极技——需要消耗**极技槽(Heat Gauge)**。极技槽通过下列方式积累: 普攻命中(+5%)、受击(+10%)、完美QTE(+15%)、队友支援(+5%)。极技是主要的爆发输出手段——每个极技有独特的QTE——成功QTE增加20%伤害+额外效果。**极技等级**: 基础极技(1槽消耗——约3000伤害)、强化极技(2槽消耗——约8000伤害+特效)、觉醒极技(3槽消耗——全屏+特殊效果——约20000伤害)。**极技使用策略**: 平时保留1槽应急——Boss破防时用满3槽觉醒极技爆发 / Each job has 3-6 Extreme Skills — consume **Heat Gauge**. Gain: Hit enemy (+5%), Hit taken (+10%), Perfect QTE (+15%), Ally assist (+5%). Extreme Skills are main burst — each has unique QTE — successful QTE adds +20% damage + bonus effect. **Extreme Levels**: Basic (1 bar, ~3k dmg), Enhanced (2 bars, ~8k dmg + effect), Awakening (3 bars, full-screen + special, ~20k dmg). **Strategy**: Keep 1 bar emergency — burst 3-bar awakening when boss is staggered.

## 职业系统 / Job System

如龙8有**25+职业**——每个角色可以自由转职。职业决定角色的技能、属性和可用装备。转职不消耗任何资源——可以随时切换(战斗中不能切换——需要提前在菜单设置)。**职业等级**: 每个职业有1-30级——升级解锁新技能和被动——推荐每个角色至少练2-3个核心职业到20级以上 / Yakuza 8 has **25+ jobs** — each character can freely change jobs. Job determines skills, stats, and equipment. Job change is free — switch anytime (not in combat — set in menu beforehand). **Job level**: 1-30 per job — leveling unlocks skills and passives — recommend 2-3 core jobs to level 20+ per character.

### 职业推荐配置 / Recommended Job Setup

**前期(1-20级)**: 春日→勇者(练到14级出全队无敌)、纱荣子→女招待(堆治疗)、足立→刑警(坦克)、难波→流浪汉(法系AOE)。**中期(20-40级)**: 韩俊基加入后给他杀手或忍者——他的输出潜力极高。春日在勇者14出无敌后可以转职玩玩其他职业——比如厨师(火系输出)或英雄(娱乐)。**终局(40+)**: 每个角色练2-3个职业到20+——为了继承辅助被动——终局Build需要堆4个强力被动配合主职业 / **Early (1-20)**: Ichiban Hero (to 14 for invincible), Saeko Hostess (heal stack), Adachi Detective (tank), Nanba Homeless (AoE mage). **Mid (20-40)**: Joon-gi → Hitman or Ninja — his DPS potential is highest. After Hero 14, Ichiban can experiment. **Endgame (40+)**: 2-3 jobs to 20+ per character for inherited passives — endgame builds need 4 stacked passives.

## 合体技 / Tag Team Attacks

当两个角色之间的**羁绊等级**达到5、10、15时解锁新的合体技。合体技消耗两个角色的极技槽各1格——造成巨额伤害+特殊效果。**羁绊等级10解锁的合体技通常最强**。**推荐合体技**: 春日+足立(勇者+刑警)——"正义的铁拳"——单体物理x3+破防。春日+纱荣子(勇者+女招待)——"光与爱"——全队回复50%HP+全属性Buff——Boss战神技。韩俊基+难波(杀手+流浪汉)——"冻结灭亡"——全体冰系+物理混合伤害+冻结——AOE最强。**使用时机**: Boss破防后使用合体技——2倍伤害加成下收益最大化 / When two characters' **Bond level** reaches 5, 10, 15 — unlock new Tag Team attacks. Costs 1 Heat bar from each — massive damage + special effects. **Bond 10 tag teams are usually strongest**. **Recommended**: Ichiban+Adachi — "Justice Fist" — 3x single phys + stagger. Ichiban+Saeko — "Light and Love" — party 50% heal + all-stat buff — boss fight MVP. Joon-gi+Namba — "Freeze Oblivion" — party ice+phys hybrid + freeze — best AoE. **Timing**: Use during boss break/stagger for 2x damage benefit.

## 羁绊系统 / Bond System

角色间的羁绊通过**战斗连携**(同队战斗+每次战斗少量)+**羁绊剧场**(特定地点触发的专属剧情+大量)+**一起活动**(吃/唱/玩+中量)提升。羁绊等级1-15——每级解锁被动加成。**满羁绊(15级)奖励**: 全属性+10、极技槽上限+1、专属合体技、队友自动支援(挡刀/增伤)。**推荐优先提升**: 春日+纱荣子(治疗+无敌=不死组合)、春日+足立(坦克+输出=稳定核心)。**快速提升方法**: 去夏威夷Waikiki海滩的餐厅吃"羁绊套餐"——一次战斗后羁绊经验x2——配合羁绊剧场效率极高 / Bonds improved via: Battle synergy (+small per fight) + Bond Drama (+large, location-specific story) + Activities (+medium, eat/sing/play). Bond levels 1-15 — each level unlocks bonuses. **Max Bond (15) Rewards**: +10 all stats, +1 Heat bar cap, exclusive tag team, auto assist (block/damage up). **Priority bonds**: Ichiban+Saeko (heal+invincible=immortal), Ichiban+Adachi (tank+DPS=stable core). **Fast leveling**: Eat "Bond Set" at Hawaii Waikiki Beach restaurant — 2x bond XP per battle — pair with Bond Drama for max efficiency.

## 进阶战斗技巧 / Advanced Combat Tips

1. **走位打背**——从背后攻击提供20%伤害加成+15%暴击率——养成习惯每次绕后——对Boss战影响巨大 / Back attacks give +20% damage +15% crit — always reposition behind — massive boss fight impact
2. **环境攻击优先用**——场地上的自行车/路标/油桶都可用——伤害约等于2槽极技但0消耗——第一回合先找环境物品 / Use environment attacks — bicycles, signs, barrels = ~2-bar Extreme for 0 cost — first turn scout for items
3. **极技不要贪**——留1格极技槽保底——遇到突发AOE时可以及时用防御极技减伤——救命的习惯 / Save 1 Heat bar minimum — emergency defense Extreme for unexpected AoE — life-saving habit
4. **职业被动继承**——每个职业习得的被动可以跨职业装备(上限4个)——终局需要规划——先把核心职业练到20+拿关键被动 / Job passives can be equipped cross-job (max 4) — endgame planning — level core jobs to 20+ for key passives first
5. **羁绊支援**——满羁绊的队友会自动支援(挡刀/增伤)——优先堆满主力队友的羁绊——前期先搞到羁绊10出第一个合体技 / Max bond allies auto-assist (block/damage up) — prioritize main party bonds — reach bond 10 first for tag team
6. **职业克制**——遇到特定弱点敌人切对应职业——职业克制效果伤害x1.5——比如弱火的Boss用厨师 / Switch jobs based on enemy weakness — job advantage x1.5 damage — e.g., fire-weak boss use Chef
7. **道具不要省**——如龙8中的道具(尤其是全体回复)非常便宜——Boss战前买满——身上常备10个全体中回复道具 / Don't save items — party heals are very cheap — stock up before boss fights — carry 10 party mid-heals at all times
8. **完美QTE练习**——每个极技和合体技的QTE时间点不同——练习完美QTE=伤害+20%——专门花时间在训练场练习主力技能的QTE / Perfect QTE practice — each Extreme and Tag Team has different timing — perfect QTE = +20% damage — practice main skills in training area
9. **Boss蓄力打断**——当Boss蓄力准备大招时——使用高冲击力极技可以打断——避免全队受伤 / Boss charge interrupt — when boss charges big attack — use high-stagger Extreme to interrupt — avoid party damage
10. **弱点属性优先**——每个Boss有3-5个弱点——优先使用弱点属性的职业和技能——破防效率翻倍 / Element weakness priority — each boss has 3-5 weaknesses — prioritize jobs and skills matching weaknesses — double break efficiency
