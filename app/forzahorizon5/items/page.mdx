## 收集系统总览 / Collection System Overview

地平线5的收集系统内容丰富，分为车辆收集、外观物品、房产和特殊发现四大类。收集不仅带来成就感，还能解锁独特的游戏内容和奖励。

The FH5 collection system is rich in content, divided into four main categories: vehicle collection, cosmetic items, properties, and special discoveries. Collecting not only brings a sense of achievement but also unlocks unique game content and rewards.

## 车辆收集 / Car Collection

### 获取途径 / Acquisition Methods

地平线5中有超过 500 辆车，获取方式多种多样。以下是所有可能的获取途径：

There are over 500 cars in FH5, with various acquisition methods. Here are all possible ways to obtain them:

**1. 车展购买 / Autoshow Purchase**
直接在车展中使用 Cr 购买车辆。价格从几千到数百万 Cr 不等。所有在车展中列出的车辆都可以随时购买。

Purchase cars directly from the Autoshow using Cr. Prices range from a few thousand to millions of Cr. All cars listed in the Autoshow are available for purchase at any time.

**2. 转盘 Wheelspins**
转盘是获取稀有车辆和高额 Cr 的最佳途径。每升一级获得一次普通转盘，每升五级获得一次超级转盘。超级转盘包含更多高级奖励。

Wheelspins are the best way to obtain rare cars and large credit amounts. You earn a regular wheelspin every level and a super wheelspin every five levels. Super Wheelspins contain more premium rewards.

**转盘奖励池 / Wheelspin Reward Pool**:
- Cr 奖励: 1,000 到 250,000
- 超级转盘超级奖励: 最高 500,000 Cr
- 普通车: D 级到 A 级车辆
- 稀有车: S1 级及以上车辆
- 服装、喇叭、表情动作等外观物品 / Clothing, horns, emotes, and other cosmetic items

**3. 成就奖励 / Accolade Rewards**
完成特定成就获得车辆奖励。这是获取某些独占车辆的唯一途径。

Complete specific accolades to earn car rewards. This is the only way to obtain某些 exclusive vehicles.

**4. 季节赛奖励 / Seasonal Rewards**
每周四更新的嘉年华播放列表提供限时车辆奖励。错过季节赛的车辆可能会在数周或数月后出现在拍卖行。

The Festival Playlist, updated every Thursday, offers time-limited vehicle rewards. Cars missed from seasonal events may appear in the auction house weeks or months later.

**5. 拍卖行 / Auction House**
玩家之间买卖车辆的场所。可以在这里购买其他玩家出售的车辆，也可以出售自己的多余车辆。热门季节限定车通常价格较高。

A player-to-player vehicle marketplace. You can buy cars sold by other players here, or sell your duplicate cars. Popular seasonal limited cars typically command high prices.

**6. 谷仓发现 / Barn Finds**
在地图上发现隐藏的谷仓位置，从中获得经典废弃车辆。每次发现后需要等待一段时间（约 15-30 分钟游戏时间），车辆才会修复完成。

Discover hidden barn locations on the map to obtain classic abandoned vehicles. After each discovery, you must wait some time (approximately 15-30 minutes of gameplay) for the vehicle to be restored.

## 外观物品 / Cosmetic Items

游戏中的服装系统让玩家可以为自己的角色定制外观。服装分为上衣、裤子、鞋子和配饰四类。获取方式包括转盘奖励、成就奖励和季节赛奖励。

The clothing system allows players to customize their character appearance. Clothing is divided into tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories. Acquisition methods include wheelspin rewards, accolade rewards, and seasonal event rewards.

### 喇叭 / Horns

喇叭是车辆在游戏中鸣笛时的音效。获取后可以在车辆设置中更换。部分喇叭有特殊音效。

Horns are the sound effects when honking the horn in-game. Once acquired, they can be changed in vehicle settings. Some horns have special sound effects.
