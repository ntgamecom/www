## 欢迎来到墨西哥 / Welcome to Mexico

《极限竞速地平线5》（Forza Horizon 5）是由 Playground Games 开发、Xbox Game Studios 发行的开放世界赛车游戏，于2021年11月9日登陆 Xbox Series X|S、Xbox One 和 PC 平台。本作以墨西哥为舞台，拥有系列史上最大、最多样化的地图，涵盖丛林、沙漠、峡谷、农场、海岸和城市六种截然不同的生物群落。游戏继承了 "Forza Horizon" 系列一贯的自由探索与节日庆典氛围，同时引入了全新的天气系统、动态季节变化以及丰富的车辆自定义功能。

Forza Horizon 5 is an open-world racing game developed by Playground Games and published by Xbox Game Studios, released on November 9, 2021 for Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, and PC. Set in Mexico, it features the largest and most diverse map in series history, spanning six distinct biomes: jungle, desert, canyon, farmland, coastal, and city. The game carries forward the series tradition of free-roaming exploration and festival celebration, while introducing a new weather system, dynamic seasonal changes, and extensive vehicle customization.

## 游戏特色 / Game Features

### 开放世界赛车 / Open-World Racing
地平线5 的地图面积是地平线4 的约1.5倍，拥有超过 500 辆可收集的赛车。玩家可以在墨西哥广袤的土地上自由驰骋，从白雪皑皑的火山口到热带雨林，从古老的玛雅遗迹到繁华的城市街道，每一种地形都带来截然不同的驾驶体验。动态季节系统让同一片区域在不同时间呈现完全不同的面貌——旱季的沙漠干燥炽热，雨季则会形成临时的河流与泥潭。

The FH5 map is approximately 1.5 times larger than FH4, featuring over 500 collectible cars. Players can roam freely across Mexico's vast landscapes, from snow-capped volcano craters to tropical rainforests, ancient Mayan ruins to bustling city streets, each terrain offering a distinctly different driving experience. The dynamic season system transforms the same areas throughout the year — dry seasons make deserts hot and arid, while rainy seasons create temporary rivers and mud pits.

### 嘉年华精神 / Festival Spirit
地平线系列的灵魂在于嘉年华。每一代游戏都以一个盛大的汽车嘉年华为核心，玩家从一名普通参与者逐步成长为嘉年华的传奇人物。地平线5 中，玩家首次抵达墨西哥时就会体验到壮观的热带风暴开场——这是系列最令人难忘的序章之一。随着游戏进程推进，玩家可以在墨西哥各地建立多个嘉年华前哨站，每个站点都解锁新的赛事类型和活动。

The soul of the Horizon series lies in its festival spirit. Each game centers around a grand automotive festival, with players growing from ordinary attendees to legendary figures. In FH5, players experience a spectacular tropical storm开场 — one of the most memorable prologues in the series. As the game progresses, players establish multiple festival outposts across Mexico, each unlocking new event types and activities.

### 车辆与文化 / Car Culture
从经典的 1953 年雪佛兰科尔维特到最新的电动 hypercar，地平线5 收录了横跨百年的汽车工业精华。游戏不仅关注车辆本身，更深入展现了围绕汽车形成的文化现象——Lowrider 低趴文化、越野改装文化、JDM 日系改装风格、漂移文化等。玩家可以通过车辆涂装编辑器（Vinyl Editor）自由创作涂装，也可以下载其他玩家的作品。社区创意工坊让每辆车都成为独特的艺术品。

From the classic 1953 Chevrolet Corvette to the latest electric hypercars, FH5 spans a century of automotive excellence. The game not only focuses on the vehicles themselves but also delves into car culture phenomena — Lowrider culture, off-road modification, JDM Japanese tuning style, drift culture, and more. Players can freely create liveries using the Vinyl Editor or download creations from other players. The community创意工坊 turns every car into a unique work of art.
