## 开始之前 / Before You Start

### 选择控制方式 / Choosing Your Controller

对于《极限竞速地平线5》来说，控制方式的选择直接影响你的游戏体验。游戏支持三种主要的输入方式：手柄、方向盘和键鼠。对于绝大多数玩家来说，Xbox 手柄或 PlayStation 手柄是最佳选择，提供了优秀的力回馈扳机震动和符合人体工学的按键布局。方向盘则是追求极致沉浸感的模拟玩家的首选。

For Forza Horizon 5, the choice of control method directly affects your gaming experience. The game supports three main input methods: controller, racing wheel, and keyboard/mouse. For most players, an Xbox or PlayStation controller is the best choice, offering excellent trigger rumble force feedback and ergonomic button layout. Racing wheels are preferred by sim enthusiasts seeking ultimate immersion.

**手柄推荐设置 / Recommended Controller Settings:**
- 振动 Vibration: 开启 On（增强路感反馈 / enhances road feel feedback）
- 扳机震动 Trigger Vibration: 开启 On（模拟ABS/TCS工作状态 / simulates ABS/TCS working status）
- 内死区 Inner Deadzone: 0 到 10（根据手柄磨损调整 / adjust based on controller wear）
- 外死区 Outer Deadzone: 100（保持默认 / keep at default）
- 转向灵敏度 Steering Sensitivity: 15 到 25（数值越高转向越灵敏 / higher values mean more responsive steering）
- 加速/刹车曲线 Acceleration/Brake Curve: 标准 Standard（新手建议保持默认 / beginners should keep default）

**方向盘推荐 / Recommended Wheels:**
- Logitech G923 / G920 — 入门级力反馈 / entry-level force feedback
- Thrustmaster TX / T300 — 中端性价比 / mid-range value
- Fanatec DD Pro — 高端直驱 / high-end direct drive
- Moza R9 / R5 — 国产直驱新秀 / Chinese direct drive newcomer

### 画面设置 / Graphics Settings

地平线5 的 PC 版优化出色，但仍有几个关键设置需要调整以获得最佳体验。建议优先保证帧率稳定在 60fps 以上，其次是画质。

FH5 PC版 is well-optimized, but a few key settings need adjustment for the best experience. Prioritize a stable 60fps or higher, followed by visual quality.

- **动态优化 Dynamic Optimization**: 开启 On（自动调整渲染分辨率 / auto-adjusts render resolution）
- **纹理 Texture Quality**: 极端 Extreme（对性能影响小 / low performance impact）
- **阴影 Shadow Quality**: 高 High 到 极端 Extreme（显存充裕时开高 / set high if VRAM permits）
- **全局光照 Global Illumination**: 中等 Medium（性能开销大 / high performance cost）
- **反射 Reflections**: 中等 Medium（环境反射质量 / environment reflection quality）
- **光线追踪 Ray Tracing**: 关闭 Off（仅ForzaVista场景开启 / only active in ForzaVista scenes）
- **MSAA**: FXAA（性价比最高 / best value）
- **视野 FOV**: 80 到 100（根据个人偏好 / based on personal preference）

## 首次驾驶 / First Drive

### 开场风暴 / The Opening Storm

游戏开场是地平线系列史上最壮观的场景之一。你将驾驶一辆 2021 款 Ford Bronco Badlands 在热带风暴中冲下飞机。这段开场本质上是教学关——你只需要跟着屏幕提示操作即可。需要注意的是：

The opening sequence is one of the most spectacular in Horizon series history. You will drive a 2021 Ford Bronco Badlands down a cargo plane during a tropical storm. This is essentially a tutorial — just follow the on-screen prompts. Key points:

- 保持速度在 80km/h 以上，否则会跟不上引导车 / Keep speed above 80km/h or you will lose the lead car
- 利用 A 键（Xbox）或 X 键（PS）开启辅助线 / Use A (Xbox) or X (PS) to enable driving lines
- 风暴会显著降低能见度，但驾驶路线引导线会清晰指示方向 / The storm reduces visibility, but the driving line clearly shows the way
- 抵达第一个嘉年华后，开场结束，自由世界正式开放 / After reaching the first festival, the prologue ends and free roam begins

## 驾驶辅助系统 / Driver Assists

### 辅助系统详解 / Assist System Breakdown

地平线5 提供了丰富的驾驶辅助选项，对新手极度友好。合理设置辅助可以在不损失太多速度的情况下大幅提高稳定性。

FH5 provides extensive driver assist options that are extremely beginner-friendly. Properly configured assists can significantly improve stability without sacrificing too much speed.

**刹车辅助 / Braking Assist:**
- **关闭 Off**: 完全由玩家控制刹车 / Full player control
- **仅限直道 Straight Only**: 仅在直道自动刹车 / Auto-brakes only on straights
- **完全辅助 Full**: 自动刹车入弯 / Auto-brakes into corners
- *推荐: 新手用"仅限直道"，熟练后关闭 / Recommended: "Straight Only" for beginners, turn off when experienced*

**转向辅助 / Steering Assist:**
- **关闭 Off**: 完全手动控制转向 / Full manual control
- **标准 Standard**: 轻微的转向辅助 / Mild steering help
- **完全辅助 Full**: 自动跟随路线 / Auto-follows driving line
- *推荐: 关闭或标准 / Recommended: Off or Standard*

**牵引力控制 / Traction Control (TCS):**
- 开启时防止起步和加速时轮胎打滑 / Prevents wheel spin during acceleration
- 关闭时加速更快但需要更精细的油门控制 / Faster acceleration but requires finer throttle control
- *推荐: 新手开启，熟练后关闭 / Recommended: On for beginners, off when skilled*

**稳定控制 / Stability Control (STM):**
- 防止车辆侧滑失控 / Prevents vehicle sliding and loss of control
- 会显著降低过弯速度 / Significantly reduces cornering speed
- *推荐: 新手开启，尽快尝试关闭 / Recommended: On for beginners, try turning off ASAP*

**ABS 防抱死制动系统 / Anti-lock Braking System:**
- 防止刹车时轮胎锁死 / Prevents wheel lock-up during braking
- 关闭 ABS 需要学会阈值刹车法 / Turning off ABS requires learning threshold braking
- *推荐: 开启 / Recommended: On*

**换挡方式 / Shifting:**
- 自动 Automatic: 游戏自动换挡 / Game shifts automatically
- 手动 Manual: 玩家控制离合和换挡 / Player controls clutch and shifting
- 手动带离合 Manual with Clutch: 最精确的控制 / Most precise control
- *推荐: 新手用自动，逐步过渡到手动 / Recommended: Automatic for beginners, gradually move to manual*

**推荐辅助组合 / Recommended Assist Presets:**

| 玩家类型 / Player Type | 刹车 Braking | 转向 Steering | TCS | STM | ABS | 换挡 Shifting |
|:---------------------|:------------|:-------------|:---|:---|:---|:-------------|
| 绝对新手 Absolute Beginner | 完全辅助 Full | 标准 Standard | 开启 On | 开启 On | 开启 On | 自动 Auto |
| 进阶初学者 Advanced Beginner | 仅限直道 Straight Only | 关闭 Off | 开启 On | 关闭 Off | 开启 On | 自动 Auto |
| 中级 Intermediate | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 开启 On | 手动 Manual |
| 高级 Advanced | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 关闭 Off | 手动带离合 Manual+Clutch |

## 赚钱方法 / Credit Earning

### 初期赚钱策略 / Early Credit Strategies

在地平线5 中，Cr（Credits）是购买车辆和改装零件的货币。初期资金紧张是每个新手都会遇到的问题。以下是最高效的赚钱方式。

In FH5, Credits (Cr) are the currency for buying cars and upgrade parts. Tight funds in the early game are a common issue for every newcomer. Here are the most efficient ways to earn credits.

### 1. 完成赛事和成就 / Complete Events and Accolades
每次完成一个赛事都会获得 Cr 和经验值（Influence）。成就系统提供一次性大量奖励。建议优先完成"地平线故事"任务线，它们提供丰厚的首次通关奖励。

Every event completion grants Cr and Influence. The Accolade system offers substantial one-time rewards. Prioritize completing Horizon Story missions, which provide generous first-time completion rewards.

### 2. 转盘抽奖 / Wheelspins
转盘是获取稀有车辆和高额 Cr 的最佳途径。每升一级获得一次普通转盘，每升五级获得一次超级转盘。初期获得的转盘建议不要立即使用，先存到一定数量后集中开启。

Wheelspins are the best way to obtain rare cars and large credit amounts. You earn a regular wheelspin every level and a super wheelspin every five levels. It is recommended to save them and open in batches.

### 3. 季节赛奖励 / Seasonal Event Rewards
每周四晚更新的嘉年华播放列表提供大量奖励。完成季节锦标赛通常奖励稀有车辆（可能是唯一获取途径）。即使技术水平有限，调整难度到"平均"水平也可以完成大部分季节任务。

The Festival Playlist, updated every Thursday evening, offers substantial rewards. Completing seasonal championships typically rewards rare cars (sometimes the only way to obtain them). Even with limited skill, setting difficulty to "Average" allows completion of most seasonal tasks.

### 4. 拍卖行 / Auction House
低买高卖是快速赚钱的高级技巧。关注拍卖行中的低价起拍车辆，尤其是季节奖励车。热门车型包括：2021 Mercedes-AMG One、2020 Koenigsegg Jesko、2019 Ferrari 488 Pista。可以在赛季结束前几周入手，之后价格上涨时卖出。

Buying low and selling high is an advanced money-making technique. Watch for low-star auction vehicles, especially seasonal reward cars. Popular models include: 2021 Mercedes-AMG One, 2020 Koenigsegg Jesko, 2019 Ferrari 488 Pista. Buy a few weeks before season ends, then sell when prices rise.

### 5. 技能点数变现 / Cashing Skill Points
通过积累技能点数（Skill Points），在车辆 mastery 树中解锁奖励后，可以把获得的 Cr 奖励或稀有车辆转卖。这是后期最稳定的被动收入来源之一。

By accumulating Skill Points and unlocking rewards in car mastery trees, you can either claim Cr rewards or sell rare cars. This is one of the most stable passive income sources in the late game.

### 升级策略 / Upgrade Strategy

初期资金有限，建议优先升级以下部件，它们对性能提升最为显著：

With limited early-game funds, prioritize upgrading these parts for the most noticeable performance gains:

1. **轮胎 Tires**: 升级到赛车胎（Race Slicks）弯道抓地力提升巨大 / Upgrade to Race Slicks for massive cornering grip improvement
2. **传动系统 Drivetrain**: AWD 转换提高加速和操控，尤其适合越野 / AWD conversion improves acceleration and handling, especially for off-road
3. **发动机 Engine**: 涡轮或机械增压器提供最大的马力提升 / Turbo or supercharger provides the biggest horsepower gain
4. **悬挂 Suspension**: 赛车悬挂降低车身，改善弯道姿态 / Race suspension lowers the car and improves cornering posture
5. **刹车 Brakes**: 赛车刹车缩短制动距离 / Race brakes shorten braking distance
6. **重量减轻 Weight Reduction**: 减重间接提升加速、刹车和操控 / Weight reduction improves acceleration, braking, and handling indirectly

**注意 / Note**: 升级车辆前先确认目标等级。如果你要参加 A 800 赛事，确保升级后 PI 值接近 800 但不超过 800。过度升级会把你推到更高等级，让比赛更加困难。

Before upgrading, confirm your target class. If entering an A 800 event, ensure the PI value is close to 800 but does not exceed 800. Over-upgrading pushes you into a higher class, making races harder.
