## 墨西哥地图总览 / Mexico Map Overview

地平线5 的地图设定在墨西哥，是系列史上最大、最多样化的一张地图。从北部的沙漠到南部的丛林，从西海岸到东部山区，游戏忠实再现了墨西哥的地理多样性。地图共划分为六个生物群落，每个群落都有独特的驾驶体验和视觉风格。

FH5 is set in Mexico, featuring the largest and most diverse map in series history. From northern deserts to southern jungles, from the west coast to eastern mountains, the game faithfully recreates Mexico geographic diversity. The map is divided into six biomes, each offering a unique driving experience and visual style.

## 六大生物群落 / Six Biomes

### 1. 丛林 / Jungle (南部 / South)
**代表地区 / Key Area**: 塞尔瓦原野 Selva Region
**驾驶体验 / Driving Experience**: 狭窄蜿蜒的泥路，高湿度，频繁降雨。能见度受限，路面湿滑。适合越野车和拉力赛车。最大特点是热带雨林的密集植被和泥泞路面。

Narrow winding mud roads, high humidity, frequent rainfall. Limited visibility, slippery surfaces. Suitable for offroad and rally cars. The defining特征 is dense tropical rainforest vegetation and muddy surfaces.

- 建议车辆: AWD 越野车 / AWD offroad vehicles
- 适合赛事: 越野赛 Dirt, 越野拉力赛 Cross Country
- 地标 Landmark: 玛雅金字塔 Maya Pyramids, 瀑布 Waterfalls, 热带野生动物 Tropical wildlife

### 2. 沙漠 / Desert (西北 / Northwest)
**代表地区 / Key Area**: 沙漠荒原 Dunas Blancas
**驾驶体验 / Driving Experience**: 开阔的沙地和沙丘，高速驾驶的理想场所。能见度极高，但沙地抓地力低。沙丘区域需要保持 momentum 来翻越沙脊。日夜温差极大。

Open sand fields and dunes, ideal for high-speed driving. Excellent visibility but low sand surface grip. Dune areas require maintaining momentum to crest sand ridges. Extreme temperature differences between day and night.

- 建议车辆: Baja Truck、越野改装车 / Baja trucks, offroad builds
- 适合赛事: 越野拉力赛 Cross Country, PR 特技 PR Stunts
- 地标 Landmark: 仙人掌森林 Cactus forests, 盐滩 Salt flats, 荒漠驿站 Desert outposts
- 特殊现象: 沙尘暴 Sandstorms（季节限定 / season-limited）

### 3. 峡谷 / Canyon (中部 / Central)
**代表地区 / Key Area**: 铜谷 Copper Canyon
**驾驶体验 / Driving Experience**: 壮观的峡谷地貌，高海拔区域有险峻的悬崖公路。道路多为蜿蜒的铺装山路，也有险峻的碎石下坡路。最佳驾驶体验在公路赛中体现。海拔最高可达约 3600 米。

Spectacular canyon terrain, with cliffside roads at high altitudes. Mostly winding paved mountain roads with some treacherous gravel descents. Best driving experience in road racing. Maximum elevation reaches approximately 3600 meters.

- 建议车辆: 操控型跑车 / Handling-focused sports cars
- 适合赛事: 公路赛 Road Racing, 漂移赛 Drifting
- 地标 Landmark: 吊桥 Suspension bridges, 观景台 Lookout points, 山间小镇 Mountain villages
- 特殊现象: 高海拔稀薄空气影响车辆性能 / High-altitude thin air affects vehicle performance

### 4. 农田 / Farmland (东部 / East)
**代表地区 / Key Area**: 大平原 Gran Pantano, 农田区域 Tierra Prospera
**驾驶体验 / Driving Experience**: 开阔的农田和牧场，道路纵横交错。路面以砂石路为主，也有一些铺装路穿插其中。适合中低速度的越野驾驶。农田区域有大量干草捆和围栏作为障碍物。

Open farmland and pastures with crisscrossing roads. Mostly gravel roads with some paved sections穿插. Suitable for medium-to-low speed offroad driving. Farmland areas have many hay bales and fences as obstacles.

- 建议车辆: 拉力赛车 Rally cars, B级越野车 B-class offroad
- 适合赛事: 越野赛 Dirt, 街头赛 Street Racing（少量/ limited）
- 地标 Landmark: 风车 Turbines, 农场 Barns, 灌溉渠道 Irrigation channels

### 5. 沿海 / Coastal (西部 / West)
**代表地区 / Key Area**: 博卡德拉维拉 Baja California
**驾驶体验 / Driving Experience**: 美丽的海岸公路，一侧是碧蓝的海洋，另一侧是悬崖或热带植被。道路多为铺装良好的滨海公路，弯道流畅，适合高速巡游和公路赛。

Beautiful coastal highways with the blue ocean on one side and cliffs or tropical vegetation on the other. Mostly well-paved coastal roads with smooth curves, suitable for high-speed cruising and road racing.

- 建议车辆: 敞篷跑车 Convertibles, 超级跑车 Supercars
- 适合赛事: 公路赛 Road Racing, 巡游 Cruising
- 地标 Landmark: 灯塔 Lighthouses, 海滩 Beaches, 渔村 Fishing villages
- 特殊现象: 日出和日落景色极佳 / Excellent sunrise and sunset views

### 6. 城市 / City (中部 / Central)
**代表地区 / Key Area**: 瓜纳华托 Guanajuato
**驾驶体验 / Driving Experience**: 墨西哥著名彩色山城，街道狭窄曲折，有大量地下隧道系统。路面为铺装路，但街道狭窄、转弯急促、视线受限。城市道路有多层立体结构。

Famous colorful mountain city in Mexico, with narrow winding streets and extensive underground tunnel systems. Paved surfaces, but narrow streets with sharp turns and limited visibility. Multi-level road structures.

- 建议车辆: 紧凑型跑车 Compact sports cars, 操控优先 Handling priority
- 适合赛事: 街头赛 Street Racing, 漂移赛 Drifting
- 地标 Landmark: 瓜纳华托大学 University of Guanajuato, 接吻巷 Callejon del Beso, 圣母教堂 Basilica

## 快速旅行系统 / Fast Travel System

### 快速旅行广告牌 / Fast Travel Boards

地图上散布着 50 个快速旅行广告牌。驾车撞毁每个广告牌可以降低快速旅行的费用。所有 50 个广告牌都被撞毁后，快速旅行变为完全免费。

There are 50 fast travel billboards scattered across the map. Driving through each billboard reduces fast travel costs. After all 50 billboards are destroyed, fast travel becomes completely free.

**广告牌分布 / Billboard Distribution**:
- 丛林 Jungle: 8 个
- 沙漠 Desert: 10 个
- 峡谷 Canyon: 9 个
- 农田 Farmland: 8 个
- 沿海 Coastal: 7 个
- 城市 City: 5 个
- 其他 Other: 3 个

**撞毁技巧 / Destruction Tips**:
- 使用高速车辆（S1 或 S2 级）更容易撞毁广告牌 / Use high-speed vehicles (S1 or S2 class) for easier destruction
- 部分广告牌位于难以到达的位置，需要使用越野车或从高处冲下 / Some billboards are in hard-to-reach locations, requiring offroad vehicles or descending from high ground
- 建议在开放地图探索时顺便撞毁沿途遇到的广告牌 / Destroy billboards encountered during open world exploration
- 购买"快速旅行"玩家房产是另一个降低费用的方式 / Buying the "Fast Travel" player house is another way to reduce costs

### 快速旅行之家 / Fast Travel House

购买 "Buenas Vistas" 房产（位于地图中央，售价 2,000,000 Cr）可以解锁免费的快速旅行，不再需要撞毁全部广告牌。这是游戏中最值得购买的投资之一。

Buying the "Buenas Vistas" property (located in the center of the map, priced at 2,000,000 Cr) unlocks free fast travel, without needing to destroy all billboards. This is one of the most worthwhile investments in the game.

## PR 特技 / PR Stunts

PR 特技是分布在地图各处的挑战性据点，分三种类型：测速区间、测速区域和危险标志。

PR Stunts are challenging locations distributed across the map, divided into three types: Speed Traps and Speed Zones, Danger Signs, and Drift Zones.

### 测速区间 / Speed Traps

在指定点测速通过时的最高速度。使用高速车辆从足够长的助跑距离冲过测速点。

Measure the highest speed when passing through a designated point. Use high-speed vehicles with a sufficiently long run-up distance.

| 海岸公路 Coast Road | 290 km/h | Lamborghini Huracan STO |

### 危险标志 / Danger Signs

从跳台飞跃，测量飞跃距离。需要足够的助跑速度和在空中的车身控制。

Jump from a ramp and measure the jump distance. Requires sufficient approach speed and airborne vehicle control.

| 跳台 Location | 三星目标 3-Star Target | 推荐赛车 Recommended Car |
|:-------------|:---------------------|:----------------------|
| 峡谷大跳 Canyon Jump | 500 米 / 500m | Rimac Nevera, Hoonigan RS200 |

**飞跃技巧 / Jumping Tips**:
- 保持车辆在落地前水平——前倾会损失距离，后仰会导致失控 / Keep the vehicle level before landing — nose-down loses distance, nose-up causes loss of control
- 助跑路线不一定要是直线——寻找最长的铺装路面作为助跑段 / The approach route does not have to be straight — find the longest paved section for run-up
- 使用改装后的车辆，特别是降低重量和提升马力 / Use upgraded vehicles, especially with weight reduction and power increases
- 在跳跃前短暂松开油门可以让悬挂预先压缩，获得更好的弹跳效果 / Briefly releasing throttle before the jump pre-compresses the suspension for better bounce
- 危险标志中 Bugatti Chiron 和 Koenigsegg Jesko 是最常用的高速跳跃车辆 / Bugatti Chiron and Koenigsegg Jesko are the most common high-speed jump vehicles for Danger Signs

在指定路段完成连续漂移并累积分数。分数基于漂移角度、速度和时间连续性。

Complete continuous drifting through a designated section and accumulate points. Scoring is based on drift angle, speed, and time continuity.

## 天气与季节 / Weather and Seasons

### 季节系统 / Season System

地平线5 的季节系统按月轮换。与现实世界不同，游戏中的季节变化影响地图的不同区域。墨西哥的季节不是四季分明，而是雨季和旱季的交替。

The season system in FH5 rotates monthly. Unlike the real world, in-game season changes affect different areas of the map. Mexican seasons are not four distinct seasons but alternating wet and dry seasons.

| 季节 Season | 持续时间 Duration | 主要影响 Main Effects |
|:-----------|:-----------------|:--------------------|
| 旱季开始 Early Dry | 1 周 / 1 week | 干燥，沙尘多 Dry, dusty |
| 旱季 Late Dry | 1 周 / 1 week | 最热，沙漠扩展 Hottest, desert expands |
| 雨季开始 Early Wet | 1 周 / 1 week | 开始降雨 Rain begins |
| 雨季 Late Wet | 1 周 / 1 week | 暴雨，河流泛滥 Heavy rain, rivers overflow |

**季节对驾驶的影响 / Seasonal Driving Impact**:
- 雨季时，越野路面变得更泥泞湿滑 / Offroad surfaces become muddier and more slippery during wet season
- 旱季时，沙漠区域产生沙尘暴降低能见度 / Desert areas generate dust storms during dry season, reducing visibility
- 河流水位在雨季上升，某些越野路线无法通行 / River levels rise during wet season, making some offroad routes impassable
- 火山口区域在特定季节出现积雪 / Crater areas have snow cover during certain seasons
- 季节变化影响 PR 特技的挑战难度——雨季漂移更容易，危险标志因路面湿滑更难 / Season changes affect PR Stunt difficulty — drifting is easier in wet season, Danger Signs are harder due to slippery surfaces
