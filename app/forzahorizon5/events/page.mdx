## 赛事系统总览 / Event System Overview

地平线5 的赛事系统丰富多样，分为基础赛事、地平线故事、PR特技和季节赛事四大类。每种类型都有独特的规则、评分方式和奖励体系。本章将逐一详细解析。

FH5 event system is rich and diverse, divided into four main categories: basic events, Horizon Stories, PR Stunts, and seasonal events. Each type has unique rules, scoring methods, and reward systems. This chapter will detail each one.

## 公路赛 / Road Racing

公路赛是地平线系列最核心的赛事类型，在封闭的柏油路面上进行。比赛通常在风景优美的公路和赛道上展开，涵盖从短途冲刺到多圈耐力赛的不同形式。

Road Racing is the core event type of the Horizon series, held on closed asphalt surfaces. Races typically take place on scenic roads and circuits, ranging from short sprints to multi-lap endurance races.

### 公路赛技巧 / Road Racing Techniques

**路线选择 / Line Selection**: 公路赛最重要的技巧是找到最佳行驶路线。遵循外-内-外的过弯原则——入弯前靠外侧，切过弯心后逐渐回到外侧。使用游戏中的驾驶线路辅助可以帮助新手快速学习最佳路线。

The most important technique is finding the optimal driving line. Follow the outside-inside-outside cornering principle — approach from the outside, cut through the apex, then gradually return to the outside. Using the in-game driving line assist can help beginners quickly learn optimal routes.

**刹车点 / Braking Points**: 在直道末端寻找路侧的"50"、"100"、"150"标识牌作为参考。通常在高速度段的直道末端的"100"标识牌附近开始刹车。刹车时保持方向盘正直，减速完成后逐渐入弯。

Look for "50", "100", "150" marker boards at the end of straights as references. Typically begin braking around the "100" marker at high-speed straights. Keep the steering wheel straight during braking, then gradually turn into the corner after slowing down.

**出弯加速 / Corner Exit Acceleration**: 等到车头指向出弯点时再开始踩油门。过早油门会导致转向不足（推头）或动力甩尾。后驱车需要更精确的油门控制。

Wait until the car nose points toward the exit before applying throttle. Early throttle causes understeer (push) or power oversteer. Rear-wheel drive cars require more precise throttle control.

- S1 900: 2020 Lamborghini Huracan STO
- S2 950: 2018 McLaren Senna

## 街头赛 / Street Racing

街头赛在墨西哥城市中的真实道路上进行，没有路障和封闭措施——这意味着有对面来车！街头赛的赛道有交通流量，玩家需要避让 AI 车辆。

Street Racing takes place on real roads in Mexican cities without barriers or closures — meaning there is oncoming traffic! The tracks have traffic flow, and players need to avoid AI vehicles.

### 街头赛策略 / Street Race Strategies

**交通规避 / Traffic Avoidance**: 街头赛的核心技能是预判交通流量。AI车辆会有固定模式，记忆关键路口的车辆出现时机可以让你在无交通阻碍的情况下通过。建议在自由漫游模式下反复练习特定路线来记忆交通模式。

The core skill is predicting traffic flow. AI vehicles follow fixed patterns; memorizing vehicle spawning timings at key intersections allows clean passes. Practice specific routes repeatedly in free roam to memorize traffic patterns.

**风险收益 / Risk vs Reward**: 近道穿行（比如越过人行道或穿过小巷）可以节省时间，但撞上障碍物会损失更多。新手建议先走标准路线，熟悉后逐步尝试捷径。

Cutting through shortcuts (like sidewalks or alleys) saves time, but hitting obstacles costs more. Beginners should stick to standard routes first, then gradually尝试 shortcuts once familiar.

**推荐街头赛车 / Recommended Street Race Cars**:
- A 800: 2015 Porsche Cayman GTS（操控灵活适合城市街道 / nimble handling for city streets）
- S1 900: 2016 Ford GT（高速路段优势 / advantage on high-speed sections）
- S2 950: 2019 Rimac Concept Two（瞬间加速适合穿梭 / instant acceleration for weaving through traffic）

## 越野赛 / Dirt Racing

越野赛在沙石、泥土和草地路面上进行。车辆需要较强的悬挂系统和四轮驱动来应对松软路面。

Dirt Racing takes place on gravel, dirt, and grass surfaces. Cars need robust suspension systems and four-wheel drive to handle loose surfaces.

### 越野赛技巧 / Dirt Racing Techniques

**走线差异 / Line Differences**: 沙石路面的最佳路线往往不是最短路线。外侧通常有更多的抓地力（因为内侧的浮土被前面的车辆扫开）。不要害怕走宽线，有时外侧更快。

The optimal line on gravel is often not the shortest. The outside typically has more grip (because loose gravel on the inside has been swept away by leading cars). Do not be afraid to take wider lines — sometimes the outside is faster.

**手刹过弯 / Handbrake Turns**: 在越野赛中，手刹是过急弯的利器。入弯前减速，拉手刹让车尾滑动，车头指向弯心后立即加油出弯。这个技巧在沙石路面比柏油路面有效得多。

In dirt racing, the handbrake is a powerful tool for tight corners. Brake before the turn, pull the handbrake to slide the rear, then immediately apply throttle once the nose points toward the apex. This technique is far more effective on gravel than on asphalt.

**推荐越野赛车 / Recommended Dirt Race Cars**:
- B 700: 2005 Subaru Impreza WRX STI
- A 800: 2019 Toyota #1 T100 Baja Truck
- S1 900: 1985 Hoonigan Ford RS200

## 越野拉力赛 / Cross Country

越野拉力赛是地平线5中最狂野的赛事类型。没有固定赛道，玩家需要在开阔地形中寻找通往检查点的最快路线。赛事穿越丛林、河流、沙丘和各种不可预测的地形。

Cross Country is the wildest event type in FH5. There are no fixed tracks; players must find the fastest route through open terrain to reach checkpoints. Events traverse jungles, rivers, sand dunes, and all kinds of unpredictable terrain.

### 越野拉力策略 / Cross Country Strategies

**路线自由 / Route Freedom**: 与公路赛不同，你不需要严格跟随赛道。查看小地图上的下一个检查点，规划直线穿越路线。有时直接从山坡冲下去比绕路走谷底更快。

Unlike road racing, you do not need to strictly follow the track. Check the next checkpoint on the minimap and plan a direct穿越 route. Sometimes charging straight down a hillside is faster than taking a valley detour.

**车辆选择 / Vehicle Selection**: 只有真正的越野车和卡车才能在越野拉力赛中竞争。离地间隙是首要考虑因素——底盘太低的车会被地形卡住。

Only true offroad vehicles and trucks can compete in Cross Country. Ground clearance is the primary consideration — cars with low底盘 will get stuck on terrain.

- A 800: 2021 Ford Bronco Raptor
- A 800: 2019 Toyota #1 T100 Baja Truck

## 直线加速赛 / Drag Racing

直线加速赛是纯粹的加速比拼。在笔直的道路上从静止起步，抵达终点线时速度最高者获胜。换挡时机和起步控制是获胜关键。

Drag Racing is a pure acceleration competition. Starting from a standstill on a straight road, the highest speed at the finish line wins. Shift timing and launch control are the keys to victory.

### 直线加速技巧 / Drag Racing Techniques

**完美起步 / Perfect Launch**: 在倒计时结束前将转速保持在最佳扭矩区间。在指示灯亮起的一瞬间松开刹车并全油门。使用手动换挡可以更精确地控制换挡时机。

Keep the RPM in the optimal torque range before the countdown ends. Release the brake and apply full throttle the moment the lights turn green. Using manual shifting allows more precise shift timing.

**换挡策略 / Shift Strategy**: 不要等到转速红线才换挡。有些车辆在红线前就已经达到峰值功率。观察转速表的功率曲线，找到每个挡位的最大功率区间换挡最佳。

Do not wait until redline to shift. Some cars peak in power before redline. Watch the tachometer power curve to find the optimal shift point in each gear.

**推荐直线加速赛车 / Recommended Drag Cars**:
- A 800: 2018 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat
- S1 900: 2018 Ford Mustang GT
- S2 950: 2021 Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+

## 漂移赛 / Drifting

漂移赛评分基于你在漂移过程中的角度、速度、持续时间和线路选择。评分系统会综合计算这些因素的组合得分。

Drifting is scored based on your angle, speed, duration, and line selection during a drift. The scoring system calculates a combined score from all these factors.

### 漂移技巧 / Drifting Techniques

**基础漂移 / Basic Drift**: 入弯前减速，打方向的同时猛踩油门或拉手刹使后轮失去抓地力，然后通过反打方向（counter-steer）控制车身角度，保持油门维持漂移状态。

Before the turn, slow down, then aggressively steer while stomping the throttle or pulling the handbrake to break rear traction. Counter-steer to control the body angle and maintain throttle to sustain the drift.

**离合器踢 / Clutch Kick**: 手动换挡模式下，在弯道中快速踩下并松开离合器（不换挡）可以瞬间打破轮胎抓地力，帮助你进入或修正漂移角度。这是高级漂移选手常用的技巧。

In manual mode, quickly pressing and releasing the clutch (without shifting) in the middle of a corner momentarily breaks tire grip, helping you initiate or correct drift angle. This is a common technique among advanced drifters.

**重量转移 / Weight Transfer**: 利用刹车将车辆重心转移到前轮，使后轮减轻负载，更容易进入漂移。入弯前轻点刹车即可实现重量转移。

Use braking to shift the vehicle weight to the front wheels, reducing rear wheel load and making it easier to initiate a drift. A light brake tap before the turn achieves weight transfer.

- 入门: 1995 Toyota MR2 GT

## 地平线故事 / Horizon Stories

地平线故事是游戏中叙事驱动的任务线，每个故事包含多个章节。完成全部章节可获得独特的奖励车辆和大量的影响力。

Horizon Stories are narrative-driven mission chains in the game, each containing multiple chapters. Completing all chapters earns unique reward cars and substantial Influence points.

### 主要故事线 / Main Storylines

**欢迎来到墨西哥 / Welcome to Mexico**: 新手教学故事线，了解游戏基本机制 / Tutorial storyline covering basic game mechanics
  - 奖励: 解锁四驱越野车 / Unlock AWD offroad vehicle

**流浪者的传奇 / The Legend of the Drifter**: 学习漂移技巧的故事线 / Storyline teaching drift techniques
  - 奖励: 1996 Ford Mustang SVT Cobra
  - 难度: 中等 / Medium

**备受赞誉 / Vocho**: 关于经典大众甲壳虫的改装故事 / Story about classic VW Beetle modification
  - 奖励: 2020 Koenigsegg Jesko
  - 难度: 中高 / Medium-High

**生而疾速 / Born Fast**: 追求速度极限的故事 / Story about pursuing speed limits
  - 奖励: 2018 Mercedes-AMG Project ONE
  - 难度: 高 / High

**玛雅神庙探险 / The Temple of the Maya**: 越野探险故事 / Offroad exploration story
  - 奖励: 2019 Toyota #1 T100 Baja Truck
  - 难度: 中等 / Medium

**瀑布漂流 / The Waterfall Drift**: 漂移技巧高级挑战 / Advanced drift challenge
  - 奖励: 稀有漂移赛车 / Rare drift car
  - 难度: 高 / High

### 地平线故事攻略技巧 / Horizon Story Tips

- 每章都有铜、银、金三档评价。获得金牌评价可获全额奖励。铜牌即可解锁下一章 / Each chapter has Bronze, Silver, and Gold ratings. Gold earns full rewards. Silver is needed for next chapter
- 可以随时重玩已完成的章节来刷金牌 / You can replay completed chapters anytime for gold ratings
- 部分故事线的章节有时间限制（夜间或特定天气），注意系统提示 / Some chapters have time restrictions (night or specific weather), watch for system提示

## 季节锦标赛 / Seasonal Championships

季节锦标赛是嘉年华播放列表的核心内容，每周四更新。每个赛季持续四天（现实时间），提供独特的赛事组合和奖励。

Seasonal Championships are the core content of the Festival Playlist, updated every Thursday. Each season lasts four days (real-time) and offers unique event combinations and rewards.

### 季节赛事类型 / Seasonal Event Types

**季节锦标赛 / Seasonal Championship**: 三场比赛系列赛，使用指定等级和车型。完成可获得季节积分和稀有车辆奖励。

A three-race series using指定 class and vehicle type. Completion rewards seasonal points and rare vehicles.

**季节特技 / Seasonal PR Stunt**: 在指定 PR 特技地点达到目标分数。通常需要特定类型的车辆。

Reach a target score at a specified PR stunt location. Typically requires a specific vehicle type.

**季节漂移 / Seasonal Drift**: 在指定漂移区获得规定分数。

Earn a specified score at a designated drift zone.

**每月劲敌 / Monthly Rivals**: 在指定赛道与开发者的幽灵车比拼圈速。

Race against developer ghost cars on a指定 track for lap times.
