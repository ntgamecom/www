《极限竞速地平线5》拥有超过 500 辆可驾驶的赛车，涵盖从 20 世纪初的经典老车到最新的电动超跑。车辆按照性能等级（PI 值）和类型进行划分。了解车辆分类体系是选择正确赛车参加比赛的基础。

Forza Horizon 5 features over 500 drivable cars, ranging from early 20th-century classics to the latest electric hypercars. Vehicles are categorized by performance index (PI) and type. Understanding the classification system is fundamental to choosing the right car for each event.

## 性能等级 / Performance Classes

PI（Performance Index）系统将车辆分为七个等级，从最低的 D 级到最高的 X 级。等级越高，车辆综合性能越强。每个等级内数值跨度越大，代表车辆差异越大。比赛通常限定在特定等级内进行。

The PI (Performance Index) system divides cars into seven classes, from the lowest D class to the highest X class. Higher classes mean stronger overall performance. Events are typically restricted to a specific class.

### 等级选择策略 / Class Selection Strategy

- **D 级和 C 级 / D and C Class**: 适合新手练习基础操控技巧 / Best for practicing basic handling skills
- **B 级 / B Class**: 地平线系列最平衡的等级，弯道与直道的完美平衡，线上比赛经常出现 / The most balanced class in the series, perfect corner-to-straight balance, common in online races
- **A 级 / A Class**: 新手到高手最常使用的等级，性能充足但不难控制 / The most commonly used class across all skill levels, plenty of performance without being hard to control
- **S1 级 / S1 Class**: 性能开始变得激进，需要一定的驾驶技巧 / Performance starts becoming aggressive, requires some driving skill
- **S2 级 / S2 Class**: 非常快的等级，适合有经验的玩家 / Very fast class, suitable for experienced players
- **X 级 / X Class**: 几乎无法控制的速度，仅在特定场合有用 / Nearly uncontrollable speed, only useful in specific situations

## 车型分类 / Car Types

### 街车 / Street Cars (ST)
适用于公路赛和街头赛的全能型车辆。悬挂偏舒适，操控线性，适合日常驾驶和轻度比赛。

All-rounder vehicles suited for road racing and street racing. Comfort-oriented suspension, linear handling, suitable for daily driving and light competition.

### 越野车 / Offroad Vehicles (OF)
专为越野赛和越野拉力赛设计的车辆。离地间隙高，悬挂行程长，适合在砂石、泥地和沙漠中行驶。

Designed specifically for dirt racing and cross country events. High ground clearance, long suspension travel, suitable for gravel, mud, and desert terrain.

代表作：Ford Bronco Raptor、Jimmy GMC 4x4、Hoonigan Ford RS200

### 拉力赛车 / Rally Vehicles
介于街车和越野车之间的混合型车辆。四轮驱动常见，悬挂经过强化，适合非铺装路面高速行驶。

Hybrid vehicles between street cars and offroad vehicles. Typically four-wheel drive with reinforced suspension, suitable for high-speed driving on unpaved surfaces.

代表作：Subaru WRX STI、Lancia Delta S4、Ford Focus RS RX

### 漂移车 / Drift Cars
专为漂移打造的车辆。大马力后驱，转向角度大，悬挂设定允许车尾自由滑动。

Built specifically for drifting. High-horsepower rear-wheel drive, large steering angle, suspension tuned to allow free rear-end滑动.

代表作：Formula Drift #64 Nissan 370Z、Hoonicorn Mustang、Toyota Trueno AE86

### 赛车 / Track Toys (赛道玩具)
为赛道性能而生的轻量化跑车。通常为双座中置发动机布局，注重空气动力学和下压力。

Lightweight sports cars born for track performance. Typically mid-engine two-seater layout, emphasizing aerodynamics and downforce.

代表作：Porsche Cayman GT4、Lotus Elise GT1、BAC Mono

### 超级跑车 / Hypercars
汽车工业的巅峰之作。价格最昂贵、速度最快、技术最先进的车型。通常在游戏中用于最高难度的赛事。

The pinnacle of the automotive industry. The most expensive, fastest, and most technologically advanced cars. Typically used for the highest difficulty events.

代表作：Bugatti Chiron、Koenigsegg Jesko、McLaren Speedtail

### 肌肉车 / Muscle Cars
美国特有的车型文化。大排量 V8 发动机前置，后轮驱动，直线加速性能出色但弯道表现不佳。

A uniquely American car culture. Large-displacement front-mounted V8 engines, rear-wheel drive, excellent straight-line performance but poor cornering.

代表作：Dodge Challenger Hellcat、Chevrolet Camaro ZL1、Ford Mustang Shelby GT500

### 经典车 / Classic Cars
生产于 2000 年之前的具有历史价值的车型。虽然原厂性能有限，但通过升级可以焕发新生。

Historically significant vehicles manufactured before 2000. While stock performance is limited, they can be revitalized through upgrades.

代表作：Jaguar E-Type、Ferrari 250 California、Mercedes-Benz 300SL

### JDM 性能车 / JDM Performance Cars
日本国内市场出口的经典性能车型。以轻量化、高转速发动机和出色的操控著称。

Classic performance cars exported from the Japanese domestic market. Known for lightweight construction, high-revving engines, and excellent handling.

代表作：Nissan Skyline GT-R R34、Mazda RX-7 FD、Honda NSX-R

### 电动与混动车 / Electric and Hybrid Cars
新一代新能源性能车。瞬时扭矩大，加速惊人，但在续航和重量方面有局限。

New-generation new-energy performance cars. Instant torque delivery, astonishing acceleration, but limited by range and weight considerations.

代表作：Rimac Nevera、Porsche Taycan Turbo S、Tesla Model S Plaid

## 特殊车辆 / Special Vehicles

### 电动车辆 / Electric Vehicles (EV)
地平线5 中收录了多款纯电动和混合动力车型。电动车的特点是瞬间扭矩输出，加速度极快，但极速通常受限于电池和电机散热。

FH5 includes several pure electric and hybrid vehicles. EVs feature instant torque delivery and extremely fast acceleration, but top speed is typically limited by battery and motor cooling.

值得关注的电动车：Rimac Nevera、Porsche Taycan Turbo S、Tesla Model S Plaid、Audi e-tron GT、BMW i8

### 卡车与SUV / Trucks and SUVs
这些大型车辆在越野赛事中表现出色。尽管公路性能不如跑车，但在沙漠和山地地形中具有统治力。

These large vehicles excel in offroad events. While road performance cannot match sports cars, they dominate desert and mountain terrain.

值得关注的车型：Ford F-450 Super Duty、Mercedes-Benz G-Class、Jeep Wrangler、Land Rover Defender

Special edition vehicles with silver and gold/purple accents. They come with unique performance enhancements and custom liveries. Each FE vehicle also includes special skill bonuses, such as +15% Influence or +15% Cr rewards.
