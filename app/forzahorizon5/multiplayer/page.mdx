## 多人模式总览 / Multiplayer Overview

地平线5的多人模式提供了丰富的在线体验，从传统的竞速比赛到创意十足的游乐场模式，再到独特的淘汰之王大逃杀。无论你喜欢竞技还是合作，都能找到适合自己的玩法。

FH5 multiplayer offers a rich online experience, from traditional racing competitions to creative Playground Games and the unique Eliminator battle royale. Whether you prefer competition or cooperation, there is a mode for you.

## 地平线公开赛 / Horizon Open

地平线公开赛是游戏的在线竞技模式，玩家可以参与排名或非排名的竞速比赛和游乐场游戏。这是测试你技术和车辆调校的最佳场所。

Horizon Open is the online competitive mode where players can participate in ranked or unranked racing and Playground Games. This is the best place to test your skills and vehicle tunes.

### 公开赛赛事类型 / Open Event Types

**竞速赛 / Racing**:
公开赛竞速包含公路赛、街头赛、越野赛和越野拉力赛四种类型。系统会随机选择赛道和等级。

Open racing includes Road Racing, Street Racing, Dirt Racing, and Cross Country. The system randomly selects tracks and classes.

- 排位模式 Ranked Mode: 基于表现进行段位评级 / Ranked based on performance
- 非排位模式 Unranked Mode: 休闲匹配 / Casual matchmaking
- 匹配人数: 最多 12 人 / Up to 12 players
- 碰撞设置 Collision Settings: 可通过设置调整 / Adjustable in settings

**游乐场游戏 / Playground Games**:
游乐场是地平线系列的特色多人模式，将赛车与各种派对游戏结合：

Playground Games are a signature multiplayer mode of the Horizon series, combining racing with various party games:

| 模式 Mode | 规则 Rules | 策略 Strategy |
|:---------|:----------|:------------|
| 感染者 Infected | 一名"感染者"追逐其他玩家，触碰后对方也变为感染者。最后未被感染的玩家获胜 / One "Infected" player chases others; touching turns them infected. Last uninfected player wins | 团队合作，包夹感染者 / Team up, trap the infected |
| 僵尸僵尸 Zombies | 类似感染者，但僵尸队伍使用重型车（慢但强硬）/ Similar to Infected, but zombies drive heavy trucks | 幸存者保持机动，僵尸可设路障 / Survivors stay mobile, zombies set blockades |

### 公开赛技巧 / Open Racing Tips

**起步策略 / Starting Strategy**:
- 倒计时最后半秒时开始踩油门，绿灯亮时全油门 / Start applying throttle in the last half-second of countdown, full throttle at green
- 如果使用自动起步，可以在倒计时中不断快速点击油门来提升转速 / If using自动起步, rapidly tap throttle during countdown to raise RPM
- 位置靠后的玩家在第一个弯道前寻找外侧超越机会 / Players starting at the back should look for outside overtaking opportunities before the first corner

**比赛策略 / Race Strategy**:
- 前几个弯道保持保守，大部分事故发生在第一圈前半段 / Stay conservative in early corners; most incidents happen in the first half of lap one
- 利用牵引区（Slipstream / Drafting）在直道末端超越前车 / Use slipstream (drafting) to overtake at the end of straights
- 在碰撞开启的比赛中，避免与对手并排入弯 / In collision-on races, avoid entering corners side-by-side with opponents

## 地平线巡游 / Horizon Tour

地平线巡游是合作性质的多人模式，多名玩家组队对抗 AI 对手。巡游模式提供轻松的游戏氛围，没有排位压力，适合新手入门多人游戏。

Horizon Tour is a cooperative multiplayer mode where multiple players team up against AI opponents. The Tour offers a relaxed atmosphere without ranking pressure, suitable for beginners entering multiplayer.

### 巡游模式特点 / Tour Mode Features

- 队伍规模 Team Size: 最多 6 名玩家合作 / Up to 6 players cooperatively
- 对手 Opponents: AI 控制的无人车团队（Drivatars）/ AI-controlled Drivatar teams
- 赛事类型 Event Types: 涵盖所有赛事类型 / Covers all event types
- 匹配方式 Matchmaking: 自动加入或创建队伍 / Auto-join or create a convoy
- 奖励 Rewards: 完成巡游获得经验和 Cr 奖励 / Earn Influence and Cr upon completion

**巡游建议 / Tour Tips**:
- 不需要最强车辆，团队合作更重要 / Do not need the best car; teamwork is more important
- 可以自由漫游前往下一个赛事，享受与队友一起探索的乐趣 / Freely roam to the next event and enjoy exploring with teammates
- 完成巡游是解锁部分成就的途径 / Completing tours is a way to unlock certain accolades

## 车队系统 / Convoy System

车队是地平线5的社交组队系统。你可以邀请好友或匹配陌生人组成车队，一起自由探索墨西哥或参加赛事。

The Convoy is the social party system in FH5. You can invite friends or match with strangers to form a convoy, exploring Mexico together or participating in events.

### 车队功能 / Convoy Features

- 语音聊天 Voice Chat: 内置语音支持 / Built-in voice chat support
- 自由漫游 Free Roam: 车队成员在同一个开放世界中 / All convoy members in the same open world
- 共享赛事 Shared Events: 一起参加赛事 / Participate in events together
- 车队聊天 Convoy Chat: 快速聊天消息 / Quick chat messages
- 车队队长 Convoy Leader: 队长可以引导车队活动方向 / Leader directs convoy activities

### 车队队长角色 / Convoy Leader Role

作为车队队长，你可以组织各种活动。这是一个类似角色扮演的体验，好的队长可以让车队体验大大提高。

As a convoy leader, you can organize various activities. This is a role-playing-like experience, and a good leader can greatly enhance the convoy experience.

**车队队长职责 / Convoy Leader Responsibilities**:
1. 确定活动主题 / Set activity theme
2. 选择合适的赛事和路线 / Choose appropriate events and routes
3. 协调队员的车辆选择（统一等级或风格）/ Coordinate vehicle choices (unified class or style)
4. 带领车队在自由漫游中巡航 / Lead the convoy in free roam cruises
5. 组织拍照活动 / Organize photo sessions

**车队活动创意 / Convoy Activity Ideas**:
- **主题巡游 Theme Cruise**: 统一使用同一品牌或类型的车辆巡游地图 / All use same brand or type for map cruise
- **摄影之旅 Photo Tour**: 前往地图上的风景点拍摄集体照 / Visit scenic spots for group photos
- **街头赛巡游 Street Cruise**: 在城市区域低速巡游 / Low-speed cruise in city areas
- **越野探险 Offroad Expedition**: 使用越野车穿越丛林和沙漠 / Cross jungle and desert in offroad vehicles
- **排位冲刺 Ranked Rush**: 一起排队参加公开赛排位 / Queue for ranked Open together
- **漂移列车 Drift Train**: 在公路上列队漂移 / Form a line drift on roads

### 车队管理技巧 / Convoy Management Tips

- 使用清晰的语音或文字指令引导队员 / Use clear voice or text instructions
- 在赛事之间留出足够间隔让队员有时间更换车辆 / Leave enough time between events for vehicle swaps
- 使用地图标记功能标注集合点 / Use map markers for meeting points
- 如果是竞速活动，事先确认碰撞规则 / Confirm collision rules before racing activities
- 尊重新手玩家的节奏，不要压得太快 / Respect new players pace, do not push too hard

## 淘汰之王 / The Eliminator

淘汰之王是地平线系列中的大逃杀模式。最多 72 名玩家在地图上空降，通过车辆对决不断淘汰对手，最终决出冠军。

The Eliminator is the battle royale mode in the Horizon series. Up to 72 players drop onto the map, eliminating opponents through vehicle head-to-head races until a champion is crowned.

### 游戏规则 / Game Rules

1. **跳伞降落 Drop**: 选择地图上的任意位置跳伞降落 / Choose any location on the map to parachute down
2. **寻找车辆 Hunt for Cars**: 在地图上寻找车辆升级图标，每次升级随机获得更高等级的车辆 / Find car upgrade icons on the map, each upgrade randomly gives a higher-level car
3. **对决挑战 Head-to-Head Challenges**: 向附近的对手发起1v1竞速对决，获胜则获得对方的车辆 / Challenge nearby opponents to 1v1 races; winning earns you their car
4. **安全区域缩小 Circle Shrink**: 毒圈不断缩小，圈外持续受到伤害 / The play zone continuously shrinks; staying outside causes damage
5. **决战到最后 Survive to the End**: 最后存活的玩家获胜 / The last player standing wins

### 淘汰之王技巧 / Eliminator Tips

**落地阶段 Landing Phase**:
- 选择靠近多个车辆升级图标的地点 / Choose locations near multiple car upgrade icons
- 避免降落在热门区域（地图中央的升级点竞争激烈）/ Avoid hot zones (central upgrade points have intense competition)
- 落地后第一时间升级车辆，即使是最低级也是有用的 / Upgrade immediately after landing, even the lowest level car is useful

**对决阶段 Head-to-Head Phase**:
- 对决的目标点由系统自动选择 / The destination point is auto-selected by the system
- 熟悉地图可以让你选择更优的路线到达目标点 / Map knowledge helps you choose better routes
- 越野路段中，越野车对比跑车有巨大优势 / Offroad sections give offroad cars a huge advantage over sports cars
- 避免在不熟悉的地区发起对决 / Avoid initiating head-to-heads in unfamiliar areas

**最终阶段 Final Phase**:
- 尽量保持在安全区边缘观察其他玩家 / Stay at the edge of the safe zone to observe others
- 保留一场对决的主动权直到决赛圈 / Save your head-to-head initiative for the final circle
- 最终对决中，车辆等级不是唯一因素，路线选择同样重要 / In the final head-to-head, car level is not the only factor; route choice matters equally
- 如果拥有顶级车辆（Level 10），可以主动发起最后的对决 / If you have a top-tier car (Level 10), proactively initiate the final head-to-head

## 地平线生活 / Horizon Life

地平线生活是多人模式的常驻大厅。每次进入游戏都会自动连接到一个 Horizon Life 服务器，其中最多容纳 72 名玩家。

Horizon Life is the persistent multiplayer lobby. Each time you enter the game, you automatically connect to a Horizon Life server hosting up to 72 players.

### 地平线生活功能 / Horizon Life Features

- **自由漫游 Free Roam**: 与其他玩家共享开放世界 / Share the open world with other players
- **自发活动 Spontaneous Events**: 地图上会随机出现玩家发起的活动 / Random player-initiated events appear on the map
- **平面临近聊天 Proximity Chat**: 靠近其他玩家时可自动进入语音范围 / Auto-enter voice range when near other players
- **共享PR特技 Shared PR Stunts**: 查看其他玩家的 PR 特技记录 / View other players PR stunt records
- **玩家玩家遭遇 Player Encounters**: 路遇其他玩家时可以发起挑战或组队 / Challenge or team up when encountering other players on the road

### 在线礼仪 / Online Etiquette

- 在竞速赛中，故意撞人是违反体育道德的行为 / Intentional ramming in races is unsportsmanlike
- 在自由漫游中，可以礼貌地按喇叭打招呼 / A polite horn honk as a greeting in free roam
- 如果不想被干扰，可以开启单人模式（Horizon Solo）/ Switch to Horizon Solo if you do not want to be disturbed
- 在车队中保持沟通，避免独自行动脱离队伍 / Maintain communication in convoy, avoid going solo
