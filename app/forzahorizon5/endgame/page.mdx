## 终局内容总览 / Endgame Overview

当你通过了开场的风暴体验、完成了新手任务、解锁了所有嘉年华站点，你会发现地平线5的真正游戏才刚刚开始。终局内容是围绕"嘉年华播放列表"（Festival Playlist）展开的长期游戏循环，同时包含两个大型DLC扩展包和声望系统。

After passing through the opening storm, completing the tutorial, and unlocking all festival sites, you will find that the real FH5 game is just beginning. The endgame revolves around the Festival Playlist as a long-term gameplay loop, along with two major DLC expansions and the prestige system.

## 嘉年华播放列表 / Festival Playlist

嘉年华播放列表是地平线5的终局核心内容。每周四更新，提供为期一周（实际上为四天）的季节性挑战。完成这些挑战获得季节积分，积累积分可解锁限时奖励。

The Festival Playlist is the core endgame content of FH5. Updated every Thursday, it offers weekly (actually four-day) seasonal challenges. Complete these challenges for seasonal points, accumulate points to unlock time-limited rewards.

## 名人堂 / Hall of Fame

名人堂是地平线5的终极成就系统。当你的总影响力达到一定阈值后，你将进入名人堂。进入名人堂后，你的游戏进度会被永久记录，并可以在游戏内查看全球排名。

The Hall of Fame is the ultimate achievement system in FH5. When your total Influence reaches a certain threshold, you enter the Hall of Fame. Once in the Hall of Fame, your progress is permanently recorded, and you can view global rankings in-game.

## 威望等级 / Prestige Levels

When your character reaches level 200 (max Influence level), you can choose a prestige reset. The reset brings you back to level 1 but retains all cars, items, Cr, and accolade progress. Each prestige reset unlocks a new badge and increases the level cap.

风火轮 DLC 是地平线5的第一个大型扩展包，将经典的橙色风火轮赛道带入了雪山环境中。这个 DLC 包含全新的地图、赛事和独特的车辆类别。

The Hot Wheels DLC is the first major expansion for FH5, bringing the classic orange Hot Wheels tracks into a snowy mountain environment. This DLC includes a new map, events, and a unique vehicle category.

## 拉力赛冒险 DLC / Rally Adventure DLC

拉力赛冒险是地平线5的第二个大型扩展包，将玩家带到壮观的 Sierra Nueva 区域，体验硬核的越野拉力赛。DLC 引入了全新的"地平线拉力赛"模式。

Rally Adventure is the second major expansion for FH5, taking players to the spectacular Sierra Nueva region for hardcore offroad rally racing. The DLC introduces a new "Horizon Rally" mode.

### DLC 内容 / DLC Content

- **全新地图 New Map**: Sierra Nueva 拉力赛区域 / Sierra Nueva rally region
- **新赛事类型 New Event Type**: 地平线拉力赛（点对点越野竞速）/ Horizon Rally (point-to-point offroad racing)
- **三个车队 Three Teams**: 选择加入不同的拉力赛车队 / Choose to join different rally teams
- **新车辆 New Cars**: 全新拉力赛专用车辆 / New dedicated rally cars
- **动态天气 Dynamic Weather**: 更加极端的环境变化 / More extreme environmental changes

### 拉力赛模式特点 / Rally Mode Features

**领航员系统 / Co-Driver System**:
在拉力赛冒险中，你有一个领航员在副驾驶座提供路线指令。领航员使用"左转"、"右转"、"急弯"、"注意"等术语提示前方路况。学会听懂领航员的指令是获取好成绩的关键。

In Rally Adventure, you have a co-driver in the passenger seat providing route instructions. The co-driver uses terms like "Left", "Right", "Hairpin", and "Caution" to提示 upcoming conditions. Learning to understand co-driver calls is key to good results.

**点对点赛事 / Point-to-Point Racing**:
与传统赛道赛不同，拉力赛是从起点到终点的点对点竞速。没有重复的圈数，每个赛段都是全新的路线。赛道宽度较窄，路面条件不断变化。

Unlike traditional circuit racing, rally is point-to-point from start to finish. No repeated laps, each stage is a completely new route. Tracks are narrower with constantly changing surface conditions.

### 拉力赛攻略 / Rally Tips

- 使用四驱越野车——后驱车在松软路面上难以控制 / Use AWD offroad cars — RWD is hard to control on loose surfaces
- 不要全油门——学会在适当的地方收油比刹车更高效 / Do not go full throttle — learning to lift off in the right places is more efficient than braking
- 保持车辆在良好路面——避免路肩和沟壑 / Keep the car on good surfaces — avoid kerbs and ditches
- 领航员的指令提前告知前方弯道，不要等到最后一刻才反应 / Co-driver calls give early warning of turns, do not wait until the last moment to react
- 直道上利用一切可用路面宽度来获得最快路线 / Use all available road width on straights for the fastest line
- 建议在拉力赛中使用手动换挡以获得更好的发动机制动效果 / Use manual shifting for better engine braking in rally
