# 剧情模式完全解析 / Story Mode Guide

铁拳8的剧情模式"The Dark Awakens"（黑暗觉醒）是本作的重要单人内容。本章节将全面解析剧情模式的故事脉络、章节攻略、角色章节以及Arcade Quest模式，帮助你深入了解铁拳的世界观和三岛家族的宿命。

Tekken 8's story mode "The Dark Awakens" is a major single-player component of this entry. This chapter provides a comprehensive analysis of the story mode's narrative arc, chapter walkthroughs, character episodes, and the Arcade Quest mode, helping you deeply understand the Tekken universe and the fate of the Mishima family.

## 剧情概述 / Story Overview

The Dark Awakens的故事承接铁拳7的结尾。在铁拳7中，三岛平八（Heihachi Mishima）被他的儿子三岛一八（Kazuya Mishima）击败并杀死。一八夺取了三岛财阀的控制权，并利用G公司（G Corporation）的力量在全球范围内展开军事统治。与此同时，风间仁（Jin Kazama）在与宿命的对抗中觉醒了新的力量，开始踏上终结三岛家族诅咒的最终旅程。

The Dark Awakens story picks up after the end of Tekken 7. In Tekken 7, Heihachi Mishima was defeated and killed by his son Kazuya Mishima. Kazuya seized control of the Mishima Zaibatsu and used the power of G Corporation to impose military rule worldwide. Meanwhile, Jin Kazama awakened new powers in his confrontation with fate, embarking on a final journey to end the curse of the Mishima bloodline.

### 核心主题 / Core Theme

本作剧情的核心主题是"选择与命运"（Choice and Destiny）。三岛家族的每一代人都面临着同样的抉择——是继承恶魔基因的力量，还是舍弃力量找回人性。风间仁的故事线聚焦于他在这个两难困境中寻找第三条道路。

The core theme of this entry's story is "Choice and Destiny." Every generation of the Mishima family faces the same dilemma: to inherit the power of the devil gene or to forsake power and reclaim their humanity. Jin Kazama's story arc focuses on his search for a third path in this dilemma.

## 章节攻略 / Chapter Walkthrough

剧情模式共分为15个章节（Chapters），每个章节包含过场动画和可玩关卡。以下为各章节的详细攻略：

The story mode is divided into 15 chapters, each containing cutscenes and playable stages. Below is a detailed walkthrough for each chapter:

### 第1章：觉醒 / Chapter 1: Awakening

**地点：东京，夜晚 / Location: Tokyo, Night**

剧情从风间仁在东京的街头开始。一八的军队正在全球范围内追捕他。本章节为教学关卡，引导玩家了解基本操作：移动、攻击、防御和Rage Art使用。仁初次遇见了神秘回归的母亲风间准的幻影。

The story begins with Jin Kazama on the streets of Tokyo. Kazuya's army is hunting him worldwide. This chapter serves as a tutorial, guiding the player through basic operations: movement, attack, defense, and Rage Art usage. Jin first encounters the phantom of his mother Jun Kazama, who has mysteriously returned.

**攻略要点 / Walkthrough Tips**：
- 跟随屏幕提示完成操作教学
- 击败3波敌人（每波2到3人）
- 在章节末尾使用Rage Art击败Boss级敌人
- Follow on-screen prompts for operation tutorial
- Defeat 3 waves of enemies (2 to 3 per wave)
- Use Rage Art at the chapter end to defeat the boss-level enemy

### 第2章：反抗 / Chapter 2: Resistance

**地点：地下抵抗组织基地 / Location: Underground Resistance Base**

仁找到了由拉斯·亚历山德森（Lars Alexandersson）领导的反抗组织。本章中仁了解了反抗军的现状和G公司的统治策略。可玩内容包括与反抗军成员的训练对战和一次对G公司补给线的突袭行动。

Jin finds the resistance organization led by Lars Alexandersson. In this chapter, Jin learns about the resistance's current situation and G Corporation's统治strategies. Playable content includes training matches with resistance members and a raid on G Corporation supply lines.

**攻略要点 / Walkthrough Tips**：
- 与拉斯进行练习对战（了解拉斯的基础招式）
- 参加补给线突袭战（对抗G公司士兵）
- 使用Heat系统击败重装甲敌人
- Practice match with Lars (learn Lars' basic moves)
- Participate in supply line raid (fight G Corporation soldiers)
- Use Heat system to defeat heavily armored enemies

### 第3章：三岛财阀 / Chapter 3: Mishima Zaibatsu

**地点：三岛财阀总部 / Location: Mishima Zaibatsu Headquarters**

仁和反抗军袭击了三岛财阀总部。本章揭示了三岛财阀在被一八控制后的内部状况。仁在总部的深层区域发现了关于恶魔基因和恶魔之力本质的研究资料。

Jin and the resistance attack the Mishima Zaibatsu headquarters. This chapter reveals the internal state of the Zaibatsu under Kazuya's control. Jin discovers research materials about the devil gene and the nature of devil power in the headquarters' depths.

**攻略要点 / Walkthrough Tips**：
- 潜入关卡：避开巡逻机器人
- 研究资料收集：击败守卫获取信息
- 最终Boss战：对抗三岛财阀精英守卫
- Stealth section: Avoid patrol robots
- Research data collection: Defeat guards for information
- Final boss: Fight the Mishima Zaibatsu elite guard

### 第4章：母亲 / Chapter 4: Mother

**地点：风间神社 / Location: Kazama Shrine**

仁追寻着母亲的幻影来到了一座古老的神社。在这里，准的幻影向仁揭示了风间家族与三岛家族的渊源，以及恶魔基因的起源。本章包含深刻的剧情转折，揭示了准在过去二十年中的经历。

Jin follows his mother's phantom to an ancient shrine. Here, Jun's phantom reveals to Jin the history between the Kazama and Mishima families, as well as the origin of the devil gene. This chapter contains a major plot twist, revealing Jun's experiences over the past two decades.

**攻略要点 / Walkthrough Tips**：
- 神社探索：解开环境谜题
- 与准的幻影进行特殊对话式战斗
- 学习新的ZEN架式派生技巧
- Shrine exploration: Solve environmental puzzles
- Special dialogue-based battle with Jun's phantom
- Learn new ZEN stance派生techniques

### 第5章：G公司 / Chapter 5: G Corporation

**地点：G公司总部 / Location: G Corporation Headquarters**

仁和反抗军突袭G公司总部。本章中玩家需要对抗妮娜·威廉姆斯（Nina Williams）和G公司的精锐部队。在本章末尾，仁首次直接面对三岛一八，但被一八压倒性的力量击败。

Jin and the resistance storm the G Corporation headquarters. In this chapter, players must fight Nina Williams and G Corporation elite forces. At the chapter's end, Jin faces Kazuya Mishima directly for the first time but is defeated by Kazuya's overwhelming power.

**攻略要点 / Walkthrough Tips**：
- 对抗妮娜·威廉姆斯（BOSS战，注意妮娜的快速压制）
- 突破G公司安全系统
- 对战一八（本战必定失败，剧情杀）
- Fight Nina Williams (boss battle, watch for Nina's fast pressure)
- Break through G Corporation security systems
- Fight Kazuya (scripted loss, story progression)

### 第6章：觉醒之力 / Chapter 6: Awakened Power

**地点：未知场所 / Location: Unknown Location**

被一八击败后，仁在神秘空间醒来。在这里，他遇到了内心的恶魔——恶魔仁（Devil Jin）。这一章是仁的内省之旅，他必须面对自己内心的恶魔基因和黑暗面。最终，仁接受了恶魔基因作为自己的一部分，但选择用自己的意志控制它，而非被它控制。

After being defeated by Kazuya, Jin wakes up in a mysterious space. Here, he encounters his inner demon — Devil Jin. This chapter is Jin's introspective journey, where he must face his inner devil gene and dark side. Ultimately, Jin accepts the devil gene as part of himself, but chooses to control it with his will rather than be controlled by it.

**攻略要点 / Walkthrough Tips**：
- 与恶魔仁的内心对战（特殊机制的BOSS战）
- 解锁仁的新形态和新招式
- 觉醒后的仁获得力量提升
- Inner battle with Devil Jin (mechanic-specific boss fight)
- Unlock Jin's new form and new moves
- Awakened Jin gains power enhancement

### 第7章至第10章 / Chapters 7 to 10

**第7章：反攻（地点：全球多个战场）**
反抗军发动全球反攻。仁与拉斯、晓雨、李超狼联手，在全球多个城市同时发起攻击。本章包含多线叙事，玩家可以在不同角色之间切换。

**Chapter 7: Counterattack (Location: Multiple Global Battlefields)**
The resistance launches a global counterattack. Jin teams up with Lars, Xiaoyu, and Lee Chaolan to attack multiple cities simultaneously. This chapter features multi-perspective narrative, allowing players to switch between different characters.

**第8章：宿命对决（地点：火山基地）**
仁和一八在G公司的火山基地中再次相遇。这一战双方都使用了完整的恶魔之力，战斗中场景遭到严重破坏。仁开始展现出觉醒后的真正实力。

**Chapter 8: Fateful Duel (Location: Volcano Base)**
Jin and Kazuya meet again at the G Corporation volcano base. Both sides use full devil power in this battle, and the scene suffers severe destruction. Jin begins to show his true awakened power.

**第9章：真相（地点：三岛家族地下设施）**
仁发现了三岛家族最深层的秘密——关于恶魔基因的真正起源、三岛平八过去的实验以及风间准消失的真相。本章包含大量的剧情揭露。

**Chapter 9: Truth (Location: Mishima Family Underground Facility)**
Jin discovers the deepest secrets of the Mishima family — the true origin of the devil gene, Heihachi's past experiments, and the truth behind Jun Kazama's disappearance. This chapter contains heavy story revelations.

**第10章：最终决战（地点：空中要塞）**
仁追上一八来到空中要塞。在经历了所有试炼后，仁最终击败了恶魔化的三岛一八，打破了恶魔基因对三岛家族的诅咒。一八在最后时刻似乎恢复了人性，与要塞一起坠入大海。

**Chapter 10: Final Battle (Location: Aerial Fortress)**
Jin pursues Kazuya to the aerial fortress. After all trials, Jin finally defeats the demonized Kazuya Mishima, breaking the curse of the devil gene on the Mishima family. Kazuya seems to regain his humanity in his final moments and falls into the sea with the fortress.

### 结局 / Ending

最终，风间仁舍弃了全部恶魔之力，变回了纯粹的人类。他在神社与母亲风间准重逢，迎来了真正的和平。彩蛋场景暗示三岛平八可能以某种形式存活...

In the end, Jin Kazama relinquishes all devil power, returning to being a pure human. He reunites with his mother Jun at the shrine, experiencing true peace. A post-credits scene hints that Heihachi Mishima may have survived in some form...

## 角色章节 / Character Episodes

除了主线剧情外，每个可玩角色都有独立的章节。角色章节通常包含2到3场对战和一段简短的过场动画。

In addition to the main story, each playable character has an independent episode. Character episodes typically contain 2 to 3 battles and a short cutscene.

### 角色章节攻略要点 / Character Episode Walkthrough Tips

**Paul Phoenix 章节**：保罗为了证明自己的实力参加了地下格斗大赛，需要连续击败3个强力对手。保罗章节的难度适中，适合新手通关。

**King 章节**：King为了保护孤儿院的孩子参加慈善格斗赛。章节中包含特殊的投技限制挑战（只能用投技击败对手）。

**Nina Williams 章节**：妮娜作为雇佣兵执行暗杀任务，需要在限时内击败目标角色。章节中有潜行要素。

**Yoshimitsu 章节**：吉光的章节最为奇特，包含隐藏通道和特殊道具收集元素。

**Paul Phoenix Episode**: Paul enters an underground fighting tournament to prove his strength, needing to defeat 3 strong opponents consecutively. This episode is moderately difficult, suitable for beginners.

**King Episode**: King participates in a charity fighting tournament to protect the orphanage children. The episode includes a special throw-only challenge (defeat opponents using throws only).

**Nina Williams Episode**: Nina executes assassination missions as a mercenary, needing to defeat target characters within time limits. The episode features stealth elements.

**Yoshimitsu Episode**: Yoshimitsu's episode is the most unique, featuring hidden passages and special道具collection elements.

## Arcade Quest模式 / Arcade Quest Mode

Arcade Quest是铁拳8新增的单人游戏模式，模拟了在游戏厅中成长的体验。玩家创建一个虚拟化身，在多个游戏厅中挑战各种对手，逐步解锁隐藏的角色和奖励。

Arcade Quest is a new single-player mode introduced in Tekken 8, simulating the experience of growing up in an arcade. Players create a virtual avatar and challenge various opponents across multiple arcades, gradually unlocking hidden characters and rewards.

### Arcade Quest流程 / Arcade Quest Flow

**第1阶段：本地游戏厅 / Stage 1: Local Arcade**
玩家从一个小型本地游戏厅开始，挑战普通的AI对手，学习游戏基础知识。这个阶段相当于进阶教学。

**第2阶段：城市锦标赛 / Stage 2: City Tournament**
玩家前往城市中的大型游戏厅参加锦标赛，面对更有挑战性的AI对手。

**第3阶段：全国大赛 / Stage 3: National Tournament**
玩家参加全国级别的比赛，面对高难度AI和特殊的Boss角色。

**第4阶段：隐藏Boss / Stage 4: Hidden Boss**
完成所有锦标赛后，玩家需要挑战隐藏Boss角色，击败后获得最终奖励。

**Stage 1: Local Arcade**: Start from a small local arcade, challenge basic AI opponents, learn game fundamentals. This stage serves as advanced tutorial.

**Stage 2: City Tournament**: Travel to a large city arcade for a tournament, facing more challenging AI opponents.

**Stage 3: National Tournament**: Participate in national-level competition, facing high-difficulty AI and special boss characters.

**Stage 4: Hidden Boss**: After all tournaments, challenge the hidden boss character for the final reward.

## 三岛家族编年史 / Mishima Family Chronicle

### 三岛家族血统 / Mishima Bloodline

三岛一族的宿命始于三岛仁八（Jinpachi Mishima），他在神秘事件后获得了恶魔之力。他的儿子三岛平八（Heihachi Mishima）继承了这份力量但并未显现恶魔基因。平八的儿子三岛一八（Kazuya Mishima）是第一个明确显现恶魔基因的后代。一八的儿子风间仁（Jin Kazama）同时继承了母亲风间准的善良之力和父亲的恶魔基因。

The fate of the Mishima clan began with Jinpachi Mishima, who acquired devil power after a mysterious incident. His son Heihachi Mishima inherited this power but did not manifest the devil gene. Heihachi's son Kazuya Mishima was the first descendant to clearly manifest the devil gene. Kazuya's son Jin Kazama inherited both his mother Jun Kazama's benevolent power and his father's devil gene.

### 恶魔基因 / The Devil Gene

恶魔基因是三岛家族血脉中潜伏的神秘力量。拥有恶魔基因的人可以在特定条件下恶魔化，获得超人的力量和恢复能力，但也会被恶魔的意志侵蚀。风间仁是唯一一个选择主动放弃恶魔基因的三岛家族成员，他在铁拳8中完成了这一壮举。

The devil gene is a mysterious power lying dormant in the Mishima bloodline. Those with the devil gene can demonize under specific conditions, gaining superhuman strength and regenerative ability, but also become eroded by the devil's will. Jin Kazama is the only Mishima family member who chose to voluntarily relinquish the devil gene, achieving this feat in Tekken 8.

### 风间家族 / The Kazama Family

风间家族与三岛家族有着古老的渊源。风间流古武术是一种可以与恶魔之力对抗的净化之术。风间准（Jun Kazama）在铁拳2中首次登场，但在铁拳3的事件后神秘失踪。在铁拳8中，她以幻影和实体两种形态回归，帮助风间仁完成净化仪式。

The Kazama family has an ancient connection with the Mishima family. Kazama-style ancient martial arts are a purification art capable of opposing devil power. Jun Kazama first appeared in Tekken 2 but mysteriously disappeared after the events of Tekken 3. In Tekken 8, she returns in both phantom and physical forms to help Jin Kazama complete the purification ritual.
