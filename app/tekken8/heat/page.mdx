# Heat系统完全攻略 / Heat System Guide

Heat系统是铁拳8最具创新性的新机制，完全改变了游戏的进攻节奏和战术格局。本章节将深入讲解Heat系统的运作方式、各种Heat状态下的策略以及每个角色的Heat能力。

The Heat system is the most innovative new mechanic in Tekken 8, completely transforming the game's offensive rhythm and tactical landscape. This chapter provides an in-depth explanation of how the Heat system works, strategies for each Heat状态，and every character's Heat abilities.

## Heat系统概述 / Heat System Overview

Heat是铁拳8新增的资源系统，每局游戏可以使用一次。启动Heat后，角色进入持续时间约10秒的强化状态。Hell在时间和招式上都有明确的设计框架，理解这些框架是有效使用Heat的前提。

Heat is a new resource system in Tekken 8 that can be used once per round. After activation, the character enters an enhanced state lasting approximately 10 seconds. Heat has a clear design framework in terms of duration and moves, and understanding this framework is the prerequisite for effective Heat usage.

### 进入Heat的三种方式 / Three Ways to Enter Heat

**Heat Burst**：按下2+3（可自定义）立即发动。Heat Burst释放一道可恢复的冲击波，具有装甲属性（可以承受一次攻击）。这是最直接的Heat启动方式，适合在需要立即改变局势时使用。

**Heat Engager**：使用特定招式（每个角色拥有1到2个Heat Engager技）命中对手后，按前方向键执行Heat Dash，直接进入Heat状态并取消Heat Engager的收招硬直。这是最常见的Heat启动方式，也是连段延伸的核心工具。

**Heat Activate**：在地面站立状态下按Heat键（2+3）直接进入Heat状态，不发动任何攻击。这种方式不会消耗Heat Burst的冲击波效果，但也会让对手立即知道你进入了Heat状态。

**Heat Burst**: Press 2+3 (customizable) to activate immediately. Heat Burst unleashes a recoverable shockwave with armor properties (absorbs one hit). This is the most direct Heat activation method, suitable for when you need to immediately change the situation.

**Heat Engager**: After landing a specific move (each character has 1 to 2 Heat Engager moves), press forward to execute Heat Dash, entering Heat状态and canceling the recovery of the Heat Engager. This is the most common Heat activation method and the core tool for combo extension.

**Heat Activate**: Press the Heat键while standing to enter Heat状态without attacking. This method does not consume the Heat Burst shockwave effect, but it immediately telegraphs to the opponent that you are in Heat状态。

## Heat状态机制 / Heat State Mechanics

### 持续时间 / Duration

Heat状态的基础持续时间约为10秒。在Heat状态下，以下行动会延长Heat持续时间：

The base duration of Heat状态is approximately 10 seconds. The following actions extend Heat持续时间：

| 行动 / Action | 延长时间 / Extension |
|---|---|
| Heat Engager命中对手 | +3秒 |
| Heat Smash命中对手 | +2秒 |
| 特殊Heat技命中对手 | +1秒 |
| Heat Burst直接发动 | -2秒 (无后续动作) |

### Heat状态增益 / Heat State Benefits

**伤害提升 / Damage Increase**：所有攻击的伤害提升约5%至10%。这个提升适用于所有招式，包括连段中的每一下攻击。

**Heat Smash**：Heat状态下可以使用一次强力的Heat Smash技。Heat Smash是每个角色独有的招式，通常具有以下特点：高伤害、装甲属性（发动中可承受一次攻击）、中段判定（不可蹲防）、优秀的追踪能力。

**回复生命 / Health Recovery**：在Heat状态下命中对手可以恢复灰色生命值（Recoverable Health）。这意味着在Heat期间积极进攻可以同时造成伤害并恢复血量。

**能力强化 / Ability Enhancement**：特定招式在Heat状态下获得额外性能，包括新增派生、增加判定范围、追加浮空效果或改变招式属性。

**Damage Increase**: All attacks deal 5% to 10% increased damage. This boost applies to all moves, including each hit in a combo.

**Heat Smash**: A powerful attack unique to each character usable once during Heat. Heat Smash typically has the following properties: high damage, armor (absorbs one hit during startup), mid属性 (cannot be ducked), and excellent tracking.

**Health Recovery**: Landing attacks during Heat recovers gray health (Recoverable Health). This means active offense during Heat simultaneously deals damage and restores health.

**Ability Enhancement**: Specific moves gain additional properties during Heat, including new派生, expanded hitboxes,追加浮空effects, or altered move properties.

### Heat结束状态 / Heat End State

Heat状态结束后，角色进入Heat Sickness（热量过载）状态，持续约5秒。在此状态下：

- 无法再次使用Heat系统
- 部分Heat强化招式失效
- 伤害提升效果消失

After Heat状态ends, the character enters Heat Sickness (热量过载) state, lasting approximately 5 seconds. During this state:

- Heat system cannot be used again
- Some heat-enhanced moves lose their properties
- The damage boost effect disappears

## Heat Engager详解 / Heat Engager Detail

Heat Engager是进入Heat状态并立即获得进攻优势的最佳方式。每个角色拥有1到2个Heat Engager招式。

Heat Engager is the best way to enter Heat状态and immediately gain an offensive advantage. Each character has 1 to 2 Heat Engager moves.

### Heat Engager的特点 / Heat Engager Properties

- 命中后对手进入特殊受击状态（Heat Engager状态），无法行动约1秒
- 在此期间按前方向键可以发动Heat Dash，直接取消收招并冲向对手
- Heat Dash后可以立即使用攻击继续连段或压制
- Heat Engager即使被防御，使用者仍然处于有利帧数（通常是+5至+8帧）

- On hit, the opponent enters a special Hit状态(Heat Engager状态), unable to act for about 1 second
- During this window, pressing forward activates Heat Dash, canceling recovery and charging toward the opponent
- After Heat Dash, you can immediately attack to continue combos or pressure
- Even if heat Engager is blocked, the user still has frame advantage (usually +5 to +8 frames)

## Heat Smash详解 / Heat Smash Detail

Heat Smash是Heat状态下每个角色可以使用一次的强力攻击。理解Heat Smash的特性和用法是Heat系统进阶的关键。

Heat Smash is a powerful attack that each character can use once during Heat状态。Understanding Heat Smash properties and usage is key to advancing your Heat system knowledge.

### Heat Smash通用特性 / Universal Heat Smash Properties

- 发动时有装甲属性（承受一次攻击）
- 中段判定（不可蹲姿回避）
- 高伤害（一般约为45至60点伤害）
- 优秀追踪属性（可击中侧移的对手）
- 命中后倒地，可以进行Okizeme压制
- 每套Heat状态只能使用一次

- Armor during startup (absorbs one hit)
- Mid属性 (cannot be crouched under)
- High damage (typically 45 to 60 damage)
- Excellent tracking (hits sidestepping opponents)
- Knocks down on hit, allowing okizeme pressure
- Can only be used once per Heat状态

### Heat Smash策略 / Heat Smash Strategies

**连段中继**：在连段中使用Heat Smash作为中继，连接前后两段连段。这是最常见的用法，可以有效提升连段伤害。

**起身压制**：在对手倒地时发动Heat Smash进行Okizeme压制。Heat Smash的装甲属性可以吸收对手的起身攻击，确保压制有效。

**全屏终结**：在远距离发动Heat Smash作为终结技。Heat Smash的冲击波范围很大，可以在对手放松警惕时出奇制胜。

**Combo link**: Use Heat Smash as a mid-combo link between two combo segments. This is the most common usage and effectively increases combo damage.

**Okizeme pressure**: Activate Heat Smash while the opponent is grounded for okizeme pressure. Heat Smash's armor absorbs the opponent's getup attack, ensuring effective pressure.

**Full-screen finisher**: Use Heat Smash from long range as a finisher. The shockwave has a large range and can catch opponents off guard.

## Heat Dash详解 / Heat Dash Detail

Heat Dash是Heat Engager命中后的衍生选项。在Heat Engager命中后快速按前方向键，角色会向前冲刺并进入Heat状态。

Heat Dash is a follow-up option after a Heat Engager lands. Quickly press forward after a Heat Engager hits, and the character dashes forward while entering Heat状态。

### Heat Dash连段结构 / Heat Dash Combo Structure

Heat Dash后的标准连段结构如下：

The standard combo structure after Heat Dash is as follows:

`Heat Engager命中 -> Heat Dash -> f+2 (或f+4) 追击 -> Tornado -> 墙壁搬运 -> 墙壁连段`

Heat Dash后推荐使用f+2或f+4作为追击技，这两种攻击速度快、判定好，是通用的Heat Dash追击选择。

After Heat Dash, f+2 or f+4 is recommended as the follow-up attack. These moves are fast with solid properties, making them universal Heat Dash pursuit options.

### Heat Dash压制策略 / Heat Dash Pressure Strategy

Heat Engager被防御时，虽然无法发动Heat Dash，但使用者仍然处于帧数优势（+5至+8）。此时可以利用帧数优势进行压制：

When Heat Engager is blocked, Heat Dash cannot be activated, but the user still has frame advantage (+5 to +8). Use this frame advantage for pressure:

- 使用加帧技继续压制
- 使用投技（对手因为帧数劣势容易被动防御）
- 使用下段攻击破防
- 使用中段浮空技测试对手的防御习惯

- Use frame-advantage moves for continued pressure
- Use throws (opponents in frame disadvantage tend to block passively)
- Use low attacks to break defense
- Use mid launchers to test the opponent's defensive habits

Each character has unique enhancements during Heat状态。Here are some representative characters' Heat properties:

## 最佳Heat使用策略 / Optimal Heat Usage Strategies

### 进攻型Heat / Aggressive Heat

适用于压制型角色（Nina, Hwoarang, Dragunov），核心思路是使用Heat Engager直接发动进攻，连续压制直到Heat结束。

Best for rushdown characters (Nina, Hwoarang, Dragunov). The core idea is to use Heat Engager to directly launch offense, continuously pressuring until Heat ends.

**步骤1**：使用Heat Engager命中对手（或利用加帧压制）
**Step 1**: Land a Heat Engager (or use frame advantage for pressure)

**步骤2**：发动Heat Dash继续追击
**Step 2**: Activate Heat Dash to continue pursuit

**步骤3**：利用Heat状态的伤害提升和回血效果持续压制
**Step 3**: Use the damage boost and health recovery effects of Heat for sustained pressure

**步骤4**：在Heat结束前使用Heat Smash收尾或重置局势
**Step 4**: Use Heat Smash before Heat ends for a finisher or situation reset

### 防守反击型Heat / Defensive Heat

适用于防守型角色（Bryan, Jack-8, Feng），核心思路是在防守中寻找机会，利用Heat Burst的装甲属性和冲击波实现逆转。

Best for defensive characters (Bryan, Jack-8, Feng). The core idea is to find opportunities during defense, using Heat Burst's armor and shockwave for reversal.

**步骤1**：在劣势时发动Heat Burst，利用装甲吸收对手的攻击
**Step 1**: Activate Heat Burst at a disadvantage, using armor to absorb the opponent's attack

**步骤2**：冲击波命中后进入Heat状态，回血并重新获得场上主动权
**Step 2**: Enter Heat状态after the shockwave lands, recover health, and regain control

**步骤3**：利用Heat状态的优势进行反击连段
**Step 3**: Use the Heat state advantage for punishing combos

**步骤4**：保留Heat Smash应对对手的后续压制
**Step 4**: Save Heat Smash for the opponent's后续pressure

### 混合型Heat / Hybrid Heat

适用于全面型角色（Jin, Claudio, Lee），根据局势灵活选择Heat的使用方式。

Best for all-rounder characters (Jin, Claudio, Lee). Flexibly choose Heat usage based on the situation.

**场景选择 / Scenario Selection**：

**领先时 / When ahead**：使用Heat Engager启动连段，追求最大化伤害输出并直接终结比赛。

**落后时 / When behind**：使用Heat Burst逆转局势，利用回血效果弥补生命值差距。

**均势时 / When even**：使用Heat Activate试探对手反应，利用Heat的威慑力创造进攻机会。

**When ahead**: Use Heat Engager to start combos, maximizing damage output for a direct round finish.

**When behind**: Use Heat Burst to reverse the situation, using health recovery to close the health gap.

**When even**: Use Heat Activate to test the opponent's reaction, using the threat of Heat to create offensive opportunities.

## Heat系统进阶技巧 / Advanced Heat Techniques

### Heat保留 / Heat Retention

在某些情况下，不发动Heat可能比立即发动更有价值。保留Heat可以作为心理威慑，让对手在防守时多一层顾虑。你可以利用这点制造投技和破防的机会。

In some situations, not activating Heat may be more valuable than activating immediately. Retaining Heat serves as psychological deterrence, adding another layer of concern for the opponent's defense. Use this to create throw and guard break opportunities.

### Heat取消陷阱 / Heat Cancel Traps

部分角色拥有特殊的Heat取消技巧。例如，在特定招式中取消进入Heat状态可以创造对手意想不到的攻击节奏。这些技巧需要在对角色的招式和帧数数据有深入理解后才能熟练运用。

Some characters have special Heat cancel techniques. For example, canceling into Heat状态during specific moves can create unexpected offensive rhythms. These techniques require deep understanding of the character's moves and frame data to execute consistently.

### Heat与Rage Art的组合 / Heat and Rage Art Combination

Heat状态和Rage Art可以产生协同效应。在Heat状态下发动Rage Art，可以获得以下好处：

- Rage Art伤害略微提升（约5%）
- Rage Art命中后恢复更多灰色生命值
- Heat状态下Rage Art的装甲性能不变

建议在Heat状态末尾（即将进入Heat Sickness前）使用Rage Art，将Heat的剩余价值最大化。

Heat状态and Rage Art can create synergy. Activating Rage Art during Heat状态provides:

- Slightly increased Rage Art damage (about 5%)
- More gray health recovery after Rage Art hits
- Armor properties remain unchanged during Heat

It is recommended to use Rage Art near the end of Heat状态(just before entering Heat Sickness) to maximize Heat's remaining value.
