# 新手入门指南 / Beginner Guide

欢迎来到铁拳8！这一章节面向完全的新手，从最基础的操作开始讲解。如果你是第一次接触铁拳系列或3D格斗游戏，请从这里开始。

Welcome to Tekken 8. This chapter is designed for complete beginners, starting from the most basic operations. If this is your first time with the Tekken series or 3D fighting games, start here.

## 控制器设置 / Controller Setup

铁拳8支持多种控制器类型：无线手柄（DualSense/Xbox Wireless Controller）、街机摇杆（Arcade Stick/Arcade Controller）和Hitbox风格控制器。选择适合自己的设备是提升的第一步。

Tekken 8 supports multiple controller types: wireless gamepads (DualSense/Xbox Wireless Controller), arcade sticks, and Hitbox-style controllers. Choosing the right device is the first step toward improvement.

### 手柄按键布局 / Gamepad Button Layout

铁拳使用1/2/3/4的按键编号系统对应拳脚攻击。默认手柄布局如下：

Tekken uses a 1/2/3/4 numbering system for punch and kick attacks. The default gamepad layout is:

| 按键编号 / Button | 攻击类型 / Attack Type | PS手柄 / PS Pad | Xbox手柄 / Xbox Pad |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 左拳 / Left Punch | 方块 / Square | X |
| 2 | 右拳 / Right Punch | 三角 / Triangle | Y |
| 3 | 左腿 / Left Kick | 叉 / Cross | A |
| 4 | 右腿 / Right Kick | 圆圈 / Circle | B |

### 摇杆与Hitbox / Arcade Stick and Hitbox

街机摇杆的标准布局遵循铁拳系列传统：1（左拳）在上排左侧，2（右拳）在上排右侧，3（左腿）在下排左侧，4（右腿）在下排右侧。Hitbox控制器使用按键代替摇杆，方向键分别为WASD布局，攻击键与摇杆相同。

The standard arcade stick layout follows Tekken tradition: 1 (LP) top-left, 2 (RP) top-right, 3 (LK) bottom-left, 4 (RK) bottom-right. Hitbox controllers use buttons instead of a joystick, with directional inputs on a WASD layout and attack buttons matching the arcade stick.

### 按键自定义 / Button Customization

游戏内提供完整的按键自定义功能。你可以在选项中重新映射所有操作的按键，包括特殊绑定如Rage Art一键发动。许多高手会将Rage Art绑定到一个方便快速按下的组合键上。

The game offers full button customization. You can remap all actions in the options menu, including special bindings like one-button Rage Art. Many high-level players bind Rage Art to an easily accessible combination.

## 基本移动 / Basic Movement

铁拳是3D格斗游戏，意味着你可以在三个维度上移动：前后（Z轴）和左右（X轴）。掌握移动是立回的基础。

Tekken is a 3D fighting game, meaning you can move in three dimensions: forward/backward (Z-axis) and left/right (X-axis). Mastering movement is the foundation of neutral game.

### 前进与后退 / Forward and Backward Dash

按住前方向键使角色向前行走，按住后方向键使角色后退。连点两次前方向键执行前冲（Forward Dash），连点两次后方向键执行后撤步（Backdash）。后撤步是铁拳中最基本的防御移动技巧。

Hold forward to walk forward, hold back to walk backward. Tap forward twice to perform a forward dash, tap back twice to perform a backdash. The backdash is the most fundamental defensive movement technique in Tekken.

### 侧移 / Sidestep

快速向上或向下拨动摇杆（或按对应方向键）执行侧移。侧移可以回避直线攻击和投技，是3D格斗游戏独特的立回工具。注意有些攻击带有"追踪"（Tracking）属性，可以命中侧移中的对手。

Flick the stick up or down (or press the corresponding direction button) to sidestep. Sidestepping evades linear attacks and throws, making it a unique neutral tool in 3D fighting games. Note that some attacks have tracking properties that can catch sidestepping opponents.

### 韩国后撤步 / Korean Backdash (KBD)

韩国后撤步（KBD）是铁拳系列最重要的高级移动技巧。通过快速交替输入后撤步和取消动作，实现比普通后撤步更远、更快的后退距离。KBD的核心输入模式是：b, b, d, b（后、后、下、后）的快速交替循环。初学者可以在练习模式中从慢速开始练习这一技巧。

Korean Backdash (KBD) is the most important advanced movement technique in the Tekken series. By rapidly alternating between backdash and cancel inputs, you achieve faster and longer backward movement than a normal backdash. The core input pattern is: b, b, d, b entered in a rapid alternating cycle. Beginners should practice this slowly in practice mode.

### 蹲姿与起身 / Crouching and Standing

按住下方向键使角色蹲下。蹲姿可以回避上段攻击，但也使你无法防御中段攻击。按下方向键加一个攻击键执行下段攻击。被击倒后，你可以选择不同的起身方式：直接站起、向后受身、原地起身或保持倒地。

Hold down to crouch. Crouching evades high attacks but leaves you unable to block mid attacks. Press down plus an attack button to perform a low attack. After being knocked down, you can choose different getup options: stand up directly, tech roll backward, get up in place, or stay on the ground.

## 基本攻击 / Basic Attacks

铁拳的操作逻辑围绕四种攻击按键展开，不同按键组合可以释放各种招式。

Tekken的操作逻辑基于四种攻击按键，不同按键组合可以释放各种招式。

Tekken的操作逻辑围绕着四种攻击按键展开。通过不同的按键组合，可以释放从普通技到特殊技再到必杀技的各种招式。

### 普通攻击分类 / Attack Type Classification

| 类型 / Type | 描述 / Description | 防御方式 / Defense |
|---|---|---|
| 上段 / High | 可以蹲下回避 / Can be ducked | 站防或蹲避 / Stand block or duck |
| 中段 / Mid | 必须站立防御 / Must stand block | 站防 / Stand block |
| 下段 / Low | 必须蹲下防御 / Must crouch block | 蹲防 / Crouch block |
| 特殊中段 / Special Mid | 无法防御 / Unblockable | 移动回避 / Evade by moving |

### 十连 / 10-Hit Combos

每个角色都有一套预设的十连击（10-Hit Combo），通过按顺序输入特定按键发动。虽然十连在高段位比赛中很少使用（因为可以被确反），但它是了解角色攻击节奏的好方法。

Every character has a preset 10-hit combo, activated by inputting specific buttons in sequence. Although 10-hit combos are rarely used in high-level play (they can be punished), they are a great way to learn a character's attack rhythm.

### 投技 / Throws

按下1+3或2+4发动普通投技。投技可以用拆投（Throw Break）来破解：在受到投技的瞬间，根据对方使用的投技类型按下对应的拆投按键。左手投（用对手的左手抓取）按1拆，右手投按2拆。

Press 1+3 or 2+4 to perform a normal throw. Throws can be broken with a throw break: at the moment of being grabbed, press the corresponding button based on the throw type. Break a left-arm throw with 1, a right-arm throw with 2.

## Rage Art /  rage Art

当角色生命值低于一定比例（约20%左右）时，角色身体会发出红色光芒，进入Rage状态。此时可以发动Rage Art——一种拥有全屏范围的超必杀技，具有华丽的演出动画和极高的伤害。Rage Art可以通过按下特定组合键发动（默认为2+3）。需要注意的是，Rage Art发动后将在命中或防御后消耗全部Rage能量，如果被防御则会产生巨大的破绽，因此使用时机至关重要。

When a character's health drops below a certain threshold (approximately 20%), the character glows red and enters Rage状态。Rage Art can be activated (default 2+3). Rage Art consumes all Rage能量 upon use, and if blocked, leaves the user at significant disadvantage, so timing is critical.

需要注意的是，Rage Art如果在攻击中被阻挡，使用者会陷入巨大的劣势（帧数不利），容易被对方确反。因此Rage Art不适合在对手防御时使用，最适合用作连段收尾或读透对手攻击后的确反手段。

Note that if the Rage Art is blocked during an attack, the user is left at a significant disadvantage (frame disadvantage) and can be easily punished. Therefore, Rage Art is not suitable for use when the opponent is blocking. It is best used as a combo ender or as a punishment tool after reading the opponent's attack.

## 场地破坏 / Stage Destruction

铁拳8的场地互动系统比前作更加丰富。每个场地都有可破坏的区域，包括墙壁（Wall）、地板（Floor）和阳台（Balcony）。将对手打入可破坏表面会导致额外的伤害和击退效果。

Tekken 8's stage interaction system is more extensive than previous entries. Each stage has destructible areas including walls, floors, and balconies. Driving opponents into destructible surfaces causes additional damage and knockback effects.

### 墙壁破坏 / Wall Break

当对手被击飞到墙壁附近并受到攻击时，墙壁会碎裂并触发墙壁破坏效果。墙壁破坏后角色会进入一个新的空间区域，继续连段。墙壁连段（Wall Combo）是铁拳中造成大量伤害的关键技术。

When an opponent is knocked toward a wall and hit, the wall shatters, triggering a wall break. After the wall breaks, characters enter a new area, allowing combos to continue. Wall combos are a key technique for dealing massive damage in Tekken.

### 地板破坏 / Floor Break

在某些场地，击倒对手可以使地板碎裂，角色将坠落到下层区域。坠落过程会中断当前的连段，但落地后双方都可以立即行动。地板破坏为连段增添了新的变化和不确定性。

On certain stages, knocking down an opponent can shatter the floor, causing characters to fall to a lower area. The fall interrupts the current combo, but both players can act immediately upon landing. Floor breaks add new variables and uncertainty to combos.

## 练习模式 / Practice Mode

练习模式是新手进步的加速器。铁拳8的练习模式功能强大，包括以下核心功能：

Practice mode is the accelerator for beginner improvement. Tekken 8's practice mode is powerful, including the following core features:

**训练设置 / Training Settings**：可以设定对手的行为，如站立防御、蹲防、连续攻击、随机反击等，帮助你针对性地练习特定场景。

**帧数显示 / Frame Display**：开启后可以看到每招的帧数数据，包括启动帧（Startup）、判定发生帧（Active）、收招帧（Recovery）和帧数优势/劣势（Frame Advantage/Disadvantage）。

**连段练习 / Combo Practice**：游戏内置了推荐连段列表，你可以选定一个连段，游戏会通过慢动作回放指导你的输入。

**记录与回放 / Recording and Playback**：可以记录对手的一系列动作并循环播放，用于练习特定对局的应对策略。

**Training Settings**: You can set opponent behavior such as standing guard, crouching guard, continuous attack, random punishment, etc., to practice specific scenarios.

**Frame Display**: When enabled, you can see frame data for each move, including startup, active frames, recovery, and frame advantage/disadvantage.

**Combo Practice**: The game includes a list of recommended combos. You can select a combo and the game guides your inputs through slow-motion playback.

**Recording and Playback**: You can record a sequence of opponent actions and loop them to practice counter-strategies for specific matchups.

## 新手角色推荐 / Recommended Starter Characters

对新手来说，选择一个操作简单、连段直接的角色可以大幅降低入门难度。以下是推荐的五个新手友好角色：

For beginners, choosing a character with simple execution and straightforward combos can significantly reduce the learning curve. Here are five recommended beginner-friendly characters:

**保罗·菲尼克斯 / Paul Phoenix**：高伤害输出，连段简单，核心招式少但有效。毁灭者拳（Deathfist）和粉碎膝盖（Shredder Knee）是主要的伤害来源。

**洛 / Marshall Law**：虽然本作中有所削弱，但仍然是新人友好的选择。连段节奏流畅，拥有优秀的下段攻击和加帧技。

**风间准 / Jun Kazama**：本作回归角色，拥有自愈能力（Izuha Recovery）和特殊的取消技，容错率较高。

**州光 / Kunimitsu II**：机动性高，招式判定强，拥有隐身和设置技，适合喜欢灵活打法的玩家。

**阿丽莎 / Alisa Bosconovitch**：飞行能力让她在移动和连段中拥有独特优势，核心连段简单且伤害可观。

**Paul Phoenix**: High damage output, simple combos, few but effective core moves. Deathfist and Shredder Knee are the main damage sources.

**Marshall Law**: Though slightly nerfed in this entry, still a beginner-friendly choice. Smooth combo rhythm with excellent low attacks and frame advantage moves.

**Jun Kazama**: A returning character with self-healing ability (Izuha Recovery) and special cancels, offering higher margin for error.

**Kunimitsu II**: High mobility, strong move properties, stealth and setup tools. Suitable for players who prefer a flexible playstyle.

**Alisa Bosconovitch**: Flight capabilities give her unique advantages in movement and combos. Core combos are simple with respectable damage.

## 常见误区 / Common Mistakes

新手在初学铁拳时容易犯以下错误：过多使用下段攻击（下段被防后通常有较大破绽）、胡乱按键（缺乏目的性的进攻容易被确反）、不会拆投（面对投技为主的对手会陷入被动）、很少使用侧移（浪费了3D游戏的核心优势）、在不利帧数下继续进攻（导致被反击）。意识到这些问题是进步的第一步。

Common mistakes beginners make include: overusing low attacks (which leave you exposed when blocked), random button pressing (unintentional offense is easily punished), failing to break throws (leaving you passive against throw-heavy opponents), rarely sidestepping (wasting the core advantage of 3D gameplay), and continuing offense at frame disadvantage (leading to counter hits). Recognizing these issues is the first step toward improvement.
