# 角色详解 / Character Guide

《街霸6》发售后拥有22名可玩角色（含DLC），每个角色都有独特的玩法风格和战术设计。本页将逐一介绍每位角色的特点、难度评级、优势和劣势。

SF6 launched with 22 playable characters (including DLC), each with unique playstyles and tactical designs. This page covers every character's traits, difficulty ratings, strengths and weaknesses.

## 角色难度评级 / Character Difficulty Ratings

| 评级 / Rating | 含义 / Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1星 / 1 Star | 新手友好，连段简单，适合初学者 Beginner-friendly, simple combos, suitable for newcomers |
| 2星 / 2 Stars | 有一定复杂度，需要基础游戏理解 Moderate complexity, requires basic game understanding |
| 3星 / 3 Stars | 技术要求较高，需要熟练执行 Advanced execution required, needs practiced input precision |

## 基础角色 / Base Roster

### Ryu（隆） - 难度 / Difficulty: 1星

传统波动拳（Hadouken）、升龙拳（Shoryuken）和龙卷旋风腿（Tatsumaki）构成经典三连。隆是本作最均衡的角色之一，拥有优秀的普通技和可靠的伤害输出。他的新必杀技Hashogeki（波掌击）提供额外的压制和连段选项。优势在于全面的招式组合和稳定的伤害。劣势是没有特别突出的单一强项。

Ryu's classic trifecta of Hadouken, Shoryuken and Tatsumaki forms his core kit. One of the most balanced characters with excellent normals and reliable damage. His new move Hashogeki adds extra pressure and combo options. Strengths: well-rounded tools and consistent damage. Weaknesses: lacks an overwhelmingly strong specialty.

### Ken（肯） - 难度 / Difficulty: 2星

相比隆，肯更强调压制和机动性。他的龙卷脚（Tatsumaki）性能优异，加上新的Jinrai Kick（迅雷脚）系列派生，让肯在中近距离拥有极强的压制力。连段伤害高且搬运距离远。优势是强大的压制能力和高机动性。劣势是连段操作相对复杂。

Ken emphasizes pressure and mobility over Ryu's all-around style. His Tatsumaki has excellent properties, and the new Jinrai Kick series gives him oppressive mid-range pressure. High combo damage with great corner carry. Strengths: powerful offense and high mobility. Weaknesses: more complex combo execution.

### Chun-Li（春丽） - 难度 / 3星

系列最高机动性角色之一，拥有优秀的普通技和特殊的蓄力型必杀技系统。她的气功拳（Kikoken）和百裂脚（Hyakuretsukyaku）是核心压制工具。Serenity Stream（流水步）系统提供多样的派生选项。优势是极强的地面控制和压制能力。劣势是需要精确的蓄力输入和对局经验。

One of the most mobile characters with excellent normals and a unique charge-based special system. Kikoken and Hyakuretsukyaku are core pressure tools. Serenity Stream stance offers diverse follow-up options. Strengths: superb ground control and pressure. Weaknesses: requires precise charge inputs and matchup experience.

### Jamie（杰米） - 难度 / 3星

Jamie通过饮酒提升酒量等级，每级解锁新的必杀技和招式性能强化。满级（4级）的Jamie拥有最强的招式组合。但他的起手伤害较低，需要主动争取饮酒机会。优势在于满级后的全面强度。劣势是前期弱势，需要时间发育。

Jamie levels up his drink gauge by consuming alcohol, unlocking new specials and enhanced properties at each level. At max level (4) he has the strongest toolset. However, his base damage is low and he must actively earn drinking opportunities. Strengths: overwhelming power at full drink level. Weaknesses: weak early game, needs time to ramp up.

### Manon（曼侬） - 难度 / 2星

柔道风格角色，通过投技和特定必杀技获得奖牌（Medal）级别，每级提升投技伤害。中距离的牵制技和指令投是她的核心。优势是逐渐增长的投技伤害和强制猜拳压力。劣势是对抗善于拆投的对手时较为吃力。

Judo-style fighter who gains Medal levels through throws and specific specials, increasing throw damage per level. Mid-range pokes and command grabs are her core tools. Strengths: scaling throw damage and forced mix-up pressure. Weaknesses: struggles against opponents who tech throws consistently.

### Marisa（玛丽莎） - 难度 / 1星

高伤害重型角色，拥有装甲技和强大的近身压制。她的角斗士风格让她可以使用蓄力拳和肩撞来突破对手防御。优势是极高的单发伤害和压迫感。劣势是机动性较差，容易被放风筝。

High-damage powerhouse with armored moves and strong close-range pressure. Her gladiator style uses charged punches and shoulder tackles to break through defenses. Strengths: massive single-hit damage and intimidation factor. Weaknesses: poor mobility, vulnerable to keep-away.

### JP（杰必） - 难度 / 3星

远程压制型角色，使用空间扭曲能力进行中远距离控制。他的陷阱和投射物可以封锁对手的移动路线。优势是强大的区域控制和反压制能力。劣势是近身战斗能力较弱，需要保持距离。

Zone-control character using spatial distortion abilities for mid-to-long range control. His traps and projectiles restrict opponent movement. Strengths: exceptional area control and anti-pressure tools. Weaknesses: weak close-range game, must maintain distance.

### Juri（朱莉） - 难度 / 2星

拥有风破槽（Fuhajin）系统的压制型角色。可以通过特殊技存储风破强化，消耗强化槽可以增强必杀技性能。优势是灵活的进攻方式和强大的连段伤害。劣势是需要管理资源槽，操作量较大。

Pressure character with the Fuhajin stock system. Stores Fuha charges through special moves, consuming them to enhance special properties. Strengths: flexible offense and strong combo damage. Weaknesses: resource management required, high APM.

### Luke（卢克） - 难度 / 1星

全能型角色，拥有强力且易用的必杀技。他的连段结构直接，伤害可观，非常适合新手入门。优势是招式全面且易上手。劣势是缺乏特别突出的独特机制。

All-around character with powerful, easy-to-use specials. His combo structure is straightforward with good damage, ideal for beginners. Strengths: well-rounded and easy to pick up. Weaknesses: lacks a standout unique mechanic.

### Kimberly（金柏莉） - 难度 / 2星

高速机动型角色，使用忍者技法和喷雾罐进行压制。她可以在墙壁上行走和使用隐身接近。优势是优秀的机动性和出奇不意的进攻方式。劣势是防御能力较弱。

High-speed mobile character using ninja techniques and spray cans for pressure. She can walk on walls and use invisibility to approach. Strengths: excellent mobility and unpredictable offense. Weaknesses: weak defensive tools.

### Lily（莉莉） - 难度 / 2星

使用巨棍的土著风格战士，通过蓄力获得旋风充能（Wind Stock），强化必杀技性能。优势是强化后强大的压制能力。劣势是蓄力过程比较缓慢，容易被对手打断。

Indigenous warrior wielding a giant club, gaining Wind Stocks through charge inputs to enhance specials. Strengths: powerful pressure with stocked wind. Weaknesses: slow charging process vulnerable to interruption.

### Dhalsim（达尔西姆） - 难度 / 3星

瑜伽战士，拥有全游戏最长的普通技攻击范围。可以传送和飞行，适合使用放风筝战术和区域控制。优势是极限的距离控制和独特的移动方式。劣势是近身战极其脆弱。

Yoga warrior with the longest normal attack range in the game. Can teleport and float, excelling at keep-away and area control. Strengths: extreme range control and unique movement. Weaknesses: extremely fragile up close.

### Blanka（布兰卡） - 难度 / 2星

野性战士，拥有电击体术和滚动攻击。他的Blanka-chan道具和多样的突进技让对手难以预测。优势是混乱的进攻节奏和强力突进。劣势是对经验丰富的玩家容易被看穿。

Wild fighter with electric body moves and rolling attacks. His Blanka-chan doll and diverse rush moves keep opponents guessing. Strengths: chaotic offense and strong rush-down. Weaknesses: predictable against experienced players.

### Honda（本田） - 难度 / 1星

相扑力士，强力突进技百裂张手（Hyakkishu）和超级头槌（Sumo Headbutt）是他的核心。操作简单，伤害高，适合新手。优势是简单暴力的进攻手段。劣势是移动慢，容易被放风筝。

Sumo wrestler whose core tools are the Hundred Hand Slap and Sumo Headbutt. Simple execution with high damage, great for beginners. Strengths: straightforward powerful offense. Weaknesses: slow movement, vulnerable to keep-away.

### Guile（古烈） - 难度 / 2星

蓄力型角色之王，音速手刀（Sonic Boom）和脚刀（Flash Kick）构成经典防守反击体系。优势是顶级的防守压制力。劣势是需要精确的蓄力操作和耐心。

King of charge characters, built around Sonic Boom and Flash Kick for classic defense-reversal gameplay. Strengths: top-tier defensive pressure. Weaknesses: requires precise charge timing and patience.

## DLC角色 / DLC Characters

### Rashid（拉希德） - 难度 / 2星

使用龙卷风进行高速战斗的阿拉伯战士。他的Ysaar（龙卷风生成器）可以产生旋风，改变战场地形。优势是极高的机动性和版边压制能力。

Arabian fighter using whirlwinds for high-speed combat. His Ysaar creates tornadoes that alter battlefield geometry. Strengths: exceptional mobility and corner pressure.

### A.K.I.（阿鬼） - 难度 / 3星

使用毒素进行战斗的阴险角色。她的毒属性攻击可以造成持续伤害。优势是独特的持续伤害机制和陷阱压制。劣势是需要精确的操作管理毒素计量槽。

Sinister fighter using poison in combat. Her toxin-based attacks deal continuous damage over time. Strengths: unique damage-over-time mechanic and trap pressure. Weaknesses: requires precise management of poison gauge.

### Ed（艾德） - 难度 / 2星

使用精神力量（Psycho Power）进行拳击格斗。他拥有独特的按键蓄力系统，可以强化必杀技。优势是中距离压制和灵活的出招方式。

Psycho Power-empowered boxer with a unique button-charge system that enhances specials. Strengths: mid-range pressure and flexible move execution.

### Akuma（豪鬼） - 难度 / 3星

系列最著名的隐藏角色之一。拥有最高的伤害输出和最快的移动速度，但生命值是全角色最低。优势是极限伤害和高机动性。劣势是容错率极低。

One of the series' most famous hidden characters. Highest damage output and fastest movement speed, but lowest health in the roster. Strengths: extreme damage and high mobility. Weaknesses: paper-thin defense, minimal room for error.

### M. Bison（维加） - 难度 / 2星

影罗组织的独裁者，使用精神力量进行攻击。重新设计的招式组更加灵活，保留了标志性的头槌和剪刀脚。优势是强大的压制力和高伤害。

Dictator of Shadaloo, fighting with Psycho Power. Redesigned moveset with improved flexibility while retaining his signature Head Press and Scissor Kicks. Strengths: oppressive offense and high damage.

### Terry Bogard（特瑞） - 难度 / 2星

来自饿狼传说系列的客串角色。使用南镇风格的街头格斗技巧。能量波（Power Wave）、燃烧指节（Burn Knuckle）和升龙摔（Rising Tackle）等标志性招式全部保留。优势是全距离适应性强。

Guest character from the Fatal Fury series. Uses South Town street fighting techniques. Signature moves like Power Wave, Burn Knuckle and Rising Tackle are all preserved. Strengths: adaptable at all ranges.

### Mai Shiranui（不知火舞） - 难度 / 2星

同样来自饿狼传说系列的客串角色。以扇子术和火焰忍术闻名。除了经典的超必杀忍蜂外，还拥有优秀的空中控制和牵制能力。

Also a guest from Fatal Fury. Famous for her fan techniques and fire ninjutsu. Beyond the classic Super Ninja Bee, she excels at aerial control and poking.

## 角色选择原则 / Character Selection Principles

选择主力角色时需要考虑以下几点：

When choosing a main character, consider:

| 因素 / Factor | 说明 / Description |
|---|---|
| 玩法偏好 / Playstyle | 压制型、防守型、平衡型、技术型 Pressure, defensive, balanced or technical |
| 操作难度 / Execution | 投入练习时间与回报比例 Time investment vs reward ratio |
| 对局适应性 / Matchups | 角色对局优劣势的广度 Breadth of favorable and unfavorable matchups |
| 角色喜爱度 / Enjoyment | 是否喜欢角色的设计和手感 Personal connection to character design and feel |

选择你最喜欢的角色，享受格斗的乐趣。

Choose the character you enjoy most and have fun fighting.
