# 新手入门 / Beginner Guide

欢迎来到《街霸6》！本页将为新手玩家介绍最基础的入门知识，帮助你快速上手这款游戏。无论你是第一次接触格斗游戏，还是从系列前作回归，这里都有你需要了解的内容。

Welcome to Street Fighter 6. This page covers the fundamentals to help you get started quickly. Whether you are a complete beginner or returning from previous titles, everything you need is right here.

## 控制方式 / Control Schemes

《街霸6》提供三种控制方式，玩家可以在选项菜单中随时切换。

SF6 offers three control schemes that can be switched at any time from the options menu.

### 现代操作 / Modern Controls

现代操作（Modern）是最适合新手的控制模式。它将原来的六键缩减为四键：轻攻击、中攻击、重攻击和必杀技键。按住辅助键并配合方向键可以一键使出必杀技（Special Moves）和超必杀技（Super Arts）。自动连段（Auto Combos）通过连续按攻击键即可触发，非常适合初学者体验连招快感。代价是部分普通技和必杀技无法使用，以及伤害略有降低（约20%）。

Modern Controls are the most beginner-friendly scheme. It reduces six buttons to four: Light Attack, Medium Attack, Heavy Attack and Special Move. Holding the Assist button with directional inputs executes special moves and super arts with one button. Auto Combos trigger by repeatedly pressing the attack button, great for beginners to experience combo satisfaction. The trade-off is reduced normal move options and approximately 20% damage reduction.

### 经典操作 / Classic Controls

经典操作（Classic）是系列传统的六键布局：轻拳、中拳、重拳、轻脚、中脚、重脚。必杀技通过特定的方向指令加攻击键输入（例如波动拳为-236-+拳）。经典操作拥有最完整的角色性能、最高的伤害输出和最大的操作自由度，是高水平玩家的首选。

Classic Controls use the traditional six-button layout: Light Punch, Medium Punch, Heavy Punch, Light Kick, Medium Kick, Heavy Kick. Special moves require traditional motion inputs (e.g. Hadouken is 236 + Punch). Classic offers complete character kit, highest damage output and maximum freedom, making it the choice for competitive players.

### 动态操作 / Dynamic Controls

动态操作（Dynamic）是专为休闲和派对场合设计的模式。系统会根据情况自动执行攻击、防御和必杀技，连按攻击键即可触发华丽的连段。此模式仅限离线使用，无法在排位赛中选择。

Dynamic Controls are designed for casual and party play. The system automatically handles attacks, blocking and special moves based on the situation. Repeatedly pressing the attack button triggers flashy combos. This mode is offline-only and cannot be used in ranked matches.

## Drive系统入门 / Drive System Introduction

Drive System是《街霸6》的核心机制，由六格Drive Gauge（驱动槽）驱动。驱动槽会自动缓慢回复，也可以通过特定行动回复更多。

The Drive System is the core mechanic of SF6, powered by six stocks of the Drive Gauge. The gauge recovers slowly over time, with additional recovery from specific actions.

| 技能 / Skill | 消耗 / Cost | 说明 / Description |
|---|---|---|
| Drive Impact（驱动冲击） | 1格 1 stock | 重攻击带霸体和护甲，命中后触发壁弹 Wall bounce with armor, triggers wall splat on hit |
| Drive Rush（驱动突进） | 1格（取消时3格） 1 stock (3 from cancel) | 高速接近对手，取消普通技硬直 Fast approach, cancels normal move recovery |
| Drive Parry（驱动格挡） | 持续消耗 Continuous | 格挡打击系攻击，完美格挡可反击 Blocks strike attacks, perfect parry enables counter |
| Drive Reversal（驱动反击） | 2格 2 stocks | 防御中的反击技，消耗2格槽 Reversal attack from blockstun, costs 2 stocks |
| Overdrive（超驱动） | 2格 2 stocks | 强化版必杀技，性能提升 Enhanced special move with improved properties |

### Drive Impact（驱动冲击）

DI是游戏中最重要的压制和反压制工具之一。它具有霸体效果，可以承受一次打击（但会被投技和超必杀技破解）。命中对手后造成壁弹（Wall Splat），可以进行追击。如果DI被对手格挡，对手可以反击。在排位赛中，DI的使用时机会在心理层面形成大量博弈空间。

DI is one of the most important offensive and defensive tools. It has armor that absorbs one hit (but loses to throws and super arts). On hit it causes wall splat for follow-ups. If blocked, the opponent can punish. In ranked play, DI timing creates significant mind games.

### Drive Rush（驱动突进）

Drive Rush是连段和立回中的核心工具。从普通技取消发动的DR消耗3格槽，可以让原本无法连上的后续攻击形成连段。在立回中单独发动的DR消耗1格槽，用于快速接近对手并施加压力。

Drive Rush is central to combos and neutral. Cancelling a normal into DR costs 3 stocks, enabling follow-up links that would otherwise fail. Raw DR from neutral costs 1 stock and serves as a fast approach tool to apply pressure.

### Drive Parry（驱动格挡）

持续按住驱动格挡键会消耗驱动槽。成功格挡打击后不会减少驱动槽。如果在攻击命中前2帧内进行格挡，会触发完美格挡（Perfect Parry），大幅击退对手并为你创造确反机会。

Holding Drive Parry continuously drains the gauge. Successfully parrying a strike costs no gauge. If performed within 2 frames before the attack connects, it becomes a Perfect Parry, pushing the opponent back significantly and granting a punish opportunity.

### Drive Reversal（驱动反击）

在被压制时，消耗2格驱动槽可以发动驱动反击，用带有霸体的攻击弹开对手并重置双方距离。这是被压在角落时的重要逃脱手段。

When under pressure, spending 2 stocks on Drive Reversal executes an armored attack that pushes the opponent away and resets distance. This is crucial for escaping corner pressure.

### Overdrive（超驱动）

消耗2格槽可以使用强化版必杀技。超驱动必杀技通常具有更多的打击次数、更高的伤害、更好的帧数数据或额外的追击性能。

Spending 2 stocks activates enhanced special moves. Overdrive specials typically have more hits, higher damage, improved frame data or additional follow-up properties.

## 训练模式建议 / Training Mode Tips

训练模式（Training Mode）是提升水平的最佳场所。以下是一些实用的训练建议：

Training Mode is the best place to improve your skills. Here are some practical tips:

| 练习项目 / Practice Item | 目的 / Purpose |
|---|---|
| 必杀技指令输入 Special Inputs | 熟练掌握所有必杀技的指令输入，做到肌肉记忆 Master motion inputs until muscle memory takes over |
| 基础连段 Basic Combos | 从简单的3-4下连段开始，逐步增加难度 Start with simple 3-4 hit combos, gradually increase difficulty |
| 确反练习 Punish Practice | 设置训练AI使用特定招式，练习确反 Set training dummy to use specific moves, practice punishing |
| 防驱反练习 Anti-DI Practice | 练习确认对手DI并进行反击 Practice reacting to and countering opponent DI |
| 投拆练习 Throw Tech | 练习在对手近身投时拆投 Practice teching throws when opponent is close |

## 角色选择建议 / Character Selection Advice

对于新手玩家，推荐从以下角色开始：

For new players, the following characters are recommended:

| 角色 / Character | 推荐理由 / Reason |
|---|---|
| Ryu（隆） | 全能型角色，基础招式扎实，适合学习游戏基础 All-around character with solid tools, great for learning fundamentals |
| Luke（卢克） | 强力必杀技和简单的连段，现代操作下极易上手 Strong specials and simple combos, very easy with Modern controls |
| Juri（朱莉） | 灵活的压制型角色，风破槽机制提供额外战术选择 Flexible pressure character with Fuha stock mechanic for extra tactics |
| Marisa（玛丽莎） | 高伤害、简单操作的重型角色，一招一式伤害可观 High damage, simple execution heavy-hitter with massive damage per hit |
| Lily（莉莉） | 旋风蓄力机制提供强力压制，操作难度较低 Wind stock mechanic provides strong pressure, low execution difficulty |

无论选择哪个角色，最重要的是享受游戏过程。格斗游戏的进步需要时间和耐心，不要因为暂时的失败而气馁。

No matter which character you choose, the most important thing is to enjoy the game. Progress in fighting games takes time and patience. Do not get discouraged by temporary setbacks.
