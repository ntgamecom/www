## 开局选择 / Starting Choice

歧路旅人2开局需要选择**主角**——选定后该角色将全程在队伍中直到通关其终章。8个角色各有独特的开场和地图指令。选谁不影响通关能力——但影响游戏体验。推荐第一次玩的玩家选择剑士或学者——操作简单、路线清晰 / OT2 requires choosing a **starting character** — that character stays in party permanently until their final chapter cleared. 8 characters each have unique starts and path actions. Choice doesn't affect clearability — but affects experience. First-time players should choose Warrior or Scholar — simple mechanics, clear route.

### 地图指令系统 / Path Action System

每个角色有两个地图指令——一个白天用、一个晚上用。指令分为"绅士"型(不降低好感度)和"野蛮"型(降低好感度但效果更强)。**剑士**: 比试(战斗胜利获得物品/情报)和决斗。**学者**: 洞察(获取情报——不消耗)和探求。**舞娘**: 魅惑(带NPC同行)和诱惑。**盗贼**: 偷窃(偷取物品——可能失败)和抢夺。**猎人**: 引导(带野兽同行)和唆使。**商人**: 收购(用钱买NPC物品)和雇用。**药师**: 调合(制作药品)和毒杀。**牧师**: 引导(带人同行)和驱逐。推荐经常使用绅士型指令——避免好感和名声降低 / Each character has two path actions — one day, one night. Divided into "Noble" (no affinity loss) and "Rogue" (affinity loss but stronger effects). Use Noble actions regularly to avoid reputation loss.

### 推荐开局路径 / Recommended Route

**新手推荐选剑士开局**——然后直奔舞娘(第一章在港口镇Canalbrine)→学者(第一章在学院都市New Delsta)。这样前三人的武器覆盖: 剑、枪、短剑、斧、弓、杖——属性覆盖: 火、冰、雷、风、光。后续再收牧师→盗贼→猎人→商人→药师。推荐第一章全部收完再开始第二章——这样队伍完整、等级均衡。**具体路线**: 剑士(库国Kozuchi)→舞娘(Canalbrine)→学者(New Delsta)→盗贼(Toto'haha)→猎人(Nameless Village附近)→商人(Oresrush)→药师(Cropdale)→牧师(Flameschurch)。结束后在任一城镇整备——进入第二章 / **Newbie recommend Warrior start** — rush to Dancer (Ch.1 Canalbrine) → Scholar (Ch.1 New Delsta). First three cover: Sword, Lance, Dagger, Axe, Bow, Staff — Fire, Ice, Lightning, Wind, Light elements. Complete all Ch.1 before Ch.2. **Route**: Warrior (Kozuchi) → Dancer (Canalbrine) → Scholar (New Delsta) → Thief (Toto'haha) → Hunter (near Nameless Village) → Merchant (Oresrush) → Apothecary (Cropdale) → Cleric (Flameschurch). Then gear up for Chapter 2.

### 章节流程建议 / Chapter Progression Tips

每个角色有4章剧情——总共32章主线——约60-80小时通关。**建议按等级顺序推进**——不要只推一个角色到4章——会严重等级不平衡。推荐所有角色同步推进: 8个第二章→8个第三章→8个第四章。每个新章节开始前——先去探索新地区的迷宫和野外——收集宝箱装备——这些装备通常比商店货好30-50%。如果遇到卡关——换个角色推进或者去刷级 / Each character has 4 chapters — total 32 main story chapters — ~60-80 hours. **Balance levels**: don't push one character to Ch.4 while others are Ch.1 — severe imbalance. Recommended: all Ch.2 → all Ch.3 → all Ch.4. Before each new chapter — explore new area dungeons and fields — collect chest gear — usually 30-50% better than shop gear. If stuck — switch characters or grind levels.

## 地图探索 / World Map

世界由多个大陆组成——通过步行或船到达。每个地区有城镇、地下城和野外区域。**重要探索点**: 传送石碑(激活后快速旅行)、宝箱(装备/金币/材料)、NPC(可互动获取情报/物品)、高等级区域(后期再回来探索)。**推荐探索习惯**: 到新城镇先找传送石碑→和所有NPC对话→搜刮城市宝箱→出城时探完周边区域。**夜间探索**: 晚上的敌人比白天强——但掉落更好——一些特殊NPC只在晚上出现——建议交替探索 / The world has multiple continents — on foot or by ship. Each area has towns, dungeons, and overworld. **Key points**: Waystone (fast travel), Chests (gear/gold/mats), NPCs (info/items), High-level areas (return later). **Night Exploration**: Night enemies are stronger but drop better loot — some special NPCs only appear at night — alternate between day and night.

## 快速升級 / Fast Leveling

推荐刷等级地点: **第二章区域外的危险怪物**——先存档然后在高级区域边缘刷怪——风险高但经验多。**被动经验加成**: 商人的"经验加成"辅助技能(获得经验x2)——优先学到。**食物Buff**: 城镇餐厅吃经验加成餐(持续效果到战斗结束)。**推荐练级区**: 15-20级→海港外海滩(经验高于平均20%)。25-30级→流沙沙漠入口——敌人多、经验效率高。35-40级→冰龙洞入口——这里一只怪约2000+经验——适合爆发期升级。45-50级→终局迷宫——通关后刷最终迷宫入口的小怪——每场约5000经验。带上商人的"经验加成"被动——效率翻倍 / **Recommended spots**: Ch.2 zone edge monsters (risky but high XP). **Passive XP boost**: Merchant's "Hard Worker" (2x XP) — learn ASAP. **Food Buff**: Restaurant meals give XP boost (until battle ends). **Leveling zones**: Lv15-20 → Beach outside Harbor (20% above avg). Lv25-30 → Sandflow Desert entrance — dense enemies. Lv35-40 → Ice Dragon Cave entrance — ~2000 XP per kill. Lv45-50 → Final dungeon entrance after clear — ~5000 XP per kill. With Hard Worker passive — 2x efficiency.

## 装备与金钱 / Gear & Money

**赚钱方法**: 盗贼"偷窃"高价值NPC(前期来钱最快)——成功率受等级影响——高5级几乎100%。商人"收购"低价卖出高价——去矿石镇买进→首都卖出。完成支线任务——每个支线给3000-10000金币。**装备获取**: 城镇商店的装备每章节更新——记得更新武器和防具。隐藏宝箱中的装备比商店货好30-50%。**推荐优先购买的装备**: 速度靴(行动顺序核心)、MP回复戒指(法系续航)、物理/属性攻击饰品。更新装备的优先级: 武器>胸甲>饰品>头盔>手套>靴子 / **Money**: Thief "Steal" from valuable NPCs (fastest early) — success rate affected by level — 5 levels higher = ~100%. Merchant "Purchase" low/sell high — buy at Oresrush → sell in capital. Side quests — 3000-10000 gold each. **Gear**: Town shops update each chapter — upgrade weapons and armor. Hidden chest gear is 30-50% better than shop. **Buy priority**: Speed Boots (turn order), MP Regen Ring (mage sustain), ATK/MATK accessories. Upgrade priority: Weapon > Chest > Accessory > Helmet > Gloves > Boots.

## 副职业系统 / Secondary Job System

达到特定地区后可以解锁副职业——每个角色可以装配一个副职业(使用副职业的技能+武器)。**副职业推荐搭配**: 剑士+豪武匠(物理核爆)、学者+魔导师(魔法核爆)、商人+发明家(辅助)、猎人+豪武匠(破防)。**副职业解锁**: 需要前往特定祠堂——世界地图上标记了12个副职业祠堂位置。推荐在第二章期间解锁4-5个关键副职业——第三章解锁剩余部分 / After reaching specific areas, unlock secondary jobs — each character equips one secondary job (uses its skills + weapons). **Recommended**: Warrior+Armsmaster (phys nuke), Scholar+Arcanist (magic nuke), Merchant+Inventor (support), Hunter+Armsmaster (break). **Unlock**: Visit specific shrines — 12 shrines on world map. Unlock 4-5 key jobs during Ch.2 — remaining during Ch.3.

## 战斗基础 / Combat Basics

**破防(Break)系统**: 每个敌人有护盾点数(SHIELD)——用弱点属性/武器攻击可以降低护盾——降到0后敌人破防(倒地1回合+受到伤害x2)。**增幅(Boost)系统**: 每回合获得1BP——最多5BP——消耗BP可以增加技能效果(攻击次数/伤害/效果)。**潜力(Latent Power)**: 每个角色有独特的潜力技能——使用时需要消耗潜力槽(通过战斗积累)。潜力技能通常非常强力——如剑士的"单挑"(和敌人1v1)、学者的"全体探弱点"——在Boss战关键时刻使用 / **Break system**: Enemies have SHIELD points — hitting weaknesses reduces shield — 0 = break (1 turn down + 2x damage taken). **Boost system**: +1 BP/turn — max 5 BP — spend BP for enhanced skills (hits/damage/effect). **Latent Power**: Each character has unique latent skill — gauge fills in battle. Use at critical moments in boss fights.

## 常见问题解答 / FAQ

**Q: 8个角色都要练级吗？** A: 推荐平均练——但主练首发3人(你选的主角+最早收的2个)——其他人可以稍微落后——但终章时全员都需要40级以上 / Balance levels across all 8 — but your starting 3 will be slightly ahead. Everyone needs level 40+ by final chapter.
**Q: 什么时候解锁副职业？** A: 第二章期间可以解锁大部分副职业祠堂——推荐在第二章收齐8人后就开始探索地图解锁副职业 / During Chapter 2 — unlock most shrines after recruiting all 8 characters.
**Q: 隐藏Boss怎么打？** A: 推荐等级55+——装备全强化——带足回复道具——使用本指南推荐的物理+魔法核爆Build——需要大约30-40分钟的战斗 / Level 55+ — fully upgraded gear — full item stock — use Phys+Magic Nuke builds from this guide — ~30-40 min fight.
**Q: 钱不够用怎么办？** A: 盗贼偷高价值NPC(每个约500-2000金)、商人低价买高价卖(利润约30%)、竞技场刷钱(每次约10000金) / Thief steal (~500-2000 gold each), Merchant trade (30% profit margin), Arena farming (~10000 gold per run).
**Q: 装备升级材料哪里刷？** A: 强化石在各地的矿点采集——高级强化石在终局迷宫掉落——或者用盗贼偷特定NPC / Ores from mining spots across world — high-level stones from endgame dungeon — or Thief steal from specific NPCs.

**宝箱探索策略**: 每个城镇有约15-20个宝箱——野外区域有10-15个——地下城5-10个。紫箱(稀有宝箱)通常藏在意想不到的地方——比如瀑布后面、需要盗贼开锁的门后、或者需要特定地图指令才能到达的位置。建议每个新区域仔细搜刮——普通宝箱即使只给金币也值得——前期缺钱 / **Strategy**: Each town ~15-20 chests — field areas 10-15 — dungeons 5-10. Purple chests (rare) are in unexpected locations — behind waterfalls, Thief-locked doors, or requiring specific path actions. Thoroughly loot every area — even coin-only chests are worth it early game.

## 场景指令详解 / Path Action Details

每个角色的地图指令在不同场景下的效果不同 / Each character's path actions have different effects per scenario:

**剑士——比试/决斗**: 比试(白天)——和NPC战斗——赢了获得物品或情报。决斗(晚上)——和更强的NPC战斗——赢了获得更好的奖励。**注意**: 失败的比试/决斗会降低该地区的名声值——不要挑战远超自己等级的NPC / Warrior — Challenge/Duel: Challenge (day) — fight NPCs for items/info. Duel (night) — fight stronger NPCs for better rewards. **Note**: Failure reduces area reputation — don't challenge far higher-level NPCs.

**学者——洞察/探求**: 洞察(白天)——获取NPC情报——不消耗——安全但只能看基本信息。探求(晚上)——获取深层情报——可能触发隐藏任务线。**使用建议**: 每到一个新城镇——先用学者的洞察扫描所有NPC——找到隐藏任务线和商店折扣信息 / Scholar — Scrutiny/Probe: Scrutiny (day) — info from NPCs — safe but basic. Probe (night) — deep info — may trigger hidden quests. **Tip**: Scrutiny all NPCs in every new town — find hidden quests and shop discounts.

**舞娘——魅惑/诱惑**: 魅惑(白天)——NPC跟随——可以用NPC的技能协助战斗。诱惑(晚上)——更强的NPC跟随——技能更强。**强力用法**: 在Boss战前魅惑一个强力NPC——战斗中释放他的技能——有些NPC的技能相当于一个免费极技 / Dancer — Allure/Seduce: Allure (day) — NPC follows — use NPC's skills in battle. Seduce (night) — stronger NPC — better skills. **Power use**: Allure a strong NPC before boss fight — their skill is like a free Extreme.
