## 关于本游戏 / About the Game

《女神异闻录5 皇家版》（Persona 5 Royal）是由 Atlus（母公司 Sega）旗下 P-Studio 开发的日式角色扮演游戏（JRPG），融合了传统回合制战斗与校园生活模拟元素。本作是2016年《女神异闻录5》的增强版，于2019年在日本首发，2020年在全球发行，登陆 PlayStation 4，后续移植至 PC、Xbox、Nintendo Switch 等多个平台。

Persona 5 Royal is a Japanese role-playing game (JRPG) developed by Atlus\' P-Studio, blending traditional turn-based combat with school life simulation. It is an enhanced version of the original Persona 5 (2016), first released in Japan in 2019 and globally in 2020 on PS4, later ported to PC, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.

### 故事背景 / Story Setting

主角是一名因被诬告而转学至东京秀尽学园的高中生。在机缘巧合下，他觉醒了"Persona"（人格面具）的力量，与一群志同道合的伙伴组成了"心之怪盗团"（Phantom Thieves of Hearts）。他们潜入腐败大人们扭曲欲望所形成的"殿堂"（Palace），偷走"秘宝"（Treasure），使其悔改。故事探讨了正义、自由与社会束缚等深刻主题。

The protagonist is a high school student who transfers to Shujin Academy in Tokyo after being falsely accused of assault. Through a twist of fate, he awakens the power of Persona and forms the Phantom Thieves of Hearts with like-minded friends. They infiltrate Palaces — manifestations of corrupted adults\' distorted desires — steal the Treasure, and force a change of heart. The story explores justice, freedom, and societal constraints.

### 皇家版新增内容 / Royal-Exclusive Content

与原版《女神异闻录5》相比，皇家版增加了大量全新内容：

- **第三学期（Third Semester）**：全新的剧情章节，发生在原版结局之后，新增了丸喜拓人（Dr. Maruki）的殿堂
- **新角色**：芳泽霞（Kasumi Yoshizawa）和丸喜拓人成为可发展Coop关系的角色
- **新区域**：吉祥寺（Kichijoji）区域开放，包含台球、飞镖等新活动
- **新系统**：Showtime 合体攻击、 grappling hook 钩索移动、 Jazz Jin 增益系统
- **改进的战斗**：新增 Technical 技术攻击系统，Baton Pass 强化
- **人格面具**：新增多个 Persona，包括 伊邪那岐大神（Izanagi-no-Okami）等

Compared to the original P5, Royal adds a substantial amount of new content:
- Third Semester: A brand-new story chapter post-original ending, featuring Dr. Maruki\'s Palace
- New characters: Kasumi Yoshizawa and Dr. Maruki as Confidants
- New area: Kichijoji with billiards, darts, and more
- New systems: Showtime attacks, grappling hook traversal, Jazz Jin stat boosts
- Improved combat: Expanded Technical system, enhanced Baton Pass
- New Personas including Izanagi-no-Okami
