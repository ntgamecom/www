# 任务攻略 / Quest Guide

《极乐迪斯科》的任务系统围绕马丁内斯（Martinaise）的各种事件展开。主线是调查一具悬挂的尸体，但真正的故事远不止于此。本页面涵盖所有主要任务和关键支线。

The quest system in Disco Elysium revolves around various events in Martinaise. The main quest is investigating a hanged body, but the real story goes far beyond. This page covers all major quests and key side content.

## 主线案件：悬挂的尸体 / Main Case: The Hanged Body

### 案件概述 / Case Overview

一名男子被发现在褴褛飞旋旅馆后院的树上悬挂死亡。你需要在四天内查明真相：他是谁，为什么死，谁杀了他。

A man is found hanged in a tree behind the Whirling-in-Rags. You have four days to uncover the truth: who he was, why he died, and who killed him.

**关键信息 / Key Information**:
- 死者为一名雇佣兵 / The deceased is a mercenary
- 尸体上有多种伤痕 / The body bears multiple types of wounds
- 案件涉及当地政治势力 / The case involves local political factions
- 时间紧迫，但不必第一天就解决一切 / Time is tight, but you do not need to solve everything on day one

### 第一步：现场调查 / Step 1: Scene Investigation

**检视尸体 / Inspect the Body**:
- 检查尸体的总体状况（五感发达/Visual Calculus判定） / Check overall condition
- 检查衣物和物品（眼明手巧/Hand-Eye Coordination判定） / Check clothing and items
- 检查绳索和树的连接点（四肢发达/Interfacing判定） / Check the rope and tree attachment
- 检查地面痕迹（能工巧匠/Perception判定） / Check ground traces

**周边调查 / Surrounding Investigation**:
- 询问旅馆老板晚餐期间的异常情况 / Ask the hotel manager about dinner anomalies
- 与码头的工人交谈 / Talk to dock workers
- 搜索旅馆后的垃圾桶 / Search the trash bins behind the hotel
- 检查旅馆的冷藏库 / Check the hotel freezer

### 第二步：身份确认 / Step 2: Identify the Deceased

**主要线索 / Main Leads**:
- 尸体上的纹身可能揭示身份 / Tattoos on the body may reveal identity
- 死者携带的物品清单 / Inventory of items found on the body
- 当地是否有失踪人员报告 / Any missing persons reports locally
- 与工会和商人的往来记录 / Records of dealings with the union and merchants

**关键判定 / Key Check**: 五感发达（Visual Calculus）等级4+可以重建死者生前的最后几小时。Level 4+ Visual Calculus reconstructs the final hours.

### 第三步：嫌疑人 / Step 3: Suspects

**主要嫌疑人 / Primary Suspects**:
- 艾弗拉特·克莱尔（Evrart Claire）——工会领袖，操控着码头区域 / Union leader who controls the dock area
- 乔伊斯·梅西埃（Joyce Messier）——野松公司代表，资本势力 / Wild Pines representative, capitalist power
- 库诺·德·鲁伊特（Cuno de Ruyter）——问题少年，可能目击了什么 / Problem child who may have witnessed something
- 卢卡·鲁伊特（Luka Ruyter）——库诺的父亲，有暴力倾向 / Cuno father, has violent tendencies

**审问技巧 / Interrogation Tips**:
- 使用争强好胜（Authority）施压 / Use Authority to apply pressure
- 使用能说会道（Empathy）获取信任 / Use Empathy to build trust
- 使用故弄玄虚（Drama）识破谎言 / Use Drama to detect lies
- 注意证人之间的矛盾陈述 / Note contradictions between witness statements

## 重要支线任务 / Important Side Quests

### 教堂任务 / The Church Quest

发现废弃的教堂，揭开它隐藏的秘密。这个任务涉及游戏中最超自然的元素之一。

Discover the abandoned church and uncover its hidden secrets. This quest involves one of the most supernatural elements in the game.

**触发条件 / Trigger**: 与码头附近的工人对话，获得关于教堂的信息。Talk to workers near the dock for church info.

**关键步骤 / Key Steps**:
1. 找到教堂入口（需要眼明手巧/Hand-Eye Coordination判定） / Find the church entrance
2. 探索教堂内部，发现地下空间 / Explore the interior, discover the underground space
3. 解决教堂的谜题 / Solve the church puzzle
4. 与克拉拉（Klara）对话 / Talk to Klara
5. 体验教堂中最超现实的场景 / Experience the most surreal scene in the church

**奖励 / Rewards**: 独特思维、稀有物品、重要世界观信息。Unique thoughts, rare items, important lore information.

### 逃兵案件 / The Deserter Case

隐藏在军事封锁区的狙击手。这个任务与主线紧密相连，揭示了案件背后的更大阴谋。

A sniper hidden in the military封锁 zone. This quest is tightly connected to the main story and reveals a larger conspiracy behind the case.

**触发条件 / Trigger**: 通过主线推进到第三天左右，获得进入军事封锁区的方法。Progress the main story to around day three, gain access to the military zone.

**关键步骤 / Key Steps**:
1. 调查运河区的线索 / Investigate clues in the canal area
2. 找到进入军事封锁区的路径 / Find a way into the military zone
3. 与逃兵对话 / Talk to the deserter
4. 了解他的动机和背景 / Understand his motives and background
5. 做出影响结局的关键决定 / Make a key decision affecting the ending

**关键判定 / Key Checks**:
- 通情达理（Conceptualization）理解逃兵的意识形态 / Conceptualization to understand the deserter ideology
- 争强好胜（Authority）对抗他的敌意 / Authority to counter his hostility
- 内陆帝国（Inland Empire）感知隐藏的真相 / Inland Empire to perceive hidden truths

### 革命任务线 / Revolutionary Questline

涉及瑞瓦肖共产主义革命历史的系列任务。可选，但提供最深厚的剧情体验。

A series of quests involving the history of the Revachol communist revolution. Optional but provides the richest story experience.

**触发条件 / Trigger**: 与特定NPC讨论政治话题，解锁对话树。Discuss political topics with specific NPCs.

**关键NPC / Key NPCs**:
- 艾弗拉特·克莱尔（Evrart Claire）——工会的实用主义者 / The union pragmatist
- 逃兵（The Deserter）——纯粹的理想主义者 / The pure idealist
- 乔伊斯·梅西埃（Joyce Messier）——资本主义的代表 / The capitalist representative
- 测量女（Measuring Woman）——中立观察者 / The neutral observer

### 金的任务 / Kim Kitsuragi Side Content

改善与金的关系，了解他的过去。这会影响游戏结局。

Improve your relationship with Kim and learn about his past. This affects the ending.

**关键互动 / Key Interactions**:
- 在他的车上聊天 / Chat in his car
- 讨论他的背景和经历 / Discuss his background and experiences
- 在关键时刻支持他 / Support him at critical moments
- 尊重他的专业意见 / Respect his professional opinions

## 支线案件 / Side Cases

### 库诺的命运 / Cuno Destiny

库诺是马丁内斯的问题少年。你的选择会影响他的未来走向。

Cuno is the problem child of Martinaise. Your choices affect his future direction.

**可能结果 / Possible Outcomes**:
- 帮助他脱离父亲的控制 / Help him escape his father control
- 引导他走向正面发展 / Guide him toward positive development
- 忽视他，任由自生自灭 / Ignore him, leaving him to his fate

### 测量女的任务 / The Measurehead Quest

与码头区的种族主义看门人测量女（Measurehead）相关的任务。涉及种族理论和哲学辩论。

Quests related to the racist dock gatekeeper, Measurehead. Involves racial theory and philosophical debate.

**关键策略 / Key Strategy**: 使用标新立异（Rhetoric）和逻辑思维（Logic）进行辩论。Use Rhetoric and Logic to debate.

### 香烟任务 / Cigarette Quest

帮助当地居民处理香烟相关的纠纷。看似小任务，但涉及多个NPC的互动网络。

Help locals with a cigarette-related dispute. A small quest on the surface, but involves a network of NPC interactions.

## 任务策略 / Quest Strategy

### 优先顺序 / Priority Order

**第一天 / Day 1**: 主线调查 + 了解城镇布局 + 收集资金。Main investigation + learn town layout + gather funds.

**第二天 / Day 2**: 深入调查 + 开始支线 + 探索新区。Deep investigation + start side quests + explore new areas.

**第三天 / Day 3**: 推进逃兵线 + 完成主要支线 + 准备审判。Advance deserter line + complete major side content + prepare for tribunal.

**第四天 / Day 4**: 解决案件 + 面对结局。Solve the case + confront the ending.

### 任务追踪 / Quest Tracking

游戏没有传统任务日志，但你可以通过思维阁和思考来回顾当前进度。对话中也会自动提示下一步该做什么。

There is no traditional quest log, but you can review progress through the Thought Cabinet and recollection. Dialogue also automatically hints at next steps.

### 错过任务 / Missable Content

**第一天晚上必做 / Must-Do on Night 1**: 检查旅馆后院的所有区域。Check all areas of the hotel backyard.

**第二天上午必做 / Must-Do on Day 2 Morning**: 与码头工人建立关系。Build relationships with dock workers.

**第三天之前必做 / Must-Do Before Day 3**: 尽可能多的完成支线，因为第三天开始剧情会加速。Finish as many side quests as possible, as story accelerates on day three.
