# 技能系统 / Skills System

《极乐迪斯科》拥有24种独特技能，分属四大属性类别。每种技能代表哈里人格的一个侧面，会在对话中主动发声。理解每个技能是掌握游戏的关键。

Disco Elysium features 24 unique skills across four attribute categories. Each skill represents a facet of Harry personality and actively speaks during dialogue. Understanding each skill is key to mastering the game.

## 技能基础 / Skill Basics

### 技能等级 / Skill Levels

技能等级范围为1到6（通过装备可超过6）。等级越高，判定成功率越大，技能在对话中的发言频率也越高。

Skill levels range from 1 to 6 (can exceed 6 with equipment). Higher levels mean greater check success rates and more frequent skill commentary in dialogue.

**等级效果 / Level Effects**:
- 1-2: 初学者水平，偶尔发言 / Novice level, speaks occasionally
- 3-4: 熟练水平，经常提供有用建议 / Proficient, frequently offers useful advice
- 5-6: 大师水平，几乎每次对话都发言 / Master level, comments on almost every dialogue
- 7+: 封神水平（需装备加成），主导内心对话 / God tier (requires gear), dominates inner dialogue

### 技能判定类型 / Check Types

**白色判定 / White Checks**: 可重复尝试。失败后，提升技能等级或装备加属性装备后可再次尝试。Retriable. After failure, level up the skill or equip bonus gear to try again.

**红色判定 / Red Checks**: 只能尝试一次。失败后无法再次尝试，会永久失去该选项。One attempt only. Failure means permanently losing that option.

**被动判定 / Passive Checks**: 自动进行，基于当前技能等级。无需主动选择。Automatic, based on current skill levels. No active choice required.

## 智力类技能 / Intellect Skills

智力决定你的推理能力、知识储备和信息处理效率。高智力角色更容易发现线索，理解复杂概念。

Intellect governs your reasoning, knowledge, and information processing. High-Intellect characters find clues more easily and understand complex concepts.

### 逻辑思维 / Logic (1)

核心推理技能。帮助你连接线索，发现矛盾，做出合乎逻辑的结论。几乎每个案件都需要逻辑思维。

Core reasoning skill. Helps connect clues, spot contradictions, and reach logical conclusions. Almost every case requires Logic.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你能自动发现推理链中的漏洞。At high levels, you automatically spot holes in reasoning chains.

### 博学多闻 / Encyclopedia (2)

世界知识。提供关于瑞瓦肖历史、人物和地点的背景信息。对了解世界观至关重要。

World knowledge. Provides background info on Revachol history, people, and places. Essential for understanding the lore.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你会记住几乎所有读过的信息。At high levels, you remember almost everything you read.

### 标新立异 / Rhetoric (3)

辩论和修辞技巧。帮助你理解政治言论，参与意识形态辩论，识破话术。

Debate and rhetoric. Helps understand political speech, engage in ideological debates, and see through rhetoric.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你成为出色的辩手，能看穿任何语言操控。High level makes you an excellent debater who sees through any verbal manipulation.

### 故弄玄虚 / Drama (4)

直觉和表演。帮助你识破谎言，评估他人动机，以及在必要时演戏。

Intuition and performance. Helps detect lies, assess others motives, and act when needed.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你几乎能感知到空气中弥漫的谎言。At high levels, you can almost smell lies in the air.

### 通情达理 / Conceptualization (5)

抽象思维和艺术理解。帮助你理解抽象概念，欣赏艺术，创造隐喻。

Abstract thinking and art appreciation. Helps understand abstract concepts, appreciate art, and create metaphors.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你看到常人看不到的联系和美感。High level lets you see connections and beauty others miss.

### 五感发达 / Visual Calculus (6)

空间推理和视觉重建。帮助你重建犯罪现场，理解空间关系，破解视觉谜题。

Spatial reasoning and visual reconstruction. Helps reconstruct crime scenes, understand spatial relationships, and solve visual puzzles.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你可以在脑海中完美再现任何场景。At high levels, you can perfectly reconstruct any scene in your mind.

## 精神类技能 / Psyche Skills

精神决定你的情商、共情能力和人际交往技巧。高精神角色能更好地理解他人，建立关系。

Psyche governs your emotional intelligence, empathy, and social skills. High-Psyche characters understand others better and build relationships.

### 平心静气 / Volition (7)

意志力和心理健康。抵御压力，抵抗恐惧，保持冷静。这是最重要的精神类技能之一。

Willpower and mental health. Resist pressure, fear, and maintain calm. One of the most important Psyche skills.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你几乎不受任何心理打击的影响。High level makes you nearly immune to psychological blows.

### 内陆帝国 / Inland Empire (8)

直觉和想象力。与无形世界联系，感知超自然元素，产生奇思妙想。

Intuition and imagination. Connect with the intangible world, sense supernatural elements, generate wild ideas.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你能与领带对话，看到现实之外的景象。At high levels, you talk to your tie and see beyond reality.

### 能说会道 / Empathy (9)

情感共情。感知他人的情绪和需求，理解他们的痛苦和快乐。

Emotional empathy. Sense others emotions and needs, understand their pain and joy.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你几乎能读懂人心。At high levels, you can almost read minds.

### 争强好胜 / Authority (10)

威压和领导力。强迫他人服从，展现权威气质，在对抗中占据上风。

Intimidation and leadership. Force others to obey, project authority, dominate confrontations.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你的一句话就能让人胆寒。At high levels, a single word from you can terrify people.

### 同舟共济 / Suggestion (11)

说服和魅惑。用语言影响他人，让他们接受你的观点。

Persuasion and charm. Use words to influence others and make them accept your viewpoint.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你能说服几乎任何人做任何事。High level lets you convince almost anyone of anything.

### 疑神疑鬼 / Esprit de Corps (12)

警察直觉和同伴意识。感知警队动向，理解警察工作，与金的羁绊。

Police intuition and camaraderie. Sense police movements, understand police work, bond with Kim.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你能感受到瑞瓦肖警察局的脉搏。At high levels, you feel the pulse of the Revachol police force.

## 体格类技能 / Physique Skills

体格决定你的身体素质、耐力和生存能力。高体格角色能更好地应对身体挑战。

Physique governs your physical condition, endurance, and survival. High-Physique characters handle physical challenges better.

### 坚忍不拔 / Endurance (13)

耐久力和生命力。承受身体伤害，抵抗疾病和疲劳。

Stamina and vitality. Withstand physical damage, resist disease and fatigue.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你几乎刀枪不入（在精神意义上）。High level makes you nearly indestructible (in a spiritual sense).

### 钢筋铁骨 / Physical Instrument (14)

身体力量和控制。进行体力活动，破坏物品，展现肌肉力量。

Physical strength and control. Perform physical activities, break things, show muscle.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你就是人形攻城锤。At high levels, you are a human battering ram.

### 能吃是福 / Electrochemistry (15)

享乐主义和感官追求。抵抗诱惑，理解成瘾机制，追求快感。

Hedonism and sensory pursuit. Resist temptation, understand addiction, pursue pleasure.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你成为享乐大师，但也可能让你沉迷堕落。High level makes you a hedonism master, but may lead to addiction.

### 同花大顺 / Shivers (16)

环境和直觉感知。感受城市的脉动，与环境共鸣，获得超自然预感。

Environmental and intuitive perception. Feel the city pulse, resonate with the environment, get supernatural premonitions.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，瑞瓦肖本身会与你对话。At high levels, Revachol itself speaks to you.

### 循循善诱 / Half Light (17)

威胁感知和求生本能。对危险过度敏感，倾向先发制人的攻击。

Threat perception and survival instinct. Hyper-sensitive to danger, inclined toward pre-emptive aggression.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你的暴力本能在任何对话中呼之欲出。High level makes your violent instincts surface in every conversation.

### 从容自若 / Pain Threshold (18)

耐痛能力和坚韧。承受痛苦，忽视不适，在困境中保持专注。

Pain tolerance and toughness. Endure pain, ignore discomfort, stay focused under duress.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你无视任何身体上的不适。At high levels, you ignore all physical discomfort.

## 运动类技能 / Motorics Skills

运动决定你的敏捷性、反应速度和实操能力。高运动角色在动手任务中表现出色。

Motorics governs your agility, reaction speed, and practical ability. High-Motorics characters excel at hands-on tasks.

### 眼明手巧 / Hand-Eye Coordination (19)

精细操作和瞄准。进行精密操作，投掷物品，使用枪支。

Fine manipulation and aiming. Perform precise operations, throw items, use firearms.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你的双手如外科医生般精准。High level makes your hands as precise as a surgeon.

### 能工巧匠 / Perception (20)

感知细节。发现隐藏物品，注意到环境中的微小变化。

Detail perception. Find hidden items, notice tiny changes in the environment.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，没有东西能逃过你的眼睛。At high levels, nothing escapes your notice.

### 鬼祟玲珑 / Reaction Speed (21)

反应速度和敏捷。快速应对突发情况，先发制人。

Reaction speed and agility. Respond quickly to surprises, act before others.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你的反射神经远超常人。High level gives you reflexes far beyond normal.

### 标新立异 / Savoir Faire (22)

风格和风度。做事有格调，展现魅力，做出华丽动作。

Style and panache. Do things with flair, show charm, perform flashy moves.

**等级效果**: 高等级让一切都看起来毫不费力且有格调。High level makes everything look effortless and stylish.

### 四肢发达 / Interfacing (23)

机械和电子操作。使用工具，修理设备，操控电子系统。

Mechanical and electronic operation. Use tools, repair equipment, operate electronic systems.

**等级效果**: 高等级下，你天生就知道如何操作任何设备。At high levels, you instinctively know how to operate any device.

### 食髓知味 / Composure (24)

情绪控制和外在表现。保持扑克脸，隐藏真实感受，维持体面形象。

Emotional control and outward appearance. Keep a poker face, hide true feelings, maintain dignity.

**等级效果**: 高等级让你成为难以看透的神秘人物。High level makes you an inscrutable mystery.

## 技能搭配建议 / Skill Synergy Tips

**调查员配搭 / Investigator Combo**: 逻辑思维 + 五感发达 + 眼明手巧 — 最大化线索发现能力。Maximises clue detection.

**社交达人配搭 / Social Butterfly**: 能说会道 + 同舟共济 + 争强好胜 — 在对话中无往不利。Unstoppable in dialogue.

**生存专家配搭 / Survivalist**: 坚忍不拔 + 从容自若 + 钢筋铁骨 — 应对任何身体挑战。Handle any physical challenge.

**神秘学家配搭 / Mystic**: 内陆帝国 + 同花大顺 + 故弄玄虚 — 体验游戏最超自然的一面。Experience the most supernatural aspects.
