## 游戏概览 / Game Overview

《极乐迪斯科》是由ZA/UM工作室开发的一款叙事驱动型侦探角色扮演游戏，于2019年首次发布，2021年推出最终剪辑版（The Final Cut）。游戏摒弃了传统的战斗系统，完全依靠对话、技能判定和选择驱动剧情发展。

Disco Elysium is a narrative-driven detective role-playing game developed by ZA/UM, first released in 2019 with The Final Cut arriving in 2021. The game abandons traditional combat systems entirely, relying purely on dialogue, skill checks, and player choices to drive the story forward.

### 核心特色 / Core Features

**独特的无战斗系统 / Unique No-Combat System** — 游戏没有一刀一枪的战斗。所有冲突通过技能判定、对话选项和逻辑推理解决。你的武器是语言，你的盔甲是思想。The game contains no combat. Every conflict is resolved through skill checks, dialogue options, and logical reasoning. Your weapon is language, your armor is thought.

**24种技能的内心对话 / 24 Skills and the Inner Voice** — 每种技能代表哈里尔·杜博阿（Harry Du Bois）人格的一个侧面。技能会在对话中主动与你交谈，提供建议、警告或误导。Each skill represents a facet of Harrier Du Bois personality. Skills actively talk to you during dialogue, offering advice, warnings, or misdirection.

**思维阁系统 / Thought Cabinet System** — 通过内化特定思维来获得永久加成或改变游戏玩法。每个思维都是一次世界观的重塑。Internalise specific thoughts to gain permanent bonuses or alter gameplay mechanics. Each thought is a reshaping of your worldview.

**政治意识形态 / Political Ideologies** — 游戏包含四种政治倾向：共产主义、法西斯主义、自由主义和道德主义。你的选择会影响剧情走向和最终结局。Four political alignments: Communism, Fascism, Liberalism, and Moralist. Your choices affect story direction and the final ending.

### 最终剪辑版新增内容 / The Final Cut Additions

最终剪辑版（The Final Cut）增加了全程语音配音、全新的政治任务线以及更多区域和对话内容。所有版本的指南内容均涵盖最终剪辑版的全部特性。

The Final Cut adds full voice acting, new political quest lines, and additional areas and dialogue content. All versions of this guide cover every feature of The Final Cut.

## ZA/UM 工作室 / ZA/UM Studio

ZA/UM由爱沙尼亚作家罗伯特·库维茨（Robert Kurvitz）创立。工作室名称源于俄语短语「za um」——意为「超越心智」。ZA/UM的核心成员包括小说家、诗人和艺术家，而非传统游戏开发者。他们的背景赋予了《极乐迪斯科》独特的文学性和哲学深度。

ZA/UM was founded by Estonian writer Robert Kurvitz. The studio name comes from the Russian phrase za um — meaning beyond the mind. Core members include novelists, poets, and artists rather than traditional game developers. Their background gives Disco Elysium its unique literary quality and philosophical depth.

**其他作品 / Other Works**: 库维茨此前写了一部小说《神圣而可怕的空气》（Sacred and Terrible Air），设定在同一个极乐世界宇宙中。《极乐迪斯科》可以被视为这部小说世界的延续和扩展。Kurvitz previously wrote a novel called Sacred and Terrible Air, set in the same Elysium universe. Disco Elysium can be seen as a continuation and expansion of that world.

## 游戏成就与评价 / Accolades & Reception

《极乐迪斯科》获得了广泛赞誉，被誉为有史以来最伟大的角色扮演游戏之一。它在2019年和2020年斩获了众多奖项，包括BAFTA、The Game Awards最佳独立游戏等多项大奖。

Disco Elysium received widespread acclaim and is considered one of the greatest role-playing games of all time. It won numerous awards in 2019 and 2020, including BAFTA and The Game Awards Best Independent Game.
