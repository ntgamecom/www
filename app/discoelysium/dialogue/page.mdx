# 对话与检视 / Dialogue & Checks

《极乐迪斯科》的核心是对话。几乎游戏的全部内容——剧情推进、角色发展、世界探索——都通过对话完成。本页面详解对话系统、技能判定机制和思维阁的运用。

The core of Disco Elysium is dialogue. Almost everything in the game — story progression, character development, world exploration — happens through conversation. This page explains the dialogue system, skill check mechanics, and Thought Cabinet usage.

## 对话系统 / Dialogue System

### 对话基础 / Dialogue Basics

游戏中的对话采用分支树结构。你的选择会影响NPC的反应、可用信息、甚至整个剧情走向。

Dialogue in the game uses a branching tree structure. Your choices affect NPC reactions, available information, and even the entire story direction.

**对话选项类型 / Dialogue Option Types**:
- **普通对话** — 推进剧情的基础选项 / Basic options that advance the story
- **技能判定选项** — 需要特定技能等级才能成功 / Require specific skill levels to succeed
- **思维封印选项** — 需要内化特定思维才能解锁 / Require internalising a specific thought to unlock
- **意识形态选项** — 与四种政治倾向相关 / Related to the four political alignments
- **白色选项** — 可重复尝试的判定 / Retriable checks
- **红色选项** — 仅有一次机会的判定 / One-attempt-only checks

### 技能在对话中的角色 / Role of Skills in Dialogue

每种技能都像哈里脑海中的一个独立声音。它们会在对话中主动发言，提供建议、警告、评论或误导。

Each skill acts like an independent voice in Harry head. They actively speak during dialogue, offering advice, warnings, commentary, or misdirection.

**技能发言示例 / Examples of Skill Commentary**:
- 逻辑思维（Logic）: 指出对话中的逻辑矛盾 / Points out logical contradictions
- 能说会道（Empathy）: 告诉你对方此刻的真实感受 / Tells you what the other person is really feeling
- 内陆帝国（Inland Empire）: 提出超自然的疯狂理论 / Offers supernatural, crazy theories
- 循循善诱（Half Light）: 建议你立刻攻击 / Urges immediate violence
- 食髓知味（Composure）: 提醒你保持面部表情管理 / Reminds you to keep your composure

**技能冲突 / Skill Conflicts**: 不同技能可能给出完全相反的建议。你需要自行判断听从哪个声音。Different skills may give completely opposite advice. You must decide which voice to follow.

## 技能判定机制 / Skill Check Mechanics

### 判定公式 / Check Formula

技能判定基于以下公式：`技能等级 x 4 + 修饰值 + 随机值` vs `难度值`。

Skill checks use this formula: `Skill Level x 4 + Modifiers + Random Value` vs `Difficulty Value`.

**难度等级 / Difficulty Levels**:
- 简单（Easy）: 12
- 中等（Medium）: 14
- 困难（Hard）: 16
- 极难（Very Hard）: 18
- 传奇（Legendary）: 20

### 白色判定详解 / White Checks Explained

白色判定是游戏中最常见的判定类型。失败后不会永久消失——它们会在你的技能等级提升或装备加成增加后重新变为可用。

White checks are the most common type. They do not disappear permanently after failure — they become available again when your skill level increases or equipment bonuses improve.

**重新尝试策略 / Retry Strategy**:
- 提升对应技能1级并获得新装备 / Level up the relevant skill and get new gear
- 消耗思维点重置白色判定（某些思维提供此功能） / Spend thought points to reset white checks (certain thoughts provide this)
- 使用药物或酒精获得临时加成 / Use drugs or alcohol for temporary bonuses
- 装备提供加成的服装 / Equip clothing that provides bonuses

### 红色判定详解 / Red Checks Explained

红色判定具有高风险高回报的特点。一旦失败，该选项永久关闭。但成功通常会带来巨大的回报——独特信息、稀有物品或关键剧情推进。

Red checks are high-risk, high-reward. Once failed, the option closes forever. But success usually brings huge rewards — unique information, rare items, or key story progression.

**红色判定注意事项 / Red Check Tips**:
- 存档后再尝试 / Save before attempting
- 通过装备和药物最大化技能等级 / Maximise skill level with gear and drugs
- 某些红色判定的失败也会产出独特内容（失败也是体验的一部分） / Some red check failures also produce unique content (failure is part of the experience)

### 被动判定 / Passive Checks

被动判定在你的技能等级达到某个阈值时自动触发。它们不需要你主动选择——当你进入一个区域或遇到某个情境时，符合条件的技能会自动发言并提供信息。

Passive checks trigger automatically when your skill level reaches a certain threshold. No active choice needed — when you enter an area or encounter a situation, qualifying skills automatically speak up and provide information.

**被动判定示例 / Passive Check Examples**:
- 五感发达（Visual Calculus）等级3+时自动分析犯罪现场 / Automatically analyses crime scenes at level 3+
- 同花大顺（Shivers）等级2+时感受城市的低语 / Feels the city whispers at level 2+
- 博学多闻（Encyclopedia）等级2+时提供历史背景 / Provides historical context at level 2+

## 思维阁系统 / Thought Cabinet System

### 思维阁基础 / Thought Cabinet Basics

思维阁是哈里的大脑地图。你可以在这里查看已解锁和正在进行内化的思维。每个思维代表一种理念、创伤或世界观。

The Thought Cabinet is a map of Harry brain. Here you can view unlocked thoughts and those currently being internalised. Each thought represents an idea, trauma, or worldview.

**思维槽位 / Thought Slots**: 初始只有2个槽位，可通过升级精神属性增加。最多可拥有4个同时内化的思维。Start with 2 slots, can increase by levelling Psyche. Maximum of 4 simultaneous internalising thoughts.

### 获取思维 / Acquiring Thoughts

思维通过对话、阅读、经历特殊事件等方式解锁。当你触发某个思维时，它会出现在思维阁中供你选择是否内化。

Thoughts are unlocked through dialogue, reading, experiencing special events, etc. When you trigger a thought, it appears in the cabinet for you to choose whether to internalise.

### 内化过程 / Internalisation Process

内化一个思维需要消耗现实时间（游戏内），通常为30分钟到数小时不等。内化期间，该思维可能产生负面效果。内化完成后，获得永久加成。

Internalising a thought consumes in-game time, typically 30 minutes to several hours. During internalisation, the thought may have negative effects. After completion, permanent bonuses are gained.

**内化注意事项 / Internalisation Notes**:
- 内化期间无法取消 / Cannot be cancelled during internalisation
- 某些思维之间互斥 / Some thoughts are mutually exclusive
- 内化完成后可以遗忘以腾出槽位 / Can be forgotten after completion to free up slots

### 推荐初期思维 / Recommended Early Thoughts

**白人问号 / Wompty-Dompty Dom Centre**: 每次与NPC对话获得额外的经验值。前期最佳经验来源。Get extra XP per NPC conversation. Best early XP source.

**扳机效应 / Trigger Effect**: 提升能说会道（Empathy）和同舟共济（Suggestion）。对话流玩家的首选。Boosts Empathy and Suggestion. Top choice for dialogue-oriented players.

**麦克风的低语 / Microphone Whisper**: 降低逻辑思维（Logic）但提升标新立异（Rhetoric）。适合政治辩论爱好者。Reduces Logic but boosts Rhetoric. Great for political debate fans.

**无所不用其极 / The Extremely Advanced Race Theory**: 极强的政治思维，大幅改变对话选项。适合极端角色扮演。Powerful political thought that dramatically changes dialogue options. For extreme roleplay.

## 对话选择如何影响剧情 / How Choices Affect the Story

### 分支叙事 / Branching Narrative

你的每一个选择都会在某种程度上改变游戏进程。有些影响是即时的，有些则在数小时后才会显现。

Every choice you make changes the game to some degree. Some effects are immediate, others appear hours later.

**选择的影响范围 / Choice Impact Scope**:
- 局部影响：改变当前对话或场景 / Changes the current dialogue or scene
- 中期影响：影响某个任务或角色关系 / Affects a quest or character relationship
- 全局影响：改变结局走向 / Changes the ending direction

### 金·吉速的关系系统 / Kim Kitsuragi Relationship

金是游戏中最关键的角色之一。你的行为会影响他对你的信任和尊重程度。这最终会影响结局。

Kim is one of the most critical characters. Your actions affect his trust and respect level. This ultimately impacts the ending.

**影响金的因素 / Factors Affecting Kim**:
- 展示专业警察素养 / Show professional police conduct
- 不做出极端丢脸的行为 / Avoid extremely embarrassing behaviour
- 尊重他的建议和判断 / Respect his advice and judgment
- 成功完成重要判定 / Succeed on important checks

## 检视（Visual Inspection） / Visual Inspection

### 环境检视 / Environmental Inspection

通过点击环境中的高亮物体进行检视。每个可互动物体都有独特的描述文本，可能包含隐藏线索。

Click highlighted objects in the environment to inspect them. Each interactable object has unique description text that may contain hidden clues.

**检视技巧 / Inspection Tips**:
- 使用Tab键高亮所有可互动区域 / Use Tab to highlight all interactable areas
- 每个物体检视多次（不同时间可能有不同描述） / Inspect objects multiple times (descriptions may change with time)
- 穿着加成感知的服装可以发现更多细节 / Wearing Perception-boosting clothes reveals more details

### 人物检视 / Character Inspection

与NPC对话时，可以检视他们的外貌、衣着和举止，获得关于他们的额外信息。

During NPC conversations, you can inspect their appearance, clothing, and mannerisms for additional info.
