# 结局指南 / Endings Guide

《极乐迪斯科》有多种结局，取决于你在整个游戏中的选择。没有哪个结局是「真结局」——每个结局都是你在马丁内斯这四天旅程的自然产物。

Disco Elysium has multiple endings, determined by your choices throughout the game. No single ending is the true ending — each is the natural product of your four-day journey in Martinaise.

## 结局的基础 / Ending Fundamentals

### 决定结局的因素 / Factors That Determine the Ending

**核心变量 / Core Variables**:
- 案件解决程度 / Case resolution degree
- 与金·吉速的关系 / Relationship with Kim Kitsuragi
- 法庭审判的选择 / Choices during the tribunal
- 逃兵的处理方式 / How the deserter is handled
- 政治意识形态倾向 / Political ideology alignment
- 是否找到关键证据 / Whether key evidence was found
- 个人状态（成瘾、精神状态等）/ Personal state (addiction, mental status)

### 结局类型概览 / Ending Type Overview

游戏大致有以下几类结局走向：

The game roughly has these ending categories:

**极乐结局 / Happy Ending**: 解决案件，与金保持良好关系，为马丁内斯带来一些希望。Solve the case, maintain good relations with Kim, bring some hope to Martinaise.

**悲剧结局 / Tragic Ending**: 案件未解决，或解决了但付出巨大代价。金可能受伤或离开。Case unsolved, or solved at great cost. Kim may be injured or leave.

**虚无结局 / Nihilistic Ending**: 一切都无所谓。哈里陷入更深的自我毁灭。Nothing matters. Harry sinks deeper into self-destruction.

**革命结局 / Revolutionary Ending**: 意识形态决定一切。世界不需要改变。Ideology decides everything. The world needs no change.

## 关键节点：法庭审判 / The Tribunal

### 审判概述 / Tribunal Overview

法庭审判是游戏中最关键的事件之一，发生在第三天或第四天。这个事件决定了多个角色的命运。

The tribunal is one of the most critical events, occurring on day three or four. This event determines the fate of multiple characters.

**审判前准备 / Preparation Before Tribunal**:
- 收集尽可能多的证据 / Collect as much evidence as possible
- 提升与金的关系 / Build relationship with Kim
- 确保自己有足够的装备加成 / Ensure sufficient gear bonuses
- 存档！存档！存档！ / Save! Save! Save!

### 审判结果 / Tribunal Outcomes

**最佳结果 / Best Outcome**:
- 金不受伤 / Kim is not injured
- 案件主要嫌疑人被控制 / Main suspect is contained
- 库诺的命运改善 / Cuno fate improves
- 工会和野松公司达成某种协议 / Union and Wild Pines reach some agreement

**普通结果 / Regular Outcome**:
- 金轻微受伤 / Kim is slightly injured
- 案件解决但不完美 / Case solved but imperfectly
- 某些角色死亡 / Some characters die
- 马丁内斯维持现状 / Martinaise stays as is

**最差结果 / Worst Outcome**:
- 金严重受伤或离开 / Kim is severely injured or leaves
- 案件陷入僵局 / Case reaches a dead end
- 多个角色死亡 / Multiple characters die
- 马丁内斯陷入混乱 / Martinaise falls into chaos

### 审判中的关键选择 / Key Choices During Tribunal

**选择武器 / Choose Your Weapon**:
- 使用争强好胜（Authority）控制局面 / Use Authority to control the situation
- 使用故弄玄虚（Drama）智取 / Use Drama to outsmart
- 使用同舟共济（Suggestion）谈判 / Use Suggestion to negotiate
- 保持沉默，让事件自然发展 / Stay silent, let events unfold naturally

**保护金 / Protect Kim**:
- 在关键时刻挺身而出 / Step up at critical moments
- 听从他的建议 / Follow his advice
- 不要做出极端愚蠢的行为 / Avoid extremely foolish behaviour
- 成功通过关键判定 / Succeed on key checks

## 逃兵线的结局分支 / Deserter Ending Branches

### 对话逃兵 / Confronting the Deserter

逃兵是游戏中最复杂的反派角色。你与他的对话决定了多个结局分支。

The deserter is the most complex antagonist in the game. Your conversation with him determines multiple ending branches.

**理解逃兵 / Understanding the Deserter**:
- 他是一个被时代抛弃的共产主义者 / He is a communist abandoned by time
- 他的行动源于纯粹但扭曲的信念 / His actions stem from pure but twisted beliefs
- 他代表了革命理想在现实中的堕落 / He represents the fall of revolutionary ideals in reality
- 你的反应方式定义了你自己的立场 / Your reaction defines your own stance

**关键判定 / Key Checks**:
- 通情达理（Conceptualization）— 理解他的意识形态 / Understand his ideology
- 能说会道（Empathy）— 理解他的情感创伤 / Understand his emotional trauma
- 内陆帝国（Inland Empire）— 感知他更深层的动机 / Perceive his deeper motives
- 逻辑思维（Logic）— 推理他的行动逻辑 / Reason his logic of action

### 逃兵路线结局 / Deserter Route Endings

**和解结局 / Reconciliation Ending**: 理解逃兵的动机但不认同他的方法，将他绳之以法。Understand his motives without endorsing his methods, bring him to justice.

**共情结局 / Sympathy Ending**: 对逃兵产生同情，但必须接受他行为的后果。Feel sympathy for the deserter but accept the consequences of his actions.

**意识形态结局 / Ideology Ending**: 与逃兵的政治观点完全对齐或完全对立。完全认同或完全否定。Align completely with or against his political views.

**暴力结局 / Violence Ending**: 以暴力方式结束与逃兵的对抗。End the confrontation with the deserter violently.

## 金·吉速关系结局 / Kim Kitsuragi Relationship Endings

### 关系等级 / Relationship Levels

**最佳伙伴（最高好感度）/ Best Partner (Max Trust)**:
- 金尊重你，信任你的判断 / Kim respects and trusts your judgment
- 他在结局中支持你的决定 / He supports your decisions in the ending
- 他分享自己的背景故事 / He shares his backstory
- 你们完成了真正的伙伴合作 / You achieve true partnership

**普通同事（中等好感度）/ Regular Colleague (Medium Trust)**:
- 金履行警察职责，但保持距离 / Kim performs police duties but keeps distance
- 他对你的某些行为不认同 / He disapproves of some of your actions
- 他提供基本支持但不深入 / He provides basic support but no depth
- 关系功能性但不温暖 / Professional but not warm

**糟糕搭档（低好感度）/ Bad Partner (Low Trust)**:
- 金对你失望 / Kim is disappointed in you
- 他怀疑你的专业能力 / He doubts your competence
- 在关键时刻他可能不站在你这边 / He may not side with you at critical moments
- 结局关系破裂 / Relationship breaks down in ending

### 提升好感度的关键行为 / Key Actions to Build Trust

**加分行为 / Positive Actions**:
- 展示警察专业素养 / Show police professionalism
- 成功解决案件的关键环节 / Successfully solve key case elements
- 尊重金的意见 / Respect Kim opinions
- 保护他不受伤害 / Protect him from harm
- 在公共场合维护他的尊严 / Maintain his dignity in public

**减分行为 / Negative Actions**:
- 极端丢脸的公共行为 / Extremely embarrassing public behaviour
- 严重成瘾失控 / Severe addiction失控
- 侮辱或贬低他 / Insult or belittle him
- 让他在执法中陷入危险 / Put him in danger during police work
- 种族主义或极端政治言论 / Racist or extreme political remarks

## 梦境序列 / Dream Sequences

### 梦境概述 / Dream Overview

哈里每晚都会做不同的梦，反映他的心理状态和游戏中的经历。梦境是了解哈里过去的关键窗口。

Harry has different dreams each night, reflecting his psychological state and in-game experiences. Dreams are key windows into Harry past.

**第一天夜晚 / Night 1**: 关于失忆和困惑的梦。关于一个叫多拉（Dora）的女人。A dream about memory loss and confusion. About a woman named Dora.

**第二天夜晚 / Night 2**: 关于案件和工作的梦。反映你的调查进度。A dream about the case and work. Reflects your investigation progress.

**第三天夜晚 / Night 3**: 关于苍白和世界的梦。深化世界观揭示。A dream about the pale and the world. Deepens world revelation.

**第四天夜晚 / Night 4**（如果游戏持续到第四天）: 关于结局的预告。A preview of the ending.

### 多拉（Dora）/ The Dream Woman

多拉是哈利的前妻。你的梦境和记忆碎片拼凑出他们的关系故事。这个情节线是游戏中最感人、最核心的叙事之一。

Dora is Harry ex-wife. Your dreams and memory fragments piece together their relationship story. This storyline is one of the most touching and core narratives in the game.

**关于多拉的关键发现 / Key Discoveries about Dora**:
- 她是哈里的前妻 / She is Harry ex-wife
- 哈里因她的离开而崩溃 / Harry collapsed after she left
- 她曾试图帮助哈里，但最终放弃了 / She tried to help Harry but eventually gave up
- 哈里对这段关系的处理方式决定了结局中的个人成长 / How Harry handles this relationship determines personal growth in the ending

## 所有结局变体 / All Ending Variations

### 结局清单 / Ending List

**1. 完美破案结局 / Perfect Investigation Ending**
- 案件完全解决 / Case fully solved
- 金安全 / Kim safe
- 马丁内斯的未来有希望 / Hope for Martinaise future
- 哈里开始自我救赎 / Harry begins self-redemption

**2. 破案但伤亡结局 / Solved but Costly Ending**
- 案件解决 / Case solved
- 但有角色伤亡 / But with casualties
- 马丁内斯前景不明 / Uncertain future for Martinaise

**3. 未破案结局 / Unsolved Ending**
- 案件未解 / Case unsolved
- 凶手逍遥法外 / Killer free
- 哈里职业生涯结束 / Harry career over

**4. 彻底的失败结局 / Total Failure Ending**
- 案件未解 / Case unsolved
- 金离开或死亡 / Kim leaves or dies
- 哈里陷入更深的沉沦 / Harry sinks deeper

**5. 意识形态结局（4种）/ Ideology Endings (4 types)**
- 共产主义结局 / Communist ending
- 法西斯结局 / Fascist ending
- 自由主义结局 / Liberal ending
- 道德主义结局 / Moralist ending

### 结局后的世界 / The World After the Ending

无论结局如何，游戏都留下了一个开放式的结尾。马丁内斯的故事继续，瑞瓦肖的命运未定，哈里的人生还在继续。这就是《极乐迪斯科》的美——没有完美的答案，只有继续走下去的路。

Regardless of the ending, the game leaves an open conclusion. Martinaise story continues, Revachol fate is uncertain, Harry life goes on. This is the beauty of Disco Elysium — no perfect answers, only the road ahead.
