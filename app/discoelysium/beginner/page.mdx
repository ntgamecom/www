# 新手入门 / Beginner Guide

欢迎来到瑞瓦肖，警官。这是你的第一天——也可能在宿醉中醒来，什么都不记得。本页面将帮助你从零开始，顺利度过游戏初期。

Welcome to Revachol, officer. This is your first day — you may wake up hungover and remember nothing. This page will help you get started and survive the early game.

## 角色创建 / Character Creation

### 警察原型 / Police Archetypes

游戏开始时，你可以从四种预设原型中选择，或者自定义分配属性点。每种原型对应不同的玩法和对话风格。

At the start, you can choose from four preset archetypes or customise your stat allocation. Each archetype offers a different playstyle and dialogue flavour.

**思维派 / Thinker** — 智力和精神属性较高。适合喜欢推理、分析和文字游戏的玩家。High Intellect and Psyche. Ideal for players who enjoy deduction, analysis, and wordplay.

**感性派 / Sensitive** — 精神属性极高，充满共情力和直觉。适合注重情感和角色的玩家。Very high Psyche, full of empathy and intuition. Ideal for roleplay-focused players.

**体力派 / Physical** — 体格和运动属性较高。适合喜欢实际行动和身体对抗的玩家。High Physique and Motorics. Good for players who prefer physical action and confrontation.

**自定义 / Custom** — 自由分配12点属性点，选择签名技能。适合有明确流派想法的老玩家。Freely allocate 12 attribute points and choose a signature skill. For experienced players with a specific build in mind.

### 属性点分配 / Attribute Point Allocation

四种基础属性：智力（Intellect）、精神（Psyche）、体格（Physique）、运动（Motorics）。每种初始为1，总共有12点可分配。每项属性的上限为6。

Four base attributes: Intellect, Psyche, Physique, Motorics. Each starts at 1, with 12 points total to distribute. Maximum per attribute is 6.

**推荐新手分配 / Recommended Beginner Allocation**: 智力4 精神4 体格2 运动2 (4/4/2/2)。这种均衡分配让你在对话和探索中都有不错的表现。This balanced spread gives you solid performance in both dialogue and exploration.

### 签名技能 / Signature Skill

签名技能可以获得+1额外加成，且升级所需经验减少30%。选择你计划频繁使用的核心技能作为签名。The signature skill gets a +1 bonus and requires 30% less XP to level. Pick a core skill you plan to use frequently.

**推荐签名技能 / Recommended Signature Skills**:
- 逻辑思维（Logic）— 适合推理派玩家 for deduction-oriented players
- 共情（Empathy）— 适合注重角色的玩家 for roleplay-focused players
- 手脚麻利（Hand-Eye Coordination）— 适合喜欢检视的玩家 for inspection-heavy play
- 坚韧（Endurance）— 适合高难度体验 for hardcore play

## 第一天攻略 / First Day Guide

### 醒来之后 / After Waking Up

你在褴褛飞旋（Whirling-in-Rags）旅馆的后院醒来，头痛欲裂，记忆全失。这是你的第一个任务：找回自己。

You wake up in the backyard of the Whirling-in-Rags, with a splitting headache and no memory. This is your first mission: find yourself.

**关键步骤 / Key Steps**:
1. 与旅馆内的每个人交谈 / Talk to everyone in the hotel
2. 检查你的物品栏，穿上衣服 / Check your inventory, put on clothes
3. 与金·吉速（Kim Kitsuragi）对话 / Talk to Kim Kitsuragi
4. 调查旅店老板的房间 / Investigate the hotel manager office
5. 开始调查悬挂的尸体 / Begin the hanged body investigation

### 时间管理 / Time Management

游戏中的时间通过对话和行动推进。每个行动消耗不等的时间。注意以下要点：

Time in the game advances through dialogue and actions. Each action consumes varying amounts of time. Keep these points in mind:

**核心时间规则 / Core Time Rules**:
- 对话本身不消耗时间 / Dialogue itself does not consume time
- 与物品互动通常消耗10-30分钟 / Interacting with objects usually costs 10-30 minutes
- 快速旅行（奔跑）会消耗时间 / Fast travel (running) consumes time
- 某些事件只能在特定时间触发 / Some events only trigger at specific times
- 第二天早上7:00重置所有NPC的位置和可对话状态 / 7:00 AM resets NPC positions and dialogue availability

**第一天必做事项 / Day 1 Must-Do**:
- 领取你的房间钥匙 / Claim your room key
- 找到至少一件上衣和鞋子 / Find at least one shirt and shoes
- 与金建立基本信任 / Build basic trust with Kim
- 检查尸体现场至少一次 / Inspect the body site at least once
- 收集足够的钱支付旅馆费用 / Collect enough money to pay the hotel

## 赚钱方法 / Making Money

### 初期资金来源 / Early Money Sources

**典当物品 / Pawning Items**：
- 找到可以典当的书籍和杂物 / Find books and junk to pawn
- 冷藏库中的领带可以典当 / The tie in the freezer can be pawned
- 注意：有些物品对任务关键，典当前确认 / Note: some items are quest-critical, confirm before pawning

**任务奖励 / Quest Rewards**：
- 完成小任务获得报酬 / Complete small tasks for payment
- 帮助当地居民获得感谢和金钱 / Help locals for thanks and money
- 主线推进到一定程度会获得警官津贴 / Main story progress unlocks officer stipend

**交易技巧 / Trading Tips**：
- 可以向旅馆老板赊账 / You can run a tab at the hotel
- 与商人讨价还价时有技能判定 / Haggling with merchants involves skill checks
- 某些任务会解锁新的交易选项 / Some quests unlock new trade options

## 存档建议 / Saving Tips

游戏没有自动存档。强烈建议频繁手动存档，尤其是在进行重要判定之前。

There is no auto-save. Frequent manual saving is strongly recommended, especially before important checks.

**存档时机 / When to Save**:
- 进入新区域前 / Before entering a new area
- 进行红色（困难）判定前 / Before attempting red (difficult) checks
- 与关键NPC对话前 / Before talking to key NPCs
- 每天开始和结束时 / At the start and end of each day
- 购买或使用重要物品前 / Before buying or using important items

**存档策略 / Save Strategy**:
- 保留至少3个不同时间点的存档槽 / Keep at least 3 save slots from different points
- 不要覆盖游戏初期的存档 / Do not overwrite early game saves
- 特殊事件前创建一个新存档 / Create a new save before special events

## 新手常见错误 / Common Beginner Mistakes

### 不要忽视白色判定 / Do Not Ignore White Checks

白色判定失败后可以重新尝试（通过提升对应技能或装备加成）。不要因为一次失败就放弃某个线索。

White checks can be retried after failure (by levelling the relevant skill or equipping bonuses). Do not abandon a lead after one failure.

### 多与金交流 / Talk to Kim Often

金·吉速是你的搭档，也是游戏中最可靠的盟友。他提供提示、背景信息，甚至能在关键判定中提供加成。

Kim Kitsuragi is your partner and the most reliable ally in the game. He provides hints, background info, and even bonuses on key checks.

### 思维阁不是摆设 / The Thought Cabinet Matters

不要忽视思维阁系统。内化合适的思维可以彻底改变游戏体验。初期建议先解锁一个实用的思维槽位。

Do not ignore the Thought Cabinet. Internalising the right thoughts can completely transform gameplay. Early on, unlock a practical thought slot.

### 阅读技能描述 / Read Skill Descriptions

每种技能都有详细的等级描述。了解每种技能在对话中的行为方式，有助于你做出更有信息量的选择。

Each skill has detailed level descriptions. Understanding how each skill behaves in dialogue helps you make more informed choices.

## 游戏难度提示 / Difficulty Tips

**简单入门 / Easy Start**: 选择思维派原型，将智力设为5，精神设为4。这样可以最大化对话选项和线索发现率。Choose the Thinker archetype, set Intellect to 5 and Psyche to 4. This maximises dialogue options and clue discovery.

**有挑战性 / Challenging**: 选择体力派原型，保证体格和运动属性较高。战斗判定更容易通过，但对话选项会受限。Choose the Physical archetype with high Physique and Motorics. Combat checks become easier but dialogue options are limited.

**极端角色 / Extreme Roleplay**: 将所有点数投入一个属性（如6智力/6精神），体验极端的角色扮演路径。某些对话会因此变得非常独特。Pour all points into one attribute (e.g., 6 Intellect / 6 Psyche) for extreme roleplay paths. Some dialogues become very unique.

## 实用快捷键 / Useful Shortcuts

- `Tab` — 高亮所有可互动物品 / Highlight all interactable objects
- `Ctrl` — 快速跳过对话 / Fast skip dialogue
- `I` — 打开物品栏 / Open inventory
- `T` — 打开思维阁 / Open Thought Cabinet
- `M` — 打开地图 / Open map
- `Esc` — 暂停/菜单 / Pause / Menu
