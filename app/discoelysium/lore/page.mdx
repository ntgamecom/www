# 思想与剧情 / Thoughts & Lore

《极乐迪斯科》的世界深沉而复杂。从「苍白」（The Pale）的存在主义隐喻到瑞瓦肖的革命历史，从哈里的个人悲剧到世界的政治格局——本页面深入解析游戏的核心理念和背景故事。

The world of Disco Elysium is deep and complex. From the existential metaphor of The Pale to the revolutionary history of Revachol, from Harry personal tragedy to the global political landscape — this page dives into the core concepts and background story of the game.

## 世界观：极乐世界 / World: Elysium

### 极乐世界简介 / Introduction to Elysium

游戏的舞台——极乐世界（Elysium）——是一个与我们相似但又截然不同的星球。这里有文明、国家、历史和战争，但也有着一种名为「苍白」的神秘现象，正在缓慢吞噬现实本身。

The game setting — Elysium — is a planet similar to yet different from ours. It has civilisation, nations, history, and war, but also a mysterious phenomenon called The Pale that is slowly consuming reality itself.

**极乐世界的关键特征 / Key Features of Elysium**:
- 科技水平大约相当于20世纪中期 / Technology roughly equivalent to mid-20th century
- 世界被多种意识形态撕裂 / The world is torn by multiple ideologies
- 通信和旅行受到苍白的严重限制 / Communication and travel severely limited by The Pale
- 存在超自然现象，但并不被广泛接受 / Supernatural phenomena exist but are not widely accepted
- 世界处于一种奇怪的停滞状态 / The world is in a strange state of stasis

### 大陆与文明 / Continents and Civilisation

极乐世界的地貌和地理与地球相似，但地名和国界完全不同。游戏故事主要发生在一座名为瑞瓦肖（Revachol）的城市。

The geography of Elysium resembles Earth, but place names and borders are completely different. The game story primarily takes place in a city called Revachol.

**已知地区 / Known Regions**:
- **瑞瓦肖（Revachol）**: 游戏的舞台，一座曾经辉煌的港口城市 / Game setting, a once-glorious port city
- **联合体（Coalition）**: 瑞瓦肖的占领者，由多个国家组成的军事联盟 / Occupier of Revachol, a military alliance of multiple nations
- **格拉德（Graad）**: 大陆上的一个大国，在背景故事中扮演重要角色 / A major continental power, important in backstory
- **其他地区 / Other Regions**: 游戏通过书籍和对话提及了许多其他地方 / Game mentions many other places through books and dialogue

## 苍白 / The Pale

### 苍白的本质 / Nature of The Pale

苍白是《极乐迪斯科》中最核心、最神秘的概念。它是一种吞噬现实的虚无——世界边缘的虚无正在缓慢向内侵蚀。苍白的存在暗示着极乐世界正在走向终结。

The Pale is the most central and mysterious concept in Disco Elysium. It is a nothingness that devours reality — the void at the edge of the world slowly eroding inward. The existence of The Pale suggests Elysium is coming to an end.

**苍白理论 / Theories of The Pale**:
- **科学解释 / Scientific Explanation**: 苍白是信息密度过高导致现实崩溃的产物 / The Pale is the product of reality collapsing under information density
- **形而上学解释 / Metaphysical Explanation**: 苍白是人类集体意识和历史创伤的具象化 / The Pale is the manifestation of collective human consciousness and historical trauma
- **神秘学解释 / Occult Explanation**: 苍白是旧神死亡后的遗骸 / The Pale is the remains of dead gods
- **灾变理论 / Catastrophe Theory**: 苍白是某次灾难性事件的后果 / The Pale is the aftermath of a catastrophic event

### 苍白对世界的影响 / How the Pale Affects the World

**现实影响 / Real Effects**:
- 通信延迟：跨苍白通讯极其困难 / Communication delays: cross-pale communication is extremely difficult
- 旅行受限：穿越苍白区域的旅行危险且昂贵 / Travel restricted: travelling through pale zones is dangerous and expensive
- 信息丢失：进入苍白的信息会失真或消失 / Information loss: information entering the pale becomes distorted or lost
- 心理影响：靠近苍白的人会产生精神不适 / Psychological effects: proximity to the pale causes mental discomfort

### 苍白在剧情中的意义 / Thematic Significance of the Pale

苍白不仅仅是一个世界设定，更是一个深刻的隐喻。它代表了遗忘、死亡、历史的终结,以及意义的虚无。在游戏中，苍白与哈里的记忆丧失形成了平行关系——个人的苍白和世界的苍白同时存在。

The Pale is more than a world-building element — it is a profound metaphor. It represents oblivion, death, the end of history, and the meaninglessness of existence. In the game, the Pale parallels Harry memory loss — a personal pale coexisting with the world pale.

## 意识形态 / Ideologies

### 四种政治倾向 / Four Political Alignments

《极乐迪斯科》中最具特色的设计之一是其政治意识形态系统。你在游戏中的言论和选择会影响你在这四个维度上的倾向。

One of the most distinctive designs in Disco Elysium is its political ideology system. Your words and choices in the game affect your alignment across these four dimensions.

**共产主义 / Communism**:

共产主义在瑞瓦肖的历史中有着深刻的烙印。几十年前的共产主义革命改变了这个城市，但革命最终被镇压。如今，共产主义在瑞瓦肖是一种怀旧的理想、一种被镇压的记忆、也是一种仍在燃烧的火种。

Communism has deep roots in Revachol history. The communist revolution decades ago transformed the city, but it was eventually crushed. Today, communism in Revachol is a nostalgic ideal, a suppressed memory, and still a burning ember.

**关键人物 / Key Figures**:
- 逃兵（The Deserter）— 旧的革命者，固守纯粹理想 / Old revolutionary clinging to pure ideals
- 艾弗拉特·克莱尔（Evrart Claire）— 实用主义工会领袖，利用共产主义话语 / Pragmatic union leader using communist rhetoric
- 测量女（Measurehead）— 并非共产主义者，但理解其历史 / Not a communist, but understands its history

**共产主义路线特点 / Communist Route Features**:
- 对话选项充满革命热情 / Dialogue options full of revolutionary passion
- 与历史人物和事件深度关联 / Deep connection with historical figures and events
- 容易与其他角色产生意识形态冲突 / May cause ideological conflicts with other characters
- 最终对革命的评价由你决定 / Final judgment on the revolution is yours to make

**法西斯主义 / Fascism**:

法西斯主义在游戏中以瑞瓦肖的种族主义历史和排外倾向为代表。选择这条路线意味着拥抱最黑暗的意识形态。

Fascism is represented in the game through Revachol racist history and xenophobic tendencies. Choosing this path means embracing the darkest ideology.

**关键人物 / Key Figures**:
- 测量女（Measurehead）— 公开的种族主义者 / Open racist
- 某些街头NPC / Certain street NPCs
- 哈里的某些内心声音 / Certain of Harry inner voices

**法西斯路线特点 / Fascism Route Features**:
- 对话选项充满排外和暴力 / Dialogue options full of xenophobia and violence
- 绝大多数角色会厌恶你 / Most characters will despise you
- 金的关系会受到严重损害 / Kim relationship will be severely damaged
- 这是最难贯彻的路线，需要刻意选择 / The hardest to commit to, requires deliberate choices

**自由主义 / Liberalism**:

自由主义代表资本主义、个人自由和市场经济的价值观。在瑞瓦肖的语境中，这意味着接受现状或拥抱资本的力量。

Liberalism represents capitalist, individual freedom, and market economy values. In the Revachol context, this means accepting the status quo or embracing capital power.

**关键人物 / Key Figures**:
- 乔伊斯·梅西埃（Joyce Messier）— 野松公司代表，温和的资本主义代言人 / Wild Pines representative, moderate capitalist voice
- 旅馆老板 / Hotel manager
- 商人NPC / Merchant NPCs

**自由主义路线特点 / Liberalism Route Features**:
- 对话选项涉及贸易、合同和个人责任 / Dialogue options involve trade, contracts and personal responsibility
- 与商业角色关系良好 / Good relations with business characters
- 强调实用主义和个体选择 / Emphasises pragmatism and individual choice
- 可能会与其他意识形态产生微妙冲突 / May subtly conflict with other ideologies

**道德主义 / Moralist**:

道德主义代表了维持现状、渐进改良和避免极端的中庸之道。它是游戏中最微妙的意识形态，也是默认的「无意识形态」状态。

Moralism represents maintaining the status quo, gradual reform, and avoiding extremes. It is the most subtle ideology in the game, and the default non-ideology state.

**关键人物 / Key Figures**:
- 金·吉速（Kim Kitsuragi）— 温和的实用主义者 / Mild pragmatist
- 联盟官员 / Coalition officials
- 大多数普通市民 / Most ordinary citizens

**道德主义路线特点 / Moralism Route Features**:
- 对话选项强调秩序和稳定 / Dialogue options emphasise order and stability
- 最不容易引起冲突的路线 / The path least likely to cause conflict
- 被其他意识形态者视为「无趣」 / Seen as boring by other ideologues
- 在游戏中最容易被自然采用 / The most natural path to take in the game

## 哈里·杜博阿 / Harrier Du Bois

### 哈里的过去 / Harry Past

哈里尔·「哈里」·杜博阿是游戏的主角，一个破碎的人。游戏开始时，他失去了所有记忆——但即使恢复记忆，他的过去也是一个痛苦的故事。

Harrier Harry Du Bois is the protagonist, a broken man. At the start of the game, he has lost all memory — but even when memory returns, his past is a painful story.

**关键背景 / Key Background**:
- 他曾是瑞瓦肖警察局的明星警探 / He was a star detective in the Revachol police force
- 与妻子多拉（Dora）的婚姻破裂后陷入崩溃 / Collapsed after his marriage to Dora fell apart
- 酗酒和药物成瘾导致他失去了生活和职业 / Alcohol and drug addiction cost him his life and career
- 被派往马丁内斯是最后一次机会 / The assignment to Martinaise is his last chance

### 多拉 / Dora Ingerlund

多拉是哈里的前妻，也是游戏中最重要但从未出现的角色之一。她存在于哈里的记忆和梦境中，代表着他失去的一切。

Dora is Harry ex-wife and one of the most important characters who never physically appears. She exists in Harry memories and dreams, representing everything he lost.

**关于多拉的重要事实 / Important Facts about Dora**:
- 她曾是哈里生活的全部 / She was Harry entire world
- 她给了哈里无数机会改变 / She gave Harry countless chances to change
- 她的离开导致了哈里的彻底崩溃 / Her departure caused Harry complete breakdown
- 在游戏过程中，哈里必须面对关于她离开的真相 / During the game, Harry must face the truth about her leaving

### 哈里的恢复 / Harry Recovery

游戏的核心主题之一是哈里能否从自我毁灭中恢复。这不仅取决于案件的结果，更取决于他在对话和思维中做出的选择。

One of the core themes is whether Harry can recover from self-destruction. This depends not only on the case outcome, but on his choices in dialogue and thoughts.

**恢复的路径 / Paths to Recovery**:
- 接受过去，放下多拉 / Accept the past, let go of Dora
- 与金建立真正的伙伴关系 / Build genuine partnership with Kim
- 在工作中找到意义 / Find meaning in police work
- 面对自己的成瘾问题 / Confront his addiction issues
- 在马丁内斯找到属于自己的一席之地 / Find his place in Martinaise

## 历史与派系 / History & Factions

### 瑞瓦肖革命 / The Revachol Revolution

几十年前，瑞瓦肖爆发了共产主义革命。工人们占领了城市，建立了革命政府。但革命最终被联军（Coalition）镇压，城市被占领。

Decades ago, a communist revolution erupted in Revachol. Workers seized the city, establishing a revolutionary government. But the revolution was eventually crushed by the Coalition, and the city was occupied.

**革命年表 / Revolution Timeline**:
1. 工人不满积累 / Accumulation of worker grievances
2. 革命爆发，旧政权被推翻 / Revolution erupts, old regime overthrown
3. 革命政府建立 / Revolutionary government established
4. 国际联军入侵 / Coalition forces invade
5. 革命被镇压 / Revolution crushed
6. 瑞瓦肖被军事占领 / Revachol placed under military occupation
7. 占领持续至今 / Occupation continues to present day

### 主要派系 / Major Factions

**瑞瓦肖警察局 / Revachol Police Force**:

在占领状态下运作的本地执法机构。资源匮乏，权威有限。哈里和金为其成员。

Local law enforcement operating under occupation. Under-resourced, limited authority. Harry and Kim are members.

**野松公司 / Wild Pines Enterprises**:

全球性的企业集团，在马丁内斯有码头业务。代表资本的力量。

Global corporate conglomerate with dock operations in Martinaise. Represents capital power.

**码头工会 / Dock Workers Union**:

由艾弗拉特·克莱尔领导的强大工会。表面上是工人的保护者，实际上是马丁内斯的影子政府。

Powerful union led by Evrart Claire. Ostensibly worker protectors, actually the shadow government of Martinaise.

**联军 / The Coalition**:

占领瑞瓦肖的国际军事联盟。名义上是维持和平，实际上是控制。

The international military alliance occupying Revachol. Nominally peacekeeping, actually controlling.

**哈迪曼集团 / Hardy Boys**:

艾弗拉特的私人武装，执行工会的 dirty work。由一些当地混混组成。

Evrart private enforcers who do the union dirty work. Composed of local thugs.

### 逃兵与他的故事 / The Deserter and His Story

逃兵是游戏中最悲剧的角色之一。他是革命时期的一名士兵，在革命失败后躲进了军事封锁区，几十年来一直独自生活，坚守着——或者说被囚禁在——他革命的纯粹理念中。

The deserter is one of the most tragic characters in the game. A soldier from the revolutionary period, he hid in the military zone after the revolution failed, living alone for decades,坚守 — or imprisoned by — his pure revolutionary ideals.

**他的世界观 / His Worldview**:
- 革命被背叛了 / The revolution was betrayed
- 世界需要一场新的革命 / The world needs a new revolution
- 他的行动是正义的，尽管手段极端 / His actions are righteous, though extreme
- 他是历史洪流中的一朵浪花 / He is a droplet in the tide of history

**他的罪行 / His Crime**:
逃兵是吊尸案的真凶。他射杀了那个雇佣兵，制造了悬挂的尸体。但他的动机不是个人仇恨——而是一种扭曲的政治行动。

The deserter is the true culprit of the hanged body case. He shot the mercenary and staged the hanging. But his motive was not personal hatred — it was a twisted political act.

## 思想深度 / Thematic Depth

《极乐迪斯科》不仅仅是一个侦探故事。它探讨了存在主义、历史唯物主义、个人责任与社会结构的关系、成瘾与救赎、爱与失去。这些主题交织在一起，创造出了游戏界最深刻的故事之一。

Disco Elysium is more than a detective story. It explores existentialism, historical materialism, the relationship between personal responsibility and social structures, addiction and redemption, love and loss. These themes intertwine to create one of the deepest stories in gaming.
