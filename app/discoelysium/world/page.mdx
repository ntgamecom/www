# 世界探索 / World Exploration

马丁内斯（Martinaise）是瑞瓦肖（Revachol）西部的一个小港口区域，也是整部游戏的主要舞台。虽然地图不大，但每个角落都充满了细节、秘密和故事。

Martinaise is a small port area in Revachol West, serving as the main setting of the entire game. Though the map is not large, every corner is filled with details, secrets, and stories.

## 瑞瓦肖世界概览 / Revachol World Overview

### 瑞瓦肖历史简介 / Brief History of Revachol

瑞瓦肖曾是一个繁荣的世界级城市，但在革命和战争后变得破败。世界陷入了某种停滞状态——被称为「苍白」（The Pale）的虚无正在从世界边缘侵蚀现实本身。

Revachol was once a thriving world-class city, but it fell into decay after revolution and war. The world is in a state of stasis — a nothingness called The Pale is eroding reality itself from the edges of the world.

**关键历史事件 / Key Historical Events**:
- 共产主义革命（The Communist Revolution） / 几十年前爆发的工人革命 / The workers revolution decades ago
- 反革命与封锁（Counter-Revolution and Blockade） / 联军粉碎革命并封锁瑞瓦肖 / Coalition forces crushed the revolution and blockaded Revachol
- 当前的占领状态 / 瑞瓦肖处于国际联盟的占领管理下 / Revachol is under coalition occupation administration

### 马丁内斯在瑞瓦肖中的位置 / Martinaise in Revachol

马丁内斯是瑞瓦肖西部的一个小港口，曾经航运繁忙，如今萧条破败。一条运河将城镇分为两部分，一座吊桥连接两岸。

Martinaise is a small port in western Revachol, once bustling with shipping, now depressed and run-down. A canal divides the town in two, connected by a drawbridge.

## 主要地点 / Major Locations

### 褴褛飞旋旅馆 / Whirling-in-Rags

你的基地和第一天醒来之处。这座旅馆是马丁内斯的社交中心，包含酒吧、厨房、后院和客房。

Your base and the place you wake up on day one. This hotel is the social hub of Martinaise, containing a bar, kitchen, backyard, and guest rooms.

**重要区域 / Important Areas**:
- **酒吧区（Bar Area）**: 与NPC交谈，获取信息，购买食物和饮品。Talk to NPCs, get info, buy food and drinks.
- **厨房（Kitchen）**: 检查冷藏库，找到重要物品。Check the freezer, find important items.
- **后院（Backyard）**: 悬挂尸体的发现地点。可以多次返回检视。Location of the hanged body. Can return for multiple inspections.
- **二楼客房（Second Floor Rooms）**: 你的房间和其他客人的房间。Your room and other guests rooms.
- **管理者办公室（Manager Office）**: 包含案件相关资料。Contains case-related documents.

**隐藏内容 / Hidden Content**:
- 冷藏库中冷冻的领带 / The frozen tie in the freezer
- 吧台下方的秘密 / Secrets beneath the bar counter
- 后院不同时间的光线变化影响可见线索 / Lighting changes in the yard at different times affect visible clues

### 码头区 / The Dock Area

工会控制的重地。这里充斥着工人、政治活动和经济纠纷。

The heavily controlled union territory. Filled with workers, political activity, and economic disputes.

**重要NPC / Key NPCs**:
- 艾弗拉特·克莱尔（Evrart Claire）——工会领袖，在集装箱办公室中办公 / Union leader, operates from a container office
- 卡里米（Karimi）——工会职员 / Union clerk
- 码头工人们 / The dock workers

**探索要点 / Exploration Points**:
- 码头起重机吊臂上可能有线索 / Clues may be found on the crane arm
- 集装箱区域藏有各种物品 / The container area hides various items
- 水边可以找到特殊的痕迹 / Special traces can be found by the water edge

### 渔村 / The Fishing Village

马丁内斯边缘的小渔村，拥有独特的生活方式和文化。

A small fishing village on the edge of Martinaise with a unique lifestyle and culture.

**重要区域 / Important Areas**:
- **渔民居所 / Fisherman Huts**: 简朴的住所，包含生活痕迹 / Simple dwellings with traces of daily life
- **码头 / Fishing Pier**: 可以在这里与渔夫交谈 / Talk to fishermen here
- **小船停泊处 / Boat Mooring**: 可能发现进入水域的方法 / Possible method to access the water

**隐藏内容 / Hidden Content**:
- 某间小屋中的秘密 / Secrets in one of the huts
- 渔村的集体记忆 / The collective memory of the fishing village
- 与水相关的特殊互动 / Special water-related interactions

### 港务局 / The Harbour

曾经繁忙的港口管理机构，现在几近废弃。包含重要的案件线索。

Once a busy port management facility, now nearly abandoned. Contains important case clues.

**重要区域 / Important Areas**:
- **办公楼 / Office Building**: 包含文件档案和电脑终端 / Contains document archives and computer terminals
- **仓库 / Warehouse**: 可能藏有货物和证据 / May hold cargo and evidence
- **码头 / Pier**: 可以远眺运河和对岸 / Overlooks the canal and opposite shore

### 费尔德大楼 / The Feld Building

一座废弃的公寓楼，住着一些最有趣的角色。

An abandoned apartment building housing some of the most interesting characters.

**重要NPC / Key NPCs**:
- 库诺和卢卡（Cuno and Luka）——住在二楼的父子 / Father and son living on the second floor
- 安妮特（Annette）——住在某个房间的女孩 / A girl living in one of the rooms
- 其他住户 / Other residents

**探索要点 / Exploration Points**:
- 各个房间可以搜索 / Various rooms can be searched
- 楼顶有特殊视角 / The rooftop has a special view
- 地下室可能包含隐藏物品 / The basement may contain hidden items

### 教堂 / The Church

废弃的教堂，隐藏着游戏中最超自然的秘密。需要特定技能或进度才能进入。

An abandoned church hiding the most supernatural secrets in the game. Requires specific skills or progress to enter.

**重要区域 / Important Areas**:
- **主厅（Main Hall）**: 破败但庄严的空间 / Dilapidated but solemn space
- **地下室（Basement）**: 真正的秘密所在 / Where the real secrets lie
- **钟楼（Bell Tower）**: 可以俯瞰整个马丁内斯 / Overlooks all of Martinaise

**关键地点 / Key Spots**:

- 排水口附近 / Near the drainage outlet
- 运河的尽头 / The end of the canal

The seaside of Martinaise, with sandy beaches and rocky shores. Special events occur here at certain times.

**关键地点 / Key Spots**:
- 海滩 / The beach
- 岩石区 / The rocky area
- 废弃的篝火点 / Abandoned campfire site

### 军事封锁区 / Military Blockade Zone

A late-game area hiding the deserter secret. Requires specific conditions to enter.

**进入方式 / How to Enter**:

## 探索技巧 / Exploration Tips

### 时间对世界的影响 / How Time Affects the World

马丁内斯的时间是流动的。NPC有作息时间表，某些地点在不同时间有不同的状态。

Time flows in Martinaise. NPCs have schedules, and some locations have different states at different times.

**时间敏感内容 / Time-Sensitive Content**:
- 早晨：NPC刚起床，可能不太愿意交谈 / NPCs just woke up, less talkative
- 中午：最活跃的时间，所有人都可对话 / Most active time, everyone available
- 傍晚：某些NPC下班回家 / Some NPCs go home after work
- 夜间：只有特定场所开放 / Only specific venues open

### 快速旅行 / Fast Travel

点击地图上的目的地可以快速移动（消耗游戏内时间）。也可以用奔跑模式快速到达附近区域。

Click destinations on the map to fast travel (costs in-game time). You can also run to nearby areas quickly.

### 反复探索的价值 / Value of Revisiting

第一次经过某个地点时可能漏掉线索。随着技能提升和时间推进，回到旧地点可能有新发现。

You might miss clues the first time you pass through. As skills improve and time advances, returning to old locations may yield new discoveries.

## 天气与氛围 / Weather and Atmosphere

马丁内斯的天气始终阴郁，偶尔下雨。雨水会影响某些地面的痕迹，也会影响NPC的情绪和对话内容。

The weather in Martinaise is consistently gloomy, with occasional rain. Rain affects certain ground traces and also influences NPC moods and dialogue.

**氛围特点 / Atmosphere Features**:
- 持续的低沉环境音 / Constant低沉 ambient sounds
- 海风带着工业废气的味道 / Sea breeze mixed with industrial fumes
- 远处的城市灯火若隐若现 / Distant city lights flicker faintly
- 巨大的船舶在码头停泊 / Massive ships docked at the harbour
