# 剧情与任务 / Story & Missions

荒野大镖客2的故事横跨6个章节加一个尾声，讲述范德林德帮从巅峰到崩溃的全过程。本章提供全章节流程指南、关键选择和结局解析 / RDR2's story spans 6 chapters plus an epilogue, chronicling the Van der Linde gang's rise and fall. This chapter covers the full chapter walkthrough, key choices, and ending analysis.

---

## 故事背景 / Story Background

1899年——美国西部的蛮荒时代正在落幕。执法官的步步紧逼和文明社会的扩张让匪帮的生存空间越来越小。道奇·范德林德领导的帮派在一次抢劫失败后被迫逃亡，试图在彻底的毁灭前找到最后的出路 / 1899 — the Wild West is dying. As law closes in and civilization expands, outlaws have fewer places to hide. After a failed heist, Dutch van der Linde's gang is on the run, desperately seeking one last score before it's all over.

你扮演**亚瑟·摩根**——帮派的资深成员，道奇最信任的手下。在忠诚与自我救赎之间，你的选择将决定一切 / You play as **Arthur Morgan** — the gang's longest-serving member and Dutch's most trusted enforcer. Caught between loyalty and redemption, your choices will determine everything.

## 结局解析 / Ending Analysis

### 亚瑟的结局 / Arthur's Endings

取决于**第六章末尾的荣誉值高低** + **一个关键选择** / Determined by **honor level at the end of Chapter 6** + **one key choice**:

| 荣誉值 / Honor | 结局 / Ending | 描述 / Description |
|:----:|------|------|
| **高荣誉 + 帮助约翰 / High Honor + Help John** | **最佳结局 / Best Ending** | 亚瑟在日出中安详离世——获得了最终的救赎 / Arthur dies peacefully at sunrise — achieving final redemption |
| **低荣誉 + 帮助约翰 / Low Honor + Help John** | 中等结局 / Medium Ending | 亚瑟死于绝望之中，被野心吞噬 / Arthur dies in despair, consumed by ambition |
| **任何荣誉 + 回去拿钱 / Any Honor + Go for Money** | **最差结局 / Worst Ending** | 亚瑟被迈卡杀死——悲剧收场 / Arthur is killed by Micah — tragic end |

### 尾声 / Epilogue

**第1部分 / Part 1**: 约翰和艾比盖尔在牧场工作——试图过上正常生活 / John and Abigail work on a ranch — trying to live a normal life.

**第2部分 / Part 2**: 约翰买下比彻之愿农场——重建生活，最终为亚瑟复仇 / John buys Beecher's Hope ranch — rebuilding and ultimately avenging Arthur.

| 尾声任务 / Epilogue Mission | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|
| **建立新生活（Building a New Life）** | 约翰在牧场工作，努力养家 / John works the ranch, supports his family |
| **比彻之愿（Beecher's Hope）** | 约翰买下农场，重建家园 / John buys the farm, rebuilds |
| **美国毒蛇（American Venom）** | **最终任务**——登上雪山，为亚瑟复仇 / **Final mission** — climb the mountain, avenge Arthur |

> **尾声完成后**：世界永久开放，以约翰的身份自由探索 / **After the epilogue**: The world is permanently open, free to explore as John.
