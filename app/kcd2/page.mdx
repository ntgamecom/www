# 天国：拯救2 / Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2

{/* 游戏概述 / Game Overview */}

欢迎来到《天国：拯救2》完整游戏指南。本指南以中英双语编写，帮助你深入了解这款以15世纪波希米亚为背景的开放世界角色扮演游戏。

Welcome to the complete *Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2* game guide. This bilingual guide helps you master every aspect of this open-world RPG set in 15th-century Bohemia.

## 游戏背景 / Game Setting

故事发生在1403年的波希米亚王国（今捷克共和国地区），延续前作剧情。你将继续扮演亨利——一位从铁匠之子成长为战士的年轻人，在内战与政治阴谋中寻找自己的道路。

The story takes place in the Kingdom of Bohemia (modern-day Czech Republic) in 1403, continuing the narrative of the first game. You play as Henry, a young man who rises from a blacksmith's son to a warrior, navigating civil war and political intrigue.

## 关于本指南 / About This Guide

本书分为五个主要章节：新手入门帮助新玩家快速上手，战斗系统详解 Master Strike 与武器技巧，技能与属性提供最佳升级路线，任务攻略揭示选择背后的影响，世界探索则带你走遍波希米亚的每个角落。

This guide is divided into five main chapters: Beginner Guide helps new players get started quickly, Combat explains Master Strike and weapon techniques, Skills & Perks provides optimal leveling paths, Quests reveals the impact of your choices, and World & Exploration takes you across every corner of Bohemia.

## 角色简介 / Character Introduction

**亨利 / Henry**: 本作主角，前作中从铁匠之子成长为战士。续作中他将继续追寻复仇与正义之路。

The protagonist, who grew from a blacksmith's son to a warrior in the previous game. He continues his journey of vengeance and justice.

**汉斯·卡彭爵士 / Sir Hans Capon**: 亨利的挚友与领主之子，幽默风趣但有时鲁莽。

Henry's close friend and the lord's son, humorous but sometimes reckless.

**拉德季·科比拉 / Sir Radzig Kobyla**: 亨利的领主与 mentor，在政治漩涡中努力保护自己的领地。

Henry's lord and mentor, struggling to protect his lands amid political turmoil.

**泰丽莎 / Theresa**: 前作中的重要角色，亨利的情感纽带。

A key character from the previous game and Henry's emotional anchor.
