# 新手入门 / Beginner Guide

欢迎新玩家！本章涵盖从角色创建到完成序章所需的一切知识。如果你首次接触《天国：拯救》系列，强烈建议仔细阅读本节内容。

Welcome, new players! This chapter covers everything from character creation to completing the prologue. If this is your first *Kingdom Come* game, we strongly recommend reading this section carefully.

## 角色创建 / Character Creation

与大多数 RPG 不同，KCD2 没有传统意义上的角色职业选择。亨利的基础属性由前作的存档继承决定，但新玩家可以使用预设模板。

Unlike most RPGs, KCD2 does not feature traditional class selection. Henry's base stats are determined by your previous save import, but new players can use preset templates.

### 初始属性分配 / Initial Stat Distribution

| 属性 / Attribute | 战斗型 / Combat | 平衡型 / Balanced | 口才型 / Speech |
|---|---|---|---|
| 力量 / Strength | 6 | 4 | 3 |
| 敏捷 / Agility | 4 | 4 | 4 |
| 活力 / Vitality | 5 | 4 | 3 |
| 口才 / Speech | 2 | 4 | 6 |
| 阅读 / Reading | 1 | 2 | 3 |

**推荐选择 / Recommendation**: 初次游玩选择平衡型。战斗能力可以通过训练快速提升，但错过对话选项则无法挽回。

Choose the Balanced preset for your first playthrough. Combat skills can be trained quickly, but missed dialogue options are permanent.

## 序章攻略 / Prologue Walkthrough

### 第一阶段：开场 / Phase 1: Opening

序章从亨利的故乡斯卡利茨开始。跟随主线指示完成基础移动和互动教学。

The prologue begins in Henry's home village of Skalitz. Follow the main quest markers to complete basic movement and interaction tutorials.

**关键提示 / Key Tips**:
- 拾取所有可见的物品，尤其是食物和草药
- Loot all visible items, especially food and herbs
- 与所有 NPC 对话以获得背景信息
- Talk to every NPC for background information
- 完成训练场的基础战斗教学
- Complete the basic combat tutorial at the training ground

### 第二阶段：逃亡 / Phase 2: Escape

{/* 剧透注意：包含序章关键情节 / Spoiler: contains key prologue events */}

剧情事件发生后，你需要在没有装备的情况下生存。这一阶段的核心目标是跟随泰丽莎或直接逃往 Talmberg。

After a critical story event, you must survive without equipment. The core objective is to follow Theresa or escape directly to Talmberg.

**生存建议 / Survival Advice**:
1. 保持隐蔽，避免与武装敌人正面冲突
2. Stay hidden; avoid direct confrontation with armed enemies
3. 利用灌木丛和地形遮挡视线
4. Use bushes and terrain to break line of sight
5. 保存体力，不要持续奔跑
6. Conserve stamina; do not sprint continuously
7. 沿河流行进可避免迷路
8. Follow rivers to avoid getting lost

### 第三阶段：Talmberg / Phase 3: Talmberg

抵达 Talmberg 后，你需要说服守卫让你进入城堡。此处有多个对话选项。

After reaching Talmberg, you must persuade the guards to let you into the castle. Multiple dialogue options are available.

**成功率最高方案 / Highest Success Chance**:
- 选择「我是斯卡利茨的幸存者 / I am a survivor from Skalitz」
- 随后选择「我需要见领主 / I need to see the lord」
- 口才技能达到 3 以上可解锁额外选项
- Speech skill of 3 or higher unlocks additional options

## 基础生存机制 / Basic Survival Mechanics

### 饥饿与饮食 / Hunger & Nutrition

亨利需要定期进食。食物分为新鲜、普通和变质三个等级。变质食物会导致食物中毒。

Henry needs to eat regularly. Food comes in three quality tiers: fresh, ordinary, and spoiled. Spoiled food causes food poisoning.

| 食物类型 / Food Type | 饱腹度 / Nourishment | 新鲜度 / Freshness |
|---|---|---|
| 烤肉 / Roasted Meat | 35 | 高 / High |
| 面包 / Bread | 25 | 中 / Medium |
| 水果 / Fruit | 15 | 低 / Low |
| 炖菜 / Stew | 40 | 高 / High |

### 睡眠与保存 / Sleep & Saving

睡眠是主要的保存方式。你可以在旅馆租床、使用自己的营地或找到免费的床位。

Sleeping is the primary save method. You can rent beds at inns, use your own camp, or find free beds.

**保存机制 / Save Mechanics**:
- 睡觉自动保存 / Sleeping auto-saves
- Saviour Schnapps 酿酒可手动保存 / Saviour Schnapps potion enables manual saves
- 重要任务节点自动保存 / Auto-save at major quest milestones
- 死亡不会丢失装备，但会回退到上次保存点
- Death does not lose equipment but reverts to last save

### 清洁与声望 / Cleanliness & Reputation

肮脏的外表会影响 NPC 对你的态度。定期洗澡或在河流中清洗可以保持清洁。

A dirty appearance affects NPC attitudes. Bathe regularly or wash in rivers to stay clean.

**清洁等级 / Cleanliness Levels**:
- 清洁 / Clean: 对话无惩罚 / No dialogue penalty
- 微脏 / Slightly Dirty: 口才 -1 / Speech -1
- 肮脏 / Dirty: 口才 -3，部分 NPC 拒绝对话
- Speech -3, some NPCs refuse to talk
- 极脏 / Filthy: 口才 -5，守卫可能攻击
- Speech -5, guards may attack

## 初期赚钱方法 / Early Money-Making Tips

1. **采集草药 / Herb Gathering**: 初期最安全的赚钱方式，每株草药可卖 0.5-2 格罗申
   Safest early method; each herb sells for 0.5-2 Groschen
2. **开锁 / Lockpicking**: 夜间潜入民宅偷取贵重物品，但失败会被通缉
   Nighttime burglary of valuables, but failure leads to a bounty
3. **狩猎 / Hunting**: 出售兽皮和肉类，前期最好选择
   Sell hides and meat; best early-game option
4. **完成支线任务 / Side Quests**: Rattay 周边有多位 NPC 发布小额任务
   Several NPCs around Rattay offer small quests

## 常见误区 / Common Mistakes

- **过度负重 / Over-encumbrance**: 不要拾取所有战利品。控制负重以保持移动速度
  Do not loot everything. Manage weight to maintain movement speed
- **忽视训练 / Skipping Training**: Bernard 教官的训练至关重要，务必完成
  Captain Bernard's training is essential; do not skip it
- **正面硬刚 / Head-on Fighting**: 初期不要挑战多名敌人。利用地形和弓箭逐个击破
  Do not take on multiple enemies early on. Use terrain and bows to pick them off
- **不保存 / Not Saving**: 长任务前务必保存，避免意外死亡损失进度
  Always save before long quests to avoid losing progress

## 新手推荐路线 / Recommended New Player Path

1. 完成序章到达 Rattay（Reach Rattay after the prologue）
2. 与 Bernard 教官完成基础战斗训练（Complete basic combat training with Captain Bernard）
3. 学习阅读技能（在 Rattay 找 scribe 学习）（Learn to read from the scribe in Rattay）
4. 采集草药积累初期资金（Gather herbs for early income）
5. 完成 Rattay 周边支线任务（Complete side quests around Rattay）
6. 探索地图熟悉各个区域（Explore the map to learn the regions）
7. 积累装备后再推进主线（Build up gear before advancing the main story）
