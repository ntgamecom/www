## 彩蛋 / Easter Eggs

**防火女彩蛋**: 在蒙德大教堂地下室——有一本日记——作者提到"我梦到了一团火"——致敬黑暗之魂的防火女 / Firekeeper reference: Mondstadt cathedral basement — diary entry "I dreamed of a fire" — Dark Souls Firekeeper reference. **米哈游办公室**: 在璃月的一个房间——有一张桌子和七神像的微缩模型——内部有"MHY STUDIO"字样——致敬米哈游办公室 / MHY Office: Liyue room — desk with miniature Seven Statues — reads "MHY STUDIO" — Hoyo office tribute. **希格雯的海报**: 枫丹水下——某处墙壁上贴着一张希格雯的海报——标注"最强看护员" / Sigewinne Poster: Fontaine underwater — wall poster reading "Best Nurse" — Sigewinne. **"非常危险的蘑菇"**: 须弥雨林中有一个红色大蘑菇——互动后角色说"这看起来很危险"——然后蘑菇爆炸——角色被弹飞——无伤害 / "Very Dangerous Mushroom": Sumeru rainforest — big red mushroom — interact = character says "this looks dangerous" — mushroom explodes — launches character — no damage.

## 未解之谜 / Unsolved Mysteries

**时间之岛**: 蒙德东边海面上有一个小岛——岛上的时间永远固定在午夜——和世界任务相关但仍有未解之处。**坎瑞亚的传送门**: 层岩巨渊地下有5个无法激活的传送门——据推测是通往坎瑞亚的通道。**派蒙的真面目**: 游戏内大量文本暗示派蒙不是看起来那么简单——她的真实身份仍是最大的谜团 / **Time Island**: small island east of Mondstadt — time always midnight — related to world quest but still has mysteries. **Khaenri'ah Portals**: 5 inactive portals in Chasm underground — speculated to lead to Khaenri'ah. **Paimon's True Identity**: much in-game text hints Paimon is not what she seems — her true identity remains biggest mystery.
