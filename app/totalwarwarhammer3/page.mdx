# 全面战争：战锤3攻略 / Total War: Warhammer 3 Guide

Lead legendary lords, command dozens of unit types, and conquer the mortal empires. This guide covers faction traits, campaign strategy, combat tactics, and legendary lord breakdowns.

## 新手开局要点 / Quick Start Tips

| # | 建议 / Tip |
|---|---|
| **1** | **新手推荐派系**: 震旦天朝(彪炳/元伯)——经济好、兵种均衡、新手友好 / **Beginner faction**: Grand Cathay (Miao Ying/Zhao Ming) — strong economy, balanced roster, beginner-friendly |
| **2** | **不要多线开战**: 初期只打1-2个敌人——签非侵略条约保后方。同时打3个敌人=崩盘 / Don't fight multiple wars — sign non-aggression pacts. Fighting 3 enemies = campaign over |
| **3** | **主城升级优先**: 每个行省的首府优先升3级——解锁高级兵种和更多建筑槽 / Prioritize capital upgrades to level 3 — unlocks elite units and extra building slots |
| **4** | **事务官行动**: 英雄/法师等事务官在战略地图上可以刺杀/破坏/阻挡军队——别让他们闲着 / Heroes and wizards can assassinate, sabotage, and block armies on the campaign map — keep them busy |
| **5** | **魔法在战斗中决定胜负**: 一场战斗的胜负往往取决于你是否在正确时机放了正确的法术——火球术(火焰风暴)清杂兵、金言护身保将领 / Magic wins battles — Fireball (flame storm) clears chaff, Earth Blood heals, Fate of Bjuna snipes lords |
