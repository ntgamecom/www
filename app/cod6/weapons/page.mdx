**LMG核心技巧 / LMG Core Tips**:
1. RPD是综合最佳——100发弹链+高伤害 / RPD is the best all-rounder — 100-round belt + high damage
2. LMG必须配**握把**（减少后座力）/ LMG needs **Grip** (recoil reduction)
3. LMG换弹极慢——副手配手枪应急 / LMG reload is very slow — pistol secondary for emergencies
4. 蹲下/趴下射击精度大幅提升 / Crouch/prone for major accuracy boost
