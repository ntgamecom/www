# 怪物猎人：荒野攻略 / Monster Hunter Wilds Guide

《怪物猎人：荒野》是Capcom开发的开放世界狩猎动作RPG，系列最新作。你将作为一名猎人，在广阔的天堑沙原中追踪并狩猎巨型怪物，使用猎物素材打造更强的装备。本攻略涵盖武器操作、怪物弱点、配装方案和地图探索 / Monster Hunter Wilds is the latest open-world hunting action RPG by Capcom. As a hunter, track and hunt giant monsters across the vast Forbidden Lands, crafting stronger gear from your prey. This guide covers weapon controls, monster weaknesses, armor builds, and world exploration.

## 新手开局要点 / Quick Start Tips

| # | 建议 / Tip |
|---|---|
| **1** | **新手推荐武器**: 片手剑(灵活+可防御)或大锤(简单高伤害+可晕怪) / **Beginner weapons**: Sword & Shield (versatile + block) or Hammer (simple high damage + stun) |
| **2** | **吃猫饭/携带道具**: 每次出发前在营地吃猫饭加血耐上限——带满回复药G和陷阱 / Eat at canteen before every quest for HP/stamina buffs — carry max Mega Potions and traps |
| **3** | **破坏部位**: 怪物头/尾/翼可破坏——断尾后尾巴可额外剥取一次素材 / Break head/tail/wings — severed tails give one extra carve |
| **4** | **捕获 > 击杀**: 怪物瘸腿(逃跑睡觉)时用陷阱+捕获玉——捕获奖励更多素材 / Capture when limping (sleeping) — trap + tranq bombs = more rewards |
| **5** | **定期升级装备**: 每打2-3种新怪就回村升级武器/防具——不要穿着初始装打上位 / Upgrade gear every 2-3 new monsters — don't fight High Rank in starter gear |
