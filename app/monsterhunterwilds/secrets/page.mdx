## 隐藏区域 / Secret Areas

**中文：** 以下是不在地图上直接显示的区域。

**English:** These areas are not directly shown on the map.

**古代森林 / Ancient Forest**:

1. **树顶秘密营地**：在区域5的树藤爬到最高处，有一个隐藏营地和宝箱 / Climb the highest vines in Area 5 for a hidden camp and chest
2. **瀑布洞穴**：区域11瀑布后面有一个洞穴，内有一个矿点和稀有虫类 / Cave behind Area 11 waterfall, contains mining node and rare insects
3. **龙之休眠处**：区域16最深处有一个隐藏的大平台，有时会有古龙在休息 / Hidden large platform in Area 16 depths, elder dragons sometimes rest here

**大蚁塚荒地 / Wildspire Waste**:

1. **地下洞穴网络**：区域8的沙坑下面有一整个地下洞穴网络，连接区域3和区域10 / Sinkhole in Area 8 leads to underground cave network connecting Areas 3 and 10
2. **隐藏的角龙巢穴**：区域14东北角有一个被藤蔓覆盖的入口，通向角龙巢穴深处 / Vine-covered entrance in Area 14 NE corner leads to Diablos lair depths
3. **沙漠绿洲**：区域9有一个隐藏的绿洲，有独特的鱼类可以捕捉 / Hidden oasis in Area 9 with unique catchable fish

**陆珊瑚台地 / Coral Highlands**:

1. **顶层的秘密通道**：区域16的珊瑚结构中有隐藏通道通往顶层秘密区域 / Hidden passage in Area 16 coral structure leads to top secret area
2. **发光虫洞**：区域8有一个布满发光虫子的隐藏洞穴 / Area 8 hidden cave filled with glowing insects
3. **彩虹平台**：特定天气下，区域12会出现彩虹色的平台，有稀有生物出现 / Rainbow-colored platform in Area 12 appears in specific weather, rare endemic life spawns

## 稀有环境生物收集 / Rare Endemic Life Collection

**中文：** 环境生物是可以捕捉的小动物，放在房间里作为装饰。以下是最稀有的几种。

**English:** Endemic life are catchable creatures that can be placed in your room as decoration. Below are the rarest ones.

| 生物 / Creature | 位置 / Location | 出现条件 / Condition |
|---|---|---|
| 黄金猴 / Gold Monkey | 古代森林树顶 / Forest treetop | 晴天白天，极低概率 / Sunny day, very low spawn rate |
| 月光蝶 / Moonlight Butterfly | 陆珊瑚台地顶层 / Coral top area | 夜晚，上层区域 / Night time, upper area |
| 翡翠龟 / Jade Turtle | 大蚁塚荒地绿洲 / Waste oasis | 大雨天气后 / After heavy rain |
| 火焰蜥蜴 / Flame Lizard | 龙结晶之地 / Elder's Recess | 岩浆边缘区域 / Lava edge area |
| 星辰水母 / Star Jellyfish | 古代森林瀑布洞穴 / Forest waterfall cave | 夜晚，洞穴深处 / Night, deep in cave |

**捕捉技巧 / Capture Tips**:
- 使用捕虫网捕捉小型环境生物 / Use bug net for small endemic life
- 使用钓鱼竿捕捉鱼类 / Use fishing rod for fish
- 部分生物需要穿"隐身衣装"才能接近 / Some require Ghillie Mantle to approach

## 彩蛋收集 / Easter Eggs

**中文：** 怪物猎人系列的传统彩蛋。

**English:** Traditional Monster Hunter series easter eggs.

1. **猪猪**：营地的猪可以摸，摸多了会跟在你后面 / Camp pig can be petted, eventually follows you
2. **系列老猎人**：基地有个NPC声称自己从初代就开始狩猎 / NPC in base claims to have hunted since the first game
3. **料理长猫**：料理长的动作致敬了系列经典的猫厨 / Chef's moves reference classic series cooking animations
4. **随从猫的装备**：随从猫可以装备系列前作的经典外观 / Palico can equip classic series appearances
5. **壁画**：基地墙壁上有系列历代怪物的壁画 / Base walls have murals of monsters from previous games
6. **隐藏对话**：特定武器装备时和NPC对话有特殊台词 / Special NPC dialogue when equipping specific weapons
