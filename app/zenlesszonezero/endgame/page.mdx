## 终局内容概述 / Endgame Overview

当玩家完成主线剧情到达绳网等级35以上后，即可挑战绝区零的终局内容。终局玩法提供最高难度的挑战和最丰厚的奖励，是检验队伍强度的终极场所。

After completing the main story and reaching Inter-Knot Level 35+, players can tackle ZZZ's endgame content. Endgame modes offer the highest difficulty challenges and the richest rewards, serving as the ultimate test of team strength.

## 式舆防卫战 / Shiyu Defense

Shiyu Defense（式舆防卫战）是绝区零的核心终局模式，类似其他游戏中的深渊爬塔系统。

Shiyu Defense is ZZZ's core endgame mode, similar to the Abyss/tower systems found in other games.

**解锁条件 / Unlock Condition**: 绳网等级35+并完成指定的主线委托。（IKL 35+ and completion of specified main commissions.

**玩法规则 / Mode Rules**:
- 多层战斗关卡，每层有独立的敌人配置和时限要求 / Multiple floors with independent enemy configurations and time limits
- 每层需在限定时间内击败所有敌人 / Each floor must be cleared within a time limit
- 通关后根据表现获得评级：S/A/B/C / Rating based on performance: S/A/B/C
- 每两层休息一次，可调整队伍配置 / Rest every two floors to adjust team configs
- 敌人阵容每期轮换，考验队伍广度 / Enemy lineups rotate each season, testing team breadth

**奖励机制 / Reward System**:
- 首次通关奖励大量菲林 / First-time clearance grants substantial Polychrome
- 每期刷新重置，提供新的挑战和奖励 / Periodically resets with new challenges and rewards
- S级评价可获得额外奖励 / S-rank clear grants bonus rewards
- 主要奖励：菲林、驱动盘、材料、电池燃料等 / Main rewards: Polychrome, Drive Discs, materials, Battery Fuel

**推荐阵容强度 / Recommended Team Strength**:
- 前5层（浅层）：角色等级40+，驱动盘+9以上 / Floors 1-5 (Shallow): Characters level 40+, Drive Discs +9 or higher
- 中层（6-10层）：角色等级45+，驱动盘+12以上 / Mid floors (6-10): Characters level 45+, Drive Discs +12 or higher
- 深层（10+层）：角色等级50+，驱动盘+15，至少两支成型队伍 / Deep floors (10+): Characters level 50+, Drive Discs +15, at least two full teams

**挑战策略 / Strategy**:
- 前几层可以保留能量和喧响值给后面的层数 / Conserve energy and decibel rating in early floors
- 注意敌人的属性抗性，选择合适的属性角色 / Watch for enemy attribute resistances, pick appropriate characters
- 时间是最关键的评判标准，优先提升输出效率 / Time is the most critical rating factor, prioritize DPS efficiency
- 熟悉每层敌人的配置，规划好技能释放节奏 / Learn enemy configurations on each floor, plan skill usage rhythm

## Hollow Zero 终局 / Hollow Zero Endgame

Hollow Zero在主线解锁后是一个终局性质的空洞探索模式。它提供了可重复挑战的高难度内容。

Hollow Zero is an endgame Hollow exploration mode available after story completion. It offers repeatable high-difficulty content.

**核心玩法 / Core Gameplay**: 深入多层空洞区域，每层随机生成地图和事件。最终层由强力Boss镇守。（Descend through multiple Hollow layers with randomly generated maps and events. A powerful boss guards the final layer.

**难度选择 / Difficulty Selection**:
- **标准模式 / Standard Mode**: 适合初入终局的玩家。敌人基础强度。（Suitable for players new to endgame. Basic enemy strength.
- **困难模式 / Hard Mode**: 敌人强度提升50%，奖励增加。新增精英敌人配置。（50% increased enemy strength, better rewards. New elite enemy configurations.
- **死亡模式 / Deadly Mode**: 敌人强度提升100%，最大奖励。适合顶尖玩家。（100% increased enemy strength, maximum rewards. For top-tier players.

**特殊机制 / Special Mechanics**:
- **鸣徽系统 / Resonia System**: 探险过程中收集"鸣徽"可强化队伍。不同的鸣徽组合产生协同效果。（Collect "Resonia" throughout the run to strengthen the team. Different combinations create synergy effects.
- **路线选择 / Route Choices**: 岔路口的决定影响后续事件和奖励。（Decisions at crossroads affect subsequent events and rewards.
- **商店 / Shops**: 探索中遇到商店可用丁尼购买有用道具。（Encounter shops during exploration to buy useful items with Dennies.
- **休息点 / Rest Points**: 部分层有休息区可恢复状态。（Some layers have rest areas for status recovery.

**奖励概述 / Rewards**:
- 每周奖励上限：大量丁尼和材料 / Weekly reward cap: substantial Dennies and materials
- 成就奖励：菲林和限定称号 / Achievement rewards: Polychrome and exclusive titles
- 首次通关奖励：高级驱动盘 / First-clear rewards: high-level Drive Discs
- S级通关额外奖励 / Bonus rewards for S-rank clears

## 战意塔 / Battle Tower

战意塔（Battle Tower）是另一项高难挑战模式，玩家需要逐层攀登，面对越来越强的敌人。

Battle Tower is another high-difficulty challenge mode where players climb floor by floor against increasingly powerful enemies.

**解锁条件 / Unlock Condition**: 通过Shiyu Defense特定层数后解锁。（Unlocked after reaching specific Shiyu Defense floors.

**特点 / Features**:
- 不限时战斗，但敌人强度极高 / Untimed combat, but enemy strength is extremely high
- 层数无上限，越往上越难 / No floor cap, increasing difficulty
- 每10层为一大关，有Boss战 / Every 10 floors is a major stage with a boss fight
- 死亡后从当前大关重新开始 / Reset to current major stage upon death
- 奖励包括称号、头像框等荣誉性道具 / Rewards include titles, avatar frames, and other prestige items

**推荐策略 / Recommended Strategy**:
- 优先提升生存能力，携带治疗邦布 / Prioritize survivability, bring healing Bangboo
- 注意Boss的机制变化和新增招式 / Pay attention to boss mechanic changes and new moves
- 备有多套队伍配置应对不同敌人 / Prepare multiple team compositions for different enemies
- 不要急于求成，战意塔是长期挑战 / Do not rush, Battle Tower is a long-term challenge

## 失落虚空 / Lost Void

失落虚空（Lost Void）是近期新增的终局玩法，结合了Roguelike元素，提供高度随机化的挑战体验。

Lost Void is a recently added endgame mode combining Roguelike elements, offering highly randomized challenge experiences.

**核心机制 / Core Mechanics**:
- 随机地图和事件，每次进入都不同 / Randomized maps and events, different each entry
- 自由选择路线和强化选项 / Free route and upgrade choices
- 多种战斗风格可供选择 / Multiple combat styles to choose from
- 高回报的稀有奖励池 / High-value rare reward pool
- 永久升级系统可逐步强化 / Permanent upgrade system for gradual improvement

**解锁方式 / Unlock**: 完成Hollow Zero的特定难度挑战。（Complete specific difficulty challenges in Hollow Zero.

**玩法建议 / Playstyle Tips**:
- 优先选择与队伍属性匹配的强化 / Prioritize upgrades matching your team's attribute
- 平衡攻击和生存强化 / Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
- 利用好每层的商店和休息点 / Make good use of shops and rest points each floor
