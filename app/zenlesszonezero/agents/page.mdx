## 代理人系统 / Agent System Overview

代理人是你在空洞中战斗使用的角色。每位代理人拥有属性、专精、阵营和稀有度四个维度。目前共有5种属性、5种专精和多个阵营。

Agents are the characters you use in Hollow combat. Each Agent has four dimensions: Attribute, Specialty, Faction, and Rarity. There are currently 5 Attributes, 5 Specialties, and multiple Factions.

## 属性 / Attributes

**物理 / Physical** — 造成物理伤害，积累物理异常效果"强击"（Assault），对敌人造成额外伤害并打断动作。

Physical damage, accumulates the Assault anomaly effect, dealing bonus damage and interrupting enemy actions.

**火 / Fire** — 造成火属性伤害，积累火异常效果"灼烧"（Burn），持续造成火属性伤害。

Fire damage, accumulates the Burn anomaly effect, dealing continuous fire damage over time.

**冰 / Ice** — 造成冰属性伤害，积累冰异常效果"冻结"（Freeze），控制敌人并增加受到的暴击伤害。

Ice damage, accumulates the Freeze anomaly effect, controlling enemies and increasing critical damage taken.

**电 / Electric** — 造成电属性伤害，积累电异常效果"感电"（Shock），打断敌人并传导伤害。

Electric damage, accumulates the Shock anomaly effect, interrupting enemies and conducting damage.

**以太 / Ether** — 造成以太属性伤害，积累以太异常效果"侵蚀"（Corruption），降低敌人防御并造成持续伤害。

Ether damage, accumulates the Corruption anomaly effect, reducing enemy defense and dealing continuous damage.

## 专精 / Specialties

**强攻 / Attack** — 队伍中的主要输出角色，造成大量伤害。代表角色：艾莲、11号、朱鸢。

Primary damage dealers in the team, dealing high damage. Representative Agents: Ellen, Soldier 11, Zhu Yuan.

**击破 / Stun** — 擅长累积失衡值，快速将敌人打入失衡状态。代表角色：安比、珂蕾妲、莱卡恩。

Excel at building daze to quickly stagger enemies. Representative Agents: Anby, Koleda, Lycaon.

**支援 / Support** — 提供增益效果、治疗或能量回复。代表角色：丽娜、苍角、露西。

Provide buffs, healing, or energy regeneration. Representative Agents: Rina, Soukaku, Lucy.

**防护 / Defense** — 提供护盾和减伤，保护队伍安全。代表角色：本、赛斯。

Provide shields and damage reduction to protect the team. Representative Agents: Ben, Seth.

**异常 / Anomaly** — 擅长积累属性异常状态，触发异常反应。代表角色：格莉丝、派派、简。

Excel at building attribute anomaly status to trigger anomaly effects. Representative Agents: Grace, Piper, Jane.

## 培养优先级 / Leveling Priority

### S级代理人 / S-Rank Agents

**艾莲 / Ellen**: 当前版本最强冰属性输出，推荐优先培养。核心技提升暴击伤害，技能倍率极高。

Currently the strongest Ice DPS. Recommended for priority investment. Core skill boosts crit damage with high skill multipliers.

**朱鸢 / Zhu Yuan**: 以太属性强力输出，适合与击破角色配合打连携爆发。

Powerful Ether DPS, excels with Stun agents for chain attack burst.

**格莉丝 / Grace**: 异常流核心，电属性异常积累效率极高。

Anomaly team core, extremely high Electric anomaly accumulation efficiency.

**丽娜 / Rina**: 顶级支援，全队穿透和电属性增益。

Top-tier support, provides team-wide PEN and Electric damage buffs.

**莱卡恩 / Lycaon**: 最强冰击破角色，失衡效率极高，并提供冰抗削减。

Best Ice Stun agent, very high daze efficiency with Ice RES shred.

**11号 / Soldier 11**: 火属性强攻，技能组简单直接，输出稳定。

Fire Attack agent with straightforward kit and stable damage output.

**猫又 / Nekomiya**: 物理强攻，高机动性，适合击破后的爆发输出。

Physical Attack agent with high mobility, excels in post-stun burst.

**珂蕾妲 / Koleda**: 火击破，连段速度快，失衡积累优秀。

Fire Stun agent with fast combos and excellent daze building.

**凯撒 / Caesar**: 卡吕冬之子首领，击破能力强，具备团队辅助功能。

Sons of Calydon leader, strong stun capability with team support utility.

### A级代理人推荐 / Recommended A-Rank Agents

**安比 / Anby**: 免费获取的电击破角色，培养成本低、效果好。

Free Electric Stun agent, low investment cost with great results.

**苍角 / Soukaku**: 冰队核心支援，提供冰伤和攻击力加成。

Core Ice team support, provides Ice damage and ATK buffs.

**露西 / Lucy**: 优秀的火队支援，提供攻击增益和召唤物辅助。

Excellent Fire team support providing ATK buffs and summon assistance.

**派派 / Piper**: 物理异常角色，持续输出能力强，适合异常队。

Physical Anomaly agent with strong sustained damage, great for anomaly teams.

**本 / Ben**: 防护角色，提供强力护盾和队伍减伤。

Defense agent providing strong shields and team damage reduction.

## 推荐配队思路 / Team Building Concepts

**直伤队 / Direct Damage Team**: 强攻 + 击破 + 支援。快速击破后打爆发。（Attack + Stun + Support. Burst after quick stun.

**异常队 / Anomaly Team**: 异常 + 异常 + 支援 或 异常 + 击破 + 支援。靠异常积累打伤害。（Anomaly + Anomaly + Support or Anomaly + Stun + Support. Damage through anomaly buildup.

**强攻输出位 / Main DPS Slots**: 艾莲、朱鸢、11号、猫又、比利、可琳

**击破控制位 / Stun Control Slots**: 莱卡恩、珂蕾妲、安比、凯撒、本

**支援辅助位 / Support Slots**: 丽娜、苍角、露西、妮可、安可

## 技能升级优先级 / Skill Upgrade Priority

**核心技 / Core Skill**: 最优先升级，提供核心机制和属性。（Highest priority, unlocks core mechanics and stats.

**特殊技 / EX Special**: 主要伤害或功能技能。（Primary damage or utility skill.

**终结技 / Ultimate**: 高伤害大招，资源允许时升级。（High-damage ultimate, upgrade when resources permit.

**普通攻击 / Basic Attack**: 常用输出手段，根据角色定位决定优先级。（Common attack tool, priority depends on role.

## 突破与晋升 / Ascension and Promotion

代理人达到等级上限后需要突破。突破材料可在每周Boss挑战和材料副本中获得。

Agents need ascension materials at level cap. Materials are obtained through weekly boss challenges and material stages.
