## 空洞概述 / Hollow Overview

空洞是绝区零世界观中的核心异常空间。这些扭曲现实的区域中充满了以太能量和名为"以骸"的怪物。绳匠需要引导代理人深入空洞，完成各种委托。

Hollows are the core anomalous spaces in the ZZZ world. These reality-distorting areas are filled with Ether energy and monsters called "Ethereals." Proxies must guide Agents deep into Hollows to complete various commissions.

**以太浓度 / Ether Concentration**: 空洞中弥漫着以太，长时间暴露会对普通人有危害，但绳匠和代理人拥有特殊的抗性。以太浓度越高，遇见的怪物越强，但奖励也越丰厚。

Ether permeates Hollows. Prolonged exposure is dangerous for ordinary people, but Proxies and Agents have special resistance. Higher Ether concentration means stronger monsters but better rewards.

**空洞等级 / Hollow Levels**: 不同空洞区域有不同的推荐等级。进入过高等级的空洞会导致敌人强度大幅提升。建议在推荐等级范围内挑战。（Different Hollow areas have different recommended levels. Entering over-level Hollows significantly increases enemy strength. Stick to recommended level ranges.

**空洞类型 / Hollow Types**: 主线空洞、支线空洞、Hollow Zero、限时活动空洞等。每种空洞有不同的规则和奖励。（Main story Hollows, side Hollows, Hollow Zero, limited event Hollows, etc. Each type has different rules and rewards.

## 电视探索模式 / TV Mode Exploration

电视模式是绝区零独特的空洞探索方式。探索在一个电视网格界面中进行，每个格子代表一个事件节点。

TV mode is ZZZ's unique Hollow exploration method. Exploration takes place on a TV-grid interface, where each tile represents an event node.

**蓝色格子 / Blue Tiles**: 安全区域，可看到周围信息。通常连接重要路径。站在上面可查看附近格子类型。（Safe zone, reveals information about surrounding tiles. Usually connects important paths. Standing on it reveals nearby tile types.

**红色格子 / Red Tiles**: 战斗区域，进入后触发遭遇战。击败敌人可获得经验和材料。战斗难度与空洞等级相关。（Combat zone, triggers an encounter upon entry. Defeat enemies for EXP and materials. Difficulty scales with Hollow level.

**金色格子 / Gold Tiles**: 奖励区域，可获得丁尼、材料或稀有道具。值得优先探索。部分金格子需要钥匙解锁。（Reward zone, grants Dennies, materials, or rare items. Worth prioritizing. Some gold tiles require keys to unlock.

**紫色格子 / Purple Tiles**: 精英战斗，敌人强度更高但奖励也更丰厚。可能掉落稀有驱动盘。建议在队伍状态良好时挑战。（Elite combat zone, tougher enemies but better rewards. May drop rare Drive Discs. Challenge when team is in good condition.

**绿色格子 / Green Tiles**: 恢复区域，可回复队伍生命值或降低压力。在长线探索中非常重要。（Restoration zone, recovers team HP or reduces pressure. Very important in extended explorations.

**问号格子 / Unknown Tiles**: 随机事件，可能是战斗、奖励或特殊剧情。每次探索的结果都可能不同。部分事件有选项分支。（Random event, could be combat, rewards, or special story events. Results may differ each exploration. Some events have branching choices.

**黑色格子 / Black Tiles**: 无法通行的区域，需要钥匙或其他道具解锁。门后通常是珍贵奖励或捷径。（Impassable area, requires keys or other items to unlock. Behind doors are usually precious rewards or shortcuts.

## 空洞委托 / Hollow Commissions

委托是空洞探索的主要形式，分为以下类型：

Commissions are the main form of Hollow exploration, categorized as follows:

**主线委托 / Main Commissions**: 推进游戏剧情的关键空洞探索，逐步解锁新区域和功能。通常会引导玩家学习新机制。不可重复完成。（Key Hollow explorations that advance the main story, gradually unlocking new areas and features. Usually teach new mechanics. Not repeatable.

**支线委托 / Side Commissions**: 提供额外经验和奖励的探索任务，通常来自各阵营的请求。完成后提升对应阵营的声望。部分支线有时间限制。（Exploration tasks providing extra experience and rewards, usually from faction requests. Completing them increases faction reputation. Some have time limits.

**周常委托 / Weekly Commissions**: 每周刷新的高难度探索，提供稀缺材料。建议每周至少完成一次。周常奖励包括电池燃料。（Weekly resetting high-difficulty explorations offering scarce materials. Recommended to complete at least once per week. Weekly rewards include Battery Fuel.

**随机事件 / Random Events**: 在空洞探索途中触发的遭遇，完成可获得额外奖励。有些随机事件有分支选择，影响后续发展。探索中随机出现。（Encounters triggered during Hollow exploration, offering bonus rewards upon completion. Some random events have branching choices affecting future developments. Appear randomly during exploration.

**限时活动委托 / Limited Event Commissions**: 版本活动期间的特殊探索，提供限定奖励。活动结束后无法再次体验。（Special explorations during version events, offering limited rewards. Cannot be re-experienced after the event ends.

## 空洞核心机制 / Core Hollow Mechanics

**调查点数 / Investigation Points**: 探索空洞时积累调查点数，达到指定数量可获得额外奖励。用于解锁空洞内的奖励节点。不同空洞的调查点数不共享。探索格子增加调查点数。（Accumulate investigation points during Hollow exploration. Reaching thresholds grants bonus rewards. Used to unlock reward nodes within Hollows. Points are not shared between different Hollows. Exploring tiles increases investigation points.

**压力值 / Pressure Meter**: 探索过程中，队伍会积累压力。压力过高会触发负面效果如降低输出或持续扣血。某些事件和道具可以降低压力。压力条满时队伍会受到惩罚。（The team accumulates pressure during exploration. Excessive pressure triggers negative effects like reduced damage or continuous HP loss. Certain events and items can reduce pressure. Full pressure meter triggers penalties.

**钥匙与门 / Keys and Doors**: 空洞中存在需要钥匙才能开启的门。钥匙可通过战斗或探索获得。门后通常是珍贵的奖励区域。钥匙有不同等级对应不同的门。（Hollows contain doors requiring keys to open. Keys are obtained through combat or exploration. Doors usually lead to valuable reward areas. Keys have different tiers for different doors.

**传送点 / Teleport Points**: 探索中发现的传送节点可用于快速移动。善用传送点可以节省大量时间。部分传送点需要激活才能使用。（Teleport nodes discovered during exploration allow quick travel. Using teleport points wisely can save significant time. Some teleport points need activation.

**隐藏区域 / Hidden Areas**: 某些空洞包含隐藏路径或秘密房间，需要通过特定操作或满足条件才能进入。隐藏区域通常包含稀有奖励。（Certain Hollows contain hidden paths or secret rooms that require specific actions or conditions to access. Hidden areas usually contain rare rewards.

**环境机关 / Environmental Mechanisms**: 部分空洞有可交互的环境机关，如移动平台、炸药桶和传送门。利用机关可以开辟新路径或对敌人造成伤害。（Some Hollows feature interactive environmental mechanisms like moving platforms, explosive barrels, and portals. Using mechanisms can open new paths or damage enemies.

## Hollow Zero（零号空洞） / Hollow Zero

Hollow Zero是终局空洞内容，在主线推进到一定阶段后解锁。这是一个大型随机生成的地下城式挑战。

Hollow Zero is endgame Hollow content, unlocked after reaching a certain point in the main story. It is a large-scale randomly generated dungeon-style challenge.

**特点 / Features**:
- 多层地图，逐层深入，每层都有不同的主题 / Multiple layers, progressively deeper, each with different themes
- 随机事件和路线，每次体验不同 / Randomized events and paths for varied experiences
- 强力Boss镇守底层 / Powerful bosses guard the final layers
- 稀有奖励包括驱动盘和晋升材料 / Rare rewards including Drive Discs and ascension materials

**鸣徽系统 / Resonia System**: 探索中收集鸣徽可大幅提升队伍能力。不同的鸣徽组合可以产生协同效果。鸣徽分为攻击、防御、辅助等类型。（Collect Resonia during exploration to significantly boost team capabilities. Different Resonia combinations can produce synergy effects. Resonia are categorized into attack, defense, and support types.

**难度选择 / Difficulty Options**:
- **勘探模式 / Survey Mode**: 基础难度，适合熟悉机制。敌人基础属性。（Basic difficulty, suitable for learning mechanics. Base enemy stats.
- **固守模式 / Defense Mode**: 中等难度，敌人强度提升50%。新增精英敌人。（Medium difficulty, 50% increased enemy strength. New elite enemies.
- **搏命模式 / Desperate Mode**: 高难度，敌人强度提升100%。适合挑战自我。（High difficulty, 100% increased enemy strength. For self-challenge.

**推荐队伍强度 / Recommended Team Strength**: 绳网等级35+，配备+12以上驱动盘。（IKL 35+, with +12 or higher Drive Discs.

## 探索技巧 / Exploration Tips

**路线规划 / Route Planning**: 优先走金色和蓝色格子积累资源，后期再挑战红色和紫色格子。规划最短路径到达Boss层。（Prioritize gold and blue tiles to accumulate resources early, then tackle red and purple tiles later. Plan the shortest path to the boss layer.

**压力管理 / Pressure Management**: 利用休息区和减压道具控制压力值，避免关键时刻队伍状态不佳。保留压力解除道具给紧急情况。（Use rest zones and pressure-relief items to keep pressure manageable, avoiding poor team condition at critical moments. Save pressure relief items for emergencies.

**道具准备 / Item Preparation**: 探索前在补给站准备足够的治疗和增益道具。推荐携带至少2个治疗道具。（Prepare sufficient healing and buff items at supply stations before exploration. Recommended to bring at least 2 healing items.

**地图记忆 / Map Familiarity**: 熟悉常见空洞地图布局可以更高效地规划路线。重复探索同一个空洞时，记住有效路径。（Familiarizing yourself with common Hollow map layouts allows more efficient route planning. Remember effective paths when revisiting the same Hollow.

**战斗策略 / Combat Strategy**: 在空洞中不要无谓消耗技能和能量。保留关键技能给精英怪和Boss。普通战斗尽量用普攻解决。（Do not waste skills and energy in Hollows. Reserve key skills for elite enemies and bosses. Rely on basic attacks for regular fights.

**道具使用 / Item Usage**: 治疗道具在压力高时使用效果最佳。增益道具在Boss战前使用。（Healing items are most effective when pressure is high. Use buff items before boss fights.

## 效率刷取 / Efficient Farming

推荐每日至少完成一次空洞探索获取周常奖励的进度。优先选择掉落你需要的材料的空洞区域。

It is recommended to complete at least one Hollow exploration daily for weekly reward progress. Prioritize Hollow areas that drop materials you need.

使用体力刷取材料副本的效率高于空洞自由探索。以材料副本为主，空洞探索为辅。Shiyu Defense的奖励高于普通空洞探索。

Using battery on material stages is more efficient than free Hollow exploration for materials. Prioritize material stages, supplemented by Hollow exploration. Shiyu Defense rewards are higher than regular Hollow exploration.

## 空洞玩法进阶 / Advanced Hollow Strategies

**速通技巧 / Speedrun Tips**:
- 记住地图布局，选择最短路径 / Memorize map layouts, choose shortest paths
- 跳过不必要的战斗格子（红色格子）/ Skip unnecessary combat tiles (red tiles)
- 使用钥匙开启通往Boss的捷径 / Use keys to open shortcuts to the boss
- 携带移动加速道具或鸣徽 / Carry movement speed items or Resonia

**全收集路线 / Full Completion Routes**:
- 遍历所有金色和紫色格子获取最大奖励 / Visit all gold and purple tiles for maximum rewards
- 使用以太视野发现隐藏区域 / Use Ether Vision to find hidden areas
- 完成所有随机事件获取额外奖励 / Complete all random events for bonus rewards
- 挑战精英敌人获取稀有掉落 / Challenge elite enemies for rare drops

**团队协作 / Team Coordination**: 空洞探索支持好友助战，可以借用好友的强力代理人。在面对高难内容时善用此功能。（Hollow exploration supports friend support, allowing borrowing of powerful Agents from friends. Use this feature wisely for high-difficulty content.

**空洞退出策略 / Exit Strategy**: 如果队伍状态不佳或探索不顺，可以选择提前退出。部分奖励会保留。（If team status is poor or exploration is going badly, you can exit early. Some rewards are retained.
