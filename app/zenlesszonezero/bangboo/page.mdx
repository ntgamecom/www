## 邦布系统 / Bangboo System

邦布是绝区零中独特的辅助机器人伙伴。它们跟随队伍进入空洞，在战斗中提供自动化的辅助功能。邦布不能在编队中手动操作，但会自主行动。

Bangboo are unique companion robots in ZZZ. They follow the team into Hollows and provide automated assistance during combat. Bangboo cannot be manually controlled but act autonomously.

**战斗定位 / Combat Role**: 邦布在战斗中自动进行攻击、积累异常值、提供增益或进行治疗。它们的行动与玩家控制无关，但会受战场局势影响。（Bangboo automatically attack, build anomaly, provide buffs, or heal during combat. Their actions are independent of player control but are influenced by battlefield conditions.

**获取方式 / Acquisition**: 通过邦布信号搜索获得，使用邦布券。邦布券可通过日常、活动、商店兑换获取。（Obtained through Bangboo Signal Search using Bangboo Coupons. Coupons are acquired through dailies, events, and shop exchange.

**编队位置 / Team Slot**: 邦布占据队伍中的第四个位置，但不占用代理人名额。所有队伍都可以携带一只邦布。（Bangboo occupy the fourth slot in the team, but do not take an Agent slot. All teams can bring one Bangboo.

## 注意事项 / Notes

邦布的投入产出比很高，建议每位玩家至少培养2-3只邦布以应对不同情境。邦布不会占用战斗体力，它们是纯增益存在。

Bangboo offer great value for investment. It is recommended to build at least 2-3 Bangboo for different situations. Bangboo do not consume battery; they are pure upside.

邦布的选择可以弥补队伍短板。如果队伍缺乏治疗，选择治疗邦布；如果缺乏异常积累，选择对应属性的攻击邦布。

Bangboo choice can compensate for team weaknesses. If the team lacks healing, pick Healboo; if lacking anomaly buildup, pick the matching attack Bangboo.
