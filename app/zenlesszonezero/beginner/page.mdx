## 初入新艾利都 / Welcome to New Eridu

欢迎来到绝区零的世界。本文档将帮助你快速上手，从创建角色到挑战高难内容，一步步引导你成为合格的绳匠。

Welcome to the world of Zenless Zone Zero. This guide will help you get started quickly, from character creation to challenging high-difficulty content, guiding you step by step to become a qualified Proxy.

## 开局选择 / Starting Choices

游戏开局会引导你完成序章剧情，解锁基础功能。你将首先结识来自狡兔屋的代理人，并学会如何操作战斗和探索。

The game begins with a prologue story that unlocks basic features. You will meet Agents from the Cunning Hares and learn combat and exploration mechanics.

**推荐路线 / Recommended Path**: 跟随主线任务（Chapter 1 through Chapter 3），不要跳过教程对话。主线不仅提供大量经验，还会解锁关键功能如信号搜索（抽卡）、训练场和空洞探索。

Follow the main story commission (Chapter 1 through Chapter 3). Do not skip tutorial dialogues. The main story provides substantial experience and unlocks key features such as Signal Search (gacha), Training Ground, and Hollow exploration.

**初始角色选择 / Starting Characters**: 游戏初期免费提供了狡兔屋的三名角色——妮可、安比和比利。他们都是非常实用的角色，安比更是顶级A级击破手，值得长期培养。

The game provides three Cunning Hares characters for free: Nicole, Anby, and Billy. They are all very useful, with Anby being a top-tier A-rank Stun agent worth long-term investment.

## 绳网等级 / Inter-Knot Level

绳网等级（Inter-Knot Level, IKL）是衡量你作为绳匠进度的核心指标。提升方式包括：

Inter-Knot Level (IKL) is the core progression metric. Ways to increase it include:

- **完成委托 / Complete Commissions**: 主线和支线委托是主要经验来源。（Main and side commissions are the primary source of experience.
- **探索空洞 / Explore Hollows**: 完成空洞区域的各个关卡和挑战。（Complete levels and challenges in Hollow areas.
- **收集调查点数 / Collect Investigation Points**: 在空洞中收集调查数据可获得额外经验。（Collect investigation data in Hollows for bonus experience.
- **日常任务 / Daily Tasks**: 每日登录和完成简单任务提供稳定经验。（Daily login and simple tasks provide steady experience.
- **推进剧情 / Story Progress**: 主线章节提供大量绳网经验。（Main story chapters grant substantial IKL experience.

每个绳网等级提升都会解锁新功能和奖励，包括更多体力上限、新委托区域和高级信号搜索权限。

Each IKL increase unlocks new features and rewards, including higher battery cap, new commission areas, and advanced signal search permissions.

**IKL里程碑 / IKL Milestones**:
- Lv.10: 解锁信号搜索常驻池 / Unlock Stable Signal Search
- Lv.15: 解锁驱动盘系统 / Unlock Drive Disc system
- Lv.20: 解锁困难空洞难度 / Unlock Hard Hollow difficulty
- Lv.25: 解锁训练场高级模式 / Unlock Advanced Training mode
- Lv.30: 解锁Shiyu Defense / Unlock Shiyu Defense
- Lv.35: 解锁Hollow Zero终局内容 / Unlock Hollow Zero endgame
- Lv.40: 解锁最高难度的材料副本 / Unlock highest difficulty material stages

## 信号搜索 / Signal Search (Gacha)

信号搜索是获取新代理人和邦布的主要方式。系统分为以下类型：

Signal Search is the primary method for obtaining new Agents and Bangboo. The system includes:

**独家信号 / Exclusive Signal**: 限定代理人卡池，使用加密母带。保底机制为90抽内必出S级代理人，180抽内必出当期UP角色。每次抽卡获得信号余烬，可在商店兑换道具。

Exclusive Signal: Limited Agent banner, uses Encrypted Master Tape. Pity system guarantees an S-rank Agent within 90 pulls, with a guaranteed featured character within 180 pulls. Each pull grants Signal Embers for shop exchange.

**常驻信号 / Stable Signal**: 常驻代理人卡池，使用加密母带或原装母带。保底机制与独家信号相同但角色池固定。常驻池包含多位S级和A级代理人。

Stable Signal: Standard Agent banner, uses Encrypted or Original Master Tape. Same pity system but with a fixed character pool. Includes multiple S-rank and A-rank Agents.

**邦布信号 / Bangboo Signal**: 邦布专用卡池，使用邦布券。保底更低，10抽内必出A级及以上。邦布无法通过其他方式稳定获取。

Bangboo Signal: Dedicated Bangboo banner, uses Bangboo Coupons. Lower pity, 10 pulls guarantee A-rank or higher. Bangboo cannot be reliably obtained through other means.

**新手信号 / Beginner Signal**: 新手专属池，50抽内保底出S级代理人，折扣力度大。完成后自动消失。（Beginner Signal: New player banner, guarantees S-rank within 50 pulls at a discounted rate. Disappears after completion.

## 体力系统 / Battery System

体力（称为"电池"）是进行大部分活动的消耗资源。上限随绳网等级提升而增加。

Battery is the stamina resource consumed by most activities. Cap increases with Inter-Knot Level.

- 每6分钟恢复1点 / Regenerates 1 point every 6 minutes
- 使用电池燃料可立即回复60点 / Use Battery Fuel to instantly restore 60 points
- 每日购买体力次数有限，价格递增 / Limited daily battery purchases, increasing cost
- 推荐优先用于：材料副本（BRF）、驱动盘副本、周常Boss / Priority: material stages, Drive Disc stages, weekly bosses

**体力规划建议 / Battery Planning**: 前期优先刷取角色晋升材料，中后期转向驱动盘副本。每周至少预留体力打3次周常Boss。

Early game: prioritize ascension materials. Mid-late game: shift to Drive Disc stages. Reserve battery for 3 weekly boss runs.

## 每日流程 / Daily Routine

以下是推荐的每日上线流程：

Here is the recommended daily login routine:

1. **登录签到 / Daily Login**: 领取每日登录奖励。（Claim daily login rewards.
2. **完成日常委托 / Complete Daily Commissions**: 4个简单任务获取活跃度。（4 simple tasks to earn activity points.
3. **消耗体力 / Spend Battery**: 根据需要刷取材料或驱动盘。（Farm materials or Drive Discs as needed.
4. **探索空洞 / Explore Hollows**: 至少完成一次空洞探索获取周常奖励。（Complete at least one Hollow exploration for weekly rewards.
5. **信号搜索 / Signal Search**: 每日免费单抽不要忘记。（Do not forget the daily free single pull.
6. **训练场 / Training Ground**: 练习连招和闪避技巧。（Practice combos and dodge techniques.
7. **活动查看 / Check Events**: 查看是否有正在进行中的限时活动。（Check for ongoing limited-time events.

**周常必做 / Weekly Must-Do**:
- Shiyu Defense刷新 / Shiyu Defense reset
- 周常Boss 3次 / Weekly boss 3 runs
- Hollow Zero周常奖励 / Hollow Zero weekly rewards
- 限定活动任务 / Limited event tasks

## 新手资源分配 / Beginner Resource Management

**菲林 / Polychrome**: 游戏高级货币，优先用于购买加密母带抽限定卡池。不要随意消耗在体力购买上。

Polychrome: Premium currency, prioritize buying Encrypted Master Tapes for limited banners. Do not waste on battery refills.

**丁尼 / Dennies**: 基础货币，用于角色升级、技能升级和商店购物。前期会比较紧缺，不要浪费在不必要的强化上。

Dennies: Basic currency for leveling up, skill upgrades, and shop purchases. Can be tight early on, do not waste on unnecessary upgrades.

**角色经验材料 / Character EXP Materials**: 优先升级主力输出角色至当前最高等级。（Prioritize leveling your main DPS to the current maximum level.

**技能材料 / Skill Materials**: 优先升级核心技，其次是主要输出技能。（Prioritize Core Skill upgrades, followed by main damage skills.

## 新手推荐抽卡 / Beginner Pulling Strategy

**优先目标 / Primary Target**: 限定强力S级主C角色，如艾莲或朱鸢。（Limited S-rank main DPS like Ellen or Zhu Yuan.

**避免的陷阱 / Traps to Avoid**: 不要为了A级角色投入大量母带；不要抽常驻池。（Do not spend heavily for A-rank characters; do not pull on the standard banner.

**理想开局 / Ideal Start**: 新手50抽获得任意S级主C + 安比（免费）+ 支援角色（苍角/露西）。（New player 50 pulls for any S-rank main DPS + Anby (free) + a support (Soukaku/Lucy).

## 常见新手误区 / Common Beginner Mistakes

- **不要分散资源 / Do not spread resources thin**: 集中培养一队三人，而不是平均分配给多个角色。（Focus on building one team of three rather than spreading across many characters.
- **不要忽视闪避 / Do not ignore dodging**: 完美闪避和极限闪避是核心生存技巧。（Perfect dodge and extreme evasion are core survival skills.
- **不要跳过空洞事件 / Do not skip Hollow events**: 空洞中的随机事件奖励丰厚。（Random events in Hollows offer great rewards.
- **不要浪费母带 / Do not waste Master Tapes**: 保留母带用于你真正想要的限定角色。（Save Master Tapes for limited characters you truly want.
- **不要忽略邦布 / Do not neglect Bangboo**: 邦布提供稳定的战斗增益，值得培养。（Bangboo provide consistent combat benefits and are worth leveling.
- **不要购买体力过度 / Do not overbuy battery**: 菲林最好留给抽卡，适度购买体力即可。（Save Polychrome for gacha, buy battery only in moderation.

From here, you can progress toward endgame content. Happy exploring.
