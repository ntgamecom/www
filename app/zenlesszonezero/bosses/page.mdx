## 首领战概述 / Boss Fight Overview

绝区零中的首领战是游戏的核心挑战内容。每位首领拥有独特的攻击模式和机制，需要玩家熟练掌握闪避、招架和输出技巧才能应对。

Boss fights in ZZZ are the core challenge content. Each boss has unique attack patterns and mechanics, requiring players to master dodge, parry, and damage output techniques.

**攻击提示 / Attack Cues**:
- **黄色闪光 / Yellow Flash**: 可招架的攻击，使用招架支援可触发强力反击。（Parriable attack, using Parry Assist triggers a powerful counter.
- **红色闪光 / Red Flash**: 不可招架的攻击，必须使用闪避或回避支援规避。（Unparriable attack, must be avoided using dodge or Evasive Assist.
- **无闪光攻击 / No Flash**: 普通攻击，可格挡或闪避。（Normal attack, can be blocked or dodged.

**Boss战通用原则 / Universal Boss Fight Principles**:
- 观察起手动作：每个Boss的攻击前都有独特的动作提示 / Observe wind-up animations: each boss has unique telegraphs
- 不要贪刀：该闪避时果断闪避，生存第一 / Do not be greedy: dodge when needed, survival comes first
- 利用失衡窗口：在Boss失衡时打满伤害 / Utilize stagger windows: maximize damage when boss is down
- 团队配合：合理切换角色，利用支援系统 / Team coordination: swap wisely, use assist system

## 死路屠夫 / Dead-End Butcher

死路屠夫是玩家在主线中遇到的首个强大Boss，也是众多新手玩家的噩梦。它手持巨大的屠刀，攻击范围广、伤害高。

The Dead-End Butcher is the first powerful boss encountered in the main story and a nightmare for many new players. It wields a massive cleaver with wide attack range and high damage.

**攻击模式 / Attack Patterns**:
- **横斩 / Horizontal Slash**: 大范围横扫，可招架。会连续挥砍2-3次。（Wide horizontal sweep, can be parried. Performs 2-3 consecutive slashes.
- **下劈 / Overhead Slam**: 垂直下劈，伤害极高，需闪避。落地后产生冲击波。（Vertical overhead slam, extremely high damage, requires dodging. Creates a shockwave on impact.
- **旋转攻击 / Spin Attack**: 连续旋转挥砍，多段伤害。招架成功后可以大幅积累失衡。（Continuous spinning slash with multi-hit damage. Successful parry builds significant daze.
- **冲刺斩 / Dash Slash**: 快速接近后斩击。有黄光和红光两种变体。（Quick approach followed by a slash. Has both yellow flash and red flash variants.
- **震地 / Ground Pound**: 重击地面产生范围冲击波，需要闪避。（Slams the ground creating an AoE shockwave, requires dodging.

**攻略要点 / Strategy Tips**:
- 利用黄色闪光攻击进行招架反击积累失衡值 / Use yellow flash attacks for parry counters to build daze
- 红色闪光攻击必须闪避，无法招架 / Red flash attacks must be dodged, cannot be parried
- Boss倒地时爆发全部技能和连携攻击 / Unload all skills and chain attacks when the boss is down
- 推荐携带击破角色快速积累失衡 / Bring a Stun character for quick daze accumulation
- Boss血量低于30%时会进入狂暴状态，攻击频率加快 / Boss enters rage mode below 30% HP, attack frequency increases
- 注意地面裂缝指示，预判Boss的移动方向 / Watch ground crack indicators to predict boss movement

## 尼尼微 / Nineveh

尼尼微是探索Hollow Zero时遇到的强大古代以骸，拥有多种形态和阶段。它是目前游戏中最具挑战性的Boss之一。

Nineveh is a powerful ancient Ethereal encountered during Hollow Zero exploration, with multiple forms and phases. It is one of the most challenging bosses in the game.

**阶段一 / Phase 1 — 以太支配者 / Ether Dominator**: 远程攻击模式，发射以太弹幕和召唤小型以骸。保持移动躲避弹幕。（Ranged attack mode, fires Ether barrages and summons small Ethereals. Keep moving to dodge barrages.

**阶段二 / Phase 2 — 毁灭形态 / Destruction Form**: 近战攻击模式，使用触手进行物理攻击。触手攻击范围大但前摇明显。（Melee attack mode, uses tentacles for physical attacks. Tentacle attacks have wide range but clear wind-ups.

**阶段三 / Phase 3 — 终末形态 / Final Form**: 同时使用远程和近战攻击，释放全屏大范围技能。需要极高的反应速度和操作精度。（Uses both ranged and melee attacks, unleashing full-screen area skills. Requires extremely high reaction speed and precision.

**攻略要点 / Strategy Tips**:
- 阶段转换时Boss会有短暂僵直，是输出窗口 / Boss has brief vulnerability during phase transitions
- 召唤的小以骸优先清理，避免被围攻 / Clear summoned Ethereals first to avoid being overwhelmed
- 携带以太或冰属性角色克制Boss / Bring Ether or Ice characters for type advantage
- 完美闪避Boss的红光技能是存活关键 / Perfect dodging red attacks is crucial for survival
- 阶段三的全屏攻击可以通过特定位置规避 / Full-screen attacks in Phase 3 can be avoided by positioning
- 携带治疗邦布可以显著降低攻略难度 / Bringing Healboo significantly reduces difficulty

## 剧情首领 / Story Bosses

**最初的以骸 / The First Ethereal**: 序章遭遇的Boss，基础攻击模式，作为教学战。攻击方式单一，容易应对。（Encountered in the prologue, basic attack patterns, serves as a tutorial fight. Simple attack patterns, easy to handle.

**双生以骸 / Twin Ethereals**: 两只同时出现的以骸，一只近战一只远程，需要同时管理两只的仇恨。推荐先集中火力消灭远程的那只。（Two Ethereals appearing simultaneously, one melee and one ranged. Requires managing aggro for both. Recommended to focus down the ranged one first.

**机械以骸 / Mechanical Ethereal**: 结合机械与以骸特征的独特Boss，拥有护甲机制。需要先破坏护甲才能造成有效伤害。使用重击和特殊技破甲效率高。（A unique boss combining machine and Ethereal traits, featuring an armor mechanic. Armor must be broken first to deal effective damage. Heavy attacks and skills break armor efficiently.

**祸潮 / Calamity Surge**: 特殊的群体性以骸涌现象，需要在一波波敌人中存活。考验玩家的持续战斗能力和走位。群体攻击角色在此战表现优异。（A special Ethereal surge phenomenon requiring survival through waves of enemies. Tests sustained combat ability and positioning. AoE characters excel here.

**都市化身 / Urban Manifestation**: 在特定区域出现的巨型以骸，与城市环境融合。战斗场景会发生变化，可利用环境物件。（A giant Ethereal appearing in specific areas, fused with the urban environment. The battlefield changes during the fight, environmental objects can be used.

## 周常Boss / Weekly Bosses

每周可挑战3次周常Boss，使用体力领取奖励。周常Boss掉落角色技能突破所需的稀缺材料。

Weekly bosses can be challenged 3 times per week, consuming battery for rewards. They drop scarce materials needed for skill breakthroughs.

**死路屠夫（强化版）/ Enhanced Dead-End Butcher**: 拥有更多的攻击组合和更高的伤害。动作速度提升，新增连段。攻击欲望更强。（Has more attack combos and higher damage. Faster attacks with new combo strings. More aggressive AI.

**提提乌斯 / Tithingus**: 冰属性以骸Boss，会冻结场地的部分区域。需要不断移动避免被冻住。火属性角色可以融化冰面。（Ice-element Ethereal boss that freezes parts of the arena. Requires constant movement to avoid being frozen. Fire characters can melt ice surfaces.

**哈提 / Hati**: 迅捷型的以骸Boss，速度极快。攻击节奏紧凑，留给玩家的反应时间很短。推荐使用闪避流打法。（A swift Ethereal boss with extremely high speed. Compact attack rhythm with very short reaction windows. Dodge-focused playstyle recommended.

**摩罗 / Moro**: 重型以骸Boss，防御高但动作较慢。需要耐心积累失衡，不要急于输出。异常队对付摩罗效果不错。（A heavy Ethereal boss with high defense but slower movements. Patiently build daze, do not rush damage. Anomaly teams work well against Moro.

**工地守护者 / Construction Site Guardian**: 出现在白祇重工相关委托中。使用重型机械臂进行攻击。弱点在背后的动力核心。（Appears in Belobog-related commissions. Attacks with heavy mechanical arms. Weak point is the power core on its back.

**遗迹守卫 / Ruins Guardian**: 出现在维多利亚家政相关委托中。以太属性的古代造物。攻击带有侵蚀效果。携带冰属性角色有优势。（Appears in Victoria Housekeeping-related commissions. An ancient Ether-attribute construct. Attacks inflict Corruption. Ice characters have an advantage.

**荒漠死神 / Desert Reaper**: 出现在卡吕冬之子相关委托中。在开阔地形中高速移动攻击。需要玩家有良好的追击能力。（Appears in Sons of Calydon-related commissions. High-speed mobile attacks in open terrain. Requires good pursuit ability from players.

## 失衡与输出窗口 / Daze and Damage Windows

所有Boss战的核心思路一致：积累失衡值，在失衡状态时爆发输出。

The core strategy for all boss fights is the same: build daze, then burst during the stagger window.

**失衡值积累 / Daze Building**: 击破角色（安比、珂蕾妲、莱卡恩）的E技能和连招可以有效积累失衡。每个Boss对失衡值的抗性不同，某些Boss需要更多攻击才能失衡。（Stun characters (Anby, Koleda, Lycaon) excel at building daze with their E skills and combos. Each boss has different daze resistance, some requiring more hits to stagger.

**失衡持续时间 / Daze Duration**: 基础约4秒，使用连携攻击可延长至6-7秒。（Base duration is about 4 seconds, extendable to 6-7 seconds with Chain Attacks.

**爆发准备 / Burst Preparation**: 在Boss即将失衡时，确保技能CD已转好、能量充足。切换至主C角色准备爆发。（When the boss is about to stagger, ensure skills are off cooldown and energy is ready. Switch to main DPS for burst.

## 每日Boss挑战规划 / Daily Boss Planning

建议每日至少挑战一次周常Boss（如果体力允许）。周一刷新后优先完成三次周常Boss以获取本周的技能材料。

It is recommended to challenge weekly bosses at least once daily if battery permits. After Monday reset, prioritize three weekly boss runs for the week's skill materials.

主线Boss战不可重复挑战，但通关后可观看剧情回顾。周常Boss和Hollow Zero Boss是日常刷取的主要对象。

Story bosses cannot be re-challenged but can be reviewed through story replay. Weekly bosses and Hollow Zero bosses are the main farming targets.

## Boss战注意事项 / Boss Fight Considerations

- 进入Boss战前确认队伍状态和能量 / Check team status and energy before engaging
- 熟悉Boss的攻击节奏，不要急于输出 / Learn the boss attack rhythm, do not rush damage
- 合理使用道具，治疗和增益在关键时刻使用 / Use items wisely, heals and buffs at critical moments
- 多练习完美闪避和招架支援 / Practice perfect dodge and parry assist often
- 观察Boss血量下方的状态图标 / Watch the status icons below the boss HP bar
- 某些Boss有隐藏机制，需要多次挑战才能完全掌握 / Some bosses have hidden mechanics requiring multiple attempts to fully understand
