## 游戏简介 / Game Overview

绝区零（Zenless Zone Zero）是一款由米哈游开发的都市奇幻动作角色扮演游戏。故事发生在被神秘灾害"空洞"侵蚀的未来都市新艾利都。玩家扮演"绳匠"（Proxy），引导代理人探索空洞、对抗名为"以骸"的怪物，并揭开世界背后的真相。

Zenless Zone Zero is an urban fantasy action RPG developed by miHoYo (HoYoverse). Set in the futuristic city of New Eridu, which is plagued by mysterious disasters called "Hollows," players take on the role of a Proxy who guides Agents through Hollows to combat Ethereal monsters and uncover the truth behind the world.

## 故事背景 / Story Setting

新艾利都是人类最后的都市，以抵御空洞灾害为核心进行建设。空洞是一种扭曲现实的异常空间，其中充斥着名为"以骸"的敌对生物。玩家扮演的是一名可自由穿梭于空洞的绳匠，通过经营"Random Play"录像店作为掩护，承接来自各方的委托。

New Eridu is humanity's last city, built around resisting Hollow disasters. A Hollow is an anomalous space that distorts reality, filled with hostile creatures called "Ethereals." The player is a Proxy who can freely traverse Hollows, operating the "Random Play" video store as a front while taking on commissions from various factions.

**历史背景 / Historical Background**: 在称为"旧都陷落"的灾难后，无数城市被空洞吞噬。新艾利都成为最后的避难所。城市依靠名为"星盾"的装置抵御空洞的蔓延，但空洞仍在不断扩张。

After a cataclysm known as "The Fall of the Old Capital," countless cities were consumed by Hollows. New Eridu became the last refuge. The city relies on a device called "Star Shield" to resist the spread of Hollows, but the Hollows continue to expand.

## 关键特色 / Key Features

**实时动作战斗 / Real-time Action Combat**
玩家操控三名代理人组成的队伍，在空洞中进行高速战斗。核心机制包括闪避、极限闪避、招架支援、连携攻击和终结技。

Players control a team of three Agents in high-speed combat within Hollows. Core mechanics include dodging, perfect dodging, parry assists, chain attacks, and ultimate skills.

**电视模式探索 / TV Mode Exploration**
在空洞中，探索通过类似电视网格的界面进行。玩家需要规划路线、避开陷阱、收集资源并面对随机事件。

Inside Hollows, exploration takes place through a TV-grid interface. Players must plan routes, avoid traps, collect resources, and face random events.

**代理人系统 / Agent System**
可招募超过30名代理人，每位拥有独特的属性（物理、火、冰、电、以太）、专精（强攻、击破、支援、防护、异常）和阵营。

Over 30 Agents can be recruited, each with unique attributes (Physical, Fire, Ice, Electric, Ether), specialties (Attack, Stun, Support, Defense, Anomaly), and faction affiliations.

**抽卡系统 / Gacha System**: 通过信号搜索获取新代理人和邦布。分为独家信号（限定）、常驻信号（标准）、邦布信号和初学者信号。

Signal Search is the gacha system for obtaining new Agents and Bangboo. Types include Exclusive (limited), Stable (standard), Bangboo, and Beginner signals.

## 绳匠系统 / Proxy System

作为绳匠（Proxy），玩家拥有自己的等级系统——**绳网等级（Inter-Knot Level）**。提升等级可解锁新的委托、区域和功能。玩家通过完成委托、探索空洞和推进剧情获取绳网经验。

As a Proxy, players have their own leveling system called the **Inter-Knot Level**. Leveling up unlocks new commissions, areas, and features. Players gain Inter-Knot experience by completing commissions, exploring Hollows, and advancing the story.

**绳网声望 / Inter-Knot Reputation**: 不同阵营拥有独立的声望系统。提升声望可解锁阵营商店中的独特奖励。（Different factions have independent reputation systems. Increasing reputation unlocks unique rewards in faction shops.

**玩家账号等级 / Account Level**: 玩家的整体进度由冒险等级或类似系统衡量，影响可参与的内容。（Overall account progress is measured by the Adventure Level system, affecting accessible content.
