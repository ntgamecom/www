# 刺客信条：影 / Assassin's Creed Shadows

{/* 游戏概览 */}

## 游戏概览 / Game Overview

**刺客信条：影**（Assassin's Creed Shadows）是由育碧（Ubisoft）开发的最新刺客信条系列作品。本作将舞台设定在16世纪的日本战国时代，玩家将体验两位截然不同的主人公交织的命运。

*Assassin's Creed Shadows is the latest installment in the Assassin's Creed series developed by Ubisoft. Set in 16th-century Japan's Sengoku period, players experience the intertwined fates of two very different protagonists.*

### 时代背景 / Historical Setting

故事发生在1579年的日本，正值织田信长崛起、战国乱世达到顶峰的时代。玩家将穿梭于京都、大阪、伊贺等历史名城，见证忍者与武士的碰撞，历史与传说的交融。

*The story takes place in 1579 Japan, at the peak of the Sengoku period with Oda Nobunaga's rise. Players travel through historic cities like Kyoto, Osaka, and Iga, witnessing the clash of ninja and samurai, where history and legend intertwine.*

### 双主角系统 / Dual Protagonists

- **奈绪江（Naoe）** — 伊贺流忍者，擅长潜行与暗杀，灵活敏捷，精通刺客信条的传统技艺
  *Naoe — Iga-style shinobi, excels in stealth and assassination, agile and swift, master of traditional Assassin arts*
- **弥助（Yasuke）** — 来自非洲的武士，力量型战士，擅长正面战斗与重武器
  *Yasuke — A samurai from Africa, power-type fighter, excels in direct combat and heavy weapons*
