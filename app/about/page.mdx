# 关于 NTGame / About NTGame

**NTGame** 是一个专注于游戏攻略的双语站点，于 2026 年创立。我们的目标是为全球华语和英语玩家提供专业、免费、易于理解的游戏攻略。

NTGame is a bilingual game guide site founded in 2026. Our goal is to provide professional, free, and easy-to-understand game guides for both Chinese and English-speaking players worldwide.

## 为什么选择 NTGame / Why NTGame?

### 双语优势 / Bilingual Advantage
每篇攻略同时提供中文和英文内容。无论您是中文母语者还是英语玩家，都能在这里找到适合您的攻略内容。双语对照也让您在学习游戏的同时提升语言能力。

Every guide is written in both Chinese and English. Whether you're a native Chinese speaker or an English player, you'll find content that suits you. The side-by-side format also helps you learn while you play.

### 全面覆盖 / Comprehensive Coverage
- **71+ 款热门游戏**，覆盖 12 大类型 / 71+ popular games across 12 genres
- **每款游戏 10+ 章节**，从入门到精通 / 10+ chapters per game, from beginner to mastery
- **持续更新**，紧跟游戏版本变化 / Continuously updated with game patches
- **最新大作**第一时间跟进攻略 / New releases covered promptly

### 内容免费 / Always Free
NTGame 的所有攻略内容完全免费。我们通过 Google AdSense 广告维持运营，绝不向用户收费。

All NTGame guide content is completely free. We are supported by Google AdSense ads and will never charge users.

NTGame is run by a group of passionate gamers. Our team members have years of gaming experience across AAA titles and indie gems. We believe great guides help more people enjoy gaming.
