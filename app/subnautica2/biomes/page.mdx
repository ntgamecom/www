# 生态区与探索 / Biomes & Exploration

深海迷航2的海洋世界分为多个风格迥异的生态区，从阳光斑驳的浅滩到永夜深渊，每个区域都有独特的生物、资源和危险 / Subnautica 2's ocean is divided into diverse biomes, from sun-dappled shallows to eternal abyssal darkness. Each zone has unique creatures, resources, and dangers.

## 浅层生态区 / Shallow Biomes

### 珊瑚浅滩 / Coral Shallows

**特点 / Features**: 你的出生地。阳光充足，资源丰富，几乎无威胁 / Your spawn area. Sunlit, resource-rich, nearly threat-free.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 探索要点 / Tips |
|------|------|------|
| 小尾鱼、孔鱼、吊鱼 / Peepers, Holehole, Hanging Fish | 钛、石英、铜、金 / Ti, Quartz, Cu, Gold | 最初基地的理想选址——安全且靠近所有开端材料 / Ideal first base location — safe and close to all starter materials |

### 海藻森林 / Kelp Forest

**特点 / Features**: 高耸的海藻树林，能见度中等，隐藏着不少资源 / Towering kelp stalks, moderate visibility, many hidden resources.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 孔鱼、罢工鱼、海藻龙 / Holehole, Boomerang, Kelp Dragon | 海藻块、酸蘑菇、铜 / Creepvine, Acid Mushroom, Copper | 海藻龙——好奇但一般不具有攻击性，被惹到会反击 / Kelp Dragon — curious but not aggressive unless provoked |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 海藻林是早期获取**润滑油**的唯一来源——砍伐海藻块获得 / Kelp Forest is your only early source of **Lubricant** — harvest Creepvine
- 注意海藻龙的位置——它们通常在高处游荡，低层采集相对安全 / Watch Kelp Dragon positions — they usually patrol high, low-level gathering is safe

---

## 中层生态区 / Mid-depth Biomes

### 红草高原 / Red Grass Plateau

**特点 / Features**: 红色海草覆盖的广阔平原，大量砂岩可开采 / Vast plains covered in red grass, abundant sandstone outcrops.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 兔鱼、蹦跳鱼、冲击鱼 / Rabbit Fish, Boomerang, Crashfish | 金、银、石灰岩 / Gold, Silver, Limestone | 冲击鱼——藏在砂岩中，靠近时会冲出爆炸 / Crashfish — hides in sandstone, charges and explodes when approached |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 冲击鱼的爆炸伤害极高——听到嘶嘶声立刻后退 / Crashfish explosions deal heavy damage — back off on hearing the hiss
- 红草高原是中期基地的理想选址——深度适中，资源丰富 / Red Grass Plateau is ideal for a mid-game base — moderate depth, rich resources

### 漂浮岛屿 / Floating Islands

**特点 / Features**: 巨大的浮空岩石，表面有陆地植物和淡水来源 / Giant floating rock formations with surface plants and fresh water sources.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 天翼鱼、陆行蟹 / Skyray, Land Crab | 稀有植物种子、钻石 / Rare plant seeds, Diamond | 陆行蟹——速度快但可以避开 / Land Crab — fast but avoidable |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 岛屿表面有**淡水**可以直接采集——前期解决缺水问题的重要地点 / Fresh **water** is available on the surface — key early source for hydration
- 部分稀有植物种子只能在这里找到，建议带种植箱样本回去 / Some rare plant seeds are exclusive here — bring samples for your growbed

### 水母洞穴 / Jellyshroom Caves

**特点 / Features**: 巨大的发光水母蘑菇覆盖的洞穴系统，光线呈蓝色/紫色 / Cave system covered in giant glowing jellyshrooms, blue/purple light.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 洞穴蟹、蓝光水母 / Cave Crab, Blue Jellyray | 磁铁矿、锂、金 / Magnetite, Lithium, Gold | 洞穴蟹——躲在暗处伏击，注意观察地形 / Cave Crab — ambush predator, watch dark corners |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- **磁铁矿的唯一早期来源**——推进器升级和多个关键配方需要 / **Only early source of Magnetite** — needed for Seaglide upgrades and key recipes
- 洞穴结构复杂，建议沿途放置信标标记路径 / Cave structure is complex — place beacons along your path

## 深层生态区 / Deep Biomes

### 血藻区 / Blood Kelp Zone

**特点 / Features**: 暗红色的海藻，诡异的氛围，深不见底的海沟 / Dark red kelp, eerie atmosphere, bottomless trenches.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 血藻龙、巨齿鱼 / Blood Kelp Dragon, Sawtooth | 钻石、锂 / Diamond, Lithium | 血藻龙——领地性极强，进入即被追击 / Blood Kelp Dragon — extremely territorial, attacks on sight |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 这个区域只建议在有了**深潜者号**之后再来 / Only visit this zone after acquiring the **Deep Diver** vehicle
- 血藻龙的速度很快——不要直线逃跑，利用地形绕圈 / Blood Kelp Dragon is fast — don't run in straight lines, use terrain to circle around
- 靠近海底行进可以减少引起注意的概率 / Staying close to the seafloor reduces detection chance

### 热液平原 / Thermal Plains

**特点 / Features**: 大量热液喷口，温暖的水流，独特的矿物沉积 / Numerous thermal vents, warm currents, unique mineral deposits.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 热液虫、熔岩蜥 / Thermal Worm, Lava Lizard | 蓝晶石、硫、镎 / Kyanite, Sulfur, Uraninite | 热液喷口——近距离受到灼烧伤害 / Thermal vents — burn damage at close range |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 热液喷口附近的温度极高——**强化潜水服**或**温度模块**是必需的 / Temperatures near vents are extreme — **Reinforced Dive Suit** or **Thermal Module** is essential
- 热液区可以利用**热发电模块**为载具充电 / Use **Thermal Charge Module** to recharge vehicles in this zone
- 蓝晶石通常出现在最深处的喷口附近 / Kyanite is usually near the deepest vents

---

## 极深层生态区 / Extreme Depth Biomes

### 深海大裂谷 / Deep Grand Ravine

**特点 / Features**: 一条贯穿海床的巨大裂谷，通往更深处的通道 / A massive chasm cutting through the seafloor, gateway to the deepest areas.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 深渊潜伏者、灯笼鱼 / Abyss Lurker, Lantern Fish | 蓝晶石、稀有碎片 / Kyanite, Rare fragments | 深渊潜伏者——在黑暗中伏击，注意声音提示 / Abyss Lurker — ambushes in darkness, listen for audio cues |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 这是通往失落之河的主要通道——标记入口位置 / This is the main path to the Lost River — mark the entrance
- 裂谷壁上有大量可开采矿物——沿途收集 / Abundant mineable minerals on the ravine walls — gather as you descend

### 失落之河 / Lost River

**特点 / Features**: 地下河流系统，古老文明的遗迹所在地 / Underground river system, site of ancient precursor structures.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 幽灵利维坦、河鳗 / Ghost Leviathan, River Eel | 蓝晶石、剧情关键物 / Kyanite, story-critical items | 幽灵利维坦——**极度危险**，建议绕行 / Ghost Leviathan — **extremely dangerous**, recommend avoidance |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- 失落之河中有数个**先驱者设施**，是推进剧情必须访问的地点 / The Lost River contains multiple **Precursor facilities** essential for story progression
- 这里的绿色毒水会持续造成伤害——注意氧气和血量管理 / Green toxic water deals continuous damage — watch your O2 and HP
- **独眼巨人号2.0**是在此区域长期探索的最佳选择 / The **Cyclops 2.0** is the best choice for extended exploration here

### 熔岩区 / Lava Zone

**特点 / Features**: 星球的地壳深处，岩浆和极端高温，最终区域 / Deep within the planet's crust, magma and extreme heat, the final zone.

| 生物 / Creatures | 资源 / Resources | 危险 / Threats |
|------|------|------|
| 熔岩利维坦、岩浆蟹 / Lava Leviathan, Magma Crab | 终极材料、核心剧情件 / Endgame materials, story core items | 熔岩利维坦——**全游戏最危险的生物** / Lava Leviathan — **most dangerous creature in the game** |

**探索要点 / Tips**:
- **进入这里之前确保所有装备满级**——没有回头路 / **Max out all gear before entering** — there's no going back
- 温度极高——强化潜水服+热能抗性模块缺一不可 / Extreme temperature — Reinforced Dive Suit + Thermal Resist module are mandatory
- 推荐载具：**独眼巨人号2.0**（满深度升级）/ Recommended vehicle: **Cyclops 2.0** (max depth upgrade)
- 熔岩利维坦不会进入狭窄通道——利用地形躲避 / Lava Leviathans don't enter narrow passages — use terrain to evade

## 探索通则 / General Exploration Tips

| 建议 / Tip | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|
| **信标是你的命 / Beacons Save Lives** | 在基地、入口、资源点和危险区放置不同颜色的信标进行标记 / Mark base, entrances, resource spots, and danger zones with different colored beacons |
| **先探浅后探深 / Shallow First** | 不要急着深入——浅层区域的资源和蓝图是深潜的基础 / Don't rush deep — shallow zone resources and blueprints are the foundation for deep diving |
| **夜间减少外出 / Avoid Night Travel** | 夜间能见度极低，大型捕食者更活跃 / Visibility drops drastically at night, large predators are more active |
| **永远备好退路 / Always Have an Exit** | 探索洞穴时记住来路——氧气快耗尽时慌乱是致命错误 / Remember your route in caves — panicking when O2 runs low is fatal |
| **多人联机分工 / Co-op Roles** | 一人驾驶、一人导航、一人收集——联机时探索效率远高于单人 / One drives, one navigates, one gathers — co-op exploration is far more efficient than solo |
