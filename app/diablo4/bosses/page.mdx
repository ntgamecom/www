## 主线Boss攻略 / Story Boss Guides

### 序章Boss：阿斯塔洛 / Prologue Boss: Astaroth

**位置**: 破碎群峰，梅里巖教堂 / Location: Fractured Peaks, Melivere Monastery
**推荐等级**: 12-15 / Recommended Level: 12-15
**Boss类型**: 火焰恶魔 / Type: Fire Demon

**阶段一(100%-60%HP)**: 阿斯塔洛使用**火焰冲击**——地面出现圆形火焰AOE，持续3秒。**冲锋**——直线冲刺，需要横向躲避。打法：保持移动，不要在火焰AOE范围内停留超过1秒 / Phase 1: Uses **Flame Impact** — circular fire AoE on ground, 3-sec duration. **Charge** — linear dash, dodge sideways. Move constantly, don't stay in fire AoE more than 1 second.

**阶段二(60%-20%HP)**: 增加**陨石召唤**——天空落下大范围火球。场地出现多个**地狱裂隙**——喷出火焰。打法：站在裂隙之间的空隙输出，优先清理裂隙 / Phase 2: Adds **Meteor Summon** — large fireball from sky. Multiple **Hell Rifts** appear — spew flames. Stand in gaps between rifts, prioritize destroying rifts.

**阶段三(20%-0%HP)**: 全屏火焰AOE——阿斯塔洛释放大范围火焰新星。场地缩小。打法：在Boss脚下输出——安全区域在Boss附近。火焰新星前有1秒蓄力——看到蓄力立刻移动到Boss背后 / Phase 3: Full-screen fire nova — large AoE. Arena shrinks. Stay close to Boss — safe zone is near him. Fire nova has 1-second charge — move behind boss immediately when you see the charge.

### 终章Boss：莉莉丝 / Final Boss: Lilith

**位置**: 烈焰地狱，净化神殿 / Location: Hell, Purification Temple
**推荐等级**: 60+ / Recommended Level: 60+
**Boss类型**: 恶魔 / Type: Demon (Unique)

**阶段一(100%-60%HP)**: 莉莉丝使用**血之波动**——地面血浪波。**暗影诅咒**——被击中减少50%伤害输出。**血翅冲刺**——连续冲刺三次。核心打法：全程不停移动，利用位移技能躲避暗影诅咒。远程输出职业有优势 / Phase 1: **Blood Wave** — ground blood wave. **Shadow Curse** — hit reduces damage output by 50%. **Blood Wing Dash** — triple dash. Keep moving constantly, use mobility to dodge Shadow Curse. Ranged classes have advantage.

**阶段二(60%-30%HP)**: 莉莉丝召唤**血之星**——全场多个旋转血球——接触受到大量伤害。增加**分裂血波**——从场地中心向四面扩散。打法：关注血之星的位置移动，利用翻滚无敌帧穿越血波。推荐带**破盾技能** / Phase 2: Summons **Blood Stars** — rotating blood orbs — massive contact damage. Adds **Splitting Blood Wave** — radial spread from center. Watch blood star positions, use dodge iframes to cross waves. Recommended to bring **shield break skills**.

**阶段三(30%-0%HP)**: **阶段二技能加强**——血之星数量翻倍。莉莉丝释放**终焉血爆**——场地范围逐渐缩小。打法：极限输出阶段——所有大招和药水全开。不要在乎走位之外的事情——活着就是输出 / Phase 3: **Enhanced Phase 2** — double blood stars. Lilith unleashes **Final Blood Explosion** — arena shrinks. Maximum DPS phase — use all cooldowns and potions. Don't worry about anything beyond positioning — staying alive is DPS.

## 世界Boss攻略 / World Boss Strategies

### 贪婪 / Greed

**刷新位置**: 破碎群峰/干燥平原/凯基斯坦随机 / Spawn: Random in Fractured Peaks, Dry Steppes, or Kehjistan
**刷新间隔**: 每6小时 / Spawn Interval: Every 6 hours
**推荐等级**: 世界3: 55+ / 世界4: 75+

**核心机制**: 贪婪每20秒释放**黄金波**——全场AOE伤害并有几率眩晕。**宝藏召唤**——在场地边缘召唤宝藏哥布林——优先击杀获得额外掉落。**黄金雨**——从天空掉落金币——接触金币获得30%移速加成持续5秒。**打法建议**: 注意躲避黄金波——跳跃或翻滚都可以规避。宝藏哥布林出现后立刻集火击杀。推荐组队4人以上击杀——单人难度非常高 / Core Mechanics: **Gold Wave** every 20 sec — full-screen AoE + stun chance. **Treasure Summon** — spawns treasure goblins. **Gold Rain** — falling coins give +30% movespeed. Dodge Gold Wave. Focus treasure goblins immediately. Recommended 4+ players.

### 疫灾 / Plague

**刷新位置**: 哈维泽/诅咒之地随机 / Spawn: Random in Hawezar or Scosglen
**刷新间隔**: 每6小时 / Spawn Interval: Every 6 hours

**核心机制**: 疫灾释放**毒雾**——全场缓慢扩散的毒雾区域——站在其中持续掉血。**疫病孢子**——投掷绿色孢子——被击中受到持续毒素伤害。**腐烂波**——地面扩散的绿色波——造成致残效果。**打法建议**: 全程保持移动——毒雾和孢子都不容易躲避但可以通过移动降低命中率。携带**解毒药水**——至少5瓶。远程职业比近战更有优势。**毒素抗性堆到60%+** / Core Mechanics: **Poison Mist** — slow-spreading poison zone — constant damage. **Plague Spores** — poison DoT on hit. **Rot Wave** — ground green wave — cripple effect. Keep moving. Carry **Antidote Potions** — at least 5. Ranged advantage. **Stack poison resist to 60%+**.

## 终局Boss攻略 / Endgame Boss Strategies

### 瓦尔申的回响 / Echo of Varshan

**获取方式**: 收集**瓦尔申的身体部位**(从梦魇地下城和地狱潮获得) / Unlock: Collect **Varshan's Body Parts** (from NMD and Helltide)
**位置**: 哈维泽，腐烂神殿 / Location: Hawezar, Putrid Temple
**推荐等级**: 75+ (世界4)

**阶段一**: 瓦尔申召唤**腐败漩涡**——地面旋转的暗影AOE。**暗影弹幕**——远程暗影弹。**触手攻击**——地面伸出触手定身玩家。**阶段二**: 增加**灵魂虹吸**——吸取玩家造成持续伤害+恢复Boss血量。**腐败新星**——全屏爆震——躲在障碍物后规避。**关键装备**: 瓦尔申掉落**独特戒指"腐化的灵魂"**——对所有暗影技能造成+30%伤害——死灵法师和暗影游侠的毕业戒指 / Phase 1: Summons **Corruption Vortex** — spinning shadow AoE. **Shadow Barrage** — ranged shadow bolts. **Tentacle Attack** — ground tentacle roots players. Phase 2: Adds **Soul Siphon** — drains player HP + heals boss. **Corruption Nova** — full-screen blast — hide behind obstacles. **Key Drop**: Unique ring "Corrupted Soul" — +30% shadow damage — BIS for Necro and Shadow Rogue.

### 督瑞尔 / Duriel

**获取方式**: 收集**苦痛碎块x2 + 黏液盖蛋x2**(从瓦尔申和地狱潮Boss获得) / Unlock: Collect **Agony Shard x2 + Mucus Egg x2** (from Varshan and Helltide boss)
**位置**: 凯基斯坦，苦痛裂口 / Location: Kehjistan, Painful Gash
**推荐等级**: 85+

**核心机制**: 督瑞尔是**终极Boss**——拥有最高掉落品质。**猛击**——正面高伤害攻击。**钻地突袭**——钻入地下后从玩家脚下冲出。**苦痛光环**——减少周围玩家50%治疗效果。**打法建议**: 组满4人队。坦克吸引正面注意力，输出在侧后方输出。钻地突袭前地面有沙尘效果——看到立刻翻滚。**抗性堆满**(75%)可以大大降低难度。督瑞尔掉落**独特坐骑"爬地者"**——掉率约2% / Core Mechanics: **Ultimate Boss** — highest drop quality. **Slam** — high-damage frontal. **Burrow Strike** — burrow then erupt. **Pain Aura** — -50% healing in range. Full 4-player party. Tank holds aggro, DPS from side/back. Sand effect before burrow — dodge immediately. Max resist (75%). Drops unique mount "Scrounger" — ~2% rate.

### 冰中巨兽 / Beast in Ice

**获取方式**: 从梦魇地下城获得**精炼恐惧**——在凯基斯坦的冰窟使用 / Unlock: **Refined Fear** from Nightmare Dungeons — use at Kehjistan ice cave
**推荐等级**: 80+

**核心机制**: 冰中巨兽是冰系Boss——使用大量冰冻技能。**冰霜吐息**——正面扇形冰霜——横向躲避。**冰柱坠落**——冰柱从天花板落下——地面有阴影提示。**全屏冻结**——需要不断移动避免被完全冻结。**打法**: 堆冰抗到70%+——使用位移技能在冰柱间穿梭。Boss在50%HP时会进入**狂暴模式**——攻速x2——此时需要风筝战术。掉落**冰系独特装备**——包括"冰霜之握"手套——冰霜技能+40%伤害 / Core Mechanics: Ice boss with freezing skills. **Frost Breath** — cone frost — dodge sideways. **Ice Pillar Fall** — falling icicles — ground shadow warning. **Full Freeze** — keep moving to avoid. Stack cold resist 70%+. Boss enrages at 50% HP — kite strategy. Drops cold uniques including "Frost Grasp" gloves — +40% cold skill damage.

### 第8赛季新增Boss：符文守护者 / S8 New Boss: Rune Guardian

**获取方式**: 收集赛季专属材料**符文碎片**——赛季玩法中掉落 / Unlock: Collect seasonal materials **Rune Fragments** — drop from seasonal activities
**位置**: 赛季专属区域——符文神殿 / Location: S8 exclusive zone — Rune Temple
**推荐等级**: 90+

**核心机制**: 符文守护者使用**符文矩阵**——场地出现多个符文圈——站在其中获得Buff或受到伤害。**符文爆发**——根据当前激活的符文释放不同元素攻击。**符文锁链**——链接玩家和Boss——需要远离切断。**打法**: 需要快速识别符文颜色——红色=火焰伤害站远——蓝色=冰霜需要移动——绿色=毒素可以站近。推荐组队——一人专门负责符文识别/指挥。掉落**符文之语装备**——第8赛季独有的可定制独特装备 / Core Mechanics: **Rune Matrix** — multiple rune circles on arena — buff or damage depending on type. **Rune Burst** — elemental attack based on active rune. **Rune Chain** — links player to boss — needs distance to break. Identify rune colors — red=frost rune far, blue=cold move, green=poison safe. Party recommended. Drops **Rune Word gear** — S8 exclusive customizable uniques.

## Boss战通用技巧 / General Boss Tips

1. **防御药水不间断**——每个Boss战前喝对应的抗性药水 / Maintain defense potions — drink resistance potion before each boss
2. **位移技能是必需**——带一个位移技能(闪避以外的)规避致命技能 / Mobility skills are mandatory — bring one mobility skill beyond dodge
3. **Boss阶段转换时开大招**——Boss阶段转换后有小停顿——最佳爆发时机 / Pop ultimates during phase transitions — bosses pause briefly
4. **世界Boss组队优先**——单人挑战世界Boss效率极低 / Party up for world bosses — solo is extremely inefficient
5. **终局Boss前检查装等**——低于推荐装等20点以上不建议挑战 / Check item power before endgame bosses — not recommended when 20+ below
6. **Boss掉落池查看**——确认Boss掉落你需要的独特装备再花材料召唤 / Check boss loot table before spending materials — confirm desired unique drops
7. **第8赛季Boss新增免疫阶段**——部分Boss有短暂的无敌期——不要浪费大招 / S8 bosses have brief immunity phases — don't waste ultimates
8. **组队时分工明确**——一人拉仇恨、一人输出、一人控场 / Clear role division in parties — tank, DPS, controller
9. **噩梦地下城词缀影响Boss难度**——"暴击抵抗"词缀的Boss难打很多 / NM dungeon affixes affect boss difficulty — "Crit Resist" affix makes bosses much harder
10. **Boss战斗中的药水管理**——不要喝完所有药水——留至少1瓶应急 / Manage potions during boss fights — save at least 1 for emergency
