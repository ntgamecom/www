| **武器核心 / Weapon Core** | 基础攻击力+约5% / +~5% base attack | 约8个 / ~8 | 主线推进、隐藏Boss、全收集奖励 / Main story, hidden bosses, completion rewards |
| **防御核心 / Defense Core** | 防御力+约5% / +~5% defense | 约6个 / ~6 | 特定支线任务、二周目增加 / Specific side quests, added in NG+ |
