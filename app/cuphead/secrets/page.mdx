## 隐藏 NPC / Hidden NPCs

茶杯头的墨池岛地图上隐藏着多位特殊 NPC，与他们互动可以获得金币、彩蛋或特殊信息。

Multiple special NPCs are hidden across the Inkwell Isle map. Interacting with them yields coins, easter eggs, or special information.

### 监狱鸟 / Jailbird

位于第一墨池岛左上角的洞穴中，有一只被关在鸟笼里的鸟 NPC。与其对话会触发一个小剧情，随后会获得一枚金币。这枚金币很容易被忽略，因为在洞穴入口处没有明显的提示。

Located in a cave in the upper left of Inkwell Isle I, a bird NPC trapped in a cage. Speaking to it triggers a short scene and rewards one coin. This coin is easily missed as there is no obvious indicator at the cave entrance.

### 幽灵钢琴师 / Ghost Pianist

第三墨池岛的酒吧中有一位幽灵钢琴师，当你靠近钢琴时幽灵会出现并弹奏一曲。这只是纯粹的氛围彩蛋，没有实际奖励。但它是游戏对 1930 年代卡通风格的文化致敬。

A ghost pianist in the bar on Inkwell Isle III. The ghost appears and plays a tune when you approach the piano. This is purely an atmospheric easter egg with no actual reward, but it serves as a cultural homage to 1930s cartoon aesthetics.

### 神秘通道老人 / Tunnel Old Man

在第二墨池岛的下方有一个隐藏洞穴入口，需要击碎一块看起来与众不同的岩石才能进入。洞穴中有一位老人 NPC，他会向玩家讲述关于墨池岛的传说故事，并赠送一枚金币。

A hidden cave entrance in the lower area of Inkwell Isle II requires breaking a distinct-looking rock to enter. Inside, an elderly NPC shares legends about Inkwell Isle and gifts one coin.

### 隐藏商人 / Hidden Merchant

第三墨池岛的某个角落存在一个隐藏商人，出售一些特殊道具。找到这个商人的方法是仔细观察场景中与众不相同的物体，通常是一块颜色略有差异的墙壁，攻击后会出现入口。

A hidden merchant exists in a corner of Inkwell Isle III, selling special items. To find this merchant, carefully examine objects that appear slightly out of place — typically a wall with a subtle color difference. Attacking it reveals the entrance.

## BOSS 隐藏秘密 / Boss Secrets

### 树根帮的隐藏对话 / The Root Pack Hidden Dialogue

在树根帮战中，如果你在第二阶段（土豆阶段）停留足够长时间不攻击，土豆会说一句隐藏台词。这是一个设计上的趣味彩蛋，不影响实际游戏进程。

If you stay in phase two (Potato phase) of The Root Pack battle long enough without attacking, the Potato says a hidden line of dialogue. This is a fun easter egg that does not affect actual game progress.

### 大饭桶的墓碑留言 / Goopy Le Grande Tombstone Message

大饭桶第二阶段召唤的墓碑上刻有文字，内容是"Here lies Goopy... bigger than ever"。这不仅是幽默设计，也可以作为判断 BOSS 位置的小提示。

The tombstones summoned in Goopy Le Grande phase two bear the text Here lies Goopy... bigger than ever. This serves both as humorous design and a small hint for judging boss position.

### 吉姆·米奇的隐藏形态 / Grim Matchstick Hidden Form

在吉姆·米奇战中，如果你在特定时机使用特定攻击，可以触发一个隐藏的动画效果。该效果不影响实际战斗，属于视觉彩蛋。

In the Grim Matchstick battle, using a specific attack at a specific time can trigger a hidden animation effect. This does not affect actual combat but serves as a visual easter egg.

## 和平主义者跑法 / Pacifist Run Guide

和平主义者挑战要求在不击杀任何敌人的情况下完成跑轰关卡。这是游戏中最考验耐心的挑战之一。

The Pacifist challenge requires completing run-and-gun levels without killing any enemies. This is one of the most patience-testing challenges in the game.

### 核心策略 / Core Strategy

和平主义者的核心是回避而非战斗。你需要：

1. 装备烟雾弹（Smoke Bomb）护符——无敌冲刺可以穿过敌人群
2. 不主动射击——任何击杀都会使挑战失败
3. 利用冲刺的无敌帧穿过密集区域
4. 学习每个敌人的巡逻路线和攻击间隔

The core of Pacifist is avoidance rather than combat. You need to:

1. Equip Smoke Bomb charm — invincible dash lets you pass through enemy groups
2. Never shoot — any kill fails the challenge
3. Use dash invincibility frames to pass through dense areas
4. Learn each enemy patrol route and attack interval

### 各关卡 Pacifist 跑法 / Level-by-Level Pacifist Route

**Botanic Panic**：从上方路线绕开敌人，利用平台跳跃跳过敌人密集区。在关卡后半段需要精确的冲刺时机，通过两排密集的敌人阵地。

**Botanic Panic**: Take the upper route to bypass enemies, use platform jumps to skip enemy-dense areas. The latter half requires precise dash timing to pass through two rows of dense enemy positions.

**Forest Follies**：沿着平台最上边走，利用粉色气球格挡弹跳到更高平台。注意不要撞上飞行敌人——在它们飞近时向反方向冲刺。

**Forest Follies**: Follow the upper edge of platforms, use pink balloon parries to bounce to higher platforms. Watch out for flying enemies — dash in the opposite direction when they approach.

**Funfair Fever**：这个关卡最难 Pacifist。需要在旋转木马和摩天轮之间找到安全的移动窗口。建议先以正常方式通关几次熟悉敌人位置，再尝试 Pacifist 跑法。

**Funfair Fever**: This is the hardest Pacifist level. Find safe movement windows between carousels and Ferris wheels. It is recommended to clear the level normally several times to learn enemy positions before attempting a Pacifist run.

## S 级评价攻略 / S-Rank Guide

S 级评价是茶杯头的最高评价，仅在专家模式下可获得。

S-rank is the highest rating in Cuphead, achievable only on Expert mode.

### S 级评价条件 / S-Rank Conditions

1. 游戏难度为专家（Expert）模式
2. 通关时间在 2 分 30 秒以内
3. 不使用任何回复生命的道具（双心护符允许使用但会影响评价）
4. 至少完成 3 次格挡
5. BOSS 战中不死亡（续关会降低评价）

1. Play on Expert mode
2. Complete within 2 minutes 30 seconds
3. Use no HP recovery items (Twin Heart charm is allowed but affects rating)
4. Perform at least 3 parries
5. No deaths during the boss battle (continuing lowers the rating)

P-rank is rarer than S-rank, obtained on top of Pacifist requirements. You must:

- 不击杀任何敌人（同 Pacifist）
- 找到并收集关卡中的所有隐藏金币

## 隐藏关卡 / Secret Levels

### 第一次世界大战飞行关 / World War I (Secret Plane Level)

游戏中最著名的隐藏关卡——一战飞行关。在第三墨池岛的某个特定位置，画面右上方有一个云层中的隐藏入口。进入后玩家会操作飞机在一战风格的空中战场中战斗，面对大量敌机和飞艇。这个关卡没有 BOSS，但难度极高，弹幕密集程度堪比最终 BOSS 战。

The most famous hidden level in the game — the World War I plane level. At a specific location on Inkwell Isle III, there is a hidden entrance in the clouds at the upper right of the screen. Players pilot a plane in a World War I-style aerial battlefield, facing numerous enemy planes and airships. This level has no boss but is extremely difficult, with bullet density rivaling the final boss battle.

### 快捷方式 / Shortcuts

在墨池岛地图上存在多个快捷方式，可以让你跳过某些关卡直接进入后面的区域。这些快捷方式通常隐藏在看似背景的物体后面，需要玩家探索发现。

Multiple shortcuts exist on the Inkwell Isle map, allowing you to skip certain levels and access later areas directly. These shortcuts are typically hidden behind objects that appear to be background elements, requiring player exploration to discover.
