## 护符系统概述 / Charm System Overview

护符（Charms）是茶杯头中提供被动增益效果的道具，每场战斗前可以在装备界面选择一枚护符携带。护符的效果涵盖生存能力提升、能量获取加速、攻击方式改变等多个方面。合理选择护符往往比武器选择更能影响战斗走向。

Charms are items that provide passive buffs in Cuphead, with one charm selectable before each battle from the equipment screen. Charm effects cover enhanced survivability, accelerated energy gain, attack pattern modifications, and more. Choosing the right charm often influences battle outcomes more than weapon selection.

## 基础护符 / Basic Charms

### 烟雾弹 / Smoke Bomb

价格：4 金币
效果：冲刺变为烟雾冲刺，全程无敌且无声
适用场景：几乎所有战斗

Price: 4 coins
Effect: Transforms dash into smoke dash, granting full invincibility and silent movement
Suitable for: Nearly all battles

烟雾弹是游戏中最实用的护符，没有之一。普通冲刺只有短暂的帧数无敌，烟雾弹则将冲刺变为全程无敌，同时不发出声音。这意味着你可以直接穿过任何弹幕，不受伤害。对于新手玩家来说，烟雾弹可以弥补操作不足带来的失误。即使是高手玩家，烟雾弹提供的容错率也无可替代。

The Smoke Bomb is quite simply the most practical charm in the game. A normal dash has only brief invincibility frames, while Smoke Bomb grants full invincibility throughout the dash and eliminates movement sound. This means you can pass through any bullet pattern without taking damage. For new players, Smoke Bomb compensates for technical shortcomings. Even for veteran players, the margin for error provided by Smoke Bomb is irreplaceable.

### 咖啡 / Coffee

价格：4 金币
效果：超级计量槽随时间自动缓慢充能
适用场景：持久战、需要高频率必杀的战斗

Price: 4 coins
Effect: Super meter slowly auto-charges over time
Suitable for: Extended battles, fights requiring frequent super arts

咖啡护符让你无需格挡也能持续获得能量，每 7 秒自动充能一格。在持久战中，咖啡可以让你多释放 2-3 次超级必杀，这在后期 BOSS 战中意义重大。适合搭配需要频繁释放 EX 技能或超级必杀的武器组合。缺点是在快节奏的短战中效果不明显。

The Coffee charm lets you gain energy continuously without parrying, auto-charging one card every 7 seconds. In extended battles, Coffee lets you unleash 2-3 additional super arts, which is significant in later boss fights. It pairs well with weapon combinations that benefit from frequent EX moves or super arts. The downside is limited effectiveness in fast, short battles.

### P·糖 / P. Sugar

价格：4 金币
效果：自动格挡触碰到的第一个粉色物体
适用场景：弹幕中含有大量粉色物体的 BOSS 战

Price: 4 coins
Effect: Auto-parries the first pink object touched
Suitable for: Boss battles with numerous pink projectiles

P·糖可以自动格挡一次粉色物体，无需手动按键。这对于经常遗漏格挡时机的玩家非常有用。在格挡难度较高的 BOSS 战中（如吉姆·米奇的火焰格挡），P·糖可以提供一次保底格挡。缺点是一次战斗只能触发一次，需要合理规划使用时机。

P. Sugar auto-parries one pink object without requiring button input. This is very useful for players who frequently miss parry timing. In boss battles with difficult parry windows (such as Grim Matchstick fire parries), P. Sugar provides a guaranteed parry. The downside is it triggers only once per battle, requiring careful timing planning.

## 生存类护符 / Survival Charms

### 心环 / Heart Ring

价格：5 金币
效果：受到伤害时有概率回复一格生命值
适用场景：开荒阶段、练习新 BOSS 时

Price: 5 coins
Effect: Chance to recover one HP when taking damage
Suitable for: Early game exploration, practicing new bosses

心环在受到伤害时有概率触发回血效果，每次战斗最多触发三次。这枚护符的效果依赖运气，但在开荒阶段确实可以提供额外容错。需要注意的是，心环不能超过生命值上限，且触发概率大约是 30% 左右。对于追求稳定性的玩家来说，心环的随机性可能不如其他护符可靠。

The Heart Ring has a chance to heal one HP when taking damage, triggering up to three times per battle. This charm relies on luck but provides extra margin for error during the learning phase. Note that Heart Ring cannot exceed maximum HP, and the trigger probability is approximately 30%. For players seeking consistency, the Heart Ring randomness may be less reliable than other charms.

### 双心 / Twin Heart

价格：5 金币
效果：增加一格生命值上限
适用场景：生存压力极大的 BOSS 战、新手玩家

Price: 5 coins
Effect: Increases maximum HP by one
Suitable for: Boss battles with extreme survival pressure, new players

双心直接增加一格生命上限，让你可以多承受一次伤害。对于新手来说是最直接的生存提升。但需要注意的是，多一格生命意味着在特定 BOSS 战中可以多犯一次错，但同时也会减少一个护符位的战术价值。高手玩家通常会选择更具进攻性的护符。

Twin Heart directly increases maximum HP by one, allowing you to survive one additional hit. For new players, this is the most straightforward survival improvement. However, one extra hit point means one more mistake allowed in specific boss battles, while sacrificing a charm slot tactical value. Veteran players typically opt for more aggressive charms.

## 攻击类护符 / Attack Charms

### 威士顿 / Whetstone

价格：4 金币
效果：格挡时对附近的敌人造成伤害
适用场景：格挡频率高的战斗、攻击窗口短的 BOSS

Price: 4 coins
Effect: Deals damage to nearby enemies when parrying
Suitable for: Battles with frequent parrying, bosses with short attack windows

威士顿护符让格挡附带伤害效果，每次格挡会对周围敌人造成约 10 点伤害。在需要频繁格挡的 BOSS 战中，威士顿可以提供可观的额外输出。尤其是面对那些经常释放粉色弹幕的 BOSS，威士顿的输出价值很高。适合搭配 P·糖使用，确保格挡触发。

The Whetstone charm adds a damage effect to parries, dealing approximately 10 damage to nearby enemies with each parry. In boss battles requiring frequent parrying, Whetstone provides substantial additional damage. This is especially valuable against bosses that frequently release pink projectiles. It pairs well with P. Sugar to guarantee parry triggers.

## DLC 护符 / DLC Charms

### 星界曲奇 / Astral Cookie

价格：DLC 获取
效果：按住下方向键加跳跃键可变身为圣杯小姐（Ms. Chalice）
适用场景：DLC 全部内容、需要高机动性的战斗

Price: DLC acquisition
Effect: Hold down plus jump to transform into Ms. Chalice
Suitable for: All DLC content, battles requiring high mobility

星界曲奇是 DLC 中最重要的护符，使用后角色变为圣杯小姐，拥有全新的能力套组：二段跳跃、翻滚闪避（带无敌帧和伤害判定）、自动格挡粉色物体（圣杯小姐使用特殊吸铁石格挡）。圣杯小姐没有普通冲刺，但翻滚闪避在实用性上全面超越冲刺。变身状态下角色 hitbox 更小，生存能力更强。

The Astral Cookie is the most important DLC charm. Using it transforms the character into Ms. Chalice, granting an entirely new ability set: double jump, roll dodge (with invincibility frames and damage), and auto-parry for pink objects (Ms. Chalice uses a special magnet parry). Ms. Chalice has no normal dash, but the roll dodge comprehensively surpasses the dash in utility. In transformed state, the character hitbox is smaller, providing enhanced survivability.

### 神圣遗物 / Divine Relic

价格：DLC 获取（完成墓园挑战）
效果：随机切换武器效果，属性随战斗时间提升
适用场景：挑战模式、追求趣味性

Price: DLC acquisition (complete Graveyard challenge)
Effect: Randomly cycles weapon effects, stats improve over time
Suitable for: Challenge mode, seeking variety

神圣遗物是 DLC 的隐藏护符，效果极为特殊。装备后武器会变成"诅咒圣遗物"形态，属性会在战斗过程中逐渐提升。随着更多格挡的完成，武器会逐步解锁更强效果，最终进化为"神圣圣遗物"。这是游戏中最强的护符之一，但获取条件极为苛刻。

The Divine Relic is a hidden DLC charm with extremely special effects. When equipped, the weapon transforms into a Cursed Relic, with stats gradually improving during battle. As more parries are completed, the weapon progressively unlocks stronger effects, eventually evolving into the Divine Relic. This is one of the strongest charms in the game, but its acquisition conditions are extremely demanding.

### 破碎圣遗物 / Broken Relic

神圣遗物的初始形态，在墓园中通过特定挑战获得。装备后武器没有特殊效果，但随着战斗持续会逐步解锁能力。

The initial form of the Divine Relic, obtained through specific challenges in the Graveyard. When equipped, the weapon has no special effect initially, but progressively unlocks abilities as combat continues.
