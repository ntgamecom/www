## 武器系统概述 / Weapon System Overview

茶杯头中玩家可以同时携带两把武器，在战斗中随时切换。武器在商店用金币购买，每把武器都有独特的射击模式、弹道轨迹、伤害数值和 EX 技能。合理搭配两把武器是通关的关键。

In Cuphead, players can carry two weapons simultaneously and switch between them during combat. Weapons are purchased from the shop with coins, each featuring unique firing patterns, trajectories, damage values, and EX moves. Choosing the right two-weapon combination is key to clearing the game.

### 武器切换策略 / Weapon Switching Strategy

战斗中灵活切换武器可以最大化输出效率。通常的搭配思路是一把主攻武器（高伤害或追踪）和一把副攻武器（特殊弹道或范围攻击）。例如散射枪加追踪枪的组合可以应对大多数战斗场景：用散射枪在 BOSS 近身时爆发输出，用追踪枪在需要躲避时保持伤害。

Flexibly switching weapons during combat maximizes damage efficiency. A typical approach pairs a primary weapon (high damage or homing) with a secondary weapon (special trajectory or area attack). For example, Spread paired with Chaser handles most combat scenarios: use Spread for burst damage when close to the boss, and Chaser for maintaining damage while dodging.

## 基础武器 / Basic Weapons

### 豌豆枪 / Peashooter

价格：免费（初始武器）
弹道：直线
伤害：中
射速：快
EX 技能：豌豆重击 — 发射大型豌豆弹丸，三倍伤害

Price: Free (starter weapon)
Trajectory: Straight line
Damage: Medium
Fire rate: Fast
EX move: Peashooter EX — fires a large pea projectile with triple damage

豌豆枪是游戏初始武器，性能均衡无特殊效果。虽然没有突出优点，但也没有明显缺点——射速快、弹道直、伤害稳定。建议作为副武器使用，在主力武器弹药不适合当前场景时切换。对于追求稳定输出的玩家，豌豆枪配合咖啡护符可以实现高频次必杀释放。

The Peashooter is the starting weapon with balanced performance and no special effects. While it has no outstanding advantages, it also has no significant drawbacks — fast fire rate, straight trajectory, and consistent damage. Recommended as a secondary weapon to switch to when your primary weapon unsuited for the current situation. For players seeking consistent output, Peashooter with Coffee charm enables frequent super art usage.

## 进阶武器 / Advanced Weapons

### 散射枪 / Spread

价格：4 金币
弹道：扇形散射
伤害：极高（近身）
射速：中
EX 技能：散射爆裂 — 扇形喷射高伤害弹片

Price: 4 coins
Trajectory: Fan-shaped spread
Damage: Very high (close range)
Fire rate: Medium
EX move: Spread EX — sprays high-damage shrapnel in a fan pattern

散射枪是游戏中最强的近战武器之一。弹道呈扇形扩散，距离越近伤害越高，在贴脸输出时可以打出全游戏最高 DPS。缺点是射程有限，远距离伤害急剧下降。适合对付体型大、移动慢的 BOSS，或者用于 BOSS 战中的近身爆发阶段。几乎所有 BOSS 战中散射枪都是最优选择之一。

The Spread is one of the strongest close-range weapons in the game. Its fan-shaped spread deals maximum damage at point-blank range, achieving the highest DPS in the game. The drawback is limited range, with damage falling off sharply at distance. Suitable for large, slow bosses or close-range burst phases in boss battles. The Spread is one of the top choices for almost every boss encounter.

### 追踪枪 / Chaser

价格：4 金币
弹道：自动追踪
伤害：低
射速：中
EX 技能：追踪连射 — 发射多枚自动追踪弹头

Price: 4 coins
Trajectory: Auto-homing
Damage: Low
Fire rate: Medium
EX move: Chaser EX — fires multiple homing projectiles

追踪枪的伤害较低，但拥有自动追踪敌人的能力，让你可以完全专注于移动和躲避。对于操作不够熟练的新手玩家来说，追踪枪可以大幅降低游戏难度。缺点是对静态目标伤害不足，且追踪弹头会被地形阻挡。建议在面对移动频繁、攻击密集的 BOSS 时使用。

The Chaser has low damage but features auto-homing capabilities, allowing you to focus entirely on movement and dodging. For less experienced players, the Chaser significantly reduces game difficulty. The downside is insufficient damage against static targets, and homing projectiles can be blocked by terrain. Recommended for bosses that move frequently or have dense attack patterns.

### 抛射枪 / Lobber

价格：4 金币
弹道：抛物线
伤害：高
射速：慢
EX 技能：抛射弹幕 — 投掷多个抛物线弹头

Price: 4 coins
Trajectory: Parabolic arc
Damage: High
Fire rate: Slow
EX move: Lobber EX — throws multiple arcing projectiles

抛射枪发射抛物线弹头，可以越过障碍物攻击掩体后的敌人。弹头落地后会在地面停留一段时间持续造成伤害。适合对付有地形遮挡的 BOSS，或者需要同时攻击地面和空中的场景。缺点是对空中目标的命中率较低，需要练习抛物线预判。

The Lobber fires arcing projectiles that can clear obstacles to hit enemies behind cover. Projectiles linger on the ground after landing, dealing sustained damage. Suitable for bosses with terrain cover or scenarios requiring simultaneous ground and air attacks. The drawback is lower accuracy against airborne targets, requiring practice in parabolic trajectory prediction.

### 充能枪 / Charge

价格：6 金币
弹道：直线（需蓄力）
伤害：极高
射速：慢（需蓄力）
EX 技能：充能爆破 — 蓄力攻击，伤害极高

Price: 6 coins
Trajectory: Straight line (requires charging)
Damage: Extremely high
Fire rate: Slow (requires charging)
EX move: Charge EX — charged attack with extreme damage

充能枪需要按住射击键蓄力，蓄满后释放一发高伤害弹丸。完全蓄力的攻击伤害是普通豌豆枪的 5-6 倍，是游戏中的 DPS 冠军。缺点是需要精确掌握蓄力和释放节奏，在弹幕密集的战斗中蓄力容易被击中。适合熟悉 BOSS 攻击节奏的高手玩家使用。

The Charge requires holding the fire button to charge, then releasing for a high-damage projectile. A fully charged attack deals 5-6 times the damage of a normal Peashooter shot, making it the DPS champion. The downside is needing precise control of charging and release timing — charging in dense bullet patterns leaves you vulnerable. Suitable for expert players familiar with boss attack rhythms.

### 回旋镖 / Roundabout

价格：6 金币
弹道：回旋弧线
伤害：中高
射速：中
EX 技能：回旋射击 — 向后发射回旋弹幕

Price: 6 coins
Trajectory: Boomerang arc
Damage: Medium-high
Fire rate: Medium
EX move: Roundabout EX — fires projectiles backward in a boomerang pattern

回旋镖发射的弹丸会向前飞行一段距离后回旋返回，可以两次命中目标。独特的弹道让它非常适合对付位于角色后方的敌人，或者利用回旋路径让弹丸多次命中同一目标。向后射击时弹丸会向前再返回，形成覆盖角色两侧的攻击范围。上手需要适应期。

Roundabout projectiles fly forward then boomerang back, potentially hitting targets twice. The unique trajectory is ideal for enemies behind the character, or for hitting the same target multiple times with the return path. When firing backward, projectiles go forward then return, creating coverage on both sides. Requires an adjustment period to master.

## DLC 武器 / DLC Weapons

以下武器需要在 DLC 美味最后一口中获取。每把武器都具有独特的机制和战术价值。

The following weapons are obtained in The Delicious Last Course DLC. Each features unique mechanics and tactical value.

### 裂纹射击 / Crack Shot

获取方式：DLC 商店
弹道：分裂追踪
伤害：中
射速：中
EX 技能：裂纹扩散 — 发射分裂弹头覆盖大范围

Acquisition: DLC shop
Trajectory: Split homing
Damage: Medium
Fire rate: Medium
EX move: Crack Shot EX — fires splitting projectiles covering a large area

裂纹射击的弹头在飞行一段距离后会分裂成三个小弹头追踪附近的敌人。兼具追踪功能和范围覆盖能力，非常适合应对有杂兵伴随的 BOSS 战。分裂后的弹头伤害不变，因此对大型 BOSS 可以造成多次命中判定。

Crack Shot projectiles split into three smaller homing projectiles after traveling a distance, combining homing functionality with area coverage. Excellent for boss battles accompanied by minions. The split projectiles maintain full damage, allowing multiple hit registrations against large bosses.

### 汇聚 / Converge

获取方式：DLC 商店
弹道：聚焦光束
伤害：高
射速：中
EX 技能：汇聚光束 — 发射集中的高伤害光束

Acquisition: DLC shop
Trajectory: Focused beam
Damage: High
Fire rate: Medium
EX move: Converge EX — fires a concentrated high-damage beam

汇聚发射一束逐渐收窄的光束，距离越远伤害越高、弹道越集中。远程攻击时可以打出接近充能枪的伤害，且不需要蓄力。适合在远距离压制 BOSS，或者在安全距离持续输出。

Converge fires a beam that narrows over distance, dealing higher damage with greater precision at long range. At distance, it approaches Charge-level damage without requiring charging. Suitable for suppressing bosses from range or maintaining safe-distance damage output.

### 扭转 / Twist-Up

获取方式：DLC 商店
弹道：螺旋上升
伤害：中高
射速：快
EX 技能：扭转旋风 — 发射旋转弹幕覆盖大片区域

Acquisition: DLC shop
Trajectory: Spiraling upward
Damage: Medium-high
Fire rate: Fast
EX move: Twist-Up EX — fires spinning projectiles covering a wide area

扭转发射螺旋上升的弹幕，可以覆盖大部分垂直空间。适合对付空中的 BOSS 或者有高度差的战场。弹幕的螺旋路径可以多次命中同一目标，对大型 BOSS 效果极佳。缺点是水平覆盖范围有限。

Twist-Up fires spiraling projectiles that cover most vertical space. Suitable for airborne bosses or battles with height differences. The spiral trajectory can hit the same target multiple times, making it excellent against large bosses. The drawback is limited horizontal coverage.
