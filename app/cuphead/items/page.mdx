## 金币收集攻略 / Coin Collection Guide

金币是茶杯头中唯一的货币，用于在商店购买武器和护符。全游戏共有 52 枚金币，但只需要 36 枚即可买齐所有商店物品。不过，收集全部金币是解锁全成就的必要条件。

Coins are the only currency in Cuphead, used to purchase weapons and charms from the shop. There are 52 coins total in the game, but only 36 are needed to buy all shop items. However, collecting all coins is required for full achievement completion.

### 第一墨池岛金币 / Inkwell Isle I Coins

**Botanic Panic**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：关卡中段，击碎右侧高台上的木箱获得
- 金币 2：在第一个检查点后的上方平台，需要跳跃加冲刺抵达
- 金币 3：关卡末尾处，隐藏在下降平台左侧的洞穴中
- Coin 1: Break the crate on the high platform on the right in the middle of the level
- Coin 2: Above the first checkpoint, reachable with jump plus dash
- Coin 3: End of the level, hidden in a cave on the left of the descending platform

**Forest Follies**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：关卡开始后不久，击败出现的三只敌人后上方出现
- 金币 2：在木桥区域，不要上桥而是从下方通过，在隐藏通道尽头
- 金币 3：关尾前最后一个大平台右上角
- Coin 1: Shortly after the start, appears above after defeating three enemies
- Coin 2: In the bridge area, go under the bridge instead of crossing it, at the end of a hidden passage
- Coin 3: Upper right corner of the last large platform before the end

**Treetop Trouble**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：关卡中段粉色气球处，格挡气球后向上弹跳可获得
- 金币 2：在旋转平台区域，耐心等待平台转到正确位置后跳跃拾取
- 金币 3：关尾处入口正上方，需要从更高处落下获得
- Coin 1: At the pink balloon mid-level, parry it and bounce upward to reach
- Coin 2: In the rotating platform area, wait for the correct platform position before jumping
- Coin 3: Directly above the exit entrance, requires dropping from higher ground

### 第二墨池岛金币 / Inkwell Isle II Coins

**Funfair Fever**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：关卡开头，阶梯状平台最上方
- 金币 2：旋转木马区域，在第二个旋转木马上方
- 金币 3：隐藏区域——在摩天轮画面的左侧背景中有一个隐藏入口
- Coin 1: Top of the stepped platforms at the level start
- Coin 2: Carousel area, above the second carousel
- Coin 3: Hidden area — there is a hidden entrance in the left background of the Ferris wheel screen

**Carnival Kerfuffle**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：跳跳球区域，在弹球平台的正上方
- 金币 2：射击场中，击倒全部靶子后出现
- 金币 3：关尾前，需要利用弹簧平台弹到最高点获得
- Coin 1: Bouncy ball area, directly above the pinball platform
- Coin 2: Shooting gallery, appears after knocking down all targets
- Coin 3: Before the end, requires using spring platforms to bounce to the highest point

### 第三墨池岛金币 / Inkwell Isle III Coins

**Perilous Piers**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：关卡开始时的码头下方水中
- 金币 2：沉船区域内部，击碎船舱内的木箱
- 金币 3：关尾大船桅杆顶端，需要精确的跳跃和冲刺
- Coin 1: Underwater below the dock at the level start
- Coin 2: Inside the shipwreck area, break the crate inside the cabin
- Coin 3: Top of the ship mast near the end, requires precise jump and dash

**Rugged Ridge**（跑轰关卡）：
- 金币 1：蝙蝠洞上方隐蔽平台
- 金币 2：滚石区域的尽头右侧
- 金币 3：关尾悬崖左侧的隐藏跳台
- Coin 1: Hidden platform above the bat cave
- Coin 2: Right side at the end of the boulder rolling area
- Coin 3: Hidden ledge on the left of the cliff at the level end

### 隐藏金币 / Hidden Coins

除了跑轰关卡中的金币外，墨池岛地图上还隐藏着多枚金币，需要与特定 NPC 互动或探索隐藏区域获得：

In addition to run-and-gun level coins, multiple coins are hidden on the Inkwell Isle map, obtained by interacting with specific NPCs or exploring hidden areas:

**第一墨池岛隐藏金币 / Inkwell Isle I Hidden Coins**:
- 与地图上的金色萝卜互动可获得 1 枚金币
- 在商店后方敲击墙壁可发现隐藏洞穴，内有 2 枚金币
- Interact with the golden carrot on the map for 1 coin
- Knock on the wall behind the shop to find a hidden cave with 2 coins

**第二墨池岛隐藏金币 / Inkwell Isle II Hidden Coins**:
- 与正在浇花的园丁 NPC 对话后获得
- 在右上角的塔楼内击碎花瓶获得
- Speak to the gardener NPC watering plants to receive a coin
- Break the vase inside the tower in the upper right corner

**第三墨池岛隐藏金币 / Inkwell Isle III Hidden Coins**:
- 与小丑 NPC 玩射击游戏获得
- 在左下角的神秘传送门中探索
- Play the shooting game with the clown NPC for a coin
- Explore the mysterious portal in the lower left corner

## 秘密成就 / Secret Achievements

茶杯头中有多个秘密成就，以下是部分重要成就的解锁条件：

Cuphead features multiple secret achievements. Below are the unlock conditions for important ones:

**和平主义者 / Pacifist**：在不击杀任何敌人的情况下完成跑轰关卡。需要使用烟雾弹护符的高速冲刺通过关卡。这是游戏中最具挑战性的成就之一。

**Pacifist**: Complete a run-and-gun level without killing any enemies. Requires using Smoke Bomb charm for speed dashes through the level. This is one of the most challenging achievements in the game.

**S 级评价 / S-Rank**：在专家模式下通关所有 BOSS 并获得 S 级评价。S 级评价的条件是：以最高难度通关、时间在 2 分 30 秒以内、不使用任何生命回复道具、至少格挡 3 次。

**S-Rank**: Clear all bosses on Expert mode with S-rank evaluation. S-rank conditions: complete on highest difficulty, finish within 2 minutes 30 seconds, use no HP recovery items, and perform at least 3 parries.

**P 级评价 / P-Rank**：在不击杀任何敌人的情况下完成跑轰关卡并获得 P 级评价。比和平主义者的要求更高，需要全程不击杀任何敌人且完成所有秘密挑战。

**P-Rank**: Complete run-and-gun levels without killing any enemies and earn a P-rank. More demanding than Pacifist, requiring zero kills throughout while completing all secret challenges.

## 墓园挑战 / Mausoleum Challenges

在墨池岛各处分布着墓园（Mausoleum），进入后会进入特殊的格挡挑战关卡。你需要连续格挡一定数量的幽灵获得奖励。

Mausoleums are scattered across Inkwell Isle. Entering triggers special parry challenge stages where you must continuously parry a set number of ghosts for rewards.

**墓园位置 / Mausoleum Locations**:
- 第一墨池岛左下角 / Lower left of Inkwell Isle I
- 第二墨池岛右上角 / Upper right of Inkwell Isle II
- 第三墨池岛中间偏右 / Center-right of Inkwell Isle III

**完成奖励 / Completion Rewards**：
- 每完成一座墓园获得一件超级必杀技
- 全部完成解锁隐藏成就
- Each completed mausoleum awards one super art
- Completing all three unlocks a hidden achievement
