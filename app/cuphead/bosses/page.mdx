## 第一墨池岛 BOSS / Inkwell Isle I Bosses

第一墨池岛共有三个 BOSS，是新手学习基础战斗技巧的练习场。这里的 BOSS 攻击模式相对简单，阶段数较少，适合熟悉游戏的格挡和冲刺机制。

Inkwell Isle I features three bosses, serving as training grounds for learning basic combat techniques. These bosses have relatively simple attack patterns with fewer phases, suitable for familiarizing yourself with the parry and dash mechanics.

### 树根帮 / The Root Pack

树根帮是游戏中的第一个 BOSS，实际上是一个三阶段连续战斗。每个阶段由一棵蔬菜统治：第一阶段是洋葱（Onion），第二阶段是土豆（Potato），第三阶段是胡萝卜（Carrot）。

The Root Pack is the first boss in the game, effectively a three-phase continuous battle. Each phase is ruled by a different vegetable: Onion in phase one, Potato in phase two, and Carrot in phase three.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段洋葱会发射粉色眼泪弹幕，记得格挡粉色眼泪来积攒能量。第二阶段土豆埋入地下后会在你脚下冒出，注意观察地面的裂缝提示。第三阶段胡萝卜会从屏幕外滚入，发射胡萝卜子弹，同时有两只小胡萝卜杂兵干扰。推荐使用豌豆枪（Peashooter）配合烟雾弹（Smoke Bomb）稳扎稳打，或者使用散射枪（Spread）在近距离快速输出。

Phase one features Onion firing pink tear projectiles — remember to parry the pink tears for energy. In phase two, Potato burrows underground and emerges beneath you — watch for ground crack indicators. In phase three, Carrot rolls in from off-screen, firing carrot bullets while two small carrot minions cause interference. Recommended loadout: Peashooter with Smoke Bomb for steady play, or Spread for close-range burst damage.

### 大饭桶 / Goopy Le Grande

一个蓝色史莱姆状的 BOSS，第一阶段会跳跃压击和伸出拳头攻击。第二阶段 BOSS 会变大并召唤墓碑阻挡移动，同时弹跳速度加快。

A blue slime-like boss. Phase one features jump stomps and punch attacks. In phase two, the boss grows larger, summons tombstones that block movement, and bounces at increased speed.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段保持中等距离，在他的跳跃间隙输出。当他跳起时向侧面冲刺躲避。第二阶段需要更加注意墓碑的位置，避免被逼入角落。格挡他召唤的粉色墓碑可以获得额外能量。推荐使用抛射枪（Lobber）的抛物线攻击绕过墓碑，或者使用追踪枪（Chaser）专注于移动输出。

Maintain mid-range distance in phase one, dealing damage between his jumps. Dash sideways when he leaps. In phase two, pay extra attention to tombstone positions to avoid being cornered. Parry the pink tombstones he summons for extra energy. Recommended: Lobber for arcing attacks that bypass tombstones, or Chaser for focused mobility-based damage.

### 卡格尼·康乃馨 / Cagney Carnation

一朵巨大的食人花 BOSS，第一阶段会发射粉色种子弹幕和召唤小食人花。第二阶段 BOSS 长出藤蔓手臂，攻击范围大幅增加。

A giant carnivorous flower boss. Phase one involves firing pink seed projectiles and summoning small carnivorous plants. In phase two, the boss grows vine arms, greatly increasing attack range.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段注意格挡粉色种子充能，优先清理小食人花避免被围攻。第二阶段 BOSS 的藤蔓手臂会横扫和拍击，建议站在 BOSS 头部正下方输出，那里通常是安全区。推荐使用散射枪（Spread）在近距离打出高爆发伤害，配合烟雾弹紧急回避。

Parry pink seeds for energy in phase one and prioritize clearing small plants to avoid being overwhelmed. In phase two, watch for sweeping and slamming vine arms — standing directly beneath the boss head is usually a safe zone. Recommended: Spread for high close-range burst damage, paired with Smoke Bomb for emergency evasion.

## 第二墨池岛 BOSS / Inkwell Isle II Bosses

第二墨池岛有五个 BOSS，难度显著提升。BOSS 的阶段数增加，弹幕更加密集，需要更好的操作技巧。

Inkwell Isle II features five bosses with significantly increased difficulty. Bosses have more phases with denser bullet patterns, requiring better technical skills.

### 冯·邦·邦男爵夫人 / Baroness Von Bon Bon

一位糖果女王 BOSS，第一阶段会召唤各种糖果士兵（华夫饼、棒棒糖、姜饼人、棉花糖等）。第二阶段 BOSS 亲自上场，变成巨大的糖果怪物。

A candy queen boss. Phase one involves summoning various candy soldiers. In phase two, the boss personally enters the fray, transforming into a giant candy monster.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段建议优先消灭召唤的糖果士兵，利用士兵之间的空隙输出 BOSS。注意华夫饼士兵会高速冲刺，棒棒糖士兵会发射糖球。第二阶段男爵夫人会变为巨大的旋转糖果，攻击范围极广，需要保持持续移动。推荐使用追踪枪（Chaser）在躲避时自动攻击，或者使用充能枪（Charge）在安全间隙蓄力打击。

In phase one, prioritize eliminating summoned candy soldiers and exploit gaps between them to damage the boss. In phase two, the Baroness transforms into a giant spinning candy with extremely wide attack range — maintain constant movement. Recommended: Chaser for auto-aim while dodging, or Charge for powerful strikes during safe windows.

### 吉姆·米奇 / Grim Matchstick

一条火龙 BOSS，三个阶段都在空中战斗。第一阶段喷吐火焰球，第二阶段分裂为三个火球，第三阶段火龙会变形为更加敏捷的形态。

A fire dragon boss, with all three phases fought in the air. Phase one involves breathing fireballs. In phase two, the dragon splits into three fireballs. In phase three, the dragon transforms into an even more agile form.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段格挡粉色火焰球充能并维持空中高度。第二阶段三个火球分进合击，注意观察每个火球的移动轨迹。第三阶段 BOSS 速度快、攻击密集，需要极高的专注力。保持贴在平台边缘方便格挡粉色火焰。推荐使用追踪枪（Chaser）让你可以专注躲避，或者使用抛射枪（Lobber）的抛物线攻击。

Parry pink fireballs for energy and to maintain altitude in phase one. In phase two, the three fireballs attack from different angles — track each trajectory. Phase three requires intense focus due to the boss speed and attack density. Stay near platform edges for easier parry access. Recommended: Chaser for focused dodging, or Lobber for arcing attacks.

### 瑞玛·大炮 / Rumor Honeybottoms

一只大黄蜂女王，第一阶段在空中飞行并派遣工蜂。第二阶段 BOSS 变得更大，召唤蜂巢和蜂刺攻击。

A queen bee boss. Phase one involves flying while deploying worker bees. In phase two, the boss grows larger, summoning hives and sting attacks.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段优先消灭工蜂，避免被数量压倒。注意地面上的蜂蜜会减速你的移动。第二阶段 BOSS 会在空中释放大量蜂刺，地面还有蜂巢弹出，需要精确的跳跃和冲刺时机。推荐使用散射枪（Spread）在 BOSS 低飞时快速输出，使用烟雾弹应对紧急情况。

Prioritize eliminating worker bees in phase one to avoid being overwhelmed. Ground honey slows movement. Phase two features numerous sting attacks from above and emerging hives from below, requiring precise jump and dash timing. Recommended: Spread for burst damage when the boss flies low, with Smoke Bomb for emergencies.

### 船长炮筒 / Captain Brineybeard

一个海盗章鱼 BOSS，第一阶段操纵海盗船发射炮弹和鱼群。第二阶段 BOSS 亲自跳入水中，用触手攻击。

A pirate octopus boss. Phase one involves piloting a pirate ship firing cannonballs and fish. In phase two, the boss jumps into the water, attacking with tentacles.

**策略 Strategy**: 第一阶段注意炮弹的落点提示，格挡粉色炮弹充能。鱼群会从两侧游来，需要跳跃躲避。第二阶段触手会从水下突然伸出，有固定的攻击节奏。观察水面波纹预判触手出现位置。推荐使用豌豆枪（Peashooter）保持稳定输出，配合追踪枪清理杂兵。

Watch for cannonball landing indicators in phase one and parry pink cannonballs for energy. Fish schools swim in from both sides — jump to avoid. In phase two, tentacles emerge from below with a fixed rhythm — observe water ripples to predict positions. Recommended: Peashooter for consistent damage, paired with Chaser for mob clearing.

### 沃纳·沃曼 / Wally Warbles

一只知更鸟 BOSS 及其儿子，飞行关卡。第一阶段父子鸟合作攻击，第二阶段大鸟变得疯狂，释放羽毛和鸟蛋。

A robin boss with his son, fought in an airplane level. Phase one involves coordinated attacks from both birds. In phase two, the adult bird goes berserk, releasing feathers and eggs.

**策略 Strategy**: 飞行关卡中操作模式不同，需要适应飞机的移动惯性。第一阶段先消灭小鸟缩小弹幕范围，再集中攻击大鸟。格挡粉色羽毛积攒能量。第二阶段弹幕极其密集，需要利用飞机的收缩 hitbox 在弹幕缝隙中穿行。推荐专注躲避，在安全时机输出。

The airplane level features different controls — adapt to the plane momentum. In phase one, eliminate the smaller bird first to reduce bullet density, then focus on the adult. Parry pink feathers for energy. Phase two has extremely dense bullet patterns — use the plane compact hitbox to navigate through gaps. Focus on dodging and attack during safe windows.

## 第三墨池岛 BOSS / Inkwell Isle III Bosses

第三墨池岛有六个 BOSS，包括最终 BOSS 战，难度为游戏之最。

Inkwell Isle III features six bosses including the final battles, representing the game toughest challenges.

### 蛋糕狂魔 / The Howling Aves

一个蛋糕形状的 BOSS（DLC 内容）。实际上这个 BOSS 在 DLC 中，这里仅作介绍。

### 骰子王 / King Dice

游戏的守门 BOSS，拥有独特的机制。你需要在一个大棋盘上前进，每个格子对应一位小 BOSS。击败小 BOSS 后获得骰子点数，到达终点后与骰子王本人战斗。骰子王的战斗是他作为赌桌庄家的一连串攻击，阶段越多攻击越复杂。

The gatekeeper boss with a unique mechanic. Players advance on a large game board, with each space corresponding to a mini-boss. Defeat mini-bosses and accumulate dice rolls to reach the end, where King Dice himself awaits. His battle features a series of casino-themed attacks that grow more complex with each phase.

**策略 Strategy**: 骰子王前的小 BOSS 战中，建议保留能量不要使用超级必杀。到达骰子王本体后，第一阶段他会释放扑克牌和骰子攻击，格挡粉色扑克牌充能。第二阶段增加指尖陀螺攻击，需要精确的冲刺时机。注意画面上的数字提示可以预判攻击类型。推荐使用散射枪（Spread）配合烟雾弹，近身快速输出。

Save your super meter during the mini-boss phases before King Dice. In phase one against King Dice himself, he attacks with playing cards and dice — parry pink cards for energy. Phase two adds spinning top attacks requiring precise dash timing. Watch for on-screen number indicators to predict attack types. Recommended: Spread with Smoke Bomb for close-range burst damage.

### 魔鬼 / The Devil

游戏的最终 BOSS，三个阶段的高强度战斗。第一阶段魔鬼会释放火球和激光，召唤小鬼干扰。第二阶段魔鬼变身更大的形态，召唤陨石和恶魔之手。第三阶段魔鬼的本体出现，攻击速度达到极致。

The final boss of the game, featuring three intense phases. Phase one involves fireballs, lasers, and imp summons. In phase two, the Devil transforms into a larger form, summoning meteors and demon hands. Phase three reveals the Devil true form with maximum attack speed.

**策略 Strategy**: 魔鬼战是游戏综合素质的检验，需要完美的格挡时机和走位。第一阶段注意格挡粉色火球，积攒能量后释放超级必杀。第二阶段恶魔之手的抓取有预兆动作，看到屏幕变暗立即冲刺躲避。第三阶段不要贪刀，魔鬼的攻击间隔很短，宁可少输出也要保证安全。推荐使用追踪枪（Chaser）让你专注躲避，烟雾弹是必备护符。在第三阶段保留超级能量，在安全的瞬间释放能量光束收割。

The Devil battle tests overall game mastery, requiring perfect parry timing and positioning. Parry pink fireballs in phase one and unleash super arts when energy is full. Phase two demon hand grabs have telegraph animations — dash immediately when the screen darkens. In phase three, avoid greed — the Devil attack windows are extremely short. Prioritize safety over damage. Recommended: Chaser for focused dodging; Smoke Bomb is essential. Save super energy for phase three and unleash Energy Beam during safe windows.
