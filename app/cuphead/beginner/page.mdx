## 基础控制操作 / Basic Controls

在茶杯头中，掌握基础操作是通关的第一步。游戏的控制设计简洁但深度十足，每个按键在实战中都有不可替代的作用。

In Cuphead, mastering the basic controls is the first step toward clearing the game. The control scheme is simple yet deep, with each button serving an irreplaceable role in combat.

### 键盘与手柄操作对照 / Keyboard and Controller Mapping

### 核心操作技巧 / Core Technique Tips

格挡（Parry）是最重要的技巧。当粉色物体（粉色弹幕、粉色光环的敌人、粉色气球等）出现时，在接触的瞬间再次跳跃（或按格挡键）即可完成格挡。成功格挡会为超级计量槽充能一整格，并且可以连续格挡多个粉色物体来快速积攒能量。需要注意的是，格挡不能在空中无限使用，每次落地后才会重置格挡次数。

Parry is the most important technique. When pink objects appear (pink projectiles, enemies with pink halos, pink balloons, etc.), performing a second jump (or pressing the parry button) at the moment of contact executes a parry. Successful parries fill one full card of the super meter and can be chained across multiple pink objects to build energy quickly. Note that parries cannot be used infinitely in the air; the parry count resets only upon landing.

锁定瞄准（Lock）是进阶玩家必须掌握的技巧。按住锁定键可以固定射击方向，此时你可以自由移动而保持火力覆盖同一方向。这在对付需要边移动边输出的 BOSS 时至关重要。

Lock is an essential technique for advanced players. Holding the lock button fixes your shooting direction, allowing you to move freely while maintaining fire in one direction. This is crucial when dealing with bosses that require moving while outputting damage.

## 墨池岛导航 / Inkwell Isle Navigation

游戏世界由多个墨池岛组成，每个岛屿都包含 BOSS 战、跑轰关卡和隐藏区域。玩家需要击败当前岛屿的 BOSS 来收集契约碎片，集齐碎片后才能挑战守门人——骰子王（King Dice），最终面对魔鬼（The Devil）。

The game world consists of multiple Inkwell Isles, each containing boss battles, run-and-gun levels, and hidden areas. Players must defeat bosses on the current isle to collect contract pieces, and only after collecting all pieces can they challenge the gatekeeper King Dice and ultimately face the Devil himself.

### 第一墨池岛 / Inkwell Isle I

新手的第一站，难度相对较低。包含三个 BOSS（树根帮 The Root Pack、大饭桶 Goopy Le Grande、卡格尼 Carnation Cagney）和三个跑轰关卡（Botanic Panic、Forest Follies 等）。这是学习基础操作和熟悉游戏机制的最佳场所。

The first stop for newcomers, with relatively lower difficulty. Contains three bosses (The Root Pack, Goopy Le Grande, Cagney Carnation) and several run-and-gun levels. This is the best place to learn basic operations and familiarize yourself with game mechanics.

### 第二墨池岛 / Inkwell Isle II

难度开始提升。包含五个 BOSS（冯·邦·邦男爵夫人 Baroness Von Bon Bon、吉姆·米奇 Grim Matchstick、瑞玛·大炮 Rumor Honeybottoms、船长炮筒 Captain Brineybeard、沃纳·沃曼 Wally Warbles）和多个跑轰关卡。这个岛屿引入了更复杂的弹幕模式和阶段转换。

Difficulty begins to ramp up. Contains five bosses and multiple run-and-gun levels. This isle introduces more complex bullet patterns and phase transitions.

### 第三墨池岛 / Inkwell Isle III

最终岛屿，汇聚了游戏中最具挑战性的内容。包含六个 BOSS（蛋糕狂魔 The Howling Aves 等）和飞机关卡。这里的 BOSS 战节奏极快，需要玩家运用之前学到的全部技巧。

The final isle, featuring the most challenging content in the game. Contains six bosses and airplane levels. Boss battles here are extremely fast-paced, requiring players to utilize every technique learned previously.

## 跑轰关卡攻略 / Run-and-Gun Level Guide

跑轰关卡是介于 BOSS 战之间的横版过关关卡，玩家需要在平台间移动的同时消灭敌人。这些关卡是收集金币的主要途径。

Run-and-gun levels are side-scrolling stages between boss fights where players must navigate platforms while eliminating enemies. These levels are the primary source of coin collection.

### 跑轰基础技巧 / Basic Run-and-Gun Tips

每关都有大量金币隐藏在角落、高台和秘密区域。建议首次通关时以探索为主，不要急于冲刺。多利用格挡粉色物体来积攒超级计量槽，在面对大量敌人时释放必杀技清理屏幕。

Each level has numerous coins hidden in corners, on high platforms, and in secret areas. It is recommended to prioritize exploration on your first playthrough rather than rushing. Make good use of parrying pink objects to build super meter, and unleash super arts to clear the screen when facing large groups of enemies.

### 各关卡金币位置指引 / Coin Location Guide

跑轰关卡中通常有 3-5 枚金币。有些金币位于显眼处，有些则需要通过隐藏通道或打破特定障碍物才能获得。在商店中，每件武器和护符都需要金币购买，因此收集所有金币是非常必要的。

Run-and-gun levels typically contain 3-5 coins. Some coins are in plain sight, while others require finding hidden passages or breaking specific obstacles to obtain. Since every weapon and charm in the shop requires coins to purchase, collecting all available coins is highly recommended.

## 商店系统 / The Shop System

在墨池岛的特定位置可以找到由猪猪商人（Porkrind）经营的商店。在这里可以用金币购买各种武器、护符和道具。建议优先购买烟雾弹（Smoke Bomb）护符和散射枪（Spread）武器，这两件道具可以显著降低游戏难度。

The shop operated by Porkrind can be found at specific locations on Inkwell Isle. Here you can spend coins on various weapons, charms, and items. It is recommended to prioritize purchasing the Smoke Bomb charm and the Spread weapon, as these two items can significantly reduce game difficulty.

### 首次购物推荐 / First Purchase Recommendations

对于新手玩家，建议按以下顺序购买：
1. 烟雾弹（Smoke Bomb）—— 提供无敌冲刺，大幅提高生存能力
2. 散射枪（Spread）—— 近距离高伤害，对付大部分 BOSS 效果拔群
3. 咖啡（Coffee）—— 自动充能超级计量槽，减少主动格挡压力

For new players, the recommended purchase order is:
1. Smoke Bomb - Provides invincible dash, greatly improving survivability
2. Spread - High close-range damage, excellent against most bosses
3. Coffee - Auto-charges super meter, reducing the need for active parrying

## 难度模式详解 / Difficulty Mode Analysis

茶杯头提供三种难度模式，不同难度影响 BOSS 的阶段数量、攻击速度和弹幕密度。

Cuphead offers three difficulty modes, each affecting the number of boss phases, attack speed, and bullet density.

### 简单模式 / Simple Mode

BOSS 通常只有两个阶段，弹幕数量较少，攻击速度较慢。击败简单模式的 BOSS 不会获得契约碎片，因此无法通过纯简单模式通关游戏。简单模式适合熟悉 BOSS 的攻击模式，但不会计入游戏进度。

Bosses typically have only two phases with fewer projectiles and slower attack speed. Defeating bosses on Simple mode does not award contract pieces, making it impossible to complete the game on Simple mode alone. This mode is suitable for learning boss attack patterns but does not count toward game progress.

### 普通模式 / Regular Mode

完整的游戏体验。BOSS 拥有全部阶段，弹幕密度适中，攻击节奏合理。击败 BOSS 后获得契约碎片，集齐后可挑战骰子王和魔鬼。建议所有玩家在普通模式下完成首次通关。

The complete game experience. Bosses have all phases with moderate bullet density and reasonable attack rhythm. Defeating bosses awards contract pieces, which can be collected to challenge King Dice and the Devil. It is recommended that all players complete their first playthrough on Regular mode.

### 专家模式 / Expert Mode

为追求白金评价的玩家准备的终极挑战。BOSS 的攻击速度大幅提升，弹幕密度显著增加，新增了多种攻击模式。通关专家模式是解锁 S 级评价的必要条件，也是获得全部成就的必经之路。

The ultimate challenge for players seeking platinum rankings. Boss attack speed is greatly increased, bullet density is significantly higher, and new attack patterns are introduced. Clearing Expert mode is required to unlock S-rank evaluations and is necessary for earning all achievements.

## 双人合作模式 / Co-op Mode

茶杯头支持本地双人合作，第二名玩家扮演马克杯人（Mugman）。合作模式下两名玩家共享生命和金币，但BOSS 的血量会相应增加。合作模式的核心在于分工配合——一人吸引火力、一人输出伤害。需要注意的是，合作模式下弹幕更加混乱，需要更高的默契度。

Cuphead supports local two-player co-op, with the second player controlling Mugman. In co-op mode, both players share lives and coins, but boss health increases accordingly. The key to co-op mode is role division — one player draws fire while the other deals damage. Note that co-op mode features more chaotic bullet patterns, requiring greater coordination.
