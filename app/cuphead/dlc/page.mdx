## DLC 概述 / DLC Overview

《美味最后一口》（The Delicious Last Course）是茶杯头于 2022 年 6 月 30 日发布的重大 DLC，由 Studio MDHR 开发。售价 7.99 美元，新增了墨池岛第四岛（Inkwell Isle IV），包含五位全新 BOSS、可玩角色圣杯小姐（Ms. Chalice）、三种新武器、两种新护符以及全新的墓园挑战模式。DLC 继承了本篇的手绘动画风格和高难度特点，同时引入了大量创新机制。

The Delicious Last Course is a major DLC for Cuphead released on June 30, 2022, developed by Studio MDHR. Priced at 7.99 USD, it adds Inkwell Isle IV featuring five new bosses, the playable character Ms. Chalice, three new weapons, two new charms, and the new Graveyard challenge mode. The DLC retains the hand-drawn animation style and high difficulty of the base game while introducing numerous innovative mechanics.

### 如何进入 DLC / How to Access the DLC

在购买并安装 DLC 后，游戏世界地图上会新增一个通往第四墨池岛的入口。入口位于第三墨池岛的右侧，需要完成第三墨池岛的所有内容后方可进入。进入 DLC 后，你可以在第一墨池岛的商店旁找到圣杯小姐的墓碑，与其互动来解锁圣杯小姐角色。

After purchasing and installing the DLC, a new entrance to Inkwell Isle IV appears on the game world map. The entrance is located on the right side of Inkwell Isle III and requires completing all content on Inkwell Isle III to access. Once in the DLC area, you can find Ms. Chalice tombstone next to the shop on Inkwell Isle I — interact with it to unlock Ms. Chalice as a playable character.

## 圣杯小姐完全指南 / Ms. Chalice Complete Guide

圣杯小姐是 DLC 新增的可玩角色，拥有完全不同于茶杯头和马克杯人的能力体系。使用星界曲奇（Astral Cookie）护符即可在关卡中变身为圣杯小姐。她的能力包括：

Ms. Chalice is a new playable character added in the DLC, featuring a completely different ability set from Cuphead and Mugman. Using the Astral Cookie charm transforms into Ms. Chalice during levels. Her abilities include:

### 二段跳跃 / Double Jump

圣杯小姐可以在空中进行第二次跳跃，大幅提高机动性。这是她最重要的能力，可以让她轻松到达普通角色无法抵达的位置。在弹幕躲避方面，二段跳跃提供了更多的滞空时间和位置调整空间。

Ms. Chalice can perform a second jump in mid-air, greatly improving mobility. This is her most important ability, letting her easily reach positions inaccessible to normal characters. In bullet dodging, double jump provides more airtime and position adjustment space.

### 翻滚闪避 / Roll Dodge

圣杯小姐没有普通冲刺，取而代之的是翻滚闪避。翻滚闪避具有以下特点：
- 全程无敌帧（约持续 0.4 秒）
- 翻滚过程中对接触的敌人造成伤害
- 可以穿过弹幕和敌人
- 翻滚后的硬直时间极短

Ms. Chalice has no normal dash, replaced by the roll dodge. The roll dodge features:
- Full invincibility frames (approximately 0.4 seconds)
- Deals damage to enemies contacted during the roll
- Can pass through projectiles and enemies
- Extremely short recovery time after rolling

### 吸铁石格挡 / Magnet Parry

圣杯小姐的格挡方式与众不同，她使用一块吸铁石进行格挡。吸铁石格挡的范围比普通格挡更大，容错率更高。成功格挡后的弹跳高度也略高于普通格挡。

Ms. Chalice parry method is unique — she uses a magnet for parrying. The magnet parry has a larger range than normal parry, providing higher margin for error. The bounce height after a successful parry is also slightly higher than normal.

## 全新 BOSS 攻略 / New Bosses Guide

DLC 中共有五位全新 BOSS，每位都经过精心设计，拥有独特的攻击方式和华丽的视觉效果。

The DLC features five new bosses, each meticulously designed with unique attack patterns and spectacular visual effects.

### 盐焗师 / Chef Saltbaker

最终 BOSS，位于第四墨池岛的顶端。盐焗师是一个疯狂的厨师，战斗在一个巨大的厨房中进行。他拥有五个阶段，每个阶段都有全新的攻击模式。

The final boss, located at the top of Inkwell Isle IV. Chef Saltbaker is a mad chef, with the battle taking place in a giant kitchen. He has five phases, each with entirely new attack patterns.

**第一阶段 Phase 1**：盐焗师投掷各种厨房用具（锅、勺、砧板）和食材。地面上的火焰区域会持续造成伤害。格挡粉色锅盖积攒能量。推荐使用追踪枪保持输出，同时专注躲避。

Saltbaker throws various kitchen utensils and ingredients. Fire zones on the ground deal continuous damage. Parry pink pot lids for energy. Recommended: Chaser for sustained damage while focusing on dodging.

**第二阶段 Phase 2**：盐焗师跳入巨大的搅拌碗中，变成搅拌机形态。碗口会喷射食材弹幕，同时地面有面糊波浪推进。这个阶段需要精确的跳跃时机来避开面糊波浪。

Saltbaker jumps into a giant mixing bowl, transforming into a blender form. The bowl opening sprays ingredient projectiles while batter waves advance across the ground. This phase requires precise jump timing to avoid batter waves.

**第三阶段 Phase 3**：盐焗师使用巨大的擀面杖攻击，场地变为砧板。擀面杖会横扫和拍击，同时有面团小兵出现。优先清理小兵避免被围攻。

Saltbaker attacks with a giant rolling pin as the arena becomes a cutting board. The rolling pin sweeps and slams while dough minions appear. Prioritize clearing minions to avoid being overwhelmed.

**第四阶段 Phase 4**：盐焗师进入烤箱形态，场地变为烤炉内部。火焰从多个方向喷出，温度持续升高。需要站在安全区域输出，注意火焰喷射的规律。

Saltbaker enters oven form with the arena becoming an oven interior. Flames喷出 from multiple directions as temperature rises. Stand in safe zones while attacking, paying attention to flame喷射 patterns.

**第五阶段 Phase 5**：最终形态，盐焗师变成巨大的恶魔厨师。攻击速度达到极致，全屏弹幕需要极高的反应速度。这是游戏中最具挑战性的阶段之一。推荐保持冷静，只在自己的安全窗口输出。

Final form — Saltbaker transforms into a giant demon chef. Attack speed reaches maximum with screen-filling bullet patterns requiring extremely fast reactions. This is one of the most challenging phases in the game. Stay calm and only attack during safe windows.

### 月光帮 / The Moonshine Mob

一个由三个动物组成的帮派：青蛙（Sheriff Frog）、蛇（Ms. Snake）和狐狸（Howling Aves 中的一员）。每个成员都有独立的阶段。

A gang of three animals: Sheriff Frog, Ms. Snake, and a member of the Howling Aves. Each member has an independent phase.

**青蛙阶段 Frog Phase**：牛仔风格的青蛙，使用左轮手枪射击，子弹轨迹为直线和弹跳两种。粉色子弹可以格挡。青蛙还会召唤沙尘暴遮挡视线。需要熟悉地图布局，在沙尘暴中靠记忆走位。

Cowboy-style frog using revolvers, with straight and bouncing bullet trajectories. Pink bullets can be parried. The frog also summons sandstorms that obscure vision. Familiarize yourself with the map layout and rely on memory for positioning during sandstorms.

**蛇阶段 Snake Phase**：蛇会在地面和天花板上移动，释放毒液弹幕。毒液落地后形成残留区域。注意蛇的移动方向预判其攻击。推荐使用追踪枪。

The snake moves across the ground and ceiling, releasing venom projectiles. Venom creates残留 zones on landing. Predict attacks by tracking the snake movement direction. Recommended: Chaser.

### 嚎叫王牌 / Howling Aces

一个三阶段的空中飞机关卡 BOSS，最初是一只机械飞艇，后续阶段会变形为不同的空中形态。

A three-phase airplane level boss, initially a mechanical airship that transforms into different aerial forms in subsequent phases.

**第一阶段 Phase 1**：巨大的机械飞艇，发射导弹和子弹。飞艇的螺旋桨会产生气流影响你的飞机移动。导弹有红色预警线，注意提前规避。

Giant mechanical airship firing missiles and bullets. The airship propellers generate气流 affecting your plane movement. Missiles have red warning lines —提前规避.

**第二阶段 Phase 2**：飞艇分解为多个小型飞行器，编队攻击。需要逐个击破，优先消灭发射弹幕的小飞行器。

The airship breaks into multiple小型 fighters attacking in formation. Eliminate them one by one, prioritizing those firing bullet patterns.

**第三阶段 Phase 3**：核心boss出现，变为一个拥有机械翅膀的怪物形态。攻击方式包括激光扫射和跟踪导弹。需要利用飞机的收缩 hitbox 在激光缝隙中穿行。

The core boss appears as a monster with mechanical wings. Attacks include laser sweeps and tracking missiles. Use the plane compact hitbox to navigate through laser gaps.

## 全新武器详解 / New Weapons Detailed

### 裂纹射击 / Crack Shot

弹道先直线飞行后分裂为三个追踪弹头。每个弹头伤害为普通射击的 80% 左右，但三发全中可以造成可观的伤害。适合大型 BOSS 和带杂兵的战斗场景。

The trajectory flies straight then splits into three homing projectiles. Each projectile deals approximately 80% of normal shot damage, but three hits together deal substantial damage. Suitable for large bosses and battles with minions.

### 汇聚 / Converge

射程越远伤害越高、精度越高。在最大射程时伤害达到峰值。适合喜欢远程风筝的玩家，可以在安全距离持续输出。近战使用时伤害较低，建议搭配一把近战武器。

Longer range yields higher damage and precision. Damage peaks at maximum range. Suitable for players who prefer kiting from distance, maintaining safe-range damage output. Close-range damage is lower — pair with a melee weapon.

### 扭转 / Twist-Up

螺旋上升的弹幕覆盖垂直空间，对空中的敌人效果极佳。弹幕的螺旋路径可以多次命中大型目标。在飞机关卡中表现优异。

Spiraling projectiles cover vertical space, excellent against airborne enemies. The spiral trajectory can hit large targets multiple times. Performs exceptionally well in airplane levels.

## 全新护符详解 / New Charms Detailed

### 星界曲奇 / Astral Cookie

让角色变身圣杯小姐。这是 DLC 中最具革命性的道具，彻底改变了游戏玩法。建议在 DLC 全程使用，圣杯小姐的能力在 DLC 关卡中占据明显优势。

Transforms the character into Ms. Chalice. This is the most revolutionary item in the DLC, completely changing gameplay. Recommended for use throughout the DLC, as Ms. Chalice abilities provide clear advantages in DLC levels.

### 神圣遗物 / Divine Relic

从诅咒圣遗物进化而来，是游戏中最强的隐藏道具。进化的条件是完成特定数量的格挡，越战越强。完全进化的神圣遗物相当于同时拥有多种武器和护符的效果。

Evolved from the Cursed Relic, the strongest hidden item in the game. Evolution requires completing a specific number of parries, growing stronger throughout the battle. A fully evolved Divine Relic combines effects of multiple weapons and charms simultaneously.

## 墓园挑战 / The Graveyard Challenge

DLC 新增的特殊挑战模式。在第四墨池岛的墓园中，你需要完成一系列特殊的格挡和战斗挑战，每完成一个挑战就会解锁新的内容。最终奖励是神圣遗物。

A special challenge mode added in the DLC. In the Graveyard on Inkwell Isle IV, you must complete a series of special parry and combat challenges, each unlocking new content. The final reward is the Divine Relic.

### 挑战类型 / Challenge Types

1. 格挡挑战：连续格挡快速出现的幽灵
2. 限时生存：在规定时间内躲避弹幕
3. 无伤挑战：不受伤完成特定 BOSS 阶段
4. 速杀挑战：在规定时间内击败特定敌人

1. Parry challenge: consecutively parry rapidly appearing ghosts
2. Timed survival: dodge bullet patterns within the time limit
3. No-hit challenge: complete specific boss phases without taking damage
4. Speed kill challenge: defeat specific enemies within the time limit
