## 欢迎来到茶杯头世界 / Welcome to the World of Cuphead

《茶杯头》（Cuphead）是由加拿大独立游戏工作室 Studio MDHR 开发的一款经典横版动作射击游戏，于2017年9月29日首次登陆 Xbox One 和 Windows 平台。游戏以其独特的 1930 年代弗莱舍尔卡通风格手绘动画而闻名，所有角色、背景和动画均由手工绘制，完美还原了早期橡皮管动画的黄金时代美学。配乐采用同期爵士乐、大乐队和 ragtime 风格，所有音效均由真实乐器录制，营造出沉浸式的复古体验。

Cuphead is a classic run-and-gun action game developed by Canadian indie studio Studio MDHR, first released on September 29, 2017 for Xbox One and Windows. The game is renowned for its distinctive 1930s Fleischer cartoon-style hand-drawn animation, with all characters, backgrounds, and animations painstakingly hand-crafted to capture the golden age of rubber hose animation. The soundtrack features period-accurate jazz, big band, and ragtime music recorded with live instruments, creating an immersive vintage experience.

### 游戏类型与核心玩法 / Genre and Core Gameplay

本作融合了跑轰（run-and-gun）与 BOSS RUSH 两种核心玩法。玩家将扮演茶杯头（Cuphead）或马克杯人（Mugman），在墨池岛（Inkwell Isle）上挑战一系列风格各异的 BOSS，穿越危险的跑轰关卡，收集金币在商店购买武器和道具。游戏支持本地双人合作模式，两位玩家可以并肩作战共享资源，但也会增加战斗难度。

The game combines run-and-gun platforming with boss rush mechanics. Players control Cuphead or Mugman as they traverse the Inkwell Isles, challenging a series of uniquely designed bosses, navigating dangerous run-and-gun levels, collecting coins to purchase weapons and items from the shop. The game features local two-player co-op mode, allowing both players to fight side by side while sharing resources, though this increases encounter difficulty.

### 难度与挑战 / Difficulty and Challenge

茶杯头以高难度著称，但这并不意味着游戏不公平。每个 BOSS 的攻击模式都有规律可循，BOSS 战中会经历多个阶段（phases），每个阶段都有全新的攻击方式和视觉变化。游戏中提供了三种难度模式：简单（Simple）、普通（Regular）和专家（Expert）。简单模式只能体验前两个阶段，击败 BOSS 后无法获得契约碎片。普通模式才是完整的游戏体验，击败 BOSS 后可获得契约碎片。专家模式是为追求极限挑战的玩家准备的，BOSS 的攻击速度更快、弹幕更密集。

Cuphead is renowned for its high difficulty, but this does not mean the game is unfair. Each boss follows predictable attack patterns, and boss battles feature multiple phases, with each phase introducing entirely new attack patterns and visual changes. The game offers three difficulty modes: Simple, Regular, and Expert. Simple mode only allows experiencing the first two phases and does not award contract pieces upon defeat. Regular mode provides the complete game experience and awards contract pieces. Expert mode is designed for players seeking the ultimate challenge, with faster attack speeds and denser bullet patterns.

### DLC 美味最后一口 / The Delicious Last Course

2022年6月30日，Studio MDHR 发布了备受期待的 DLC《美味最后一口》（The Delicious Last Course）。该 DLC 新增了全新的墨池岛（Inkwell Isle IV），包含五位全新的 BOSS、一位可玩的新角色——圣杯小姐（Ms. Chalice）、全新的武器（裂纹射击 Crack Shot、汇聚 Converge、扭转 Twist-Up）、全新护符（星界曲奇 Astral Cookie、神圣遗物 Divine Relic）以及全新的墓园挑战模式。圣杯小姐拥有独特的二段跳跃、翻滚闪避和格挡能力，为游戏带来了全新的战术维度。

On June 30, 2022, Studio MDHR released the highly anticipated DLC The Delicious Last Course. This expansion adds a brand new Inkwell Isle IV, featuring five new bosses, a new playable character Ms. Chalice, new weapons (Crack Shot, Converge, Twist-Up), new charms (Astral Cookie, Divine Relic), and the new Graveyard challenge mode. Ms. Chalice possesses unique abilities including double jump, roll dodge, and parry, bringing a completely new tactical dimension to the game.

### 墨池岛世界 / The World of Inkwell Isles

游戏世界由多个墨池岛组成，每个岛屿都有独特的主题风格和 BOSS 阵容。第一墨池岛是田园风光、第二墨池岛是狂欢节主题、第三墨池岛是工业港口风格、DLC 的第四墨池岛则是西部荒野和厨房主题。每个岛屿的地图上都隐藏着秘密通道、隐藏 NPC 和额外金币，鼓励玩家探索每一个角落。

The game world consists of multiple Inkwell Isles, each with a distinct theme and boss roster. Inkwell Isle I features pastoral scenery, Inkwell Isle II has a carnival theme, Inkwell Isle III adopts an industrial harbor style, and the DLC Inkwell Isle IV embraces western wilderness and kitchen themes. Each isle map hides secret passages, hidden NPCs, and extra coins, encouraging players to explore every corner.

### 动画与美术 / Animation and Art Style

茶杯头的所有动画均采用传统手绘方式制作，使用水彩背景和手工绘制赛璐珞动画。Studio MDHR 与加拿大知名的动画工作室合作，逐帧绘制了所有角色动画。游戏中的每一帧画面都经过精心设计，确保还原 1930 年代卡通影片的质感。整个游戏包含了超过 50 万帧手绘动画，每一帧都是艺术品。

All Cuphead animations are created using traditional hand-drawn techniques, featuring watercolor backgrounds and hand-painted cel animation. Studio MDHR collaborated with renowned Canadian animation studios to draw every frame of character animation. Each frame of the game is meticulously designed to replicate the texture of 1930s cartoon films. The entire game contains over 500,000 frames of hand-drawn animation, each one a work of art.

### 评价与荣誉 / Critical Reception and Awards

茶杯头发售后获得了业界一致好评，被誉为独立游戏界的里程碑之作。游戏在 Metacritic 上获得了 86 分的高分，并荣获多项年度大奖，包括 Gamescom 的最佳独立游戏奖、Game Critics Awards 的最佳原创游戏奖，以及 2017 年 The Game Awards 的最佳独立游戏和最佳美术设计奖。其手绘动画风格被广泛认为是电子游戏视觉艺术的巅峰成就之一。

Cuphead received universal acclaim upon release, hailed as a landmark achievement in indie gaming. The game holds an 86 Metascore on Metacritic and won numerous annual awards, including Best Independent Game at Gamescom, Best Original Game at Game Critics Awards, and Best Independent Game and Best Art Direction at The 2017 Game Awards. Its hand-drawn animation style is widely regarded as one of the pinnacle achievements in video game visual artistry.
