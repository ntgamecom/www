## 超级必杀技系统 / Super Art System

超级必杀技是茶杯头中最强大的攻击手段，消耗满格超级计量槽（5格能量）释放。玩家可以在关卡开始前的装备界面从三种超级必杀技中选择一种携带。每场战斗通常可以释放 2-4 次超级必杀，合理规划释放时机是取胜的关键。

Super arts are the most powerful attacks in Cuphead, consuming a full super meter (5 cards of energy). Players select one of three super arts before each level from the equipment screen. Each battle typically allows 2-4 super art activations, making proper timing planning crucial for victory.

### 超级计量槽管理 / Super Meter Management

超级计量槽通过以下几种方式充能：
1. 格挡粉色物体（+1 格）
2. 咖啡护符自动充能（每 7 秒 +1 格）
3. 使用部分 EX 技能后返还的微量能量
4. 对 BOSS 造成伤害时产生的微量充能

The super meter is charged through several methods:
1. Parrying pink objects (+1 card)
2. Coffee charm auto-charge (+1 card every 7 seconds)
3. Minor energy return after using certain EX moves
4. Minor charge generated when dealing damage to bosses

### 能量管理策略 / Energy Management Strategy

在大多数 BOSS 战中，建议的策略是：
- 开局尽快通过格挡积攒第一管能量
- 不要在 BOSS 即将转换阶段时释放 EX 技能（浪费能量）
- 保留能量应对 BOSS 的困难阶段
- 在 BOSS 血量见底时保留能量用超级必杀终结

In most boss battles, the recommended strategy is:
- Build the first super meter quickly through parrying at the start
- Avoid spending energy on EX moves right before phase transitions
- Reserve energy for difficult boss phases
- Save energy for super art finishers when boss health is low

## 三种超级必杀技详解 / Three Super Arts Detailed

### 能量光束 / Energy Beam

效果：发射一道覆盖全屏的巨大光束，对路径上所有敌人造成持续伤害
充能需求：5 格超级计量槽
伤害总量：约 35-40 点
持续时间：约 2 秒

Effect: Fires a massive screen-spanning beam dealing continuous damage to all enemies in its path
Energy requirement: 5 super meter cards
Total damage: Approximately 35-40 points
Duration: Approximately 2 seconds

能量光束是最直接的超级必杀，拥有最高的瞬间爆发伤害。光束在发射后持续约 2 秒，对 BOSS 造成多次伤害判定。最佳使用时机是在 BOSS 处于硬直状态、转换阶段的动画期间、或者 BOSS 暂停攻击的时候。能量光束的优点是伤害高且稳定，缺点是释放时你无法移动，需要确保自身安全。

Energy Beam is the most straightforward super art with the highest burst damage. The beam persists for approximately 2 seconds, dealing multiple damage ticks to the boss. Best used when the boss is stunned, during phase transition animations, or when the boss pauses its attacks. The advantage is high, consistent damage. The disadvantage is immobility during casting, requiring safety assurance.

**最佳使用场景 Best Scenarios**:
- BOSS 阶段转换动画期间 / During phase transition animations
- BOSS 被击晕或暂停攻击时 / When boss is stunned or pauses
- 需要快速结束战斗的收尾阶段 / During final phase for quick kill
- 配合咖啡护符实现高频释放 / Combined with Coffee for frequent casts

### 无敌冲锋 / Invincibility

效果：角色进入短暂无敌状态，自动对周围敌人造成伤害
充能需求：5 格超级计量槽
无敌持续时间：约 3 秒
每秒伤害：约 10 点

Effect: Character enters brief invincibility, automatically damaging nearby enemies
Energy requirement: 5 super meter cards
Invincibility duration: Approximately 3 seconds
Damage per second: Approximately 10 points

无敌冲锋提供了短暂的无敌时间和自动范围伤害。最佳使用时机是在弹幕密集到难以躲避的 BOSS 阶段。这枚必杀技的战略价值大于伤害价值——它让你在 3 秒内可以无视任何攻击，同时持续输出。对于防御能力较弱的玩家，无敌冲锋是比能量光束更安全的选择。

Invincibility provides brief invulnerability with automatic area damage. Best used during boss phases with bullet patterns so dense they become nearly undodgeable. This super art strategic value outweighs its damage value — it lets you ignore all attacks for 3 seconds while maintaining damage output. For players with weaker defensive skills, Invincibility is a safer choice than Energy Beam.

**最佳使用场景 Best Scenarios**:
- 弹幕密度极高无法躲避的阶段 / Phases with undodgeable bullet density
- 被逼入角落需要救命技能时 / When cornered and needing a lifesaver
- 不熟悉 BOSS 攻击模式的开荒期 / During learning phases for unfamiliar bosses
- 双人合作时掩护队友 / Covering for a co-op partner

### 巨型幽灵 / Giant Ghost

效果：召唤一个巨大的幽灵追击全屏敌人，造成多次伤害
充能需求：5 格超级计量槽
伤害总量：约 30-35 点
持续追击时间：约 5 秒

Effect: Summons a giant ghost that pursues all enemies across the screen, dealing multiple hits
Energy requirement: 5 super meter cards
Total damage: Approximately 30-35 points
Pursuit duration: Approximately 5 seconds

巨型幽灵释放后会自动追击 BOSS，你可以在幽灵输出期间专注于躲避。这枚必杀技的伤害虽然略低于能量光束，但胜在无需瞄准、不影响移动。对于移动频繁的 BOSS 或弹幕密集的战斗，巨型幽灵可以让你在保持全速移动的同时获得伤害输出。

The Giant Ghost automatically pursues the boss after summoning, allowing you to focus on dodging while it deals damage. Although its damage is slightly lower than Energy Beam, its advantage lies in requiring no aiming and not affecting mobility. For frequently moving bosses or bullet-dense battles, Giant Ghost lets you maintain full mobility while gaining damage output.

**最佳使用场景 Best Scenarios**:
- BOSS 频繁移动难以瞄准 / Bosses that move frequently and are hard to aim at
- 需要全神贯注躲避的战斗 / Battles requiring full attention on dodging
- 飞机关卡中使用效果优良 / Excellent in airplane levels
- 搭配追踪枪实现全自动输出模式 / Combined with Chaser for full auto-damage mode

## EX 技能完全指南 / Complete EX Move Guide

EX 技能消耗 1 格能量，是超级必杀的"轻量版"。合理使用 EX 技能可以让你在维持能量的同时获得额外的爆发伤害。

EX moves consume 1 card of energy, serving as lightweight versions of super arts. Proper use of EX moves lets you gain additional burst damage while maintaining energy reserves.

### 各武器 EX 技能详解 / Weapon EX Move Breakdown

**豌豆重击 / Peashooter EX**：
发射一枚大型豌豆弹丸，造成 9 点伤害（普通射击的三倍）。弹丸飞行速度快，弹道直，适合在 BOSS 站定时补刀。能量性价比高，但伤害上限有限。

Fires a large pea projectile dealing 9 damage (three times normal shot). Fast projectile speed with straight trajectory, suitable for finishing blows when the boss is stationary. Good energy efficiency but limited damage ceiling.

**散射爆裂 / Spread EX**：
近距离扇形喷射 8 枚弹片，每枚 3 点伤害，全中约 24 点。是游戏中能量性价比最高的 EX 技能，但需要贴脸使用。在 BOSS 近身时的爆发力甚至超过部分超级必杀。

Fires 8 shrapnel pieces in a close-range fan pattern, each dealing 3 damage, totaling approximately 24 at full hit. The most energy-efficient EX move in the game, but requires point-blank range. Its close-range burst potential even exceeds some super arts.

**追踪连射 / Chaser EX**：
发射 4 枚追踪弹头，每枚 3 点伤害。自动追踪目标，适合在需要频繁移动的战斗中使用。伤害分散但稳定持续。

Fires 4 homing projectiles, each dealing 3 damage. Auto-tracks targets, suitable for battles requiring frequent movement. Damage is distributed but consistently reliable.

**抛射弹幕 / Lobber EX**：
投掷 4 枚抛物线弹头，每枚 4 点伤害。弹头落地后残留伤害区域。适合封锁 BOSS 走位或攻击掩体后的目标。

Throws 4 arcing projectiles, each dealing 4 damage. Projectiles leave damage zones on landing. Suitable for area denial or hitting targets behind cover.

**充能爆破 / Charge EX**：
一发蓄力攻击，伤害 20 点。是全游戏单发伤害最高的 EX 技能，但需要较长的前摇时间。在 BOSS 硬直期间使用效果最佳。

A charged attack dealing 20 damage. The highest single-hit damage EX in the game, requiring longer startup time. Best used during boss stun windows.
