## Boss系统概述 / Boss System Overview

**中文：** 对马岛之魂的Boss战主要由决斗（Duel）形式呈现——一对一的正面对决。决斗中不能使用战鬼武器（苦无、烟雾弹等），只能依靠剑术、格挡和闪避。每个Boss有不同的攻击模式和节奏，需要观察和适应。最终Boss汗王赫通汗是一场多阶段的史诗战斗。

**English:** Boss fights in Ghost of Tsushima are primarily Duels — one-on-one standoffs. During duels, ghost weapons (kunai, smoke bombs, etc.) are disabled. Only swordsmanship, blocks, and dodges are available. Each boss has unique attack patterns and rhythms. The final boss, Khotun Khan, is a multi-phase epic battle.

## 决斗机制 / Duel Mechanics

**中文：** 决斗是对马岛之魂中最具挑战性的战斗形式。以下是决斗的核心规则和技巧。

**English:** Duels are the most challenging combat form in Ghost of Tsushima. Core rules and tips below.

**决斗规则 / Duel Rules**:
1. 不可使用战鬼武器 / Ghost weapons disabled
2. 不可使用弓箭 / Bow disabled
3. 可使用天之一刀、流水等剑术架势 / Sword stances and techniques available
4. 完美闪避和完美格挡是关键 / Perfect dodges and perfect parries are key
5. 决斗中有气力条管理 / Resolve management during duels

**决斗技巧 / Duel Tips**:
- 先观察：前30秒不要进攻，熟悉Boss的攻击节奏 / Observe first 30 seconds, learn boss attack rhythm
- 完美格挡后接重击可以造成大量气力伤害 / Perfect parry then heavy attack deals massive resolve damage
- 流水架势对枪兵类敌人有克制效果 / Water Stance counters spear-type enemies
- 磐石架势适合对抗剑术型Boss / Stone Stance is good against sword-type bosses
- 躲避红圈攻击（不可格挡）/ Dodge red-circle attacks (unblockable)

{/* ===== 龙三 / Ryuzo ===== */}

## 龙三 / Ryuzo (暴徒首领)

**中文：** 龙三曾是境井仁的童年好友，后来成为暴徒的首领。这场决斗在岩井山进行，是游戏中期的重要转折点。

**English:** Ryuzo was Jin's childhood friend who became leader of the Straw Hats. This duel takes place at Mount Iwai and is a significant mid-game turning point.

**阶段一 / Phase 1 (HP 100%-50%)**:
- 攻击模式：快速三连斩 → 突刺 → 横扫 / Fast 3-hit combo → thrust → sweep
- 应对：格挡前两刀，闪避第三刀（横扫）/ Block first two, dodge third (sweep)
- 反击窗口：三连斩结束后有1秒硬直 / 1s recovery window after his 3-hit combo

**阶段二 / Phase 2 (HP 50%-0%)**:
- 新增攻击：不可格挡的蓄力突刺 / New: unblockable charged thrust
- 攻击频率增加 / Increased attack speed
- 应对：看到红圈时向侧面闪避 / Side-dodge when red circle appears
- 蓄力突刺后是他最大的硬直窗口，接3-4次轻击 / Charged thrust leaves him wide open for 3-4 light attacks

**奖励 / Reward**: 暴徒之剑（新武器） / Straw Hat Sword (new weapon)

{/* ===== 巴 / The Batsu ===== */}

## 巴 / The Batsu (弓术大师)

**中文：** 巴是游戏中唯一的女性Boss，使用弓箭和太刀的组合攻击。战斗在废弃的渔村中进行。

**English**: The Batsu is the game's only female boss, combining bow and katana attacks. The fight takes place in an abandoned fishing village.

**阶段一 / Phase 1 (远程阶段)**:
- 战术：巴会在远处射箭，保持移动 / Batsu stays at range, shooting arrows
- 应对：不断横向移动接近，不要在开阔地停留 / Constant lateral movement, don't stop in open areas
- 注意：她的箭可以连射三发 / She fires three arrows in quick succession

**阶段二 / Phase 2 (近战阶段)**:
- 切换为太刀近战 / Switches to katana melee
- 攻击模式：快速的斜劈→上挑→下劈 / Fast diagonal slash → uppercut → overhead
- 应对：她的攻击速度快但伤害低——可以用格挡硬吃 / Fast but low damage — can block through
- 完美格挡两到三次可以让她进入喘息状态 / Two to three perfect parries stun her

**特殊机制 / Special Mechanic**: 战斗过程中巴会阶段性地撤退到高台射箭，需要追击 / Batsu periodically retreats to high ground to shoot arrows — pursue her

**奖励 / Reward**: 巴的护身符（增加弓箭伤害）/ Batsu's Charm (increases bow damage)

{/* ===== 志村大人 / Lord Shimura ===== */}

## 志村大人 / Lord Shimura (最终Boss之一)

**中文：** 志村大人是境井仁的叔父兼养父，也是对马岛的武家统治者。这场决斗在黄金寺前的雪地进行，是游戏最感人的Boss战之一。

**English**: Lord Shimura is Jin's uncle and adoptive father, the samurai ruler of Tsushima. This duel takes place in the snow before the Golden Temple and is one of the game's most emotional battles.

**三个阶段 / Three Phases**:

**阶段一 / Phase 1**: 标准剑术对决，双方试探性攻击 / Standard sword duel, probing attacks. 完美格挡2-3次可进入阶段二 / 2-3 perfect parries pushes to phase 2

**阶段二 / Phase 2**: 志村速度加快，加入突进技和回旋斩 / Speed increases, adds lunges and spinning slashes. 注意他的假动作——有时他会佯攻后接横扫 / Watch for feints — sometimes he feints then sweeps

**阶段三 / Phase 3**: 志村进入狂暴状态，攻击频率极高 / Shimura enters enraged state, extremely high attack rate. 此时不要进攻，专注格挡和闪避 / Don't attack, focus on blocking and dodging. 等他精疲力尽时反击 / Counter when he exhausts himself

**结局 / Outcome**: 根据剧情选择——给予志村荣耀之死或放他一条生路 / Story choice — give Shimura an honorable death or spare him

{/* ===== 汗王赫通汗 / Khotun Khan ===== */}

## 汗王赫通汗 / Khotun Khan (最终Boss)

**中文：** 赫通汗是蒙古入侵军的总帅，游戏的最终Boss。战斗在对马城进行，分为四个阶段。

**English**: Khotun Khan is the leader of the Mongol invasion and the final boss. The fight takes place at Castle Tsushima in four phases.

**阶段一 / Phase 1 — 城门前 / Castle Gate**:
- 赫通用大刀，攻击范围大但速度慢 / Khan uses a large blade, wide range but slow
- 策略：格挡他的重击后反击，不要贪刀 / Block his heavy attacks then counter, don't overcommit
- 注意他的抓取技（不可格挡）/ Watch for his grab attack (unblockable)

**阶段二 / Phase 2 — 城墙上 / Castle Walls**:
- 赫通召唤蒙古弓箭手支援 / Khan summons Mongol archers for support
- 优先处理弓箭手，然后继续对决 / Prioritize archers, then resume the duel
- 场景狭窄，注意走位 / Narrow area, careful positioning

**阶段三 / Phase 3 — 城堡大厅 / Castle Hall**:
- 赫通使用火焰附魔武器，攻击附带燃烧 / Khan uses fire-enchanted weapon, attacks cause burn
- 他的攻击不可格挡，只能闪避 / His attacks become unblockable, only dodgeable
- 等待他攻击后的大硬直窗口反击 / Wait for his large recovery window after attacks

**阶段四 / Phase 4 — 最终对决 / Final Stand**:
- 赫通进入最终形态，全屏攻击 + 连续突进 / Khan's final form, full-screen attacks + charges
- 使用天之一刀可以快速削减他的气力 / Heaven's Strike quickly depletes his resolve
- 保持耐心，一次只打1-2下然后闪避 / Patience is key, 1-2 hits then dodge

**击破后 / After Defeat**: 选择杀死赫通或俘虏他——两种选择都合理，但结局动画不同 / Choose to kill or capture Khan — both are valid but with different ending cinematics

## 决斗装备推荐 / Duel Equipment

**中文：** 决斗中不能使用战鬼武器，因此剑术相关的装备和护身符是唯一的选择。以下装备在决斗中表现最佳。

**English:** Ghost weapons are disabled in duels, so sword-related gear and charms are the only options. These perform best in duels.

**推荐铠甲 / Recommended Armor**:
- 境井家传铠甲：完美格挡后攻击力+40%持续10秒 / Samurai Clan Armor: +40% atk after perfect parry for 10s
- 剑圣铠甲：增加完美格挡窗口+近战伤害+20% / Sword Saint Armor: extended parry window +20% melee dmg

**推荐护身符 / Recommended Charms**:
- 天之一击：完美格挡后攻击力+50%持续10秒 / Heaven's Strike: +50% atk after perfect parry
- 不屈：格挡不消耗气力 / Unyielding: blocking costs no resolve
- 流水：闪避后攻击速度+30%持续6秒 / Flowing: +30% atk speed after dodge for 6s
