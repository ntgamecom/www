{/* ===== 秘密铠甲 / Secret Armor ===== */}

## 秘密铠甲获取 / Secret Armor Acquisition

**中文：** 以下铠甲需要通过探索获得，不在常规任务线中。

**English:** These armor sets are obtained through exploration, not through questlines.

**战国武士铠甲 / Samurai Warlord Armor**:
- 位置：上县隐藏地牢 / Kamiagata hidden dungeon
- 解锁条件：找到地牢入口（在一个被树藤覆盖的井中）/ Find dungeon entrance (vine-covered well)
- 效果：增加最大气力+2，近战伤害+15% / +2 max Resolve, +15% melee dmg

**暗影战鬼铠甲 / Shadow Ghost Armor**:
- 位置：丰玉的坟墓群 / Toyotama burial grounds
- 解锁条件：夜晚在特定墓碑前跪拜10秒 / Kneel at specific tombstone at night for 10 seconds
- 效果：潜行时移动速度+20%，暗杀时噪音-50% / +20% stealth speed, -50% assassination noise

**古代浪人铠甲 / Ancient Ronin Armor**:
- 位置：严原隐藏海岸洞穴 / Izuhara hidden sea cave
- 解锁条件：在满月时穿过洞穴中的水潭 / Cross the cave pool during a full moon
- 效果：生命值低于30%时攻击力+30% / +30% attack power when below 30% HP

{/* ===== 荣誉之柱 / Pillars of Honor ===== */}

## 荣誉之柱 / Pillars of Honor

**中文：** 荣誉之柱是散落在对马岛各处的剑刃外观收集品。收集后可以在装备界面更换刀刃外观。

**English:** Pillars of Honor are sword kit collectibles scattered across Tsushima. Collected kits can be equipped as blade skins.

- 全岛共有20+个荣誉之柱 / 20+ pillars across the island
- 每个柱子插着一把剑，拔出即可获得外观 / Each pillar has a sword — pull it for the skin
- 荣誉之柱通常位于偏僻的山顶或隐秘的角落 / Pillars are usually on remote peaks or hidden corners
- 全收集解锁隐藏奖杯"剑刃收集家" / Full collection unlocks "Blade Collector" trophy

## 彩蛋收集 / Easter Eggs

**中文：** 对马岛之魂中的隐藏彩蛋。

**English:** Hidden easter eggs in Ghost of Tsushima.

1. **黑泽明模式**：游戏设置中可以切换黑白电影风格，致敬黑泽明 / Kurosawa Mode: black-and-white film style tribute to Akira Kurosawa
2. **宫本武藏**：某个NPC讲述的故事中提到了宫本武藏的五轮书 / NPC story references Miyamoto Musashi's Book of Five Rings
3. **风之引导**：按触摸板上划的风是游戏最具标志性的功能 / Swipe up on touchpad to call wind — the game's signature feature
4. **彩蝶**：在特定地点可以找到彩色蝴蝶跟随仁 / Colorful butterflies that follow Jin at specific locations
5. **俳句彩蛋**：完成俳句小游戏后获得的诗句可以在游戏内查看 / Completed haiku poems are viewable in-game
6. **狐狸窝**：摸狐狸可以获得好运气（实际效果是增加护身符槽）/ Petting foxes brings good luck (actually increases charm slots)
7. **开发者温泉**：在黄金寺某处隐藏温泉中有开发者的留言 / Developer messages in a hidden onsen at Golden Temple
