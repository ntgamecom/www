## 欢迎来到城市天际线2 / Welcome to Cities: Skylines 2

《城市天际线2》是由Colossal Order开发、Paradox Interactive发行的城市建造模拟游戏，于2023年10月24日发布。作为《城市天际线》的续作，本作在画面、模拟深度、AI系统和经济模型上进行了全面革新，为玩家带来前所未有的城市建设体验。无论你是初次接触城市建造游戏的新手，还是从一代回归的老玩家，这份指南都将帮助你建立并管理一座繁荣的现代化大都市。

Cities: Skylines 2 is a city-building simulation game developed by Colossal Order and published by Paradox Interactive, released on October 24, 2023. As the successor to the original Cities: Skylines, this sequel brings comprehensive innovations in graphics, simulation depth, AI systems, and economic models, delivering an unprecedented urban construction experience. Whether you are a beginner new to city-building games or a veteran returning from the first game, this guide will help you build and manage a thriving modern metropolis.

### 游戏特色 / Game Features

本作引入了许多令人兴奋的新特性和改进机制。与前作相比，城市天际线2拥有更加真实的市民模拟系统，每个市民都有独立的工作、生活和出行需求。全新的经济系统模拟了从原材料开采到成品生产的完整产业链。改进后的交通AI让车辆能够智能选择车道和路径，大大减少了不合理的交通行为。此外，动态季节系统、天气变化和自然灾害为城市管理增添了更多挑战和变数。

The sequel introduces many exciting new features and improved mechanics. Compared to its predecessor, Cities: Skylines 2 features a more realistic citizen simulation system where each citizen has independent work, life, and travel needs. A brand new economic system simulates the complete production chain from raw material extraction to finished goods manufacturing. The improved traffic AI allows vehicles to intelligently choose lanes and routes, significantly reducing unreasonable traffic behavior. Additionally, dynamic seasons, weather changes, and natural disasters add more challenges and variables to city management.

### 游戏模式 / Game Modes

城市天际线2提供了多种游戏模式供玩家选择。沙盒模式（Sandbox Mode）去除了金钱和里程碑限制，让你可以自由发挥创意建造理想城市。标准模式（Standard Mode）需要谨慎管理预算和资源，逐步解锁建筑和服务。地图编辑器（Map Editor）允许你创建自定义地形和地图布局。资产编辑器（Asset Editor）则让你可以设计和导入自定义建筑模型。

Cities: Skylines 2 offers several game modes for players to choose from. Sandbox Mode removes money and milestone restrictions, allowing you to freely build your ideal city. Standard Mode requires careful budget and resource management, progressively unlocking buildings and services. The Map Editor allows you to create custom terrain and map layouts. The Asset Editor lets you design and import custom building models.

### 系统要求 / System Requirements

了解游戏的最低和推荐配置有助于优化游戏体验。最低配置要求包括 Intel Core i7-6700K 或 AMD Ryzen 5 2600X 处理器、8GB 内存和 NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 或 AMD Radeon RX 480 显卡。推荐配置为 Intel Core i7-12700K 或 AMD Ryzen 7 5800X、16GB 内存和 NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080 或 AMD Radeon RX 6800 XT。固态硬盘显著缩短加载时间。大型城市的后期发展对CPU性能要求较高。

Understanding the minimum and recommended system requirements helps optimize the gaming experience. Minimum requirements include Intel Core i7-6700K or AMD Ryzen 5 2600X processor, 8GB RAM, and NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 or AMD Radeon RX 480 graphics card. Recommended specifications include Intel Core i7-12700K or AMD Ryzen 7 5800X, 16GB RAM, and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080 or AMD Radeon RX 6800 XT. Solid-state drives significantly reduce loading times. Late-game development of large cities demands high CPU performance.

## 新手建议 / Tips for New Players

如果你是第一次接触城市天际线系列，以下建议将帮助你更快上手。首先，不要急于扩张，初期集中精力建设一个小而高效的社区。确保住宅区靠近工作地点，商业区靠近居民区，以减少通勤距离。其次，密切关注预算，避免过早建设昂贵的公共服务设施。第三，合理规划道路网络，为未来的交通流量预留扩展空间。最后，多观察市民的行为反馈，及时调整你的规划策略，城市会告诉你它需要什么。

If this is your first time with the Cities: Skylines series, the following tips will help you get started faster. First, do not rush expansion; focus on building a small but efficient community initially. Ensure residential areas are close to workplaces and commercial areas are near residential zones to reduce commuting distances. Second, monitor your budget closely and avoid building expensive public service facilities too early. Third, plan your road network wisely, leaving room for future traffic flow expansion. Finally, observe citizen behavior feedback and adjust your planning strategies in time. Your city will tell you what it needs.

## 游戏机制概览 / Core Mechanics Overview

城市天际线2的核心机制围绕六个主要系统展开。区域规划系统决定了城市的功能分区和发展密度。交通系统涵盖了从道路建设到公共交通的方方面面。经济系统管理着城市的财政收入、支出和产业链。公共服务系统维持市民的幸福度和城市运营效率。水电设施系统为城市提供必需的能源和资源。发展系统通过里程碑和升级路径引导城市成长。所有这些系统相互影响，一个领域的决策会波及其他领域，这正是城市建造游戏的魅力所在。

The core mechanics of Cities: Skylines 2 revolve around six main systems. The Zoning system determines the functional districts and development density of the city. The Transportation system covers everything from road construction to public transit. The Economy system manages municipal revenue, expenditure, and production chains. The Public Services system maintains citizen happiness and city operational efficiency. The Utilities system provides essential energy and resources. The Progression system guides city growth through milestones and upgrade paths. All these systems interact with each other, and decisions in one area ripple through others. This interconnectedness is what makes city-building games so fascinating.

### 市民模拟系统 / Citizen Simulation

本作的市民模拟系统是游戏最突出的改进之一。每个市民都有独特的身份特征、技能水平、收入状况和个人偏好。市民会根据这些因素选择居住地、工作地点和出行方式。市民会经历出生、成长、工作、退休和死亡的生命周期。教育水平影响市民的职业选择和收入水平。高学历市民倾向于从事办公和商业工作，低收入市民多在工业和低端服务业就业。市民的幸福感由多方面因素综合决定。

The citizen simulation system in this sequel is one of the most prominent improvements. Each citizen has unique identity traits, skill levels, income status, and personal preferences. Citizens choose residences, workplaces, and travel modes based on these factors. Citizens go through life cycles of birth, growth, work, retirement, and death. Education level affects career choices and income levels. Highly educated citizens tend to work in office and commercial jobs, while low-income citizens mostly work in industrial and basic service sectors. Citizen happiness is determined by multiple factors comprehensively.

Cities: Skylines 2 was initially released on PC platform, supporting Windows systems. The game has certain hardware requirements. It is recommended to use a computer with at least 16GB RAM, a mid-to-high-end graphics card (such as NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 or equivalent), and an SSD for smooth gameplay. The game supports both Steam Workshop and Paradox Mods platforms, providing players with vast community-created content. Console versions are planned for later release on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S platforms.

## 与一代对比 / Comparison with Cities: Skylines 1

与前作相比，城市天际线2在多个方面有显著提升。画面引擎全面升级，支持更精细的模型和更真实的光影效果。市民AI从简单模拟升级为个体化模拟，每个市民都有独立的行为模式。经济系统从线性模型升级为产业链模拟，资源流动更加真实。道路系统支持更灵活的车道管理和更丰富的道路类型。区域系统引入了中密度选项和更精细的密度控制。公共服务设施支持升级扩展，不再是单一的放置建筑。这些改进让城市天际线2成为真正的次世代城市建造游戏。

Compared to its predecessor, Cities: Skylines 2 has significant improvements in multiple areas. The graphics engine has been fully upgraded, supporting more detailed models and more realistic lighting effects. Citizen AI has evolved from simple simulation to individual simulation, with each citizen having independent behavior patterns. The economic system has progressed from a linear model to production chain simulation, with more realistic resource flow. The road system supports more flexible lane management and richer road types. The zoning system introduces medium-density options and more refined density control. Public service facilities support upgrade expansion, no longer being single-placement buildings. These improvements make Cities: Skylines 2 a true next-generation city-building game.

关于城市天际线2玩家经常询问一些问题。城市启动初期如何快速提升人口？确保住宅区划分充足并建设基本服务可以提高吸引力。交通拥堵如何解决？优化道路层级结构和发展公共交通是根本方法。财政赤字怎么处理？减少非必要支出并适当调整税率。模组安装后游戏崩溃怎么办？逐个排查冲突模组并确保所有模组已更新到最新版本。如何解锁高级建筑？提升城市人口等级并满足特定的里程碑条件。这些问题涉及游戏各个方面，理解其答案可以帮助玩家更顺利地进行游戏。

Players frequently ask various questions about Cities: Skylines 2. How to quickly increase population in the early game? Troubleshoot conflicting mods one by one and ensure all mods are updated to the latest version. How to unlock advanced buildings?
