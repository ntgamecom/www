# 流派构建 / Builds & Synergies

以撒的结合的道具之间有着复杂的协同效果——正确的道具组合可以让你的角色从一个弱小婴儿变成毁灭一切的怪物。本章覆盖主流Build和经典配合 / Binding of Isaac items have complex synergies — the right item combo can transform your character from a weak baby into a screen-wide destroyer. This chapter covers meta builds and classic synergies.
