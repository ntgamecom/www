# 漫威争锋 / Marvel Rivals

欢迎来到漫威争锋——由网易游戏（NetEase Games）开发，漫威正版授权的第三人称6v6团队英雄射击游戏。本指南将帮助你从新手成长为高手。

Welcome to Marvel Rivals — a third-person 6v6 team-based hero shooter developed by NetEase Games under official Marvel license. This guide will help you progress from beginner to expert.

## 游戏概览 / Game Overview

漫威争锋汇集了漫威宇宙中最具标志性的超级英雄与反派，在快节奏的团队战斗中展开对决。每位英雄都拥有独特的技能组和终极技能，鼓励玩家探索不同的战斗风格和团队配合。

Marvel Rivals brings together the most iconic Marvel superheroes and villains in fast-paced team battles. Each hero features a unique kit and ultimate ability, encouraging players to explore different playstyles and team synergies.

## 核心特色 / Key Features

- **英雄阵容 / Hero Roster**: 不断扩大的漫威英雄与反派阵容 / Ever-expanding roster of Marvel heroes and villains
- **团队合作 / Team Play**: 强调阵容搭配与技能组合 / Emphasis on team composition and ability combos
- **动态战场 / Dynamic Battlegrounds**: 可破坏环境与互动地图元素 / Destructible environments and interactive map elements
- **连携技能 / Team-Up Abilities**: 英雄之间独特的组合技能 / Unique combo abilities between heroes
- **排位系统 / Ranked System**: 竞技性天梯匹配 / Competitive ladder matchmaking
